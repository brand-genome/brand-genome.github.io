## Brand Guidelines

### 2023

**Logo Information:**

- Full color logo version (positive and reversed-out shown).
- One color logo version (positive and reversed-out shown).
- Black & White logo version (positive and reversed-out shown).
- Clear space rules (ensure no other elements encroach; spacing allows design to breathe).
- Minimum width requirement (digital logo width not less than 30 px; print version no less than 3 cm).
- Do's: Use positive or reversed logo versions. Ensure enough contrast with the background.
- Don'ts: Do not recolor the logo, rotate, compress, or distort it. Avoid insufficient contrast.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: Oceanic Blue; HEX: #0A20CD.
- Secondary color: Midday Sun Yellow; stands as an accent tone (e.g., visible in logo dot).
- Other secondary colors: Twilight Blue (#403C35), Aquamarine, Mediterranean Sky, Sand Shells, Wooden Pier.
- Neutral colors: Abyssal Black and Light Gray, used for text or minimalistic backgrounds.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: Roboto Flex. Range from Thin to ExtraBlack, Regular and Italic styles available.
- Font attributes: Multilingual support; includes accents; anatomy is characterized by single-story letters and rounded shapes.
- Font usage examples provided for headlines, subheadings, and callouts.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style: Emphasis on context (landscapes, storytelling). Contextual or geometric details are integral.
- Subjects: Relaxed students, maritime references, and dramatic compositions scaling meaningful features. Includes themes of exploration and sustainability.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grid system layout defined as flexible and adaptable. Structured, leaving white areas for content readability.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone: Friendly, accessible, clever, and bold. Statements use direct language with maritime references.
- Messaging examples: 'Sail into your future,' 'A tide of salty wisdom,' 'Ride the wave of knowledge.'

**Notes:**

- The guidelines feature applications on merchandise, including t-shirts, notebooks, and caps. Logo maintained across all executions.

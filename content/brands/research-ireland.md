## Brand Guidelines

### 2024

**Logo Information:**

- Our logo is inspired by Irish Insular scripts (4th-5th century) and migratory birds like the swift—combining geometric precision with calligraphic and natural references.
- Logo versions must appear only in core brand colors—Pale Lavender or Bush Green. Black or white versions are acceptable for limited use cases.
- Logo misuse guidelines—e.g., don't change orientation, typeface, proportions, scale relation, or create versions emphasizing one language or two-color versions.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors: Pale Lavender (#DCD0FF) and Bush Green (#112B19). Secondary palette includes Christine (#E2740E), Fuel Yellow (#ECAF30), Port Gore (#2A2457), Scampi (#7169B2), Zucchini (#307159), Granny Smith (#9CD696), Brown Derby (#472616), Santa Fe (#C47965).
- Accessible color contrasts per WCAG 2.0 Level AA/AAA—e.g., Pale Lavender (#DCD0FF) contrasts with multiple options like Bush Green (#112B19) and others with contrast ratios exceeding 4.5:1.

**Typography Information:**

- Main typeface is BW Gradual (Bold and Regular weights)—a geometric sans-serif typeface with high contrast details. Alternate typeface is Inter from Google Fonts.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography approach highlights heroes (researchers, staff, partners) in action. Avoid posed setups or direct eye contact in stock imagery. Represent diversity in gender, ethnicity, etc. Favour natural lighting.
- Gradient overlays improve consistency when incorporating third-party images of varying style and quality. Color pairings can use any colors from the brand palette.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logomark built using overlapping geometric squares and circles. These forms serve as a template for patterns, icons, and image overlays.
- Social media overlays use logomark components for format consistency across posts, stories, and videos. Overlays help establish clear text or logo placement areas.
- Rounded corners in illustrations and photographs reflect the curves in the logomark geometry. Rounding is always circular and never elliptical.

**Brand Voice:**

- Research Ireland promotes collaboration and curiosity, focusing on the global exchange of ideas. Messaging aims to inspire research excellence while maintaining consistency in appearance and tone.

**Notes:**

- Sample applications like PowerPoint templates can be requested from the organization. Design examples cover content slides, breakers, and covers.

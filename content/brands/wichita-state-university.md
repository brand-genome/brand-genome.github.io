## Brand Guidelines

### 2022

**Logo Information:**

- The primary institutional logo incorporates wheat as a symbol, representing the university's history and values. Variations for usage on colored backgrounds and photographic placements are described.
- Clear space guidelines require at least one-half of the logo height as free space surrounding all logos.
- Guidelines prohibit animating the institutional logo (e.g., exploding, eroding, flipping, melting, spinning, extruding, becoming three-dimensional).
- Incorrect usage examples of the institutional logo are outlined; improper alterations like stretching, applying filters, or adding non-standard colors are disallowed.

**Color Information:**

- Official colors are Shocker Yellow™ and black. Pantone, CMYK, RGB, and HEX representations are described for accuracy. Alternate colors require approval.
- Use of institutional logos on photographic and colored backgrounds must follow rules to maintain visibility and integrity.

**Typography Information:**

- Core identity fonts include Klavika, Bodoni, and Adobe Garamond. Klavika Bold Caps is used for official content and signage.
- Typography standards apply across all media, including video projects where the identity fonts should be prioritized.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Use of photography should complement the institutional logo. Guidelines emphasize avoiding visually busy or patterned backgrounds.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum clear space around logos: one-half the height of the logo. This applies to all backgrounds and placements.
- Guidelines for signage layouts, including dimensions and placements of building identifiers, directories, vehicular, and pedestrian wayfinding signage.

**Brand Voice:**

- Wichita State's brand voice is built on consistent quality, community engagement, and unified communication. Messaging emphasizes hard work and commitment to excellence.

**Notes:**

- Use of institutional logos and trademarks requires licensing approval. Separate logos for university entities (colleges, departments, etc.) are not permitted, except for sanctioned student organizations.
- Trademark designation symbols are required for all merchandise bearing Wichita State marks.
- For questions or assistance with visual identity standards, contact Strategic Communications or visit the official website.

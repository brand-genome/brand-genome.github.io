## Brand Guidelines

### 2023

**Logo Information:**

- The Ayvens logo consists of three components: the symbol, the brand's name, and the Societe Generale Group endorsement. It is advised to use the logo in Freedom White or Serene Blue whenever possible.
- The logo can be used in various scenarios: 1. In Serene Blue on Freedom White, Sustainable Turquoise, or light photographic background; 2. In Freedom White on Serene Blue, Sustainable Turquoise, or photographic background; 3. In monochromatic black on Freedom White or light photographic backgrounds.
- Minimum size requirement for the logo is 35mm (276px) to ensure good legibility. A protection zone is defined around the logo for optimal legibility and to prevent interference from nearby graphic elements. The protection zone is equal to "X" around the logo.
- Do's and Don'ts for the logo: Avoid color changes, distortion, omission of the symbol, modification of proportions, and placing the logo on inappropriate or overly busy backgrounds. The logo should not be used vertically or with any additional elements.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes: Sustainable Turquoise (R:115 G:210 B:210; Pantone 2227 C), Freedom White (R:255 G:255 B:255; RAL 9016), Serene Blue (R:15 G:53 B:73; Pantone 302 C), and Optimistic Yellow (R:255 G:220 B:70; Pantone 108 C).
- The secondary color palette is mainly applied in websites and PowerPoint presentations, considering accessibility requirements for digital use.
- The additional color palette is reserved for graphics and data representations, adhering to accessibility standards.
- Colors should be used with accessibility standards in mind, particularly for digital media and presentations.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Chillax. It is used for titles, headlines, and key figures due to its optimistic and rounded quality. Typeface weights include Medium and Regular.
- Secondary typeface: Source Sans 3. It is used for composing body copy across various typographic weights such as Regular, Italic, Medium, Semi-bold, and Bold.
- For non-Latin alphabets: Almarai (Arabic), Commissioner Thin (Greek and Cyrillic).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photographic styles follow a light and sustainable aesthetic. The symbol can be integrated in the background to enhance compositions and visual appeal, ensuring legibility.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The logo and symbol have defined protection zones. For the logo, spacing of 'X' around it is required. For the symbol, a minimum space of half 'X' is required.
- Minimum size for the symbol is 4 mm (22 px) for small applications like favicons or branding on small items.

**Brand Voice:**

- Core tagline: 'Seamless and more sustainable mobility for more people.' The tagline can be applied in three ways: 1. Opposite the logo, 2. Centered under a block of text, 3. As a closure to a piece of copy, always following the brand name.

**Notes:**

- Logo usage exceptions: The logo may be used without endorsement for specific manufacturing cases like engraving, embroidery, or very small sizes.
- The symbol from the logo can be used independently as an outline, full color, black, and white versions. It can also be used as a design mask to integrate visuals or images.
- Accessibility standards must be a focal point for all color usage to ensure inclusivity and ease of visibility.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The logo features four letters of the acronym 'De.C.O.' in a geometric, concentric arrangement representing different types of plates: D for flat plates, C and O for deep plates, and E for dessert plates. It includes a stylized fork with a germinated sprout on one tine, symbolizing natural authenticity.
- Clear space rule: The area of respect specifies the minimum space (X distance) required between the logo and other elements such as text or visuals. This proportional space is integral to the logo identity.

**Color Information:**

- The logo primarily uses the color orange, signifying harmony, creativity, balance, and ambition. It is particularly fitting for products associated with health, wellness, and appetite.

**Notes:**

- The manual emphasizes alignment and uniformity in applying the logo based on guidelines to ensure effective communication and brand representation.
- Special cases of logo usage not detailed in the manual require approval from the Comune di Cuneo, owner of the De.C.O. logo.

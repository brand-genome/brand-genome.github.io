## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The logotype is primarily used in its gray-blue color. For boutiques, the logo can also be used in white or interpreted with materials such as wood or metal. The logo must always include its baseline.
- Other brand identities such as AVRIL MEN and AVRIL BABY adhere to the same basic usage rules as the parent brand.
- Logo key color: gray-blue, CMYK: 75 58 45 50, RGB: 55 65 75, Pantone: 432 C, RAL: 70 2 6.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes gray-blue (CMYK: 75 58 45 50 / RGB: 55 65 75 / Pantone: 432 C), off-white (CMYK: 00 18 15 05 / RGB: 242 214 205), white (CMYK: 00 00 00 07 / RGB: 242 242 242), black (CMYK: 00 00 00 100 / RGB: 29 29 27), sage green (CMYK: 25 10 20 00 / RGB: 203 214 208), and greige (CMYK: 12 10 15 00 / RGB: 230 226 218).
- Additional colors such as blue-green, sage green, and greige can be used sparingly, especially in boutique environments.
- Black is prominently used in text to establish modernity and contrast with the color palette.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typography for print: Gotham Medium and Book.
- Web typography includes Lato Bold, Lato Light, and Playfair Display Bold.
- Typography usage: Typography must ensure coherence across all communication platforms (print, web, social media).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style: Modern, minimalist, and natural compositions with soft materials, design details, and natural lighting. Textures and materials should evoke the senses such as fabric touch and wood grain.
- Images should have a human touch, zoomed-in compositions, or an intimate feel. Simplicity and minimalism are central.
- Photos emphasize harmony, simplicity, and an emotional connection, guided by the brand essence.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Consistency between boutiques and overall brand identity is achieved through a unified graphic line and minimalist design.
- Boutique layouts feature noble materials, discrete lines, and zones dedicated to different product ranges.

**Brand Voice:**

- Key tone descriptors: Soft, simple, modern, with minimal text. The messaging reflects peace, well-being, and authenticity.
- Writing style: Uses varied sentence styles (single words, subject-verb-object phrases, enumerations). Avoids excessive anglicisms except for rare instances.
- Messaging themes evoke well-being through sensory references (e.g., touch, smell) and often reference natural beauty.
- Messaging focuses on brand values such as accessibility, simplicity, and harmony.

**Notes:**

- The brand encourages sustainable practices by using renewable energy in boutiques, reusing cartons for online shipping, and implementing designs like passive or energy-positive offices.
- Avril's website reflects the same minimalist aesthetics found in boutiques and printed media.
- Social media encourages storytelling to enrich brand values and engage audiences. Images evoke well-being and highlight the product in lifestyle settings.
- Social media guidelines suggest limiting the use of hashtags, ensuring placement below the main body of text.

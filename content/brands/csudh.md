## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The logo should be used in its entirety with no alterations or additional elements added to it. The logo must always be reproduced from a digital master reference available in eps, jpeg, and png format. File formats: eps (professional usage), jpeg (desktop publishing), png (digital usage).
- The logo only appears in four color variants: Burgundy, yellow, gray (both 75% and 40% Black), and single-color Black, grayscale, or white. Other color combinations should be avoided. Ensure good contrast to maximize impact and accessibility.
- Use of the logo requires permission from the CSUDH, University Communications, and Public Affairs.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette uses: Pantone 209 C (Burgundy), Pantone 124 C (Yellow). Grayscale colors include 75% Black and 40% Black. CMYK, RGB, and HEX equivalents are provided for application usage.
- Accent color palettes include colors such as Turquoise (Pantone 7466 C), Green (Pantone 367 C), Purple (Pantone 526 C), Navy (Pantone 302 C), and others, along with their CMYK, RGB, and HEX presentations.

**Typography Information:**

- University-sanctioned typefaces are Proxima Nova (sans serif) and Mercury (serif). These are designed to offer flexibility while ensuring consistency across communication materials. Alternative fonts for non-designed communications include Arial and Times New Roman.
- Digital communications may use Proxima Nova, Open Sans, or Arial for cases where preset style licenses are unavailable. Use Calibri or Arial for general correspondence.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should use real CSU Dominguez Hills community members, representing the diversity of the campus. Images should feature individuals actively engaged in settings emphasizing quality academics and a vibrant campus life.
- University communications should maintain a consistent photography style reflecting a fresh, contemporary look, avoiding dark or cluttered designs.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Stationery and business cards maintain Proxima Nova typography with specified font sizes and alignments (e.g., 10.5pt for names, 6pt for credentials). Layouts include alignment to text boxes and consistent use of margins.

**Brand Voice:**

- Messaging must ensure clarity, professionalism, and positivity, focusing on promoting academic excellence and cultural significance.

**Notes:**

- The guidelines encourage light, clean, and contemporary designs with lots of white space. Color palettes and fonts are standardized to create unified branding.

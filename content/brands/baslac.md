- The baslac® logo is the key element of baslac® brand communication. It is used on all baslac® communications. 
The logo does not need to be copied individually. It is available to you as vector and pixel graphics in 
various file formats and color modes. The graphic structure of the logo must not be modified. Color modifications, distortions and graphic effects such as shadows, curves, etc. are not permitted. The baslac® logo is always used horizontally. The ratio of the logo's width to its height is 3:2.
- Color logo
For the color logo, the typeface and frame are black and the half circles are presented in the light blue corporate color. This version of the logo is available as a vector EPS file in the CMYK color mode and as a two-color version with black and the special color Pantone PT 2925 C. It is also available as a pixel graphic in the color modes CMYK and RGB and as JPG and TIF files.

**Color Information:**

- The colors black and light blue from BASF's six corporate colors are used. The endorsement and page headers color area correspond to BASF light blue with 50% transparency.

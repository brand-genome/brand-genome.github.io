### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- HP branded assets use a white background with primary color as HP Blue, and specific spacing is maintained between the HP logo and partner's logo. Clear space and minimum size requirements are described to ensure appropriate relationships between HP and partner logos.
- Logo arrangement guidelines specify that logos should not be stacked, placed too close to each other, overlapped, or placed in diagonal positions on corners.
- Proportion and arrangement guidelines: The HP logo remains visually larger in HP-led initiatives, while in partner-led initiatives, the HP logo plays a supporting role. Divider line specifications include black color and 1pt stroke size.
- For multiple logos, the HP logo typically leads, appearing in a primary position, and must be visually larger if HP leads the co-branding initiative. Spatial arrangement guidelines prohibit logos from being scattered across an asset.
- If an event requires separating HP's logo using a line, approval from the respective brand representative is mandatory.

**Color Information:**

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Minimum size: The minimum size for digital use of the HP logo is 20x20 pixels at 72ppi. The minimum size for print use of the HP logo is 0.25" square (6.35mm square).
- The HP logo is a blue circle with transparent HP letters. Use the inverse (white) logo on color or dark backgrounds or premium product assets. Use the black logo on one-color print or premium product assets.
- Do not place the HP logo on complicated or patterned backgrounds. Avoid altering the HP logo in any circumstances, including use of an outline version, adding effects (e.g., drop shadows), rotating, or using unapproved colors.
- Lock-up configurations: A logo lock-up is reserved for unique scenarios where both partners are truly contributing equally. Measurements for the lock-up use clear space of 0.5x and minimum size of 0.25" or 20px.
- Clear space: Maintain clear space equivalent to 0.5x around each lock-up configuration. Partner logos must align proportionally based on clear space guidelines.
- Multiple logos: HP should lead and be visibly larger within multiple-logo designs. Avoid scattering logos throughout the asset.
- What not to do: Do not modify HP partner insignias in any way, including color or proportion. Avoid displaying multiple insignias in the same content.

**Color Information:**

- Color specifications for HP logo: Coated - PMS 2925C, CMYK (C:79, M:18, Y:0, K:0), RGB (R:0, G:150, B:214), HEX #0096D6; Uncoated - PMS 2955U, CMYK (C:79, M:18, Y:0, K:0).
- Color rules for HP logos: Use the appropriate color versions: Full color, inverse (white), black depending on background and asset type.
- Partner insignia usage: Insignias should be displayed in color unless manufacturing limitations exist, in which case black-and-white is allowed; grayscale is prohibited.

**Imagery Photography:**

- HP maintains an extensive image library within the HP Asset Hub, accessible after specific enrollment. Lifestyle photography and product usage photography must align with HP brand context.

## Brand Guidelines

### 1999

**Logo Information:**

- The Hitachi Wordmark is the sole visual identifier for Hitachi Group Companies. It cannot be reproduced using typesetting and must use authorized camera-ready or electronic artwork.
- Clear area around the Hitachi Wordmark: preferred clear area is 1/2 height of 'H', minimum allowable clear area is 1/3 height of 'H'. Frames or boxes should ideally not be used and must remain outside the minimum allowable clear area.
- Logo minimum colors: Hitachi Red (PMS 185c), with alternatives in Hitachi Silver (PMS 8180), black, or white. Avoid bright colors as backgrounds.
- Do's: Use Hitachi Wordmark in positive form (default Hitachi Red or alternatives). Do not use the positive version over illustrations/photos. Observe strict color background rules for the reverse version (white).
- Don'ts: Examples of incorrect usage include drop shadows, altering spacing, changing colors, patterns, outlines, distortion, and adding modifiers or graphic elements. Incorrect clear area violations highlighted.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: Hitachi Red (PMS 185c). Secondary colors: Hitachi Silver (PMS 8180), black, and white. Recommended complementary colors and non-recommended colors listed.

**Typography Information:**

- Approved fonts for corporate communications: Helvetica Family and Sabon Family. Helvetica is for technical information, captions, and headings; Sabon is for longer texts like reports. If Sabon is unavailable, New Times Roman can be substituted.
- Font weights/styles: Helvetica (Light, Light Oblique, Regular, Oblique, Bold, Bold Oblique, Black, Black Oblique) and Sabon (Regular, Italic, Bold, Bold Italic).

**Imagery Photography:**

- The reverse Hitachi Wordmark may appear on photographs or illustrations, provided contrast is sufficient for readability. Positive versions should generally be avoided on images.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear area specifications for the logo: 1/2 height of 'H' preferred, 1/3 height of 'H' minimum. Frames or boxes should be outside the minimum clear area.

**Brand Voice:**

- The Hitachi brand emphasizes clarity, boldness, reliability, and quality. Communications should maintain consistent representation without compromise.

**Notes:**

- Questions about logo usage and obtaining logo art can be directed to Hitachi America Corporate Communications.

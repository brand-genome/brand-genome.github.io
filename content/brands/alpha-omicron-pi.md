## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Primary logomark for Alpha Omicron Pi Fraternity includes bold sans-serif font with interchangeable taglines (Inspire Ambition or Fraternity script). Colors include Cardinal (PMS 200) and Pink (PMS 191C). Recommended for use wherever possible.
- Logomark can be displayed in alternative styles: One-Color versions (Cardinal PMS 200C, Pink PMS 191C), patterned with vector rose, and reversed for single-color use.
- Wordmark alternative of the primary logomark excludes taglines and can be used in the primary color palette (Cardinal PMS 200, Pink PMS 191C), or with a vector rose pattern or as a reversed logo.
- Incorrect usage guidelines for the primary logomark and wordmark: Do not change orientation, fonts, or color palette; do not tilt, distort, add transparencies, or alter elements.
- Fraternity tagline 'Inspire Ambition' exemplifies the mission of Alpha Omicron Pi and can be used with the primary logomark or independently following color palette guidelines.
- Infinity Rose logo integrates a thornless rose symbolizing the founders and values of the fraternity; usage should align with primary colors (Cardinal and Pink), vector patterns, or reversed designs.
- Greek Letter logos incorporate the Infinity Rose in place of the 'O' and are permissible in two-color, one-color, black, reversed, or vector rose styles. Exception for sports balls in special events.
- Traditional Greek letters (without Infinity Rose) can be used with taglines or script, following the primary color palette and layout guidelines. Adapted for aesthetic versatility.
- Clear space around the logos must equal the height of 'A' from 'Alpha' in the logomark.
- Minimum size for the primary logomark and wordmark: Printed width 1.5 inches or 108 pixels for digital use. Greek letters minimum size: 0.35 inches or 25 pixels.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Cardinal (PMS 200C: CMYK C17 M100 Y86 K6, RGB R194 G32 B50, HEX #C22032) and Pink (PMS 191C: CMYK C0 M88 Y39 K0, RGB R239 G67 B109, HEX #EF436D). Mandatory for Fraternity logos and Greek letters.
- Secondary colors include Coral (PMS 7416C: CMYK C3 M74 Y70 K0, RGB R235 G104 B81, HEX #EB6851), Dark Gray (PMS Cool Gray 11C: CMYK C65 M57 Y52 K29, RGB R85 G86 B90, HEX #55565A), and Light Gray (PMS Cool Gray 1C: CMYK C13 M11 Y12 K0, RGB R219 G217 B214, HEX #DBD9D6). For accents, not primary usage.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space around the logo must equal the height of 'A' in the primary logomark to avoid interference with text or graphics.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tagline 'Inspire Ambition' reflects the mission of Alpha Omicron Pi to foster courage and achievement. It communicates passion, purpose, and shared sisterhood values.

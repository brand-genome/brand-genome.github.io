## Brand Guidelines

### 2024

**Logo Information:**

- Standard horizontal logo is the primary visual element. Alternatives include vertical logos. Positive full color, monochrome blue, negative blue, and black color versions are available.
- Color versions include full color (CMYK and RGB), monochrome blue (Pantone Reflex Blue), negative blue (Pantone 298C), and black. Usage differs for print and digital.
- The logo has a protection area that must not overlap with other elements, calculated as half the height of the letter 'a' in the logo.
- Minimum size requirements for the horizontal logo are 2.6 cm x 1.05 cm, and for the vertical logo, 1.3 cm x 1.05 cm.
- Logo usage restrictions include no outlines, no altering proportions, no removing elements, no incorrect fonts or gradient effects, and no capitalization changes in the wordmark.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Colors: EU BLUE (Pantone Reflex Blue, RGB(3, 78, 162), HEX #034EA2, CMYK(100, 80, 10, 0)), YE TURQUOISE (Pantone 2915 C, RGB(53, 171, 226), HEX #35ABE2, CMYK(68, 16, 0, 0)), EU YELLOW (Pantone Yellow, RGB(255, 237, 0), HEX #FFED00, CMYK(0, 0, 100, 0)), BLACK (Pantone Black, RGB(0, 0, 0), HEX #000000, CMYK(69, 69, 69, 100)), and WHITE (RGB(255, 255, 255), HEX #FFFFFF, CMYK(0, 0, 0, 0)).
- Secondary Colors: YE BRICK (Pantone 7523 C, RGB(150, 80, 70), HEX #965046, CMYK(31, 73, 70, 21)), YE CORAL (Pantone 170 C, RGB(252, 153, 119), HEX #FC9978, CMYK(0, 49, 51, 0)), YE SALMON (Pantone 487 C, RGB(253, 209, 176), HEX #FDD1B0, CMYK(6, 72, 70, 0)), YE CREAM (Pantone 7506 C, RGB(200, 235, 250), HEX #C7EAFB, CMYK(0, 20, 30, 0)), YE SKY-BLUE (Pantone 290 C, RGB(230, 107, 84), HEX #E66B54, CMYK(45, 36, 35, 0)), and YE GREY (Pantone Cool Gray 1 C, RGB(150, 152, 154), HEX #929497, CMYK(20, 0, 0, 0)).
- Color rules prohibit gradients and emphasize the exclusion of green shades. Any exceptions require formal requests.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Rubik, available in Regular, Bold, Italic weights. Used for all communication with roles including body text (Regular and Italic) and headings (Bold).
- Typography design is light, airy, and left-aligned. All text uses sentence case.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery prioritizes diversity and inclusion, portraying people as central figures and emphasizing natural, dynamic, and optimistic depictions. Avoid staged or studio-like settings.
- Use authentic and realistic emotions; avoid posed or artificial expressions.
- Images must have full copyright clearance and be adaptable for social media use.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Mandatory layout elements include the YE logo, Rubik font, color palette, imagery or icons, and organic graphic shapes.
- Logo size is defined as 10% of the total height of the graphic. Margins are determined using the EU emblem height. Logos should be placed in one of the four graphic corners.
- Adhering to layout variations, full image graphics, illustrated landscapes, and illustrations with people are valid. Follow prescribed design rules.
- Do not combine imagery with vector people illustrations to prevent redundant depictions.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice is conversational, informal, jargon-free, and seeks to engage audiences with accessible and practical communication.
- Focus on informative content showcasing the impact of EU policies in the daily lives of citizens. Highlight critical facts and numbers clearly.

**Notes:**

- All sections in the guidelines emphasize consistency in visual identity for 'Your Europe', especially in logo placement, typography, color use, and layout elements.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Logo lockup types include One-Line, Two-Line, and Three-Line lockup systems for different entities within Havas, depending on name length and structure.
- Not recommended changes: Do not change highlighted section colors, use single colors, uppercase 'H,' stretch or scale disproportionately, or use other words outside lockup.
- Minimum size: The recommended lockup height is 0.5 inches (1.3cm or 48px). Anything smaller is not allowed.
- Clear space rules include using a square-based grid where the recommended clear space around the logo lockup is 2(x), defined as the distance of a single unit 'x' between logo and the company name.
- Logo color tinting rules specify using 100%, 60%, and 40% tints of the same base color on different sections of the logo (top/middle/bottom).
- Primary logo color options include several HEX codes: #77c043, #00aed0, #265ba8, #00aa93, #ec536b, #000000, #ca5ba2, #6858a5.
- Use white logos when working with colored backgrounds. Avoid color logos over color backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Primary logo color options with HEX and CMYK values provided: #77c043 (C:58 M:0 Y:100 K:0), #00aed0 (C:74 M:9 Y:13 K:0), #265ba8 (C:91 M:70 Y:1 K:0), #ec536b (C:1 M:83 Y:44 K:0), etc.
- White space usage recommendation: Maintain 60% dominant proportion of white space in all applications for visual cleanliness and hierarchy.
- Color pairings examples include Warm Red with Cool Magenta and Cool Green with Warm Green. Pairings should be intentional to enhance visuals.
- Not recommended: Avoid color pairings that are not mentioned in the guidelines.

**Typography Information:**

- Recommended typeface: Helvetica Neue LT Std Roman and Bold, chosen for clarity and neutrality.
- Typography pairing rules: Use bold for headlines of smaller sizes and Roman for body text or understated elegance.
- Typography weight: Use only recommended weights (Roman and Bold) for consistency throughout the system.
- Typography size recommendations: Use a maximum of three type sizes, such as x, 1/2x, and 1/4x. Sizes should relate proportionally.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Recommended photography principles: Focus on people, storytelling, and capturing real moments. Represent global diversity with humanized visuals that embrace local culture.
- Not recommended: Avoid black-and-white imagery, aerial photos from earlier brand systems, or staged stock photographs.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Maintain recommended white space proportions in designs (60% white space, 30% main colors, 10% other shades). This ensures balance and hierarchy.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice reflects clarity and unity, avoiding overstatement. It should project a unified vision, look, and feel.

**Notes:**

- Additional assets such as logos, color palettes, templates, and stationery are downloadable from the brand site.
- Contact for support: brandhelp@havas.com; brand portal: tobettertogether.com.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- A full-color vertical representation of the logo is designated as the primary logo for most communication situations.
- A full-color horizontal representation of the logo is designated for use in horizontal spaces such as email templates, headers, etc.
- A simplified horizontal logo version may only be used when space is extremely limited, such as on signage or pencils.
- The three-color spot logo may only be used when printing in spot colors, though CMYK is preferred.
- A white version of the logo is to be used when the background does not contrast well with the full-color logo.
- A one-color spot blue version is designated for use when printing in one spot color.
- The black logo version is to be used when color versions cannot be utilized.
- Incorrect usages include: rotating/tilting the logo, stretching the logo, using the tagline on its own, altering logo colors, using the logo without the bug element, placing the logo in a box, applying visual effects like glow behind the logo.
- Clear space around the logo must equal the height of the 'T' Sphere. Minimum width of the full-color logo must not be smaller than 0.75 inches wide.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes: Blue (PMS 2945, CMYK 100/52/2/12, RGB 0/99/164, HEX #0063a4), Dark Blue (PMS 2965, CMYK 100/38/0/64, RGB 0/58/99, HEX #003a63), Green (PMS 376, CMYK 53/0/96/0, RGB 132/197/71, HEX #86c447), Orange (PMS 137, CMYK 0/35/90/0, RGB 251/176/52, HEX #fcb034).
- Secondary/expanded colors include Green (PMS 363, CMYK 72/0/100/27, RGB 51/143/56, HEX #2f8f38), Cyan (PMS 638, CMYK 89/0/7/0, RGB 0/179/226, HEX #00b3e3), Gray (PMS 433, CMYK 33/3/0/95, RGB 21/33/40, HEX #2e404c), and Cream (PMS 461 20%, CMYK 1/1/6, RGB 253/251/239, HEX #fdfbef).
- Accent colors are designated for specialties: Corporate Travel (PMS 2945 PC), Loyalty (PMS 376 PC), Technology (PMS 137 PC), Vacations (PMS 638 PC), Events (PMS 363 PC).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface is Myriad Pro, in multiple weights: Light, Light SemiCondensed, Regular, Black, Condensed, and Bold Condensed.
- Grandesign Neue Serif is used as an accent font for emphasis, such as headlines and pull quotes.
- Allura is an accent font sparingly used by the marketing department.
- Email body copy should be in Calibri or Georgia fonts.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should reflect Travel and Transport's role in helping customers experience the world personally and professionally, evoking emotion and resonating with audiences. Photos should never be sourced from internet sites like Google Images or Bing.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Business cards must adhere to standardized layout rules regarding placement of names, titles, addresses, and logos.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand voice emphasizes one company, one brand, one voice, avoiding division-specific terminologies. Travel and Transport should never be abbreviated as T&T or TandT in communications, except as TAT for file-saving purposes.

**Notes:**

- Promotional sub-brand marks include eTTek, Points2Points, Travel and Transport Affiliate, and Travel and Transport Travel Academy.
- Contact marketing for access to corporate artwork files: mkbranch@travelandtransport.com.

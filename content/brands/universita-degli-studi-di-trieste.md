## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Logo construction grid with measurements including 1 unit, 3 units, 8.8 units, 12 units, and 21.5 units for precise alignment.
- Logo safe zone specifies a clear space around the logo equivalent to the height of the letter 'E' from the logo design.
- Minimum usage size for logo: 35mm for 3-line version, 50mm for 2-line version, and 21mm for vertical version.
- Logo color specifications: Pantone 534C, CMYK C100 M82 Y35 K27, RGB R41 G56 B99, HEX #29385c, RAL 5017.
- Improper logo usage examples include: oblique positioning, distortion, outline versions, altered relationships between elements, and removal of the seal.

**Color Information:**

- Primary logo color: Pantone 534C, utilizes a blue shade. CMYK: C100 M82 Y35 K27, RGB: R41 G56 B99, HEX: #29385c.
- Guidelines for using logo colors on different backgrounds: Positive logo for light backgrounds, negative version for darker backgrounds. Intensity thresholds detailed for Pantone 534C from 10% to 100%.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Brandon Grotesque (used in the logo design). Available in weights Light, Medium, Regular, Bold, and Black.
- Secondary communication typeface: Avenir LT for publications and general communication. Available in Light (LT 35), Medium (LT 65), Roman (LT 55), Heavy (LT 85), and Black (LT 95).
- Replacement typeface: Arial (Regular, Bold, Black) suggested for office applications and email communications when Avenir is unavailable.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo construction grid includes unit measurements for spacing: 1 unit, 3 units, 8.8 units, 12 units, and 21.5 units.

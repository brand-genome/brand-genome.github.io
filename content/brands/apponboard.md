## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- AppOnboard logo should only be used in full color or white. It should be used either with the icon + type or just the icon. No altering of the logo's shape, style, proportions, or colors.
- AppOnboard Studio logo should only be used in full color or white. It must always include the icon + type.
- AppOnboard Academy logo allows both full logo and text-only versions. Must be used in full color or white and not altered in any way.
- When using AppOnboard logo alongside partner logos, a dividing line with a stroke weight of 0.5 pt should be used. Ocean Blue color is preferred, and the height/width of the line should match the partner logo's dimensions.
- Clear space rule: The logo should always be surrounded by clear space equivalent to 'x' (logo height) and 1/4 'x' on all sides.
- Application on background: Full-color logo used on white backgrounds; white logo on colored backgrounds.
- Incorrect logo usage includes: altering colors, stretching, outlining, rearranging elements, changing size proportions, using the type logo without the icon.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Ocean Blue - Pantone 3035C (CMYK: 97 68 47 37, RGB: 1 62 83, HEX: #013E53) and Sky Blue - Pantone P 116-7 U (CMYK: 82 33 17 0, RGB: 12 139 180, HEX: #0C8BB4). White is also in the primary palette.
- Secondary palette includes Wilshire Grey - Pantone 427 CP (CMYK: 14 10 11 0, RGB: 217 217 217, HEX: #d9d9d9) and Seafoam Green - Pantone 3245 U (CMYK: 56 0 36 0, RGB: 80 227 194, HEX: #50E3C2).
- Tertiary colors, such as Lava Pink (Pantone 807C, HEX: not visible), Sunset Purple (Pantone 2368C, HEX: #4828fb), Pacific Blue (Pantone 915 U, HEX not shown), among others, are limited-use accents.

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate font family is Source Sans Pro, with weights Bold, Semibold, Regular, Light, and ExtraLight.
- Examples of type pairings: Use larger/heavier weights for headings such as Source Sans Pro Bold and smaller/lighter weights like Light for body text. Specific sizes include: Header - 34pt/Bold or Semibold, Subheader - 30pt, Body - 11pt or smaller.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Phone renderings should use angled mockups whenever possible. Ensure screenshots match relevant device type (e.g., iOS on iPhones).
- AppOnboard Studio rendering guidelines suggest angled laptops or 'exploded' views for mockups.
- Photography should feature bright, clean backgrounds and natural/candid rather than staged shots. Lighting is key.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Ensure at least 50% white space when using the Lava Graphic Device. Logos placed atop lava must use solid backgrounds for visibility.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand tone emphasizes community, passion, innovation, gratitude, professionalism, and respect. Communication avoids bragging or criticizing competitors, and focuses on optimism and support for both clients and collaborators.

**Notes:**

- Taglines approved for use: 'Your App Starts Here' and 'From Art to App'. Old taglines like 'Demo Before Download' should not be used. Approved hashtags include #YourAppStartsHere and #NoCode.
- High-resolution Lava Graphic Devices, .ase color swatches, font families, and templates for decks and sheets can be downloaded through respective links in the document.

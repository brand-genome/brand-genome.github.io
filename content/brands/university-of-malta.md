## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The new brand includes the official University of Malta ceremonial crest, a simplified UM crest (one color), a stacked wordmark in Cera PRO © Medium, and a combined crest and stacked wordmark logo. The logo must not be modified except for proportionate resizing to minimum sizes for print and digital applications.
- Logo minimum size: For print, the crest height must be at least 15mm. For digital, at least 10mm. Minimum clear space for logo use is 5mm and increases proportionally with size. A generic clear space rule of 10mm applies for most applications.
- The UM logo must retain its elements (crest and stacked wordmark) and only the official versions are allowed for use. The red logo is the default choice, with black and white versions allowed under legibility or design constraints.
- Incorrect logo types: Any logo versions in varying color schemes other than those provided (red, black, or white) must not be used.

**Color Information:**

- Red logo color specifications: Pantone 200C, CMYK: 3 100 70 12, RGB: 186 12 47, HEX: #BA0C2F.
- Black logo color specifications: Process Black, CMYK: 0 0 0 100, RGB: 39 37 31, HEX: #27251F.
- White logo (reversed-out): Transparent sections appear in white, and reverse sections transparent. For use under legibility or design constraints.
- The red logo is the default and must be prioritized over black and white versions. Other variations or color customization are prohibited.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo sizes: Minimum crest height is 15mm for print, 10mm for digital. Clear space: Mandatory 5mm minimum, increasing proportionally with size. Generic clear space is 10mm for normal applications.

**Notes:**

- The official logos, crest, and wordmarks are rooted in historical heritage and evolved through stakeholder consultation to represent a modern, competitive identity.

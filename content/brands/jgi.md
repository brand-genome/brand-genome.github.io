## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- The JGI Logo consists of four elements: initialism, icon (part of the indicia), logotype, and DOE tag. The primary logo is full color and is the preferred visual identification.
- Horizontal variation is the preferred logo layout. Variants include CMYK (preferred), grayscale, 1-color black, and 1-color white (for dark backgrounds).
- Stacked variation includes CMYK with and without DOE tag, grayscale, 1-color black, and 1-color white (for dark backgrounds).
- Indicia variation is available in 1-color black and 1-color white (for dark backgrounds).
- Icon variation is available in CMYK, 1-color black, grayscale, and 1-color white (for dark backgrounds).
- Unacceptable logo usage includes: using on backgrounds with insufficient contrast or conflicting colors, resizing the icon, repositioning the icon, or replacing colors.
- Clear space around the JGI logo requires one full-letter height on all sides, protecting the logo from clutter and brand confusion.
- When the JGI Logo and Berkeley Lab Logo are used together, the Berkeley Lab logo comes first. Height of the lab logo equals the height of the JGI icon, and spacing aligns to the width of the italic letter 'I.'

**Color Information:**

- Official color palette includes: PMS Black (CMYK: 70/63/62/58, RGB: 52/52/52), PMS Cool Gray 11C (CMYK: 62/55/54/28, RGB: 90/90/90), PMS Cool Gray 8C (CMYK: 46/38/38/2, RGB: 145/144/144), PMS 173C (CMYK: 0/65/100/15, RGB: 211/105/27), PMS 652C (CMYK: 50/20/5/25, RGB: 100/140/170), PMS 5405C (CMYK: 58/17/0/46, RGB: 59/110/143), PMS 397C (CMYK: 46/8/100/0, RGB: 153/189/60), PMS 7490C (CMYK: 45/0/80/35, RGB: 96/154/62), and PMS 143C (CMYK: 0/30/80/0, RGB: 253/186/77).
- For PowerPoint, darker blue and green shades should be used.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary fonts: Myriad Pro (weights: light, light italic, regular, italic, semibold, semibold italic, bold, bold italic, black, black italic) and Trade Gothic Condensed (weights: regular, italic, bold No.18, bold No.18 oblique, bold No.20, bold No.20 oblique). For Microsoft PowerPoint and Word, Arial is used.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Poster layouts have a 1-inch border clearance for framing. Horizontal posters: sizes 42"x36" and 48"x36"; Vertical posters: sizes 36"x42" and 36"x48."
- White space is important to focus the viewer on content and prevent clutter caused by effects such as gradients, unnecessary shadows, and 3D effects.

**Notes:**

- Contact for additional information: David Gilbert, degilbert@lbl.gov.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Logo is available in three color versions: full-color (preferred for most use), black (used in absence of color), and white/diapositive (used on colored background with sufficient contrast). Full color for print is defined with CMYK (C:95 M:5 Y:50 K:0) and Pantone 3272 U. RGB values for screen are R:59, G:158, B:152.
- Minimum width of complete logo with light bulb and SmartAgriHubs text is 30 mm for print and digital; for icon-only light bulb use, the minimum width is 8 mm.
- Whitespace around the logo must be at least 1x "T" of the SmartAgriHubs logo, ensuring proportional spacing.
- Dos and Don’ts for logo usage: Avoid resizing, rotating, changing proportions or adding shadows. Always use horizontally, with the same color scheme (three predefined colors).

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors with codes: Dark Green (Pantone 3272 U, Hex #4dcc98, RGB R:59, G:158, B:152), Light Green (Pantone 375 U, Hex #abce59, RGB R:171, G:206, B:89), and Yellow (Pantone 136 U, Hex #ffcc33, RGB R:255, G:204, B:51).
- Secondary colors include Orange (#ff7300, R:255, G:115, B:0), Pink (#f8759d, R:248, G:117, B:157), and Red (#fb1b2a, R:251, G:27, B:42).

**Typography Information:**

- Meta Pro is the primary font for printed materials, used for headings, body text, and streamers.
- Verdana Regular is used for body text on digital screens, while Verdana Bold is used for headers in PowerPoint presentations, newsletters, and other digital applications.

**Imagery Photography:**

- There is a division between 'real images' (from stakeholders) and 'standard images' (stock photos for corporate branding). Real images are used to strengthen SmartAgriHubs identity.
- Maps of Europe are used with connections represented as lines and dots, focusing on regional clusters for visual representation.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo placement preference is at the top center of pages.
- Logo and partner logos should maintain the same height and a minimum spacing of 1x "T" around the logo.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice should be professional and personal, using first-person language ('we') over third person. Active voice is encouraged to show energy, and full terms should be written before abbreviations.
- Short sentences with minimal commas are preferred to enhance reader engagement.

**Notes:**

- PowerPoint and Word templates are made available for consistency in materials, covering agendas, minutes, memos, and reports.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The logo consists of the font and a jumping puma design. Font is Arial Black, bold, sliced in half for edge, symbolizing sports and fashion. The puma is kept simplistic to match the minimalistic feel of the logo.
- Logo variations include a black background with a white puma and font, inverted colors, or black puma and font on a beige background.
- The PUMA logo can be used standalone, especially for promotional and product placement purposes. Special emphasis is needed on placement and usage.
- The background and color of the font and jumping puma cannot be changed. The simple puma cannot have a background or color alteration.
- Logo size minimum is 3 cm, while the maximum size depends on placement and usage. Scaling must be proportional and consistent; objects cannot be scaled independently.
- Logo dimensions are fixed and cannot be altered as this confuses consumers and weakens the brand identity.
- Logo placement must appear in a top or bottom corner based on the photo it is placed onto. Variations in logo should match photo colors.

**Color Information:**

- Main colors include black, white, and red. Black variations: Web RGB (0,0,0), Print CMYK (100,100,100,100). Red variant changes to subtle, RGB (186,32,38), CMYK (19,99,99,9). Beige added for collaborations.
- Facelift adjusted tones: White RGB (255,255,255), CMYK (0,0,0,0). Beige collaboration RGB (174,148,109), CMYK (32,38,62,4).

**Typography Information:**

- Typography uses Avenir Black for titles (uppercase), Avenir Black for subtitles, Avenir Book for body text. Clean typeface complementing detailed logo typeface. Can be used in black or red.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grid alignments require corner placement matching image designs. Colors of logos must suit placement context (contrast rules enforced).

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone emphasizes speed (FAST), bravery, determination, joy, game-changing attitude targeting youthful athletes. Mission: To be fastest sports brand.
- Tagline: YOUR LIFE, YOUR STYLE, YOUR SPORT; engages market sentiment emphasizing personal and lifestyle sports alignment.
- Slogan for 'Forever Faster' campaign uses Arial Black font paired with jumping PUMA design, emphasizing speed and collaboration with Rihanna & Usain Bolt.

**Notes:**

- Brand integration mandates coherent logo on all packaging, promotional placements. External branding extends coherence into newsletters, billboards.
- PUMA collaborates with high-end fashion brands, merging lifestyle with core sports identity. Packaging reflects branding cohesiveness.

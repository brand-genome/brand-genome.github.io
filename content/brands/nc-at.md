## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The university logos include primary, secondary, and icon versions. These are trademarks of North Carolina Agricultural and Technical State University to establish a uniform visual identity. Usage of these logos is restricted to recognized university entities; personal use of the logos is prohibited.
- College-level logos have three levels of usage: (1) Full college name for formal applications like business cards or letterheads, (2) Contextual college logos for viewbooks or external communication, (3) Simplified logos for applications already within the university context, such as signage.
- Clear space must be maintained around all logos to distinguish them from adjacent text or images. Details for measurements are not specified.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors are 'Aggie Blue' (PMS 288, CMYK: 100, 65, 0, 31, RGB: 0, 70, 132, HEX: #004684) and 'Aggie Gold' (PMS 123, CMYK: 0, 30, 94, 0, RGB: 253, 185, 39, HEX: #FDB927).
- Accent color is Gray (PMS 7536, CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 30, RGB: 194, 194, 194, HEX: #C2C2C2). It is intended for supporting primary colors and adding hierarchy but must not dominate the design.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: Montserrat. Montserrat Black is for headlines or emphasized titles (uppercase only), and Montserrat Regular is for body copy.
- Secondary font: Alfon, used for secondary headlines in college-level applications and accent purposes such as quotes.

**Spacing Layout:**

**Notes:**

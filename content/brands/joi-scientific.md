**Typography Information:**

- The corporate logo uses a bespoke typeface unique to Joi Scientific and cannot be used adaptively. The Gotham Rounded typeface is employed for all other branding communications and conveys simplicity.
- Gotham Rounded is utilized in Light, Medium, Book, and Italic weights to create a modern yet accessible aesthetic.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography philosophy highlights natural, clean imagery focused on sustainability and global empowerment, incorporating core brand tones of black, white, and blue.
- Photography elements include dynamic energy and movement, further symbolized by the visual motif of bubbles in water.

**Brand Voice:**

- The Joi Scientific brand voice is defined by its focus on social responsibility, innovation, simplicity, friendliness, and uniqueness, presenting itself as a global problem-solving company.

**Notes:**

- Trademark filings include 'Hydrogen 2.0', 'H2.0', and 'Joi Scientific'. These enhance brand recognition and reinforce proprietary identity.
- The guide serves as a foundational document for all branding, representing Joi Scientific's identity as an ethical and sustainable innovator.

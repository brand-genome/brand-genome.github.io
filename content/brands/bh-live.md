## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- BH Live logo versions include white-out logos, corporate blue logos, and reversed logos visible on background images or color.
- Logo clear space is defined by the 'b' symbol. No other graphic elements or headlines are allowed within this area.
- Do's include using correct Pantones or CMYK/RGB swatches, and original image files of the logo. Don'ts include adding shadows, effects, outlines, textures, patterns, poor contrast usage, or modifying the signature.
- Examples of incorrect logo usage include stretching, squeezing, color splitting, and scaling. Logos must not be used on busy photo backgrounds with drop shadows or without adjustments to contrast.
- Minimum size and file formats related information was not visible in provided pages.

**Color Information:**

- Primary corporate color is Pantone 300 C (CMYK: C86 M59 Y0 K0, RGB: R41 G106 B179). Primary palette also includes tints: 20%, 40% and 70%.
- Additional secondary colors include CMYK/RGB codes such as 100% Cyan and variations of Magenta (e.g., C50-M100-Y3-K0 or R146-G38-B139).
- Usage guidelines encourage exclusive application of primary tints for branding assets, avoiding alternative colors.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family is Gotham, which includes weights such as Extra Light, Light, Book, Bold, and Black.
- Secondary font family includes Arial, Regular weight for body text, bold for headers usage optimization.
- Typography recommendation: Gotham typeface family is suitable for both smaller scales and digital assets without visual degradation due to its readable counters.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Dynamic and engaging imagery is emphasized. High-resolution event shots are utilized to showcase experiences. Examples include active environments and cultural events.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear spacing defined using widths and allocated boundaries for captions and graphical assets. Space guidelines are explicitly noted around logos.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand voice emphasizes trust, community-driven purpose, and inspiration. It aims to promote active lifestyles, cultural events, and knowledge sharing to enrich lives. It aligns with the principles of social enterprise and engagement.

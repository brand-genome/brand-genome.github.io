### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Two versions of the Vidyo Corporate Signature are available: Vertical and Horizontal. Minimum sizes are 0.5 inches for the vertical logo and 0.25 inches for the horizontal logo.
- Clear space around all Vidyo logos must equal the x-height of 'Vidyo' in the logotype. This ensures optimal visual impact and trademark protection.
- Approved Vidyo logo versions: Full-color (preferred for white backgrounds), color reverse signature (infrequent, for black backgrounds), solid black (for extreme reproduction limitations), and solid white reverse signature (for medium-to-dark backgrounds).
- Incorrect usage examples for Vidyo logos: altering any part of the logo, using non-approved colors/typefaces, distorting/resizing disproportionately, placing it on patterns, gradients, or photos, and combining logos with elements besides clear space and approved tagline.
- File usage formats for Vidyo logos: EPS (preferred), GIF (for web only), RGB, Spot Color (Pantone), CMYK (print only). No modifications are allowed—the logo in provided formats must be used.

**Color Information:**

- Vidyo corporate colors: Pantone (123C, 368C, Blue Hex Cyan), RGB (#fbc21e, #8bc334, #008dd0), and CMYK specifications are provided. Consistent use of these colors is mandatory across all print and digital materials.

**Typography Information:**

- Lato Typeface: Variants include Light, Regular, Bold, and Italic. Use Lato Regular for body, Lato Light for all-caps headlines, and avoid italicizing headlines. Typeface reflects Vidyo branding's dynamic yet approachable personality.
- Swiss 721 Typeface: Previously used for Vidyo branding. Weights include Thin, Thin Italic, Light, Light Italic, and Bold. As this typeface is deprecated, use of Lato Font is replacing it.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Vidyo branding materials using images of Vidyo's products or services must remain unaltered and attributed with titles or captions using Vidyo's approved branding. Titles/captions must appear above or below the image, not overlapping.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space requirements for Vidyo logos are based on the x-height of 'Vidyo' in the logotype. No elements can encroach on this area, aside from pre-approved watermarks in marketing materials.

**Brand Voice:**

- Vidyo brand elements (logos, colors, trademarks) must represent the company as dynamic yet approachable. All communications should adhere to these guidelines to reflect Vidyo's personality.

**Notes:**

- Vidyo logos are legally protected corporate assets. Consistent use of approved versions supports brand trust and ensures compliance with intellectual property law.
- Swiss 721 font, once part of Vidyo's typography, should no longer be used in new materials. Replace all usage with the Lato typeface.

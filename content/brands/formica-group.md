## Brand Guidelines

### 2010

**Logo Information:**

- The Formica® logo includes a two-color anvil logo with specifications for breathing room.
- Clear spacing for the anvil logo requires at least 2X the height of the 'X measurement' around it.
- The Formica® red box logo includes variations such as white anvil logos and different positioning (top-anchored, bottom-anchored, etc.).

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors defined as Formica® red, black, and white. Examples include PMS 1655 Orange and black tones (70%, 90%, 30%, etc.).
- Color combinations include PMS 1655 Orange against PMS 485 Red, and black variations for sufficient contrast.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography includes two font families: Bauer Bodoni Roman and Helvetica Neue.
- Examples of character sets (uppercase, lowercase, numbers, special characters) for Helvetica Neue provided for consistency.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should evoke modernity and focus on minimalism, adhering to the 'less is more' philosophy.
- Avoid stock imagery and maintain relevance by selecting current and contextual images.
- Guidelines include model usage and clear compositions reflecting Formica's tone.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grid and layout rules include clear margins for logos and elements, ensuring balance and impact.

**Brand Voice:**

- The Formica brand focuses on clarity, confidence, and professionalism.

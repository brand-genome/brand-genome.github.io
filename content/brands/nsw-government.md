## Brand Guidelines

### 2022

**Logo Information:**

- Primary logo consists of the Waratah icon and full NSW Government wordmark to be used in most cases where size permits.
- Two small-use logos exist for ensuring legibility at all sizes: 'NSW Only' and restricted-use 'Waratah Only'.
- Clear space rule: 100% of the width of the 'N' across all applications except app icons and social media profiles.
- Logo dos: Ensure sufficient contrast, legibility, clear space, and appropriate logo size. Logo should not be altered, stretched, separated, rotated, or placed on busy backgrounds.
- Co-brand logo positioning: NSW Government logo must appear first, above or to the left of co-brand logo.
- Decoupled placement: Logos anchored to separate corners of a page/panel. Coupled placement allowed in specific cases (e.g., website headers, email signatures).

**Color Information:**

- Full color palette breakdown including RGB, HEX, CMYK, and Pantone specifications for colors like White, Grey (01-04), Green (01-04), Teal (01-04), Blue (01-04), Purple (01-04), Fuchsia (01-04), Red (01-04), Orange (01-04), Yellow (01-04), and Brown (01-04).
- Core brand colors listed: Blue 01 (#002664) and Red 02 (#d7153a).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Public Sans family available in 8 weights with roman and italic styles. Alternative typeface: Arial for cases where Public Sans is unavailable or inappropriate. Specific guidance for language translations provided.
- Public Sans weights listed: Extra Light, Light, Regular, Medium, Semibold, Bold, Extra Bold, Black.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Illustration principles described as vibrant, human, diverse, clear, meaningful, and uniquely NSW. Various styles provided including keyline, flat color, realistic, hand-drawn, geometric, modular, isometric, and 3D.
- Photography approach emphasizes true-to-place landscapes and diversity in visual tone. Editorial-style photographs capture meaningful experiences.
- Photography don’ts: Avoid unnatural, staged, overly stock imagery, non-NSW imagery, and over-treated images with unnatural filters.

**Spacing Layout:**

- 12x12 flexible grid system described with column arrangements (12, 6, 4, 3, 2 columns). Guidance includes margins, gutters, and grid flexibility advice.
- Line system described with hairlines and bands aiding in content organization. Combination patterns demonstrated.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand emphasizes a visual identity of vibrant, human, meaningful, and uniquely NSW ideas.

**Notes:**

- Document subject to regular updates. Branding assets and queries should be directed to NSW Government branding team (email: nswgovbranding@customerservice.nsw.gov.au).

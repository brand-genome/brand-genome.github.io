## Brand Guidelines

### 2010

**Logo Information:**

- The Dayton Area Chamber of Commerce signature must be used only in Black or White. The primary blue can be used exclusively in DACC Marketing Department produced or approved materials.
- Clear space for the logo should equal the height of the 'Dayton Area Chamber of Commerce' text at the bottom of the logo to maximize visibility and prevent overlap with other elements.
- Do's for logo usage include always using the provided electronic artwork, ensuring proper clear space, and preventing distortion when resizing the logo by holding the Shift key.
- Don'ts for logo usage: Do not alter or modify colors, resize without maintaining proportions, add animation, or introduce other graphic elements. Avoid removing logo elements or using them separately, rotating, or editing beyond approved DACC permissions.
- Examples of incorrect logo use are provided, demonstrating improper modifications such as distortion, rotation, or unauthorized stylistic changes.
- Logos suitable for member and sponsor use are available for download on the official Dayton Area Chamber of Commerce website. Consistent usage ensures brand integrity.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: PMS 7462 (CMYK: C=100 M=45 Y=6 K=27; RGB: R=0 G=90 B=139; HEX: #005a8b). This color emphasizes the primary color for the brand.
- Strategic Priorities accent colors: 'Workforce' - PMS 140 (CMYK: C=13 M=42 Y=100 K=56; RGB: R=117 G=84 B=24; HEX: #755418), 'Transportation' - PMS 1235 (CMYK: C=0 M=30 Y=95 K=0; RGB: R=255 G=182 B=18; HEX: #ffb612), 'Business support' - PMS 7495 (CMYK: C=30 M=4 Y=85 K=30; RGB: R=135 G=150 B=55; HEX: #879637), 'Retention & Expansion' - PMS 179 (CMYK: C=0 M=80 Y=94 K=10; RGB: R=219 G=82 B=38; HEX: #d2492a), 'Public policy' - PMS 542 (CMYK: C=64 M=19 Y=1 K=4; RGB: R=100 G=160 B=200; HEX: #4DA2D2).
- Colors are used strategically: the primary blue color is emphasized, and thematic colors for strategic priorities act as accents or patterns.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary fonts used are 'Bell MT' and 'Univers Condensed' families for all print and digital materials. Proper use includes headers, subheaders, and body text roles.
- Bell MT fonts include Bold, Roman, and Italic weights.
- Univers fonts include Ultra Condensed, Thin Ultra Condensed, Light Ultra Condensed, Light Condensed, and Condensed.
- Typography usage examples are provided: 'Headline' uses Bell MT Bold, 'Subhead' uses Univers Light Ultra Condensed, and 'Body Copy' uses Univers Light Condensed.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space guidelines: Surround logos with clear space equal to the height of the 'Dayton Area Chamber of Commerce' text.

**Brand Voice:**

- E-mail signatures must be unified and consistent. Graphical logos, icons, colors, scripted fonts, animations, backgrounds, or phrases are not permitted, maintaining professionalism.

**Notes:**

- The Dayton Area Chamber of Commerce brand illustrates a connection between historical achievements and future technological aspirations, focusing on strength, perseverance, and optimism.
- The US Chamber of Commerce accreditation signature must remain unaltered and unmodified, and it must include an active link to the US Chamber's homepage when used online.

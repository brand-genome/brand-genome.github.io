## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- Logotype with two versions: quadrichromie (full-color) and noir et blanc (black & white). The zone of protection is defined as a square containing the circle of the logotype. Minimum size: 16x18 mm. The text block 'COTE D'EMERAUDE' cannot be dissociated from the overall logo.
- Logotype color codes:
  - Quadrichromie: CMYK codes as C100 M00 Y30 K06, C00 M00 Y00 K100, C100 M60 Y00 K00.
  - Additional accents: CMYK codes C00 M100 Y100 K00 and C00 M05 Y100 K00.

**Color Information:**

- Primary and accent colors for the logotype specified in CMYK:
  - C100 M00 Y30 K06, suitable for mains.
  - Black: C00 M00 Y00 K100.
  - Highlight color: C100 M60 Y00 K00.
  - Accent colors: C00 M100 Y100 K00 and C00 M05 Y100 K00.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography for administrative documents follows DIN typeface in different weights:
1. DIN Light for standard content.
2. DIN Regular for standard text.
3. DIN Medium for headings.
Examples:
  - Uppercase: ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXY.
  - Lowercase: abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz.
  - Numbers: 0123456789.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Zone of protection for the logo: A square around the central circle of the logo. Minimum size for the logo is defined as 16x18 mm.

**Notes:**

- The document outlines administrative and editorial norms tied to typography, color, and signature placement. It prescribes consistency in the logo's visual usage and prohibits the logo's text from being dissociated from the design.

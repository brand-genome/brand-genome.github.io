## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The SMC Corporate Mark must always appear at least in one of the two interior pages. Respect the given templates for the position of SMC Corporate Mark & Claim within the blue/white box on covers.
- SMC Corporate Mark placements do not allow unconventional changes like added colors, mix of fonts, text overlays, or deviation from the prescribed gradients and dimensions of the blue box.
- Back covers should contain the SMC Corporate Mark & Claim and blue line. Back covers must retain the white overall design without additional information or added colors.

**Color Information:**

- Avoid using too many colors in promotional materials as it risks de-emphasizing the SMC Blue and undermining the white overall image aesthetic.
- For catalogues, all text should be 80% grey, not black, to maintain uniformity across designs.

**Typography Information:**

- Maintain a size hierarchy and do not mix different font colors unnecessarily in designs. Typography rules apply uniformly, especially in catalogues.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Images used should have consistency in angle and shadow placement. Avoid overly white images as the final blue patina effect can make them appear too plain.
- Treated images should maintain accurate skin color for any persons depicted, with blue patina not overlapping skin.
- Images larger than 2 cm should include a shadow for consistency. Consistency must be maintained across all materials.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Margins must be consistently respected for all interior layouts.
- Content like big grey backgrounds should be avoided to retain the white, clean design theme across materials.

**Brand Voice:**

- SMC materials emphasize automation expertise, reliability, and clean design principles. Avoid visual clutter, ensure consistency across templates, and maintain the brand’s professional tone.

**Notes:**

- The PDF specifies extensive product cataloging and promotional strategies alongside design guidelines. Promotional restrictions include avoiding clutter and ensuring uniform branding consistency. Text grey scaling, SMC Blue usage, white overall focus, and general proportions play a vital role.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2022

**Logo Information:**

- Clear space for logo defined based on height and width of the letter 'u' in Scouts. Minimum size for linear logo is 20mm and for stacked logo is 25mm.
- Logotype placement guidelines specify the logo should only appear once on a page or cover, with preferred positions illustrated.

**Typography Information:**

- The primary font is Nunito Sans, a free Google font. It is described as friendly, approachable, and highly legible. Font weights include: Black for headlines/hashtags, Extra Bold for localization, Bold for highlights, Regular for body text, and Light for specific use cases.
- Headlines use Nunito Sans Black (24pt), body text uses Nunito Sans Regular or Bold (10pt), and call-to-action uses Nunito Sans Bold or Black (16pt). Italicized versions should only be used sparingly.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style captures diversity, fun, friendship, and adventure, emphasizing emotional connections, learning, sharing, and achievement over simply documenting activities.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grids are recommended for consistent organization of text, graphics, and photographs. A 2-4 column layout is advised for smaller prints, and a 5-10 column layout is recommended for larger formats.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand voice focuses on inclusivity and accessibility. Guidelines include using plain English, avoiding jargon, keeping text clear with appropriate spacing, and avoiding unnecessary capitalizations or italics.

**Notes:**

- The brand guidelines emphasize accessibility and inclusivity across all communications, with a focus on using clear, consistent design and language.

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Clear space for logo measured by the height and width of the letter 'u' in 'Scouts'. Minimum sizes are specified: Horizontal logo must be at least 20mm, while stack logo must be at least 25mm. Placement preferences exist to ensure consistency and visibility.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: Purple (Pantone Violet C, HEX #7413dc, CMYK c72 m80 y0 k0), Scouts Teal (Pantone 3275 C, HEX #00a794, CMYK c74 m0 y45 k0), Black (HEX #000000, CMYK c0 m0 y0 k100), White (HEX #ffffff, CMYK c0 m0 y0 k0).
- Additional colors and pairings: Scouts Navy (Pantone 294 C, HEX #003982, CMYK c100 m88 y12 k0), Scouts Pink (Pantone 183 C, HEX #ffb4e5, CMYK c2 m30 y0 k0), Scouts Yellow (Pantone 108 C, HEX #ffe627, CMYK c0 m6 y90 k0), Scouts Red (HEX #e22e12, CMYK c0 m90 y100 k0), Scouts Blue (Pantone 285 C, HEX #006ddf, CMYK c95 m35 y0 k0), Scouts Green (Pantone 347 C, HEX #23a950, CMYK c75 m0 y91 k0).
- Guidance advises colors to be used alone or in specified pairings, limiting colors at a time, and adhering to correct breakdowns (CMYK, Pantone for print, HEX for digital).

**Typography Information:**

- Brand font: Nunito Sans. Five font weights available: Light, Regular, Bold, Extra Bold, Black. Free for use from Google's font repository (fonts.google.com/specimen/Nunito+Sans).
- Italicized font allowed for distinguishing specific words, used sparingly (e.g., publication references). Suggested hierarchy includes headline sizes/differentiation and typography for body text with Light weight for white backgrounds and Black weight for solid color backgrounds.
- Grid usage guidelines include: small print communications use a 4-column grid, while larger banners may use up to 10 columns. Digital style guide link provided (scouts.org.uk/styleguide).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should emphasize fun, friendship, adventure, and belonging. Focus on connection, learning, sharing, achievement, and friendship moments, evoking emotional responses beyond showing activities.
- Authenticity emphasized over posed shots. Uniforms and scarves should appear naturally but need not be in every photo. Examples showcase Scouts engaging in real activities with consistent themes.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clean and modern design identity prioritizes simplicity, boldness, and branding effectiveness. Examples include banners and layout outlines with consistent spacing approaches.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice aligns with 'optimism with attitude': Active (empowering young people with skills for life), Challenging (advocating against intolerance), Inclusive (welcoming and supportive communication), Optimistic (inspiring strong communities and positive futures).
- Style guide link provided for digital and print applications of the tone at scouts.org.uk/styleguide.

**Notes:**

- Application examples showcase visual identity and branding materials, with shop resources available at scouts.org.uk/shop for more assets to maintain brand consistency.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- LOGO GUIDELINES Prepared by the Office for Corporate Communication Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations For Graphic Designers
- 2. Logo 2.1 Primary version The multi-coloured version of the logo is the primary logo. It should only be used on white or Pantone© Light Grey backgrounds, to guarantee maximum prominence and legibility.
- 2. Logo 2.2 Secondary version The Nutrition Decade logo in white is the secondary logo version. It is used when photography or set coloured backgrounds are required as well as mono-printed materials.
- All versions of the logo have exclusion zones around them to help them stand out. The minimum exclusion zone is equal to the width of two round elements.
- Minimum size requirements apply for clarity. Minimum logo size for Height is: 15mm or 43 pixels across languages.
- Guidelines for logo branding and alignment with UN or other logos maintaining hierarchy.
- Special usage includes a solid logo version for embossing or engraving products.
- Structure of the logo is proportionally designed around repeating round elements.
- Don'ts for logo usage include not altering, distorting, or improper placement on color or photography backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Primary palette includes Pantone-blue shades, light gray, and black. Example colors: BLUE PMS 7461C CMYK: C 96 M 41 Y 6 K 0 HEX: #0A97D9
- Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) colors are used for round elements of the primary logo. Example colors: RED PMS 185 C CMYK: C 1 M 100 Y 92 K 0 HEX: #E5243B.
- Do not use gradient or photography as background for logo palette or secondary logo application.

**Typography Information:**

- Museo Sans is the primary typeface for Nutrition Decade. Examples include Museo Sans - 100 and Museo Sans - Bold.
- Open Sans is the secondary typeface recommended for use only when Museo Sans cannot be applied.
- Typography Don'ts: Avoid using non-brand fonts, excessive font weights, and colored text against color backgrounds.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery guidelines specify secondary logo use and prohibition of primary logo usage on photographic backgrounds.
- Photography backgrounds should align with SDG palettes for secondary logo.

**Brand Voice:**

- Primary logo aligns visually with operational and practical objectives underlined by ACTION. SDG colors reinforce sustainability.
- Color palette vital for maintaining brand visibility and its contributions to SDGs in communication.

**Notes:**

- Introduction page describing the purpose of the 'Nutrition Decade' branding and sustainability goals tied to the SDGs palette.
- Citation of guidelines prepared by FAO with structured sections of logo, typography, and color branding rules.
- Numerous example pages showing application of guidelines in exhibitions, symposiums, brochures, posters, and program materials.

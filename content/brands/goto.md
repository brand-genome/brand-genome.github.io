## Brand Guidelines

### 2022

**Logo Information:**

- Minimum size requirements for logos. Digital: Minimum width is 45px; Print: Minimum width is 0.375 inches. Clear space for logos is defined by 'X', which includes half the logo height on all sides.
- Do's and don'ts for logo usage: Always use logo artwork provided. It is prohibited to change the logo color, outline the logo, redraw the logo, add shadows or effects to the logo, remove the underline, distort, rotate, or stretch the logo.
- Partners are allowed to use the 'GoTo Partner Network' logo in email signature blocks and on social media, but it must be secondary to and smaller than the company's own primary logo.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography for product logos uses 'Atyp Display Semibold'.

**Imagery Photography:**

- GoTo prohibits the use of their logos or brand design elements (e.g., wallpapers) on websites or social media channels without explicit permission. Stock photography provided by GoTo should only be used for GoTo promotion, not for other purposes. Content from GoTo's social media can only be shared by re-sharing (e.g., retweeting or sharing a post) and must not mimic original GoTo posts.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Ensure sufficient clearance space for the logo to maintain legibility in the margins of documents.

**Brand Voice:**

- Partners must clearly identify themselves as representing their own company, not GoTo, in all communications. Employees of partner companies should not represent themselves as GoTo employees, and their LinkedIn or other professional profiles must not list GoTo as their employer.
- Social media posts must clearly appear as coming from partners, not GoTo. While joint posts may be created, any social media usage of GoTo branding must adhere to brand standards and receive prior approval.

**Notes:**

- GoTo emphasizes transparency in partnerships: partners must clearly identify their relationship with GoTo, and not position themselves as GoTo using branding or visual elements. Any content featuring GoTo or its products must gain prior written approval from the GoTo marketing team.
- Advance notification is required for shared customer communications concerning GoTo updates, which enables partners to prepare their own messaging.
- Partners can only use content from official GoTo product websites and must avoid content from third-party sources.

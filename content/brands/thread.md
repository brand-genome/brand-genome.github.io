## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The primary Thread logo is described. The loop and curves in the 'T' represent the organic nature of Thread and a rotated '&' symbolizing connectivity.
- Minimum size of the logotype is specified as 0.75 inches (3/4 inches) wide. Approved colors: THREAD GRAY or KNOCKED-OUT WHITE.
- Clear space around the logotype must be at least '2X' on all sides, where X equals the height of the logo.
- Logomark minimum size is 0.25 inches (1/4 inches) wide. Approved colors: THREAD GRAY or KNOCKED-OUT WHITE. Note: Logotype and logomark should never be combined.
- Incorrect use of logo: Do not use non-approved colors, combine logotype and logomark, place on patterned backgrounds, use gradients, create patterns, crop the logomark, place on busy images, or violate the clear space.
- Horizontal and vertical versions are available for Thread Group's logotype. Minimum size: 0.75 inches (3/4 inches) wide. Approved colors: THREAD GRAY or KNOCKED-OUT WHITE.
- Thread Member logo: Available in horizontal and vertical versions for marketing materials. Minimum size: 0.75 inches (3/4 inches) wide. Approved colors: THREAD GRAY or KNOCKED-OUT WHITE.
- Thread-Certified logo: Available in horizontal and vertical formats for alliance members (e.g., software stacks, modules). Minimum size: 0.375 inches (3/8 inches) for narrower spaces and 0.75 inches (3/4 inches) wide for normal use.
- Built on Thread consumer logo: Used on packaging and collateral materials for consumer-facing products. Available in horizontal and vertical formats. Minimum size: 0.375 inches (3/8 inches) wide for narrower spaces and 0.75 inches (3/4 inches) wide for normal use.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: THREAD GRAY (PMS: Black 7, HEX: #464646, RGB: 70,70,70, CMYK: 5,5,5,85), THREAD MEDIUM GRAY (PMS: Cool Gray 9, HEX: #9B9B9B, RGB: 155,155,155, CMYK: 0,0,0,50), THREAD ORANGE (PMS: Warm Red, HEX: #FF6633, RGB: 255,102,51, CMYK: 0,75,85,0).
- Secondary colors: DARK BLUE (PMS: 7451, HEX: #96AABE, RGB: 150,170,190, CMYK: 45,25,15,0), MEDIUM BLUE (PMS: 278, HEX: #A2C1DB, RGB: 163,193,219, CMYK: 35,15,5,0), LIGHT BLUE (PMS: 656, HEX: #D9E2ED, RGB: 217,226,237, CMYK: 15,5,0,0), LIGHT GRAY (PMS: Cool Gray 1, HEX: #F7F7F7, RGB: 247,247,247, CMYK: 2,1,1,0).
- Tertiary colors: DARK GREEN (PMS: 345, HEX: #A0CDB9, RGB: 160,205,185, CMYK: 40,5,30,0), MEDIUM GREEN (PMS: 331, HEX: #BEE1D2, RGB: 190,225,210, CMYK: 25,0,20,0), LIGHT GREEN (PMS: 5523, HEX: #E6F5F0, RGB: 230,245,240, CMYK: 8,0,6,0).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Gotham. Available weights: Bold, Medium, Book, Light.
- Web safe font: Arial. Available weights: Bold, Regular.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logotype clear space: Requires 2X clear space around all sides, where X is the height of the logo.
- Logomark clear space: Requires X clear space on all sides, where X is the height of the logomark.
- Clear space requirement for Thread Group, Member, and Thread-Certified logos: 2X on all sides, or X for specific emblem height.

**Brand Voice:**

- Thread is described as warm, reliable, and user-friendly. The brand identity emphasizes connectivity between products in the home.

**Notes:**

- Illustration style: Products should be depicted as simple, continuous-line drawings to illustrate connections. Separate pictograms should be spaced with visually equal distances.
- Additional icons and illustrations provided depict connected systems like Home, Home Network, Commercial Cityscape, and Certification Processes.

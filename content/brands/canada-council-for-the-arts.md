## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The version of the Council logo should always be used without modification 
- Our logo has been designed in multiple configurations to accommodate different applications 
- Apply a protective area equal to the width of the logo wordmark around the logo
- Minimum size for the logo is specified as 25mm width
- Don't modify, distort, rotate, or recolor the logo
- Logo versions detailed: horizontal style, vertical variant
- Logo file formats include EPS and PDF
- The corporate wordmark specified as mandatory
- Examples shown of acceptable reversed logo application
- Color options include Full-color, reversed (white on color) variations
- Incorrect usage examples include logo distortion, applying gradients, and incorrect alignments

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: 'Council Blue' Pantone: 2925, HEX: #009ADD, RGB: R:0 G:154 B:221
- Neutral palette includes Black (HEX: #262626), White (HEX: #FFFFFF)
- Supporting palette includes Cork (Pantone: 7502u, HEX: #B5945F)
- Avoid using all neutral or all vibrant colors together when creating palettes

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate font is Gibson, used on logo and materials
- Gotham Narrow is designated as official house font
- Typeface details are standardized and should not be altered

**Brand Voice:**

- Mandate mentions enriching lives of Canadians through art
- Positioning outlines values of connection and foundation in art

**Notes:**

- Guidelines emphasize consistency and professionalism in brand communication

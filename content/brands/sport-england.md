## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The Sport England logo consists of a logo mark and word mark. These elements must always be used together with unchanged positioning and proportions. Standard version is in Sport England blue.
- Logo also available in white for colored backgrounds or strongly contrasting images, and black for one-color printing.
- Examples provided of white logo on colored backgrounds and its use on suitably contrasting images.
- Exclusion zone rule: An invisible space around the logo, at least the height of the letter 'E', must remain clear of other elements.
- Logo placement: Should be consistently used in one corner of the page. Examples provided.
- Minimum logo sizes: 150px wide for screen and 30mm for print to ensure legibility.
- Format specifications: EPS (high resolution for scaling), JPG (for white backgrounds), PNG (for colored backgrounds with transparency). RGB recommended for screen/online; CMYK, Pantone, and black/white formats for print.
- Incorrect logo uses: Not using logo mark and word mark separately, changing logo positioning, using low contrast backgrounds/images, placing graphics inside the exclusion zone, disproportionate logo sizing, placing too close to corners.

**Color Information:**

- Sport England logo colors include Sport England blue, black (for single-color printing), and white (for contrasting backgrounds). Other brand colors are available upon request.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Sport England image library provides free photos for promoting physical activity in non-commercial contexts. Accessible via sportengland.org/imagelibrary.
- Recommendations for social media image sizes: 1,200 x 627 pixels for Facebook/Twitter/LinkedIn/Google+, 1,080 x 1,080 pixels for Instagram.
- Image resolution requirements for print: Standard A4 size at 297 x 210mm and higher resolution needed for large formats (e.g., posters, banners). Maintain 3mm bleed around edges.
- Incorrect image usage: Avoid cropping images excessively, as this can compromise context and image quality.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Exclusion zone to maintain clear space: At least the height of the letter 'E'.
- Logo placement guidelines: Position in corners but avoid placing too close to edges.

**Brand Voice:**

- Sport England emphasizes connecting with individuals promoting physical activity via social media handles: @Sport_England, @sportengland, #ActiveNation.
- Encourages tagging Sport England in posts about sport, physical activity, or related topics using #ActiveNation.

**Notes:**

- For questions about branding, logos, photography, or social media, contact brandhelp@sportengland.org.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The identity integrates logotypes of Porto, Câmara Municipal, and Empresas Municipais into quadrangular grids formed by 2 or 3 horizontal quadrants.

**Color Information:**

- Gold is introduced as an occasional thematic accent color paired with blue (Pantone Blue 072U).
- Pantone Blue 072U is widely mentioned as the primary brand color in stationery applications, printed on Munken Lynx paper.
- RGB code specification for digital applications: (49,39,131) applied to content with a white background.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography settings for stationery applications: Regular Bold (7pt/8.5pt), Regular Semibold (same size) for contact and address details.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Examples of mutative grids including visual themed contexts like gastronomy, São João, and narrative construction related to Porto imagery.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grids should extend to the document margin for maximum alignment clarity. Cuts in grids should occur less visibly when context does not allow preservation of the quadrangular structure.

**Notes:**

- Stationery branding involves symbolic depth with recurring emphasis on dignity (gold) and historical ties (traditional blue).
- Digital press releases mandate strict visual specifications, including width limits and color-code adherence to ensure uniform identity.
- Guidelines repeatedly emphasize modular interaction between logotype grids and identity extensions for diversified media implementations.

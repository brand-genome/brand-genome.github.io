## Brand Guidelines

### 2011

**Logo Information:**

- Horizontal and vertical formats of the Tasmanian Government logo defined. Minimum size for vertical format: 20mm width; Minimum size for horizontal format: 30mm width. Exclusion zone specified by height of 'T' in vertical logo or 'a' in horizontal logo.
- Logo appears in three colors: Blue PMS 653, Olive PMS 618, and Black. Associated color codes: HEX, CMYK, and RGB provided.
- One-color options for logo defined: Black or Blue PMS (653). Logo can appear in positive or reversed (e.g., black logo on white vs. white on solid). Use mono versions on colored backgrounds.
- Incorrect logo usage outlined: Do not add outlines, create own versions, change colors, or place color logo on image/background. Examples included.
- Default strapline version required; alternate 'Tasmanian Government' logo allowed for specific instances like gambling/road safety material and co-branding.

**Color Information:**

- Color breakdown for logo: Blue PMS 653 (HEX #21578a, RGB R:33 G:87 B:138, CMYK C:96 M:59 Y:4 K:17), Olive PMS 618 (HEX #aea444, RGB R:174 G:164 B:68, CMYK C:11 M:10 Y:68 K:25), Black (HEX #000000).
- One-color options of logo allowed in either black or Blue PMS 653 for positive/reversed application.

**Typography Information:**

- Recommended font for Tasmanian Government materials: Gill Sans Light, 12pt for A4, 80 kerning (optional), 15pt leading.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Basewave device provides a graphic divide between design content and logo. Logo positioned in bottom right and department name in bottom left. Text restrictions: one line (department name/URL) or two lines for URL+department combo.
- Exclusion zones defined: Above logo (10mm max white border allowed), basewave along the entire width of a page. Minimum logo size: vertical 20mm wide; horizontal 30mm wide.

**Brand Voice:**

- Reinforces Tasmanian Government credibility and unified service across departments. Positioning statement 'Explore the possibilities' complements brand essence.

**Notes:**

- Sub-brands like schools, police, ambulance, and fire services have exemptions for uniforms/vehicles but must comply with generic communication guidelines.
- Extra-wide formats (width>height*2) and extra-narrow formats (two-columns or less) can exclude basewave, replaced by horizontal logo maintaining spacing rules.

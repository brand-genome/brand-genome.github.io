## Brand Guidelines

### 2010

**Logo Information:**

- The Chicago Bears mark should not be altered in any way. The marks and logotype should be highly visible and unmistakable across all applications.
- Primary Mark Color Variations: The full-color Bears mark is the preferred version for brand applications when placed on white or light-colored backgrounds. A full-color reverse mark is used for dark backgrounds. Grayscale and one-color black variations are for one-color printing.
- Secondary Mark Color Variations: Designed to complement the primary mark, follows the same usage guidelines as primary marks on different backgrounds and in printing.
- Logotype Color Variations: The full-color Bears logotype complements the primary mark and reproduces best on white or light-colored backgrounds. Specific variations for dark and orange backgrounds exist, along with monochromatic variations.
- Descriptor Logotype Color Variations: Includes the descriptor 'Chicago' above the Bears logotype. Variants for different background colors include full-color reverse and monochromatic options.
- Minimum Sizes: Primary and secondary marks should not be smaller than ½ inch in width. Logotype should not be smaller than 1 inch, and marks with logotype should not be smaller than ¾ inch.
- Incorrect Logo Usage: Do not alter angles of marks, add outlines, interchange colors, flip marks, change color percentages, distort proportions, crop marks, or use with conflicting backgrounds.
- Chicago Bears helmet available in vector format in full-color and one-color. White outline is used for dark backgrounds to maintain definition.
- Do not use Bears marks/logotype to replace text in sentences, phrases, e.g., 'Bears' or 'Chicago Bears.' Logos are symbols and not text substitutes.

**Color Information:**

- Bears Blue: Pantone 5395, CMYK (C100, M60, Y0, K80), RGB (R0, G22, B42), HEX (#0B162A).
- Bears Orange: Pantone 1665, CMYK (C0, M75, Y100, K0), RGB (R200, G56, B3), HEX (#C83803).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Background Controls: Full-color marks can appear on solid backgrounds using Bears palette colors or photographic backgrounds offering enough contrast. Avoid using on photographs with insufficient contrast or highly detailed areas.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clearspace Minimum: Equal to 50% of the height for primary and secondary marks, and 100% of the height for the logotype. Illustrated examples provided.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand Promise: The Chicago Bears embody the values of Chicago and its people, uniting them through an indelible connection with their city.
- The Chicago Bears are deeply rooted in the city's work ethic, founded on George Halas' philosophy of grit, determination, and authenticity as core values.
- Tagline 'One City. One Team.' emphasizes the generational bond between the Bears, Chicago, and its citizens—woven into the social, cultural, and emotional fabric of the city.
- The 4th Phase: Bears fans are described as the '4th Phase' essential for inspiration and energy, motivating and supporting the organization both on and off the field.

**Notes:**

- Partner Logos and Placement: Various designations (e.g., Hall of Fame Partner, Proud Partner) have specific size/placement/proportion rules alongside Bears logos in applications.
- Brand Extensions: Event logos include partner marks, with placement/size determined by the Bears. Required approval for new logos.

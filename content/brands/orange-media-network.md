## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Primary logo includes full-color logo (with and without tagline), one-color logo, and one-color logo on a colored background. Includes an icon and logotype.
- Vertical logo includes full-color logo (with and without tagline), one-color logo, and one-color logo on a colored background. Includes an icon and logotype.
- Horizontal logo for limited vertical space applications (e.g., website headers or pens). Only contains the full-color logo (without tagline), one-color logo, and one-color logo on a colored background. Also includes an icon and logotype.
- Logo clear space: Leave a minimum of the x-height of the letter 'o' in 'orange media' on all sides of the logo.
- Minimum size: The logo's x-height must not be smaller than 0.1 inches.
- Incorrect logo usage: Do not modify media logos without approval from Orange Media Network, do not crowd words into the bubble, do not use colors outside OSU brand guidelines, do not apply a drop shadow, do not place the logo on photo backgrounds, do not place the full-color logo on a color background, do not rotate the logo, and do not use typefaces outside OSU brand guidelines.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: OSU Orange with CMYK (0, 76, 100, 0), RGB (216, 90, 26), and Pantone 1665.
- Secondary colors: Light Blue (CMYK 29, 0, 10, 14; RGB 156, 197, 202; Pantone 5503), Dark Gray (CMYK 0, 0, 0, 85; RGB 77, 77, 79; Pantone 7540), Dark Orange (CMYK 9, 81, 100, 1; RGB 218, 85, 38; Pantone 7579), Light Gray (CMYK 2, 0, 9, 36; RGB 171, 175, 166; Pantone 7539).
- The Orange Media Network logo uses OSU brand colors with the addition of a dark orange for the overlap of the bubbles and tint of black for the words “Orange Media.”
- Color usage should follow OSU color guidelines available at communications.oregonstate.edu/brand/color-palette.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary brand font is Leitura Sans. Additional OSU-approved typefaces are allowed. Further font details are also available on OSU's branding guidelines at communications.oregonstate.edu/brand/fonts.
- Leitura Sans fonts available: Italic 1-4, Grot 1-4 with sample characters (Aa Bb Cc 123, etc.).
- Messaging typography: Set text in white Leitura Sans Italic 2, centered vertically and horizontally with capitalized words.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography used in co-branding applications must be dark or simple enough for white text to remain legible.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rule: Minimum x-height of the letter 'o' in 'orange media' on all sides of logo.
- Minimum size rule: The x-height of the logo must not be smaller than 0.1 inches.

**Brand Voice:**

- Messaging tone emphasizes action-oriented keywords such as 'Play', 'Lead', 'Create', 'Inspire', 'Listen', and 'Express'. The text is applied within a 'Bubble' for sub-brand messaging.

**Notes:**

- Co-branding elements include the full logo, bubble only, or logotype only (available in horizontal variation). Messaging should relate to the media format of the co-brand (e.g., radio, print).
- Business cards and letterheads use elements and patterns from the Orange Media logo icon.
- Incorrect usage warnings specify avoiding modifications to logos, inappropriate typography, and non-OSU-compliant colors.
- For additional support or inquiries, contact Don Boucher, Graphics Advisor (contact details provided).

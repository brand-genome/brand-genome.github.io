## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The primary Alpha logo consists of a red tilted question mark at a 45° angle, paired horizontally with the text 'Alpha.' Minimum size is specified as '6 pt' or '9 mm,' with minimum clear space requirements.
- Secondary logos are allowed only for specific contexts like presentations for religious leaders or donors and should maintain the brand's visual identity. Alternate color versions include black and white.
- Logo restrictions include prohibition of multicolor designs, patterns, rotation, or adjustments to the typeface, color, or angle. Previous iterations with flexible logo designs are no longer permitted.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Red: C0 M95 Y100 K0 | R228 G35 B18 | HEX #e42312
Black: C60 M40 Y40 K100 | R0 G0 B0 | HEX #000000
Bright White: C0 M0 Y0 K0 | R255 G255 B255 | HEX #ffffff
- Secondary Colors:
Navy Blue (C95 M80 Y34 K27 | R39 G55 B94 | HEX #27375e),
Saffron Yellow (C0 M39 Y91 K0 | R248 G169 B31 | HEX #f8a91f),
Sage Green (C60 M41 Y65 K32 | R96 G105 B81 | HEX #606951).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Font: ITC Avant Garde (Weights: Bold, Medium, Book, Extra Light). Requires authorization for download.
- Alternative Font: Helvetica (Weights: Bold, Regular, Light). To be used when ITC Avant Garde is unavailable.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style: Inclusive, friendly, natural, and aligned with Alpha's 'authentic' brand values. Photos should capture the natural atmosphere with a relaxed and spontaneous vibe, avoiding posed or static imagery.
- Guidelines for photography: Talent showcased should reflect diversity and authenticity (avoid stock images or models). Clothing should be clean and logos-free. Use natural lighting and capture subjects in natural contexts.
- Video guidelines: Ensure alignment with brand typography, logo, and colors. Background music should complement, not distract, the narrative. Maintain natural conversation settings, apply the 'rule of thirds' for framing, and collect signed consent forms for all participants.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum clear space around the primary logo is specified. The requirements ensure the logo maintains visibility and integrity in various layouts.

**Brand Voice:**

- Alpha's tone of voice: Inclusive, positive, friendly, inspiring, creative, bold, authentic, and simple with a global perspective across 50+ offices and 100+ countries.

**Notes:**

- Alpha International retains copyright for all logos and designs as outlined in this document. The chapter includes guidelines for referencing partnerships in related materials.
- Alpha resources are adapted for contexts such as prisons, youth, and professional environments, with distinct branding assets provided for each.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The JTB Business Travel Logo exists in 3 versions (Cube, Stacked, and Horizontal). These serve for different design contexts, such as better proportions or readability. Examples of usage and appropriate scenarios are detailed on pages covering the topic.
- Variations include: Cube (primary), Stacked, and Horizontal formats. Stacked and Horizontal logos should be reserved for situations where the primary logo format is unsuitable (e.g., small items such as pens).
- Clear space rule: Maintain the height/width of the 'T' in 'Travel' around the logo to ensure visibility and distinctiveness across designs. This applies regardless of scale.
- The logo is single color for application. It is required to maximize contrast on background colors or images. Visual simplicity in background design is emphasized.
- Logo do's and don'ts: Examples include avoiding color changes, distortions, combinations with non-brand imagery, effects (e.g., 3D), and usage on dark or overly complex/color-clashing backgrounds. Proper brand fidelity is enforced.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors:
- Dynamic Red: PMS 187, Web-Safe: #CC0000, RGB(204, 0, 0), CMYK(C20, M100, Y90, K0)
- Sophisticated Gray: PMS Cool Gray 7, Web-Safe: #999999, RGB(153, 153, 153), CMYK(K40%). An additional alternate web color ‘Light Gray Background (#f0f1f3)’ and ‘Dark Gray Text (#808183)’ are mentioned.
- Secondary colors:
- Business Blue: PMS 2198, Web-Safe: #4AC9E3, RGB(74, 201, 227), CMYK(C61, M0, Y15, K0)
- Zen Peach: PMS 474, Web-Safe: #F3CFB3, RGB(243, 207, 179), CMYK(C0, M25, Y36, K0)
- Rich Green: PMS 2243, Web-Safe: #00957A, RGB(0, 149, 122), CMYK(C90, M5, Y63, K6). The BusinessTravel palette emphasizes relaxation and happiness visually.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Background images for logos should be simple and light. Contrasts between logo and background must be maximized for visibility.
- Prohibited design applications include placing logos on busy, patterned, or dark imagery that disrupts or diminishes visual clarity.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space (protected area) around the logo must equate to height/width of the 'T' in 'Travel'. This separates text, graphics, or page boundaries and is consistent across sizes or formats.

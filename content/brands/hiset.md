## Brand Guidelines

### 2025

**Logo Information:**

- LOGO
Only the states and authorized partners are licensed by PSI to use the HiSET logo.
The primary logo is available in Blackboard or white. Use guidelines depending on the background overlay.

- Clear space is defined as x = clear space around logo (1.27cm for print).
Layouts must include enough clear space around the HiSET logo to ensure clarity.

- Trademark Guidelines:
- Always use the correct capitalization as HiSET (Uppercase 'H', lowercase 'i', uppercase 'SET').
- Include the registered trademark symbol (®) consistently.
- Use the trademark footnote: 'PSI, the PSI logo and HiSET are registered trademarks of PSI used under license.'
- Never use the trademark in a way implying endorsement by PSI for non-PSI products/services.

- Internet and Social Media Use Guidelines:
- Use the HiSET trademark/logo graphics exactly as provided.
- Include a trademark footnote ('PSI trademarks must be referenced.') on all applicable pages.
- Avoid using PSI trademarks in mobile app product names.


**Color Information:**

- Primary Color Palette:
- Ochre (#DD8128, PMS 138, RGB: R221 G129 B40).
Primary colors prominently used for key elements/backgrounds.

- Secondary Color Palette:
- Blackboard (#1A212B, PMS Black 6C, RGB: R26 G33 B43).
- Peach Sunset (#FF9A66).
- Sunburst Yellow (PMS 123C).
- Coral Red (#B33B34, PMS 7626C).
Usage includes icons and photograph frames.

- Application Examples:
- Ochre is prominently used for branding and key elements.

- Color Combinations:
- Coral Red on Ink for button fills and highlights.
- Sunburst Yellow on Ink for emphasis.
Displays proper accessibility and cohesion in combinations.


**Typography Information:**

- Brand Fonts:
- Poppins (Use for headlines/subheadings with bold/lightweights).
- Lato (Use for body text in regular weights).

- System Fonts:
- Arial (Fallback for Poppins).
- Verdana (Fallback for Lato).
Usage suggested for consistent and professional appearance.


**Imagery Photography:**

- Secondary palette colors applied to photography frames.
Focus on accessibility and responsiveness.

- Photography style:
- Bright, natural imagery emphasizing test takers.


**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space around the HiSET logo:
1.27cm for print layouts.

- Primary colors are used prominently in layout designs (e.g., backgrounds, headers).


**Brand Voice:**

- Brand tone and style:
- Poppins (Modern, approachable personality).
- Lato (Readable, professional).


**Notes:**

- In 2024, ETS adopted 'The Source' as its brand symbol, forming the basis for HiSET's visual identity.

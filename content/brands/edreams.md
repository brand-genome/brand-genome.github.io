## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Brand guidelines
Symbol
Logotype
Logo
Architecture
The logo is a graphic comprised of the mark (symbol).
The lettering is created using the Netto typeface, and the three dreaming bubbles are inseparable.
Use the complete logo. The only time you should use the symbol alone is on the eDreams website or social media channels where there are other elements to help the user recognise the brand. The logo can also be accompanied by a slogan.
- Brand guidelines
Vertical version
The horizontal version of the logo is used in all media. However, in exceptional cases where there is not enough space for the usual logo, you can use the vertical version of the eDreams logo.
- Brand guidelines
Legibility
An exclusion zone around the logo has been created to protect its integrity and make sure the logo is easy to read.
Minimum size online: 60px
Minimum size print: 20mm
- Brand guidelines
Positive & Negative version
Logo versions include positive, negative, grayscale, and monochromatic variants.
- Brand guidelines
Don'ts
It is not allowed to alter the structure, colour, proportions, elements, or the direction of the logo. Examples include: removing or moving elements, stretching, outlining, changing colours, and adding shadows.
- Brand guidelines
Background colours
The logo may be applied over specific background colours (white, blue, yellow). Use negative versions or black monochromatic variants to ensure legibility.
- Brand guidelines
Background images
Use the negative version on dark backgrounds which do not contrast with the blue part of the logo. For complex backgrounds, apply a solid corporate colour label behind the logo.
- Brand guidelines
Favicon and app icon
The eDreams favicon is linked with the eDreams website and serves as a smaller representation of the brand, typically at 32x32px.

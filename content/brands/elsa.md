## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The ELSA logo consists of two parts: the design and the subtitle. National or Local Groups use their names written in capital letters using EB Garamond. Dimensions for the ELSA logo usage include specific pixel measurements.
- The Protected Zone is determined by the cap height of the letter 'L' in the ELSA logo, ensuring no interference from other logos or graphical elements. Placement rules specify the bottom center, right, or left corner, and alphabetical placement for National/Local Groups. Logos of projects or sponsors should be placed to the right with balanced size ratios.
- Incorrect usage rules include: do not animate, recolor, rotate, skew, separate elements, or create ornaments. Use official templates and logo generator only.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Logo Colors: ELSA Blue—RGB (10, 48, 135), CMYK (100, 85, 0, 0), HEX '#0a3087', Black—RGB (0, 0, 0), CMYK (0, 0, 0, 100), HEX '#000000', White—RGB (255, 255, 255), CMYK (0, 0, 0, 0).
- Supportive color palette includes various RGB, CMYK, and HEX codes for corporate design elements, charts, and graphs such as RGB (27, 105, 238), CMYK (84, 60, 0, 0), HEX '#1b69ee', among others. Anchor color remains ELSA Blue.

**Typography Information:**

- Font families include EB Garamond for the logo and body copy in extended documents like brochures. Lato is used for headlines, subheads, and digital materials—specific weights are mentioned: Light, Regular, Bold, and Black.
- Alternative fonts Garamond Monotype and Adobe Garamond Pro can be used for intricate material when different font weights are needed. EB Garamond must be used in the logo.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Use professional, high-resolution imagery portraying ELSA's brand and personality. Ensure legal usage rights and credits when applicable. Recommended sources: pixabay.com, pexels.com, stocksnap.io.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The ELSA logo's Protected Zone ensures no infringement by other graphical elements, defined by the cap height of 'L'. Placement rules ensure logo consistency and visual balance in designs.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice descriptors: Familiar, Brief, Humane, Personal, Active, Trustworthy, Truly Honest, and Daring. Messaging focuses on building trust and treating individuals as people rather than entities.
- Core purpose: Contributing to legal education, fostering mutual understanding, and promoting social responsibility of law students and young lawyers.

**Notes:**

- Flagship projects and special event branding have unique sub-brands beyond the main ELSA logo and identity while maintaining ELSA's core philosophy.
- Various flagship projects have specific design, color, font, and promotion timeline guidelines tailored to their unique activities, e.g., ELSA Moot Court Competition, EHRMCC, STEP, etc.

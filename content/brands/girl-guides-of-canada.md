## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- All merchandise must include the official Girl Guides of Canada logo (Trefoil or wordmark designs). The Trefoil alone is permitted where space is limited.

**Color Information:**

- Suppliers must adhere to the GGC color palette for all merchandise. Crests, pins, and jewelry must also conform to the color palette, with exceptions for metallics like gold, silver, or bronze on pins.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Merchandise guidelines stress considering diverse member needs (e.g., apparel fit/sizing, cost, and longevity) when developing materials for camps, events, or other activities.

**Brand Voice:**

- Usage of copyrighted or trademark-protected text and imagery (e.g., Disney characters) is strictly prohibited unless explicit permission is secured.

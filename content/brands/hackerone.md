## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Wordmark and logo overview: Emphasis is placed on the word 'hacker' in the wordmark design, highlighting HackerOne's core focus.
- Logo is a versatile, abbreviated version of the wordmark. Use the logo in situations where legibility or visibility is compromised, or there are space constraints.
- Wordmark and logo usage: The logo and wordmark can be displayed in any color that ensures legibility and contrasts against the background, preferably solid backgrounds.
- Clear space around the wordmark: The wordmark must always be surrounded by clear space equal to the x-height of the lowercase letter forms in the wordmark.
- Minimum size requirements for wordmark: Digital - 75 pixels wide; Print - 0.75 inches wide.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Palette includes colors such as Hex #EC1075 (R:237 G:16 B:117 | C:0 M:99 Y:23 K:0), and Hex #ED2884 (R:237 G:41 B:133 | C:0 M:95 Y:10 K:0).
- Secondary Palette includes Hex #28B04A (R:41 G:177 B:75 | C:78 M:1 Y:100 K:0), and Hex #6BBD45 (R:107 G:190 B:70 | C:66 M:0 Y:99 K:0).
- Web typography integrates a structured color usage: headlines (Hex #EC1075), subheadlines (Hex #EC1075), body copy (Hex #494649).

**Typography Information:**

- Font selection prioritizes clear and dynamic typography for communication problems, specifying sentence case and variations suitable for branding.
- Typography attributes: Headlines configured to use tracking set to 0, paragraph spacing to 10px, aligned for clarity in both digital and print formats.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography emphasizes hackers as the focal point, integrating 31° diagonal lines in the background to symbolize the brand identity.
- Diagonal guidelines: Must be white at 40% transparency when used in photography. When standalone, it must be pink (Hex #EC1075) and displayed at 100% transparency.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The 'Diagonal' concept integrates 31° angles from the logo design, reflecting HackerOne's modern approach to both brand identity and cybersecurity solutions.
- Spacing rules for typography: Paragraph spacing set to 10px ensuring legibility, and tracking set to 0 across both print and digital formats.

**Notes:**

- The guidelines emphasize integrating hacker-centric principles and imagery while adhering to modern design paradigms.
- Key visual elements, including the diagonal design, must always align with the specified transparency and placement rules for consistency.

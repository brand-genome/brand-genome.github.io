## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- BRAND IDENTITY GUIDE outlines proprietary usage of ReechCraft logos, including acceptable versions, color specifications, and spacing requirements as listed.
- Logo must adhere to color standards: Red (C:4 M:100 Y:95 K:0, HEX: #E41E2B), Medium Gray (C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:40, HEX: #A7A9AC), Black (C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:100, HEX: #000000), White (C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:0, HEX: #FFFFFF).
- Not acceptable logo versions include modified colors, distorted proportions, and improper backgrounds violating set design rules.

**Color Information:**

- Proprietary colors specified for branding: Red (Pantone N/A, C:4 M:100 Y:95 K:0, HEX: #E41E2B, RGB: 228,30,43), Medium Gray (Pantone 877C, C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:40, HEX: #A7A9AC, RGB: 167,169,172).
- Color usage rules emphasize consistency for internal and external communications using specified color palette, tints allowed (50%-80%).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Typeface: HandelGothic BT for logos and materials headlines; Univers family for body text. Subsidiary logos use Serpentine Bold Oblique and Bold-Bold.
- Typography consistency ensures unified branding across all marketing content.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Printed communications prioritize primary color logos for brand alignment, using spaced placement and avoiding repetition within a piece.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum clear space around the logo is set to the width of the red bar continuation from the letter 'L' in 'Lift'.

**Brand Voice:**

- Messaging focus: innovation, durability, and ease of use. Brand tone emphasizes efficiency and customer-first principles.
- Tagline examples: 'The Go-Anywhere Lift for Anyone,' 'Portability Redefined,' accompanying specific product logos for marketing impact.

**Notes:**

- Subsidiary logos require trademark symbols ('TM', '®') and associated Pantone colors as part of their design usage.
- Comprehensive logo and branding compliance include adherence to OSHA and ANSI guidelines.

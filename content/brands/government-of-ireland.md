### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Design and use guidelines for logos and marks within the government identity system, specifying alignments, colors, and applications.

**Color Information:**

- Specifications for primary and supporting colors including:
- State Green (Pantone: 3292, CMYK: 100, 0, 50, 60, RGB: 0, 77, 68, HEX: #004D44)
- State Gold (Pantone: 872, CMYK: 0, 11, 50, 40, RGB: 163, 145, 97, HEX: #A39161).
Supporting colors include Black, Dark Grey, and Light Grey.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography system includes two primary fonts:
- Quadraat OT (Bold, Regular, Italic, OpenType).
- Lato (Sans-serif, styles include Light, Regular, Bold, with italics).
Fallback system fonts include Arial and Georgia.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery guidelines highlight the use of clean, professional photography representing diversity in government activities. Examples include single and multiple image banners.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Layout guidelines specify page margins and header/footer sizes:
- Left/Right margins: 30mm
- Header: 45mm
- Footer: 30mm.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone emphasizes simplicity, clarity, and accessibility, aligned with government communication objectives.

**Notes:**

- Correct use of marks, colors, and typography.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- The primary logo is shown as a 'hi' disc with white lettering on a green background. It represents accessibility, friendliness, and informality suitable for audiences of all ages.
- The minimum size for the logo is specified as 10mm in height. A smaller version is available for extreme circumstances with the logo mark and text separated.
- A black-and-white version of the primary logo is available for monochrome applications.
- A reversed version of the primary logo can be used when the green background does not work.
- Reversed black-and-white versions of the logo are available for situations where the primary black-and-white logo does not work.
- Clear space rules specify the exclusion zone around the logo as at least 100% of the logo's height/width (or 50% in extreme circumstances).
- When co-branding, the Healthy Ireland logo should appear equal in scale and value to third-party logos and centered for proportional alignment.
- Suggested usage of logo in community spaces such as schools, workplaces, clubs, and public areas.

**Color Information:**

- The primary corporate color is Healthy Ireland green: Pantone 376 C with CMYK values (C: 50, M: 0, Y: 100, K: 0). Use Pantone color wherever possible.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Exclusion zone rules specify a minimum clear space of 100% of the X height/width of the logo, reducible to 50% in extreme circumstances.
- Co-branding alignment guidelines specify equal scaling and centering between Healthy Ireland and third-party logos based on proportions.

**Brand Voice:**

- Healthy Ireland promotes values of fairness, accountability, accessibility, and friendliness. Its tone is informal and invites conversation about health and wellbeing.
- The brand tagline 'hi - Good To See You!' emphasizes positive national spirit around collective health and wellbeing.

**Notes:**

- Contact details for Healthy Ireland include email (healthyireland@health.gov.ie), website (www.healthyireland.ie), and phone (+353 1 635 3000).

## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- Logo includes the horizontal version with 'Qualiopi - Processus certifié' accompanied by the 'REPUBLIQUE FRANCAISE' emblem with the 'Marianne' icon followed by 'RF' in a horizontal layout.
- Horizontal layout with specific proportions and paddings; the logo must not be modified, altered, or used without the 'Marianne + RF' band.
- Minimum size specified as 23mm for 'REPUBLIQUE FRANCAISE' in the logo. Clear zone enforced with padding equal to the height of 'Y' around the logo.
- Do not: modify the colors, transform elements (e.g., logos and triangles), change typography, apply transparency, use unapproved effects, or associate it with any unauthorized logos.
- Spacing rules specify the distance between the Qualiopi logo and partner logos. The separation is defined by the width of the Marianne element ('Y').
- The logo must be contained in its white cartouche regardless of the background (e.g., photographs, colors). The cartouche ensures readability and compliance.

**Color Information:**

- Color specifications include:
Red: CMYK C0 M100 Y100 K0, RGB R225 G0 B15, HEX E1000F.
Blue: CMYK C100 M90 Y20 K0, RGB R0 G0 B145, HEX 000091.

**Typography Information:**

- Recommended typefaces:
- Titles: Arial Black.
- Body text: Arial Regular.
- Typography for certification explanations is flexible but must be clearly legible and a size equivalent or slightly smaller than 'processus certifié'.

**Imagery Photography:**

- The logo on photographic backgrounds must remain in its white protective cartouche, ensuring visibility and compliance.

**Spacing Layout:**

- A clear zone equivalent to the height of 'Y' is required around the logo. Partner logos must be placed at least 'Y' away from the Qualiopi logo.

**Notes:**

- The document specifies that 'Qualiopi - Processus certifié' is a certification logo and cannot be modified. Compliance with all prescribed visual standards is necessary.
- Version of the guidelines is from '7 décembre 2020'.

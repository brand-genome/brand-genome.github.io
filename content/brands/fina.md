## Brand Guidelines

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- The six golden stars are a symbol of the six aquatic disciplines governed by FINA (last star marks the introduction of High Diving since 2013).
- FINA Logo: Name, corporate logo, colours, typeface - these are the pillars of the identity of the organization.

**Color Information:**

- Pantone 1235C, CMYK: 2/30/97/0, RGB: 249/182/33, Hex: #F9B621.

**Typography Information:**

- Toupee (Regular/Bold): FINA's official font.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Recommended usage involves dynamic aquatic imagery to promote engagement.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logos must adhere to safe zone guidelines - defined by the width of the 'F'.

**Brand Voice:**

- FINA represents universality and excellence in aquatics, embracing its mission to celebrate true stars of aquatics.

**Notes:**

- Special attention is given to the history and evolution of the FINA logo (specific versions found in the document).

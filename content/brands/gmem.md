## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The GMEM logo consists of a circular shape representing continuity and perpetual movement. It has two main complementary versions: a simpler logo with only the name of the structure and a 'deployed' version with a baseline.
- The simple logo with no baseline is suitable for three contexts: (1) materials belonging to GMEM where redundancy due to the baseline would occur, (2) partner materials with limited space, and (3) visually-focused formats with reduced informative requirements.
- The deployed logo with a baseline is suitable for: (1) partner structures or events where GMEM's identification needs clarification, (2) video signature elements, and (3) institutional materials with more informational importance.
- Safe zone for the simple logotype: Minimum clearance of 1/2X on all sides. Minimum printed size: X = 12mm. Minimum digital size: X = 32px.
- Safe zone for the deployed logotype: Minimum clearance of 1/2Y on all sides. Minimum printed size: Y = 9mm. Minimum digital size: Y = 100px.
- The logotype must not appear in colors or gradients, except within systems requiring direct tones. Black or white versions (monochromatic) are used depending on background brightness. No external alterations are allowed unless pre-approved by GMEM.
- Avoid placing the logotype on backgrounds with high contrast, to preserve legibility. These guidelines refer to background neutrality and gradient avoidance.
- Logo version usage by background: On light tones, use the black logo. On darker tones, use the white logo. For vibrant or medium tones, both options are permissible depending on clarity.
- Incorrect usage examples include: (1) Adding framing elements, (2) applying rotation or distortion, (3) using gradients or shadows, (4) converting logo parts into outlines, and (5) changing typeface or structural components.

**Color Information:**

- Logo color options are limited to monochromatic variants in black or white, adapted to light, dark, and vibrant backgrounds.

**Typography Information:**

- The GMEM typeface must remain unaltered, including its design structure, character proportions, and spacing modules as integral elements.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space setup for the simple logo mandates a 1/2X margin on all sides. Similarly, for the deployed logo, a 1/2Y clearance is required.

**Notes:**

- Static branding principles emphasize clarity, simplicity, and preservation of the logo's design elements, avoiding external modifications in all contexts.

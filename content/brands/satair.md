## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- There are two positive versions of the logo: one in brushed steel and one in black. Positive versions should be used on white or pale backgrounds.
- Negative version of the logo is used when applied on black or dark backgrounds.
- Channel-specific versions are to be used when products or services are exclusive to one trading entity (Airbus or Satair). The standard logo should be used otherwise. After integration, only the default Satair logo will suffice.
- The business cards feature a black front with a silver logo and a white back with a black logo, along with 'An Airbus Services Company' endorsement text at the bottom.
- Letterhead features clean, professional style, underlining simplicity with an 'An Airbus Services Company' tagline at the bottom.
- Roll-up banners feature the tagline 'An Airbus Services Company' at the bottom.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors are defined using CMYK, RGB, and HTML codes with options to use tints at 25%, 50%, and 75%. Pantone references are included for printed applications. For usage, direct color Pantone is favored.
- Examples of specific Pantone values include Pantone 2210, Pantone 7706, Pantone 3282, and Pantone 7635 among others, covering both primary and secondary color palettes.

**Typography Information:**

- The corporate typeface is Helvetica Neue LT. Users are encouraged to apply typographic rules for consistency, and the typeface provides a structured, aerial feel due to its range of weights.

**Notes:**

- The corporate visual guidelines emphasize integration with Airbus branding and a consistent professional tone throughout stationery designs.
- Business cards, envelopes, and roll-up designs emphasize the 'An Airbus Services Company' tagline to strengthen the brand association.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The Great-West Lifeco logo should not be used either on its own or dual-branded with Irish Life in external communications with customers or financial advisers. It is primarily used for internal and corporate communications, with the Irish Life logo taking primary position as the consumer brand.
- Great-West Lifeco logo is required to be a supporting parent brand for internal corporate communications purposes.

**Brand Voice:**

- Irish Life brand values align with the Consumer Protection Code (CPC). Marketing collateral must adhere to mandatory inclusions and approval by compliance. Regulatory disclosure requirements include guidelines for elements such as disclaimers and font sizes.

**Notes:**

- All marketing material must include Irish Life’s regulatory disclosure: 'Irish Life Assurance plc is regulated by the Central Bank of Ireland.'

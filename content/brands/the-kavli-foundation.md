## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Clear space/safe zone rules: 'X' clearance is shown around the logo.
- Alternative versions of the logo are provided for print limitations and small-scale usage, which include a simplified version and the full logo lockup (logomark + logotype).
- Notes specify that the full logo lockup should always be prioritized, but alternative versions can be used for cases where legibility is compromised due to resolution or scale.
- The logo is intended to exemplify architectural strength and stability with origins in sacred geometry.
- Only approved colors and placements for the logo are permitted.
- When placing the logo on a photograph, it should use a white typeface. If legibility is compromised, the all-white logo should be used, and the background should be modified or changed if necessary.
- Notes specify that the black logo should only be used where printing restrictions limit colors, and the light gray logo is restricted for subtle use like watermarking.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Colors: PANTONE Red 032 C (HEX: #EF3340, CMYK: C:0, M:90, Y:86, K:0, RGB: R:239, G:51, B:64) and PANTONE 288 C (HEX: #003399, CMYK: C:100, M:94, Y:0, K:23, RGB: R:0, G:33, B:121).
- Secondary Colors: Palette includes complementary colors identified for the Kavli Sciences, such as PANTONE 3135C, PANTONE 254C, PANTONE 583C, among others.
- Color usage rules: Primary branding should prioritize the logo on blue backgrounds where applicable. Secondary usage includes the red/blue logo on white backgrounds.
- Black logo should only be used when a single black ink restriction applies. Light gray is restricted to subtle use cases like watermarking.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family: Avenir Next, chosen for its modern update of a classic sans serif style and alignment with the shapes found in the Kavli logo.
- Font weights and roles: Demi Bold and Bold used for headlines and titles, Medium used for large headlines, titles, and names, Regular and Medium used for body text and subtitles.
- Italic font specifically for captions, quotes, and accents.
- Bold Caps and Demi Bold Caps used for labels and subtitles.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clearance for logo placement uses 'X' measurement for spacing.

**Notes:**

- The Kavli visual identity emphasizes contrast with a bold use of red and blue, aided by a secondary palette highlighting the Kavli Sciences specifically.
- The sacred geometry inspiration behind the logo conveys focus, energy, discovery, and the fundamental properties of the universe.
- When placing the logomark on photography, a white logo or all-white logo can be used to enhance visibility, and background modifications may be needed.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- Bitdefender corporate logo uses geometric, precise lines symbolizing the Dacian wolf, associated with sharpness, vigilance, and supreme defense.
- Minimum logo size requirement: Standard version minimum width is 30mm. Optimized versions usable for 20mm–30mm dimensions.
- Clear space rule for main logo: Surrounding area must equal height of the 'f' glyph to maintain readability. For extended exceptions, specific proportions include f-height multiplied for left, right, top, and bottom spaces.

**Color Information:**

- Corporate logo allows positive, negative, colored, and black/white versions, ensuring usability across various production and branding contexts.

**Typography Information:**

- Brand typography includes Bitter Regular and Bold for headlines, Roboto Thin, Regular, Bold, and Black for body text in corporate communication, and Exo 2 typeface for technical uses.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Bitdefender brand avatar represents the Dacian wolf in a modern, digital style. It symbolizes strength, brilliance, and technological precision.

**Spacing Layout:**

- For corporate communication layouts, the logo is centered on the upper portion of pages, with its dimensions calculated as a divisor of page width.

**Brand Voice:**

- Bitdefender values intellectual provocation, innovation, heroism, and maintaining brilliance in data security through clarity and sharp insights. Encourages boldness and adaptability.

**Notes:**

- There are strict logo usage interdictions: No arbitrary scaling, deformation, use of effects (shadow, glow), or placement on overcrowded or non-complementary backgrounds.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2012

**Logo Information:**

- Zone de protection defined using 'x' with preferences for alignment of the symbol with the partner name, ideally not overlapping with .brussels. Maximum height of the symbol is 5x.
- Baseline typography for logos with Mija Bold (30 pts body) and Mija Regular (16.5 pts baseline, 20 pts interline). Alignment options depend on the length of the partner name and placement of the symbol.
- Minimum requirement to use at least two colors of the regional palette (blue, yellow, or gray). Partner colors may adapt to the regional palette. Color use should favor readability for the partner's name, not .brussels.
- Incorrect logo compositions include: insufficient regional colors, colors not belonging to the regional palette, unauthorized typographies or sizes, overly large or mispositioned symbol, non-standard line spacing, improper alignment, and the use of uppercase letters.
- Logos may be composed in one line or two lines if a suitable solution isn't possible for single-line format.

**Color Information:**

- Mandatory usage of at least two regional palette colors (blue, yellow, gray) in partner logos. Other usages favoring readability of partner names over .brussels.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography for logos uses Mija Bold for the main body text (30 pts) and Mija Regular for baselines (16.5 pts), with an interline spacing of 20 pts.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Zone de protection defined as 'x' and symbol height restricted to 5x. Align symbol to the left with partner name where feasible. Placement over .brussels should be avoided unless necessary for short names.
- Preferred spacing and alignment for single-line or two-line logo compositions. For two-line compositions, left alignment is the only authorized setting.

**Brand Voice:**

- Harmonization objectives: reinforce graphical coherence and underline regional affiliation of organizations using the branding guidelines.

**Notes:**

- Contact details for questions regarding application of guidelines include Ministry of the Region of Brussels-Capital, Direction of External Communication, Boulevard du Jardin Botanique 20, B-1035 Brussels, Belgium.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Reversed logo versions: Used on dark backgrounds as white or metallic; guidelines prohibit standard logo usage on dark backgrounds.
- Minimum size rule: Provide clear space equivalent to '1x the height of the nurse and child icon' around the logo.
- Logo reproduction in black & white restricted to contexts like forms, event advertisements, and business documents.
- Single-color logo use permitted, including knockout (white reversed) forms.
- Harvard Medical School Teaching Hospital logo must be scaled to half the size of the Boston Children’s logo.
- Incorrect usage: Avoid standard logo on dark backgrounds, ensure reversed versions are used for high contrast visibility.

**Color Information:**

- Pantone, RGB, HEX, and CMYK codes for core brand colors like Boston Blue (Pantone 287 C), Boston Ocean, and white are provided.
- Secondary accent colors with formulas: Boston Sky, Boston Pink, among others. Boston Red Alert reserved for concepts like stop or emergency.
- Core brand colors should dominate designs, and secondary colors can highlight subheads or accents.

**Typography Information:**

- Museo Sans typeface selected for brand communications. Weight usage: 500 for large headlines, 700 for smaller headlines, 300 for body copy.
- Use alternate typeface Arial when Museo Sans is unavailable; Arial Bold for headlines, Arial Regular for body copy.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style: Genuine, sensitive, unstaged. Reflect diversity of patients, families, and community. No stock images for hospital staff representation.
- Photos should have cooler tones, slightly desaturated look reflecting core brand colors; approved visual library strongly encouraged.
- Videography: Handheld, documentary style with natural light. Typography art cards use Museo Sans 700, Boston Ocean or white.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Brochure and collateral grids allow flexibility in photo and copy areas; subheads may be placed in margins or copy areas.

**Brand Voice:**

- Voice descriptors: Optimistic, confident, thoughtful; messaging balances hope and possibility with clarity.

**Notes:**

- Social media principles ensure posts align with brand guidelines; multiple platforms managed by Boston Children’s Marketing and Communications.
- Emails: Recommended format incorporates logo in upper left, balanced imagery and text, call-to-action linked to website.

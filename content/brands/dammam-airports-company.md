## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Our logo is composed of a symbol, logotype, and dual-language descriptor, representing connections across North, East, South, and West, resembling a propeller or compass.
- There are multiple versions of the logo for specific purposes, including primary and secondary logos, single-color and full-color versions, distinct light and dark backgrounds.
- Shorthand logo without descriptor for collateral and merchandise use, minimum size rules at 20mm.
- Guidelines emphasize always using master artwork to ensure consistent communication.
- Logo animations for dynamic branding across mediums.
- Incorrect usages like altering logo proportions or colors not specified are strictly prohibited.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors include Pantone 267 C (HEX 66318F), Pantone 2765 C (HEX 1C1550), Pantone Cool Gray 11 C (HEX 4D4D4D), and Pantone Black C (HEX 000000) with specified CMYK and RGB values.
- Secondary supporting colors, such as Pantone 3278 C (HEX 009877), Pantone 215 C (HEX b01657), Pantone 299 C (HEX 00a1df), and Pantone 2024 C (HEX fd6e39), allow differentiation with tints permissible at 60%, 40%, and 20%.
- Guidance provided for consistent CMYK, RGB, and HEX color usage tailored to print and on-screen applications.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: FS Albert, a sans-serif, friendly font available in various weights and styles. Headline and body font set in FS Albert Light; Arial is to be used for on-screen applications.
- Font purchase available through Fontsmith.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style characterized by crisp, desaturated tones, dynamic shots, and angles. Purple emphasis helps establish a branded, uniform aesthetic.
- Images within this document are illustrative and not for publication.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Charts and graphs integrated with DACO orange and blues for consistent style across print and online formats.
- Icons follow DACO color schemes and carry simplicity in line work for utility across mediums like websites and screens.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand voice conveys professionalism and insight in aviation with visual consistency and clarity of communication.

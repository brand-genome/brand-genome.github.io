## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The brand symbol of Portugal 2020 is a dynamic, engaging design based on the national flag with a modern, original style. The logotype uses the font 'Lucida Bright Demibold,' a serif typeface for optimal readability. The visual identity consists of two inseparable elements: the symbol and the logotype.
- Logo file formats are defined as JPG or PNG for screen display (e.g., web, email, and slides), while print materials should always use print-ready formats.
- Minimum size guidelines for the logo dictate a minimum height of 4.6mm to maintain formal characteristics and readability.
- Clear space around the logo is defined to ensure better visual representation and maintain brand identity.
- Restrictions on logo usage include not deforming the identity, not altering positioning or proportions, avoiding incorrect typefaces, and not using parts of the logo independently.

**Color Information:**

- Primary institutional colors: Green Pantone 7727C (HEX: #005f30, RGB: 0, 95, 48, CMYK: 100, 0, 90, 50) and Red Pantone 485C (HEX: #d21317, RGB: 210, 19, 23, CMYK: 9, 100, 100, 2). These colors must always maintain the specified values.
- Monochrome guidelines: High contrast in positive (50% Black for the logo) and high contrast in negative (100% Black), ensuring the logo remains distinguishable in grayscale applications.
- Guidelines for color backgrounds dictate that the logo must maintain maximum contrast with the background to ensure visibility.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family for brand identity: 'Lucida Bright Demibold' for the logotype. Complementary fonts include 'Lucida Bright Regular' and 'Lucida Sans Unicode' for additional communication elements.

**Imagery Photography:**

- No specific imagery or photography styles were mentioned; only certain guidelines on logo usage over photographic backgrounds — logos should maintain high contrast for visibility.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rules and minimum size constraints (minimum height: 4.6mm) are defined to ensure better visibility and brand consistency.

**Notes:**

- The guidelines also provide examples of brand applications across various mediums like merchandising (pens, bags, etc.) and digital assets (homepage, banners). Consistency in visual identity is emphasized across all platforms.
- The guidelines emphasize not distorting or modifying the brand identity globally, including improper usage such as incorrect tagline placement, resizing logos beyond specified dimensions, and altering typeface used.

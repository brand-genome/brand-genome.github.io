## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The primary logo version consists of an image (a windmill design) and corresponding text. Symbolizes dynamism and adaptability.
- Clear space around the logo must be maintained, with defined construction proportions and guidelines for spacing.
- Minimum size requirements are defined: For print: 30mm; For digital: 123px. Additional guidance for scaled-down versions.
- Variations of the logo for different backgrounds (corporate colors, negatives). Avoid using color backgrounds that compromise visibility.
- Prohibited uses include distortion, changing colors to non-institutional ones, and replacing typography. Incorrect usage examples provided.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: Blue and green with the following codes: Blue Pantone 2728C, CMYK: C093 M065 Y017 K002; Green Pantone 2421C, CMYK: C089 M016 Y100 K005.
- Rules for using logo on color backgrounds: Use contrasting text colors (white or black) on non-white backgrounds.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Optima. Elegant and legible sans-serif typeface designed by Hermann Zapf (1958), offering neutrality and adaptability.
- Secondary typeface: Helvetica LT Std. Provides a wide range of styles and weights for versatility across applications.
- Recommended font weights and sizes for business cards: Optima Normal 8/10 for name, Optima Italic 8/9 for position, Helvetica LT Std Italic 8/9 or Optima Nova LT Pro Light Condensed for contact information.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Defined grid and spatial rules for logo construction: area of protection must be maintained and clear of other graphical elements.

**Notes:**

- This brand manual serves as a guideline for constructing and maintaining the CRUP corporate identity consistently.
- For production, request original production files from the Press and Institutional Communication Department.
- Institutional materials include uses for merchandise, stationery (A4 specs: 210x297mm, institutional colors or greyscale), and business cards (90x50mm, matte paper, 260g).

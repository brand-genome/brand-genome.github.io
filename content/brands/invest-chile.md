## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Isologotype components include Isotype, Logotype, Tagline. The tagline must always accompany the isologotype, except in call-to-action scenarios.
- Minimum clear space is defined by the width and height of the largest star in the isologotype.
- Minimum size: The smallest version with tagline should be at least 2 cm in height. For a version without the tagline, the minimum permissible size is 1 cm.
- Color versions of the logo include: full-color, grayscale, plum variant, and monochrome. Colors are institutional to the brand.
- Correct logo applications on photographs: Photographs should be clean, light backgrounds close to white. For darker images, the plum version of the logo may be used, provided there's enough contrast.
- Incorrect logo applications include: using unapproved colors, incorrect coloring, outlines, adding elements, changing angles or proportions, removing stars, adding effects, background gradients, or busy photo backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors and codes include Pantone 632C (#0093B2, CMYK: 93%, 2%, 15%, 7%, RGB: 0, 147, 178) and Pantone Cool Gray 11C (#53565A, CMYK: 44%, 34%, 22%, 77%, RGB: 83, 86, 90). These define the institutional colors.
- Additional colors for key industry areas include Pantone 326C (#00B2A9), Pantone 7447C (#5D4777), Pantone 110C (#DAAA00), Pantone 7699C (#34657F), and Pantone 7416C (#E56A54), with corresponding CMYK and RGB values provided.
- Color differentiation is chosen within the same spectrum and saturation to reflect the brand identity as reliable and forward-looking.

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate font: Lato family. Variants include Light, Regular, Bold, and Heavy. Consistent use required in all brand communications.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography guidelines emphasize clean and white-dominant backgrounds. Photographs should showcase dynamic aspects of focused industries (e.g., tourism should display activity rather than static landscapes).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Primary layout requires a 35-part vertical division for calculating element positioning within formats. The base measure X divides the piece into equal sections.
- Secondary layout for horizontal formats uses an 18-part horizontal division where X is calculated accordingly.
- Graphic icons follow a grid system divisible into 100x100 squares, ensuring visual consistency with triangle-based patterns.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice aligns with a professional, strategic, and approachable executive aged 35-45. The tone is formal, informed, and credible, avoiding excessive jargon.
- Third-person singular is used to maintain objectivity and reinforce credibility of InvestChile.
- Metaphors are used sparingly to enhance communication without diminishing professionalism or clarity. Adjectives should avoid hyperbole and focus on credibility.

**Notes:**

- The isologotype and its components, placement, and usage examples are provided across guidelines to prevent distortion or misrepresentation.
- Co-branding restrictions: Partner logos should not surpass the height of the second-tallest star in the isologotype.

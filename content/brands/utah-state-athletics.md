## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The athletics mark must have proportionally weighted role and should be resized proportionally in larger publications. It must never be smaller than 5/8 inch tall for the vertical logo and 1/2 inch tall for the horizontal logo.
- Secondary athletic marks can only be used with the primary mark but not combined or placed close together. They can be used for appropriate print, web communications, and merchandise.
- Clear space rules for athletics logos dictate that area of isolation should be equal to the height of the 'S' in the Utah State wordmark.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Athletic Colors: Aggie Blue (PMS 539; CMYK 100-65-22-80; RGB 0-38-58; HEX #00263A), White, Light Gray (PMS Cool Gray 2; CMYK 5-3-5-11; RGB 208-208-206; HEX #D0D0CE), Gray Blue (PMS 430; CMYK 133-18-13-40; RGB 124-135-142; HEX #7C878E).
- Secondary Colors: Light Blue (PMS Process Blue; CMYK 100-13-1-2; RGB 0-133-202; HEX #0085CA), Copper (PMS 729; CMYK 26-50-76-7; RGB 181-129-80; HEX #B58150), Blue Gray (PMS 432; CMYK 65-43-26-78; RGB 51-63-72; HEX #333F48), Silver (PMS 877; CMYK 45-34-34-0; RGB 138-141-143; HEX #8A8D8F).
- Approved color variations for athletic logos and wordmarks must use the designated primary and secondary color palette; alternate colors for special promotions require coordination with the Trademark Licensing office.

**Typography Information:**

- Univers font family is required for all brand communications including condensed and non-condensed variations. Usage of two contrasting fonts is encouraged for publication consistency.
- A custom athletics typeface 'Utah State Bold' has been designed with distinctive bold and readily identifiable features. This font must not be used for large bodies of text and is limited to short statements.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Area of isolation rules specify the space around logos must remain clear of competing text and visual elements, with specific measurements based on the size of logo features like the 'S' or 'head' dimensions.

**Notes:**

- Guidelines emphasize consistent use and high-quality reproduction of logos on branded goods, ensuring positive associations with Utah State University's name and identity.
- Trademark licensing policies require pre-approval for designs appearing on branded merchandise, adherence to color palette, and restriction of logo placement on red products.

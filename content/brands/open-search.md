## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The OpenSearch mark consists of negative spaces of 'O' and 'S', forming geometric shapes visually centered. It can be used alone or alongside the logotype. The mark is intended to convey a sense of open playfulness.
- Mark use guidelines include using the full-color mark on a white background or over solid 'San Francisco Fog' or 'Pacific Sky'. Minimum size: Online: 16x16 pixels; Print: 0.25x0.25 inches. Clear space: 0.25x height of the mark from surrounding elements.
- The primary logo includes the OpenSearch mark locked up with the logotype. Use the full-color logo on a white background or 'San Francisco Fog' or 'Pacific Sky'. Separation of mark and logotype: 3/16x; Alignment: logotype optically centered with the mark (baseline and cap-height used for measuring).
- Monochrome logo allowed as an alternative. Minimum sizes: Online: 92.88 pixels wide; Print: 1.4519 inches wide.
- Do's and don'ts for logo use include avoiding distortion, effects, space adjustments, rotation, transparency changes, rearranged colors, re-creation with other typefaces, or logo alterations. Use light monochrome logo over images; maintain contrast and legibility.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette inspired by the West Coast skyline: Pacific Blue (HEX: #005EB8), Open Sky (HEX: #00A3E0), Deep Blue Sea (HEX: #003B5C), Pacific Sky (HEX: #B9D9EB). Pantone codes and RGB/CMYK details are included.
- Secondary color palette includes complementary colors: Purple Sage (HEX: #963CBD), Golden Poppy (HEX: #FFB81C), Midnight Sky (HEX: #002A3A), Malibu Sunrise (HEX: #F65275), Seafoam Mint (HEX: #2CD5C4), San Francisco Fog (HEX: #D9E1E2). Highlights accessibility and WCAG compliance.
- Tints and shades of the primary and secondary palettes can be created by mixing base color with white or black.

**Typography Information:**

- The primary brand typeface is 'Open Sans'. Font families include regular, light, bold, extra-bold, and italic variations. Optimized for print, web, mobile interfaces with excellent legibility. Recommended web adjustments: 0px letter-spacing initially.
- The secondary typeface is 'Noto', used for longform text ('Noto Serif Regular') and multilingual needs ('Noto Sans'). Font families include regular, bold, and italic variations. It emphasizes removing glyph rendering issues ('tofu').

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space around the logo must be 0.25x the height of the logo. Separation between logotype and mark: 3/16x.

**Notes:**

- The guidelines explicitly allow brand flexibility for merchandising, permitting monochrome marks/logos in any approved primary or secondary color, or white logos over solid backgrounds of primary/secondary colors.

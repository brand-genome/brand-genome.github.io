**Logo Information:**

- Primary athletic marks including primary logo, school wordmark, mascot wordmark, department logos, and secondary logos with restrictions and examples for use.
- Primary logo restrictions applied: do not alter strokes, distort, stretch, fade, or use text over these marks; rotation guidelines are defined.
- Wordmark restrictions include no straight form, no gradients, no excessive letter spacing, no small caps mixed with large caps, and others.
- Stripe restrictions include proper breaks, consistent scaling, alignment rules, and avoidance of overlapping with text or logos.

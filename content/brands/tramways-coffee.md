## Brand Guidelines

### 2022

**Logo Information:**

- Sans serif, geometric wordmark kerned to support illustrations wrapping around characters. Text is kept left-aligned to match illustrations and applications.
- Clear space is defined using the letter 'O' as a measurement guide ('x'). x dictates the minimum space required from all sides.
- Two logo types: Wordmark (usage: 3.5 x 1 in for Print, 300 px for Web) and Symbol (usage: below 1/2 in for Print, 100-200 px for Web).
- Guidelines for successful logo usage include maintaining the recommended logo designs.
- Avoid unsuccessful logo usages such as: changing the color, orientation, adding stroke, tweaking, or distorting.

**Color Information:**

- Primary palette comprises colors for packaging origins, blends, and flavor identification:
- Flavor HEX: FED925 (RGB: 254, 217, 37; CMYK: 2, 12, 94, 0)
- HEX: 6DBF54 (RGB: 109, 191, 84; CMYK: 61, 0, 90, 0)
- HEX: F27C9E (RGB: 242, 124, 158; CMYK: 0, 65, 14, 0)
- HEX: 45C4D8 (RGB: 69, 196, 216; CMYK: 63, 0, 15, 0)
- HEX: FFDA00 (RGB: 255, 218, 0; CMYK: 2, 11, 100, 0)
- HEX: CCA1DD (RGB: 204, 161, 221; CMYK: 20, 39, 0, 0)
- HEX: 000000 (RGB: 0, 0, 0; CMYK: 91, 79, 62, 97)
- HEX: F5F4F2 (RGB: 245, 244, 242; CMYK: 5, 4, 6, 0)
- HEX: F47C46 (RGB: 244, 124, 70; CMYK: 0, 64, 79, 0)
- HEX: 3CB4E5 (RGB: 60, 180, 229; CMYK: 65, 10, 1, 0)
Pantone references: PANTONE 298 C and PANTONE 115 C.
- Color pairing rules include:
- Cool colors paired with warm bases.
- Warm colors paired with cool bases.
- Warm colors applied on cool packaging and vice versa to enhance visual hierarchy and differentiation for coffee types.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography hierarchy defined using:
- Logotype: All Round Gothic.
- Stickers: Basic Gothic Pro (Uppercase: Bold for flavors, Lowercase: Medium for body text).
- Typeface usage involves bold settings, intertwining with illustrations wrapping around characters to ensure visual harmony.
- Medium and regular weights for Basic Gothic Pro used on stickers ensure legibility based on sticker sizes.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Illustrations represent Tramways' whimsical attributes and visual ideologies including thin black-and-white lines, color blocking aligned with packaging colors, and thematic elements reflecting coffee centralities.
- Stickers describe roast profile, estate origins, elevation, lot numbers, roast dates, and flavors for consumer knowledge.
- Flavors represented in packaging stem from the coffee flavor wheel branching into floral, spicy, earthy, citrus, fruity, bitter, nutty, chocolatey, and sweet profiles.
- Star-shaped stickers denote brewing methods to enhance front-of-pack visibility.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo spacing utilizes the letter 'O' as a guide ('x') for determining minimum spacing from all sides.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone characterized by exploration and discovery:
- Traits: Original, risk-taking, unique.
- Drawback: Unnecessary/impractical risks.
- Encourages consumers to try new things boldly.
- Tagline reflected in a cyclic phrase 'Pause to brew. Brew to pause', embodying the essence of Tramways Coffee.

**Notes:**

- Packaging adapts for different weights (150g, 250g, 1kg) scaled proportionately.
- Stationery includes founders' visiting cards, reflecting their favorite brews via icons and personalized illustrations.

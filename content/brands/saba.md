## Brand Guidelines

### 2011

**Logo Information:**

- The corporate logo represents the unified identity of the Saba brand and includes both the symbol and logotype.
- A safe zone has been established for the logo to ensure optimal application and avoid interference. This minimum distance is equivalent to the logo's symbol area. The minimum reproducing size of the logo is 10mm in width.
- Correct logo versions include: the full-color main version, a grayscale version, and their respective negative variations.
- Do's for logo usage: Ensure high visibility, legibility, and contrast. For application on non-corporate backgrounds or photographs, use the logo in white or black per background luminosity.
- Don'ts for logo usage: No modification of sizes or proportions. Avoid color application errors, deformation, concealment, incorrect spacing, and using improper typography with the logo.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand color: Pantone 3272 C, CMYK (C:99, M:0, Y:48, K:0), RGB (R:0, G:165, B:153), HEX (#00a599).
- Secondary brand color: Pantone Orange 021 C, CMYK (C:0, M:71, Y:100, K:0), RGB (R:255, G:88, B:0), HEX (#ff5800).
- Color usage rules: The primary corporate color must dominate. When Pantone use is unfeasible, the brand may be printed in CMYK or black.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary corporate typography: Pirenaica family in Light, Regular, Bold, and Small Caps.
- Secondary corporate typography: Calibri (Regular, Bold, Italic, Bold Italic); used when technical restrictions prevent the use of Pirenaica.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Safe zone rules require a minimum clear space around the logo equivalent to its symbol area.
- Logo visibility on backgrounds: When applied over non-corporate colors or photographs, the logo must appear in white or black depending on the background's luminosity.

**Notes:**

- The manual aims to ensure consistent and correct use of the Saba brand's visual identity elements to achieve recognition and reinforcement of the brand.
- The manual includes applications of the brand on corporate stationery, high-level stationery, corporate folders, fax sheets, internal communications, press notes, PowerPoint templates, and notepads.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2025

**Logo Information:**

- Clear space around the logo must be 0.25 of the Y-height (height of the Brandmark).
- Minimum size requirements for Landscape Logo: 24mm width for print, 160px width for digital.
- File formats: EPS, PDF for vector; JPEG, PNG, SVG for digital (RGB).
- Portrait Logo should be used predominantly in portrait applications. Minimum size: 15mm for print, 100px width for digital.
- Brandmark to be used when Landscape/Portrait Logos are illegible or space constraints are high. Minimum size: 8mm for print, 45px width for digital.
- Wordmark should be used on occasions with emphasis on name recognition. Minimum size: 15mm for print, 100px width for digital.
- Alternative (Extra Landscape) Logo to be used for extended horizontal applications (e.g., web, signage). Minimum size: 52mm for print, 346px width for digital.
- Do not manipulate logos, brandmark, or wordmark. No skewing, altering, compressing, resizing inconsistently, changing colors, fonts, placing on low-contrast backgrounds, or rearranging elements.
- Corporate stationery and legal documents should use the Landscape Logo placed in the top left-hand corner only.
- For sponsorships and partnerships, only Landscape or Portrait logos are permitted. Brandmark or Wordmark should not be used.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Colors: AA Grey (#7d878e), AA Green (#00a594), AA Yellow (#fac600), AA Orange (#d06516), AA Black (#000000), White (#ffffff), AA Blue Green (#192c34). Includes CMYK, Pantone, RGB, and HEX values.
- 'Bold and impactful' color usage is recommended. Core colors in the logo can be applied as solid backgrounds for dynamic and vibrant designs. Shades of white and AA Grey can be used for understated designs.
- Gradient color backgrounds should be avoided. Avoid white text on AA Yellow backgrounds. Use white text only on AA Orange and AA Green colors at 14pt font size or larger.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typefaces: Wondra Regular (logo font), Rustica Medium (titles/headings), Montserrat family (subheadings, body copy). Font weights: Bold, SemiBold, Regular. Usage roles and size specifications are provided for each font role in design applications.
- Email/internal communication typeface: System Typeface - Aptos (headings/subheadings: Bold and SemiBold; body copy: Regular).
- Detailed guidance on font sizes and formatting: Titles (Rustica Medium 29pt), Headings (Rustica Medium 14pt), Subheadings (Montserrat Bold 12pt), Body copy (Montserrat Regular 8pt).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery should feature 'real-life' vibrant and inspirational action pictures, inclusive of diverse sports, ages, ethnicities, genders, and abilities. Images should convey warmth and genuineness without looking staged or cliched.
- Ensure images are color-corrected for contrast, presenting sharp and crisp photos in high resolution.
- Avoid images without a clear focal point, low-quality, or staged photography.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Graphic devices: Brandmark and elements like Wave and Thick/Thin Bands are designed to add visual interest. Specific guidance includes scaling, cropping, recoloring methods, and alignment with other elements.
- Charts and infographics should maintain simplicity, use brand colors (AA Blue Green, AA Grey, White, and tints), and ensure high contrast for clarity.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand tone should be vibrant, inclusive, warm, welcoming, and genuine, reflecting the energy and engagement in athletics.

**Notes:**

- Use of sponsorship and partnership logos should feature a dividing line in AA Grey with proportions matching the Australian Athletics logo. Approvals must be obtained from the Marketing & Communication team.
- Brandmark or wave graphic elements must not be skewed, compressed, distorted, or recolored improperly outside the outlined methods.

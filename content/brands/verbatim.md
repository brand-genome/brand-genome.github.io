## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The Verbatim logo with the tagline 'Technology You Can Trust' is the preferred logo. It can also be used without the tagline for packaging if the tagline font is too small or if 'Technology You Can Trust' is used as a headline.
- The Verbatim logo can also be used in black or white on a colored background. The trademark (TM) symbol is now preferred as the standard logo on all branding instead of the registered (®) symbol.
- A minimum clearance zone must surround the Verbatim mark, ensuring clarity and preventing overcrowding. The minimum size for the Verbatim logo is 15mm, and the trademark symbol should be removed at such sizes.
- The 'Perfect Fit Notch Graphic' is a holding device used with the Verbatim logo. This graphical element can be placed on blue, white, or gray backgrounds and positioned at the top or bottom depending on design requirements.
- Verbatim's logo misuse guidelines prohibit altering logos in any way, including adding graphic elements, incorrect color combinations, distortion, using on busy backgrounds, incorrect element orders, and incomplete versions.
- Freecom brand logos have a standard clearance zone to ensure clarity. The logo must not be less than 35mm wide, and the payoff line should not be used below this size.
- Alternative Freecom logos are available in black or white on colored backgrounds. Clearance zones and sizing rules for Freecom logo usage follow the same principles as the Verbatim brand.

**Color Information:**

- The primary Verbatim corporate color is Pantone 072 blue (brand or primary color). Red (Pantone 485) is part of the logo and used sparingly as an accent. Black and white are key parts of the palette.
- The Verbatim color palette includes secondary and tertiary colors. Selected Secondary colors: Pantone 328 (RGB: R0 G113 B101, CMYK: C100 M0 Y50 K30, HEX #007165), and Pantone 124 (RGB: R236 G172 B0, CMYK: C0 M35 Y100 K0, HEX #ECAC00).
- Additional Verbatim secondary and tertiary colors include Pantone 363 (RGB: R57 G137 B47, CMYK: C80 M0 Y100 K20, HEX #39892F), Pantone 1525 (RGB: R201 G77 B0, CMYK: C0 M80 Y100 K10, HEX #C94D00), and Pantone 307 (RGB: R0 G114 B177, CMYK: C100 M20 Y0 K20, HEX #0072B1).
- Freecom brand colors consist of an 85% cyan shade for primary use. There is also a black tonal variation at CMYK Black (15% cyan, key level 60%).

**Typography Information:**

- Verbatim uses Helvetica Neue LT Std for corporate materials. For single-branded materials, this is consistent across business cards, letterheads, literature, and adverts.
- Freecom uses Myriad Pro for all corporate materials and marketing (business cards, literature, and exhibitions).
- For Chinese and Korean Typography: Verbatim uses MHeiHK for Traditional Chinese, MHeiHKS for Simplified Chinese, and Yoongothic320 for Korean.
- For Freecom: DF Hei W5/W9 is used for Traditional Chinese, and Adobe Hei Std is used for Simplified Chinese (bold requires a stroke addition of 0.1).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clearance zones for the Verbatim logo are mandatory, ensuring the logo does not appear crowded with other design elements. The zone still applies with the 'Perfect Fit Notch Graphic'.
- For Freecom logos, a strict clearance zone standard is maintained, ensuring clarity and proportional spacing.

**Notes:**

- Clear guidance is provided on the appropriate use of alternative logos, including black/white versions, and correct logo usage practices for both Verbatim and Freecom brands.
- Verbatim's tertiary color palette is described as complementary to both primary and secondary branding schemes and ensures flexibility in visual communication.

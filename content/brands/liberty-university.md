## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Liberty wordmark available in stacked and horizontal formats. Minimum size is 1.5 inches wide.
- Departmental wordmarks include five unique variations for flexibility.
- Official Liberty University seal reserved for use by the President and Provost; adjusted visually for small sizes.
- Academic Seal used by departments under the Office of Provost; incorporates 'Liberty University.'
- LU Monogram designed for merchandise; consists of an 'L' and 'U' with unique shadow accents.
- Athletics logo combines LU monogram and an eagle head. Alteration (e.g., proportions, colors) not permitted.
- Examples of incorrect logo usage, such as stretching, tilting, color changes, manipulating positions, or cropping.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: Liberty Red (PMS 187, HEX #990000, RGB 153,0,0) and Liberty Blue (PMS 282, HEX #0A254E, RGB 10,37,78). Secondary colors: Light Blue (PMS 283), Charcoal Gray (PMS Cool Gray 11), Athletics Gray (PMS Cool Gray 4).

**Typography Information:**

- Official typefaces: Adobe Garamond Pro (Regular, Italic, Bold), Trajan Pro 3, Gotham (Light, Black, etc.).
- Athletics typography includes Champion Sans and Champion Serif, exclusive for NCAA and Club Sports materials.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography must represent students, campus life, events, seasons, etc., ensuring diversity and adherence to the university's dress code.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum size requirements specified for logos and seals to maintain visual integrity, e.g., 1.5 inches wide for the wordmark.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice emphasizes Liberty's mission of 'Training Champions for Christ' and its academic excellence.

**Notes:**

- Observations emphasize proper branding through official stationery, merchandise approvals, and vendor licensing requirements.

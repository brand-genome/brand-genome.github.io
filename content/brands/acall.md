## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- ACALL logo usage restricted to cases supporting ACALL, its services, or in compliance with its guidelines. All rights, including copyright and trademark, belong to ACALL, Inc.
- ACALL corporate logo represents ACALL, Inc. and cannot be used to represent its services.
- The 'WorkstyleOS' logo represents the WorkstyleOS service provided by ACALL, Inc. The logo includes a symbol mark and logotype, available in horizontal and vertical arrangements. The symbol mark may be used independently when appropriate.

**Color Information:**

- Main color logos are the primary recommendation; negative color logos should be used for visibility on specific backgrounds.
- ACALL BLACK: CMYK (95, 18, 0, 0), RGB (34, 23, 20), HEX #221714.
- ACALL WHITE: CMYK (0, 0, 0, 0), RGB (255, 255, 255), HEX #FFFFFF.

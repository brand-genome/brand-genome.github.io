## Brand Guidelines

### 2024

**Logo Information:**

- SKAKO Corporate Identity specifies placement, variations, and safe space for the logo, ensuring strategic alignment with branding principles.
- Color variations for the logo include full color, black, white, and Mango-specific combinations for advertising purposes.
- Minimum logo size is set to 7 mm height for key branding visuals, with smaller allowances (2.5 mm) in secondary contexts.
- Clear space around the logo must be at least equal to the height of the Logo-A to maintain branding consistency and visibility.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: Mango (#FFC222, CMYK: 0-24-87-0, Pantone 123C), Gunpowder Blue (#374B5A, CMYK: 39-17-0-65). Secondary colors include Platinum (#EBEBEB) and Squirrel Grey (#7A888E). White and Black are used prominently for text and background.
- Tertiary colors are provided for supplementary branding such as graphs, with defined HEX, CMYK, and Pantone codes; examples include Cardinal (#C5283D, CMYK: 16-95-69-5), Cadet Blue (#37A1AD, CMYK: 73-16-32-1), among others.

**Typography Information:**

- The recommended typeface for SKAKO branding is set as Arial and Helvetica fonts, utilizing specific weights: Regular for paragraph text and Bold for headlines.
- Color usage in text (Gunpowder Blue or White on darker backgrounds) is prescribed alongside font roles and limitations for size and visibility.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography styles for SKAKO include full color, grayscale, and Gunpowder blue overlay applications for brand-specific image treatments.
- Images treated with Gunpowder Blue overlays must adhere to opacity guidelines ensuring branding consistency across visuals.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear spacing around the logo is defined based on the proportional Logo-A height, ensuring consistent placement across mediums.
- The A-mark component, derived from the logo, may be used to support branding through overlays, patterns, or watermarks.
- Pattern elements using the A-mark are intended solely for large-format media like posters or roll-ups with Gunpowder Blue backgrounds.

**Brand Voice:**

- SKAKO brand voice emphasizes innovation, engineering, and clear communication through consistent use of visual elements such as logos, colors, and typography.
- The updated visual identity underscores SKAKO’s position as a vital and innovative company in manufacturing industries.

**Notes:**

- Minimum logo height defined as 7 mm for front pages and adjusted to 2.5 mm for less prominent placements such as footnotes.
- Paper specifications for SKAKO branding materials include coated surfaces (minimum 170 gsm) treated with matte finishes for durability and cleanliness.

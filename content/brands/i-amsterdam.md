## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The I amsterdam motto is used on a red label (black-and-white), white label (on red backgrounds), and gray label for grayscale advertisements. Label sets are available in EPS, JPG, and GIF formats. Proper placement requires careful alignment, and 1/4 height of the label must be left free around the label for placement.
- Color variations include:
- Red Label: PMS 2035 C, RGB (255, 0, 0), CMYK (0, 100, 100, 0), RAL 3020
- White Label: RGB (255, 255, 255), CMYK (0, 0, 0, 0), RAL 9003
- Gray Label: PMS Cool Gray 8, RGB (156, 156, 156), CMYK (0, 0, 0, 50)
- Incorrect usage rules include:
1. Do not place the label at the bottom of the page.
2. The label must remain distinct from sponsor logos and cannot visually form a whole with any other elements.

**Color Information:**

- Core colors for labels:
- Red Label: PMS 2035 C, RGB (255, 0, 0), CMYK (0, 100, 100, 0), RAL 3020
- White Label: RGB (255, 255, 255), CMYK (0, 0, 0, 0), RAL 9003
- Gray Label: PMS Cool Gray 8, RGB (156, 156, 156), CMYK (0, 0, 0, 50)
- Contrast must ensure labels remain legible in all environments. Examples for grayscale use emphasize high readability.

**Typography Information:**

- The main font family is Avenir, with weights including Light, Book, Roman, Medium, Heavy, and Black. Italics are reserved for emphasis and sparing use.
- Avenir is part of the Linotype Library and Adobe Font Folio. It is widely available through common retail channels.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Online use of I amsterdam relies on appropriate proportional placement to areas of visual emphasis, with backgrounds integrating photography, illustrations, or motion.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Label placement rules:
1. Never at the bottom of a page.
2. A minimum of 1/4 label height is required as clear space around the label to avoid overlap with text/images.
- Label sizing formula:
- Ratio defined by the short-to-long side.
- Minimum proportions range from 30% (1-1.99 ratio) to a maximum of 150% (>7.5 ratio) of the short side.

**Brand Voice:**

- The I amsterdam brand focuses on core values: Creativity (original & surprising), Innovation (trend-setting & innovative), and Entrepreneurship (active & enterprising). All messaging emphasizes diversity, individuality, and connectedness.

**Notes:**

- All use of the I amsterdam slogan or labels needs prior approval from amsterdam&partners. Contact brand@iamsterdam.com for approval, with a 3-day review period.

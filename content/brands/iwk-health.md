## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The logo comprises a wordmark and a logomark, designed to capture optimism and balance friendliness with professionalism.
- Clear space around the logo is defined by 'H' character in the logo and is the minimum required safe zone.
- Minimum size requirements for logo usage: 0.75 inches for print and 64 pixels for digital screens.
- Incorrect logo usage examples include stretching, changing colors, adding shadows, and other distortive applications.
- Examples of department/service lockups showcase how sub-brands are visually integrated, accommodating various title lengths.
- Guidelines for co-branded partnership lockups ensure balanced spacing and the use of baselines or weighted graphics for alignment.
- The wordmark can be used in special scenarios where reproduction requirements demand simplicity.

**Color Information:**

- Primary palette includes HEX #104C98 (PMS 2945 C, RGB 16 76 152, CMYK 100 80 7 0), #B31E8C (PMS 2405 C, RGB 179 30 140, CMYK 30 100 2 0), and white.
- Supporting palette includes HEX #1F2944 (PMS 533 C, RGB 31 41 68, CMYK 91 82 45 47), #5B6771 (PMS 431 C, RGB 91 103 113, CMYK 67 52 44 16), and white for balance and sophistication.
- Extended color palette designed for accents and highlights includes additional colors with HEX codes #CF152D (PMS 186 C), #6A1F74 (PMS 2613 C), and #3AAE2A (PMS 361 C) among others.
- Grayscale and alternative shades include HEX #5B6771 (PMS 431 C, 80% Black) and other tones derived from primary grays for use in neutral settings.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: Fira Sans, chosen for its humanist structural qualities and functional versatility. Available in Light, Regular, Medium, Bold, and Black weights and italics.
- Default alternatives: Open Sans and Arial (web or emails) when Fira Sans is unavailable.
- Accent font: Literata Book, used for logotypes and occasionally for pull quotes or titles for modernity and sophistication.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style should feel natural, active, and avoid overly posed or rigid compositions. Stock images should align with the organization's identity.
- Relevant photography should support core messaging and use appropriate cropping for storytelling.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space around the logo maintains clarity and recognition. It reduces visual tension and is essential for proper emphasis.

**Brand Voice:**

- The organization expresses optimism and professionalism, grounded in compassionate health care values.

**Notes:**

- The updated logo removes 'Centre' to reflect the organization’s broader reach while maintaining its emotional and professional brand equity.
- Illustrations should maintain a friendly, accessible, and contemporary design while avoiding stereotypes. Simplicity is key for comprehension.
- Icons should be universally understood and primarily bound in circles or square containers to maintain uniformity. Labels help contextualize meaning.

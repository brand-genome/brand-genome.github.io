## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The ECAM logo includes versions with and without legends. The legend version includes the entity's full name. Digital originals must be used for reproduction to ensure proper identification and communication.
- Clear space/safe zone rules specify a minimum area of two times the width of the 'E' character around the logo where no other graphic elements or folds should occur.
- Minimum size rules for the ECAM logo specify 20 mm for print reproduction and 80 pixels for digital use.
- Additional minimum reproduction sizes for other dispositions are represented: 30 mm for print and 180 pixels for digital use.

**Color Information:**

- The corporate blue color specifications: Pantone 5423 C, CMYK: C:54 M:32 Y:26 K:0, RGB: R:126 G:153 B:170, HEX: #7E99AA.
- The corporate black color specifications: CMYK: C:50 M:20 Y:20 K:100, RGB: R:0 G:0 B:0, HEX: #000000.
- On non-corporate colored backgrounds and color images, the black logo is used for light-colored backgrounds, and the white logo is used for dark backgrounds for clarity.
- On a black-and-white image, the logo can be used in its corporate color, white, or black, ensuring good contrast with the background.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The logo must have a minimum safety area equivalent to double the width of the 'E' character around it. No other elements, graphics, or folds should interfere within this area.

**Notes:**

- Digital versions of the logo must always be used for any reproduction to maintain integrity and brand identity consistency.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The logo rationale defines the Airport Authority logo as being used for corporate communications like corporate ads and stationery. The design contains a dark purple symbol representing a plane taking off, referred to as 'the dart,' and negative (white) space forming the silhouette of the Peace Tower.
- Preferred logos provided in color options: YOW Purple, Black, and Reverse/White (for dark backgrounds). This is the primary logo.
- The stand-alone icon logo can be used in contexts where the full name of the organization is already known, including certain promotional material and web assets. Color options include YOW Purple, Black, and Reverse/White.
- Stacked logo provided as the secondary horizontal logo option, only to be used in constrained spaces. Color options: YOW Purple, Black, and Reverse/White.
- Incorrect logo treatments prohibited include: usage of the graphic by itself, distorting the logo, changing the font, rearranging logo elements, altering sizes, changing color standards, and using the logo in one language only. Examples of incorrect treatments are provided.
- Logo's use on different backgrounds: Black should be used on light backgrounds, and White should be used on dark backgrounds. Legibility must always be ensured.
- Minimum width requirements for the Airport Authority logos are specified:
- Preferred logo: At least 1.5 inches (print) and 172px (web).
- Stacked logo: At least 1.2 inches (print) and 153px (web).
- Icon-only logo: At least 0.5 inches (print) and 57px (web).
- The exclusion zone around the logo should always be the height of the 'O' in 'Ottawa' to ensure integrity. Preferred and minimum spacing is demonstrated.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors:
- YOW Purple: Pantone 268 CVC, CMYK C:91 M:94 Y:0 K:23, RGB R:52 G:42 B:97.
- Black: CMYK C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:100, RGB R:35 G:31 B:32. Tints (60%) can be used for design materials, but tinted logos are not allowed.
- Secondary colors:
- YOW Orange: Pantone 1385C, CMYK C:13 M:51 Y:100 K:2, RGB R:217 G:121 B:42.
- YOW Navy: Pantone 2965C, CMYK C:96 M:86 Y:42 K:42, RGB R:0 G:37 B:69.
- YOW Sky Blue: Pantone 2190C, CMYK C:69 M:25 Y:0 K:0, RGB R:43 G:172 B:228. These are supplementary colors and should not be used for logo variations.

**Typography Information:**

- The corporate font is Arial, available on Mac and PC. Weight variations include Regular, Italic, Bold, and Bold Italic. Arial must be used for all media, presentations, documents, signage, advertisements, web, and print.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Images with the Airport Authority logo must always ensure legibility by using black for light backgrounds and white for dark backgrounds. The logo should be contrasted with its background for visibility.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The exclusion zone around the logo should be proportionate to the height of the 'O' in 'Ottawa.' The exclusion zone must be maintained to ensure the logo remains visually distinct.

**Notes:**

- The manual is version 1.1, dated 2017, and outlines guidelines for maintaining brand consistency for the Ottawa International Airport Authority. It applies to employees and suppliers designing internal or external communications, advertisements, visual materials, and more. Questions can be directed to the Vice President of Communications & Public Affairs, Krista Kealey (krista.kealey@yow.ca, Tel: 613-248-2099).
- All logo variations and formats are available for download via the Communications & Public Affairs section of the Airport Authority website.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Logo backgrounds include 'Marine on white', 'White on Marine', 'Obsidian on white', 'White on Obsidian', and photo watermarks in white.
- Logo requires sufficient white space around it. The 'u' in the logo acts as the measurement guideline for white space.
- The logo can use just the icon (beeldmerk) on dark backgrounds, watermarks, and other formats. White space around the beeldmerk must equal 1/3rd of its size.
- Incorrect usage examples include changing background colors (non-brand colors), using gradients, visual effects, or inappropriate sizes.
- Minimum size for the logo and beeldmerk is 1cm in height for print and 24px for digital use.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: Marine (#19AAFF). Secondary colors: Deep Marine (#084993) and Ruby (#F91B56). Tint variations allowed for 33%, 66%, and 100%.
- Supporting colors include Obsidian (#021C3A), Stone (#A0AFBC), Silver (#758496), Steel (#4C5A6B), and Snow (#FFFFFF). Tint variations allowed for 33%, 66%, and 100%.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography includes Cairo for headings (fixed weight) and Nunito Sans for body (all weights except italics).
- Font specifications for digital: Cairo and Nunito Sans with defined sizes, line spacing, and color combinations.
- Font specifications for print range from Cairo (headings) and Nunito Sans (body text). Sizes and line spacing vary for print use cases.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery must have a message and support content. Avoid abstract, unrealistic, or inappropriate stock images. Allowed: mock-ups and realistic imagery.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The branding element 'De Hoek' occupies 95% of a space on covers, with 2.5% remaining space for white edges. The rounded corner occupies 20% width.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice is informal yet professional, problem-solving, and advisory. Customers are treated as part of the team, maintaining flexibility.

**Notes:**

- Social media posts use fixed templates and logo elements for consistency. All titles and colors are adjustable, ensuring brand recognition.
- Mock-up guidelines for showcasing web and app designs include photorealism (devices like iPhone, MacBook) rather than screenshots.

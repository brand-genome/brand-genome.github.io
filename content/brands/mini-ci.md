## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- 2-D logo: Used only for OOH and certain print/gear applications. 3-D logo: Used for all other communications. Clear space must equal half the height of the wings. The logo must always be 3-D, chrome, and black. Only 1 logo per ad is allowed. Logos must not appear in body copy or substitute the wordmark.
- Positions for MINI wordmark: It must always be in ALL CAPS. MINI is never italicized or hyphenated, and no other logotypes or symbols are allowed. The wordmark should align with the top of the MINI logo wings to the top of the headline.

**Color Information:**

- Headlines and short leads may appear in color. Body copy always appears in black on white or white on black. Colors must complement the design.
- Primary MINI Colors include PMS 106 C, 368 C, 2925 C, 1797 C, 1375 C, 103 C, and more. Each color is expressed in terms of CMYK formulations for print outputs (e.g., PMS 106 C: C = 0, M = 0, Y = 85, K = 0).

**Typography Information:**

- Custom-made MINI Type font is highly irregular. Roles: MINI Type Bold for headlines/titles; MINI Type Regular for body texts, captions, margin notes, or tables. Special attention must be paid to kerning.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Images should be high-contrast and energetic, with surprising or cheeky tones (e.g., urban visuals). Frame colors must emphasize prominent colors in an image or align with car design colors. White or gray frames are permitted for multi-color artwork.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space around the MINI symbol must equal the height of the wings ( 'x' measurement). Symbol positioning must adhere to media-specific rules.

**Notes:**

- MINI's brand identity emphasizes modern and energetic tones. Key considerations include appropriate clear-space handling, typography consistency, and frame alignment with images.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The Belgard logo is bold, simple, and rooted in the heritage of the brand's products, yet modern for a contemporary audience. Symbolically, it combines hardscape and outdoor living.
- Logo color options include full color (primary), black, and white versions to suit different applications and backgrounds.
- Maintain a clear space around the logo equivalent to the height of the 'B' in the logo.
- Incorrect logo usage examples include distortion, recoloring, adding effects, or placing on busy/low-contrast backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- The color palette is sophisticated and inspired by natural outdoor hues, conveying a premium and aspirational feel. Print design should use PMS colors, while CMYK is a fallback.
- Primary colors include:
- PMS 297 (Light Blue)
- PMS Black 7 (Deep Gray)
- PMS 7497 (Forest Green).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Cargan, a transitional serif typeface for headlines, body text, and layout details. Its geometric style is approachable and complements the Belgard logo.
- Secondary typeface: Arial, used primarily for internal documents or digital applications when Cargan is unavailable.

**Brand Voice:**

- The Belgard brand voice is aspirational, inspirational, and sophisticated, focusing on outdoor living and enriching everyday experiences. The tone reflects elegance and durability.

**Notes:**

- The guidelines are designed to ensure consistency and premium presentation of the Belgard brand across all media.

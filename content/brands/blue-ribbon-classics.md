## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Custom word mark housed in a ribbon with blue gradients, featuring a unique design where the letter 'i' in 'ribbon' contains an ice cream scooper, and the 'o' has a novelty shape to reflect quality and offerings.
- One-color brand marks are to be used only when limited printing capabilities are present or within the Blue Ribbon pattern. Use PMS 7462 for Blue Ribbon Blue or black-and-white options if color printing isn't available. For white brand marks, back the ribbon in Blue Ribbon Blue (PMS 7462) when possible.
- Clear space rule: The space around the logo must be at least equivalent to the width of the popsicle graphic in the 'o' of Ribbon on all sides. Minimum brand mark size is defined at 1.8 inches wide by 2.5 inches tall for printed applications.
- Do's and Don'ts: Do not rotate the logo, change the size of its elements, remove elements, change its colors, or skew the brand mark; these ensure consistent use and brand recognition.
- The 'no ribbon' version of the logo is to be used when the full brand mark cannot be accommodated, with dimensions of 1 inch by 1.3 inches as the minimum printed size.
- The one-color 'no ribbon' version of the logo should be used only with limited space and printing capabilities. Black-and-white options can be used if color printing is not available.

**Color Information:**

- Blue Ribbon Classics Blue - PANTONE 7462. It is highlighted as a core part of the brand identity.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space: Defined as the width of the popsicle in the 'o' of Ribbon surrounding the mark on all sides.

**Notes:**

- The brand mark integrates conceptual elements: the ice cream scooper (on the letter 'i'), a novelty shape (on the 'o'), and gradient blue ribbons for a sense of quality and offerings.
- Do's and Don'ts list clear visual misuses of the logo to avoid, ensuring brand integrity as a priority.

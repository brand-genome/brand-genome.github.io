## Brand Guidelines

### 2007

**Logo Information:**

- The five-point logo is presented in two configurations: Stacked (preferred) and Horizontal. Clear space around the logo protects the branding, and files are available for download in the MediaBin repository.
- Banding options are available for vertical/horizontal layouts when paired with the logo in a red square. Additional configurations include acceptable black-and-white or outline versions.

**Color Information:**

- New Balance corporate color palette includes PMS185 (Red) for CMYK, RGB, and HEX applications (#ED3237). Guidelines specify appropriate usage formats.

**Typography Information:**

- New Balance typography includes Avant Garde Gothic Bold for sports naming and ZIP branding explicitly styled with capitalization and italicized text where applicable.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography styles are divided into four categories: Achievement (emotional portraits), Performance (action and apparel), Product (catalog shots), and Environment (studio or situational). Strict guidelines govern lighting and focus.
- Rules highlight choosing angles that showcase the product's design while maintaining focus on the brand through clear visibility of the 'N' logo.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grid systems may be arranged vertically or horizontally and should integrate the brand while maintaining structured box alignment.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone reflects authenticity, performance-driven spirit, and honesty. It's centered on independence and nobility in action.

**Notes:**

- All brand materials recommend using uncoated stock containing 30% post-consumer waste.

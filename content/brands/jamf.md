## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Corporate logo includes both the icon and wordmark which should remain in a fixed relationship and not separated or altered. Separate use of the icon or wordmark is restricted to Jamf for approved purposes.
- Clear space around the Jamf logo must equal the height of the 'm' in the wordmark. This proportion should be maintained when scaling the logo.
- Logo variations: full color corporate logo, full color corporate knockout logo, one-color corporate logo, one-color corporate knockout logo.
- Stacked logo variations: full color stacked corporate logo, full color stacked corporate knockout logo, one-color stacked corporate logo, one-color stacked corporate knockout logo.
- Product and property logos have specific colors: HEX #778eb1 (PMS 7454C), HEX #ffffff (PMS White), HEX #bfbfbf (PMS 428C), HEX #929292 (PMS 429C). Full-color logos and full-color knockout logos are included.
- Logo treatment rules: do not add elements, place on complex images, place on patterns, rearrange elements, distort proportions, place within another shape, apply drop shadows or filters, rotate, or alter logo colors.
- Logo hierarchy: when multiple Jamf logos are present, the corporate logo should take precedence by removing the icon from subsequent logo instances.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo clear space rules specify a height equal to the 'm' in the Jamf wordmark. This should be applied proportionately.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand voice reflects the personality of the brand, aiming to be genuine and personable. Consistency in voice across customer touchpoints is emphasized.
- Brand attributes include being aligned with Apple, customer-first, having genuine humility, showing heart, and technical expertise.
- Brand traits: passionate (expressive, heartfelt, action-oriented), authentic (genuine, trustworthy, humble), spirited (engaging, playful, enthusiastic).

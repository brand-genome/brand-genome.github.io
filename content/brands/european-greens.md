## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The logo incorporates the image of a sunflower with twelve petals representing the EU flag, symbolizing commitment to European values.
- The logo comprises three elements: logo mark, logo type, and mandatory logo shape.
- Isolation area rules: The logo is surrounded by a minimum area based on green space derived from a lowercase character size.
- Application rules for green background: The logo can disappear into the background only when used for specific contexts.
- Misuse rules: No shadow, reflection, rotation, or aggressive contrast allowed. Maintain white or green color.
- Logo variations include a full logo and a version with just the logo mark without text.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Green (Pantone 376 C) and secondary colors are outlined but details not visible in extracted text.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography for headlines: Font family includes specific choices (unreadable details in text).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery emphasizes open and responsible care; specific style details described in text format.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing/layout grid ensuring logo alignment via isolation rules (see page 8).

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand voice aligns with openness, accountability, honesty, care, and responsibility. Emotionally positive with active tone.

**Notes:**

- Visual identity aims to unify online and offline communication for strong recognition, especially for elections.

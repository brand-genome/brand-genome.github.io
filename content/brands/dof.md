## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- DOF Group and DOF Brasil logos are officially used for brand representation since 2013. Norskan Offshore and DOF Subsea Brasil logos are discontinued. Logo variation rules specify usage in cases of square layout requirements.
- DOF logos cannot be adapted in colors and proportions. Official logos must always be used.
- It is mandatory to use one of the DOF logos in any promotional product layout.

**Color Information:**

- All colors used must follow the predefined DOF color scheme as shown. Promotional product examples also adhere strictly to DOF color guidelines.

**Typography Information:**

- Galaxie Polaris font family is used for profile typography. Styles include Heavy, Bold, Bold Italic, Medium, Book, Book Italic, Light, and Light Italic. This font is not serif, and hence not recommended for large text amounts.
- Chronicle Text G1 is used for extensive text and detailed typography, with weight variations such as Roman, Semibold, Italic, and Bold.
- Arial and Calisto MT are Windows System Fonts used for word templates but must never be used for advertising materials.

**Imagery Photography:**

- DOF photography emphasizes safety (use of PPE), teamwork (shooot in groups unless portrait), accessibility (smiling subjects), pioneering (emotion or technical expression), and strength (organized backgrounds). Images must portray professionalism and cleanliness.
- Vessels must portray activity and liveliness. Decks should be organized with minimal maintenance issues. Offices should be bright, representing dynamism.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Visual communication-based layouts for banners, posters, and stickers must conform to brand standards. Prior approval by the local Communications department is required.

**Brand Voice:**

- Social media guidelines dictate employees behave professionally when representing DOF on platforms. Posting company-specific content or edited materials without prior approval is prohibited.
- Tagline 'Safety, Teamwork, Accessibility, Pioneering Strength' is reinforced through imagery.

**Notes:**

- Employees should act professionally and refrain from sharing unauthorized company or operation materials on social media channels. LinkedIn is currently the only official social platform used by DOF Group.
- All materials are accessible on DOF Identity Portal. Employees have varying access levels depending on regional coverage.
- Promotional products with custom department or vessel names must maintain strict adherence to DOF brand standards and visual identity.
- Any communication alterations at offices or vessels must have written approval from communication departments.

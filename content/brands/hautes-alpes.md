- Protective zone around the logo extends to 2 H's from each side based on the 'Hautes-Alpes' logo.

**Brand Voice:**

- Communication strategy highlights richness and uniqueness of Hautes-Alpes, focusing on geographic placement and the differentiator aspects of the region.
- Global tagline emphasizes discovery and differentiation: 'You’ve never been this close...'highlighting encounter, discovery, and distinctiveness.

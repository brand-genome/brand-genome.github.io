## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Grade de Construção - Escudo de Armas do Amazonas (Vertical Logo): X = 1/4 of the crest's height; Margins and logo proportions are specified by fractions of X, such as 1/2X and 1/3X; Height of the word 'AMAZONAS' is defined to be 2X.
- Grade de Construção - Escudo de Armas do Amazonas (Horizontal Logo): Follows the same construction grid as the vertical logo; X = 1/4 of the crest's height; Proportions within logo are also 1/2X and 1/3X; The height of the word 'AMAZONAS' is 2X.
- Limites de Segurança (Vertical Logo): Safety zone defined around the logo; the limit is specified at 2X.
- Limites de Segurança (Horizontal Logo): Safety zone defined around the logo; the coverage should be 2X around the logo frame.

**Color Information:**

- Primary/branding colors are defined with their CMYK and RGB values: CMYK 80, 0, 90, 0; RGB 51, 162, 61.
- Secondary color 1: CMYK 100, 88, 36, 27; RGB 41, 50, 88.
- Secondary color 2: CMYK 0, 0, 0, 33; RGB 171, 171, 171.
- Accent color: CMYK 0, 0, 100, 0; RGB 255, 237, 0.
- For the web, apply the desired logo format at 72 DPI and in RGB color settings. For print applications, use CMYK color profiles.

**Notes:**

- Logo construction grids (Grade de Construção) are detailed for both vertical and horizontal arrangements. Safety zones around the logo are defined as a minimum of 2X, where X is 1/4 of the crest height.
- Specific color codes for primary and secondary branding colors are provided along with instructions for web (RGB, 72 DPI) and print (CMYK).

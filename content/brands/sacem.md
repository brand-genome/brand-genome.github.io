## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The logo is unique, symbolic of inclusiveness, solidarity, and engagement. The standard version is shown on a gradient institutional background, while the secondary version is used on a white background.
- The logo color includes Pantone 7426 C with defined CMYK and RGB values. Specific gradients are also listed for brand graphics.
- The logo includes a white reserve version (text evicted with a transparent effect), intended to be applied on gradient backgrounds.
- A black-and-white version of the logo is provided for exceptional cases, used only when standard or secondary colored versions are impossible.
- Applications for the logo on colored backgrounds, white backgrounds, and photographic backgrounds are described. The primary choice is a gradient institutional background, with fallback gradients optionally used for other contexts.
- Clear space rule: A protection zone equal to half the logo height must surround the logo. Minimum size: 13mm (print) or 100px (digital).
- Incorrect uses of the logo include changing colors, misplacing elements, adding elements, using inappropriate typography for the word 'Sacem,' modifying the cartouche, or placing the logo on non-readable backgrounds.
- The logo can be paired with a signature 'Ensemble, faisons vivre la musique.' Two versions exist — one with a 2-line signature for co-branding or space-constrained usage, and a 1-line version as the primary.
- Logo with signature applications follow the same background rules as the main logo: prioritize the institutional gradient background, with other gradients used minimally.
- Clear space rule (with signature): Additional spacing applies similarly to the primary logo. Minimum size: 13mm (print) or 100px (digital).
- Restrictions also apply to the logo with signature: no changing colors, repositioning, or using non-designated typography. Placement must always ensure high visibility.

**Color Information:**

- Pantone 7426 C is the primary brand color. CMYK: C 0, M 100, Y 45, K 12; RGB: R 188, G 32, B 75. Gradients based on cool and warm tones are specified.
- The institutional gradient is prioritized for use, with three additional gradients listed as optional complements.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: Galano Grotesque, available in 10 weights with italicized equivalents. This font is used across all print and digital assets, except for Microsoft Office documents, where Arial replaces it.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Logo imagery recommendations suggest prioritizing institutional gradients for branding, ensuring high contrast and readability.
- Logos can be used on photographic backgrounds if appropriate contrast and visibility are ensured.

**Spacing Layout:**

- A defined protection zone ensures clarity around the logo. The space equals 50% of the logo height ('X/2').

**Brand Voice:**

- The tagline 'Ensemble, faisons vivre la musique' accompanies the logo in specified cases. This reinforces the tone of partnership and celebration of music.

**Notes:**

- Subsidiary logos ('logotypes marques filles') are derived from the main logo or accompanying typography, and they follow the same usage rules as the principal logo.

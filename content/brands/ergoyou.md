## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- Logo inspired by the Vitruvian man for ergonomics, available in two forms: with and without the outer circle.
- Logo usage rules: On white, the logo with the outer circle is used; on black backgrounds, the logo without the outer circle is used. Original blue used on non-brand colors.
- 'Ergo' emphasized in the wordmark, distinct 'you' in regular weight. Requires ample space, aligned with Kade typeface.
- Minimum scale for web and print applications specified.
- Logo misuse guidelines: No colors outside brand colors; no effects, patterns, gradients; do not rotate, change orientation, or modify typeface.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color is Pantone Blue. Orange highlights key details, grey for backgrounds or text on blue. No orange fills; use strokes only. White and black allowed.
- Primary color is Pantone Blue. Orange highlights key details, grey for backgrounds or text on blue. No orange fills; use strokes only. White and black allowed.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typefaces: Kade (titles, product names, medium or regular weight, bold with tracking); Aileron (subtext, body copy, italics, all caps), spacing specifications provided for 50pt and 20pt sizes.
- Primary typefaces: Kade (titles, product names, medium or regular weight, bold with tracking); Aileron (subtext, body copy, italics, all caps), spacing specifications provided for 50pt and 20pt sizes.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Brand uses icons styled with an underlying grid; hints of orange highlight important features.
- Technical drawings use brand colors to highlight product features unique to Ergo You.
- Photography captures how products are used. Sparse backgrounds focus on the product, using natural light or realistic renders.
- Stickers are either on plain grey or transparent vinyl with key product details, company name, tagline, and icons.
- Stickers are either on plain grey or transparent vinyl with key product details, company name, tagline, and icons.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Underlying logo grid: Distance between icon and text equals the width of the letter 'r' and minimum distance around the logo.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tagline aligned to wordmark, used sparingly on brochures/print but avoided on small surfaces. Aligns with grids; uses Aileron Italic (regular weight) with a line under it.
- Social media focuses on consumer education with ergonomic insights. Combines graphics and photographs for impact.

**Notes:**

- Emphasis on digital brand presence through Google Ads using soft graphics mixed with educational content.

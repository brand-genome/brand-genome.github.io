## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The institutional symbol (simbol) is defined by an escut (shield) in a red square with rounded corners. Construction proportions are shown explicitly and are fixed to maintain consistency.
- The red corporate color cited for the symbol is Pantone 186.
- Black-and-white (single-tone) versions of the symbol can be used when color is unavailable. They include a positive (black on white or light background) and negative (white on dark background) variant.
- The logotype (Logotip) of the Ajuntament de Barcelona (City Council) uses the Akkurat Bold font, in title case, and is always accompanied by the symbol.
- The marca (overall municipal logo or brand) is a combination of the symbol and logotype. Clear construction guidelines are detailed for alignment and proper usage.
- Color, single-tone (black), and negative-tone (white for dark backgrounds) versions of the marca are specified for different contexts, ensuring usability in various formats.
- The minimum recommended size for the marca ensures legibility and reproduction quality.
- Improper usages of the marca include altering proportions, deforming, adding graphic elements, or modifying typography. Examples highlight proper protocol.

**Color Information:**

- Primary corporate colors specified as Pantone 186 (red) and black. Details provided for process black suitable for coated and uncoated paper, CMYK values (0%C 0%M 0%Y 100%K), and RGB values (R:0 G:0 B:0).
- The Alcaldessa’s (Mayor’s) graphic signature includes color details: 3 tones and 2 tones specifying symbolic red shades.
- Additional official colors used in the city banner: Pantone 186 (red) and Pantone 1235 (yellow).

**Typography Information:**

- The corporate typeface is Akkurat, with primary and secondary weights. Akkurat Bold is used for the logotype, sectors, districts, and web assets. Akkurat Regular and Italic are for text and corporate stationery. Arial serves as a fallback option for technical limitations.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Construction details of the symbol use proportional grids to ensure reproducibility on various media.
- Specific layout construction guidelines for the marca, showcasing proportions and relationships between logotype and symbol. Adherence to these guidelines is mandatory unless pre-approved exceptions are specified within the manual.

**Notes:**

- Institutional seals and stamps include predefined proportions, appearing at sizes of 54mm x 24mm and 30mm x 30mm for different use cases.

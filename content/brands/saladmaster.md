## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The Saladmaster logo includes geometric figures with overlapping circles representing connection and life. A circular symbol in the center represents steel, reflecting the strength of the product and brand.
- Clear space for the logo is equivalent to the height/width of the 'M' in the main logotype. This ensures the logo remains visually distinct.
- Minimum size for the wordmark is 1 inch tall in print or web to maintain legibility.
- Wordmark version of the logo is to be used for minimal and small space designs.
- Available logo lockups include full lockup with/without tagline, horizontal lockup with/without tagline, corporate badge, watermark.
- Dealer logos include Independent Authorized Dealer badge and horizontal versions, maintaining corporate consistency.
- Do's and Don'ts for logo usage include: avoid tilting, disproportionately resizing elements, mixing or altering colors, using opacity, applying the logo on dark backgrounds without sufficient contrast.
- Violations include examples of incorrect usage: dark logo overlay on dark backgrounds, tilted logos, mixed color applications, alterations to aspect ratio or design elements.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Brand Blue - Pantone 7694 C, HEX #133E5B, RGB 17, 62, 91, CMYK 97/74/41/31.
- Primary Brand Green - Pantone (not specified), HEX #03313A, RGB 3, 49, 58, CMYK 93/65/57/56.
- Accent Navy - Pantone 303 C, HEX #003045, RGB 0, 48, 69, CMYK 98/74/49/47.
- Accent Gray - Pantone Cool Gray 3, HEX #C6C6C6, RGB 198, 198, 198, CMYK 22/17/18/0.
- Secondary Lemon - Pantone 113 C, HEX #FFDD63, RGB 254, 221, 98, CMYK 1/11/73/0.
- Secondary Tangerine - Pantone 715 C, HEX #F7913E, RGB 247, 145, 62, CMYK 0/52/85/0.
- Textures include marble, woodgrain, concrete, and other natural textures for visual interest.
- Secondary colors are sparingly applied (less than 10% of designs) for emphasis, avoiding prominent areas like headers.

**Typography Information:**

- Headline Font: Playfair Display. Styles available include Bold, Regular, Italic. Used for headlines, titles, section numbers, and page numbers. Italics are for emphasizing specific text.
- Body Font: Raleway. Styles include Light, Regular, Bold. Used primarily for body copy, subheads, tables, and utility text. Free font available.
- Raleway provides balance between sophistication and approachability. Emphasis can be added using italics or bold.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style is intimate, organic, and inviting, showcasing food and lifestyle for emotional connection.
- Photographs incorporate neutral and textured backgrounds with minimal color correction and natural lighting.
- Copy overlays on photographs use primary or accent colors in negative space. Layers of opacity enhance the premium brand aesthetic.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space for logos equals the height/width of the 'M' in the main logotype.
- Logo placement rules ensure visual cleanliness and contrast with neutral backgrounds.

**Brand Voice:**

- Saladmaster brand personality combines traits of a teacher, entertainer, and dreamer. It empowers and inspires transformations through a fun-loving, knowledgeable tone.
- Direction for Saladmaster communications emphasizes positivity, premium quality, encouragement, and trustworthiness. Tone guidelines balance professionalism and fun.

**Notes:**

- Trademarks: Use ® for the first appearance of the logo or the brand name in copy. ™ is required for the tagline 'We Change Life' in document copies if not already included in the logo.
- Contact for branding, marketing, and questions: Marketing@Saladmaster.com.

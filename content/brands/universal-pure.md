## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Primary brand mark with hexagon and embedded arrow symbolizing forward movement. Represents the new identity with elements of science, strength, and technology.
- Word mark emphasizes 'Universal Pure' as separate words with capital U and P. Reflects foundational strength (Universal) and cleanliness and modernity (Pure).
- Logo variations for different backgrounds: primary logo for light and dark backgrounds, single-color options, and flat designs with and without dynamic gradients. Six options are provided.
- Clear space rule: Use a segment of the hexagon to measure the clear space around the logo. For the wordmark, use the capital 'U' for Universal to calculate spacing.
- Minimum logo sizes: Full logo minimum size is 0.5 inches or 55 pixels wide. Hexagon icon minimum is 0.25 inches or 16 pixels. Wordmark minimum is 1 inch or 70 pixels.
- Logo usage restrictions: Do not stretch or distort logos, separate elements, tilt logos on uneven axes, use non-brand colors, or create the wordmark in different typefaces or colors.

**Color Information:**

- Brand primary colors: Blue (suggests safety and stability), Orange (symbolizes enthusiasm), Green (represents sustainability and natural elements). Color codes: Blue - Pantone 7462 C (C99/M72/Y21/K5, R0/G84/B138, #00538a); Light Blue - Pantone 7461 C (C85/M44/Y4/K0, R11/G125/B187, #0a7cba); Green - Pantone 381 C (C24/M0/Y100/K0, R205/G220/B41, #cddb28); Green Gradient with 4-color variation; Orange - Pantone 152 C (C5/M67/Y100/K0, R232/G116/B37, #e87324); Orange Gradient with 4-color variation; Dark Gray - Pantone Cool Gray 10 C (C61/M53/Y48/K19, R101/G101/B106, #656569); Light Gray - Pantone Cool Gray 5 C (C31/M25/Y25/K0, R179/G178/B179, #b2b2b2).
- Core colors can be used to emphasize the company's main attributes of safety, sustainability, and enthusiasm. The color palette uses a wide range of colors to reinforce the brand’s dynamic nature.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family: Exo 2, characterized as contemporary, clear, and impactful. Available in weights: Thin, Regular, and Bold. Suitable for both small- and large-scale applications.
- Complementary font family: Open Sans, an accessible, precise, and visually appealing sans-serif font. It is to be used for body text.
- Typography guideline: Universal Pure marketing team provides approved font family assets.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography guidelines: Images must emphasize food safety, freshness, and contemporary ideas of shopping, cooking, and eating. Food photography should be warm, inviting, and reflective of the brand's values.
- Approved images are stored in the Universal Pure marketing team library and additional sourcing support is available by contacting Universal Pure Marketing.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo spacing rules: Clear space must be measured using a component of the logo (e.g., segment of the hexagon for the full logo or the capital 'U' of Universal for the word mark).

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand personality communicates safety, trust, innovation, and collaboration, emphasizing a customer-focused, results-oriented approach with integrity.
- Brand manifesto highlights commitment to food safety, collaboration, and continuous improvement while eliminating uncertainties and earning customer trust.

**Notes:**

- The Universal Pure brand is built on care, results, service, collaboration, and integrity. Core messaging focuses on trust, collaboration, food safety, and advancement.
- 'Universal Pure' must always be written as two words, with a capital U for Universal and a capital P for Pure.
- Never separate the hexagon and arrow brand mark. Use only approved files for all branding purposes provided by the Universal Pure marketing team.
- Two versions of a branded hexagon pattern have been designed for use in communications: high-frequency and low-frequency. These patterns must only be used with the brand mark and cannot have the hexagon logo placed on top of them.

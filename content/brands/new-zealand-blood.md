## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The New Zealand Blood Service logo is hand-drawn and should not be recreated using typefaces. Always use supplied artwork files.
- Preferred logo version is a hand-drawn piece designed to link visually with the 'Save Lives, Give Blood' logo. Supplied files must be used.
- Available logo versions include full color, one-color (red or black, for single-ink printing), and reverse (white on colored backgrounds).
- Minimum logo clear space is based on the width of the blood drop. Minimum reproduction size should be 28mm in print materials.
- When logo size is under 45mm, the Māori tagline should not appear beneath it. For media larger than A3, logo should occupy 25% of the shortest side.

**Color Information:**

- Spot colors (Pantone) are recommended for printing due to brightness and depth. Pantone codes provided for brand colors.
- Full-color (CMYK) printing is recommended for print materials involving newspapers, magazines, or exhibitions.
- Digital (RGB) specifications provided for use in screen applications such as PowerPoint or websites.
- Brand color specifications: Pantone 485 (Red), Pantone 3435 (Green), Pantone Black, Pantone Cool Grey 11C with respective CMYK and RGB values.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family is Avant Garde, used for headlines and text above 13 pt. Available weights include Book, Medium, Demi, and Bold.
- For body copy below 13 pt, Helvetica Neue (Light, Regular, Bold) is recommended. Equivalent font Arial (e.g., 11 pt) can be used in limited systems.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand is described as a consistent experience that people should easily recognize and positively associate with the New Zealand Blood Service.
- The tagline 'Save Lives, Give Blood' embodies the core message and call to action of the organization.

**Notes:**

- The document stresses the importance of consistent brand communication across all materials and interactions to meet the organization's vision.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The Principia School wordmark and shield, including guidelines for use with navy or white reverse options, and athletics logos with specific treatments like one-color and reverse.

**Color Information:**

- Primary palette includes HEX #8d7e4a (gold), HEX #20213e (navy); Neutrals include HEX #f4f4f4, HEX #323333, with secondary palette featuring PMS 2925C (HEX #009add), PMS 144C (HEX #f38a00), PMS 338C, and PMS 109C (HEX #ffd100).

**Typography Information:**

- Typography hierarchy utilizes ROBOTO SLAB for primary serif (character styles: Regular, Italic, Bold, Bold Italic), and ROBOTO for sans-serif (weights: Light, Regular, Bold). Roles include headlines, callouts, captions, and fast facts.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style emphasizes personal, joyful, and light themes; illustrates a range of activities like academics, athletics, and character development.
- Use the wheat graphic to frame moments, compositions, and portraits. Guidelines focus on enhancing emotional resonance and emphasizing individual expressions.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Ensure minimum width equals the height of two lines when designing with an offset border or framing headlines.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone includes personal, consistent, and effective messaging tailored for diverse audiences. Social media messaging balances casual and formal elements to suit channels and demographics (e.g., Millennials, Gen Z, Boomers).

**Notes:**

- Templates, letterheads, and marketing assets are available through the school's Marketing department.
- Use authorized vendors for Athletics branding materials to ensure consistency with school guidelines.

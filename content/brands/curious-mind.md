## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The Curious Minds logo consists of typography and neurons and is only available in horizontal format. The logo is shown in positive (gold and ink for white backgrounds only), negative (ink and white for gold backgrounds), and reverse (white and gold for dark backgrounds). A positive grayscale version is also available for white backgrounds only.
- Logo files are pre-configured to include minimum clear space requirements. The minimum clear space is determined by 2x the width of the 'U' in Curious. This space must remain clear of text or imagery, except for accepted background colors.
- Minimum logo size is an overall width of 30mm to ensure legibility.
- Incorrect usage rules: Do not alter the direction or angle of the graphic, do not alter relationships between elements, do not recolor elements, do not use the type without the graphic, and do not use a box to contain the logo.
- The Curious Minds logo must be used in conjunction with the investing agency’s logo and the Curious Minds website address in a logo hierarchy. Examples for footer placements are provided.

**Color Information:**

- The primary color palette includes 'Ink' and 'Gold'. Secondary colors include White, Red, Purple, Blue, and Green. These colors symbolize environment, passion, creativity, knowledge, and inspiration.
- Primary Color Specifications: Ink - Pantone 7547, CMYK (100, 79, 44, 93), RGB (47, 50, 57), HEX (#2F3239). Gold - Pantone 1245, CMYK (12, 35, 100, 0), RGB (250, 173, 35), HEX (#FAAD23).
- Secondary Color Specifications: Red - Pantone 1788, CMYK (0, 95, 89, 0), RGB (206, 36, 36), HEX (#CE2424). Purple, Blue, and Green also have individual Pantone, CMYK, RGB, and HEX specifications.
- Pantone and CMYK colors are recommended for printed items, while RGB and HEX colors should be used for digital and online applications.
- Secondary colors are recommended for wayfinding systems within publications and websites, as well as for sparing use in graphs and accents.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The clear space around the logo must be at least 2x the width of the 'U' in Curious. The proportional area must scale with the size of the logo.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand narrative aims to inspire New Zealand communities to engage with science and technology by fostering a nation of curious minds where science is inspiring and personally relevant.

**Notes:**

- The guidelines are intended for third parties delivering science and technology activities with Curious Minds funding or official support. A simplified logo kit, including usage instructions, is available upon request through the Curious Minds website.
- The Curious Minds logo and the investing agency’s logo are used in conjunction with footers for all collateral, including online and offline materials.
- The logo kit provides Curious Minds logo files and guidance on application. Users are advised to contact the organization administering the funding or MBIE Design Team if they have questions about brand guideline usage.

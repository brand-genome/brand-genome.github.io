## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The DMD Diamond logo comprises two elements: the logo symbol (evoking empowerment culture) and the logotype presented through color, shape, and form. Preferred formats: .eps, .ai, .png, .jpg, .tif.
- Main logo is vertically assembled; preferred on white or light backgrounds. For dark backgrounds, the primary reversed logo is recommended. Strict rules prohibit use of stylized, animated, hand-drawn, or unofficial versions.
- Logo variations include: general logo, logo symbol, and reversed variations for darker backgrounds.
- Clear space computation: Logo requires a clear space surrounding it to preserve brand aesthetics. Clear space calculations are specified for vertically aligned and horizontally aligned versions.
- Minimum size requirements: Full logo minimum width is 1 inch (25.4mm); logo symbol minimum width is 0.625 inch (15.875mm); horizontal logo minimum width is 1.5 inch (38.1mm).
- Logo misuse guidelines: Do not apply outlines, rotate/stretch, apply gradients, add drop shadows, use unauthorized colors, or apply special effects.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Colors: Saphire (HEX: #030A49, RGB: 3/10/73, CMYK: 100/98/31/47, Pantone: 2765 C), Cobalt (HEX: #0145B2, RGB: 1/69/178, CMYK: 95/81/0/0, Pantone: 7685 C), Summer Sky (HEX: #27B7ED, RGB: 39/183/237, CMYK: 66/8/0/0, Pantone: 298 C), Turquoise Blue (HEX: #58C6EB, RGB: 88/198/235, CMYK: 57/1/3/0, Pantone: 305 C).
- Secondary Colors: Dark Gray (HEX: #353B3E, RGB: 53/59/62, CMYK: 73/62/58/50, Pantone: 447 C), White (HEX: #FFFFFF, RGB: 255/255/255, CMYK: 00/00/00, Pantone: 1-1 C).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface is Arial, selected for its energy injection. Font styles include Arial Regular and Arial Bold. Classification: Sans Serif. Publisher: Monotype.
- Secondary typeface is Gotham Rounded. Styles include Light, Book, Medium, and Bold. Classification: Sans Serif. Publisher: Hoefler & Co.
- Typography hierarchy examples for print (font size / leading): Captions (Arial Regular, 6pt / 9pt), Copy-text (Gotham Rounded Book, 9pt / 11pt), Headlines (Arial Bold, 10pt / 12pt), Sub-lines (Arial Bold, 16pt / 18pt), Titles (Gotham Rounded Bold, 25pt / 27pt), Marketing Sequencer (Arial Bold, 40pt / 43pt).
- Typography hierarchy examples for screen display (font size / line height): Captions (Arial Regular, 14px / 1.5), Body text (Gotham Rounded Book, 16px / 1.62), Header 1 (Arial Regular, 40px / 1), Header 2 (Gotham Rounded Bold, 20px / 1), Header 3 (Arial Bold, 16px / 1), Header 4 (Arial Bold, 14px / 1.25).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Logo-on-image rule: Use white logo for light backgrounds. For images with light backgrounds, apply 10-20% black tint to maintain legibility of the logo.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grid and alignment specifications: Logo clear space measurement specified as percentages and proportional values to ensure brand aesthetics. Examples provided for vertical and horizontal aligned logos.

**Brand Voice:**

- Mission and Values: Scarity, value, security, innovation, integrity. Focused on creating stable wealth storage and profit-generating solutions. Vision encompasses establishing DMD Diamond as digital gold and luxury commodity.

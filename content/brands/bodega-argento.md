## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- Introduction of the New Roundel Logo.
- Primary combined logo showcased.
- Secondary logo detailed.
- Secondary 'word only' logo presented.
- Monochrome logo: All elements are 100% black without the grey drop shadow; for light backgrounds.
- White-out logo: Text and roundel inverted to white, without drop shadow; for dark backgrounds.
- Logo A: for use over 25mm; Logo C: for 4mm-14mm sizes; No maximum size for Logo A.
- Greater detail on logo sizes, designed between 15mm to over 80mm based on use.
- Exclusion zone specifies the area around the logo free of other elements, proportional to logo size.
- Incorrect logo usage examples: Distortion, poor color selection, and improper placement elaborated.

**Color Information:**

- Black and Warm Grey (Pantone Warm Grey 4) are the primary colors. Black 100% specified as C=0 M=0 Y=0 K=100. Warm Grey: C=0 M=4 Y=9 K=24.
- Roundel logo colors include 100% black, Pantone Warm Grey 4 (C=0 M=4 Y=9 K=24), and Pantone Cool Grey 4.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Exclusion zone for logos explained; the margin around logos calculated based on their sizing.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone described as 'sophisticated, real, proud, sociable, expressive, vibrant, and stirring.'
- Brand attributes focus on 'craft, generosity, pleasure, friendship, authenticity, and respect for life.'

**Notes:**

- The PDF consistently emphasizes the Argentine connection for creating strong cultural associations.
- Design adjustments based on logo size/medium are given specific attention for consistency.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2022

**Logo Information:**

- The Yembo.ai logo should always appear in its full intended form, clearly visible on white, light blue, or light grey backgrounds. Positive and negative logos require distinct artwork and should not be used interchangeably. The full-color, black and white, and reversed logos are provided.
- The logo's clear space must be equal to the height and width of the 'o' element in the logo, termed 'the area of isolation.'
- Recommended minimum logo size: Print - 1 inch wide; Web - 100px wide.
- Do not recolor, distort proportions, move elements, place on complex images, tilt or angle, or add elements to the logo. Incorrect usage examples are provided.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Yembo Blue (#1771F1, Pantone 2927 C, RGB 23 113 241, CMYK 79, 57, 0, 0), White, and Yembo Dark Blue (#04264B, Pantone 7463 C, RGB 4 38 75, CMYK 100, 87, 41, 42).
- Secondary colors include Green (#27AE60, Pantone 3405 C), Bright Pink (#F34B77, Pantone 184 C), Yellow (#FFCC49, Pantone 1225 C), Purple (#9B57B6, Pantone 2583 C), Orange (#FF9400, Pantone 2013 C), and Coral (#FF847C, Pantone 170 C).
- Greyscale palette includes Light Grey (#F8F8F8, Pantone 7541 C), Platinum Blue (#D5E0E8, Pantone 642 C), Titan White (#EBF1F4, Pantone 656 C), Dark Grey (#909499, Pantone Cool Gray 7 C), Rock Blue (#A1B2BF, Pantone 2176 C), Midnight (#303A42, Pantone 539 C), White (#FFFFFF), and Illustration Blue (#9FCDFF, Pantone 291 C).

**Typography Information:**

- Montserrat is used across all types with recommended weights: Medium, Regular, Light, SemiBold, Bold, and ExtraBold. Examples and sizes for print include Level 1 (16pt/19pt), Level 2 (10pt/12pt), Level 3 (16pt/22pt), Level 4 (10.5pt/16pt).
- For digital and web, typography hierarchy includes: H1 - Montserrat Bold (48px/60px), H2 - Montserrat ExtraBold (18px/21.6px), H3 - Montserrat Regular (20px/24px), Button - Montserrat Bold (17px/21.6px).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography showing industry practices is recommended. Stock imagery should have natural coloring, realistic expressions, and avoid saturation. Photography guidelines prioritize diversity, natural light, and expressions.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Design elements such as circles, polka dot patterns, and a grid system are utilized to visually connect to the brand and product. Background textures should be used as secondary visuals.

**Brand Voice:**

- Yembo.ai written communication should always use titlecase. The tagline cannot appear without mention of Yembo.ai.

**Notes:**

- Custom illustrations follow a thin, modern linear style using approved color palettes. For stock illustrations, a linear modern quality mimicking the brand's style should be preferred.
- Icons utilize a thin linear style with grey and primary color palettes. New icons should mimic this style.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Brand Voice:**

- The introduction states that the brand guideline is part of the redesign project aiming to simplify understanding, identification, and perception of governmental actions. It emphasizes citizen clarity and rationalizing communication formats.
- Three objectives of the brand guidelines: (1) Prolonging the heritage of the République Française's symbols like Marianne, the French flag, and the national motto, (2) Updating and rationalizing the common identity system according to new formats and usage, (3) Simplifying citizen understanding and perception of public action.

**Notes:**

- The content discusses the visual principles based on Marianne's dynamized symbol, a specific typography (Marianne font), and a refined motto that adheres to traditional French typographic design.
- Systemized modular approach to national, local, and international representation of the government's block mark. For national-level use of the block mark, ministries are represented individually, whereas joint communications use the official 'Gouvernement' designation.

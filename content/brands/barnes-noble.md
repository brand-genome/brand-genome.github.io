## Brand Guidelines

### 2012

**Logo Information:**

- Primary logo uses color genres such as full color, single color (PMS 632), grayscale (100% black, 55% black), and one-color black.
- Usage of the secondary logo in full color, single color, black, and white genres.
- Logo can be set on varying backgrounds using single color PMS 632, black and white, or reversed white (PMS 5455) depending on visibility needs.

**Color Information:**

- Brand colors include PMS 632 (HEX #00ffad), PMS 1817 (HEX #620007), PMS 179 (HEX #ff3303).
- Secondary colors include pairs such as PMS 179 (Fiery Red) and PMS 1817 Shadow; PMS 390 (Crisp Green) and PMS 7490 Shadow.
- Sub-brand colors: NOOK uses PMS 179 (HEX #ff3303), B&Ncollege uses PMS 7547 (HEX #0f1a21), pubit! uses PMS 390 (HEX #b0e814).

**Typography Information:**

- Barnes & Noble Heading: Bold typeface used for all caps headings in print, posters, billboards, and web applications.
- Barnes & Noble Regular: Thin typeface for larger bodies of text, usable in all caps or upper and lowercase.
- Barnes & Noble Serif: Italic typeface used for captions and small text to convey class and softness.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style emphasizes airiness and welcoming atmosphere with brighter exposure and minimal contrast.
- Images should display reading, music, food, and store activities, making viewers feel part of the photograph.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Tagline clear space guideline: .5x spacing around the logo elements, where x represents the height of the tagline font size.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tagline: 'More Than Just Books' emphasizing the environment and home-like feeling Barnes & Noble offers to customers.

**Notes:**

- Logo guidelines stress visibility and legibility, recommending reversal on dark backgrounds using PMS 5455 for better contrast.

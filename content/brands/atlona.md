## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Primary logo is an inline version with a balance of Atlona Orange and 100% Black. It reflects the innovative nature of the company.
- Secondary logo includes 100% Black and 100% White variants to accommodate different marketing needs. Designed for contrasting backgrounds.
- Do not use three-dimensional renderings or apply 3D effects to the logo.
- Improper usage examples include altering the relationship of logo components, stretching or skewing the logo, or using color combinations not outlined.
- The logo must always include the 'a Panduit company' statement, and modifications to this element are not allowed.

**Color Information:**

- Atlona Orange - Pantone 165 C, RGB 242 104 41, CMYK 0 73.61 94.9 0, HEX #FF6633
- Black variant designated as 100% BLACK.
- White variants designated as 100% WHITE.

**Typography Information:**

- PowerPoint typeface: Arial.
- Printed material typeface: Myriad Pro.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum size requirement for logo is 3/8 inch (10mm). Logos should not be scaled below this size to maintain clarity.
- Minimum clear space is 1/2 inch (13mm) around the logo with no visual elements intruding on this zone.
- In relative sizing applications, keep the distance of the letter 'A' away from the logo and other elements.

**Brand Voice:**

- Mission statement: 'We are passionate about providing innovation to enable our customers’ ability to connect and collaborate with simplicity.'
- Vision: 'To be the go-to provider for audiovisual connectivity needs in enterprise, higher education, corporate, government, and residential spaces.'
- Core values include professionalism, respect, integrity, innovation, diversity, and excellence.

**Notes:**

- Atlona is now a Panduit company, combining Atlona's range with Panduit's global infrastructure to support AV over IP systems.

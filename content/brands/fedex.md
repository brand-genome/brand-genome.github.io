## Brand Guidelines

### 2009

**Logo Information:**

- FedEx requires registration marks such as 'TM', 'Registered Trademark', and others. Specifics regarding placement are provided. For example, the 'Express Sign Signature' logo includes a registration mark that is 9.5mm high, positioned half the distance below the baseline of the logo.
- Clear space around FedEx Express Logo is defined. Specifically, the width of the clear zone is described as equal to the width of the 'downstroke' of 'F'.
- Mandatory placements of GSP (Global Service Participant) endorsements apply to the exterior of facilities unless restrictions apply. Alternatives are provided for compliance.

**Color Information:**

- FedEx corporate identity includes specific colors for each business unit: 'FedEx Orange', 'FedEx Green', 'FedEx Red', and others. These colors are applied distinctly for category designation across services like Express and Ground.

**Notes:**

- FedEx sign layouts are subject to landlord restrictions/local codes, which must be integrated during the sign creation and approval process. Custom solutions, if required, must be approved by Global Brand Management (GBM). Detailed dimensions and positioning rules for facility signs are also provided.

### 2003

**Logo Information:**

- FedEx signatures include the wordmark and operating company descriptor. Approved artwork must be downloaded from fedexbrand.com.
- The registered service mark symbol (®) is attached to each FedEx signature and should always be at least 5 points in diameter. Positioned on the baseline of the wordmark at a distance equal to its radius.
- FedEx has multiple signature configurations: preferred (large two-color) configurations and special horizontal use configurations.
- Maintaining clear space around the FedEx signature equal to or greater than the x-height of the wordmark is mandatory.
- FedEx signatures are available in the following color variations: Two-Color Positive, One-Color Purple, Two-Color Reverse, One-Color Black, Company Color, and One-Color Reverse.

**Color Information:**

- FedEx Purple: Pantone 2685, CMYK C90 M100 Y0 K0, RGB R102 G0 B153, Hex #660099.
- FedEx Light Platinum: Pantone 877, CMYK C0 M0 Y0 K37, RGB R153 G153 B153, Hex #999999.
- FedEx Light Gray: Pantone Cool Gray 6, CMYK C0 M0 Y0 K37, RGB R153 G153 B153, Hex #999999.
- FedEx Orange: Pantone 021, CMYK C0 M65 Y100 K0, RGB R255 G102 B0, Hex #FF6600.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface for FedEx is Univers, specifically Univers Condensed 47 Light, Univers Condensed 57 Regular, and Univers Condensed 67 Bold. Suitable for all printed communications.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space surrounding trademarks: equal to or greater than the x-height of the FedEx wordmark.

**Brand Voice:**

- Attributes of the FedEx brand: Simplifying, Optimizing, Inventive, Connecting, Certain, Personal.

**Notes:**

- FedEx brand emphasizes clear and consistent communication via global brand management. Artwork must be sourced from fedexbrand.com.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The logo depicts a fireball — a symbol of energy, igniting the marketing mechanics.
- The inner circle of the inverted version of the logo is slightly larger than the circle of the default version. This detail ensures balance within the logo design.
- The logo is a symbol to be used beyond the main key visual mechanism. It is highly recommended that the logo be used in a small scale. The logo's height is equal to a lowercase letter in the headline.
- It is recommended that the logo be used in the hot orange color. Alternatively, it is permitted to use in white for specific backgrounds. However, no other color usage is allowed beyond hot orange, black, and white.
- Usage specifications by background: Basic version is for white backgrounds; inverted version applies for main and photo backgrounds.
- The logo is aligned with the wordmark at the center of the letter 'S'. Clear spacing rules: The distance between the logo and the wordmark is equal to the width of the logo's glint.
- Vertical versions are needed when the logo must adapt to narrow media spaces. However, vertical versions should only be used at small scales and are discouraged as primary branding.

**Color Information:**

- The friendly and vivid character of the brand communication is articulated by the vast color palette and bold, yet soft graphics. Primary colors such as Hot Orange, Blue, and Salmon define the palette.
- Main brand colors include Hot Orange (#FF642D), Blue (#6EDBFF), and Salmon (#FF788F), with detailed RGB and CMYK codes. These colors align with Pantone references Orange 021, Blue 297, and Salmon 184 respectively.
- The extensive additional color palette includes Carmine, Emerald, Mint Green, Yellow, and Dark Indigo alongside their respective HEX, RGB, CMYK, and Pantone equivalents. The palette is expanded for nuanced design uses.

**Typography Information:**

- The typeface Factor A is a geometric grotesque used primarily for headlines and jobbing compositions. It ensures high legibility while reflecting modern brand aesthetics.
- Inter is a neutral grotesque typeface selected for long texts and product use. This style balances readability for extensive content.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography complements the vivid brand character with bold and soft graphics. The clear and precise marketing mechanics are highlighted through sharp compositions.

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The logo comprises a symbol (Fireball) and a typeface.
- The main version of the logo includes a composition with the Fireball to the left of the typeface. An alternative version has the Fireball above the typeface. The Fireball can also be used as an independent element.
- Logo versions are available in AI, EPS, SVG, and PNG formats.
- Examples of logo inversions with SEMrush Red filled base and various color combinations.
- The logo is available in the branded colors: SEMrush Red, SEMrush Grey, SEMrush Anthracite, and White.
- Clear space/safe zone for the main logo version, alternative logo version, and Fireball symbol. Minimum safe zone equals the height of the Fireball (marked as X).
- Minimum logo size requirements: Main logo version (30mm for print, 150px for digital); Alternative logo version (20mm for print, 100px for digital); Fireball symbol (20mm for print, 100px for digital).
- Logo usage guidelines on various backgrounds, with instructions for proper readability. Incorrect examples and forbidden backgrounds provided.
- Incorrect logo usage includes: replacing texts/fonts, transforming the logo, recoloring, adding elements, using filters, slanting, using low-quality images, and using outdated versions of the logo.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include SEMrush Red (Pantone 1675 C), SEMrush Anthracite (Pantone 7540 C), SEMrush Grey (Pantone 429 C), and White. Detailed CMYK, RGB, and HEX values are provided.
- SEMrush Red is the main branding color associated with energy, robustness, and force.
- The supporting (secondary) palette includes various SEMrush colors with names, Pantone, CMYK, RGB, and HEX values detailed for each.
- SEMrush color guidelines explicitly state no gradients are allowed, and secondary colors are used for contrast and UI elements.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family: Ubuntu. It is a clean, modern sans-serif typeface available under an open-source license and supports multiple styles and languages.
- Main styles for Ubuntu used: Bold, Medium, and Regular. Example type sizes are provided for headlines and general usage.
- Alternative font: Roboto. It is geometric and friendly with natural letter widths. Used for advertising materials and stationery.
- Main styles for Roboto used: Bold, Medium, and Light. Example type sizes are provided for headlines and general usage.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Business cards have vertical orientation (85x55mm). Specific layout rules: 35mm logo width centered on the top, Roboto font usage for text content divided into three sections (First/Last Name, Job Title, Contact Info).
- Examples of other display materials provided for mugs, shopping bags, and T-shirts with appropriate brand representation.

**Notes:**

- The document provides ample guidelines for correct logo usage, colors, and typography but explicitly prohibits unapproved alterations or misplacements of the logo elements.
- SEMrush emphasizes adhering to the brand guidelines and contacting their design team for clarifications at brand@semrush.com.

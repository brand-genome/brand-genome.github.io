### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The PIRELLI logotype consists of red lettering placed within a yellow rectangle. It must not be manipulated or altered, and its components cannot be used separately.
- Color version: Pantone 485C (red) and Pantone 109C (yellow). RGB and CMYK specifications provided.
- Exclusion zone or clear space defined as the width of the base of the letter 'P' in PIRELLI.
- Minimum width of the logotype must not be less than 15mm.
- Logotype should primarily be used on dark-colored backgrounds. It should not appear on yellow, red, or other colors that reduce its visibility.
- Examples of incorrect usage include incorrect colors, cropping, distortion, background clashes, exclusion zone violations, and rotation.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors are Pantone 485C (Red: R222 G33 B27, HEX: #d52b1e, CMYK: C0 M100 Y100 K0) and Pantone 109C (Yellow: R255 G221 B0, HEX: #fed100, CMYK: C0 M10 Y100 K0).

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate Typeface: Gotham, available in variations like Gotham Bold, Gotham Black, and Italics.
- Secondary Typeface: Arial, to be used for electronic text (e.g., PowerPoint presentations, Word templates, electronic communications).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Exclusion zone or clear space must be maintained around the logotype, defined as the base width of the 'P' in PIRELLI.
- Minimum size for the logotype is 15mm in width to ensure legibility.

**Notes:**

- All usage of the PIRELLI logotype not specified in the manual must be authorized by the Brand Image and Equity Management Department.
- Any usage by dealers must be approved on a case-by-case basis with prior authorization by the Industrial Property Department.

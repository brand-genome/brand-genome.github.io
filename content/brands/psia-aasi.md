## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- PSIA and AASI logos are meant to be used grouped as preferred, with independent use acceptable for representing one discipline.
- Grouped logos must maintain proportions and can be produced in one (black, white, or Pantone Reflex Blue), two, or four-color process.
- PSIA logo color versions include one-color black, one-color PSIA Blue, two-color PSIA Blue and Red, and four-color process. Clear space requirements are defined, and the logo cannot be reproduced smaller than 3/8-inch width.
- Accepted AASI logo color versions include one-color black, two-color combinations (blue and black, beige and black), and four-color process versions. Clear space rules apply, and it cannot be reproduced smaller than 0.4-inch width.
- Alternate logo marks are defined for social media. Minimum size rules apply (3/8-inch width for PSIA logo and 0.4 inch for AASI logo). Only produced in four-color process for print and RGB for web use.
- Horizontal logo mark preferred for web headers, PowerPoint slides, and newsletters; minimum size rules apply (3/8-inch width for PSIA, 0.4 inch for AASI). Usage in four-color or RGB.
- The correct logo version must be selected based on medium (CMYK, Pantone, RGB, one-color black) for printing, banners, and embroidery. Specific file formats are mentioned for each.
- Member Benefit logo adds emphasis to member benefits. Typically used in upper left or right corners of a page.

**Color Information:**

- Core colors include PMS Black, PMS Reflex Blue (286), PMS Red (186), along with hex codes and CMYK specifications. Secondary colors provided include PMS Beige (451), PMS Yellow (716), PMS Green (582). RGB color specifications also listed.

**Typography Information:**

- Headline fonts include Neusa and Trade Gothic. Trade Gothic acceptable in all weights (Light, Medium, Bold, Black, Condensed Bold, and italic variations).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Hash mark bar anchoring design specified as bottom layer on graphics, adaptable for holding text lines. Example provided on page 29 indicates usage with specific color guidelines.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space is mandatory around logos to avoid crowding by typography or graphical elements. Exceptions apply only for social media.
- Minimum logo size set at 3/8-inch width for PSIA and 0.4 inch for AASI logos. Applies to alternate and horizontal logo marks.

**Brand Voice:**

- The PSIA-AASI identity emphasizes passion, professionalism, knowledge, trust, friendliness, relevance, and lifelong learning among members. Traits include approachable, fun, legitimate, inclusive, sincere, progressive, and responsive.
- Vision statement: 'Create lifelong adventures through education.' Mission relates to inspiring excitement in improving skills and passion for snowsports.
- Brand promise: PSIA-AASI commits to getting people excited about skiing and snowboarding by promoting professional instruction.
- Association naming requirements: Prefer use of 'Professional Ski Instructors of America and American Association of Snowboard Instructors (PSIA-AASI)' at first reference publicly. Specific guidelines about informal naming and improper abbreviation avoidance also noted.

**Notes:**

- Additional guidelines are included regarding specific logo applications, color combinations across media, and member benefit usage.
- Hash mark bar design should conform to PMS Reflex Blue color (548) guidelines, adding a visual anchor to designs.

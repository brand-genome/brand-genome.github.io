## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Main logo lockups provided for print and digital applications including publications, billboards, flyers, advertising, packaging. Available in Black & Green or Negative (White). Supported file formats: Vector (.ai & .psd), JPEG, PDF, PNG, EPS. Layout includes Left-Justified and Centered options.
- Clear space rules: Minimum clear space of 2.5x around the logo and additional 0.25x spacing noted.
- Dealership logo guidelines include centered and justified variations. Minimum clear space measurements provided: referencing x, 1/2x, and 2x spacing.
- Logo application do’s and don’ts: Avoid loud backgrounds like Red, Orange, Yellow, etc. Do not rotate or embellish the logo. Use solid colors or neutral spaces for logo placement. Prefer negative logos over busy backgrounds.
- Rules for overlaying logo onto images: Focus on high-contrast areas and avoid image focal points.
- Recommended logo sizing for specific media: Folders (9"x12"), Flyers (4"x9" folded), One PAGERS (8.5"x11"), Owner’s Guides (5.5"x8.5"), Business Cards (3.5"x2"), Brochures (7"x10"), along with spacing specifications.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes Brand Green (#55910C), RGB (85, 152, 12), CMYK (72, 18, 100, 4). Secondary palette includes Orange (#FF7800), Grey (#4D4B4E), Tan (#ECEBEO), Light Green (#85B52E), Dark Green (#235608). Tertiary palette (Accent colors) includes Alert Orange (#FF4A12).
- Core brand color (medium green) recommended to be used moderately. Lighter and darker variations allowed for contrast. Secondary (Grey and Tan tones) for all brand graphics. Tertiary (Red and Orange tones) accent colors for digital use and highlighting promotions.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface is Proxima Nova for all text in print and digital media. Lexia is used for lifestyle typography, specifically headlines.
- Typography hierarchy specified: Primary headline (Proxima Nova Extra Bold), Secondary headline (Lexia Light, 50% size), Tertiary headline (Proxima Nova Regular, 30% size). Usage guidelines and prohibitions clarified.
- Lifestyle headline specifications: Lexia XBold, 80% size of Primary headline. Proxima Nova Bold (Tracking 180, Uppercase) for Secondary Headline (2). Body Copy: Proxima Nova Regular. Usage guidelines outlined for each role.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Rules for image background applications: Ensure high contrast between logo and background. Avoid placing logos over image focal points.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum clear space rules for logo application: 2.5x around logo, accompanied by 0.25x measurements.

**Brand Voice:**

- Primary tagline: 'It’s all about your dog.®'. Incorrect variations include uppercase and title case formats. Supportive tagline: 'Smart products for today’s pets.®'.
- Taglines for product categories: Specifications for Hidden Fence, Indoor Boundaries, Training Products, BigLeash, SmartFence, ProFence, BarkCollar, SideWalker. Must align tagline with respective product categories.
- Trademarks requiring '®': DogWatch®, BigLeash®, FastReact®, SafeLink®, 'It’s all about your dog.®'.

**Notes:**

- Consumers Digest Best Buy requirements: Hidden Fence must include required text indicating award. Optional supportive claims can be used with applicable disclaimers.

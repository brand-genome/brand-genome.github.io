## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The logo is our most valuable brand asset. Minimum clear space should ideally exceed the star guideline. Minimum size is 75 pixels or 1 inch in width. Available in black, white, and full color (red/gray).
- The symbol can replace the logo where appropriate. Minimum clear space follows the same star guideline. Minimum size is 36 pixels or 0.5 inch in width. Colors include black, white, and full color (red/gray) in original or reversed/filled versions.
- Lockups include the official logo and other graphics. Partner logo clear space adheres to specific proportions (e.g., 1.5x and 2y). Partner logos should be monochrome in black, gray, or white.
- Do's and Don'ts for logo usage: Do not stretch, squish, break apart, recolor, shadow, pattern, crop, overlap, or alter logos, symbols, or official lockups.

**Color Information:**

- Primary palette includes red (Pantone 185), black, white, sea green (Pantone 3248), and yellow (Pantone 109). Neutral support colors include three shades of gray. Color values are given in HEX, CMYK, and RGB for digital and print use. Usage ratios and legibility guidelines provided for text on colored backgrounds.
- Extended palette includes additional shades for diversity and usability, such as chili (Pantone 187), coral (Pantone 170), lemon (Pantone 7406), peacock (Pantone 7719), and mint (Pantone 573). These are to be used sparingly and not as background colors. Usage ratios apply.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary corporate fonts: Museo Slab and Museo Sans (various weights from 100 to 900). Replacement system fonts: Roboto Slab and Roboto Sans, available in weights such as Bold, Regular, Light, and Thin.
- Typography examples: Highlights hierarchy principles such as headlines, subheaders, body text, links, and callouts. Good hierarchy enables legibility and ease of scanning.
- Typography tips: Capitalize headlines, subheads, and buttons. Avoid widows (single words on separate lines). Do not use Museo Slab 1000 or inappropriate quotation marks.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photo style focuses on simplicity, genuine emotions, and natural movements. High-resolution images with minimal clutter, diversity, and authenticity are emphasized. Avoid overused or computer-generated images.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Lockup clear space rules: Partner logos should maintain a 1.5x and 2y spacing around them. Icons and symbols should maintain adequate clear space as guided by the star symbol.

**Brand Voice:**

- Fonts, icons, and graphic styles are aligned with simplicity, authenticity, and professional appearance. Typography emphasizes clarity, hierarchy, and avoiding over-complication.

**Notes:**

- Brand assets such as logos, fonts, and imagery are accessible via internal HireVue links. External vendors can obtain these assets following specified procedures.

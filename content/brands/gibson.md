## Brand Guidelines

### 2005

**Logo Information:**

- Never remove any element from the original logo artwork (e.g., register mark, etc.). Never distort the logo (i.e., never condense or extend the logo, or make it bolder or thinner). Logo must remain proportionate to the original artwork at all times. Never modify any of the letterforms. Never reproduce the logo in an unauthorized color or on a background color that is not authorized. Never use the logo in conjunction with any other logo, as a headline type, over complicated or patterned backgrounds, or surrounded by shapes that could form another mark. This includes not applying drop shadows, altering proportions of typography, and rotating the logo. Always adhere to the relative size proportions and positioning as well as approved color standards.
- Clear space rules: A protective area of clear space must be maintained around the logo. Clear space should be at least 1/2 the height of the 'G' in the Gibson logo. No graphic elements, typography, pictures, art, or borders can intrude in this space.
- Minimum size requirement: The Gibson logo should never be reproduced in a size smaller than 3/4 inch in horizontal length. Exceptions require approval.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space ensures that the area surrounding the logo remains uncluttered. The clear zone is integral to maintaining the brand's integrity and maximizing visibility.

**Notes:**

- The document contains strict instructions not to create items bearing the logo without corporate approval. Deviations from the standards are to be avoided under all circumstances.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The logo is presented in vertical and horizontal arrangements with the vertical arrangement prioritized, provided reduction and margin rules are met.
- The maximum permitted reduction for the vertical logo is 12.5mm or 48px.
- The maximum permitted reduction for the horizontal logo is 6.5mm or 28px.
- The clear space around the vertical and horizontal logos is defined and must be equivalent to the height of the letter 'C' in the logo, maintained perimetrically.
- The construction grid for the vertical logo defines proportions with dimensions of 5x, 2.5x, and 1.25x. No variation to the grid is allowed.
- The construction grid for the horizontal logo defines proportions with dimensions of 6x, 3x, and 0.5x. No variation to the grid is allowed.
- Additional logo color variations include corporate and non-corporate colors, logos on color backgrounds, and negative (reversed) logos.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Black, CanabayGold, and White. Black: Pantone Process Black, CMYK 0,0,0,100, RGB 0,0,0, HEX #000. CanabayGold: Pantone 875, CMYK 33,48,63,30, RGB 143,109,79, HEX #8F6D4F. White: CMYK 0,0,0,0, RGB 255,255,255, HEX #FFF.
- For sub-brands like Canabay Houses, colors include Black, Houses DeepGreen (Pantone 569, CMYK 90,9,60,15, RGB 0,136,114, HEX #008872), Houses LightGreen (Pantone 3255, CMYK 48,0,25,0, RGB 143,206,202, HEX #8FCECA), and White.
- For Coral House, additional colors include DeepCoral (Pantone 875, CMYK 0,100,46,27, RGB 185,0,100, HEX #B90064) and LightCoral (Pantone 177, CMYK 0,40,43,5, RGB 242,146,139, HEX #F2928B).
- For Ocean House, colors include DeepOcean (Pantone 307, CMYK 100,33,0,30, RGB 0,120,179, HEX #0078B3) and LightOcean (Pantone 319, CMYK 49,5,0,20, RGB 104,195,205, HEX #68C3CD).
- Similarly, specific colors and HEX codes are outlined for Surf House, Beach House, Captiva House, and Casa del Mar with respective associated palettes.

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate typography uses the Berlingske Serif font family, available in weights Light, Regular, Medium, Demibold, Bold, Extrabold, and Black, along with their italic counterparts. It is versatile for text and titles, including logo use.
- The Gotham HTF typeface serves as another corporate font. It includes weights from Thin to Ultra, offering a range of expressive tones.
- Web typography consists of Montserrat as the sans-serif font (weights Thin to Black and corresponding italics) and Playfair Display as the serif font (weights Regular, Bold, and Black, along with italic styles).

**Spacing Layout:**

- For the symbol, clear space rules dictate a margin of 1/5 of the symbol's height, perimetric.

**Notes:**

- This manual emphasizes the importance of adhering to strict guidelines for all logo arrangements, including vertical/horizontal layouts and sub-brand color schemes.

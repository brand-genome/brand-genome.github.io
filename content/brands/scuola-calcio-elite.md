## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The logo consists of two elements: the FIGC commercial shield and the 'Scuola Calcio Élite - Settore Giovanile e Scolastico' logotype. The accent on the 'E' is the distinctive feature of the logo.
- The negative version of the logo is illustrated.
- Examples of correct reproduction of the logo in black and white, color gradients, and two-color printing are provided.
- Clear space (area of exclusion): The space around the logo should be equivalent to 'X', determined by the diameter of the FIGC sphere within the shield.
- Prohibited uses include: modifying the composition or proportions of the logo, distorting its appearance, changing typefaces or colors, and associating it with commercial logos.

**Color Information:**

- Azzurri Blue (Pantone: 2935 C, CMYK: C100 M50 Y0 K7, RGB: R0 G87 B184, HEX: #0057b8)
- Pointsettia Red (Pantone: 185 C, CMYK: C0 M100 Y90 K0, RGB: R228 G0 B43, HEX: #e4002b)
- Bright Green (Pantone: 7482 C, CMYK: C90 M0 Y100 K0, RGB: R0 G159 B77, HEX: #009f4d)
- Italian White (RGB: R255 G255 B255, HEX: #ffffff)
- Team Gold (Pantone: 4525 C, CMYK: C10 M15 Y50 K20, RGB: R197 G183 B131, HEX: #c5b783)

**Typography Information:**

- The primary typeface is 'FIGC Azzurri' created exclusively for the Italian Football Federation. Available styles: Light, Regular, Bold, and Italic.
- Arial Narrow is used as a substitute typeface when 'FIGC Azzurri' is unavailable, in weights: Light, Regular, and Bold.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The clear space rule dictates a margin of space equivalent to 'X' (diameter of the FIGC sphere within the shield). No other graphic elements or logos should encroach upon this area.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand emphasizes visual identity through consistent use of institutional colors and adherence to the provided guidelines.

**Notes:**

- The affiliated soccer school must strictly follow the rules indicated in this manual. Non-recognized soccer schools are not authorized to use the logo.
- Affiliated soccer schools are required to ensure the legibility and correct application of the logo, following its usage guidelines strictly.

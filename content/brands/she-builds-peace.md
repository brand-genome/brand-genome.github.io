## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The She Builds Peace logo mark consists of the words 'she builds peace' in lowercase to communicate warmth, accessibility, and inclusivity. The tagline 'Stand With Women Peacebuilders' is in uppercase to communicate authority and a call to action. Typography used: Avant Garde (geometric sans-serif). The icon represents a Simurgh, a mythical Persian bird symbolizing unity.
- Logo variations include full-color, full-color on blue, black & white, and black & white on black. These versions are suited to different uses based on background color and printing processes.
- Logo variations are also available without the tagline for smaller sizes or when the tagline appears prominently elsewhere. Minimum size information: 1 inch or 96 pixels wide. Clear space requirements equal to the height and width of the letter 'e' in 'she builds peace'.
- Guidelines for incorrect logo usage: avoid distortions, rotations, and placement on non-brand colors.
- Lockup of She Builds Peace logo with ICAN logo: visually balanced in size and aligned at the baseline.
- Co-branding guidelines for ICAN and She Builds Peace alongside partner logos: equal importance and alignment to baseline. Spacing between logos equals the width of the She Builds Peace logo mark.

**Color Information:**

- Campaign color palette inspired by ICAN's palette. Includes dark and light blues for cool, calming tones, and reds/oranges/yellows for urgent and actionable tones.
- Gradients used in logo text and icon: blue and gold gradients symbolize positive change and progress.

**Typography Information:**

- Headline, subhead, and callout fonts: Poppins Bold, Medium, Regular. Body copy and attribution fonts: Roboto Bold, Regular. Both are Google Fonts and free to use/distribute.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone for the She Builds Peace campaign: strong, pragmatic, thoughtful, encouraging, accepting, and proud. Emphasizes that peace is actively created.

**Notes:**

- Background context and narrative: highlights stories of women peacebuilders leading efforts in resiliency, resolution, and recovery amid global conflicts. Encourages broader global support.

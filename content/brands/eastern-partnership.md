## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The Summit logotype comprises three main elements: the Generic illustration, the acronym with the full name, the specific event ('Summit 20XX'), and the European Union flag. The date in the logotype changes annually as per the event year.
- Minimum clear space definition for the Summit logotype: The surrounding area must remain open and uncluttered, with clear space maintained relative to the European Union flag size.
- The minimum size of the Summit logotype is specified as 45mm in width to ensure legibility.
- The black version of the Summit logotype is designed for use on white and light backgrounds for print materials.
- The Summit logotype should not be used on dark or colored backgrounds as shown in examples of incorrect usage.
- Correct usage rules for the Summit logotype: It should be used only on white or very light backgrounds while maintaining the minimum clear space.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The Summit logotype should adhere to specific spacing requirements with a minimum clear space around the logo area. The clear space is relative to the dimensions of the European Union flag element within the logo.

**Notes:**

- The EaP visual identity guidelines include ready-made templates for Summit and side events. These templates and detailed guidelines are available for download on the Directorate-General for Neighbourhood and Enlargement Negotiations (DG NEAR) website.
- An example of a website link provided for visual identity guidelines and templates: https://ec.europa.eu/neighbourhood-enlargement/neighbourhood/eastern-partnership/visual-identity.

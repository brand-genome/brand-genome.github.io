## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Various logo variations including Parent Logo, Acronym Logo, Star Designation Logos, and Bulk Packaging Logos are shown. Logos include 1-Star, 2-Star, 3-Star, and 4-Star Logos.
- Minimum logo size requirements: Print Minimum is 0.39 inches / 1.0 cm; Digital Minimum is 30px at 72dpi.
- Clear space rule requires a distance equal to or greater than the width of the 'X' (distance between the fish eye and tip).
- Prohibited uses include stretching, adding shadows, changing text placement, using inappropriate colors, and placing on busy photographs.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Blue color specifications: CMYK (100, 52, 0, 0), Pantone (2935 C), RGB (0, 84, 164), HEX (#0055A4).

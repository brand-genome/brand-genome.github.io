## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The Ocean Decade logo must always integrate all components (waves, years, title) and maintain a horizontal alignment. It prohibits vertical variants and simplified versions such as logomarks. Logo must retain its strong identity as a singular unit.
- Preferred logo color version: Full-color aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals. Black monochrome version is for white/light grey backgrounds; white monochrome version for dark/grey backgrounds. Formats available include EPS, AI, PDF, PNG, and JPEG.
- The official logo comprises waves, lettering, and circular dynamism. It symbolizes the Ocean Decade's dynamism and science inclusivity. Formats include EPS, AI, PDF, PNG, and JPEG. Alterations are prohibited.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes Oceans Decade Teal (#005C6D, Pantone 7470, RGB: 2, 92, 109, CMYK: 93, 52, 44, 21), and Oceans Decade Blue (#00A7D9, Pantone 2995, RGB: 0, 167, 217, CMYK: 74, 15, 3, 0). Helps emphasize consistent brand association with ocean and science.
- Secondary color palette features additional seven colors like Dark Blue (#1A3668, Pantone 534, RGB: 226, 54, 104), Bright Red (#EA1D2D, Pantone 1788, RGB: 234, 29, 45), Mid Blue (#007DBB), and others labeled for accents and differentiation. Specified to enhance design emphasis without alterations.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography section highlights DIN Pro (official typeface) and Arial (alternative typeface). Design hierarchy adheres to clear roles and sizes indicated on promotional items and layouts.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Promotional prints like magazines, brochures must display the Ocean Decade logo prominently on covers. Materials should demonstrate environmental protection like recycled paper of grammages 80-400 g/m² worldwide.
- Social media usage prioritizes simplified logo versions due to restricted space. Official hashtag: #OceanDecade, related hashtags include #OceanScience and #SDG14. Linked official accounts: @UNoceandecade across platforms, UNESCO mentions encouraged.
- Video production mandates animated transition logos for releases, inserted at movie start or end with strict adherence to availability formats. Duration: 5 seconds.

**Brand Voice:**

- The Ocean Decade logo embeds dynamism with symbolic circularity and brush strokes denoting strength. Representation focuses inclusive scientific collaboration, sustainability goals alignment, and reversing negative oceanic trends.

**Notes:**

- Appendices highlight logo short-term authorization specific to promotional events (4 weeks limit). Contact: UNESCO Communications Team at ioclogos@unesco.org for requests on valid usage permissions.
- General rules for UN Decade branding restrict usage matching non-fundraising, illustrative sharing efforts with aligned sustainability indicators. Institutional promotions require UNESCO IOC approval.

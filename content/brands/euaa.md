## Brand Guidelines

### 2022

**Logo Information:**

- The official EUAA logo includes the acronym 'EUAA', the full name 'European Union Agency for Asylum', and a designed symbol. Minimum size for the logo is specified as 25 mm in length. The logo must appear on all official materials and adhere to the guidelines in the document.
- The logo is available for both digital and print use. The Blue and Yellow color components represent the colors of the European Union.
- Clear space/safe zone around the logo is shown. The area surrounding the logo should be free from other elements, ensuring a clear and consistent appearance.
- Logo versions include: - Official logo (primary colors with blue and yellow) - Dark background version - Black & white and negative versions - Co-branding and visibility logos.
- The logo should not be distorted, shaded, have opacity changed, or color altered. Outlining the logo is also prohibited. These are specified as 'Not to do' examples for logo usage.

**Color Information:**

- The color palette is divided into main and complementary colors. Official logo colors are: Blue (R36 G35 B76, C100 M97 Y38 K35) and Yellow (R253 G197 B2, C0 M24 Y94 K0).
- Additional publication color codes are provided in CMYK. Examples include: C0 M24 Y94 K0, C40 M97 Y28 K24, and C0 M89 Y84 K0.

**Typography Information:**

- Font family used for the logo is not explicitly mentioned. However, 'Font style for publication' includes specified fonts for body and heading levels.
- For business cards, the font 'Proxima Nova' is used with weights as follows: Bold (14pt), Regular (10pt), Condensed Regular (9pt), and Condensed SemiBold (9pt). These sizes are provided as an example template.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery used for covers includes the main graphic elements of the logo (arrow and square). The layout is flexible but must ensure it does not interfere with face objects within photographs. Standard colors are blue, yellow, and white.

**Spacing Layout:**

- For letterheads, a grid system enforces 25mm margin spacing on all sides.
- For co-branding back covers, a clear separation of 2x distance must be maintained between logos.
- For templates like factsheets and envelopes, fixed grids are used. Envelope backs maintain a layout with a centered address and a clear design system.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone emphasizes coherence with the EUAA corporate identity and highlights creating a distinctive, professional look aligned with organizational values.

**Notes:**

- QR code guidelines emphasize consistent positioning on the lower-right corner of back covers, avoiding placement inside other document sections.
- Email signature templates are to be strictly followed, including Calibri font (Bold 11pt for headings, Regular 11pt for details). Logo dimensions provided: .jpg 362mm x 180mm.

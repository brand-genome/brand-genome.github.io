## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Leading Through Excellence logo serves as a visual foundation of the APSU brand; it can be adapted to focus on various aspects of APSU's core foundations. The logo can also be paired with the primary University lettermark for more focused messaging. It is important to match all aspects of logo examples if creating new versions.
- Logo placement examples include pairing with advertising materials such as flyers, brochures, banners, and PowerPoint templates.

**Color Information:**

- APSU color palette includes Red (Pantone 200), Black, and Silver Gray (Pantone 429). Detailed specifications: Red - CMYK: 0, 100, 65, 15; RGB: 196, 30, 58; HTML: #C41E3A. Black - CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 100; RGB: 0, 0, 0; HTML: #000000. Silver Gray - CMYK: 6, 0, 0, 34; RGB: 173, 175, 170; HTML: #ADAFAA.
- Color standards apply to all APSU logos and materials; color placement and specifications cannot be altered.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family used for APSU materials is Gotham. Heading and body typography examples include: Gotham Bold for headings and subheads, Gotham Light Black for body, Gotham Regular for PowerPoint elements. Flyer quotes are set in 10/12 Gotham, while body copy is set in 10/13 Gotham Light Black.
- Additional typographic usage includes Revolution Gothic Bold in brochures. Subheads use Revolution Gothic in Red color, body copy examples are included with leading reasoning.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery guidelines emphasize large, high-quality visuals dominating space for materials such as brochures, flyers, and advertising materials. Images can include transparent overlays for copy treatment, and bleed images are preferred where possible.
- Photography style includes a single large image dominating the space, with minimal border iconography. Advertising and marketing emphasize bold placement.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Brochure examples with layout specifications: External borders measure .625 inches bleeding off the top edge; Front cover includes Leading Through Excellence campaign logo centered in red full-bleed bar serving as foundation content guidelines.
- Spacing and alignment in PowerPoint templates include angular footer designs with logos except on title pages.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone and ethos are described as bold, direct, and honest, reflecting APSU’s theme Leading Through Excellence. Avoid hyperbolic or unsubstantiated claims; communication must emphasize a clear and direct narrative grounded in APSU values.
- Messaging Ethos emphasizes student personality: APSU students are bold, confident, and seek excellence. Key example phrases include 'We make more out of opportunities because we expect more out of ourselves.'

**Notes:**

- Critical guidelines for APSU include rigid color standards that cannot have variations, matching logo usage exactly as specified where paired (advertising, digital, print). Ethos principles guide messaging practices heavily towards individual empowerment and community unity.
- Contact APSU Public Relations for permissions or questions related to logos and guidelines. Details: Office, Browning Room 205, Clarksville.

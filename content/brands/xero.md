## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The Xero partner brand guidelines contain a series of guidelines and examples of how to display Xero brand assets on a range of mediums, including logos and badges.
- Information on file formats for logos and badges such as EPS, PNG, and PDF, as well as minimum sizes for print (12.5mm height excluding clear space) and screen (40px height excluding clear space).
- Rules for the space around the badge, which should have a minimum clear space equal to the width of the 'x' in the Xero logo.
- Preferred colored version of certification badges on light backgrounds and white versions on darker backgrounds. Instructions to keep the badge smaller than the business logo for correctness.
- Usage rules for partner badges and certification ribbons ensuring proper spacing and avoidance of layout hierarchy issues.

**Color Information:**

- Xero Blue: HEX #13BCE1, RGB 19/189/225, CMYK C#100, M#5, Y#0, K#0, and PMS 306C. White: HEX #FFFFFF, RGB 255/255/255, CMYK C#0, M#0, Y#0, K#0.
- Correct use of partner and certification badge colors, highlighting Xero Blue, Partner Blue, and alternative white versions.
- Certification ribbons: Slate — RGB 20/20/50, HEX #141432; Black — RGB 0/0/0, HEX #000000. Alternative white versions for dark backgrounds.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Recommendation for placing badges over photos with minimal detail or out-of-focus areas. Ensuring photography remains professional and supports messaging.
- Use clear, professional photography for print advertising, ensuring logo and badge placement adheres to hierarchy guidelines.
- Social media posts should balance partner badge size against visual clarity without interfering with imagery or messaging.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing rules for partner badge and certification ribbon: minimum clear space between them is twice the width of the 'X' in the Xero logo. Ribbon height cannot exceed one third of the partner badge height.

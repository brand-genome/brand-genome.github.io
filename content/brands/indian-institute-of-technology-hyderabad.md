## Brand Guidelines

### 2011

**Logo Information:**

- The standard logo consists of two parts: logo mark on top and centrally aligned text at the bottom.
- Logo should adhere to dimensions and proportions for consistent reproduction. Distortion or deformation is prohibited.
- Logo must have central alignment for text, aligning text to logo mark following specified proportions.
- Short versions of the logo include abbreviations in Roman and Devnagari scripts.
- Horizontal configuration logo is provided as an alternative option.
- Usage examples include logo on black and white backgrounds, grey background, and colored backgrounds.
- File formats available: EPS, AI, PDF, CDR for print, and JPEG, GIF, PNG for digital usage.

**Color Information:**

- Color schemes for logo include CMYK breakdowns for Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Black (various percentages listed).

**Typography Information:**

- Typography specifications: Central alignment for text. Typefaces used include Frutiger 67 Bold Condensed and CDAC GIST Yogesh Bold.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rule specified: surrounding clear space must be at least 1/8th the height of the logo.

**Brand Voice:**

- The logo design philosophy is rooted in the ideas of 'Innovations and Inventions,' fostering creativity, research, and intellectual expansion.
- Mission reflects the foundation of IIT Hyderabad—ambitions for higher knowledge, scientific discovery, and technological creation.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The 'India Handloom' brand has a primary logo format. Detailed guidelines advise using the logo in vector formats such as Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Corel, QuarkXpress to ensure no pixelation or diffusion.
- Logo minimum size is specified as 2.5 cm to maintain proportion and clarity in design.
- Clear-space rules state that spacing around the logo must be 'X', and at least half 'X' must be maintained around the edges.
- Logo variations include greyscale, black-and-white, and reversed versions as demonstrated.
- Unacceptable usage of the logo includes examples of distorted, tilted, rotated, or color-inconsistent logos.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors include Orange (CMYK: 0, 65, 100, 0; RGB: 238, 115, 11), Green (CMYK: 75, 0, 100, 0; RGB: 62, 171, 55), and Blue (CMYK: 100, 85, 0, 0; RGB: 43, 58, 140).
- Text colors: Headlines use brand blue, Subheads use any brand color, and Body text is strictly 100% black.
- Greyscale logo uses gray tones (K: 60 or 40, 100). Black-and-white usage maintains 100K for black areas.

**Typography Information:**

- Font: Helvetica Neue Bold is used for Headlines, sized at 22 points with Auto leading.
- Font: Helvetica Neue Regular is used for Subheads, sized at 14 points with Auto leading.
- Font: Helvetica Neue Light is used for body text, sized at 9.5 points with Auto leading.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Images should depict cheerfulness and positive moods.
- Product images should be shot in an engaging and visually appealing manner.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grid and margin guidelines include stipulating breathing space around the logo in ads and collaterals.
- Ensure that proportions are maintained, especially for logos placed on white backgrounds in banners and standoff displays.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand aligns with the Prime Minister's 'Zero defect and Zero effect' vision, emphasizing quality and environmentally friendly practices.
- The brand manual focuses on reflecting India's traditions, emphasizing uniqueness and quality of handloom products.

**Notes:**

- The brand emphasizes creating global markets for Indian handloom products, offering both economic benefits to Indian weavers and authenticity to consumers.
- The manual includes specific sections on sub-branding, stationery, and sales collateral detail — these sections emphasize distinct branding and uniformity.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Graphic standards guide emphasizes consistent use of LU Athletics logos including primary and secondary identifiers. Logos must not be altered in any way.
- The LU Athletics marks are available in multiple versions: Full Color, Black & White, and Red & White. File formats include PNG and EPS.
- Examples show placement of LU Primary Athletics marks on various backgrounds, including colored, photographic, and complex. Built-in outlines ensure compatibility. Exceptions noted for white versions on inappropriate backgrounds.
- Secondary marks (Cardinal Head, LU Monogram, LU Horizontal) are complementary identifiers meant for decorative or retail use and not as primary identifiers.
- All secondary marks are adaptable to color fields and complex backgrounds. Guidelines for Red & White, All White, Full Color, and other variations provided.
- Safe zones for LU Athletics marks are defined as 1/4 the total height of the logo, applied uniformly on all sides.
- Minimum size requirements ensure clear legibility for LU Athletics marks. Larger sizes are preferred.
- List of logo violations includes improper resizing, color alterations, unapproved text, and inappropriate placements on backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors are Lamar Red (Pantone 199, CMYK: 0C - 100M - 72Y - 0K, RGB: 220R - 0G - 50B) and White.
- Support colors include Black, Lamar Gold (Pantone 137, CMYK: 0C - 35M - 90Y - 0K, RGB: 252R - 176G - 52B) and Lamar Green (Pantone 374, CMYK: 24C - 0M - 57Y - 0K, RGB: 200R - 223G - 142B).
- Application guidelines specify color use for one-color or multi-color printing on retail merchandise or apparel. All branding must match LU official colors wherever applicable.

**Typography Information:**

- Lamar Bold: A custom font designed exclusively for LU Athletics, used in marks.
- Bank Gothic: A clean and versatile typeface used in branding and retail settings.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Safe zones are defined around Athletics marks as always being 1/4 of the logo's total height. Visual elements or edges should not intrude into this space.

**Notes:**

- LU Athletics trademarks (e.g., Cardinal Head, Lamar University Cardinals) must adhere strictly to trademark guidelines and include (R) marks where applicable.

- Legal note: All logos and marks are property of Lamar University and may only be used with prior written permission.

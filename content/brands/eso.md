## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Combination of ESO Supernova logo and other logos should leave horizontal free space of at least a third of the width of the logo. Both logos should have the same height. Minimum free space: 1/3 of logo width.
- Minimum logo width: 20 mm for readability of lettering. If below 20 mm, use the icon (rotating stars) separately from text.
- Acceptable logo variations for the ESO Supernova logo: corporate color Pantone 3005, black (K=100%), white outline for dark backgrounds, and RGB color (R:0, G:119, B:190).
- Prohibited use cases for the logo: Do not place it on a background with the same color or a similar shade. Ensure contrast is above 50%. Do not alter proportions or remove elements.
- Logo elements explanation: The lettering uses Helvetica Neue LT Com in weights 75 Bold for 'ESO' and 35 Thin for accompanying text.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color for ESO Supernova: Pantone 3005, CMYK: C=100%, M=37%, Y=0%, K=0%; RGB: R=0, G=119, B=190; HEX code: #0077be; RAL: RAL 5015. Other colors: White (RGB: 255, 255, 255, HEX: #ffffff, K=0%) and Black (RGB: 0, 0, 0, HEX: #000000, K=100%).
- Flag colors: Pantone 3005 printed on a white background. Flag dimensions: 250 cm × 150 cm.
- Gray: RAL 7021, Blue: Pantone 3005 used for building signage systems.

**Typography Information:**

- Typeface for ESO branding and visual identity: Helvetica Neue LT Com family. Recommended weights: 35 Thin, 25 Ultra Light for titles; 45 Light for body text with minimum size of 9pt; 65 Medium for emphasis. Line spacing table provided: e.g., 9pt = 3.75mm spacing.
- Example usage of Helvetica Neue LT Com 35 Thin for headlines.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography and imagery incorporation not explicitly detailed; some applications like lamp post flags and merchandise allow logo use purely as graphic design.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Stationery: Place logos and text consistently at the upper left corner with equal margins regardless of envelope size.
- Combination of ESO branding logos requires free space of at least one-third of logo width between them when placed side-by-side.

**Brand Voice:**

- Ensure designs maintain consistent tone: avoid shadows or effects on print logos.

**Notes:**

- Merchandise and stationery usage allow standalone ESO Supernova logo use for branding purposes.

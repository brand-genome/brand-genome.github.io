## Brand Guidelines

### 2012

**Logo Information:**

- The Mankato Brandmark is the graphic nucleus of marketing communications. Several components detailed (wordmark, symbol, colors including Midnight Blue, Sky Blue, Solar Orange, Sweet Pink, Apple Green, Ivy Green).
- Clearspace is defined as the height of the letter 'a' from the wordmark. Minimum clear space required between the brandmark and other edges.
- Minimum sizes detailed for the Mankato Brandmark and wordmark with and without taglines. Measurements listed (e.g., 5/8", 9/16" for dimensions).
- Incorrect usage examples: No rearrangement, color alteration, or background textures violating clear space. No halos, gradient blends, drop shadows. Placement only on white/off-white textures.

**Color Information:**

- The brand color palette includes 6 Pantone colors: Midnight Blue, Sky Blue, Sweet Pink, Solar Orange, Apple Green, Ivy Green. CMYK, RGB, HEX codes provided along with tints.
- Black and white applications: Single color versions reproduced with screen percentages of black. Restrictions detailed.
- Two color versions with dominant base color Midnight Blue. Both variations demonstrated.

**Typography Information:**

- The tagline font is derived from Annifont Italic (customized). Formata font family specified for print. Tahoma or Wayfinding Sans Condensed Bold provided for web or vehicular signage.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery must be professional, simple, energetic, and vivid in color. Activities such as biking, trails, live entertainment emphasized. Overlays for reversed text supported.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Co-branding rules: Inline pairing of logos centered either vertically or horizontally. Distance equals width of the letter 'n' from the Mankato wordmark. Height restrictions applied.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tagline 'Now Playing' captures Mankato's essence as a vibrant, culturally enriching destination with bike-friendly trails and a lively downtown. Reflects tourism and lifestyle perspective.

**Notes:**

- Special treatments for wordmark reproduction in rare cases. Colors can be applied creatively within the palette but restricted to specific occasions.
- Application examples for coffee cups, street banners, signage, brochures, apparel to illustrate recommended design styles.

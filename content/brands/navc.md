## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Full color and one-color versions are shown for the NAVC primary logo. The logo is proprietary and should not be changed or amended in any manner. Full-color logo details include Dark Blue (Pantone PMS 2748 C, RGB: R 14, G 34, B 84, HTML: #003366), Bright Blue (Pantone PMS 299C, RGB: R 0, G 170, B 231, HTML: #00a9e7), and Orange (Pantone PMS 151C, RGB: R 245, G 130, B 32, HTML: #f5821f). One-color logo is shown in Black.
- Minimum size for the logo: Do not reduce the logo width below 1 inch to avoid legibility issues. Clear space rule established: Minimum acceptable clear zone (x) outlined surrounding the logo.
- Usage rules for patterned or photo backgrounds: Use the logo in knocked-out solid white on such backgrounds.
- Sub-brand logos for NAVC are shown, including Conference, Industry Services, Institute, and OnCampus. All sub-logos incorporate the line "The North American Veterinary Community."
- Icons are used to represent NAVC benefits. Five primary icons are identified: Connections, Knowledge, Innovation, Discovery, Skills. The icons are color-coded for sub-brands and general use.
- Ready-made logo lockups are developed for combining logo, tagline, and identity elements. These three major visual identity elements can be used individually or combined.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include: Dark Blue (Pantone PMS 2748C, Process: C100/M95/Y2/K10, RGB: R14/G34/B84, HTML: #003366), Bright Blue (Pantone PMS 299C, Process: C86/M8/Y0/K0, RGB: R0/G170/B231, HTML: #00a9e7), Orange (Pantone PMS 151C, Process: C0/M60/Y100/K0, RGB: R245/G130/B32, HTML: #f5821f). Secondary colors utilized for sub-brands and highlights include Lavender (Pantone PMS 528C), Gray (Pantone Cool Gray 10 C), Pink (Pantone PMS 223C), Yellow (Pantone PMS 109C).

**Typography Information:**

- Bryant font is the primary company font to be used for headlines and other prominent text. Variants include: Bold, Medium, Regular, Light. Bryant communicates a friendly, inviting, modern tone with wide letterforms and rounded corners.
- Avenir is the secondary company font intended for body copy. Preferred color is very dark gray (85% black). Sanchez font is the accent font for subheadlines, pull-quotes, bylines, and highlighting short passages.
- Raleway font is designated for web typography, with Raleway bold used for titles and navigation headings, and Raleway regular for body copy.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography guidelines emphasize veterinary professionals, pet owners, and animals in natural or real environments. Photos should have dramatic close cropping. Avoid white backgrounds, snapshots, and subjects with their backs to the camera.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space surrounding the logo is defined as minimum clear zone (x), ensuring no crowding by text or other elements.

**Brand Voice:**

- Taglines for NAVC and specific sub-brands include: "Better Care For Animals Everywhere" (Dark Blue), "Better Care Through Collaboration" (Bright Blue), "Better Care Through Precision" (Orange), "Better Care Through Commitment" (Lavender). Taglines should always use Bryant Bold in all caps.
- Messaging guidance emphasizes the mission to provide world-class continuing professional development and promises focused on passion, listening, horizon expansion, high-quality experiences, and driving veterinary care advancement.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The Fletcher Steel logo is bold and directional, representing strength. The tagline 'Building on Trust' is trademarked.
- Logo color variations include two-color options as primary and single-color variants. Reversed logo versions are for darker backgrounds.
- Clear space of at least the height and width of ‘e’ must be maintained from surrounding elements to ensure visibility and legibility.
- Minimum logo size is 40mm wide with the tagline and 25mm wide without the tagline.
- Company logos should be separated using a grey vertical line when displayed together.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors are Ink Blue (PMS 3405, HEX: #1fab6b, CMYK: 100, 0, 80, 0, RGB: 32, 171, 108) and Bright Green (CMYK: 100, 0, 80, 0, RGB: 32, 171, 108).
- Secondary colors include Mid Grey (HEX: #939597, CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 50, RGB: 147, 149, 152) and Light Grey (HEX: #e6e7e8, CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 10, RGB: 230, 231, 232).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font is Apex New in Book and Medium weights for brand communications.
- Digital font is Arial, available in Regular and Bold weights for electronic applications.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Greyscale images unify product shots while color images are used for dynamic, graphic hero shots showcasing finished products and people.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Business cards are sized at 55 x 90mm and printed in CMYK on offset 350gsm paper. Font specifications: Apex New Medium 16pt, Apex New Medium 7pt, and Apex New Book 7pt.
- Letterhead specifications: A4 size, CMYK print.
- Pull-up banners are designed for promotional use at events, highlighting the company's product and service scope.
- Display suites offer various large-format display options.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tagline 'Building on Trust' is a core value and reflects the company’s commitment to reliability, quality, and customer relationships.
- Email signatures must follow styling guidelines: Arial Bold for name and title, Arial Regular for details.

**Notes:**

- Brand video content includes text animation over visuals, examples of product handling and distribution, and styles for logo reveal animations.

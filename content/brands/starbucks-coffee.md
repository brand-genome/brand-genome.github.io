## Brand Guidelines

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- The primary WPS logo consists of the Siren symbol and the phrase 'We Proudly Serve.' The WPS wordmark is composed of the phrase 'We Proudly Serve' vertically aligned with the Starbucks wordmark. Variants include U.S., International, and Canada (dual language) logos.
- Incorrect usage examples include: rearranging or resizing elements of the wordmark, stretching or altering the logo, overlapping the logo on graphics, combining primary logo with wordmark, and changing logo color.
- File format references include variations such as WPS wordmark in black and white, primary WPS logos, and dual-language options. Primary logo and wordmark styles are available for U.S., Canada, and International markets.
- Clear space requirements for primary WPS logo: 25% of the Siren logo diameter. Clear space for wordmark: 150% of the height of the 'B' letterform.
- Minimum size requirements: Siren minimum width for print: 0.375" (9.5mm), web: 50 pixels. Wordmark minimum width for print: 0.5" (12.7mm), web: 200 pixels.

**Color Information:**

- Preferred color usage includes Starbucks Green (Pantone 3425 C, CMYK: 100/0/78/42, RGB: 0/112/74, HEX: #00704A) and white, used on light or white backgrounds.
- Optional color usage includes one-color or two-color printing (Black: PMS Black, CMYK: 0/0/0/100, RGB: 0/0/0, HEX: #000000) available when Starbucks Green cannot be used.
- Backgrounds should be light-colored whenever possible to ensure sufficient contrast with the Starbucks Green. Avoid using logos on backgrounds that do not provide sufficient contrast.
- Incorrect color usage examples include changing logo colors, using inverse Siren colors, adding a ring around the logo, placing the logo within shapes, or using graphic effects and outlines.

**Typography Information:**

- Minimum height of registration marks (® or Ž) is 4 pt Avenir Medium. Print size: 0.04 inches (1mm). Web size: 10 pixels.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Proper placement of logos should avoid any visual confusion between Starbucks and the venue, ensuring Starbucks branding remains secondary to the venue's own branding.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space around the primary WPS logo must be 25% of the Siren logo diameter, and clear space around the wordmark must be 150% of the height of the 'B' letterform.

**Brand Voice:**

- Starbucks brand identity emphasizes high product quality, genuine personal connections, and uplifting experiences. Marketing materials should align with these principles.

**Notes:**

- Unauthorized usage examples include logo application on delivery trucks, street-level branding (e.g., windows, doors), or apparel. Logo may not appear on customer-designed cups, napkins, or co-branded packaging.
- Discounting guidelines prohibit using the WPS logo for discounts (e.g., generic cents-off or free offers). Promotion must not associate Starbucks products with other brands' customer acquisition programs.
- Approval process requires Starbucks Foodservice Marketing Services to review and email artwork approvals or changes within five business days.

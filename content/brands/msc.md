## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The corporate logo proportions are fixed, with measurements specified using X as the unit. Outer proportions: X = 20, text height: X = 4, compass rose height: X = 47, and spacing: X = 8.
- Minimum clear space rules for the logo require X (unit of logo proportions) to maintain space around all sides.
- Corporate logo must use only one color — Pantone 282C (coated paper) or Pantone 282U (uncoated paper). LAB values: L=12, A=1, B=-28; HEX: #000033.
- Positive and negative color versions require specific logo designs for compass rose structure.
- Black and white logo usage is restricted to monochromatic documents like newspapers and faxes.
- Incorrect usage examples highlighted: rotating the logo, squeezing proportions, misaligning text, or using incorrect colors.
- Country logos follow the same fixed proportions but translate 'cruises' into localized languages. List of translations provided.
- Department logos shown include variations for technical departments, management, food & beverage, and multimedia agencies.
- Product logos listed include MSC Yacht Club, MSC Aurea Spa, MSC Excursions, and others, with positive and negative variations provided.

**Color Information:**

- Primary corporate color is Pantone 282C (coated paper) or 282U (uncoated paper). LAB: L=12, A=1, B=-28; HEX: #000033; RGB: R=0, G=0, B=51; CMYK: C=86, M=66, Y=13, K=71.
- Additional colors include Pantone Warm Gray 10C, Pantone Orange 021C, Pantone Metallic 8640C, Pantone 661C, and others, with RGB and CMYK profiles provided.
- Color profiles used: RGB Profile (Adobe RGB 1998) and CMYK Profile (Coated FOGRA39 ISO 12647-2:2004).

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate materials must use MSC GS fonts in Regular, Bold, and Italic styles. For Word documents and email signatures, Verdana Regular is specified.
- For ATL communication, Georgia Regular/Bold is specified for headlines and body text. For BTL and PRESS use, Open Sans Light/Bold is combined with Georgia Regular.
- Web and social media content must use Open Sans SemiBold, Regular, and Bold. Social media explicitly uses Open Sans Regular/Bold.
- CRM materials are specified to use Tahoma Regular/Bold.
- Ship names for technical documents are restricted to Times New Roman Bold Italic. Horizontal layout only. Font color: Pantone 282C or white, depending on the background.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography must focus on unique MSC angles, Mediterranean elegance, sunny moods, and authenticity. Recommended framing includes close-ups of actions and expressions.
- Landscape destination imagery should evoke optimism and show unique, refined angles. Wherever possible, include sea views.
- Food imagery emphasizes human touch, Mediterranean culinary roots, and spontaneity in preparations.
- Ship interiors should be clean and well-lit, highlighting newness and passenger activity.
- Ship exteriors must avoid crowded ports and highlight ambitious, futuristic perspectives.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo proportions and spacing defined by X units. Specific rules for minimum clear space, translations for localized logos, and alignment for horizontal/vertical configurations provided.

**Brand Voice:**

- Key tone values: competence, dedication, respect, happiness, and Mediterranean elegance. Positioning strongly emphasizes hospitality and guest satisfaction.

**Notes:**

- Icons categorized based on onboard activities: entertainment, food & beverage, shopping, spa, excursions, etc.

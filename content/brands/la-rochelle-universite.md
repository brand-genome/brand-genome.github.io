## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The logo comes in versions for light backgrounds (quadri), dark backgrounds, and responsive layouts. A black version is permissible only in exceptional cases with authorization.
- Minimum size for standard version (V1) of the logo is 15mm. If smaller size is required, use the responsive version (V2).
- Logo must be positioned in the top left corner of all documents with margins equal to half of the logo's width. Example: if the logo width is 5cm, position it 2.5cm from the top and left edges.
- Clear space or protection zone must surround the logo. No elements may be attached directly to the blue block of the logo.
- Incorrect usage: elements of the logo must not be distorted, typography modified, colors altered (except black/white versions), or logo elements separated.

**Color Information:**

- Blue color is the primary identity color retained for familiarity. New secondary and institutional colors are introduced for a dynamic environment.

**Typography Information:**

- The logo uses 'Source Sans Pro' typography.

**Imagery Photography:**

- No specific imagery or photography rules detailed, except guidance on using the logo optimally on photographic backgrounds.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grid specifications dictate positioning the logo 2.5 cm from the top and left edges when the logo width is 5 cm.
- The clear space zone ensures no other graphical element interferes with logo visibility.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tagline 'D'ici, on voit + Loin' emphasizes inclusivity and engages, valorizes, and unifies the community.
- The guide encourages identity sharing and uses standardized templates for consistent communication.

**Notes:**

- For logo file access and usage advice, contact the Direction of Communication at communication@univ-lr.fr.
- The graphic design support was developed by Stephane Peneaud (www.stephanepeneaud.com).

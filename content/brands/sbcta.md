## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Construction and clear space for logos is defined, ensuring clear visibility without any invading graphic elements. The clear space is calculated as the height and width of the lowercase letter 's'.
- Logo versions include full logo, logo mark, logotype, black version, and greyscale for various usage scenarios.
- Minimum logo sizes include a minimum height of 0.375 in for smaller uses. Minimum size correctly keeps primary elements visible and legible.
- Dual logo lockup guidelines for documents requiring both SB CTA and SB COG logos. Guidelines include fixed spacing and no independent scaling.
- Placement guidelines for SB CTA logo are described for standard letter-sized layouts.

**Color Information:**

- Primary and secondary color palettes with precise codes for CMYK, Pantone, RGB, and HEX are introduced for consistency. Examples include Sun Orange (#FF6E00), Sky Blue (#047DBA), and Grass Green (#228848).
- Consistency in applying defined agency colors across all media ensures visual cohesion.

**Typography Information:**

- Agency font is Akzidenz-Grotesk BE, providing modern and legible appeal. Substitution for general use includes Arial for standard collateral, such as emails and PowerPoint presentations.
- Typography hierarchy demonstrates sizes and usage for headlines, captions, and other textual emphases.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Custom iconography set created for the SBCTA, including icons for transportation, environmental, and infrastructure themes. Development of new icons must be approved by the Public Affairs Office.
- Blending modes guidelines for imagery include adjusting tint colors and layer styles like 'multiply' for professional cohesiveness.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing rules and layout alignment are essential for consistency in applying logos and preserving visual balance across materials. Particular emphasis placed on maintaining minimum clear spaces and ratios.
- Example layouts for standard stationery, including business cards, ID badges, and thank-you cards, are detailed with dimensions and spacing.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand tone emphasizes a blend of San Bernardino’s landscapes and strengths, creating relatable clean geometries and identity cohesion.
- Use clear, effective visuals and typography to communicate the identity of the agency consistently.

**Notes:**

- The SBCTA brand manual includes agency-specific guidelines for logos, color systems, typography, and layouts. Created December 23, 2016, updated June 2017.
- The document includes agency branding components to maintain cohesive communication and design implementation.

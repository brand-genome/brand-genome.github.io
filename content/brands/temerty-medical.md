## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The official Faculty Signature connects the Temerty Faculty of Medicine with the University of Toronto.
- Clear space for the Faculty Signature equals 50% of the crest height.
- Minimum width for Faculty Signature: 2.5 inches (print) and 180 pixels (digital usage).
- Incorrect usage of the signature includes altering the crest, resizing elements independently, stretching, or color deviations beyond Pantone 655.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: U of T Blue (Pantone 655). Secondary color: Light Blue (Pantone 633).
- Signature brand colors are Pantone 655 and Pantone 633. The reverse wordmarks are used in limited scenarios, like dark or busy backgrounds.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface is Trade Gothic, complemented by Bembo as the secondary typeface. Font replacements include Arial and Times New Roman.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Merchandise example illustrations highlight usage with official branding (e.g., Insulin 100 logo). Limited white/blue treatments are reserved.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Custom spacing applied to characters in the wordmark, adjusting proportions and alignment for precision aesthetic.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand highlights legacy excellence, using consistent design themes to connect to the University's premier academic identity.

- Faculty-specific guidelines outline key pillars (notes on maintaining clarity, adaptability, historic brand relationships).

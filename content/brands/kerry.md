## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The Kerry logotype must always be used as the master artwork and should not be re-created. Always use the original digital master artwork.
- Clear space around the logo should use the height of the 'K' in Kerry for isolation. Minimum size of the logo is 25 mm for print and 95 pixels for screen.
- Logo available in standard full color, keyline version with single color (often used as a reversal on solid color backgrounds), and solid version for single color printing (recommended in black, Kerry blue, or metallic on light backgrounds).
- Prohibited logo usages: Do not stretch, squash, rotate, flip colors, change typography scale, modify the box shape, use multi-colors, adjust typography color, use drop shadows, or use screenshots of the logo.
- An approved variation includes the tagline 'Leading to Better' paired with the Kerry logo. Recommended for external communications and document covers. Tagline must not be translated; only regional translation within body copy allowed.
- A Mandarin version of the logo exists without the tagline 'Leading to Better'. The message should instead be conveyed in accompanying text or copy.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color codes for the Kerry logo:
- Blue: CMYK 100,47,0,47; RGB 0,70,119; HEX #004577; Pantone 540U
- Yellow: CMYK 0,34,91,0; RGB 252,177,49; HEX #fbb130; Pantone 137U
- Keyline logo developed for single solid color backgrounds, with recommendations to ensure legibility and contrast.
- Solid logo usage recommended in black, Kerry blue, or metallic colors on light backgrounds for single-color printing.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Keyline logo and solid logo are developed to ensure logo legibility on solid-colored backgrounds or reduced single-color printing scenarios.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clearance area defined using the height of the Kerry logo's 'K' to preserve isolation. Minimum spacing prevents encroachment by text or headlines.
- Tagline pairing guidelines: 'Leading to Better' tagline must follow the cap height as spacing when set near the Kerry logotype.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tagline 'Leading to Better' should appear in English and must not be translated in logo contexts, but can be translated within accompanying body copy by regional teams.
- Mandarin version of Kerry logo conveys brand voice; tagline should be reflected in accompanying copy rather than visual implementation.

**Notes:**

- Contact Melissa Seipel, Visual Brand, for more information or guidance on Kerry branding related inquiries via melissa.seipel@kerry.com.

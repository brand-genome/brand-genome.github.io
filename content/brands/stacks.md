## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Three different color versions of the logo are provided to account for varying color palettes. A 'Co-branding colored' logo is the preferred choice, with light and dark alternatives included for flexibility.
- Spacing rules state that white space around the logo should be approximately the width of the letter 'S' in 'EBSCO'.
- Minimum size guidelines aim to ensure the logo is readable, although adjustments can be based on judgment if perfect conditions aren't met.
- Partner logos should not exceed the size of 'EBSCO'. The partner logo is scaled proportionally to fit specific areas.
- Dedicated improper usage guidelines: do not alter, add effects (e.g., drop shadows), stylize, modify, stretch/skew, change font styles, or add imagery. The logo’s color should not be altered.
- Horizontal logo usage is specifically for email campaign headers.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors defined: Stacks Green (Hex #69bc45, RGB 105,188,69, Pantone 364 U), Stacks Icon Dark Gray (Hex #5e5f5e, RGB 94,95,94, Pantone Cool Gray 9 U), and EBSCO Blue (Hex #002f56).
- Font color options are simple and minimal: white (#FFFFFF) for maximum contrast and black (#000000).
- Brand element colors, inspired by the co-brand logo, include various shades of blue and green with examples like #4974b9 (blue) and #368341 (green).
- Blue tones symbolize EBSCO as a solid foundation, while green tones represent specific Stacks products and section separations as seen in the booklet.

**Typography Information:**

- The brand uses the Montserrat font family, chosen for its versatility on various mediums. Montserrat is free to download from Google Fonts.
- Font variants include Montserrat Extra-Light (for body text), Regular (for bold emphasis), and Black (used with Extra-Light in headings).
- Verdana is suggested as a fallback font if Montserrat cannot be installed, particularly for systems without administrative installation privileges.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Images are designed to display Stacks products in action within devices. Backgrounds or artificial elements should not overshadow the product.
- Google's material design icon library is used with rules ensuring the clear emphasis of icons and alignment of design intent with Stacks branding standards.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Geometric shapes, inspired by Canada’s Rocky Mountains, are included as brand elements to create visual breaks. They should be used sparingly.
- Spacing requirements dictate white space around logos equivalent to the width of the 'S' in the EBSCO logo.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tagline: 'The New Library Experience', positioned below the logo, left-aligned, and in readable font size.
- Writes in a style that reflects a single cohesive brand identity. Text and titles follow APA formatting, avoiding unnecessary capitalization. Necessary emphasis is applied with bold/italics only when fitting.
- Naming Conventions: 'Stacks' (not STACKS), 'Stacks Mobile' (not StacksMobile), and 'Onboarding' (not hyphenated). These conventions ensure proper branding identity across content.

**Notes:**

- Contact for branding help: branding@stacksdiscovery.com
- The brand guidelines also provide detailed map icon and color specifications for different market segments like Academic (orange hat icon), Government (yellow institution icon), etc.

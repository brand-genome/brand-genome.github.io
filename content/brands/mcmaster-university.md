## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

### 2010

**Logo Information:**

- The McMaster brandmark consists of two elements: the McMaster lettering (in Garamond typeface) and the shield (featuring the phoenix). These elements have a fixed relationship and must never be separated, modified, or altered. Must be used in approved colors and font only.
- Do Not modify size, relationship, or position of type and shield; alter colors beyond approved variations; overprint or interfere with the brandmark; add borders or graphics; or separate brandmark elements.

**Brand Voice:**

- McMaster's brand emphasizes an innovative and engaging identity, fostering an intellectually welcoming environment celebrating diversity and research dedication.
- Three core descriptors of McMaster's voice are: Innovative (fostering research and diversity), Engaging (prioritizing relationships across faculty, students, and alumni), and Inspiring (helping others achieve their maximum potential).

**Notes:**

- Brand guidelines must be followed for all print and online communication materials.
- The manual states the importance of consulting the Associate Director, Public Relations (Marketing), for more details on brand application.

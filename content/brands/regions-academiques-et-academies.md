**Typography Information:**

- Spectral is an accompanying typeface for secondary information (e.g., citations, historical text). Marianne is preferred.
- Detailed guidelines for font sizes: headings (14pt Bold), subtexts (10/8pt Regular), customized alignments confirm strict layouts for presentations or letters.

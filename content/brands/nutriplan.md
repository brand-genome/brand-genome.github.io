## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The Nutriplan brand has multiple logo versions: full color (Preferred 1), negative version (Preferred 2), black with retícula for printed materials, and monochromatic versions in positive and negative (black or white).
- The primary logo orientation is vertical. A horizontal logo can be used only when space restrictions apply.
- Icon-only version of the logo can only be used upon explicit approval from marketing.
- Clear space rule: The spacing around the logo (x) should be equal to the height of the letter 'l' in the logo. This ensures visual integrity.
- Minimum size requirement: The logo should not be smaller than 20 mm or 59 pixels in width for clear legibility.
- Incorrect logo application rules include: no distortion, no changes to colors or typography, no application on busy backgrounds, and no detachment of logo components.
- The construction grid is provided for manual reproduction of the logo, but redrawing without the digital file is prohibited.

**Color Information:**

- Brand primary colors include PANTONE® 370C (Green) and PANTONE® 7619C (Red). Their respective values: CMYK C66/M25/Y100/K6, RGB R113/G140/B52, HEX #718B33 (Green); CMYK C16/M82/Y80/K5, RGB R173/G76/B59, HEX #AD4B3A (Red).
- Additional colors used for sub-brands: e.g., PANTONE® 7748C and PANTONE® 7505C for Nutriplan 'Produtor', PANTONE® 7698C and PANTONE® 7490C for 'Vitaplan'. Exact values are provided in respective sections.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font used in Nutriplan brand is PLUTO (modified) in Medium style.
- Additional fonts include: LEWIS HAND (for specific designs and promotional material) and others, depending on sub-brands (e.g., SANS CULOTTES).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Reference to brand image application: Placeholder visuals from the Internet are provided for material alignment, but no specific photography style or mandatory imagery rules are described.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The brand’s clear space rule requires spacing equal to the height of the 'l' in the logo (x) around all sides.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand's tone emphasizes themes of nature, togetherness, sustainability, and prosperity. It promotes themes like growth, family, and connection to nature.

**Notes:**

- All logo files must be used digitally as defined to preserve integrity. Changes or uses outside these guidelines require approval from Nutriplan Marketing.
- Different sub-brands (Produtor, Jardins, Vitaplan, Teraza) have distinct typographic standards and palette colors.

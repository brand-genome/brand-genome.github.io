## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The official logo of the Government of El Salvador (2019-2024) is mandatory in documents issued by institutions under the government.
- Vertical logo version explained, composed of three elements: (a) National Coat of Arms, (b) 14 stars pointing north, and (c) the text 'Gobierno de El Salvador' in uppercase.
- Horizontal logo version is used when the vertical version cannot be employed due to space or visibility constraints.
- Minimum size requirements for the logo: 1.6 cm wide by 1.9 cm tall for print. For digital applications, the minimum size is 85 px wide by 103 px tall.
- Do's and don'ts for logo usage: Do not modify the logo's proportions, composition, or colors. Incorrect usage includes rotating elements, changing element positions, modifying thickness, incorrect scaling, or changing colors.

**Color Information:**

- Corporate color specifications for the emblem: Pantone 432 C (HEX #313945, RGB 49, 57, 69, CMYK 79, 67, 52, 46), Pantone 7604 C (HEX #c9a892, RGB 201, 169, 146, CMYK 22, 33, 41, 0), and Pantone 281 C (HEX #1c1e4d, RGB 29, 31, 77, CMYK 100, 96, 39, 38).
- Applications of the emblem on backgrounds: Pantone 432 C is the standard background color for negative applications.

**Typography Information:**

- The official typeface for headlines is Bembo Std.
- The official typeface for body text is Museo Sans.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photographs used officially by institutions must include the logo of the Ministry and the Government of El Salvador. A white or institutional gray band can be omitted if the photograph design allows.

**Spacing Layout:**

- For letterheads: Margins are 5.29 cm (top), 2.5 cm (bottom), and 2.54 cm (left and right). Watermarks should have 5% transparency, and Museo Sans is recommended for body text with sizes 10-12 pt.

**Notes:**

- For any flexibility or permissions regarding the use of the Government of El Salvador brand, contact the Secretaría de Comunicaciones de la Presidencia.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The HAGS logo is available with the brand promise within a white or black box or just the HAGS letters on a solid color background. When using the logo on light backgrounds, use the black logo. On darker backgrounds, use the reverse, white version.
- Clear space for the logo is defined visually as '1/2 x' on all sides of the logo. The minimum space must always be respected.
- The logo must not be scanned, recreated, or used in part. It must not be stretched, compressed, or otherwise distorted. The typeface embedded in the logo is exclusive to the logo and not used for any other design elements.
- HAGS is a registered trademark. Only use logo versions with the registration symbol '®' included.
- If the logo with letters only is used on a solid color background, it must be paired with one from the approved palette shown on page 35.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: Red (CMYK 1,99,97,13, RGB 204,18,21), Dark Red (CMYK 25,97,100,22, RGB:157,32,12), White (CMYK 0,0,0,0, RGB 255,255,255).
- Additional colors: Green (CMYK 66,0,99,0, RGB 95,187,72), Dark Green (CMYK 88,35,83,24, RGB 19,106,72), Beige (CMYK 8,19,54,21, RGB 191,166,112).
- Neutrals and accents: Yellow (CMYK 6,0,96,0, RGB 246,235,20), Orange (CMYK 3,67,100,0, RGB 238,114,3), Black (CMYK 75,68,57,90, RGB 18,16,17).
- Neutrals (continued): Blue (CMYK 76,24,21,3, RGB 43,149,177), Dark Blue (CMYK 100,76,10,0, RGB 0,83,153), Grey (CMYK 0,0,0,50, RGB 147,149,152).

**Typography Information:**

- HAGS uses Stag and Stag Sans font families for advertising and printed materials. These are compact and provide a contemporary look.
- Arial is the official font for the web, branded presentations, and digital newsletters. Bitter (a Google font) is a permissible alternative for web headlines.
- The typeface embedded in the HAGS logo is exclusive to the logo and should not be used for any other purposes.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Three categories of visuals: (1) Products in action (e.g., kids playing in playgrounds), with focus on genuine and positive depictions, avoiding product misuse. (2) Products in environments or studio settings for clean, dramatic shots. (3) CAD renderings and scale drawings for clarity.
- Images should align with the clean, crisp design ethos of the HAGS brand while forming a strong focal point.
- Photographs involving people require legal clearance and approvals before publication.

**Spacing Layout:**

- 1/2 x minimum clear space around the HAGS logo. Respect this spacing across all applications.
- Ensure appropriate margins for clean layout applications based on grid designs not explicitly detailed.

**Brand Voice:**

- HAGS emphasizes a tone of voice that is genuine, collaborative, educational, creative, and inspirational. The brand values clarity, respect, and a personal touch in all communications.
- The tone of voice combines friendly familiarity with professionalism. It aims to create dialogue and engages with audiences with a warm and helpful attitude.
- Values reflected by HAGS include being genuine, creative, confident without arrogance, and collaborative but not divisive.
- Communications should avoid technical jargon where possible. They should be accessible and convey the enjoyment and benefits of HAGS products.

**Notes:**

- HAGS product archival includes modular systems, sports, and urban designs, supported by a rich legacy in product development with an emphasis on customer solutions.
- Digital platforms (website, email, social media) are central to communication. Centrally created content is made adaptable for regionalized local audiences.
- The glossary provides terms for consistent descriptions. For example, MUGA stands for Multi-Use Games Area, and RPII stands for Register of Play Inspectors International.

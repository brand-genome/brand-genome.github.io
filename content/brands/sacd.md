## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- SACD provides 3 versions of its logo: Institutional Logo, 'Un combat pour la création / sacd.fr' version, and the Monogram logo.
- Clear space rules defined as an 'Espace vital' around the logo to ensure clarity from surrounding elements. This space must remain free of external graphic elements.
- Minimum size specifications: Logo with baseline requires a minimum size of 3 cm, while logo without baseline requires a minimum size of 1 cm.
- Logo usage prohibitions ('Les interdits'). Specific incorrect usage scenarios detailed including distortion, color changes, and inappropriate alterations.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color specifications provided: Pantone 485 C, Quadri (CMYK): M100 / Y100, RGB: R255. Secondary color variant: Pantone Cool Gray 10 C, Quadri: K80, RGB: R78, G78, B78.
- Specific treatment for logo placement on red backgrounds.
- Specific treatment for logo placement on dark backgrounds.
- Black and White color guidelines: Pantone Black (Quadri N100, RGB R0, G0, B0), Pantone Cool Gray 10C (Quadri K80, RGB R78, G78, B78). Adjustment for contrast based on light and dark backgrounds.

**Notes:**

- The guideline mentions that all graphic elements and related queries should be directed to the SACD's communications department for formal inquiries.

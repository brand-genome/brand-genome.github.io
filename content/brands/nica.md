## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The NICA logo portrays one of the first student-athletes to participate in a NICA league as a symbol of youth mountain biking. The NICA wordmark is an alternative approach using the organization’s initials and abbreviated name.
- The logo comprises two key elements: a brandmark and a wordmark. The wordmark is customized based on the metric font. Variations include brandmark alone, brandmark with text, wordmark alone, and a lockup version with both elements.
- Color options for the wordmark include red (PMS 185 C), white, and black. The brandmark version must appear in blue (PMS 285 C) and red (PMS 185 C), or in black and white under specific conditions.
- Guidelines note specific contexts such as using white logos on dark backgrounds and maintaining clarity with white outlines when displaying brandmark on dark backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors for brand visuals include PMS 185 C (red) and PMS 285 C (blue). Alternative options include black and white for specific conditions.

**Notes:**

- The NICA brand guidelines emphasize consistent logo integrity and recommend adherence to outlined rules for all applications.

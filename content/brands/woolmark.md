## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The Woolmark Licensing Brands consist of three logos: Woolmark logo (100% pure new wool), Woolmark Blend logo (50%-99.9% new wool), and Wool Blend logo (30%-49.9% new wool). Each logo indicates specific wool content and performance measures.
- The Woolmark Licensing Brands are comprised of two elements: the relevant Woolmark device and the logotype, both of which must always be placed together in a fixed relationship. Neither element may be used alone.
- The registered trademark symbol (®) must always appear on the bottom right-hand side of Woolmark Licensing Brand logos. Failure to include the symbol is not permitted unless special permission is obtained.
- Minimum size requirements for the logo: Logos must never appear smaller than 10mm in height. For different sizes, logos are available in small size (10mm-20mm) and large size (21mm and above).
- The Woolmark Licensing Brand logos must never be redrawn, modified, adapted, or distorted. They must always retain a proportional scale.
- Correct logo usage examples include black on white/light background and reversed white on black/dark background.

**Color Information:**

- The main Woolmark logos should appear in black on white/light backgrounds or in white on solid dark backgrounds. The logos must not appear in any other colors to ensure legibility.
- The Woolmark Blend and Wool Blend logos should appear in grey shades: Pantone Cool Gray 9U and Pantone 421U respectively. These logos should not appear in other colors, including reversed (white), even on black backgrounds.

**Imagery Photography:**

- It is advised to avoid placing Woolmark Licensing Brand logos on images with varying colors, as this backdrop can affect the logo's clarity.

**Spacing Layout:**

- A clear space must surround the Woolmark Licensing Brand logos on all four sides, using at least half the logo's width as the minimum clear space measurement.

**Notes:**

- The Woolmark and Woolmark Blend logos are certification trademarks in many countries. The Wool Blend logo is a standard trademark.
- The Woolmark Certification Program ensures that products meet content, quality, and testing standards. Licensees must follow these guidelines to use Woolmark logos.
- Legal disclaimers must accompany the Woolmark Licensing Brand logos in all marketing and promotional materials. Disclaimers notify consumers about the trademarks' certification status.

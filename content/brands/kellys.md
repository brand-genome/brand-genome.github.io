## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- Basic shape of the logo symbolizes a wheel, representing motion and dynamics. The letter 'K' in the logo represents a winding road viewed from a perspective.
- Protective zone defined by a square proportional to the logo circle and height of the letter 'e'. This ensures discernment and legibility of the logo; external elements must not interfere within this zone.
- Two basic versions of the logo are allowed: Centered version with circular symbol above the brand name; Horizontal version with symbol next to the brand name.
- Negative logo version is recommended for patterned or textured backgrounds; monochromatic representation allowed if permitted by technology.
- Black-and-white logo versions supported for positive and negative contexts. Includes both centered and horizontal configurations.

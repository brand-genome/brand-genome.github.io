## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The primary logo design references the barrel-vaulted roof of Olympia Grand and represents the venue's dynamic and ever-changing event experiences. The logo may include the strapline 'Inspiring Spaces' when deemed appropriate.
- Monotone logo versions are available in grey and white.
- Minimum size requirement for logo usage is 12mm or the height of 'London' within the logo being 6pt.
- File formats for large transparencies include Adobe Illustrator to preserve details, especially for the fade at the bottom of the arch. The recommended dimensions are 190mm x 170mm for larger applications and 65mm x 78mm for smaller applications.
- Partnership logos are to be displayed in grayscale, separated by a vertical line of the same height as the Olympia London logo. The vertical line has a stroke weight of 1pt.

**Color Information:**

- Primary logo colors include Blue, Purple, and Grey. Blue CMYK: 100/0/30/0; RGB: 0/171/188; Pantone: 3135U/3135C. Purple CMYK: 22/30/0/0; RGB: 195/176/214; Pantone: 2635U/2635C. Grey CMYK: 6/6/0/40; RGB: 155/156/166; Pantone: Cool Grey 7U/C.
- Extended primary palette includes Blue (#004F6C, #00ABBC), Purple (#680160, #524FA1), Pink (#E45A7D, #D70B8C), Green (#00646B, #00A99D), and Grey (#4D4D55).
- Background colors include a primary background Blue (a 12% tint of the primary blue) used for corporate stationery, signage, and folders.
- Secondary background tints are derived from primary colors and are used for illustrations, 'What's On' materials, and the website.

**Typography Information:**

- The primary typeface is 'Brown,' chosen for its modern but classic characteristics. It provides four weights: Thin, Light, Regular, Bold.
- Secondary typeface guidelines for internal use are provided on page 17.
- Brown typeface must be purchased independently by suppliers at £60 from Lineto Foundry.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The exclusion zone around the logo must ensure adequate padding; specifics cited on page 6.

**Notes:**

- All digital files of the logo require Adobe Illustrator format to retain the fade transparency at the bottom of the arch.
- Primary color tints and swatches are available by request from the Olympia London marketing team.

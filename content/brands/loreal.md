## Brand Guidelines

### 2011

**Logo Information:**

- 9 / L’Oréal_Charte graphique
The L’Oréal logo is presented in a double-tone design (black and gold lettering) to evoke corporate distinction. Protective zones and placement rules for optimal logo visibility are established.
- 30 / L’Oréal_Charte graphique
On business cards, the L’Oréal logo maintains immutability in its positioning. It is printed in stamped gold, with Pantone replacement guidelines specified (Pantone 423 in exceptional cases). Offset gold printing is prohibited.
- 31 / L’Oréal_Charte graphique
Corporate business cards reflect L’Oréal's hierarchical structure with the country of operation shown alongside the logo. Strict spacing and layout parameters are maintained.
- 32 / L’Oréal_Charte graphique
Operational division cards highlight the department/service name beneath the job title for clear affiliation. Local language usage is encouraged for divisions operating in different countries.
- 33 / L’Oréal_Charte graphique
Functional division cards introduce service names beneath the issuer's details. The division name is emphasized above the information block, with strict typography and spacing rules applied.

**Color Information:**

- 9 / L’Oréal_Charte graphique
The corporate palette includes Pantone 423 (gray) and gold colors. Environmental friendliness in design choices (e.g., sustainable paper and ink) is emphasized.

**Typography Information:**

- 30 / L’Oréal_Charte graphique
Typography guidelines for business cards: Bauer Bodoni Bold is used for the name in black font at 11.5 pt size, while ITC Avant Garde Book is used for function and address details at varying sizes (6.5 pt, 5.5 pt). Specific kerning and interline spacing are advised (10 pt approach, 8-13 pt line spacing).
- 31 / L’Oréal_Charte graphique
Typography details for country-specific corporate cards remain consistent with Bauer Bodoni Bold and ITC Avant Garde Book specifications. Functional division cards add ITC Avant Garde Demi at size 6.5 pt for specific department names.

**Spacing Layout:**

- 30 / L’Oréal_Charte graphique
Business cards follow strict layout measurements (85x55 mm), prioritizing logo positioning (pinned spacing zones applied) and include kerning and baseline adjustments for all textual elements.
- 31 / L’Oréal_Charte graphique
Corporate card layouts emphasize consistent spacing (23 mm, 5 mm, 3 mm margins) and fixed areas for logo placement.

**Brand Voice:**

- 30 / L’Oréal_Charte graphique
Business cards highlight brand consistency across languages (front in local language, back in English). Corporate identity reflects exclusivity via gold-stamping design emphasis.

**Notes:**

- 26 / L’Oréal_Charte graphique
Eco-responsible communication advises using environmentally certified paper (ISO14001, FSC™, and PEFC standards) and vegetable-based inks for sustainable practices.

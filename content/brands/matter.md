## Brand Guidelines

### 2022

**Logo Information:**

- Connectivity Symbol with rules on bounding box, clear space (half the width of a symbol piece), minimum size (Digital: 16 px, Print: 3 mm), and permitted colors (Night/Day or black). Rules specify its usage with the Matter Wordmark or in isolation in restricted space applications.
- Matter Wordmark with bounding box and clear space rules (double the width of the letter 'r'). Minimum size: Digital: 16 px, Print: 2 mm. Specified usage mandates proximity with the Connectivity Symbol. Colors limited to Night, Day, or black.
- List of improper brandmark uses, including distortion, incorrect colors or effects, and improper lock-up arrangements. Emphasized not using the wordmark without the Connectivity Symbol.

**Color Information:**

- Core brand colors: Day (RGB: 243, 255, 255; HEX: #f3ffff; CMYK: 8, 0, 7, 0; Pantone: 656 C) and Night (RGB: 19, 25, 38; HEX: #131926; CMYK: 100, 46, 0, 89; Pantone: 296 C). Colors must match specified values precisely.
- Secondary colors for data visualization include Mint (#adf2bd), Aqua (#8beff2), Lavender (#bbc7f2), Dawn (#c7d1d4), Twilight (#565c65), and Dusk (#9aa3a8) with corresponding RGB and CMYK values.
- Prohibited color uses include gradients behind brandmarks, separate gradient layers, non-prescribed gradient methods, and non-standard usage of data visualization colors.

**Typography Information:**

- The primary brand typeface is Poppins (a geometric sans-serif font). Guidelines recommend keeping typography light and airy, adjusting weights optically at different sizes, and using large shifts in scale to create hierarchy.
- Typesetting guidance uses proportional size collateral, recommending ratios based on margins, x. Minimum font size is 7pt for legibility. Specific combinations of weight, size, leading, and tracking are prescribed for Headline (Thin, 1.5xpt, 1.7xpt), Subhead (Extra Light, 0.5xpt, 0.7xpt), and Body Copy (Regular, 0.25xpt, 0.35xpt).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery contextualizes Matter's role in connecting smart devices. A custom series of 3D renderings emphasizes the Connectivity Symbol and avoids referencing specific brands. Objects span product categories and appear to float in space.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The brand uses a 12-column grid for most communications, with exceptions for extreme vertical layouts using 8 columns. Margins are derived by dividing the diagonal length by 30, with bottom margins larger by 1/5. Gutters are 1/3 the top and side margins.

**Brand Voice:**

- Voice characteristics include a positive and conversational tone, use of vivid language (e.g., 'imagine' and 'enjoy'), and rhythmic, punchy phrasing. Writing should use top-of-mind word choices and relatable storytelling with selective industry-specific language.
- E-commerce descriptions highlight Matter-enabled device compatibility, emphasizing interoperability, security, and effortless use with phrases like 'just turn it on' and 'industry-unifying technology'.
- Point-of-sale messaging uses an informal tone with phrases like 'We belong together' to emphasize interoperability and simplicity. Messaging prioritizes consumers' perspectives, using relatable language.

**Notes:**

- Contact information for marketing-related queries: marketing@csa-iot.org.

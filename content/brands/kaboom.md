## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The logo positions KABOOM! as the spark of playspace equity, incorporating a refined, contemporary aesthetic. When referring to KABOOM! in formal contexts, the full name must always be used in all caps.
- Clearspace is defined as X, where X equals the width of the 'M' in the word mark. Minimum sizes: Print - 0.5 inches; Screen - 40px.
- Approved logo usage includes primary purple (used on white or neutral colors), true black (for limited use when color printing is unavailable), and white knocked out on images or solid colors. Secondary colors also applicable.
- Incorrect logo usages include stretching or distorting the logo, modifying the typeface in the wordmark, adding effects, using unspecified colors, placing the logo on congested or low-contrast parts of images, and outlining the logo.
- Guidance for social avatars includes gradually using the mark-only logo for recognition while ensuring consistency across channels.
- Co-branding guidelines emphasize balance in logo size and clearspace for partnership logos. Additional space may be added for balance depending on usage context such as playground signage.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Violet, Lavender, and Tangerine. Accent colors include Sunflower, Mint, and Dragonfruit. Specific color codes: Violet (HEX: #3E1C66, RGB: R62 G28 B102, CMYK: C91 M100 Y27 K18, Pantone: 2617) and others detailed in full.
- Color distribution guidelines clarify proportional usages for Violet, Lavender, Tangerine, Sunflower, Mint, and Dragonfruit, including specific roles like text headers or accents.
- Color pairings demonstrate successful and unsuccessful combinations. Soft Black/Grayscale palette uses HEX #242428 and its tints for body copy and editorial uses.

**Typography Information:**

- Campton is the brand typeface with weights including Bold, SemiBold, Medium, Book, ExtraBold, each with Italic variants.
- Typography hierarchy rules: Headlines use Campton ExtraBold with title case and Violet color. Other roles (Sub-headlines, Body Copy, Pull Quotes) follow defined typefaces, leading, kerning, tracking, and color specifications.
- In cases where Campton cannot be used, system fonts like Arial should be substituted, supporting weights Bold, Regular, and Black with Italic variations.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Lifestyle imagery qualities include full-color natural light, high resolution, close cropping, positive expression, no overstyled hair or clothing.
- Studio imagery showcases kids in motion, mid-air shots, positive expressions, and high-resolution standards without overstyled elements.
- Build-Day photography features candid moments in natural light, high resolution, emphasizing community members equally with volunteers.
- Kids at play imagery emphasizes joyful moments, harmonious interactions, positive expressions, and natural lighting.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clearspace guidelines specify X as the width of the 'M' in the word mark for logo spacing.
- Mark pairing and placement rules for studio imagery maintain optical centering and specific rotation restrictions.

**Brand Voice:**

- Voice attributes include bold, inspiring, energetic, empathetic, driven, and fresh, avoiding descriptors like overwhelming or rigid. Collaborative and optimistic tone.
- Tagline emphasizing playspace equity: 'Spaces to Play. Sparks for Change.'
- Mission statement outlines the commitment to ending playspace inequity through community-built inspiring playspaces.

**Notes:**

- Photos showcasing past branding elements remain acceptable while transitioning to updated branding.
- Equity-focused image guidelines recommend diversity in representation, avoiding stereotypes, and ensuring dignity and consent in photography.

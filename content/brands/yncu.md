## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- YNCU Corporate Symbol comprises an Emblem and a Logotype. Elements must not be altered in size, position, or scale.
- Primary logo color to be used in web, screen, or print applications using 4-color process (CMYK) or spot color (PMS).
- Secondary color logos include Black + White versions, Reversed versions for usage on dark backgrounds or visuals, and Green and Grey brand background-specific logos.
- Logo minimum size: Standard Logo — 1.125 inches. Full Version — 2.625 inches. Emblem — 0.3 inches.
- Clear space rules: The area around the logo must remain free of other graphics or text, maintaining a minimum clearance based on half-circle width of the house in the logo.
- File formats provided: EPS, PNG; includes Black & White, White, Green and Grey Background alternatives.
- Division-specific Community First logo has primary and secondary color options, clear space rules, and minimum sizes similar to YNCU.
- Logo contrast rules: Use specific logo colors based on background contrast — 10% black or lighter for full color, 50% black or darker for reverse (white).

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: Green (Pantone 7480 C, Pantone 339 U, RGB: 40, 182, 122, HEX: #28B67A, CMYK: C75 M0 Y71 K0) and Dark Grey (Pantone 446C, Pantone Neutral Black U, RGB: 67, 67, 69, HEX: #434345, CMYK: C68 M61 Y57 K43).
- Color contrast guide provided for dark and light backgrounds. Adjust logos based on black percentage equivalent (e.g. black, white, or reverse).
- RGB color usage recommended for online and screen-based applications (e.g., PowerPoint, TV). CMYK or PMS preferred for print.

**Typography Information:**

- Montserrat Typeface is the approved corporate font family. Roles: Headline — Montserrat ExtraBold, Heading — Montserrat Bold, Body Copy — Montserrat Regular.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Posters and branch screenscapes feature consistent elements: new photography, Cityscape graphic, 'Welcome to the Neighborhood' tagline, and appropriate logo placement.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space for logos adheres to a half-circle measurement derived from the house emblem. No encroachment by text or graphic elements allowed.
- Cityscape graphic placement and scale can vary depending on layout execution but must follow approved examples. Text and graphic proportions are not to be altered.

**Brand Voice:**

- Event materials include interactive and team-focused elements such as Plinko boards, banners, tents, tablecloths, and brochures emphasizing the welcoming tone of the brand.
- General social media posts use Montserrat ExtraBold with text contrasting the Cityscape corner graphic, promoting clarity and engaging tone.

**Notes:**

- Exterior signage utilizes unique stacked logo treatments for branch customization purposes but is restricted to signage use only.
- Logos and graphics used on community event materials aim to maximize interactivity and visibility of the brand.
- File directory includes various logo formats (EPS, PNG) for specific use cases such as Black Background, White Background, and alternate layouts.

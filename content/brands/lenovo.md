**Logo Information:**

- Logo versions include: Lenovo logo with containing shape, logo without containing shape for packaging purposes, and vertical or horizontal logo placements based on spatial constraints.
- The Lenovo Visual Identity comprises a logo with a rectangular containing shape for branding materials. Unique patterns derived from the logo are included in design elements to establish consistent identity.
- Logo application and allowed color variations are specified, with positive and reversed being prominent representations.
- Usage of logos and wordmarks is outlined in terms of formats. The document specifies use of vectors like EPS for high-resolution print applications, PDFs for cross-platform sharing, and JPGs/PNGs for web or image purposes.
- While the rectangular containing shape is typically used, for labels on hardware, the Lenovo logo can be detached and used without the shape due to production challenges.
- Incorrect usage examples are provided, including unauthorized modifications like changing colors outside of the specified palette or altering the proportions of the shape.

**Color Information:**

- Color palette codes are specified, including various Pantone, CMYK, RGB, and HEX values for colors such as Lenovo Red (Pantone 1795 C, CMYK: 0/94/100/0, RGB: 238/49/36, HEX: #EE3124), Cyan, Yellow, and Teal variations.
- Primary colors: Lenovo Red and an extended color palette consisting of vibrant and mid-tone values. Specific colors named include Mandarin Orange, Peony Pink, Dark Gray, Sapphire Blue, Medium Gray, Style Blue, Light Gray, and Envy Green.
- Color usage rules include using mid-tone colors as background for improved visibility and to differentiate from competitors using plain black or white.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Gotham, includes weights such as Extra Light, Thin, Light, Book, Medium, Bold, Black, Ultra with corresponding italic versions, recommended for all branding to create a consistent identity.
- Web font alternative: Raleway is suggested for online titles and headlines to mimic Gotham's characteristics. Arial and Helvetica continue as primary fonts for body text in web applications.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Product photography emphasizes movement, action, and energy. Backgrounds should use brand colors and avoid plain black or white backgrounds.
- Social media imagery should focus on people, momentum, and optimism with a human-centric approach.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Product packaging designs ensure consistency. The Lenovo logo is oriented vertically on consumer boxes, while specific layouts vary based on branding and model specifications.
- Event signage and booth layouts incorporate multiple corporate colors, branded patterns, photography, and positioning statements.
- Exterior signage layout recommendations provided for vertical or horizontal logo placement, depending on structural considerations.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand's philosophy centers around 'never stand still,' emphasizing vitality, movement, innovation, optimism, and energy.
- Social media voice is inspirational and focuses on enabling people to make progress and create momentum through actions and innovations.
- The Lenovo screensaver reflects an energetic and fun visual identity that is consistent with the brand's tone.

**Notes:**

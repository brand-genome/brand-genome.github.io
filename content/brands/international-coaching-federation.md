## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Centered ICF logo displayed with gradient background on business cards.
- Do not combine gradient wordmark with white text.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Colors: ICF Blue (Pantone 072 CP, CMYK: 100, 90, 0, 7; RGB: 48, 57, 141; HEX: #30398D), ICF Gold (Pantone 136, CMYK: 0, 22, 83, 0; RGB: 255, 191, 63; HEX: #FEBD3B), Deep Blue (Pantone 2757, CMYK: 100, 81, 0, 51; RGB: 0, 30, 96; HEX: #001E60), Bright Yellow (Pantone 603, CMYK: 4, 0, 78, 0; RGB: 246, 237, 78; HEX: #F6ED4E).
- Family Organization Colors: Light Blue (Pantone 3125, CMYK: 89, 0, 19, 0; RGB: 0, 174, 199; HEX: #00AEC7), Orange (Pantone 716, CMYK: 0, 59, 100, 0; RGB: 234, 118, 0; HEX: #EA7600), Red (Pantone 485, CMYK: 0, 95, 100, 0; RGB: 218, 41, 28; HEX: #DA291C), Purple (Pantone 7656, CMYK: 45, 91, 0, 3; RGB: 142, 58, 128; HEX: #8E3A80), Green (Pantone 340, CMYK: 100, 0, 81, 0; RGB: 0, 150, 94; HEX: #00965E), Magenta (Pantone 1925, CMYK: 0, 100, 52, 0; RGB: 224, 0, 77; HEX: #E0004D).
- Neutral Palette: Warm Gray 3 (Pantone Warm Gray 3, CMYK: 21, 20, 21, 1; RGB: 191, 184, 175; HEX: #BFB8AF), Warm Gray 9 (Pantone Warm Gray 9, CMYK: 40, 40, 42, 19; RGB: 131, 120, 111; HEX: #83786F), Cool Gray 11 (Pantone Cool Gray 11, CMYK: 63, 52, 44, 33; RGB: 83, 86, 90; HEX: #53565A), Light Blue (Pantone 271, CMYK: 49, 44, 0, 0; RGB: 149, 149, 210; HEX: #9595D2), Medium Blue (Pantone 2727, CMYK: 49, 44, 0, 0; RGB: 86, 126, 191; HEX: #567EBF).
- Guidelines for Tint Usage: Use a 20% tint step system. Tints below 60% used as backgrounds require dark text; ICF Blue should not be used in tints to preserve its integrity.
- Approved Color Pairings: White text and icon on ICF Blue, Blue text on White, Blue text and Gold gradient icon on White, and others ensuring legibility and contrast.
- Common Errors: Avoid placing ICF Blue on Dark Blue or Gray, mixing ICF Gold and Yellow, modifying brand colors, using unauthorized tints, and combining gradient logos with white text.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Typeface: Montserrat, variable-weight sans-serif font suitable for all brand executions. Acceptable alternatives include Proxima Nova, Helvetica, and Arial.
- Montserrat Weights: Light, Regular, SemiBold, Bold. Guidelines for establishing hierarchy using varying weights.
- Secondary Typeface: Lora, variable-weight serif font suitable for body text and headings, promoting a friendly feeling. Available in Regular, Italic, Bold, and Bold Italic weights.
- Typography Guidelines: Stay left-aligned, avoid full justification, align baselines or x-heights, provide sufficient negative space, ensure contrast using weights and sizes appropriately, and keep line lengths between 45-70 characters.
- Prohibited Typography Practices: Avoid unauthorized fonts, distortion of fonts, excessive spacing changes, strokes, shadows, justified alignment, and angled text other than 0° or 90°.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography tone: Clean, fresh, inspirational, reflecting brand values and inclusivity. Seek excellent lighting and prefer real people over stock photos.
- Composition Guidelines: Use foreground and background elements creatively, employ shallow depth of field and lighting effects to enhance visual interest.
- People Photography: Subjects should appear relaxed, happy, and engaged. Avoid identifying features unless release forms are signed; avoid direct gaze at the camera.

**Notes:**

- Business card templates use Montserrat SemiBold for names, Lora Italic for titles, Montserrat Medium for details, all set on brand colors. Logos printed reversed on brand gradient.
- Letterhead and envelopes available as digital templates, provided by the ICF Marketing and Communications team, formatted strictly per templates.
- Presentation templates provided to staff and ICF chapters, pre-customized with chapter logos and approved brand colors.
- Contact information for guidelines clarifications: icfpr@coachingfederation.org.
- This is a living document; the latest version is available online at coachingfederation.org/brand.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Master logo (3-color logo) - Primary placement on 'Yakima Red' or 'Kraft'; secondary over 'Valley Wheat'; only over white when necessary.
- 1-color logo rules - Appears in white or 'Chief Blue'; not in 'Yakima Red' or photographs; restricted for certain media conditions.
- 2-color logo: Used for merchandise when ink resources are limited, and background color closely matches 'Kraft'.
- Clear restrictions on logo distortion: No disproportional scaling, color changes, rotations, adding shadows (except for video), or mixing with other logos.
- Prohibition against standalone usage of the logo without accompanying primary brand representation.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors used in the brand identity include 'Yakima Red' (Pantone 1795), 'Chief Blue' (Pantone 7463), and 'Valley Wheat' (Pantone 9202).
- Specific secondary placement color rules based on consistent branding, e.g., over 'Kraft' or 'Valley Wheat'.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Only official YCH photography may be used for promotional purposes, especially for images of hops and branded products.
- Official YCH photography ensures correct lighting and color quality to give customers accurate product representation.
- High-resolution, accurate photographs of Yakima Chief Hops products are made available upon request (reach out to marketing@yakimachief.com).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo placement ensures prominence of the primary branding logo of the distributor, brewer, or retailer, following the three P's (Placement, Proportion, Presentation).

**Brand Voice:**

- Correct written brand representation includes capitalization of 'Yakima Chief Hops' with no missing elements. Acceptable abbreviations: 'YCH'.
- Trademark usage for YCH product names like 'Cryo Hops®', 'American Noble Hops™', and hop varieties (e.g., Citra®, Mosaic®).
- Ensure consumer-facing communication identifies the proper relation with Yakima Chief Hops (e.g., 'YCH Brewing Partner', 'Brewing with Yakima Chief Hops').

**Notes:**

- All logo usage across media, including printed, digital, or merchandised applications, requires prior approval from the YCH marketing department (marketing@yakimachief.com).
- Approval process requires submission of draft designs at least 10 business days before publication or printing. Explicit email approval is necessary before use.
- Unauthorized use of YCH trademarks or logos, including third-party recreations, is prohibited. Violating parties are responsible for reprinting and removal costs.

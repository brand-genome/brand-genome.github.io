- Imagery should depict moments in airport life authentically, showing real passengers and environments without feeling posed or staged. It should emphasize storytelling, such as 'Making every journey better.'
- Planes and places photography can include epic imagery with impact and energy to showcase Heathrow's soul and scale. Avoid images of hi-vis vests unless related to construction.
- Cut-out images may be used in scenarios where budget or time does not allow location photoshoots. These should still emphasize diversity and customer satisfaction while grounding full-length cutouts with shadows.

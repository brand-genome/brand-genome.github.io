## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The logo has responsive versions: it can be displayed as a full logo, imagotype only, or the wordmark. There is also a negative version of the logo and specific prohibited uses, such as changing typography, distortions in vertical or horizontal proportions, altering spacing, and changing the color.
- Clear space/safe zone rule: An invisible field called the 'Respiration Box' surrounds the logo. The safe zone is defined based on the height of the letter 'B' in Bakery.
- Prohibited logo usage includes: changing typography, distorting vertical or horizontal proportions, removing elements, altering letter spacing, and changing logo colors.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color is Pantone 154 U, a brown reminiscent of coffee, chocolate, and pastries (HEX: #926844, CMYK: 35 56 78 19, RGB: 146 104 68). Secondary color is Pantone Warm Grey 1 U (HEX: #DBD5CD, CMYK: 13 13 170, RGB: 219 213 205). Accent color is Pantone 7702 U (HEX: #5DABC6, CMYK: 62 18 16 0, RGB: 93 171 198).
- Color usage rules: The primary color (brown) is used for a warm, familiar aesthetic; the secondary grey is used for high-contrast scenarios; and the blue accent color is used to highlight important elements such as web links.

**Typography Information:**

- Main typeface: Roboto Bold and Roboto Regular, selected for legibility across digital and printed media. Supporting typeface: Gran Hotel, used primarily in digital materials to convey warmth and a handmade aesthetic.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Promotional photography should be black-and-white with a 40% opacity Pantone 154 U overlay. Product images should use color with the same Pantone 154 U overlay applied with 40% opacity.
- Iconography guidelines: Icons should have a mono-line style, be simple, and align with the brand's visual identity. Icons are used to represent products available in the bakery or cafe.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo's Respiration Box is based on the height of the 'B' in Bakery, defining the minimum required clear space around the logo.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand wants to convey a young, fresh, cool, and friendly image, evoking a sense of home and comfort.

**Notes:**

- This brand guide establishes Bakery Hours as a modern and stylish bakery chain, focusing on simplicity, warmth, and a handmade aesthetic.
- Additional branding materials include aprons, polos for staff, branded bags, and coffee cups with the logo prominently displayed.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2025

**Logo Information:**

- The logo consists of an icon combined with a wordmark. The icon and wordmark are always used together.
- Clear space around the logo must equal the height of the 'x' in the logo's design.
- The logo is available in light and dark versions for different uses.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: #0E2F34. Secondary color: #3ECDB1. Neutral colors: #FFFFFF, #F7F7F7, #333333.
- Gradients: From #0E2F34 to #3ECDB1, from #3ECDB1 to #FFFFFF.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: 'Syne'. Attributes: Bold, modern, and impactful. Its unique character adds personality and reinforces authority in messaging.
- The font family Syne includes weights such as Regular, Medium, SemiBold.
- Secondary font: 'Inter'. Clean and readable, designed for clarity and versatility across screens and devices. Complements the brand's human-centered approach.
- The font family Inter includes weights such as Regular, Medium, SemiBold.
- Typography usage: Headlines use Syne, while body text uses Inter to balance innovation and usability.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space around the logo must equal the height of the 'x' in the logo's design.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand voice is bold, strategic, efficient, engaging, and trustworthy.
- Brand tone includes a balance between funny and serious, casual and formal, irreverent and respectful, enthusiastic and straightforward.
- Key tones: Funny (breaking robotic tone of AI with humor and human relatability), Casual (positioning as a friendly expert), Neutral (balancing fun and professionalism), Enthusiastic (conveying passion and confidence in AI-powered content).

**Notes:**

- Slogan: 'AI-Powered. Human-Perfected'. Tagline: 'Where AI Meets Human Creativity'.
- Brand messaging emphasizes making AI-enhanced content creation simple and stress-free for marketers, entrepreneurs, and businesses at every level.
- Ideal customers: Business professionals, marketing teams, entrepreneurs, e-commerce brands, consultants, corporate trainers, public speakers, and more.
- Common customer challenges include time constraints, adapting content across platforms, maintaining brand voice, creating visuals, simplifying complex information, and refining AI-generated content for accuracy.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The logo consists of a wordmark and an icon (beeldmerk). The icon uses EDBA colors, while the wordmark carries the colors of the Municipality of Alphen aan den Rijn.
- The Alphen Linkt label is a slanted, rounded white segment used together with the logo in promotional materials. Label usage rules are detailed on page 33.
- Custom illustrations/images can be incorporated into the icon, provided that the logo style remains consistent. A template version is available to ensure logo recognizability.
- For images, elements such as the endorsement logo, 'Alphen Marketing' branding, and Alphen Linkt label must be included. The Alphen Linkt label occupies 1/5th height of the image and is placed at the top or bottom. Optional hashtag outlines may also be used.
- Shows examples of endorsed events using the Alphen Linkt logo.
- Further examples provided for logo usage in endorsed promotional materials.

**Typography Information:**

- The typeface 'Bungee' is used characteristically at an angle of 8°, matching the hashtag's orientation. It brings bold, strong characteristics to the brand. Available via Adobe Typekit or free from external sources.

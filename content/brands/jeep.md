## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The Jeep brand mark is a specially designed and distinctive graphic image, consisting of the Jeep logotype and registration mark ® as one unit. It must be used correctly, in its original form, without alteration to its style, proportions, or letterforms.
- The area of isolation for the Jeep brand mark must preserve legibility and visual impact. This free space is equal to the height of the 'J' in the Jeep logotype.
- The Jeep brand mark must not be combined with any graphic elements like text, illustrations, or logotypes, and may not be used in headlines or text.
- Incorrect logo usage includes those without the ®, with altered position or scale of the ®, drop shadows, repeated patterns, screens or tints, and improper proximity with FCA US LLC branding.
- The preferred metallic 3D version of the Jeep brand mark may not be reproduced smaller than 17mm in width, and embossed or engraved versions must not be smaller than 13mm.
- Logo must not be reproduced in poor legibility, improperly reversed, or altered colors.

**Color Information:**

- The preferred color reproduction for the Jeep brand mark is the metallic 3D version, with options for Jeep Black (HEX: #000000, RGB: 0, 0, 0, CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 100), Jeep Cool Gray 8 (Pantone Cool Gray 8, HEX: #9D9FA2, CMYK: 48.22, 40.28, 38.21, 3.5). Can also include 45% tint of black for one-color reproduction and reversed for dark backgrounds.

**Typography Information:**

- Use of the Jeep brand name in text requires it to be typed in the font used and capitalized when it is in headline or text (e.g., Jeep® or Jeep models). Never use plural or possessive forms like 'Jeeps' or 'Jeep's'.
- The term Jeep® may appear in a legible complimentary typeface if combined with product logos or names.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Strict areas of isolation must be maintained—equal to the height of the 'J' in Jeep.

**Brand Voice:**

- Maintains capitalized Jeep® in branding communications. Utilization as an adjective (e.g., Jeep vehicle or Jeep Cherokee) is encouraged. Must not appear as a standalone noun or verb.

**Notes:**

- All marketing materials incorporating the Jeep Brand Mark must be approved by FCA US LLC Identity Office.
- Preferred logo reproduction format is 3D metallic, with guidelines ensuring high-resolution output. Reproduction sizes have minimums depending on type—17mm for printed, 13mm for embossed.
- In one-color reproduction scenarios, Jeep branding can use Jeep Black, Jeep Cool Gray 8, or a 45% tint of black background.

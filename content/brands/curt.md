## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Logo forms: primary logo and secondary variants labeled specifically. White background preferred, though blue is acceptable. Alternate logos include color variants (horizontal and vertical orientations are detailed).
- Logo color variants: Standard (black and white), horizontal and reverse available, with restrictions on incorrect variations.
- Incorrect logo usage examples are stated explicitly. Avoid condensing, expanding, using patterns, altering relationships, switching colors, rotating, or using unapproved colors.
- Clear space rule: Isolation space is defined as 25% of the CURT Group X-height.
- X-height for scale factor is based on the CURT Group symbol height.

**Color Information:**

- Orange: C:000 M:080 Y:100 K:000, R:241 G:090 B:034, HEX: #F05A22, Pantone PMS 1665 C.
- Greyscale shades: 80% (HEX: #58585B, C:000 M:000 Y:000 K:080), 70% (HEX: #6D6E70, C:000 M:000 Y:000 K:070), 60% (HEX: #808284, C:000 M:000 Y:000 K:060), 40% (HEX: #A7A9AB, C:000 M:000 Y:000 K:040), etc., down to 10%.
- Color importance discussed for brand identity. Consistent color palette strengthens recognition across communication and visual perception.

**Typography Information:**

- Fonts: DIN OT and Helvetica Neue LT Std unified for branding. Various weights and point sizes defined for headers, subheadings, body text, etc.
- Font roles defined with weights: Headline styles include DIN OT Black (56 pt, 35 pt, 29.5 pt). Body text uses Helvetica Neue LT Std (ranges from 7.75 pt to 11 pt in weights like Roman, Light, Medium).
- Font colors may vary based on design hierarchy or context.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Isolation space (clear space around logo elements) is 25% of the CURT Group's X-height.
- Table styles highlighted for consistent application guides and product offerings. Includes Helvetica Neue LT Std Condensed (ranges of font weight for headers, body text). Variations allowed but must maintain general branding look and feel.

**Brand Voice:**

- CURT's mission statement emphasizes product excellence, leadership, customer-centric focus, and associates as its greatest asset.
- Tone qualities: Adventurous, hardworking, American, family fun. Consumer persona ties brand to relatable personality traits.

**Notes:**

- Brand guidelines heavily emphasize consistency, such as capitalization rules (CURT always in all caps). Acceptable uses include CURT and curtmfg.com.
- Marketing Services department is responsible for maintaining complete style guides and ensuring brand image consistency.
- Corporate signature and logo usage guidelines heavily emphasize unaltered application for consistent brand identity.

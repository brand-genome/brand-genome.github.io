## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Arnold Ventures' logo consists of the icon and wordmark combined into a single lockup for flexibility. Primary lockup is stacked for maximum clarity and impact. Variations include a large icon lockup (with the icon scaled to the full height of the wordmark to work at smaller sizes) and a horizontal lockup (used when there is limited vertical space).

**Color Information:**

- Blue and yellow are primary accents that pair with black, white, or navy. Blue and yellow should not be used together as it creates a garish effect and dilutes their impact.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: GT America, used for headlines. Secondary typeface: Sharp Grotesk 10 for numbers and big headers. Serif typeface: Tiempos for editorial/storytelling use. Tiempos Text, a legible variation of Tiempos, is used for body text and captions. Substitutes include Arial, Helvetica, or Times when primary fonts are unavailable.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Illustrations are detailed and subtle, aligning with focus areas of Arnold Ventures. They should be used at sizes where intricacies can be appreciated, such as hero images on landing pages.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand emphasizes being a catalyst for lasting impact, driven by research, evidence-based solutions, deep thinking, and public advocacy.

**Notes:**

- Mission: 'To improve lives by investing in evidence-based solutions that maximize opportunity and minimize injustice.' The mission serves as the foundation for Arnold Ventures' purpose and vision.

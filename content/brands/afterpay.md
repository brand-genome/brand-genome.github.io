## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- Master logo with alternative combinations for contrast: Black logo for light backgrounds, White logo for dark backgrounds, Bondi Mint version for Black backgrounds only.
- Badge guidelines: Bondi Mint badge for Black background, alternative legible badge versions for light and dark backgrounds (Black and White respectively).
- Clear space rules: Maintain specified clear space for both logo and badge. X-height or stroke weight metrics provided for lockups and spacing.
- Minimum size requirements: Logo and Badge - 64px/32mm; Sticker and App Icon - 32px/14mm.
- Incorrect usage examples: Do not distort, rotate, rearrange, outline, gradient, or color outside specified palette for the logo. Ensure background does not render logo illegible.

**Color Information:**

- Primary palette includes Afterpay Bondi Mint (#b2fce4, RGB 178 252 228), White (#FFFFFF, RGB 255 255 255), and Black (#000000, RGB 0 0 0).
- For digital applications, use provided screen formulas. For print applications, contact Afterpay for Pantone® swatch.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Italian Plate No2 Expanded. Available in Regular, Regular Italic, Demibold, and Bold styles.
- Typography guidelines: Maintain geometry, balance, and soft angles for readability. Do not use all caps.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photo backgrounds for banners should include negative space for legibility of text and logos.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Use consistent visual balancing for partner logos and Afterpay lockups. Maintain clear space using X-height metrics.
- Top strip banners: Ensure clear marketing messages, with mono typography (Black or White) for legibility.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice: Customer-centric, approachable, promoting responsible spending, and discouraging overspending or association with gambling/criminal activities.
- Approved messaging examples: 'Shop now. Pay later. Always interest-free.' 'Afterpay it in 4. Always interest-free.'

**Notes:**

- Ensure marketing banners and educational materials prominently include disclaimers for eligibility criteria, late fees, and terms.
- Afterpay branding must not use altered forms of the company name such as 'AfterPay,' all caps in body text, or legal name unless required.

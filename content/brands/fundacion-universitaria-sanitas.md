## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The logo consists of an isotipo and logotexto. The isotipo is a simple, stylized element, and the logotexto can include a corporate identifier. The logotexto should always be accompanied by the isotipo, while the symbol (Isotipo) can be used iconically on its own.
- The composition of the escudo (shield) includes a snake entwined around a staff made of Cypress wood, symbolizing Aesculapius in Greek myth, a serpent-entwined protector of healing and humanistic health care.
- Morphology of the isotipo and logotexto: The entire logo fits into a modular space with proportions of 16.8x by 2.6x. A measurement unit 'X' defines the proper proportion for correct representation across formats.
- Logo versions: Vertical and Vertical Extreme versions are defined with specific modular proportions, e.g., Vertical 2.4x by 2.7x, and Vertical Extreme with distinct measurements.
- A version of the logo exists without OSI (Organización Sanitas Internacional).
- The shield version of the logo includes the name of the institution.
- Minimum reproduction size requirements are 45mm for offset, 50mm for screen printing, and a minimum of 200px for digital formats. Larger sizes are recommended for embossed or engraved systems.
- Clear space / safe zone around the logo: Defined as 3x from all sides, without interference from other graphic elements. Increasing the space is recommended wherever possible.
- Incorrect logo usages include changing the typography, proportions, colors, removing elements, or altering their layout.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color for the logo is Pantone Solid Uncoated Process Cyan U. Equivalent codes are: CMYK: C100 M5 Y0 K0, RGB: R0 G159 B218, HEX: #009FDA.
- Chromatic applications for the logo: It can be applied as Pantone Cyan U on white, black on white, white on black, or on dark and light photographic backgrounds, as well as on gray.

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate typography: ITC Garamond Std (Book, Light) and News Gothic Std (Medium, Bold). These fonts are used for internal communication, signage, and external communication.
- Font sizes for functional, official use: Name (Arial 16 Bold), Job position (Arial 14 Bold), Address (Arial 12 Regular), City and Country (Arial 12 Regular).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Material layout specifications for corporate cards and envelopes: Cards: 14.8x21cm, with specific padding and margins. Envelopes: 11.5x21.5cm with centralized logo and address information.

**Notes:**

- The document is a comprehensive manual for the corporate identity of Fundación Universitaria Sanitas, including guidelines for logo usage, colors, typography, and applications on various materials such as cards, uniforms, and digital documents.
- The manual discourages any modifications to the logo's proportions, typography, or colors that deviate from the stated guidelines.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The logo combines serif and sans-serif font styles, embodying Polvijärvi's dual identity of being both traditional and modern.
- Logo clear space is defined as at least '1x the height of the O character in the logo.'
- Horizontal logo minimum width is 20 mm. Vertical logo minimum width is 9 mm. Scaling must consider print quality and ensure visibility of fine lines.
- Logo versions include horizontal and vertical layouts, with variations for print and digital use.
- Primary usage: white logo on colored background or blue logo on white background. For busy backgrounds, use a background block or overlay for contrast.

**Color Information:**

- Core brand colors include shades of blue, pink, yellow, and white. Each color corresponds to a brand value: blue (stability and tradition), pink (youth and community), yellow (happiness).
- Full list of color codes: PMS 2038C (#ff5fbe), PMS 2450C (#cd3c82), PMS 7647C (#a02364), PMS 2925C (#0082dc), PMS 4151C (#004182), PMS 2767C (#001e50), PMS 101C (#fff550), PMS 1235C (#e6aa00), PMS 108C (#ffd800). CMYK, RGB, and HEX values are also defined for each.
- Guidance on combining colors to create pairings: blue and pink provide contrast, while different shades of the same color create a calmer feel.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary fonts: Recoleta (e.g., Recoleta Black), Termina Bold for headings. Montserrat is used for body text.
- Alternative fonts for office applications include Georgia Bold and Calibri Bold for headings, and Calibri for body text.
- Each font's role is detailed: headlines (Recoleta Black or Termina Bold), larger subheaders, and body copy details (Montserrat Light or Calibri).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Illustrations prominently feature geese inspired by Polvijärvi's values—community and collaboration. Geese are depicted in mundane activities or hobbies.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Wave patterns, inspired by local water bodies, are used for separating content and enhancing layouts. These patterns can integrate images or illustrations.

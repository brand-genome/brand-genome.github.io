## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The logo comes in vertical and horizontal versions with both displaying 'UNIEMPRESAS' and 'asesores en seguridad social.'
- Clear space for the logo is established by a module of 1 cm², which defines the area that should remain free from other graphic elements.
- Minimum size requirements specify that the logo's reduction must not compromise legibility, with specific dimensions mentioned for offset printing and digital use. Offset minimum size: 2.5 cm; Digital minimum sizes: 107 px for UNIEMPRESAS and 70 px for 'asesores en seguridad social.'
- Do's specify careful usage of vector files to preserve legibility and avoid loss from resizing or distortion.
- Incorrect logo usage examples include issues in size, deformation, and inappropriate positioning, such as lateral distortion or incorrect scaling.
- Incorrect uses of color include using unapproved green, white, or background colors.
- Incorrect uses of form include changes to the shape of the isologotype, incorrect typography usage, and altering the organizational structure of the logo elements.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Green (Pantone 165 C, HEX #A5BE3D, RGB: R:165, G:190, B:61, CMYK: C:51, M:7, Y:61, K:0), White (HEX #F2F2F2, RGB: R:242, G:242, B:242, CMYK: C:0, M:0, Y:0, K:0), and complementary elements.
- Grayscale usage is specified for cases requiring grayscale printing or formatting. Shades are defined with their respective HEX, RGB, and CMYK codes.
- Positive and negative logo applications with monochrome options are recommended for use cases where corporate colors cannot be applied.

**Typography Information:**

- The logo typography uses two fonts: 'Langdon' for the name 'UNIEMPRESAS' and 'Fairview' for the descriptor 'asesores en seguridad social.' Both fonts have been customized to align with the brand theme.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Brand logo alignment and scaling are managed through a technical plan using a grid system composed of proportional 1 cm modules.
- Secure spacing for the logo is defined by modules of 1 cm² around the logo to avoid interference with legibility.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand philosophy focuses on saving customers time, allowing them to spend it on family and cherished moments. The messaging emphasizes reliability and enjoyment of life.
- Key tagline: 'Tu te encargas de disfrutar la vida.' (You take care of enjoying life.)

**Notes:**

- The brand's construction and applications extend to digital and print formats, identity across social media, stationery, and branded merchandise.

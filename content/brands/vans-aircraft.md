## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Logo versions include horizontal and stacked layouts with or without tagline.
- Clear space around the logo must equal the height of one 'V' and a width of two 'V's in relation to the outer edge of the logo.
- Minimum logo size with tagline: 1.25 inches wide; without tagline: 1 inch wide.
- Color versions available: full color (orange and black), single color variations (black, white reverse, 1-color reverse) for restricted applications, and full color reverse (on dark backgrounds).
- Do not place the van's aircraft logo on unsanctioned background colors unless they are grayscale or silver, provided contrast remains sufficient.
- Illustrations of misuse include: altering element spacing, distorting, recoloring, restyling, or applying effects like drop shadows to the logo.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors include: PMS 1655 C (Orange, HEX: #FC4C02), PMS 1665 C (Dark Orange, HEX: #DC4405), PMS Cool Grey 9C (Medium Grey, HEX: #75787B), White (HEX: #FFFFFF), and Black (HEX: #121212).
- Dark Grey PMS 418 C (HEX: #212322) is part of the palette.
- Background colors primarily include white, black, dark grey, and dark orange floods for high-contrast. Other orange elements must use PMS 1655 C.
- Color usage ratios recommendation: White (45%), Black (20%), Dark Grey (20%), Orange and Dark Orange (6% each), Medium Grey (3%).
- Strict rules for using Dark Orange PMS 1665 C exclusively for background floods, while primary PMS 1655 C is restricted to design elements.
- Incorrect usage examples include using primary orange instead of dark orange for full-color backgrounds and vice versa.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family is Eurostile LT Std with weights including Roman, Medium, Demi, and Bold (and their oblique variants).
- Uses prescribed styles like: Bold Oblique for large headlines, Demi for subheads, and Medium for body text. Additionally, Condensed and Extended versions are to be used sparingly.
- Pre-defined styles include Eurostile Bold Oblique (all caps) for large/extra-large headlines, and Eurostile Demi Oblique for headlines/subheads.
- Left-alignment recommended for layouts; avoid centering unless outlined in specific cases.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Background textures should be meaningful, low contrast, and avoid degrading or overshadowing content above.
- Logo symbols may serve as background elements on orange or black backgrounds, provided design rules (e.g., proper cropping and positioning) are followed.
- Examples of improper usage of logo symbols include incorrect cropping, turning at an angle, layering symbols, or overlaying on unsanctioned colors.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Gridded layouts are advised for easy organization and clarity in design elements.
- Text-heavy elements should favor organization through distinct type styles and maintain balance in hierarchy.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand tone emphasizes balance between a technical and operational safety focus and an approachable message.
- Key brand priorities include promoting pilot safety and the enjoyment of flying RV aircraft.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Logo must be used in entirety according to established guidelines. Centennial mark must be approved for use and is only valid through the academic year of 2018-2019.
- The 'We Fly' logo lockup, must adhere to specified proportions. Units may use this mark but may not modify it or add additional taglines.

**Color Information:**

- Cardinal red must be used (RGB: 186,12,47). Colors have specific blend mode requirements such as white font on red bars.
- Video graphics and lower-thirds design specify RGB values for cardinal red (186,12,47) and white (255,255,255).

**Typography Information:**

- Brand font is Helvetica Neue LT Std (a.k.a Neue Helvetica), selected for clarity, honesty, and forward-thinking aesthetics. Six weights available, as regular or italic.
- Fallback fonts include TeX Gyre Heros, Roboto, and Open Sans (for digital body only). These substitutes require approval and must be used sparingly.
- Helvetica Neue LT Std weights: Thin, Light, Roman, Medium, Bold, Heavy, each with pairs of italics for contrast.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography must be strong, vibrant, and authentic, portraying diversity, interaction, aspirational themes, and negative space. Hands-on learning should be emphasized.
- Silhouette and unposed shots are encouraged for imagery style. Images require authentic emotion and context for brand messaging.
- Textural photos should evoke campus details and use blending mode Multiply with cardinal red overlays applied via Photoshop.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Official letterhead margins are 1.25 inches left/right, 1.5 inches top, and 1 inch bottom. Center alignment is required.
- Business cards adhere to strict typography and layout dimensions: 3.5x2 inches with Helvetica Neue fonts.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand uses themes such as 'We Fly' for optimism and aspirations.

**Notes:**

- Official stationery (letterhead, envelopes, and business cards) must only use approved watermarked stock, printed exclusively by university services.
- Marketing and Communications team must approve any deviations from typography, design, or photography usage.

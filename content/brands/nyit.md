**Typography Information:**

- Helvetica Neue: Regular, Bold, Medium, and Light weights recommended for usage in NYIT materials.
- Times New Roman: Regular, Italic, Bold, and Bold Italic weights recommended for legal documents, letters, and formal communications.

- Use of varying depth-of-field in photography, including drone perspectives and extreme close-ups, to add visual interest.

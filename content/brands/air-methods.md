## Brand Guidelines

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- The Air Methods logo is comprised of three elements: the company name (Air Methods), the art element ('The Wings'), and the tagline ('Defenders of Tomorrow'). The logo can work in unison or as separate elements. Slight italic tilt provides energy and vibrancy even when used individually. The logo must always include a registered trademark (®).
- Logo variations are specified: RGB screen logo is used for screen applications (like websites, banners, or presentations) and has a richer color spectrum for screen use. CMYK logo built for printed materials such as ads, billboards, and flyers. A one-color version exists for limited color printing or dark backgrounds, adaptable to black as well.
- Do not stretch/skew the logo; do not alter the colors of the logo; avoid replacing logo typefaces. No effects (e.g., gradients or shadows) should be applied. Do not rotate or resize the wings and typography parts independently.
- When using the tagline ('Defenders of Tomorrow'), it must always use a special Ž symbol, appear in all caps, and the word 'OF' should appear 1/5 smaller than the rest of the tagline.

**Color Information:**

- Air Methods' primary brand color is 'AMC Blue,' corresponding to PMS 289. Attributes: HEX: #0C223F, RGB (R:12 G:34 B:63), CMYK (C:98 M:84 Y:46 K:51).
- Air Methods' secondary color is grey (PMS 424). Attributes: HEX: #707170, RGB (R:112 G:113 B:112), CMYK (C:8 M:4 Y:10 K:23).
- Spot White is also used in the color palette: RGB (255, 255, 255), CMYK (C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:0).

**Typography Information:**

- The primary typeface for Air Methods is Trade Gothic. It is described as a strong, serious, and versatile font suitable for headings and body text.
- Trade Gothic Light is the variant used for body copy over solid colors.
- Trade Gothic Bold is used to emphasize titles and subtitles.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery should reflect the brand's spirit of confidence, assurance, and readiness to be present when needed.
- Authenticity is prioritized in brand imagery. Images should involve real situations (not staged), proprietary aircraft or technology, real employees (not models), and portray individuals as strong, confident, and self-assured.
- Diagonally striped assets (blue and white) are acceptable. The angle must be between -50° to -60° for horizontal and -40° to -50° for vertical layouts, max transparency maintained at 80%, and colors strictly PMS 289 or white.

**Brand Voice:**

- Air Methods' voice is described as confident and strong — reassuring and supportive, but never arrogant or condescending. It reflects certainty, industry leadership ('Patient Critical Care Logistics'), and high adaptability to customer needs.
- Editorial voice rules: The brand tone must communicate expertise in safety and innovation. The focus is on setting industry standards and offering cutting-edge solutions.

**Notes:**

- Trademark usage rules: The Ž or ® superscript should be used for proprietary products/logos/names at least once. All approved logos/templates already include TM or registered marks.
- Brand imagery assets, including the manifesto poster and mantra video, are not intended for sales purposes. Licensing rights constrain their usage beyond original intent, and permission must be obtained before reusing imagery.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2009

**Logo Information:**

- The Havis logo is a unique drawing of the Havis brand name, combining strong, solid letterforms with dynamic movement. It is the centerpiece of the brand visual identity. Preferred color variation: Two-color positive (Havis Blue and Havis Orange) communicates attributes like intelligence, integrity, energy, and responsiveness. Artwork available for reproduction using spot or process colors.
- Four approved color versions: Two-color positive (Havis Blue and Havis Orange), Grayscale positive (Black and 55% grayscale), Two-color reverse (White and Havis Orange), Grayscale reverse (White and 30% grayscale). Two-color positive preferred for optimal clarity.
- Clear space around logo must equal the height of the letter 'H'. Minimum size requirements: Print - Logo minimum 0.5" wide; tagline lock-up minimum 1" wide. Online - Logo minimum 60px; tagline lock-up minimum 85px.
- Prohibited logo uses: Do not stretch or compress the logo, change tagline placement, exaggerate stripes, use unapproved colors or add effects like drop shadows. Ensure consistency and clarity in all applications.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors: Havis Blue (PMS 2955, CMYK 100/55/10/48, RGB 0/60/105, HEX #003c69) and Havis Orange (PMS 7413, CMYK 1/62/95/2, RGB 212/123/34, HEX #d47b22). Secondary brand colors: Havis Light Gray (PMS Warm Gray 1, CMYK 2/3/4/5, RGB 224/222/216, HEX #e0ded8), Havis Dark Gray (PMS Warm Gray 11, CMYK 23/32/31/64, RGB 103/92/83, HEX #675c53), Havis Heritage Red (PMS 1807, CMYK 7/94/65/31, RGB 158/48/57, HEX #9e3039).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Typeface: The Serif Basic (Light, Plain, SemiBold, Bold). Secondary Typeface: The Sans Basic (Light, Plain, SemiBold, Bold). Default Typeface: Arial for Office applications (Regular and Bold). Serif Basic used for headlines and display copy; Sans Basic used for body text. Arial used for Microsoft Word and PowerPoint.
- PowerPoint text slide format: Level one text - Arial 22pt (orange bullet); Level two - Arial 20pt (dash); Level three - Arial 18pt (dash); Level four - Arial 16pt (dash); Level five - Arial 16pt (dash). Tips include using 'Shift + Return' for a bullet-free line return.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Supergraphic rules: Derived from logo stripes, maintains Havis Orange color or tinted black. Used at significant scale difference (minimum 300% larger than logo stripes) to avoid confusion. Positioned to overlap or touch other elements at dynamic angles. Semi-transparent application allowed to show underlying content.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Positioning the Havis logo at the top left with sufficient clear space activates layouts and reinforces brand visibility.

**Brand Voice:**

- Havis brand promise and personality: Promise - 'Defining the mobile workspace; delivering comfort, safety, quality to maximize mobile worker productivity.' Personality attributes - Practical, Open, Intelligent, Responsive.

**Notes:**

- Authorized resellers may use the Havis logo with an 'Authorized Reseller' descriptor but cannot use the tagline lock-up. Reseller accounts require qualification and approval.
- Product naming strategy: Havis masterbrand ensures all product naming builds on the corporate brand. Temporary usage of transitional range brands such as Ledco, ChargeGuard, Kwik-Kit, IdleRight, Consolidator, MagnaFire, FX. Moves toward straightforward descriptive names to increase clarity and align with Havis reputation.

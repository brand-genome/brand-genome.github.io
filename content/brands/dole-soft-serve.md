## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The DOLE SOFT SERVE® logo is referred to as the signature. It has clear space rules defined by a minimum spacing area around the signature. Minimum size requirement is 1.25 inches in width. Full-color leaf logo showcased.
- Unacceptable logo uses include stretching, rotating, changing colors, altering the inner leaf color, removing the shadow, separating elements without consent, and other deformities.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes Pantone Reflex Blue (#001489), PMS186 (#C8102E), PMS368 (#78BE20), and Process Yellow (#F6E500). RGB and CMYK values are provided for each.
- Secondary palette includes Pantone colors: 116 C (#FFCD00), 151 C (#FF8200), 178 C (#FF585D), 676 C (#960051), among others. RGB and CMYK values accompany each Pantone.
- Flavor-specific CMYK values for product representation: Lemon (C: 4 M: 4 Y: 27 K: 0), Mango (C: 2 M: 7 Y: 37 K: 0), Pineapple (C: 4 M: 5 Y: 28 K: 0), and others for all flavors.

**Typography Information:**

- Font used is Lato. Headline text uses 'Lato Black', while body text uses 'Lato Medium'. Example alphabets and characters provided for reference.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography guidelines: Imagery should include finished dishes, with or without toppings. Fresh fruit may be used as garnish or topping but should not imply real fruit content. Backgrounds should be light, airy, and neutral to highlight product colors.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space for the logo is defined as an area around the signature. Exact measurements referenced as a safe zone requirement.

**Brand Voice:**

- Approved brand language includes terms like 'natural' or 'all-natural ingredients.' Unacceptable terms include 'organic,' 'healthy,' or 'original Dole Whip.'

**Notes:**

- The guidelines are specific to the U.S. market as indicated across various pages.

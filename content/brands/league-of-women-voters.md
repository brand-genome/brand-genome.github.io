## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Proper use and reproduction of the organization's trademarks, including logos with registered trademark symbols protected under US law.
- Clear space around logos must be maintained at a minimum distance of .25" on all sides for all versions of the logo.
- Minimum size requirements state that the logo should never be used at letter heights smaller than .25" (1/4").
- Incorrect usage examples include stretched/distorted logos, altered typographic relationships, unclear backgrounds, and use of unapproved colors.
- All logos are made of fixed components (e.g., LWV mark, typographic elements, and unique third components) that must never be altered.
- Logo files are provided in .jpg and .png formats for various contexts, and master vector files are marked as non-editable.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include LWV Red (#be0f34), LWV Blue (#005596).
- Secondary colors include LWV Light Purple (#820263), LWV Dark Purple (#521C50), LWV Gold (#ECA400).
- CMYK formulas are the preferred printing method; RGB and HEX values must be used for digital formats according to specified values.

**Typography Information:**

- Merriweather and Lato families are the web typefaces for LWV branding. All weights are permissible.
- Serif typefaces such as English Times are suggested for print, emphasizing readability and compatibility.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should focus on authenticity, avoiding posed shots and emphasizing natural compositions with a focus on expressions and connections.
- Images should highlight diverse ages, races, and intergenerational settings, illustrating action and interaction.
- Photos should visually tell stories about the impact of voting rights and emotional moments, while maintaining appropriate permissions for subjects.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logos require a minimum clear space of .25" (1/4") on all sides, ensuring visibility against competing text, images, and graphics.
- Fixed relationships between components of logos (e.g., trademarks, typography) must be maintained as supplied in the master files.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone emphasizes nonpartisanship, transparency, and alignment with the LWV mission of empowering voters and defending democracy.
- Social media channels must uphold branding standards, avoiding custom interpretations of LWV logos as icons or avatars.

**Notes:**

- File formats for logos include JPG (white backgrounds), PNG (transparency), and master vector formats. Only authorized use is allowed.
- Requests for additional formats or access to proprietary assets must be approved by LWV Communications.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The primary logo consists of a compass rose graphic element and Cal Maritime logotype, which must always be used together as a unit.
- The primary logo should appear in two-color (navy and gold) or monotone (black or white) versions. Monotone or reversed versions are allowed for production constraints.
- Approved logo variations include stacked/vertical versions available in all approved variations, which should only be used when space limitations make the primary logo impractical.
- Minimum size for the primary logo: 1.25 inches in width to ensure legibility.
- Minimum clear space: the width of the letter 'E' in 'Maritime' around the primary logo, with no text or images to intrude into this area.
- Prohibited logo usage: Do not combine the logotype with the seal or coat of arms, do not place logo on busy backgrounds, distort proportions, rearrange elements, or transpose colors.
- The seal is only for formal documents such as diplomas, legal documents, etc., and requires permission from Public Affairs & Communications. Must not be distorted, redrawn, or scanned from previous materials.
- The coat of arms is used for ceremonial purposes and cannot replace the primary logo. Similar rules apply as for the seal regarding reproduction size, distortion, and restricted use.

**Color Information:**

- Approved colors for the Cal Maritime logo are Pantone PMS 110, PMS 117 (Gold), PMS 871 (Metallic Gold — for formal printing), PMS 295 (Navy Blue), and Black.
- CMYK values for approved colors: 
PMS 110 - (C:0 M:18 Y:100 K:15), PMS 117 - (C:0 M:12 Y:100 K:7), PMS 295 - (C:100 M:57 Y:0 K:40).
- RGB values for use in presentations or visual media: 
Navy Blue - (R:0 G:49 B:101), Gold - (R:231 G:215 B:65).
- The following complementary colors can be used as accents: PMS 298 (California Sky), PMS 370 (Palm Tree), PMS 200 (Hull Red), Cool Gray 4, PMS 422 (Quad Gray), PMS 544 (Mt. Tam), PMS 718 (Mayo Brick).

**Typography Information:**

- The logotype font is Optima. Recommended body fonts: Helvetica (including Light, Regular, Oblique, Bold, Bold Oblique) and Garamond (including Regular, Italic, Bold).
- Optima font family includes Regular, Italic, Bold, Bold Italic, and Extra Black. It is also used for stationery and signage.
- Suggested type size for letters and memos is 12pt Garamond. Recommended range for font size is 9.5-12 points.

**Imagery Photography:**

- The Training Ship Golden Bear publications and correspondence must conform to brand standards and the ship name should accompany the bear graphic in communication.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Letterhead formatting: Left & right margins should be 1.25 inches, top & bottom margins should be 1.5 inches. Address information must appear at the bottom using 200 Maritime Academy Drive, Vallejo, CA 94590-8181.
- Stationery color: Letterhead should be printed with two colors: PMS 110 and PMS 295.

**Brand Voice:**

- Telephone greetings must reference the academy as 'Cal Maritime' or 'California State University Maritime Academy.'

**Notes:**

- All visual identities, including logos, seals, coat of arms, Keelhaulers logo, etc., are property of California State University Maritime Academy and require permission from Public Affairs & Communications for use.
- Approved logo files for all uses (print, web, presentations) can be provided by Public Affairs & Communications.

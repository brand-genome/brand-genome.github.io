## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Primary Logo and Secondary Logo are mentioned, including 'signature logo' and 'pole sign logo'. Two versions exist: one as the signature and another as the newer pole sign logo.
- Approved color options are Red (Pantone 1795), Yellow (Pantone 108), and Black. Additional color variations include Red/White, Black/White, Black/Yellow, Black/Red, Red/Yellow, and White.
- Do's and Don'ts emphasize that the logos must not be altered in shape, proportion, typography, or appear in unapproved colors.
- Pole sign logos have reversed or white variation, identified to be used sparingly.
- Spacing rule: Use 'BAR' as spacer for logos to ensure proportional consistency.

**Color Information:**

- Rudy's Red: Pantone 1795, HEX #d83a1f, RGB R:217 G:59 B:32, CMYK C:0 M:90 Y:100 K:10.
- Black: HEX #1a1615, RGB R:26 G:23 B:31, CMYK C:70 M:67 Y:67 K:80.
- Rudy's Yellow: Pantone 108, HEX #ffe413, RGB R:255 G:228 B:19, CMYK C:2 M:5 Y:98 K:0.

**Typography Information:**

- Sign Painter Slant: Headline and primary font for brand materials.
- Sign Painter Showcard: Used along with Slant font as an accent to create interest.
- Knockout HTF50: Regular font style used as needed.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Yellow background texture and brush effects contribute to the visual design attributes.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo spacing rule specifies 'BAR' as a standard spacer measurement for consistent use.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tagline is primarily used as a sign-off for print pieces.

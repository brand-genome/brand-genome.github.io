## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The primary ONE logo is simple, direct, and versatile. It should only appear in black or white, and must be used with 100% full color without any transparency or opacity. The logo may be placed on brand colors or imagery, but black or white should be used for best readability.
- Minimum size requirements for the ONE logo: For print, the logo should not be smaller than 5mm or 0.25 inches. For web, it should not be smaller than 30px.
- When writing about the organization, 'ONE' should always appear in uppercase. The URL should also use uppercase (ONE.ORG) and must be placed separately in a footer or corner.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone of voice for ONE is described as bold, energetic, human, fact-based ('factivist'), actionable, inclusive, and simple. It aims to inspire through data and human-centered storytelling.
- ONE's communication emphasizes positivity and possibility over peril. Jargon is avoided, and messages should be concise. Everything should be framed as a global movement, valuing diverse voices.
- Communication styles vary across formats (e.g., policy papers, social posts), but the tone remains uniform. The guidelines promote storytelling backed by facts.

**Notes:**

- The brand guidelines are intended to provide practical guidance for consistent representation of the brand across global audiences. Consistency and clarity are emphasized as key to building brand equity.

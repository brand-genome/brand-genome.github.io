## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Primary Logo: The mark is used on websites, mobile apps, or printed materials but should not imply official status or core team endorsement.
- Approved colors: The red logo is preferred for use on black or white backgrounds. A solid black or white logo can be used to contrast against other backgrounds. Only the white logo is permissible over Ember-red backgrounds.
- Tomster lockup: An alternative logo with a white stroke designed to work on all background colors without modification.
- Logo Usage Restrictions: Don't remove the ® mark, add effects, use unapproved colors, stretch, distort, box the logo in, or alter the logotype.

**Color Information:**

- Red, black, and white are official logo colors. The red logo is preferred for black or white backgrounds, while black and white logos can contrast against other background colors.
- White logo: Exclusive use over Ember-red backgrounds.

**Notes:**

- Logo file access is available on GitHub, making it convenient for stakeholders.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Main full color logo designed for white or off-white backgrounds. Includes variations intended for use on solid-colored backgrounds.
- Logo version without tagline, full color for white or off-white backgrounds, including variations for solid-colored backgrounds.
- Clear space/safe zone for the logo: clearance should be based on the height of the 'R' glyph. Minimum of three 'R' glyphs clearance when positioned towards a corner.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Colors: Navy (#001440), Sunset (#ff5400), Moss (#124040), Mint (#57ccab), Pink Rose (#f78cc7), Lemon (#fff700).
- Secondary Colors: Dark Grey (#45474d), Light Grey (#e5e5e5).
- Brand color usage includes guidelines on appropriate color combinations with do's and don'ts for contrasting and complementary shades.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family: Stevie Sans. Available weights: Thin, Light, Book, Regular, Medium, Bold, Black.
- Typography roles: Headlines use Stevie Sans Medium. Body text uses Stevie Sans Light.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Patterns categorized into landscapes (e.g., water, mountains) and animals (e.g., hummingbird, cheetah).
- Examples of pattern usage: under cutout imagery, overlay on images, and radiating from holding shapes.
- Guidelines on proper usage of patterns, including maintaining contrast with background, wrapping around important image subject matter, and ensuring the pattern doesn't obscure the key image items.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Holding shapes: multiple variations including Mound_Out, Mound_In, Hill_Out, Hill_In, Sun_Out, Sun_In, Landscape variations with '_Out' and '_In.'
- Layout examples showcasing text-data combinations (e.g., jobs created, hectares under restoration) integrated with imagery and graphical elements.

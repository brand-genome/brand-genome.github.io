## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The MassiveMusic logo includes stacked and horizontal versions featuring the wordmark.
- Logo allowed to be reproduced in Orange 021 C Pantone or 100% Black when Orange is not possible.
- Incorrect usage involves reproducing the logo in gradient tints of black or non-approved colors.
- Logo used in case studies positioned in top-left in white with 40% transparency.
- Logo dynamic modification allowed in playful cases but must seek approval; logo color need not always be orange.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes MassiveMusic Orange and MassiveMusic Black. Specifications: Pantone Orange 021 C - RGB: 255, 55, 0; CMYK: 0, 75, 100, 0; Pantone Black - RGB: 0, 0, 0; CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 100.
- Secondary palette provides complementary colors associated with energy and excitement: RGB color ranges such as RGB: 255, 100, 0; CMYK: 0, 60, 100, 0.
- White used for open feeling contrast; black supports sophistication.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font used: Basefont ITC Lubalin Graph BT 'Extra Light'.
- Stationery font suggestion: Akzidenz-Grotesk Bold & Regular in 10pt/12pt leading, flush left; fallback to Arial.
- Creative application copy amplified in bold typography that speaks confidently.

**Imagery Photography:**

- MassiveMusic visuals involve bold styling, modernist principles, and juxtapose minimal bold energy-driven campaigns.
- Web-level design emphasizes playfulness with creative layers of toggling (unique ON-OFF filters).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Newsletter template adheres aligned logical-gauge grid templates for strict neat coherence.
- Overall templates maintain coherent space balance uniform by organizational-wide stationery shown leveraged letter-worthy headers with proper fonts flush left.
- Posters disregarding rules intentionally fitted standout texts within dynamic spreads expressed takeaway memorability anchoring distinct messages.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice is characterized as 'serious fun', combining confident attitude with irreverence.
- Messaging rules maintain playful adaptation yet minimal clever phrasing central avoiding corporate tone contradiction.

**Notes:**

- MassiveMusic dynamic identity flexibility allows creative reinterpretivity within limited predefined playful bounds pre-approval.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- Core logo developed based on hand-drawn script with adjustments for consistency across various communication formats. It represents the unique identity of St. Ives with a neatened structure through its rugged coastline theme suited for both B2B and B2C applications.
- Reverse core logo: This version is inverted to white when used against sufficiently dark backgrounds to ensure legibility. It should remain unaltered and only used in approved formats.
- Primary logo: Two-tone blue signifying the sea surrounding St. Ives. Secondary logo works in black or reversed in images, while the events logo allows customizable block colors for the event theme.
- Seasonal versions: Specialized logotypes for Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter to encourage seasonal appeal and aid businesses in promoting out-of-season marketing opportunities.
- Logo element usage: Icons derived from the core logo, including symbolic wave and seagull. Used for branding items like ceramic marks, food packaging, and abstract print designs.
- Minimum size requirements: Preferred minimum size for the logo is 3.5cm wide. Other suggestions are outlined for particular use cases.
- Clear space rules: Exclusion zones require space equal to the height of the wave element in the logo. Protective spacing ensures clarity and brand prominence.
- Logo usage do's and don'ts: Guidelines include maintaining color palette, avoiding distortion or recreation of the logo, and adhering to layout rules and specified formats.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes bold and vibrant blue shades, providing distinct character. PMS Pantone codes - Uncoated 7703U and Coated 7459C. CMYK 69/19/0/27, RGB: 57/151/186, HEX: #3997ba.
- Secondary color palette adds Dark Blue, and Seasonal palettes for Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter featuring specific Pantone/CMYK/RGB/HEX details.
- Guidelines on color usage: Monochrome logos are 100% blue, usable on light backgrounds to ensure proper contrast.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: Merlo. Selected for versatility and legibility for body copy. It offers weights including Light, Regular, and Medium.
- Typography guidelines: Avoid scaling or altering the original font settings.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style: Unique, unexpected, and inspiring perspectives reflective of St. Ives values. Imagery should convey emotion, vibrancy, and a sense of place. Seasonal images show diverse weather conditions as positive elements, while avoiding empty streets.
- Image guidelines: Use complementing color palettes to enhance photographs. Avoid staged/posed shots and unaltered scaling or recoloring.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grid layout and protective spacing require maintaining the height of the logo wave for exclusion zones. This rule applies to every side of the logo.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice: Informal, friendly, and direct with inclusivity and plain-speaking. Messaging highlights quality and timelessness by promoting the community values of St. Ives.
- Taglines: 'St. Ives - the unique light of the South West', 'St. Ives - where memories are made for generations', 'Experience the best of Cornwall in St. Ives'. Seasonal taglines include descriptions for Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter.

**Notes:**

- Illustration: Custom illustrations created in-studio or via commissioned artists to reflect the brand identity, including patterns and infographics.
- Brand summary: Overview of St. Ives’ appeal as a year-round destination promoting unique local businesses and trades.

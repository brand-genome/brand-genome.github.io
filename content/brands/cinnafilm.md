## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The branding includes a full-color logo hierarchy with symbol, wordmark, and registration mark alongside reversed versions, grayscale versions, black-and-white bitmap, and single-color bitmap variations accessible upon request. Logo usage rules and examples are provided.
- Safe area guidelines mention maintaining visual clarity and legibility around the logo components. Standards given for spacing and alignment.
- Logo do's and don'ts are outlined to ensure consistent and professional usage.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors include 'Cinnabar Orange', 'Dark Energy Blue', and 'RadiantGrid Gray'. Corresponding HEX, RGB, and CMYK values are provided for each. Cinnabar Orange: HEX #EA4F12, RGB 234/79/18, CMYK 0/80/90/11.
- Secondary product colors specified for usage alongside primary colors include 'Wormhole Gold' and 'PixelStrings Purple'. Codes are defined for each. Wormhole Gold: HEX #FFC700, RGB 255/199/0, CMYK 0/22/100/2.
- Tertiary colors include Dark Gray (RGB: 95/95/95, CMYK: 62/53/53/24) and Rich Black (RGB: 0/0/0, CMYK: 75/68/68/90). Guidelines state limited usage for high-contrast and black & white formats.

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate font specified as 'Roboto' typeface available in Light, Regular, Bold, and Italic weights. Styles include headlines, subheadings, and body text depending on weight selection.
- Guidelines for fallback system fonts such as 'Helvetica Neue' (MacOS), 'Segoe UI' (Windows), and 'Tahoma' if 'Roboto' is unavailable.
- Roboto Mono specified as the preferred monospace font for code examples. Use regular and bold italic styles for syntax formatting.
- Display font specified for marketing materials: 'Neusa Next Condensed Medium'. Pragiti Narrow Bold mentioned as fallback when unavailable.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Mentioned details include maintaining designated spatial layouts and following safe area spacing rules for logos.

**Brand Voice:**

- Vision statement emphasizes developing tools for extraordinary moving images. Messaging guidelines include clarity and professionalism.
- Taglines to maintain consistency and evoke brand values. Presentation suggestions and word usage outlined.

**Notes:**

- Contact details for inquiries about guide assets or application mentioned as marketing attention to Laquie TN Campbell (Email: Laquie@Cinnafilm.com).

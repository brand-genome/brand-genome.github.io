## Brand Guidelines

### 2006

**Logo Information:**

- The horizontal corporate signature is the primary and preferred mark. The centred corporate signature may be used in limited circumstances where horizontal width is insufficient, with written approval from GHQ GMQ.
- Corporate signature with strapline showing both an English and long strapline version. Strapline must never be used independently.
- Minimum reproduction size of the corporate signature is 40mm. Protected area equals the height of the hexagon or HSBC lettering.
- Incorrect usage: Protected area must not be used as a frame for corporate signatures.
- Artist-created EPS files are available for corporate signature. A4 PDF specification sheets are provided with color reproduction details.
- Monochrome reproduction permitted: On light backgrounds, all elements must be black; on dark backgrounds, all elements must be white.
- The corporate signature is to be reproduced in black and HSBC red on white or very light backgrounds. Hexagon's interior must always remain white.
- The hexagon, main visual element of the brand, consists of four red triangles and two white triangles. It normally accompanies the HSBC logotype and must appear in white and red unless explicitly authorized otherwise.
- Reproduction artwork includes guide marks for measuring protected area and proper scaling. Minimum reproduction size is 40mm wide.

**Color Information:**

- HSBC corporate colors are: HSBC red (Pantone 1795c), black, white, and HSBC grey (Pantone 423c). Alternative light and dark color versions are defined.
- Four-color halftone reproduction of HSBC red: Cyan 0%, Magenta 94%, Yellow 100%, Black 0%. HSBC grey: 44% of Black.
- Monochrome reproduction options defined: all black on light backgrounds, all white on dark backgrounds, adaptable to HSBC grey and red.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary corporate typeface is Univers, including weights light (45), light italic (46), regular (55), regular italic (56), bold (65), bold italic (66), and semi-bold (unique to HSBC).
- Secondary corporate typeface is Monotype Times New Roman, used for stationery and supporting Univers. Approved variants include bold and italic styles.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum exclusion area around corporate signatures is equal to the height of the hexagon or HSBC lettering. Clearance should be increased wherever possible.

**Notes:**

- All marketing, promotional, sponsorship material, and merchandising must be approved by Group Marketing (GHQ GMQ) and comply with corporate identity policies.
- Brand guidelines include approved PDF specification sheets, designed for full-screen viewing. Navigation and viewing instructions provided.

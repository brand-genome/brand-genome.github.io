## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The Tiger S is the primary visual mark for Sewanee Athletics and should be used most often. Consistent use has made it a recognizable symbol. The secondary logo is 'The Sewanee Tiger,' which should always face right. It is the only officially approved tiger image.
- One-color options and two-color options with outlines are provided for primary logos in configurations such as with signature above, below, or on one line.
- Secondary logos are supported with one-color options, full-color options, and grayscale options.
- Primary and secondary logos are also provided with athletic identification incorporated, using both one-color and two-color options.

**Color Information:**

- Purple is the primary color (Pantone 268 C, CMYK c82/m98/y0/k12, RGB r88/g44/b131, HEX #582C83). Secondary colors include Vegas Gold (Pantone 7502 C, CMYK c6/m14/y39/k8, RGB r206/g184/b136, HEX #CEB888), White (CMYK c0/m0/y0/k0, RGB r255/g255/b255, HEX #FFFFFF), and Process Black (CMYK c0/m0/y0/k100, RGB r0/g0/b0, HEX #000000). Thread options are also specified for embroidery.

**Typography Information:**

- Superstar M54 is the primary font for Sewanee Athletics. It is the only font that can accompany the Tiger S or the Sewanee Tiger mark. The font does not have a lowercase version, and attempts to create one are prohibited.

**Notes:**

- The guidelines emphasize the importance of adhering to the standards provided in the document to achieve consistent and powerful branding. These rules apply to all materials created for Sewanee Athletics.

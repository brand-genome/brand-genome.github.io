## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Basic logo version is full-color positive designed for white background use in both print and digital.
- Black-and-white logo variants for single-color printing or when a colored version is not possible.
- Inverted logo version intended for black or very dark backgrounds.
- Logo usage on colorful backgrounds - negative version of the trademark is used when full-color positive is not possible.
- Clear space or safe zone around the logo defined as the height of the 'text block' accompanying the symbol (x); no elements should encroach in this space.
- Minimum size of logo: at least 4 mm height for print.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: Green. CMYK (100-0-100-0), RGB (0-150-64), Pantone 7482 C, HEX #009640.
- Primary color: Blue. CMYK (100-60-0-0), RGB (0-92-169), Pantone 3005 C, HEX #005ca9.
- Accent color: Yellow. CMYK (0-20-100-0), RGB (255-204-0), Pantone 109 C, HEX #ffcc00.

**Typography Information:**

- Typeface: Metropolis, used for titles and texts. Available weights: Light, Regular, Medium, Semibold, Bold, ExtraBold.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Definition of the protective area: an area representing the height of the 'text block' (x), to ensure good readability and visual strength of the logo.

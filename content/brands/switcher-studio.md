## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Primary Logotypes: The go-to logos for all brand communications. Includes the full logo with text and icon mark, logo without text, and strictly icon logo for small sizes.
- Black & White Versions: Use an inverted white logo for dark backgrounds or an all-black version when colors are unavailable.
- Clear Space: Maintain space around the logo equal to the height of the capital 'S' in Switcher. No graphic elements, texts, rules, or images should be within these bounds.
- Minimum Size Requirements: Print - 0.75 inches, Web - 60 pixels (Full Logo); Print - 1 inch, Web - 100 pixels (Horizontal/Vertical Logo); Print - 0.25 inches, Web - 30 pixels (Icon Only).
- Correct Usage: The logo can be used on primary/secondary palette colors, white, black, or gray backgrounds. Logos placed on images must have sufficient contrast to ensure readability.
- Incorrect Usage: Do not alter logo color, distort proportions, adjust icon placement, add effects, crop, rotate, resize individual elements, or place on images with poor contrast.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Colors: Orange (#ED5623, Pantone 1655C, CMYK 1 | 82 | 99 | 0, RGB 237 | 86 | 35), Dark Blue (#042C3B, CMYK 95 | 71 | 53 | 55, RGB 4 | 44 | 59, HEX #042C3B).
- Secondary Colors: Teal (#1EBDC1), Dark Gray (#138F99, CMYK 93 | 58 | 47 | 28, RGB 12 | 79 | 96, HEX #0C4F60), and Light Orange (#F37022, CMYK 0 | 70 | 53 | 0, RGB 243 | 112 | 34, HEX #F37022).
- Neutral Colors: White (CMYK 4 | 2 | 2 | 0, RGB 242 | 242 | 243, HEX #F2F2F3), Gray (#3F4E57), and Dark Gray (#515151).
- Color Usage: Lean heavily on the primary orange; use secondary and tertiary colors thoughtfully to maintain visual identity. Adhere to a general ratio of 60% primary, 30% secondary, and 10% tertiary colors.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Typeface: Montserrat (used for most body copy and headings).
- Secondary Typeface: Omnes.
- Substitute Typeface: Verdana, recommended when Montserrat or Omnes are unavailable.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery Style: Photos should carry the Switcher brand voice and not appear excessively staged. Keep the lighting and camera positioning purposeful.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo Clear Space: Maintain an area equal to the height of the capital 'S' of the logo as a buffer from competing elements, including text and images.
- Iconography Guidelines: Icons are created in a 0.4 square-inch space with a 1-pt stroke width and should not use black as a color. Ensure outlined strokes before scaling.

**Brand Voice:**

- Social: Video brings people together, emphasizing shared experiences and community-building.
- Limitless: Enable boundless creativity and remove constraints for creators to unleash potential.
- Simple: Strive for simplicity, avoiding complexity and focusing on a clear, user-friendly approach.
- Imaginative: Support creative ideas by providing tools to make any live video creation possible.
- Purpose: Video has the power to connect people and tell captivating stories from multiple perspectives.

**Notes:**

- Switcher Studio's story is shared on why they built the platform and what problem it addresses (lowering barriers to live video creation for businesses).
- Use icon gradients and patterns for visual cohesion. Gradients feature brand colors like orange (#F37022) and blue shades (#0C4F60, #042C3B).

## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The PlayStation®4 Pro logo is used to identify products in the PlayStation®4 Pro product group, including systems, supporting materials, supplied materials, advertisements, and sales promotion materials developed by the SIE Group. Use in third-party game content is prohibited.
- The authorized form of the PlayStation®4 Pro logo is shown as 'PS4 Pro_A'.
- The logo must be displayed in single colors from the basic palette (Black, White, PlayStation® Blue - Pantone 287C, CMYK: C100 M80 K0, RGB: R0 G55 B145). Secondary colors include PlayStation® Light Blue - Pantone 285C, CMYK: C70 M30 K0, RGB: R0 G120 B200, Pantone Cool Gray 5C (K40), Pantone Cool Gray 10C (K70), and Pantone 877C (Silver).
- Examples indicate logo use must always be in combination with the PlayStation® family mark. In videos, use at the beginning, body, and end.
- The vertical version of the logo should follow format 'PS4 Pro_F_A_V' for space limitations.
- Minimum size requirements are shown for different logo types:
- 'PS4 Pro_A': Minimum height 3mm (12 pixels); at 3-6.2mm (12-37 pixels), TM symbol must be deleted.
- 'PS4 Pro_F_A': Minimum height 5mm (20 pixels); at 5-10mm (20-59 pixels), adjust '®' to 1mm diameter.
- 'PS4 Pro_F_A_V': Minimum length 6.5mm (20 pixels); at 6.5-13mm, adjust '®' to 1mm diameter.
- Isolation zone rules specify space around the logo:
- 'PS4 Pro_A': 0.5a around all sides.
- 'PS4 Pro_F_A': 0.5H around all sides.
- 'PS4 Pro_F_A_V': 0.5a around all sides.
- Examples of incorrect logo usage:
- Do not change shape, proportions, placement of TM symbol, colors, or fonts.
- Avoid adding shadows, gradations, multiple colors, combining with other logos, or placing on complex/low-contrast backgrounds. Do not partially display or use the logo as a background element.

**Color Information:**

- Colors authorized for PlayStation®4 Pro logo use:
- Basic: Black, White, PlayStation® Blue (Pantone 287C, CMYK C100 M80 K0, RGB R0 G55 B145).
- Secondary: PlayStation® Light Blue (Pantone 285C, CMYK C70 M30 K0, RGB R0 G120 B200), Pantone Cool Gray 5C (K40), Pantone Cool Gray 10C (K70), Pantone 877C (Silver).

**Typography Information:**

- Recommended fonts: SST font family for hardware, software, and marketing materials.
Available weights and styles:
- UltraLight, UltraLight Italic, Light, Light Italic, Roman, Italic, Medium, Medium Italic, Bold, Bold Italic, Heavy, Heavy Italic, Condensed, Condensed Bold, Typewriter Bold.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo isolation zone specifications:
- 'PS4 Pro_A': 0.5a around.
- 'PS4 Pro_F_A': 0.5H around.
- 'PS4 Pro_F_A_V': 0.5a around elements.

**Brand Voice:**

- Correct trademark usage:
- Use 'PlayStation®4 Pro' with the registered trademark symbol (®) or quotation marks.
- Avoid altering spelling, capitalization, or spacing.
- In running text or headings, plain font without stylized elements is recommended.
- Usage of abbreviation:
- Full trademark use required for first reference; 'PS4TMPro' or 'PS4 Pro' can be used subsequently.
- Avoid changing spacing, line breaks, or capitalization.

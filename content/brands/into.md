## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The effectiveness of the INTO brand depends on its consistent application. Basic rules include: do not stretch the logo, do not place it on similarly toned backgrounds or photographs, do not recreate or translate the logo, do not use parts of it incorrectly, and adhere to exclusion zone rules.

**Color Information:**

- INTO primary color palette includes INTO Red (Pantone 1795 C, HEX #ed1c24, CMYK 0-100-100-0), Medium Red, Dark Red, and Black. INTO secondary palette includes other Pantone shades for differentiation (e.g., Turquoise Pantone 7466 C, CMYK 100-0-40-0).

**Typography Information:**

- The Neo Sans type family is primary, with variants like Light, Medium, Bold, Black, and Italic. Secondary typeface includes Arial for Microsoft Office use.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Appropriate photography guidelines include selecting multicultural, balanced student groups with world-class facilities and direct engagement shots. Examples available via INTO's digital asset bank.
- Prohibited images include poor quality, irrelevant stock photography, culturally insensitive content, and outdated technology. Specific items to avoid are mixed couples alone or bars/alcohol-related images.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing and grid alignment instructions specify adhering to exclusion zones for logos, assignment zones for straplines, and correct alignment for digital applications.

**Brand Voice:**

- Messaging tone focuses on values of partnership and success. Visual identity includes roundel geometries, bold typography, and effective iconography.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Primary Logo: Includes Wordmark (Nutanix) and Logomark (X symbol). Represents movement, velocity, and innovation.
- Clear space: Minimum clear space insulates the logo from distracting elements. Clear space is equal to the height unit of the Nutanix word.
- Minimum size: Logo width must be at least 1.5 inches for print (110px for digital). Under this size, only the Logomark should be used.
- Incorrect Usage Guidelines: Examples include not compressing, stretching, altering, or manipulating the logo. Avoid changing colors, breaking it apart, adding effects (shadows, outlines, etc.), or placing it on unapproved/distracting backgrounds.
- Correct Usage: Logo can be full-color on a white or light background, or in white on dark or approved high-contrast imagery.
- Usage of standalone X: The standalone Logomark (X) is an impactful option for smaller spaces. It should not be manipulated in size or proportion.
- Event branding: The .NEXT conference logos follow the Nutanix style, maintaining consistent visual representation. Event logos are available in white or gray.
- Logo pairing guidelines: When placing Nutanix's logo next to others (e.g., in collaborations), all elements should use the same color (all-white or all-black versions) based on the background.
- Spacing guidelines for logo alignment in partnerships: Defined proportional spacings, including consistent X-height measurement and Y divisions for the Nutanix logo and other elements.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Colors: Nutanix Blue (#024DA1 / RGB: 3, 78, 162 / CMYK: 100, 80, 0, 0 / PMS 2728), Nutanix Green (#AFD135 / RGB: 176, 210, 53 / CMYK: 36, 0, 100, 0 / PMS 382), and Dark Gray (#4C4C4E / RGB: 76, 76, 78 / CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 85 / PMS Neutral Black 85%).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Font: Gotham (used for headings and body copy). Encourages professional yet impactful messaging with various weights suitable for different content types.
- Gotham Acceptable Weights: Bold, Black, Ultra Black. Unacceptable weights include Light, Book, Thin, and Extra Light.
- Gotham Typeface Leading and Tracking: Leading is 120% of the font size, and tracking is generally +10% of the font size.
- Typography Hierarchy for Gotham: Includes recommended sizes and weights for H1 Headline (46pt, Light, Title Case), H2 Headline (32pt, Book, All Caps), H3 Subtitle (12pt, Book, Title Case), Body Text (9pt, Bold/Light), and Footnotes (8pt, Light).
- Secondary Font: Gotham Rounded, a supporting typeface for headlines and variations. Avoid using bold for Gotham Rounded.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography Style: Utilize high-definition, clean, and user-friendly images that align with Nutanix branding. UI shots and overlays are encouraged for branding consistency.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing between elements: Maintain proportional clear zones, guided by logo X/Y heights or proportional tracking and leading in typography.
- Collaboration and Co-Branding Layout: Maintain consistent logo sizes, paired with dividing lines, spacings, and alignments.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand Voice Guidance: The brand should be approachable, confident, and friendly. Videos should convey confidence and excitement about Nutanix products.

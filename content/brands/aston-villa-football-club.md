## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The AVFC crest is the primary logo, to only be reproduced as supplied and placed on clean, non-busy backgrounds. There are unboxed and boxed composite logo options, as well as stacked and mono options for specific use cases. Minimum height requirements: 35mm for print, and 130px for web.
- Do's: Use the crest as supplied on clean backgrounds. Don'ts: Do not distort, fade, crop, rotate, remove keyline, change/remove elements, edit colors, place on busy/colorful backgrounds, or apply shadows/watermarks.
- File format requirements: Logos must be supplied in high-resolution EPS format in full color, white, and in horizontal and vertical orientations.
- The AVFC crest includes clear space rules that must be maintained around the logo. The crest should not exceed a specified height relative to other elements such as partner logos.
- Specific use contexts for logo options: The unboxed option is for clear, dark backgrounds; boxed options are for complicated backgrounds; mono and stacked options are for rare use cases.

**Color Information:**

- Primary club colors: Claret (Pantone 229 C, HEX #660033, RGB 102, 0, 51, CMYK 0, 100, 15, 60) and Blue (Pantone 278 C, HEX #94BEE5, RGB 148, 190, 229, CMYK 40, 15, 0, 0).
- Accent colors: Yellow (Pantone Yellow C, HEX #FFE600, RGB 255, 230, 0), Green (Pantone 360 C, HEX #70B824). Additional metallic colors include Gold (Pantone 871 MC, HEX #D9A857) and Silver (Pantone 877 MC, HEX #8C9595).
- Color usage frequency: Predominantly claret and blue with a splash of yellow. Secondary palette is used only for occasional purposes, with metallics for special applications.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Hero shots of players involve roaring stances and an explosion of ferocity. Treatments include strong lighting and expressions of intensity, embodying the 'Fight Like Lions' ethos.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone: Passionate, proud, and purposeful. All communication should reflect the intensity, pride, and purpose of Aston Villa and its history. Examples provided include elevating excitement and passion in messaging.

**Notes:**

- The guidelines emphasize partnership branding and approval workflows, requiring partners to submit assets for review by the AVFC team before use.
- Contact details for queries are provided: Peter Wright (Head of Partnerships: peter.wright@avfc.co.uk) and Luke Smith (Partnerships Services Manager: luke.smith@avfc.co.uk).

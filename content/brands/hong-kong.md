### 2012

**Logo Information:**

- The Brand Hong Kong signature includes the logo type, the brandline, and the Dragon symbol with extending ribbons graphic. All elements are set in fixed format and must not be altered.
- The Dragon symbol and ribbons graphic represent elements like blue ribbons for the sky, red ribbons for Lion Rock (symbolizing the can-do spirit), and green ribbons for a sustainable environment.
- The minimum size for the Brand Hong Kong logo with the brandline is 40mm for all versions (English, Chinese, and bilingual).
- The primary signature without the brandline can be used if the brandline appears elsewhere or if space is limited. The minimum size is 30mm for all language versions.
- Minimum clear space around the logo must be at least 0.5x the height of the 'X' unit of the logo to preserve its integrity.
- Full-color versions of the logo should be reproduced using 4-color printing on a background offering sufficient contrast. Solid color versions may be used when gradient printing is not possible.
- Single-color logo versions in red (Pantone 485C), yellow (Pantone 1235C), or black may be used in specified situations and are intended for usage when the full-color version cannot be reproduced or lacks visibility.
- Preferred background colors are white and black for full-color logo applications.
- The single-color red logo (Pantone 485C) and yellow logo (Pantone 1235C) can be used on light and dark brand-negative backgrounds respectively.
- Incorrect logo usage includes flipping the symbol, rearranging logo gradients, altering typography, repositioning or resizing elements, distorting proportions, using on complex backgrounds, or placing additional graphics.

**Color Information:**

- Dragon colors: Red (Pantone 485C, RGB R:255 G:0 B:0, CMYK 100M 100Y) and Yellow (Pantone 1235C, RGB R:227 G:164 B:19, CMYK 40M 100Y). Ribbon colors: Purple (Pantone 254C), Blue (Process Cyan C), Green (Pantone 361C), and Orange (Pantone 144C).

**Typography Information:**

- Typefaces include Trade Gothic LH Extended, Trade Gothic LH Bold Extended, Trade Gothic LT Standard Light, Trade Gothic LT Std Regular, and Trade Gothic LT Std Bold No.2.
- The Brand Hong Kong logotype and brandline have specific color guidelines, including black and white, red and black, red and white, all red, and all yellow variations.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum clear space around the logo to ensure visibility; 0.5X rule applies for clear areas around all logo elements.

**Notes:**

- The logo is copyrighted. Final decisions about the logo application are subject to approval by the BrandHK Management Unit.
- The dragon symbol may only be used on its own in exceptional cases.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2012

**Logo Information:**

- The Jaguar logo consists of two elements: the leaper and the wordmark. These elements must never be separated or cropped.
- The chrome version of the Jaguar logo is used across all communications unless material or finish compromises its visibility. The outline version of the logo should then be used, with variations for larger and smaller applications.
- A specific chrome logo version is available for use on black backgrounds.
- Chrome logos should primarily appear on white backgrounds. Exceptional circumstances like TV end frames may use black backgrounds, but photographic mid-tone backgrounds should not be used due to legibility compromise.
- Clear space rules for print: Clearance above and below the logo = 0.15x width of the logo; Clearance to the sides = 0.25x width of the logo. For digital: Clearance above, below, and sides = 0.1x width.
- Minimum print size for chrome logo is 18mm; for monochrome logo is 12mm. Minimum digital size for chrome logo is 90 pixels; for monochrome logo is 60 pixels.

**Color Information:**

- Accent Color: Deep Red

RGB: 158, 27, 50

- Supporting Colors: Silver and Black.
Silver Pantone: 877

RGB: 167, 169, 172
HTML: #A7A9AC. Black is used for text and monochrome applications. Tints of black are applied at intervals of 10% between 90% and 20%. Silver gradation: 100% silver or 45% black to 90% black.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Typeface: Jaguar; Weights: Bold and Regular; Usage Roles: Headlines, titles, and subheads. Preferred usage is Uppercase (All Caps) for primary messaging.
- Secondary Typeface: Gotham; Weights: Bold, Book, Light; Usage Roles: Body copy and quotations (not for headlines).
- Fallback Typeface: Arial; Usage Roles: Websites and PowerPoint live copy when Gotham is unavailable.
- Headlines Styling: Set to uppercase over a maximum of four lines using Jaguar Bold font with leading set to 110% of the point size (e.g., 40pt text should have 44pt leading). Occasional kerning might be needed.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear Space Area Guidelines: Avoid placing visual elements within defined clear spaces around the logo.
- Nameplates and Range Placement: Nameplate for vehicles must be directly above the headline and sized twice as large as the range positioned alongside, sharing the same baseline. Applies only to XK, XJ, and XF ranges.

**Notes:**

- Fonts directly downloadable from referenced URL link (Gotham font): http://www.typography.com/fonts/font_styles.php?productLineID=100008
- Ensure nameplates use correct hyphen positioning and uppercase formatting with 'All Caps' option turned on.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2007

**Logo Information:**

- The Vale logo is composed of the 'Vale' symbol and logotype, which have fixed position and size relationships. The symbol and logotype are inseparable and should not be used in isolation. The name 'Vale' must not be translated into other languages. The design, relationships, and colors of the logo are defined in the digital files provided.
- The minimum clear space required around the Vale logo is half the height of the 'Vale' symbol. This area must be free of any interference.
- The minimum size of the Vale logo is 10mm in width. The proportion between the logo's height and width is fixed at 1:2.164.
- The logo must not be centered on any visual piece. It can be placed to the left or right, at the top or bottom, but not in the center. The logo should occupy no more than 1/4 of the total visual area of any piece. Exceptions include the Vale flag or cases with minimal fields such as branded gifts.
- The logo can only be applied on a white background. If placed on photos or colored backgrounds, a predefined white area must be created around it.
- Examples of incorrect use of the logo include distortion, rotation, inclination, usage in vertical orientation, altering typography, reversing or changing the colors, changing the proportions of the symbol and logotype, rearranging the elements of the logo, associating words or slogans with the logo, adding visual effects or outlines to the logo.

**Color Information:**

- The primary colors of Vale are special shades of green (Pantone 328), yellow (Pantone 124), and gray (Pantone Cool Gray 9). RGB, CMYK, and HEX codes are provided for Internet and print applications, with variations for shiny and matte surfaces.
- Green-Vale codes: Pantone 328 (shiny/matte), CMYK (100, 10, 55, 10 - shiny; 100, 20, 60, 0 - matte), RGB (0, 126, 122), HEX (#007E7A). Yellow-Vale codes: Pantone 124 (shiny), Pantone 7406 (matte), CMYK (00, 30, 100, 08 - shiny; 00, 20, 100, 05 - matte), RGB (237, 177, 17), HEX (#ECB11F). Gray-Vale codes: Pantone Cool Gray 9 (shiny/matte), CMYK (00, 00, 00, 65), RGB (145, 145, 145), HEX (#919191).

**Typography Information:**

- For titles and headings, the typeface 'PMN Caecilia' is used, with titles being large and light-weight. Roman and bold styles are only used for low-visibility or reduced-size text. Running text uses 'Myriad Pro', ideally in regular weight, though light and bold styles are also allowed. Italic styles should generally be avoided, except for foreign expressions. On the Internet, 'Arial' and 'Arial Bold' should be used.
- The name 'Vale' in running texts must always be capitalized in a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters (e.g. 'Vale') instead of all uppercase.
- Institution and business line names should use 'Myriad Pro Regular' in all uppercase letters.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery guidelines instruct avoiding mosaic or kaleidoscope images, epic images shot looking up at people, and posed/artificial photos. Instead, images should focus on an 'eye-level' perspective inviting dialogue, with characters interacting in dynamic and action-oriented scenes. Color palette should avoid competitor colors (e.g. red/blue).
- The visual rhythm in films and photographs should be dynamic, vibrant, and lively, conveying movement and energy. The storytelling theme should align with the concept of a 'dynamic landscape'.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The proportion between the logo's height and width is fixed at a ratio of 1:2.164.
- Business cards are designed in the 86mm x 54mm format, printed using serigraphy with specified text size and alignment rules to ensure clarity. Color specifications for the text follow corporate color codes.
- Electronic presentations must follow templates provided with predefined colors and layouts. The text should maintain a minimum font size of 12pt, written in dark gray with highlights in the selected palette color for the presentation.

**Brand Voice:**

- Vale's tone of voice emphasizes creating light, inspiring, and dynamic communication aligned with the core attributes and values of the brand.

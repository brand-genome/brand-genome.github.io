## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The Brandlective logo comprises core brand colors and is provided in horizontal, stacked, and standalone 'B' icon versions. Preferred use is with typography in black or white. White-out versions can be used with white boxes as necessary.
- Minimum size requirements for logo usage are specified as 20mm x 18mm (stacked) and 45mm x 12.5mm (horizontal).
- The diagonal lines within the logo are set at 45°. The 'B' in the logo must always remain centered relative to typography.
- A 25% spacing margin should be maintained around the logo, with specific increased spacing requirements of 50% horizontally.
- Unacceptable logo usage includes altering the logo, fading, using non-brand colors, rotating, placing typography over elements, adding effects like strokes or backgrounds, using distorted or pixelated versions, or violating minimum size requirements.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes: HEX #5bbc99, CMYK 63-2-51-0, RGB 089-186-153; HEX #3283C3, CMYK 78-41-1-0, RGB 048-130-194; HEX #3FA9DF, CMYK 67-17-0-0, RGB 061-168-222; HEX #4E4897, CMYK 83-84-4-0, RGB 077-071-150; HEX #ffba00, CMYK 0-30-100-0, RGB 255-186-000.
- Secondary color palette includes: HEX #ff7700, CMYK 0-66-100-0, RGB 255-119-000; HEX #df0062, CMYK 7-100-41-0, RGB 223-000-098; HEX #e5008c, CMYK 4-100-0-0, RGB 229-000-140; HEX #2f3552, CMYK 86-79-42-37, RGB 047-053-082.
- Background colors include black and white, used where necessary.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family: Roboto (Roboto Black, Roboto Bold Italic, Roboto Bold, Roboto Italic, Roboto Regular, Roboto Light Italic, Roboto Light, Roboto Thin); Used in print and online, with Roboto Light for headings/subheadings (uppercase) and Roboto Black for body text.
- Logo-specific font family: Diavlo (Diavlo Black, Diavlo Book); Used for the logo with kerning set at 200. Diavlo also used for headings (uppercase). Subheadings/body text may use lower case or sentence case.

**Imagery Photography:**

- General photography: Use only HD images, with 72 dpi for digital use and 300 dpi for print. Avoid enlarging images above the original resolution.
- Imagery style: Images should convey a team-like and creative atmosphere. Background imagery with Brandlective overlay colors (80% opacity) is encouraged.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing requirements for the logo include a 25% margin around the logo, with 50% spacing requirements horizontally.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand voice character is defined as curious and bold. The tone is personal and informal, aiming to engage and educate. Language is savvy and inviting.
- Messaging examples emphasize clear, concise, and entrepreneurial themes, such as 'Does your brand ooze credibility?' and 'It's time to rise above the noise.'

**Notes:**

- Trademark information: Registered under UK trademark number UK00003068833, filed on August 15, 2014, in class 35.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The primary optile logo is green and should be used only on a white background. Other variations include white logos for colorful, dark, or busy backgrounds.
- Exclusion zone rules: The exclusion zone is defined by clear space equivalent to the height of the Möbius top element (marked as 'x'). This is the minimum safe distance recommended for logo visibility and prominence.
- Do not alter, change orientation, color, font, composition, or gradient of the logo. Examples include avoiding position changes, outline additions, and masking photos.
- Examples of incorrect usage include: using the green logo on busy backgrounds, using the grey logo on random colors or dark grey, and using the white logo on light grey backgrounds. Place logos on areas with minimal clutter.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors used in the logo: Mantis (#66CC66, RGB 102, 204, 102; CMYK 73, 0, 86, 0; Pantone #7481U/#7480C), Spanish Green (#008940, RGB 0, 137, 64; CMYK 100, 0, 100, 21; Pantone 355U/348C), and Dim Grey (#B6B6B6, RGB 107, 107, 107; CMYK 0, 0, 0, 72; Pantone 446U/424C).
- The green logo incorporates a gradient comprising Mantis and Spanish Green. Digital and print applications emphasize consistency.

**Typography Information:**

- No explicit typography details provided. Any fonts mentioned might be embedded geometrically within the logo wordmark as Dim Grey.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Background treatment for imagery: Logos must be placed on minimal clutter zones and should avoid busy, heavily textured backgrounds.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Exclusion zones and spacing are defined using the Möbius top element ‘x,’ ensuring no competing visual elements infringe on logo clarity.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand aims to portray simplicity, seamlessness, and consistency in visual identity, which reflects its promise and values as a provider-independent payment platform.

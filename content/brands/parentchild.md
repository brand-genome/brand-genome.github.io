## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- New logo design inspired by a supportive and optimistic spirit, showcasing rounded letter forms, soft corners, and bright colors for a playful energy.
- Clear space rule: Minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the 'e' in the logo.
- Minimum size requirement: Logo width cannot be less than 1.5 inches for legibility purposes.
- Color variations for the logo include full color, black, and white single-color logos, adaptable for different backgrounds such as photography and fields of color to ensure legibility.
- Logo usage over photography: Guidelines provided for using full-color logos on lighter backgrounds, knocked-out white logos on darker backgrounds, and appropriate logo variations on fields of color to maintain legibility and contrast.
- Incorrect logo usage examples: Do not stretch, outline, rearrange, apply non-approved colors, rotate, or add a drop shadow to the logo.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette: Deep blue (PMS 2955 C, CMYK: 100/60/10/53, RGB: 0/56/101, HEX: #003865) and teal (PMS 3268 C, CMYK: 86/0/53/0, RGB: 0/171/142, HEX: #00ab8e).
- Secondary color palette: Bright yellow (PMS 7549 C, CMYK: 0/22/100/2, RGB: 255/181/0, HEX: #ffb500) and vibrant pink (PMS 1925 C, CMYK: 0/97/50/0, RGB: 224/0/77, HEX: #e0004d).
- Tertiary color palette: Rich gray (PMS 424 C, CMYK: 30/20/19/56, RGB: 112/115/114, HEX: #707372).
- Color breakdowns provided for coated stocks (Pantone, CMYK, RGB, HEX) and uncoated stocks.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Montserrat, a free Google font with various weights: Bold, Regular, Light, and Black.
- Fallback typeface: Arial for Microsoft applications and emails, including styles Bold, Regular, Italic, and Black.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Logo placement guidelines on photography: Use full-color logos for lighter backgrounds, white logos for darker backgrounds, and adjust logo variations to maintain legibility when placed on fields of color.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rule around the logo: Minimum space equal to the height of the 'e' in the wordmark.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone described as supportive, optimistic, joyful, and agent of social change. Reinforced through logo design and tagline lockups.

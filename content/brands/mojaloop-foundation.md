## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The Mojaloop Foundation logo should be used in reference to the entity itself. The Mojaloop logo should be used in reference to the project.
- Three approved versions of the Mojaloop logo include Color (standard for digital/full-color print), Black (for limited-color print and light-colored backgrounds), and White (for dark-colored and photo backgrounds).
- Clear space: Minimum clear space is equal to the width and height of the 'm,' ignoring ascenders and descenders. Minimum size: In print, the logo should appear no smaller than 20 mm (0.75 in) wide. On screen, the logo must be at least 50 px wide.
- Proper use cases include a contrast ratio minimum of 5:1 for logo usage on backgrounds and when overlaying on photography. Always use the color or white versions based on background tone.
- Improper use cases include stretching, compressing, adding effects such as shadows or gradients, changes to logo color, placing on busy textures, or modifications like changing font.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: White (RGB: 255, 255, 255; HEX: #ffffff; CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 0) and Blue.
- Secondary colors provided for visual interest and differentiation of information. They should be used sparingly.
- Color proportion wheel defines usage of the brand palette, emphasizing blue introduction first, white space, and neutral tones like black and gray for typography.
- Tints and shades of primary and secondary colors can be used for highlights, shadows, charts, and graphs for more complex data sets.

**Typography Information:**

- Varela Round is the display typeface chosen for its legibility and modern look. Open Sans is the body typeface chosen for its clean, versatile, Humanist sans-serif design.
- Font sizes, leading, and tracking: Varela Round uses metric kerning and tracking at -10; leading is 18 pt or smaller at 120% or 18 pt at 110%. Open Sans at 12 pt or smaller has leading at 140% and above 12 pt at 120%.
- Open Sans weights: Regular, Italic, Bold, Bold Italic for smaller than 10 pt text; Light, Light Italic, Semibold, and Semibold Italic for 10 pt or larger text.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style prioritizes authenticity, avoiding hired models, stock photography, or artificial staging. Focus on real individuals benefiting from Mojaloop.
- Best practices include shooting subjects to appear empowered (below eye level), using warm light and rich colors, applying the rule of thirds, and avoiding clutter.
- Bad examples include lack of clear focal points, overly staged or stock imagery, subjects not representing Mojaloop users, and black and white photography.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The color proportion wheel recommends prioritizing blue, maintaining white space, and integrating neutral tones like black and gray primarily for typography.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand photography humanizes Mojaloop's mission, emphasizing empowered subjects and positive, hopeful moods.

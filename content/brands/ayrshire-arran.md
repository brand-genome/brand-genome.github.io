## Brand Guidelines

### 2010

**Logo Information:**

- The logo includes versions such as Process Colour, Greyscale, Black & White, and Reversed.
- Spot colour logo created for exceptional circumstances like vinyl signage or embroidery.
- Clear space around the logo must be defined by the letter 'A', which ensures legibility. Minimum size with the strapline: 33mm x 15mm; minimum size without the strapline: 22mm x 10mm.
- Do not alter proportions of the logo, change fonts, resize or reposition its elements, or place on busy backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes specific Pantone shades such as Pantone 7496c, Pantone 7534c, Pantone 7486c, and others.
- Complementary colors inspired by the coast, sky, and landscape. E.g., for the coast: Pantone 364c, Pantone 357c; for the sky: Pantone 361c; for the landscape: Pantone 376c, Pantone 298c.

**Typography Information:**

- Main typeface to be used: Frutiger. Mandatory for all print publications, logos, and straplines. Variants: Light, Light Italic, Roman, Roman Italic, Bold, and Bold Italic.
- Arial may be used in digital documents or applications where Frutiger isn't available, such as in Word or PowerPoint.

**Imagery Photography:**

- The swoosh element can be used as an overlay on photographs. Suggested usage involves 30% opacity; alternatively, 'Multiply' and 'Softlight' effects can be used in tools from Adobe CS3.
- The swoosh can be modified to create areas that support titles or other information within photography.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Define a clear space around the logo using the letter 'A' as a measurement guide. Avoid placing graphic elements in this space to maintain legibility and integrity.
- Minimum size guidelines: 33mm x 15mm for logos with straplines and 22mm x 10mm for logos without straplines.

**Brand Voice:**

- The slogan emphasizes different sectors using lines like 'Just be here', 'Just be active', 'Just be relaxed', and more, catering to tourism, business, and lifestyle markets.

**Notes:**

- The brand emphasizes rural charm, outdoor activities, and cultural heritage, targeting varied sectors including tourism, business, and relocation. Each target audience has personalized messaging.
- Art files (logos, colors, swoosh elements) are provided in the Brand Toolkit, compatible with Adobe CS3 and available in EPS, JPEG formats.

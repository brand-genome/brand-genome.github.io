## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- WordMark, Horizontal WordMark, ChallengerMark, Horizontal ChallengerMark, Team WordMark, Reversed Logos. Minimum sizes and exclusion zones specified.
- Exclusion zones require clear space equivalent to the height of the primary letters (X).
- Minimum sizes for WordMark and ChallengerMark are specified for print (1 inch and 1.5 inches, respectively) and digital (250px).
- Incorrect usage of Partner Lockup is defined to ensure vertical centering, correct size, and avoiding improper backgrounds or clear space violations.
- Team logos using the NYYC Burgee must follow strict approval requirements and placement rules.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors: Blue (Pantone 282, HEX #041E42), Red (Pantone 193, HEX #BF0D3E), Gray (Pantone 420, HEX #C7C9C7).
- Secondary brand colors: Black (HEX #000000), White (HEX #FFFFFF), Gold (Pantone 464, HEX #8F5A28).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: Metropolis (variations include Bold, Extra Bold, Italic).
- Secondary fonts: Helvetica Now Text (Bold, Light, Thin variations), Calluna (Regular, Bold, Black).
- Typography roles: Metropolis for headlines, Helvetica Now Text for subheadlines and body copy, Calluna for formal or stylized uses.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Apparel branding specifies consistent logo placements (e.g., Adorned Burgee on left chest, Event Patch on left sleeve, Airbus on right arm).
- Helly Hansen logo placement rules for apparel include specific sizes (e.g., 60mm for tech outerwear).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Exclusion zones for logos (WordMark, ChallengerMark, Team Logos, Partner Lockups) require clear space equivalent to the height of primary letters (X).
- Partner Lockup logos have minimum sizes of 3 inches (print) and 275px (digital). Master & Staged logos must follow background clarity rules.

**Brand Voice:**

- Approved Challenger titles include 'Challenger 36th America's Cup' and 'AC36.' Titles must capitalize America's Cup and Challenger.
- New York Yacht Club must always be fully spelled out, never abbreviated as NYYC.
- America's Cup trademarks (including the silhouette mark) must adhere to ACPI guidelines and proportions.

**Notes:**

- All logos and marks require prior approval from the New York Yacht Club or ACPI; unauthorized use is not permitted.

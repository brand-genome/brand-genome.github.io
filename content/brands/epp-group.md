## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- EPP Group logo versions include: full logo with text and glyph, glyph-only version for decorative or icon use (social media, favicon, etc.).
- Safe area (clear space) is defined as the height of the letter 'e' in EPP. Minimum logo size: 12mm or 16px for digital, glyph minimum size: 30mm.
- Logo variations for use include: full-color on white or clear grey backgrounds, monochrome options, and white logo for EPP Blue background, EPP Pattern, or photographs.
- Logo usage guidelines prohibit distortion, changes to font, color, rotation, 3D effects, or position changes.
- Specific logo cohabitation rules state the EPP Group logo must not be smaller than other logos in proximity. Exceptions are allowed for 3rd-party website or print applications where recoloring is permitted for matching purposes.
- EPP logo variations are provided in multiple languages (BG, CS, DA, DE, EL, EN, ES, ET, FI, FR, HR, HU, IT, LT, LV, MT, NL, PL, PT, RO, SK, SL, SV).

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes EPP Blue (Pantone 3015 C, CMYK C96 M66 Y3 K0, RGB R1 G85 B160, HEX #0155A0) and EPP Yellow (Pantone 1365 C, CMYK C0 M36 Y84 K0, RGB R251 G176 B52, HEX #FBB034).
- Secondary colors include RGB and CMYK codes: Yellow (#FFE600), Teal (#369A9D), Orange (#FC8037), among others with gradients allowed (-60° angle, gradient slider 30%).
- Guidance provided for EPP Group services with defined color palettes (e.g., Parliamentary Works: RGB R140 G151 B204, HEX #8C97CC, CMYK C51 M39 Y0 K0).
- Prohibits the use of old corporate identity color codes or names.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: PF Beau Sans Pro for headings, supporting Latin, Greek, and Cyrillic scripts.
- Secondary font: Garamond for print body text. Trebuchet MS is the secondary font for PowerPoint, Word documents, and newsletters.
- Web-specific primary font: Open Sans, available in weights from Light to Extrabold and supporting extended Cyrillic and polytonic Greek.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Background imagery should highlight the subject and avoid simply placing images with reduced opacity. For clarity, increase contrast with dark EPP Group palette colors (85% opacity) or blur images. Avoid background images for text-heavy layouts as it can be tiring for readers.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear spacing for icons and glyph safe space must be maintained. For icons, allow small spacing between glyphs; for 32px and 16px favicons, no spacing is required. Background should be EPP Blue, except for white backgrounds in 16px favicons.
- Graphic heart element as part of the logo may not be distorted but can be integrated within designs without an exclusion zone.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone guidelines emphasize clarity, avoiding jargon, overuse of adjectives, or unnecessary enthusiastic language. Communication should be approachable, optimistic, collaborative, and human, avoiding overly professional or emotional tones.
- Active rather than passive voice is encouraged: 'The party increased in size' instead of 'Numbers were increased by the party.'
- Address the audience directly using pronouns where appropriate, and ensure a storyline approach in messaging.

**Notes:**

- This style guide is intended as a reference for communication materials across mediums (online, print, gadgets) to ensure cohesion with the EPP brand identity.
- Templates for newsletters and other materials are to be based on these guidelines. Graphic design rules apply to visual identity, font usage, color choices, and illustrative principles.
- Access specific logo versions and variations via www.eppgroup.eu/logos.
- Social media guidelines: Use glyphs over EPP Blue and avoid overcrowding banners with text. Icons should be white or blue.

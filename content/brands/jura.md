## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The logotype may be used in negative versions (white reserves) on colored and black-and-white backgrounds.
- The logo can be used as a monochrome white version on dark-colored backgrounds or dark images and as monochrome black version on light-colored backgrounds or light images.
- When placed beside another logo, the logo of Conseil Départemental must be aligned according to a specific schema where the body of the logo and the height and base of the letters 'URA' are the reference volumes.
- Displayed examples for usage on white, dark, and light backgrounds, and mixed usage in institutional contexts.
- The peripheral blank space around the logo (clear space) must be two times the height and width of the thickness of the letter 'J'. Minimum size requirement for reproduction: 12 x 16 mm.
- The shape, typography, and color of the logo must never be transformed, deformed, or moved. The components of the logo are inseparable.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: Open Sans Condensed Light for body text and captions.
- Secondary font: Open Sans Condensed Bold for emphasis and headings.
- The fonts can be downloaded from: http://www.fontsquirrel.com/fonts/open-sans-condensed.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The blank surrounding space (clear space) for the logo must be two times the height and width of the thickness of the logo's letter 'J'.
- Minimum size requirement for logo reproduction is 12 x 16 mm.

**Notes:**

- For all other inquiries, use the communication contact: communication@jura.fr.

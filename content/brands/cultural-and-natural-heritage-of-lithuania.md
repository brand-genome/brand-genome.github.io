## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The main brandmark has the form of a postage stamp, combining the wordmark 'LITHUANIA' with the slogan 'REAL IS BEAUTIFUL.' It is used as a lock-up and its graphic shape cannot be altered.
- Clear space around the brandmark must be at least 1/4 of the height (X) of the main brandmark. Additional space is recommended where possible. Minimum width for print is 20 mm and for digital use is 160 px.
- Dark green brandmark should be used on dark backgrounds. A light grey outline ensures legibility when used sparingly on light backgrounds. Exceptional versions are available for etching, engraving, or restricted color printing.
- Unacceptable usage includes altering proportions, using unofficial colors, changing the typeface, or omitting the slogan.
- The wordmark and slogan can be used without the postage stamp only in exceptional cases like thin objects (e.g., pens) where space is constrained. It is available in dark green, black, or white versions.
- Additional marks can be combined with the main brandmark to denote specific tourism purposes. These marks cannot be altered, including their colors and text.

**Color Information:**

- Main colors include Dark Green (CMYK: 85 25 60 10, RGB: 0 130 115, HEX: #008273, Pantone: 569), Dark Grey (CMYK: 0 0 0 80, RGB: 88 89 91, HEX: #575656, Pantone: Cool Gray 11), and others like White and Black for text and background use.
- Additional marks use tertiary colors, such as CMYK 0/20/30/0 (RGB 250/210/180) and CMYK 25/0/5/0 (RGB 180/225/240). These colors are used sparingly for charts and graphs.

**Typography Information:**

- The main typeface is 'TT Chocolates' in Regular, Bold, Italic, and Bold Italic. It is used in external applications. Arial is the secondary typeface for usage in software like Word and PowerPoint. Roboto is recommended for digital use.
- Regular weight is recommended for most text, including body copy and headlines. Bold and Italic weights are used for emphasis. Minimum font size is 7 pt, with dark grey or black recommended for small text.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography must feel real and believable, avoiding excessive retouching. High-quality images by professionals are mandatory. Environments can appear unpolished as long as they convey authenticity.

**Spacing Layout:**

- 'X' measurement determines lock-up sizes, margins, and distances in layouts for A-format applications. Brandmark lock-ups may be positioned in any corner, taking adjacent imagery into account.
- For a roll-up poster (2000 x 850 mm), the recommended size for the brandmark and additional marks is 180 x 142 mm.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone emphasizes authenticity and avoids being boastful. The slogan 'REAL IS BEAUTIFUL' aligns with the brand's focus on authenticity.

**Notes:**

- Master artworks and working files are provided by the State Department of Tourism. All photographs originate from sources like Shutterstock and Unsplash.
- Templates for roll-up posters, publications, and souvenirs (e.g., canvas bags, umbrellas, USB sticks) are provided in AI (Adobe Illustrator) format.

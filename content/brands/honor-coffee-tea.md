## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The logo system includes a Primary Icon, Wordmark, and Lockups. Lockups feature combinations between the icon and wordmark, including vertical and horizontal formats designed for large-scale and small formats.
- The primary logo is referred to as the 'H' icon. It symbolizes the brand's identity and must be uniform across applications. Alterations are prohibited.
- Vertical stack logo lockup mandates exact proportions and spatial relationships. Alterations are strictly prohibited.
- Horizontal stack logo lockup similarly maintains exact proportions and spatial relationships. Alterations are prohibited.
- Horizontal signature format is specifically for instances requiring small graphic footprints while respecting exact proportions.
- Vertical signature lockups retain strict spatial relationships and proportions similar to other configurations.
- An accent Red Dot Logo is available for special occasions surrounded by primary brand elements. Proportions and relationships are fixed.
- Clearance rules mandate at least 25% empty space around the logo and strict adherence to specific centering of the icon.
- Logo violations mentioned include prohibited fake effects such as drop shadows, strokes, 3D effects, stretching, color changes outside the palette, and use on unapproved backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Gold Foil (Pantone 872 M) and White. Secondary colors include Pantone 7626 C (red), Cool Gray 1, Black 7, and Pantone 289 (blue). Color tints are permissible for subtleties.
- Specific color swatches include CMYK, RGB, HEX, and RAL codes for Gold (#8E774D), Ripe Coffee Cherry (#C63527), Wave Accents (#D9D9D6), Italian Marble (#7D7773), Blackened Steel (#3d3935), and Raw Denim (#0C2340).
- Color hierarchy emphasizes dark foundational colors, accented whites, and subtle accents for mid-dark tones.

**Typography Information:**

- The headline font is Craw Modern (Regular & Italic), paired with Trade Gothic for complementary text styles. Univers LT Std Condensed is used for captions.
- Typography hierarchy specifies Craw Modern Italic (16 pt), Craw Modern Regular CAPS (45 pt), Trade Gothic (20 pt, bold condensed), and Univers (6 pt for captions).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Brand patterns derived from the 'H' icon include the wave pattern (top portion of the icon) and diagonal icon patterns (dots as pearls from the Marquess crown). Patterns should be tone-on-tone and subtle.

**Spacing Layout:**

- For logo placement, leave at least 25% clearance space around the logo. Adhere strictly to horizontal and vertical centering rules with the crown and 'H' icon.

**Brand Voice:**

- Honor Coffee focuses on respect for guests and honoring the craft while exuding hospitality, intentionality, graciousness, and other servant-hearted qualities.
- The brand represents confidence and aesthetic excellence without arrogance, akin to subtle luxury.

**Notes:**

- The crown logo is inspired by ancient Chinese noble ranks and family lineage; it includes symbolic pearls, a crest, and the 'H' icon.
- Brand applications include touchpoints like drinkware designs, printed collateral, wall menus, blade signage, and exterior signage concepts.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The 'Blason' (shield) logo is derived from artisan and pirate symbolism, reflecting a 'makers' spirit. The logo may also be redrawn manually.
- 'Blason' logo in a cartouche requires a clear space equal to 25% of its height for safe usage. Minimum size for legibility is 8 mm in width.
- The centered composition of the logo has a vintage artisan touch. It may also be manually redrawn for a 'makers' aesthetic.
- Centered logo composition in a cartouche requires clear space equal to its height, and needs generous space on communication materials. Minimum size is 60 mm in width.
- Examples of centered composition usage provided.
- Improper usages of the centered logo: cannot be off-center, misshaped, tilted, disassembled, or colored differently from black & white.
- The left-aligned composition is simpler and practical for narrow materials. It can also be manually redrawn.
- Left-aligned composition within a cartouche requires clear space equal to 50% of the Blason height. Minimum size is 30 mm in width.
- Examples of left-aligned composition usage provided.
- Improper usages of the left-aligned logo: cannot be aligned right, tilted, misshaped, disassembled, or colored differently from black & white.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Black (C:0, M:0, Y:0, K:100, HEX: #000000) and White (C:0, M:0, Y:0, K:0, HEX: #FFFFFF). The logo should primarily be used in black, and white on black as needed.
- Accent colors: Warm Red (C:0, M:87, Y:80, K:0, HEX: #FA443F) and Metallic Gold (C:21, M:41, Y:100, K:2, HEX: #CA941D) for packaging (red for red wine, gold for white wine).
- Preferred paper materials are textured, matte, and uncoated (e.g., kraft or brown cardboard) to convey brand authenticity. Bright white papers should be avoided.

**Typography Information:**

- Bureau Grotesque: A condensed sans-serif typeface ideal for titles and small paragraphs. Variants include Condensed Book, Condensed Light, and Light styles.
- Mrs. Eaves: Serif typeface for body text and emphasized titles using small capitals. Minimum size: 9 pts, below which Bureau Grotesque is preferred.
- These typefaces reflect the brand's historical and artisan roots. Examples of typographic compositions provided for various applications.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery should align with artisan, vintage, and authentic aesthetics, reflecting the mood of the brand.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space around the 'Blason' logo must be at least 25% of the logo height in general usage or 50% for left-aligned compositions.
- Minimum size requirements are 8 mm width for the Blason logo, 30 mm width for the left-aligned logo, and 60 mm width for the centered logo.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone is authentic, artisanal, experimental, and slightly rebellious, aligning with 'makers' and nonconformist values.
- Key phrases: 'Live Authentic,' 'Because perfection is boring,' 'Made in Paris,' embodying the spirit of the brand.

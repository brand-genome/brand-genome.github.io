## Brand Guidelines

### 2009

**Logo Information:**

- The primary logo for NC State University is referred to as 'The Brick,' approved for all branding materials and communications from or about the university.
- There are two approved color options for the logo: (1) Red (Pantone PMS 186) and white, and (2) black and white.
- Clear space requirements around the logo are based on the x-height of 'N' in NC STATE. No design elements, type, or photos should come closer than ½x.
- The brick logo should maintain its proportions. Always use approved files, and constrain proportions when scaling.
- Minimum size requirement: The logo should not appear smaller than 1⅜ inches in print or 100 pixels wide on screen.
- The logo should always be placed in the upper-left corner, between ¼ inch to ¾ inch from edges. It should avoid being placed as a bleed at any page edge.
- Do not recreate or redesign logos independently. Use approved versions only.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The social media logo is designated for official University accounts. The torch emblem should never be used alone as an identifier in social media.
- Department wordmarks have limitations (e.g., must not exceed three lines), provide flexibility for internal use, and require approval for external applications.
- Department lockups are centered on alignment and require consistent spacing half the height of the torch box between the logo and department name.
- Department lockups must feature the department name in Gotham Bold, right-aligned, uppercased, and colored uniformly with the logo.
- In incorrect usage of department lockups, multiple lockups on a page should not be used or logos resized disproportionally.

**Color Information:**

- NYU Violet primary palette includes PMS 2597, HEX #57068c, RGB R:87 G:6 B:140, and CMYK C:86 M:100 Y:0 K:0.
- Neutral palette includes HEX codes such as #2b0346 (R:47, G:27, B:80).
- Arabesque patterns should strictly adhere to monochromatic palette rules.
- Color overlays allowed only using NYU Abu Dhabi palettes; ensure legibility of images and headings remains intact.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary fonts for English are Gotham and Mercury Text with variations such as Gotham Extra Light, Light, Medium, Black, and Ultra.
- Primary Arabic fonts for print include TheSansArabic and Nassim Arabic, whereas Droid Arabic Naskh is used for online Arabic text.
- Alternative substitution fonts for administrative use in English include Arial (replacing Gotham), Georgia, and Times (replacing Mercury).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography aims to capture energy and enthusiasm within the NYU Abu Dhabi community, using reportage style for dynamic images or natural light for refined portraiture.
- Color overlays are utilized for images to add graphic depth but should maintain legibility when applied.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing between the bottom of the logo and department name in department lockups must be equivalent to half the torch box height.
- Department lockups must be properly aligned to the right edge of the logo text block with equal distances maintained.

**Brand Voice:**

- Content distributed on behalf of NYU Abu Dhabi should reflect formal language and emphasize professionalism aligned with institutional branding.
- Video guidelines emphasize storytelling, dynamic aesthetic, and use of captions or title text readable across devices.

**Notes:**

- NYU Abu Dhabi requires prior approval for the use of its brand elements such as the Arabesque pattern, logo, and department lockup on merchandise or creative projects.

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- Primary logo includes bilingual elements (English and Arabic). Designed with torch in box logomark and NYU logotype. Alternative vertical usage is permissible for spatial reasons, rotated counterclockwise by 90 degrees. Clear space requirement ensures no text, photos, or graphics within the clear space. Minimum size of .5 inches in print or 60 pixels digitally for full logo. Logomark and logotype sizing must remain proportional.
- Color versions supported include NYU Violet (PMS 2597), black, and white. Violet preferred when available. Logo can appear as white on dark backgrounds to ensure readability.
- File format recommendations are not explicitly mentioned but references to print (.5 inches minimum height) and digital (60 pixels minimum height) suggest compatibility with digital and print formats.
- Clear space rule defines spacing as equivalent to the size of the torch in the logomark.
- Minimum recommended size for logo is .5 inches height (print) or 60 pixels height (digital). Minimum size for logomark alone is .25 inches (print) or 30 pixels (digital). Full logo and logomark must always be grouped and sized proportionally.
- Logo misuse examples include incorrect usage or alterations: use of incorrect colors; rearranged elements or removal of elements (torch, Arabic text); stretching/distorting the logo; redrawing the logo; incorrect placement with Arabesque background.
- Placement suggestions: Recommended in corners instead of center-aligned. Use white logo over dark or busy backgrounds only with sufficient contrast.
- DO NOT: Use dark versions of the logo (purple or black) on dark backgrounds where contrast doesn't make it stand out. Ensure the torch in the logo remains white.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include: NYU Violet (PMS: 2597, CMYK: C85, M100, Y0, K0, RGB: R87, G6, B140, HEX: 57068c); Black (PMS: K100, CMYK: C0, M0, Y0, K100, RGB: R0, G0, B0, HEX: 000000); White (CMYK: C0, M0, Y0, K0, RGB: R255, G255, B255, HEX: FFFFFF).
- Secondary colors for print include: Oasis (PMS: 320, CMYK: C100, M0, Y19, K23, RGB: R0, G141, B169, HEX: 008ba9); Desert Sun (PMS: 7413, CMYK: C0, M53, Y100, K4, RGB: R239, G139, B33, HEX: ef8b21); Sunshine (PMS: 7406, CMYK: C0, M18, Y100, K0, RGB: R244, G205, B48, HEX: f4cd30); Turquoise Sea (PMS: 5503, CMYK: C29, M0, Y10, K14, RGB: R127, G190, B198, HEX: 7fbec6); Sea Port (PMS: 292, CMYK: C70, M28, Y0, K0, RGB: R65, G152, B211, HEX: 4198d3); Sunset (PMS: 1935, CMYK: C0, M83, Y80, K10).
- Secondary digital-only colors include: Avocado (RGB: R111, G154, B72, HEX: 6f9a48); Amethyst (RGB: R137, G102, B160, HEX: 8966a0).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Guidelines suggest avoiding busy backgrounds for placement of the logo. Ensure contrast sufficient for logo readability (use white logo on a dark background).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Use clear space around logo equivalent to torch size in logomark. Recommended placement aligns logo in corners to suggest hierarchy.

**Brand Voice:**

- Consistency across visual identity contributes to representing NYUAD's uniqueness and differentiation. Visual identity should positively reflect the institution.

**Notes:**

- The guidelines emphasize consistent and cohesive use of visual identity standards to reinforce NYU Abu Dhabi’s global identity.
- Merged branding of NYU logotype and bilingual Arabic script ensures recognizability and prevents unauthorized usage.

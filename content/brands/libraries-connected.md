## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Brand Voice:**

- Libraries Connected describes itself as a forward-thinking and collaborative organization that believes in the power of libraries to enrich lives and build inclusive societies. It champions positive impacts in communities and facilitates connections among individuals, professionals, and stakeholders.
- Key values include being inspiration-seekers — pursuing innovative solutions and excellence, forward-thinkers — leading on best practices and sustainable library systems, collaborators — encouraging partnerships, and connection-makers — fostering national and international collaborations.
- The organization's vision is to create a modern, sustainable, inclusive, and high-quality public library service at the heart of UK communities.

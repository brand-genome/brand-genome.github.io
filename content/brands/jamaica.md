## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The JAMAICA® brandmark is the cornerstone of our visual identity, representing our spirit and pride. It should primarily be used in color but is also available in black and as reversed out of black. Examples of incorrect use include stretching, skewing, adding shadows, embossing, or changing colors beyond the approved palette. The tagline should always be paired with the brandmark, positioned either below or beside it. Minimum size for reproduction is 0.9 inches in width.
- Illustrated icons and taglines must align with the brandmark. Icons should never appear without the tagline or in unapproved colors. Positioning of tagline elements and relationships between logo, tagline, and icons must be preserved.

**Color Information:**

- The color palette includes vibrant tones indicative of JAMAICA's culture. Primary colors include Caribbean Pink (PMS Process Magenta C, Hex #D40F7D), Jammin Yellow (PMS 3965 C, Hex #EECC00), and Bayside Blue (PMS 7459 C, Hex #4298B5). Secondary shades include Tropical Tangerine, Avocado Green, Paradise Purple, Cool Grey, Bold Black, and White. Color combinations must adhere to accessibility and brand representation rules.

**Typography Information:**

- Earthwerk is the official campaign typeface. This hand-drawn, quirky type emphasizes the tone of the campaign, aligning with the illustrations and icons. Body text uses 'Intro Regular,' while headlines are capitalized in Earthwerk with consideration of ligatures for stylistic effects.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography focuses on vibrancy and reflects themes of nature, culture, food, romance, and activities. Images must be saturated and appropriately masked to complement the logo and other brand assets. A texture pattern further unifies photographs with campaigns.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum spacing is defined for brandmarks and associated tagline lock-ups. Layouts must ensure consistency across print, digital, and out-of-home applications. Grids and justified alignments maintain alignment with visual guidelines.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone is vibrant, joyous, and welcoming, reflecting JAMAICA's essence: positivity, energy, and spirit. Messaging emphasizes connection, optimism, and a unique sense of belonging.

**Notes:**

- The document provides an overview of the campaign, detailing asset integration in print ads, taxis, and digital banners. Icon libraries, color palettes, and typography ensure uniformity across mediums.

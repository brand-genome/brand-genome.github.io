- Logos
Masterbrand logo
The masterbrand logo is one of our most recognisable brand assets. It should be used when the Live Well For Less brand promise doesn’t apply. The default colour of the masterbrand logo is orange on white.
The absolute minimum width of the masterbrand logo is 27mm in print and 100px online.
The minimum amount of clear space around the masterbrand logo is equal to the capital S. For campaigns where the masterbrand logo is positioned freely, it must have twice that amount of clear space all around it.
- Logos
Live Well For Less logo
The LWFL logo is used whenever the Live Well For Less brand promise is relevant. The default colour of the LWFL logo is orange on white.
The absolute minimum width for the LWFL logo is 24mm in print and 95px online.
For formats where the logo size is not specified, the general rule to determine the LWFL logo’s size is to multiply the diagonal length by 0.13 for portrait, and 0.1 for landscape.
The minimum amount of clear space around the LWFL logo is equal to the capital S. For campaigns where the LWFL logo is positioned freely, it must have twice that amount of clear space all around it.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography
Typeface
Our typeface is Mary Ann. Created for Sainsbury’s, it is unique to our brand and is the only typeface we use.
Extra bold, medium, regular and light weights are used; italics for emphasis but not style.
Size ranges from 6pt in print to 9pt online minimum.
- Typography
Principles
Leading settings:
This is 24pt type with 26pt leading.
Paragraph spacing is set to half leading size.
Character tracking is zero by default.
Headlines: Large orange headline flips subheading color to plum.

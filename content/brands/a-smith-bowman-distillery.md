## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The primary logo consists of the still, the signature, and 'DISTILLERY'. Recommended file formats include .eps, .ai, .png, .jpg, and .tiff.
- The light version of the logo is used when the background color is dark-colored, and the dark version of the logo is used when the background color is light-colored.
- Alternate logo version is used when the primary logo does not fit the space required due to height, such as in website banners, small promotional goods, or ads with limited space.
- Clear space is determined by the width of the still and should surround the logo to ensure visibility and impact.
- Examples of incorrect logo usage include rotating, stretching, squashing, changing the color, or placing the logo inside shapes.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color system includes Brown (Pantone: 7519 CP, HTML: #5E5145, RGB: 94 81 69, CMYK: 24 42 45 68) and Yellow (Pantone: 7506 CP, HTML: #F2DBB3, RGB: 242 219 179, CMYK: 00 07 25 01). They are to be used dominantly for internal and external presentations.
- Secondary color system includes complementary colors such as HTML #9C4124 (Pantone: 174 CP, CMYK: 08 86 100 36, RGB: 155 65 36) and #C92A39 (Pantone: 186 CP, CMYK: 02 100 85 06, RGB: 201 42 57). Metallic (HTML: #89764B, RGB: 137 118 75) colors are also mentioned.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font is Caslon's Egyptian, available in bold and regular weights, suitable for uppercase and lowercase alphabet styles.
- Secondary font is Caslon Antique, available in bold and regular weights. It is intended to evoke the Colonial era with its decorative style.
- Specific typography hierarchy includes: Headlines (Caslon's Egyptian Bold—48pt/48pt), Title Typography (Caslon's Egyptian Bold—34pt/30pt), Sublines (Caslon's Egyptian Bold—16pt/16pt), Context Text/Inner Headlines (Caslon's Egyptian Light or Regular).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery guidelines recommend warm tones, genuine candid shots of people, depth of field, and etching illustrations as a part of A. Smith Bowman Distillery's brand image system.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space around the logo is defined using the width of the still, ensuring consistent visibility and impact.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand voice is described as: humble, conversational, knowledgeable (not a know-it-all), friendly, familiar, fun, and generous, reflecting Southern hospitality and friendliness.
- Strict discipline is maintained for the brand name 'A. Smith Bowman Distillery' to distinguish it from past associations with 'Bowman' and reposition it as a high-quality crafts spirits brand.

**Notes:**

- The brand guidelines emphasize consistency in usage across mediums and adherence to recommendations provided in the document.
- 'Pioneer Spirit' acts as a tagline and creed, representing bravery, exploration, and the quest for discovery, tied deeply to the Bowman family's historical legacy.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The GreenPower Accredited logo is to be used for primary brand communications and comes with a Product Disclosure Badge showing percentage accreditation. Logos must maintain clear space equal to the height of lowercase letters (x). Minimum logo width is 45mm.
- Minimum clear space around logos equal to height of lowercase letters (x). Minimum width of Product Disclosure Badge set to 55mm.
- Full color logos preferred for integrity and consistency. Mono versions (black/white) allowed for newsprint and WCAG 2.1 accessibility compliance.
- Incorrect usage restrictions: No misleading usage of logos with unaccredited products or outside brand contexts. Logos cannot appear simultaneously with non-accredited renewable electricity promotions.

**Color Information:**

- Primary logo colors include Pantone 7482 C (Green) and Pantone 2965 C (Blue) with respective HEX (#009F4D and #00263E), RGB, and CMYK values.
- Secondary color palette available for background, charts differentiation. Pale Mint and similar tints for accessible contrasts. Colors must comply with WCAG 2.1 contrast ratios.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography used must be owned/licensed by GreenPower, with commercial printing requiring 300 DPI resolution.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rules for logos: minimum clear space equal to height of lowercase letters (x). Width guidelines include minimum widths of 45mm for standard logos and 55mm for disclosure badges.

**Brand Voice:**

- Messaging guidelines prohibit references to unaccredited portions of products and restrict claims if below 100% renewable accreditation. All environmental claims must be backed by research and Australian Consumer Law compliant.

**Notes:**

- WCAG 2.1 color contrast compliance applies to all design elements for accessibility.
- File formats specified for publishing include PNG, JPEG, EPS, RGB, CMYK, and PMS. Usage is defined by medium (print vs digital).

## Brand Guidelines

### 2025

**Logo Information:**

- The primary color version of the logo is red on white or light backgrounds. The secondary version is white on various colored backgrounds, and there is a monochrome version (black on white or light backgrounds). These are the only allowed versions.
- The clear space around the logo must correspond to the height and width of the letter 'V' in the logo. Associated text, if needed, can be placed within the clear space but should ensure good legibility and a balanced design.
- Co-branding guidelines recommend respecting clear space rules and arranging logos in a visually balanced way.
- Do not use colors other than those specified in the guidelines. The logo must always be reproduced consistently and accurately to maintain brand integrity.

**Color Information:**

- Primary logo color: Pantone 185 (CMYK: 0, 94, 78, 0; RGB: 224, 0, 52; HTML: #E00034).
- Secondary colors include Pantone 169, 184, 630, 631, 187, 633, and complementary colors (e.g., Pantone 1205, 1225, Cool Grey 11, and Black 6). CMYK, RGB, and HEX codes for each are listed for usage.
- Color guidelines allow tinting colors to lighter variations for graphic content, charts, and supplemental uses. The purpose is to maintain legibility and communicative intent.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Euclid Flex. Used for headings and shorter messages in advertising. Available in multiple weights.
- Secondary typeface: Roboto. Used for complementary messages in advertising and body text. Roboto Serif is preferred for printed material, and Roboto Sans Serif for digital materials.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography captures reality authentically, with an emphasis on atmospheric and personal elements. Images are not staged, except for portraits, and should highlight the environment and culture of Nuuk.
- Event photography aims to preserve the atmosphere of the moment. Images should be taken at eye level to maintain authenticity and connection with the events.
- No AI-generated images are allowed. All visuals must authentically represent the real experiences of Nuuk, grounded in transparency and integrity.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The clear space of the logo is defined as the height and width of the letter 'V'. Text or elements inside this area must be balanced and legible.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone of voice is respectful, real, open, quirky, and proud. It reflects the personality of Nuuk and aims to connect personally with the audience.
- Communication levels include: ATTEND (create attention with bold and memorable concepts), ENGAGE (create passion and inspire the audience), and INFORM (provide clear and necessary information).

**Notes:**

- The logo and website address are sometimes used together or separately, with Euclid Flex Medium specified for the web address.
- AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action) is a key concept in structuring ads, ensuring clear communication and call-to-action.
- Derived pictograms are used for signposts and markers, designed with international standards and local cultural inspiration.

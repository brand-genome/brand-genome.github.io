### 2010

**Logo Information:**

- The Herman Miller logo consists of a circle with a stylized 'M' and text in the Meta font. The logo must not be altered in typography, proportions, or colors. The logo should be sized at a minimum of 16-point type.
- The Herman Miller logo must be used with adequate clear space around it — measured as at least the diameter of the logo's mark. The logo must not be used as a background, combined with other graphics, or obscured in any way.
- When presenting a dealership, the Herman Miller logo is secondary to the dealer's name and logo. The relationship must follow a rule where the dealership's identity is scaled to four times the height of the Herman Miller tagline for prominence.
- The Herman Miller logo can appear in three color variations: Pantone Warm Red 2, black, or white. In RGB, Herman Miller Red translates to R:255, G:0, B:0. In CMYK, the values are C:0, M:93, Y:100, K:0.
- The stylized 'M' within the logo circle must match the background color if placed on a colored background.
- Approved digital formats for the logo include 72 DPI (for web or screen), 300 DPI (for print), and vector files for purposes like embroidery or engraving.
- Do not distort, recolor inconsistently, use as bullets, or manipulate the logo creatively in ways that hinder its recognition.

**Color Information:**

- The official Herman Miller Red is Pantone Warm Red 2. Its RGB code is: R:255, G:0, B:0. CMYK breakdown: C:0, M:93, Y:100, K:0.

**Typography Information:**

- The Meta font family is crucial for company branding. The specified weights are Meta Plus Book, Normal, Medium, Bold, and Black. The logo itself uses a modified version of Meta Plus Medium.
- Meta font usage: book weights are recommended for long documents like proposals; normal weights for larger sizes; bold and black weights for emphasis.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Ensure clear space around the logo: at least the size of the logo's diameter should remain free of surrounding graphics or text. No background applications allowed.
- In dealership branding, the dealer's logo must prominently feature above the Herman Miller tagline, following the rule of maintaining a 4:1 height ratio.

**Notes:**

- Dealerships may request the Herman Miller logo font for maintaining consistent proportions in digital usage. Fonts include optimized weights for micro, normal, and large display purposes.
- Trademarks, copyrights, and other branding elements of Herman Miller must be credited appropriately. Refer to HermanMiller.com for the updated list of trademarks.

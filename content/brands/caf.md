## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Guidelines for CAF's standard logo emphasize a strong and consistent corporate image for both internal and external communications. The logo’s proportions, colors, and formats are structured and accessible in Adobe Illustrator, PDF, and JPG formats.
- Logo protection zone: The visual space surrounding the logo (equivalent to the height of the 'C' in the logo), which protects it from other graphical elements. Sub-logos are authorized if applied within these bounds.
- Authorized versions of the logo: Preferred applications respect 100% of the color scheme, including versions for specific printing constraints (e.g., grayscale for faxes or monochromatic reproduction for stamps). Variations for anniversaries and milestones are also detailed.

**Color Information:**

- Corporate colors for CAF: CMYK (C:0 M:100 Y:100 K:5), RGB (R:187 G:17 B:25), Pantone 711. Complementary grey is given as CMYK (C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:70), RGB (R:111 G:111 B:110), and Pantone 7540.
- Various color usages: CMYK for full-color catalog printing, two-spot colors for cards, and RGB for digital mediums like websites.

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate font family: Helvetica Neue. Titles are styled with Helvetica Neue Light or Bold, depending on the text’s prominence (headlines vs. minor highlights). Subtitles are red for a unique contrast.
- Text layout examples include Helvetica Neue Bold (20pts with 22pts spacing), Light (20pts, 22pts), and a smaller size for body text (9.5pts with 11.4pts spacing).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography and visual styles emphasize consistency and alignment with corporate branding.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing rules for written texts: Titles in corporate grey, using Helvetica Neue Light. Highlighted sections should use red color sparingly — no more than 5% of the text should contain highlights.
- Corporate stationery layout: Consistency in logo positioning and adherence to standardized dimensions is mandatory across applications like business cards and letterheads.

**Brand Voice:**

- CAF's tone of voice emphasizes confidence, innovation, and state-of-the-art technical expertise. Messaging avoids redundancy, focusing on specific technical achievements and milestone-driven narratives.

**Notes:**

- Cases not covered within this manual must receive explicit approval from the CAF Marketing Department. All deviations must be submitted via 'identidadcorporativa@caf.net.'
- Historical revisions of the CAF logo were consolidated to eliminate inconsistencies in proportions, color, and formatting.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Logo usage guidelines including requirements to use the Trapeze logo on all external communications, maintaining specific components (wordmark and curves). The corporate tagline is 'Here for the journey.'
- Clear space requirement: keep a border of space around the logo equal to the height of the 'T' in the Trapeze logo. Prevent clutter around the logo.
- Minimum size for print is 1 inch (25.4 mm) wide. For web, it is 100x24 pixels. Exceptional cases like pen logos allow smaller sizes.
- Logo color variations: two-color (Trapeze Gray and Trapeze Red) and black and white variants are allowed. Colors must not be altered.
- Logo violations include: incorrect colors, drop shadows, borders, placing the logo on unsuitable backgrounds, distorting/stretching, outlining the logo, adding patterns, tints or gradation, and removing text or logo portions.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Trapeze Gray (#333333) and Trapeze Red (#990000). [Trapeze Gray] Pantone 432, CMYK C23+M2+Y0+K77, RGB R45+G52+B55. [Trapeze Red] Pantone 1955, CMYK C0+M100+Y60+K37, RGB R157+G0+B32.

**Notes:**

- Guidelines suggest reaching out to the Marketing group if there are questions regarding proper implementation.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The logo is a wordmark designed with custom geometric type. Each character is custom built, carefully spaced for legibility and optical balance. It is designed to work on light and dark backgrounds with duochrome versions for contrast.
- Logo versions on backgrounds: logo on image, logo on dark background, logo on bright background.
- Minimum size for logo usage: Avoid using the wordmark at a size smaller than 27mm or 100px.
- Clear space rule: A minimum area of surrounding space is required across all visual communication. The exclusion zone must be relative to the size of the character 'a' of the wordmark.
- Incorrect usage examples include altering colors, stretching, disproportionate scaling, using the logo in a pattern, adding graphical effects, or changing the composition or contrast of the logo.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: Eerie Black (#1B1B1B), White (#FFFFFF), Purple (#5920B1). Purple is noted as the primary color for backgrounds.
- Secondary colors: Medium Slate Blue (#8C58FF), Ultramarine Blue (#2162F7), Sky Blue Crayola (#08E3FD). Secondary colors are paired with primary colors and used when the primary palette cannot be utilized.
- Gradient colors created for key visuals: #8C58FF, #6623AC, #08E3FD, #6A2EFD, #2162F7, #3C66CC, #382275.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: Sharp Sans No.1, inspired by Herb Lubalin's design for Avant Garde magazine. Weights include Light, Book, Medium, Semi-Bold, and Bold.
- Secondary font: Chivo, a grotesque sans-serif font. Weights include Light, Regular, and Bold. Chivo Black is ideal for highlights and headlines, while Chivo Regular is used for body text.
- Typesetting rules: Heading uses Sharp Sans Bold with auto leading and default tracking; Sub-Headings use Sharp Sans Bold with leading 1 point above font size; Body Copy uses Chivo font in Light, Regular, or Bold with automatic line spacing.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style: Images should capture detail, depth, and dimension with a clean modern look. Subjects should be in focus with incidental captures that portray diversity.
- Prohibited imagery: Avoid staged photography, low-resolution images, and overly complicated compositions. Stereotypical and stock images lacking warmth are not allowed.

**Spacing Layout:**

- All compositions for digital and print communications should be 1500px by 1500px with a minimum resolution of 150 DPI.
- Layout rules: Logo should be placed at the top-left or bottom-left. Main captions use Sharp Sans No.1 Bold at 60pts or 36pts; footer text uses Chivo Light or Bold at 18pts.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand tone is modern, professional, and clean. Layouts prioritize simplicity and clarity in all media formats.

**Notes:**

- Application guidelines include designs for stationery such as letterheads, business cards, and identity cards.
- Social media designs follow all visual identity guidelines and can be used for both digital and print materials.

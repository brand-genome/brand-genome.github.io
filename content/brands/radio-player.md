## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Primary logo is used on mediums with a white background. For print collateral (publications, advertising, billboards, posters, flyers, product packaging), the reverse logo on a red background is recommended.
- Minimum logo size should not be smaller than 160px x 70px. For smaller sizes, 'Canada' text is removed from the logo for clarity.
- Logo is designed using a modular grid square base to ensure proportional management.
- Black & white publications require grayscale logo version.
- Color publications should use the Radioplayer red as a background and the reverse logo.
- Negative space guidelines: Always leave space around the logo with white or neutral backgrounds. Minimum print size is .5 inches in width.
- Logo misuse examples include changes in element distribution, font, distortion of proportions (horizontal/vertical), and poor quality (pixelation/low resolution).

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: Red (HEX: #eb1e23, CMYK: 0/100/100/0, RGB: 235/30/35).
- Gradient Dark: HEX: #8e191c, CMYK: 30/100/100/30, RGB: 140/25/30.
- Gradient Light: HEX: #e11e25, CMYK: 5/100/100/0, RGB: 225/230/40.
- The primary colors reflect the Radioplayer personality.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: Myriad Pro Regular. Used for manuals, graphics, and advertising pieces.
- Secondary font: Myriad Pro Bold. Used for headlines and key brand messaging. Headlines are red on white background and white on red background.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone and manner: Optimistic, ambitious, patriotic, futuristic, polite, and confident.
- Brand personality: Adventurous, polite, and confident.
- Brand voice/sound: Curious and adventurous.

**Notes:**

- Radioplayer Canada offers almost 400 radio stations in English and French. It provides music, news/talk, sports, and entertainment content.
- Radioplayer began in the UK and has expanded internationally. Radioplayer Worldwide is a partnership with 7digital and other countries adopting this model.
- Website and social media guidelines: Website text is in Myriad Pro Bold with capitalization of the first letter of each word. Social media icons are red on a white background and vice versa, appearing in the order Facebook, Twitter, Instagram. Icons' height matches the font size of the website URL.
- Brand framework highlights brand DNA, role, personality, beliefs (inclusive and diverse), and behaviors (self-discovery and adventure).
- Core brand attributes: Freedom, Canadian, authentic, adventurous, inclusive (differentiating attributes include diverse tastes and optimism).
- The brand archetype is 'Explorer' with the aim of delivering unique experiences and freedom to choose your adventure.

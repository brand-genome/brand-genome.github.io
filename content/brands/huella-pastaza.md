## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- Logo illustrated version and simplified version shown.
- Horizontal and vertical logo orientations demonstrated. Variant vertical and horizontal logos are shown.
- Small horizontal logo and small isotipo version presented.
- High contrast logos demonstrated.

**Color Information:**

- Simple and complete color palette shown with RGB codes: (215,174,123), (222,200,174), (214,186,153), (192, 19, 46), (82,71,65), (51,51,51), (196, 156, 109).
- CMYK codes provided for multiple colors: (12,26,42,3), (11,19,23,2), (13,21,30,3), (19,93,64,6), (19,30,46,6), (51,46,49,34), (62,48,48,48).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typography is Fira Sans Bold. Complementary typography also provided.
- Typography inspiration drawn from petroglyphs and jaguar footprints.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery emphasizes Amazonian themes such as women with baskets, agriculture (chacra), agro-productivity, cosmetics, and traditional crafts.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Horizontal and vertical alignment for branding elements shown under 'Aplicaciones de marca' (Brand Applications) including signs, labels, and merchandise.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand values: Social Responsibility, Organic Production, Benefit to Small Entrepreneurs, and Amazonian Identity.
- Mission: To be the best Amazonian EcoStore and expand to regional and national markets, forming a network of EcoStores.
- Vision: Promote quality, fair price, healthy production, and customer satisfaction.

**Notes:**

- Brand heavily incorporates Amazonian cultural themes such as traditional crafts, farming, and local ingredients.
- Decorative elements include high-contrast negative and positive variations shown for branding.

- Which version do I use?
The word mark logo is our primary logo.
The appearance of our name in the world is important as it creates awareness for our brand. Therefore, this logo should be used in almost every circumstance.
Shorthand okay only in specific uses:
Social app icon, Medallia product navigation, favicons, swag (only if full logo exists elsewhere on piece).
If the logo is smaller than 90x24px, please use the shorthand.
- Do note use an adjusted small version of both the logo & shorthand.
Please use the small version when the logo or shorthand is 24px in height or smaller.
- DO
Use color logo on white, sand or light gray when possible.
Use color shorthand logo on white, sand, or light gray when possible.
Use one-color shorthand on dark backgrounds — use indigo when possible.
Use one-color logo on dark backgrounds — use indigo when possible.
- DO
Allow logo to breathe by preserving a minimum clearance, equal to the height of the letter 'M'.
- DON’Ts:
Change color of the diamond.
Use color logo on backgrounds without appropriate contrast.
Use color logo on a background where it becomes unreadable.
- DON’Ts:
Remove the diamond from either mark.
Add text or other logos to the logo or shorthand. Consult the brand team for exceptions.
- DON’Ts:
Stretch the logo or shorthand.
Print the logo smaller than 100px or smaller than an inch in width or height.
Rotate the logo or shorthand.
Change the diamond's shape in the logos.

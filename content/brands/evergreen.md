## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The foundational elements of the updated logo include the evergreen tree, shoreline, mountain, and sun, which have been refreshed to align with Evergreen's energy and personality. The primary logo is labeled as 'Your Way to the World' and works better for digital applications. Variations include the primary, secondary, and full-name versions, with or without the tagline. Guidelines are provided on specific usage for each version.
- The logo suite includes Primary Logo (with tagline, without tagline, and with location), Secondary Logo (with tagline, without tagline, and with location), and Full-Name Logo (with tagline, without tagline, and with location). The tagline version is used in most applications.
- The minimum size for logo reproduction: Primary and full-name logos must be at least 1 inch wide, and secondary logos must be at least 1.375 inches wide. Clear space rules state that the clear space around the logos should equal half the size of the mark.
- Logo color versions include full-color, 1-color green, 1-color black, and full-reversed. Guidance for usage includes using full-color for most materials, 1-color when cost is a consideration, and reversed for applications on colored or photographic backgrounds.
- Improper logo usage guidelines: Do not substitute colors for any elements in the logo. Do not squeeze or alter proportions, add graphic elements, place logos on densely patterned backgrounds, or use incorrect fonts. Do not add shadows or special effects.
- Logo usage files must be sourced in approved digital formats from Marketing and Communications. Mentioned formats include high-resolution files but exact formats (e.g., EPS, PDF) are not explicitly listed.

**Color Information:**

- Core Palette consists of colors used across all communications and applications, including PMS, CMYK, RGB, and HEX. Colors are: Forest (PMS: 7743 C, CMYK: 71.8.100.50, RGB: 38, 103, 38, HEX: #266726), Stone (PMS: 430 C, CMYK: 33.18.13.40, RGB: 114, 128, 138, HEX: #72808a), and Cloud (White, PMS: White, CMYK: 0.0.0.0, RGB: 255, 255, 255, HEX: #ffffff).
- Greenery Palette includes colors inspired by the natural beauty surrounding Evergreen campus: PMS 580 C (CMYK: 20.0.36.0, RGB: 207, 229, 181, HEX: #cfe5b5), PMS 576 C (CMYK: 54.5.94.24, RGB: 103, 150, 58, HEX: #67963a), PMS 7743 C (CMYK: 71.8.100.50, RGB: 38, 103, 38, HEX: #266726), and PMS 350 C (CMYK: 80.21.79.64, RGB: 0, 73, 44, HEX: #00492c).
- General color usage guidelines include using PMS for offset printing and signage, CMYK for print collateral or business materials, and RGB for digital uses such as PowerPoint or TV. 1-color logos are used where costs are a consideration. Reversed logos are used on colored or photographic backgrounds.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary print font is Avenir. Usage includes all caps for short headlines, sentence case for longer headlines, sub-headlines, and text. Specific font weights include Light, Book, Medium, Heavy, and Black, with corresponding Oblique variants.
- Supporting print font is Caecilia LT, used for body copy, callouts, and highlighted text. It should never be used in headlines and flipped type should use 10-pt size or larger.
- Desktop fonts for staff are Arial (Regular and Bold) and Georgia (Regular and Bold Italic). Arial is used for subheads and headlines; Georgia is used for headlines, body text, and highlighted text in desktop applications.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should evoke feelings of community and creativity, focusing on interactions between students, faculty, and the broader world. The themes include lushness, genuine moments, natural lighting, and tactile nature.
- Specific photography guidelines recommend showcasing the beauty of campus, intimate student moments, interactions with nature, diverse collaboration, teaching moments, work-in-progress, unique events, and off-campus activities.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo clear space requires a predetermined breathing area equivalent to half the size of the mark. Minimum logo sizes: primary and full-name logos (1 inch) and secondary logo (1.375 inches). These ensure legibility of identity elements.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand voice reflects individuality, creativity, invention, and readiness to shape a world in flux. The slogan 'Your Way to the World' captures these themes.
- Messaging guidance suggests avoiding abbreviations like 'TESC,' prioritizing 'Evergreen.' Communications should use real-world examples to connect college activities with its community and avoid jargon.

**Notes:**

- For additional questions or guidance about the implementation of the brand, contact Evergreen State College's Marketing and Communications department.
- The Evergreen State College emphasizes using its full name in the first reference and 'Evergreen' in subsequent mentions to maintain brand identity.

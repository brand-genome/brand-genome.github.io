## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- simatec manufacturer logo with slogan for company communications.
- simalube, simatherm, and simatool product logos used for specific communications.
- Swiss Made logo appears as an addition to manufacturer or product logos.
- Logo colors include CMYK: Black 0, 0, 0, 100; Magenta 0, 100, 0, 0. Pantone: Black C, 219 C. Two-color logos: 100% magenta and black; Monochromatic logos: black positive or white negative.
- Logo font: Futura Extrabold; Logo claims font: Futura Condensed. Line beneath logo measures 50% of font size of slogan.
- Protected area for logos measures the X-height of the logo lettering.
- Placement of Swiss Made logo: above or below the logo maintaining protected area spacing. Width equals the 'sim' part of the logo.
- Manufacturer or product logo is positioned preferably at bottom-left or top-left in DIN A4 format. In business stationery templates, logos are placed at top-right or bottom-right.
- Logo size: The 'sima' part (magenta bar) measures 10% of total format width.
- Minimum logo width: 28mm. For smaller images, 'smart technologies' claim is omitted.
- Incorrect usage: Monochromatic logos used incorrectly on certain backgrounds, logos in unpleasant colors, or without adherence to protected zone or size guidelines.

**Color Information:**

- Base colors defined: Black (Pantone Black C/U, CMYK 0,0,0,100, RGB 18,18,18), Magenta (Pantone 219C/226U, CMYK 0,100,0,0, RGB 221,0,130).
- Spot color details: Light grey (CMYK 0,0,0,20, RGB 213,214,214) and Dark grey (CMYK 0,0,0,80, RGB 72,72,71).

**Typography Information:**

- Font family: Futura Std. Styles: Book, Bold, Medium. Usage includes headlines, titles, captions, running text, and enumerations.
- Font size and color details:                 - Headlines: Futura Std Bold, 16pt (Color: black or white).                 - Running text: Futura Std Book, sizes 11pt, 8.7pt, 7pt (Color: black).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logos must adhere to protected area rules (X-height of the logo lettering). Placement of 'Swiss Made' logos must follow defined positions.
- Logo positioning in DIN A4 format: Bottom-left preferred, bottom-right for business stationery templates. Type area and basic grid must be respected.

**Notes:**

- Corporate Design manual emphasizes correct implementation of house color, logo use, and design/layout guidelines.
- Names of manufacturer or products (simatec, simalube, simatherm, simatool) are always written in lowercase.
- Running text in general printed matter has ragged margin alignment. Hyphenation is not used for product or manufacturer names.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2010

**Logo Information:**

- Basic logo composition includes stylized text 'TÚ SR' and the name 'Telekomunikačný Úrad Slovenskej Republiky'.
- Clear space rule specifies that the protective zone is equal to the height of the 'S' in the stylized text surrounding the entire logo.
- Minimum size requirements include: Full logo at 12 x 19.5 mm and icon-only logo at 5 x 13 mm.
- Color versions include the full colored logo, black and white variants, and monochromatic style for single-color printing.
- Do's and Don'ts: It is prohibited to modify the logo's construction, colors, deform them, or compromise the protective zone.

**Color Information:**

- Corporate colors include: Red (Pantone 186 - CMYK: 0/100/81/4, RGB: 229/25/55), Blue (Pantone 300 - CMYK: 100/44/0/0, RGB: 0/121/194), and Black (Process Black - CMYK: 0/0/0/100, RGB: 0/0/0).
- Primary color use recommends prioritizing full color reproduction wherever possible. Monochromatic reproduction is for single-color scenarios.

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate font is Helvetica Neue LT Pro with variants: Light Condensed (47), Condensed (57), Medium Condensed (67), Bold Condensed (77).
- Typography standards specify Arial Narrow for all text materials. Recommended sizes: Headlines - 14pt, Secondary headlines - 12pt, Body text - 12pt, Small text - 10pt. Coloring accents include corporate colors.

**Spacing Layout:**

- All printed materials follow standard dimensions (A4 - 210 x 297 mm) with strict borders to ensure visual uniformity. White paper mandatory for printing.

**Notes:**

- Business cards are 90 x 50 mm, printed on white matte paper with a weight of 250 g/m². Consistency in corporate identity elements is emphasized for all printed materials.
- Email signature templates are provided in HTML and plain text formats. HTML signatures use Arial font with specific formatting and corporate color application.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Logos include the badge (restricted use), patch (used for visibility when square logo lettering isn’t visible), banner (primary logo), square logo (for spatially constrained cases), and Mission Statement logo (internal use only).
- Use black-and-white versions of the patch for mass distribution. Ensure color makes the emblem stand out and is visible in various applications, e.g., internal or external documents.
- Guidelines prohibit distorting, shadowing, recoloring, rotating, or partially removing elements of logos. Avoid grayscale printing of color logos and mixing logos with external symbols.

**Color Information:**

- Core colors are specified for all graphics and distributed content related to the Oakland University Police Department.

**Typography Information:**

- Font family: Breuer Text for taglines, headers, and simple signage.
- Arial or Helvetica for body text and headings in documents or larger signage. Sans serif fonts must be used for all aspects of the document.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Police badge is the only graphic allowed on police vehicles. All graphics must use non-metallic vinyl. Service Aide vehicles use altered patches displaying 'Service Aide' instead of 'Police.'

**Spacing Layout:**

- Patch scaling: height-to-width ratios must be maintained at 1:0.8745. Example: 12 inches tall patch should be 10.49 inches wide.
- Police department's letterhead must maintain professional standards and should be ordered through the Service Desk.
- Printing stationery, including business cards, letterheads, and envelopes, must be done professionally through Oakland University's services.

**Brand Voice:**

- Mission statement: Providing exemplary service with DEDICATION, VIGILANCE, & RESPECT. Key words must always be capitalized and appear on footers of documents and stationeries.

**Notes:**

- The OU Police Service Desk must approve all designs and usage of the department brand before application. Contact provided: (248) 370-3407, servicedesk@oupolice.com.

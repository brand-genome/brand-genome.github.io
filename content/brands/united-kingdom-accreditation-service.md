## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The logo for UKAS should be 30mm deep for documentation, and other materials like letterhead or envelopes maintain specific measurements where the logo is included. For email signatures, the logo should be 45mm deep and can be a PNG or JPG file.
- The email signature positions the logo to the left of text with a clear spacing of 'the width of the crown.'

**Color Information:**

- UKAS branding mentions the usage of colors 'UKAS Grey' and 'UKAS Dark Blue' for design elements like text, headers, and logos. Letterhead text is in UKAS Grey, while compliments slips and addresses on envelopes are printed in UKAS Dark Blue.

**Typography Information:**

- UKAS branding mandates Arial and Arial Bold as the primary typefaces. For letterhead headers, 6pt on 7pt Arial is specified. For email signatures, text should use 11pt Arial, with designated elements (e.g., name, 'United Kingdom Accreditation Service,' and initials T, F, E, and W) set in Arial Bold.
- PowerPoint presentations use Arial in various sizes: 42/48pt for titles, 24pt for presenter names and headings, and 21/24pt for body text and subheads.

**Imagery Photography:**

- PowerPoint slide templates emphasize minimal text usage while incorporating color and imagery to engage audiences.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Letterhead specifications include a 3mm fold mark positioned 99mm from the top of the page in UKAS Grey. Address details are centered in 6pt Arial on 7pt spacing and also printed in UKAS Grey. The logo is 30mm deep and centered.
- For agenda and minutes documents, margins remain constant, with standardized grid and layout rules: titles in 21pt Arial/Arial Bold in the Title Band; body text in 10pt Arial with 6pt space between items and further indents for sub-bullets.
- Publications maintain consistent margins with headers and text in specific Arial sizes (e.g., Reference No: 21pt, Edition: 12pt, Title: 18pt). Page headers and pagination are specified in 8pt and 9pt Arial, respectively.
- Email signature text must align to the right of the UKAS logo with specified spacing for headlines (equal to the width of the crown).

**Notes:**

- Miscellaneous UKAS promotional materials must be organized through the Corporate Marketing and Communication Department.
- Standard layouts for UKAS business cards are mentioned, with one example provided showing elements like logo placement and contact details.
- Details about how to use the brand identity, particularly in official documents, PowerPoint presentations, envelopes, and publication styles, are outlined.
- Email design specifics include removing social media icons set at a consistent size below text signatures.

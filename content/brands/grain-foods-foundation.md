## Brand Guidelines

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- Primary logo is the horizontal version, preferred for communication. Includes symbol and logotype.
- Grayscale, 1-color black versions of the logo are available for use in circumstances where full color isn't practical.
- Special usage logo designed for limited horizontal areas like banners or social accounts. Should only be used when necessary.
- Clear space rules: For all logos, maintain 50% of symbol height as clear space; in special circumstances, 25% clear space can be used.
- Special usage logo clear space rule: Minimum of 25% of symbol height.
- Minimum size requirements: Print minimum size for primary logo is 1.4 inches wide, special logo is 0.7 inches wide, and symbol is 0.4 inches wide. Digital minimum sizes are 100px for primary logo and 50px for special usage logo.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: Grain Foods Foundation Red (Pantone 7597 C, HEX #d64123, RGB R:214 G:65 B:35, CMYK: C:10 M:89 Y:100 K:2).
- Primary Gold color: Pantone 7408 C (HEX #f8bf00, RGB R:248 G:191 B:0, CMYK C:3 M:26 Y:100 K:0).
- Secondary color: Pantone 7568 C (HEX #795136, RGB R:121 G:81 B:54, CMYK C:40 M:63 Y:80 K:33).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font Bebas Neue used for headlines and titles.
- Din Pro Medium used for standard body copy.
- Eagle Book (modified version) used exclusively for logo logotype.
- Alternative acceptable fonts: Helvetica Neue Regular/Bold, recommended for PowerPoint presentations.
- Corporate tagline 'Come to the table...' uses DJBLemonHead typeface.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grid Layout: Clear space of 50% symbol height should surround the logo for optimal visibility; can use 25% minimum in special cases.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tagline 'Come to the table...' reflects Grain Foods Foundation's brand identity and must always be used consistently alongside the logo.

**Notes:**

- Grain Foods Foundation targets specific audiences such as medical professionals, influencers, investors, and consumers. Audience-specific strategies focus on delivering relatable and simplified information.
- Healthcare professionals prioritize science-based information delivered in an easy-to-understand manner.
- General influencers (e.g., bloggers, fitness experts) often seek simplified scientific knowledge to share with their audiences.
- Investors focus on consumer trends and resources provided by Grain Foods Foundation to streamline promotional processes.
- Consumers span conscious foodies seeking balance between taste and health, and health-focused moms prioritizing nutritional choices.

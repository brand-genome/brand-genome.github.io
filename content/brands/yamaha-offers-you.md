## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- YOU Yamaha Offers You uses fixed artwork logos for various usages. Includes YOU BLACK, YOU RED, and YOU GREY color versions. Negative logo usage advised for dark backgrounds (e.g., YOU BLACK).
- B&W logo usage strictly necessary on light and dark backgrounds. Greyscale logos are explicitly for B&W printers (K values: 100% and 40%).
- Exclusion zone rules specified as 'Proportion & exclusion zones' with measurements such as X, X/2, and 6X.
- Strict do's and don'ts for logo usage, such as not tilting, adding effects, resizing individual components, rotating, changing colors, adding transparency, changing typography, or distorting.
- Minimum logo size requirements: 25mm in print and 70px on digital screens. Logos must remain legible at reduced size.

**Color Information:**

- Negative logos (YOU BLACK and YOU RED) specifically advised for appropriate colored backgrounds.
- Greyscale logos specifically tailored for black-and-white modes (100% K values, 40% K values).

**Typography Information:**

- Text references usage of Ubuntu Typeface. Visual blocks suggest bold and light font weights.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Exclusion zones defined based on measures like X, X/2, and 6X for logo placement to ensure visibility and proper layout.

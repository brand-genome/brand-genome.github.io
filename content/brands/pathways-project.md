## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- PATHWAYS logo illustrates livestock sectors targeted by the project in a simplistic icon, incorporating a path running across three livestock sectors. Variations include Main logo and Vertical logo.
- Logo with tagline emphasizes the project's mission and clarifies purpose.
- Color variations of the logo: Orange Red (default version for white or light backgrounds), White (version for black or dark backgrounds), Soft Cyan (not to be used on highly saturated backgrounds unless it's brand's orange). Logo usage must ensure sufficient contrast.
- Safe area: Minimum spacing around the logo should be at least twice the height of the capital 'P'. Minimum size requirements: Printing — logo length should never be smaller than 25 mm; Digital media — logo length should never be smaller than 80 px.

**Color Information:**

- Extended color palette provided for communication needs: - Main Colors: Dark Blue: CMYK: 93/63/42/26, RGB: 24/77/100, HEX: #184D64. - Anthracite: CMYK: 69/63/62/58, RGB: 51/51/51, HEX: #333333. - Brown: CMYK: 26/85/100/22, RGB: 155/61/32, HEX: #9B3D20. - Pine: CMYK: 78/24/51/4, RGB: 51/144/134, HEX: #339086. - Orange: CMYK: 3/42/86/0, RGB: 242/160/62, HEX: #F2A03E. - Yellow: CMYK: 4/18/74/0, RGB: 245/204/93, HEX: #F5CC5D. - Green: CMYK: 61/4/58/0, RGB: 102/185/140, HEX: #66B98C. - Orange Red: CMYK: 0/78/74/0, RGB: 255/95/69, HEX: #FF5F45. - Soft Cyan: CMYK: 32/0/16/0, RGB: 165/230/223, HEX: #A5E6DF.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Agenda. Title uses Agenda Bold; Subtitle uses Agenda Semibold; Body text uses Agenda Regular or Agenda Light (minimum font size: 9pt).
- Fallback fonts: System font Corbel for titles and Calibri for body text (minimum font size: 11pt).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Defined safe area ensures minimum space around logo equal to twice the height of the capital 'P'. Minimum size prescription for print (25 mm) and digital media (80 px).

**Brand Voice:**

- Tagline: States the project mission and clarifies purpose.

**Notes:**

- PATHWAYS visual identity guidelines should be respected in all external and internal communication materials, such as presentations, posters, business cards, flyers, social media, etc. These guidelines emphasize consistency, reliability, and high quality in visual communication.
- As a Horizon 2020 project, all PATHWAYS communication material must include the EU flag and the H2020 funding disclaimer.
- For questions or adjustments to guidelines, contact Danielle Kutka, Project Manager at REVOLVE, via email: danielle@revolve.media.

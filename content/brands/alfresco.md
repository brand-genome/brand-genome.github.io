## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Icons are an essential branding element with solid and outlined versions. They are adaptable to gray (#717174) for light backgrounds and white (#ffffff) for color backgrounds. Examples provided clarify usage.

**Color Information:**

- Alfresco's color palette is divided into three main categories: Green (Primary), Blue (Secondary), and Orange (Tertiary). Green (#8bc34a; R:139 G:195 B:74; C:51 M:2 Y:94 K:0) should be the first choice, Blue (#2196f3; R:33 G:150 B:243; C:71 M:34 Y:0 K:0) follows, and Orange (#ff9901; R:255 G:153 B:1; C:0 M:47 Y:100 K:0) is used for actions or emphasis. Secondary colors within each primary color group are detailed along with their HEX, RGB, and CMYK codes.
- Detailed color palette specifications: Dark Green (#43a047), Dark Blue (#01579b), Dark Orange (#e65100), Jade Green (#22be73), Aqua Blue (#00bcd4), Gold Orange (#f2b20f), Pear Green (#cddc39), Baby Blue (#81d4fa), Sunlight Orange (#ffd600). Usage and alignment with Google Material Design standards are specified.

**Typography Information:**

- Headline text uses Museo Slab, Alfresco's personality typeface, for headlines, titles, or distinguishing text. Museo comes in options Museo 700 and Museo 300.
- Body text uses Roboto Regular across subtitles, body copy, etc. Roboto Regular (Weight: 16 pt, 13 pt, 14 pt, 18 pt, 22 pt, 24 pt, 30 pt, 32 pt, 36 pt) is highlighted.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography falls under Access to Content, Collaboration, and Interaction Sets. Access to Content focuses on candid images of business professionals using devices. Collaboration depicts professionals working together, often using devices. Interaction Sets maintain consistency by using images of the same group of individuals in various activities.
- Imagery treatments include Shape Mask, High Pass Filter, and blending modes for consistent styling. Created using Adobe Products.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing and layout instructions for headline-text divider includes a 1px divider separating sections.
- Card style specifications include a 1px stroke and an outer shadow. Cards float above a gray background (#eef0f0). Inspired by Google Material Design.

**Brand Voice:**

- Core messaging aligned with 'make business flow,' leveraging Flow Wave graphics. Bold Style makes strong statements, whereas Subtle Style hints at the idea.
- Vision and Mission: Vision—'We make business flow faster, more seamlessly, and intelligently.' Mission—'Create a better, effortless way to unite people with information and processes.'

**Notes:**

- Additional styles include Web Animations and Web Icons, ensuring adaptability for Alfresco's digital environments.
- The Brand Asset Library serves as an internal resource for logos, icons, and supporting materials.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- The FIA Master Logo consists of a blue globe and motor cog symbol, representing motoring and Motor Sport associations. It is designed to show authority and unity, with uppercase letters 'F' and 'A', and a lowercase 'i'. A tagline 'A World in Motion' accompanies the logo, representing the mission of FIA.
- Positive and negative formats of the FIA Master Logo are shown. The blue positive logo should be used as the primary logo.
- The Logotype includes the text 'FEDERATION INTERNATIONALE DE L’AUTOMOBILE' in either stacked or horizontal arrangement. The preferred format is horizontal for best practice.

**Color Information:**

- The primary color for the FIA brand is blue. Additional information on the color palette to be provided later.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography is grouped into several categories, including Corporate Imagery, Motor Sport Imagery, Motoring Imagery, FIA Campaigns Imagery, and FIA Events Imagery. Specific guidelines may follow.

**Brand Voice:**

- FIA attributes include derivatives such as EXCELLENCE IN MOTION, IDEAS IN MOTION, ACTION IN MOTION, PASSION IN MOTION, SAFETY IN MOTION, HISTORY IN MOTION. These derivatives strengthen the FIA brand across collateral and campaigns.
- FIA's mission is to act as a professional and caring Global Federation driving innovation, passion, sustainability, safety, and governance in motoring and Motor Sport, and to be the Global Voice for a World in Motion.

**Notes:**

- The guidelines aim to ensure brand consistency across online, print, and event communications. Applications such as translations cannot be presented due to extensive use. Contact FIA for clarification.
- The contents page outlines various brand assets covered in the guidelines, such as FIA division logos, FIA flags, sub-brand logos, typography usage, and campaign guidelines.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The ATL logo has both vertical and horizontal orientations with and without the full acronym 'Atlanta-Region Transit Link Authority'. Specific designs include Vertical, Horizontal 1-A, Horizontal 1-B, Horizontal 2-A, and Horizontal 2-B.
- The ATL logo clear space rule is defined as requiring the width of the 'A' from the logo on all sides to ensure maximum legibility and prominence.
- Minimum size requirement: The ATL logo should not be resized below 1 inch in height for vertical versions, or below 1.5 inches for horizontal versions.
- Proper logo usage allows two-color and one-color versions for different background contrasts. Two-color versions use ATL Phoenix (red) and ATL Twilight (blue). One-color variants use ATL Twilight (blue), ATL Silver (light gray), ATL Iron (dark gray), or white.
- Improper logo usage includes placing the logo on low-contrast backgrounds, using drop shadows or borders, distorting, rotating, resizing, changing colors arbitrarily, or altering typography. Pantone or CMYK formats should not be used digitally.
- The symbol of the logo is designed as an 'A' icon with shapes resembling arrows that create movement and a compass-like clarity, pointing to inclusivity and progress in the Atlanta region.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors are ATL Phoenix (red) and ATL Twilight (blue). These are used for most of the logo applications.
- A full color palette includes secondary colors such as ATL Creek, ATL Violet, ATL Wisteria, ATL Mint, ATL Ginkgo, ATL Lime, ATL Forest, ATL Oak, ATL Peach, ATL Monarch, ATL Rose, and ATL Fox.
- Color formats for digital use are limited to RGB and HEX codes. Pantone (PMS) and CMYK are designated for print-only use.

**Brand Voice:**

- The ATL brand personality includes traits such as SMART, OPTIMISTIC, HARMONIOUS, and INNOVATIVE to represent the Atlanta-Region Transit network.

**Notes:**

- The brand guidelines emphasize maintaining consistency and following these rules without exception for visual materials related to ATL branding. Unauthorized reproduction is prohibited.
- Pantone references in the document are not 100% accurate due to lacking actual Pantone ink printing.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The TRICARE logo consists of three elements: the symbol (waves and stars), the wordmark ('TRICARE' in Futura Bold), and the required registration mark (®). The registration mark is positioned in the top-right corner of the 'E' in 'TRICARE' and is mandatory for all uses in print and electronic formats.
- Minimum size requirements for the TRICARE logo: 0.8" x 0.4" dimensions to ensure legibility. The preferred size is 2" x 1". Proportions of height and width must always be maintained.
- The clear space around the TRICARE logo must be at least one-half the height of the logo. No distracting elements, such as text or photography, should encroach upon this space.
- Logo color standards specify Pantone 280 for the stars, Pantone 186 for the waves and wordmark, and an optional 100% Black color version. RGB, HEX, and CMYK equivalents are also provided.
- When placing the TRICARE logo on a solid color background, the 100% black version of the logo must be used, or the background opacity should be set at 15% or lower.
- Incorrect uses of the TRICARE logo include reversing the logo, tinting colors, modifying spacing between the symbol and wordmark, stretching, cropping, applying effects, or using the logo as a motif or graphic design element.
- For co-branding, the TRICARE logo must not be smaller than the partner logo. They must be separated by a vertical rule of 1pt, with a distance of 0.25" from the rule.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typefaces for the TRICARE brand are ITC Franklin Gothic (used for headers and subheaders) and Minion Pro (used for body text).
- Alternate fonts: Arial can replace ITC Franklin Gothic, and Garamond can replace Minion Pro for Microsoft Word and PowerPoint. Lucida Sans Unicode can replace both for web use.
- ITC Franklin Gothic font family: includes Book, Heavy, Demi Condensed, and Medium Italic styles. It is a sans-serif that is highly legible and suitable for both display and small sizes.
- Minion Pro font family: includes Regular, Italic, Medium, Semibold, Bold, and Bold Condensed styles. It is a serif font proposed for body text with a partially condensed style.
- Do not distort typefaces, apply special effects (e.g., drop shadow), or substitute unapproved fonts unless using specific approved alternatives.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Maintain a minimum of half the height of the TRICARE logo clear space on all sides, free from text, photography, or other logos.
- For the TRICARE wordmark, clear space around it should equal the full height of the wordmark.

**Notes:**

- The TRICARE marks must include the registered trademark symbol (®) on logos and wordmarks in both print and electronic formats but not within general body text.

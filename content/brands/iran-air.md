## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- IranAir logo has multiple versions: positive version, negative version, black and white, vertical alignment, and a silver option for merchandising. The logo must follow corporate colors.
- Clear space requirements: The reserve area around the logo must use 'x' (height of the 'n' in IranAir). Minimum size requirements are 15 mm for offline and 60 px for online usage.
- Examples of incorrect logo usage include placing on busy backgrounds, distorting the logo, using unapproved colors, or adding frames.
- The IranAir logo symbol (icon-only version) is used in small spaces or if logo size falls below minimum requirements. Symbol usage is focused and limited to specific applications.

**Color Information:**

- IranAir main colors: Dark Blue (Pantone DS187-1 C, CMYK 100/100/0/40, RGB 27/20/100, HEX #1b1464) and White (Pantone DS325-9 C, CMYK 0/0/0/0, RGB 255/255/255).
- IranAir secondary colors: Blue, Grey, with supporting colors including Royal Blue, Light Blue, Turquoise Green, Orange, and more listed with exact color codes (Pantone, CMYK, RGB, HEX).
- Color application sequence: IranAir Dark Blue and White are used first, followed by Blue and Grey. Additional supporting colors are reserved for charts, infographics, and digital use.

**Imagery Photography:**

- IranAir photographic style excludes posed, artificial-looking photographs, wide/narrow-angle shots that distort images, and photographs with outdated objects. Images must reflect modernity and avoid mosaics or collages.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo position and size on layouts: In vertical/square formats, logo width equals 1/5th of the page height ('Y'). In horizontal formats, it equals 1/6th. Margins use a measurement of 3x 'n' for top/right and 4x 'n' for bottom.
- Layouts use a grid system: Vertical layouts have 8 to 13 columns, while horizontal layouts use between 13 and 16. Signature system occupies 3 columns on the right.

**Notes:**

- The co-branding system aligns IranAir with partner logos equally, separated by a vertical line, with defined clear space and consistent styling across all instances.
- Cover and interior backgrounds: IranAir Dark Blue, Blue, and Grey are the main options. Photographic/illustrative and white backgrounds are additional choices for specific applications.
- Layouts for extensive corporate deliverables like PowerPoint presentations use white backgrounds for clarity and professionalism.

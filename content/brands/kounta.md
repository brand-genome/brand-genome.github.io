## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The primary logo is meant for brand contexts such as websites and apps. It is used where the audience is already aware of the brand name or instances accompanied by the full Kounta name (e.g., social media).
- The wordmark logo is the secondary variant to be used outside brand contexts where the full Kounta name must be articulated clearly. Additionally, it may supplement the primary logo, but both logos must not appear together.
- Logo colors: Grey logo as the primary color on light backgrounds and white logo on dark backgrounds or over photography.
- Clear space around logos derived from the line width of letters ensures visibility and impact.
- Minimum size requirements for logos: Maintain original proportions without stretching or distorting.
- Incorrect logo usage: Avoid using colored logos over images, orange logos on dark backgrounds, and charcoal on dark backgrounds. Avoid logo rotation, splitting, altering, using circles/shapes, and colors outside brand specifications.

**Color Information:**

- Kounta Orange (Primary): RGB: 255, 128, 10; CMYK: 0, 60, 100, 0.
- Kounta Charcoal (Primary): RGB: 74, 74, 74; CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 86.
- Secondary branded colors include Beige Half, Kraft Half, Copper Half, Konkrete, Beige, Kraft, Copper with varying RGB/CMYK codes.
- App colors are uniquely designated for app usage with exclusive RGB combinations.

**Typography Information:**

- Headings use Berlingske Serif with circular forms echoing circular logo elements. Available in Light and Medium weights.
- Body copy uses Akkurat. A neutral, legible font available in Light, Regular, Mono, and Italic variants.
- Usage guidelines: Berlingske Serif Light for headings against light backgrounds. Medium-weight Serif for dark backgrounds or images. Akkurat typography is reserved for body copy, CTA buttons, links, etc.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography categories include 'Front of House' reflecting successful stores and customer interaction and 'Back of House' showcasing real moments reflecting priorities.
- 'Functional Product Shots': Overhead photos using natural surfaces to feature product usage.
- Photographic style must align natural/photojournalistic tones, avoiding stock-like visuals and complementing earthy brand colors.
- Photography categories should integrate together vibrantly for ads and web interfaces.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo spacing utilizes character width for consistent proportional balance.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand tone descriptors include Optimistic, Vocal, Transformative, Responsible, Bold Thinker, and Holistic.
- Circular design theme embeds cohesive user-brand interaction styling.

**Notes:**

- Kounta branding adapts typographic, graphical, and collaborative palettes directly influenced by the hospitality sector it services.
- Specific guidelines outline adaptability within the app allowing partner color substitution under predefined conditions.

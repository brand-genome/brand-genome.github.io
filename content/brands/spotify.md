## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The logo consists of a Wordmark and an Icon, forming the brand's focal point. The exclusion zone ensures legibility and impact by maintaining a minimum safe distance. The spacing between the Icon and Wordmark equals the counter space of the ‘o’ in the Wordmark.
- Minimum size requirements: 20mm/0.8in for print, 70px for digital applications.
- Incorrect logo usages include: distorted or warped logos, using gradients or custom colors, outlining or removing parts of the logo, modifying typefaces, or separating the Wordmark from the Icon.
- Logo color: Spotify Green should only appear on white, black, or non-duotoned photography. It should never directly compete with other colors from the brand palette.
- Logo placement: The logo can only be placed in five specific locations—top-left corner, top-right corner, center, bottom-left corner, or bottom-right corner. When aligned with type on the left side, both should be left-aligned.

**Color Information:**

- Spotify Green: RGB (30, 215, 96), HEX #1ED760, CMYK (80, 0, 80, 0), Pantone 7479 C (Coated), Pantone 7479 U (Uncoated).
- Spotify Green fallback colors include variations with slightly different RGB and HEX values to account for device or print discrepancies.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo exclusion zone is equal to half the height of the Icon.

**Brand Voice:**

- Spotify messaging should focus on music accessibility and positivity, emphasizing Premium offers, features like unlimited songs on devices and proper terminology for promotions.

**Notes:**

- The document includes contact details for assistance regarding brand usage, like email: brandapproval@spotify.com.

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- Primary Black Logotype is the main logo for Spotify and should be used as the visual identifier for the brand.
- Primary White Logotype version is available to be used in contexts where it is suitable, such as on a dark background.
- Minimum size for Primary Logotype: 0.4 inches (10 mm) wide for print applications and 45 pixels wide for digital applications. Registration mark must be included.
- Clear space rules for Primary Logotype: Minimum clear space around the logotype is equivalent to 1/3 of the width of the logotype.
- Horizontal Logotype is an alternative to the Primary Logotype if it is impossible to use. Horizontal Black Logotype and Horizontal White Logotype are showcased.
- Clear space rules for Horizontal Logotype: Minimum clear space around the logotype is equivalent to 1/2 of the height of the logotype.
- Minimum size for Horizontal Logotype: 0.6 inches (15 mm) wide for print applications and 80 pixels wide for digital applications. Registration mark must be included.
- Monochromatic Logotype versions: Black and White variants of the logo are provided to be used in situations where color reproduction is not possible.
- Minimum clear space for Monochromatic Primary Logotype: equivalent to 1/3 of the width of the logotype. For the Monochromatic Horizontal Logotype, the clear space is equivalent to 1/2 of the height.
- Minimum size for Monochromatic Logotype (Primary): 0.4 inches (10 mm) wide for print and 45 pixels wide for digital. For Monochromatic Horizontal Logotype: 0.6 inches (15 mm) wide for print and 80 pixels wide for digital.
- Modified versions of the Spotify logo should not be used. Always use official approved digital files for the logo artwork.
- Partner logo sizing rules: For text-based logos: same height as the logotype. For symbol-based logos: 2x the height of the logotype.
- Alignment rules: Partner logos should always be center-aligned with Spotify's Preferred or Alternate logotypes. Follow the partner's guidelines for logo usage, sizes, etc.
- Do not separate the icon from the wordmark in the logo.
- Avoid stylistic treatments such as outlines, glows, or other effects on the logo.
- Do not use the Spotify logo against a green background.
- Do not distort the logo by skewing, rotating, or stretching.
- Respect spacing guidelines to maintain breathing room for the logo.
- Only use specified colors to represent the logo.

**Color Information:**

- Spotify Green is the primary brand color. Used to signify Spotify's presence. Solid PMS code: Pantone 376C; CMYK: C54 M0 Y100 K0; RGB: R132 G189 B0.
- Spotify Black: Secondary brand color with PMS code Pantone Process Black C; CMYK: C0 M0 Y0 K100; RGB: R0 G0 B0.
- Spotify Grey: Secondary neutral brand color with PMS code Pantone Cool Gray 8C; CMYK: C0 M0 Y0 K60; RGB: R130 G130 B130.
- Spotify White: Supporting neutral color for contrast. CMYK: C0 M0 Y0 K0; RGB: R255 G255 B255.
- Spotify Sand: Supporting neutral color with PMS code Pantone Cool Gray 1C; CMYK: C6 M5 Y6 K0; RGB: R236 G235 B232.
- Spotify Green should be used prominently for brand identities like type and backgrounds.
- Solid colors work best for printed applications. Gradients are more suited for digital applications like websites and emails.
- Ensure high contrast between Spotify Green and other supporting neutral colors.
- Spotify Green (hero color) PMS value should always be PMS 376 for brand consistency.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space for Spotify Primary Logotype: Minimum clear space is 1/3 of logo width. For Horizontal Logotype: Minimum clear space is 1/2 of logo height.
- Respect spacing rules for partner logos: Ensure center alignment with Spotify's logotypes and adequate clear space.
- Avoid using the Spotify logo more than once in the same space for layout elegance and minimalism.

**Brand Voice:**

- Spotify is about having the right music for every moment, allowing users to access a vast range of music instantly.
- Partner messaging reinforces Spotify's role in creating music moments - focus on availability and personalization.
- Use clear messaging for Premium benefits, emphasizing usability (offline mode, better sound quality, no ads).
- General partnership messaging includes phrases like: 'Listen everywhere with unlimited music.'

**Notes:**

- Partner logos should follow Spotify's size and alignment rules while also conforming to the partner's brand guidelines.
- For logo usage, always refer to Spotify's official approved artwork files to ensure consistency and trademark compliance.
- Questions about copy approval should be directed to copy@spotify.com or the Head of Copy.
- Spotify branding contact information: Christian Wilsson (Stockholm) - christian@spotify.com, Sophia Bendz (New York) - sophia@spotify.com.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- Safe zone indicated around logos, includes examples for proper placement in printed materials and exceptions for URLs and social media. Safe area rules are visually depicted.
- Family of logos includes Columbia Pacific CCO and Jackson Care Connect. Tagline "Part of the CareOregon Family" used as a closing remark on printed materials, but not letterhead or stationery items. Spanish-language tagline to be used in relevant material.
- Guidelines on placement for logos in horizontal and vertical designs. Multiple logo placement configurations provided for cobranding with Health Share of Oregon and other business lines, including far right and left lower corners.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: Pantone PMS 151, PMS 541. Secondary palette and tints can extend creative options for expression. Use Pantone® Color Matching System for printed color matches.
- Columbia Pacific CCO colors: Primary Pantones PMS 7530, PMS 3285, PMS 7700. Guidelines for usage similar to primary color palette.
- Jackson Care Connect palette includes Pantones PMS 464, PMS 626, PMS 7540; primary and secondary guidelines apply here as well.
- Housecall Providers palette uses Pantones PMS 1225, PMS 542, PMS 2101, PMS 541; same primary and secondary application rules.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary fonts are Proxima Nova and Jubilat (serif). Specific weights including Light, Bold, and Black defined for both families. Substitute font: Calibri; Jubilat requires +10 tracking.
- Condensed versions of fonts used for dense text areas like graphics and tables. Includes the same weights as primary fonts e.g., Proxima Nova Condensed Light, Bold, Extrabold, etc.
- Headlines and subheads can use Jubilat or Proxima Nova, depending on content and design specifics. Required +10 spacing for Jubilat Bold and Light.
- Body copy specified with Proxima Nova Light, minimum point sizes defined: 12-point and at least 16-point leading. Large print minimum is 15-point, smaller text minimum is 14-point. Italics usage kept minimal.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style guidelines emphasize sharp, clear, aspirational images reflecting members’ ethnicity, goals, vibrancy and avoiding sadness or distracting elements. No backs turned, no trash cans visible.

**Brand Voice:**

- Social media voice informed by CareOregon Core Values - Genuine, Brave, Optimistic, Thoughtful, Steadfast, Caring.
- Narrative reflects Steadfast nature in shifting healthcare space; showcases Brave work in health care for all, Optimistic sharing around outcomes and resources. Social Media Customer Service and PR managed through teams.
- Websites and apps require fluid UX across devices, ADA 508 compliance for accessibility, and alignment with general brand guidelines.

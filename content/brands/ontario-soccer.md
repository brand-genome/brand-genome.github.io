## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The primary logo should be used in 4-color process for most print and digital communications materials. This version should always be placed on a white or light-colored background for optimum contrast.
- Horizontal logo version is permitted when there is insufficient space to use the primary logo.
- Tag logo is only permitted as a prominent option when the background is too busy for the primary or knock-out logo.
- A stroke logo version may be used but requires express approval from Ontario Soccer.
- Alternate logo versions including 2-color, supplementary, 1-color (red or black), and knock-out white are described for use in select situations while observing application-specific rules.
- Clear space rules mandate surrounding space equal to or greater than one scallop width of the logo. Minimum sizes specified as 1.25 inches high, reduced to 0.75 inches if 'Established 1901' is removed.
- Logo usage guidelines emphasize avoiding inappropriate backgrounds and respecting adequate visual spacing around the logo. Improper examples demonstrated.
- Special partner logo guidelines stipulate visual balance and alignment rules with minimum divider stroke weight of 0.5pt.

**Color Information:**

- Red, PMS 185 (Primary Color) — CMYK (C:0, M:100, Y:100, K:0); RGB: (R:234, G:0, B:42); HEX: #ea002a
- Black (Primary Color) — CMYK (C:0, M:0, Y:0, K:100); RGB: (R:35, G:31, B:32); HEX: #231f20
- 50% Black (Accent) — CMYK (C:0, M:0, Y:0, K:50); RGB: (R:147, G:149, B:152); HEX: #939598
- 20% Black (Accent) — CMYK (C:0, M:0, Y:0, K:20); RGB: (R:209, G:211, B:212); HEX: #d1d3d4

**Typography Information:**

- Primary fonts include Stratum 2 Bold for headlines (in Ontario Soccer Red), Roboto Bold for subheadings, and Roboto Light or Regular for body text. Body text below 8pt should not use Roboto Light for legibility purposes.
- Roboto is advised for general use and is web-compatible. Substitution font is Arial when Stratum 2 Bold or Roboto are unavailable.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should evoke a positive response, showcasing emotion, pride, and sportsmanship. Images should represent Ontario Soccer's diverse membership across all ages, ethnicities, genders, and abilities.
- Avoid images showing the old OSA logo or branding other than Adidas.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing guidelines ensure adequate clear zones around the logo (equal to or greater than one scallop width). Minimum height specified at 1.25 inches, reduced to 0.75 inches without the 'Est. 1901' tagline.

**Brand Voice:**

- Core values include Modern & Progressive, Innovative, Relevant, Playful, Inspiring, and United.
- Writing style should be professional, informative, and objective. Terminology includes referencing the organization as 'Ontario Soccer' without acronyms and adopting uniform terms for age groups (e.g., U#).
- Tagline rules: 'Play. Inspire. Unite.' The tagline must always visually complement the logo and can use Roboto Light/Regular in specified colors.

**Notes:**

- The Feature Graphic involves both red and gray knock-out versions to overlay on images, with specific masking and layering techniques discussed.
- Social media presence emphasizes a consistent look. Sizes for social media graphics include 400x400px for profile icons, and other resolutions for covers/post images.
- Partner logos must be visually balanced with mandatory use of dividers and the primary Ontario Soccer logo.
- Ontario Soccer's branded apparel mandates the use of Adidas or unbranded designs in Ontario colors. Embroidery placement standards are provided.

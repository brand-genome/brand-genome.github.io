## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Logos are available in print and screen formats.

**Color Information:**

- Boomi brand colors include Green (Pantone 876 CFF, CMYK 0, 100, 0, 32, RGB 0, 178, 56) and Steel (Pantone 2165, CMYK 49, 28, 4, 101, RGB 119, 146, 65). Additionally, gradients are used in digital media and require specific angles (-60°) and percentage locations (66%).

**Typography Information:**

- Typography includes Arial for employees, Helvetica Neue for internal teams/agencies, and Roboto for web/digital platforms with respective usage examples. Guidelines discourage italics and shadows.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should emphasize authenticity, clean and fresh visuals, candid portraits, and avoid clichés, words in images, or staged subjects.

**Brand Voice:**

- Communications follow the Four Cs: clear, concise, concrete, and compelling. Writers should explain terms for widest audiences, avoid jargon and clichés, and follow American English standards.

**Notes:**

- PowerPoint templates, stock photos, and logos are provided in Boomi's Wiki and resources repository. Refer to wiki.boomi.com for more detailed guidelines.
- Email signatures must use Arial font without images/banners unless approved. Templates for the same are available on the Wiki.

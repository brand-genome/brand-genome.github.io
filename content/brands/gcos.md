## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Standard logo (acronym without tagline) is used along with the sponsor lockup in different alignments: left-aligned, center-aligned, right-aligned, and horizontal.
- Negative version of the logo includes only the colors of the three circling lines, while all other elements are white. The WMO acronym logo in negative should use WMO gold (Pantone 130, CMYK equivalent: C:0 M:40 Y:100 K:0).
- Full spelling logo without tagline should be used for audiences unfamiliar with GCOS (e.g., reports), whereas the tagline 'Keeping Watch Over Our Climate' is used for public-facing products (e.g., posters).
- Minimum size for the logotype is specified based on the readability of the cosponsors' names: minimum width is 32 mm for some layouts and 35.5 mm for others.
- Logo placement (e.g., upper left corner) is detailed along with proper scaling ratios for vertical and horizontal A4 format. This ratio applies to all formats and logo versions.
- The GCOS logo can also be paired with the SDG color wheel, which must maintain specified ratios and a dividing line of 0.5 pt in 100% black.
- On colored backgrounds, the logo should always be in negative if placed on one of the four main identity colors or against colors of similar brightness.

**Color Information:**

- Primary palette includes 4 colors: WMO blue (Pantone 293 C, CMYK: C:100 M:70 Y:0 K:0, RGB: R:0 G:91 B:170, HEX: #005baa), Ground orange (Pantone 144 C, CMYK: C:2 M:50 Y:100 K:0, RGB: R:242 G:146 B:32, HEX: #f39200), Water teal (Pantone 7712 C, CMYK: C:85 M:33 Y:33 K:3, RGB: R:0 G:134 B:154, HEX: #00869a), and Sky blue (Pantone 299 C, CMYK: C:74 M:23 Y:0 K:0, RGB: R:30 G:157 B:216, HEX: #009fe3).
- Tints of primary colors can be used for different publications (not for logos). Available tint percentages include: 85%, 70%, 55%, 40%, 25%, and 10%.

**Notes:**

- The document includes use guidelines for templates like letterhead, business cards, website logo placement, and email signature. Example: GCOS business cards have logos left-aligned with sponsors placed above text on the front side, and centered on the back side.
- Contact and assistance details for queries regarding guidelines are provided, including email (gcos@wmo.int) and other links for downloading design elements.

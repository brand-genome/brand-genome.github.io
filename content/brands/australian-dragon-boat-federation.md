## Brand Guidelines

### 2022

**Logo Information:**

- Logo variations include positive (preferred in all contexts), greyscale, and mono versions (white, only if other options cannot be reproduced).
- Clear space for the logo is defined as half the width of the background panel for padding, free of text, other logos, or visual clutter.
- Minimum size for print is 25mm wide; preferred size for A4 materials is 45mm wide.
- Incorrect logo usage rules: Alterations to colors, patterns, or proportions are prohibited; no rotation, stretching, or skewing.
- Graphic elements, text, and images must not overlap or interrupt the logo. Exclusion zones must be maintained around the logo at all times.
- AusChamps logo is neutral, captures essence of AusDBF brand without state bias, usable for all AusChamps events with date and location updates.
- Specific usage rules for laid-out AusChamps logo: 25mm minimum size for print; 45mm preferred width on A4 materials.
- Logo improper practices include color alteration, random backgrounds, reduced exclusion zones, and unaligned member logos.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include PMS Black, PMS 109C, and PMS Black 30%. In CMYK: 0,0,0,100; 0,10,100,0; 0,0,0,30. In RGB: 0,0,0; 255,220,0; 188,190,192. HEX values are #000000, #ffdc00, and #bcbec0.
- Primary colors serve core branding. Use secondary colors for typography or headlines for contrast and better legibility.
- Using gradients with primary colors (e.g., text backgrounds and overlays) is permitted if adding contrast.
- Primary colors include PMS 553C, PMS 158C. In CMYK: 59,0,53,80; 0,61,97,0, etc. Full list in document. HEX: #124432, #fe8025.
- AusChamps colors include PMS 7438C (#885200, CMYK 85,0,100,55, RGB 136,82,0).

**Typography Information:**

- Tahoma font recommended with medium weight for applications like e-newsletter; example template provided.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photographs with the logo must have nondescript backgrounds that provide suitable contrast.
- Photo backgrounds cannot interrupt or break the visual of the logos.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space ensures uncluttered communication and is defined as half the width of the surrounding background panel.
- For AusChamps, width of the white channel through the circle guides the clear space.
- Guidelines for business cards layout (logo placement, contact details formatting provided with examples).

**Brand Voice:**

- Core mission: 'To connect people with dragon boating across Australia.' Used under branding, signage and business card design or digital communications.
- Auroras logo highlights vibrant light bands, dynamic elements of dragon boating, and emphasizes Australian identity.

**Notes:**

- Guidelines for digital communications: AusChamps or AusDBF brand distinctions to be adhered to as per source of communication.
- Electronic versions of logos provided by AusDBF should always be used; logos must not be recreated.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The ideal position for the Irish Red Cross Brand Mark can be achieved by measuring the distance from the edge of the page to the Brand Mark. The minimum distance for print is measured by the height and width of the cross as shown here. This unit of measurement is used on all stationery and should be adhered to at all times. The axis between the cross and the logotype should be used as a graphic device to align type and graphic elements.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors for the Irish Red Cross include Red (Pantone 485) and Black. If printing in two colors, only these two should be used. Secondary colors include a palette of warm greys for flexibility and warmth. Exact CMYK, Pantone, RGB, and HEX values are provided for Red (Pantone 485), Warm Greys (10, 8, 6, 4), and Black. Use tints for text boxes and cut-out photograph backgrounds.
- Color values for screen designs differ from print. Utilize RGB or HEX values as outlined below for Red (Pantone 485 Red: RGB: 204, 0, 0, HEX: #EE2E24), Black (RGB: 0, 0, 0, HEX: #000000), and Warm Greys with their respective values.

**Typography Information:**

- The primary typeface for Irish Red Cross Brand is Helvetica Neue LT, available in a range of weights and styles. Arial is used for all internal communications and digital applications like the Irish Red Cross website. Various weights and roles specified include Helvetica Neue 45 Light (Body Copy), Helvetica Neue 77 Bold Condensed (Headlines), etc.
- The primary screen typeface for Irish Red Cross Brand is Helvetica Neue eText Pro. Arial may be used for internal communications if Helvetica eText is unavailable. Examples include Helvetica Neue eText Pro Light Italic, Roman, Medium, Bold, and others.

**Imagery Photography:**

- The Irish Red Cross Brand Mark should be professional artwork provided and standalone. It must always appear on a plain white background. The brand mark should not be redrawn or placed over an image.
- A square box may be used as a holding device for the Brand Mark and copy. This device can be used when placing the Brand Mark on an image or solid color and as a graphic element in layouts, presentations, posters, advertisements, etc.

**Spacing Layout:**

- A 32-field grid on an A4 page is recommended with four columns of eight fields each. Utilizing a baseline grid ensures consistent look and feel across publications and advertisements.
- Examples of layouts utilizing a 32-field grid on an A4 page are provided, with alignment and clear margin spacing guidelines.

**Brand Voice:**

- The Irish Red Cross tone and typographical style have been established to communicate the values of the organization effectively. The tone emphasizes professionalism and inclusivity.
- When communicating with youth, the Red Cross Youth visual identity adopts an illustrative style to remain distinctive and relevant to ever-changing youth culture.

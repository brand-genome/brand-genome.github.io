## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Master logo versions are shown, including white-out and grayscale versions. The exclusion area rules are provided to maintain the integrity and legibility of the logo.
- Horizontal logo version is displayed with white-out and grayscale variants. Exclusion area rules for the horizontal format are specified.
- Horizontal logo with tagline variation provided, including white-out and grayscale versions. Exclusion zone and size requirements are detailed.
- Horizontal logo with URL version is displayed. It includes white-out and grayscale options alongside minimum size and exclusion area rules.
- Minimum size requirements for logos are provided: Master logo - 15mm for print and 100px for screen; Horizontal logo - 25mm for print, 150px for screen; Horizontal logo with tagline - 80mm for print, 500px for screen; Horizontal logo with URL - 65mm for print, 350px for screen.
- Rules against incorrect logo usage include no redrawing, altering, recoloring, stretching, squeezing, or applying graphic effects like drop shadows or 3D effects.

**Color Information:**

- Core color palette includes KARUNA RED (Pantone 187c, CMYK: C:0 M:100 Y:95 K:35, RGB: R:167 G:13 B:23, HEX: #a70d17), DARK GREY (Pantone 425c, CMYK: C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:90, RGB: R:64 G:64 B:65, HEX: #404041), DONATION GREEN (Pantone 3275c, CMYK: C:85 M:0 Y:55 K:0, RGB: R:0 G:176 B:149, HEX: #00b095), ACTION BLUE (Pantone 7466c, CMYK: C:90 M:0 Y:30 K:0, RGB: R:0 G:176 B:189, HEX: #00b0bd), CREAM (Pantone WARM GRAY 1c, CMYK: C:5 M:7 Y:7 K:0, RGB: R:240 G:234 B:231, HEX: #f0eae7).
- Extended color palette outlined with guidance on picking colors from images to support positive and bright aspects of the brand.

**Typography Information:**

- The primary typeface is FF DIN (Light, Regular, Medium, Bold, and Black weights), noted for its contemporary and highly legible style. It is used for various purposes to maintain visual hierarchy.
- For shared applications or where FF DIN is unavailable, Verdana (Regular and Bold) is used as a substitute for excellent legibility on screens and availability on most computers.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Images play a key role in highlighting the positive aspects of Indian and Nepali culture. Focus is on using bright and positive imagery to convey the brand’s values.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Exclusion area or safe zone around logos is required to maintain clarity. Measurements depend on the logo variant, e.g., Master logo exclusion area rules detailed.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone of communication guidelines includes plain speech, non-corporate, sincere, positive, empowering, concise, and factual language. Avoid verbose, flowery, or patronizing language.
- Messaging focuses on independence, shared values, and partnerships. Examples provided for avoiding cliches and for using empowering and empathetic language.
- Correct pronunciation of the word 'Karuna' (meaning 'Compassion in Action') is important to maintain the integrity of the brand.

**Notes:**

- The brand guidelines were created in April 2017 and explicitly outline Karuna's work and values inspired by Buddhist teachings and the mission to end caste-based discrimination, poverty, and inequality.
- Dame Judi Dench, Patron of Karuna, is quoted: 'Triumph over prejudice and ignorance is a triumph for us all.'

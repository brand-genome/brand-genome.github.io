## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The Quantum logo is the primary identifier of the company and products, appearing on literature, advertising, signs, and all promotional communications. Approved logo artworks are available on the Brand Builder website.
- Clear space requirement for the logo: a margin defined by the x-height of the lowercase letters. This space must remain free of text, imagery, and graphics to avoid crowding the logo.
- Minimum size requirement: 1 inch (25mm, 72px). Logo must not appear smaller to ensure readability. Registration mark is omitted for logos smaller than minimum size or exceeding 3 feet in width.
- Standard size for print: 1.5 inches (38mm, 108px). Common logo sizes unify communication materials.
- Approved logo color variations include Pantone® 2945 blue, grayscale, one-color black, reversed, and four-color applications. Blue logo recommended for primary use.
- Incorrect logo usage examples: Do not distort, change colors, skew, or rotate. Do not add outlines or enclose in shapes. Avoid placing on cluttered backgrounds or combining with other logos.

**Color Information:**

- Corporate Blue (primary color): CMYK (100:50:0:0), RGB (15:115:195), HEX (#0f73c3). Used for logo, headlines, and color bars.
- Neutral colors: CMYK (0:0:0:0) with RGB (255:255:255, HEX #ffffff) for typography; black/grayscale variants for balance.
- Secondary colors: Accent colors complementing Corporate Blue. Includes orange (CMYK 0:30:100:0, HEX #ffba00), green (CMYK 50:0:100:20, HEX #74a534), and pink (CMYK 0:76:6:0, HEX #f0649d), among others.
- Approval and consistent use of the palette maximize communication recognition.

**Typography Information:**

- DIN sans serif family for headlines, body text, subheads, bullet points, and more. Weights include Light, Regular, Medium, and Bold. Arial can be used as an alternative.
- Headline: DIN Medium, 20 pt., Intro Copy: DIN Regular, 11 pt., Body Copy: DIN Light, 9 pt., Bullet Copy: DIN Light, 9 pt., Footnote: DIN Light Italic, 6 pt.
- Font licensing requirements outline usage on equipment and external parties.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Corporate image library forthcoming. Approved photography accessible for brand use.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum size and clear space rules applied to logo usage in layouts to preserve legibility and brand alignment.

**Brand Voice:**

- Unified brand identity using consistent logo, typography, and colors across all communication channels.

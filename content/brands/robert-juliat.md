## Brand Guidelines

### 2012

**Logo Information:**

- The Robert Juliat logo is designed with two elements: the symbol and the typography. The 'RJ' symbol may be used standalone on occasion. Accepted orientations include vertical and horizontal. The typography and logo must not be altered.
- Technical details for the logo include black, white, and gray versions. Color specifications for black: RGB(0,0,0), Pantone Black C.
- Guidelines for resizing include proportional scaling using uniform scale options. Example: a 50% scale requires the logo's line (symbol) to be 1.5pt, typography to be 0.5pt.
- Protected area around the logo equals one-third of the logo's height. No graphic, textual, or visual elements may enter this area.

**Color Information:**

- Yellow (Profiles): CMYK (0%, 27%, 95%, 0%), Pantone 116 C, RGB (252, 193, 0), HEX #fcc100.
- Pink (Luminaires - PC): CMYK (20%, 100%, 10%, 0%), Pantone 226 C, RGB (197, 0, 115), HEX #c50073.
- Blue (Followspots): CMYK (76%, 18%, 11%, 0%), Pantone 299 C, RGB (19, 159, 200), HEX #139fc8.

**Typography Information:**

- The Vandiana Platin Lite font is used for Robert Juliat's logo, conveying elegance and modernity. This font is mandatory for the logo.
- The font is utilized for head titles in all publications (online and offline). Other examples include Myriad Pro or Arial for general contents.

**Imagery Photography:**

- In advertising, a colored content band covers 30% of the height with the Robert Juliat logo in black. Font details include Myriad Pro for majority content and Vandiana Platin for headings.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The Robert Juliat logo must be positioned with proper protected space (one-third of the logo height) to ensure good visibility.
- Catalogs must incorporate the logo on front and back covers. The placement depends on the design.

**Notes:**

- Multimedia designs (website and CD/DVD covers) require the standard logo on the top left. Custom CD/DVD design approval must be obtained from the communication department.

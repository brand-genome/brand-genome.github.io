## Brand Guidelines

### 2008

**Logo Information:**

- The updated Gap logo features three white uppercase letters inside a blue square. It is noted for its simplicity and adjusted proportions for increased clarity and legibility across corporate communication and retail usage.
- Clear space rules: Maintain a minimum clear space around the Gap logo equivalent to 'X-width' (the width of a single letter in 'Gap').
- Minimum size requirement states the logo must never be reproduced smaller than 1/8 inch wide.
- Pantone 655 is specified as the corporate color for the Gap logo, with CMYK equivalents for coated, uncoated, and matte printing processes. RGB equivalent color values are R:0, G:14, B:66.
- Incorrect Usage: Examples include altering or redrawing the logo, changing its colors, removing the square, scaling or skewing the logo improperly, applying gradients, or using the logo diagonally.

**Color Information:**

- Corporate color is Pantone 655 blue, default for all Gap corporate logos. CMYK and RGB equivalents are specified for print reproduction.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family for Gap corporate communications and advertising is Helvetica Neue. Specific weights include Light (subheads and body copy), Medium (headlines), and Bold (posters, in-store signage).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rules require maintaining uncluttered space around the Gap logo equivalent to the X width.

**Notes:**

- The guidelines emphasize consistent application for the Gap brand and sub-brands like Gap Kids, Gap Body, and babyGap. The distinct logos share a common corporate identity for recognition.

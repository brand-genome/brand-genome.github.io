## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The logo is presented with the corporate colors of the university. Components include the symbol, acronym, and logo merged with corporate colors for versatility.
- The symbol may be presented in corporate brown (Pantone 1605 C), positive, or negative depending on the project requirements.
- Minimum size for logotype application is specified as 5 mm for the symbol.
- The acronym 'UAB' is the basic expression of the identity, available in full chromatic, positive, or negative variations based on project usage.
- Minimum size for the acronym UAB is specified as 14 mm.

**Color Information:**

- The color accompanying the symbol is Pantone 1605 C indicated as the corporate brown.
- Corporate colors should be consistently applied across the symbol, acronym, and logotype.
- The logo and accompanying elements must retain their corporate color configurations (e.g., full chromatic, black and white, positive/negative).

**Typography Information:**

- The logotype text uses Helvetica Neue 75 Bold with specific line divisions for better readability: 'Universitat Autònoma' and 'de Barcelona'.
- The corporate typography is Helvetica Neue, with variants including Light, Roman, Medium, and Bold. It ensures graphical and stylistic uniformity.
- Helvetica Neue 75 Bold is identified as the primary and most recognizable font, utilized in the logo text and acronym.

**Brand Voice:**

- The identity of the brand includes an adaptable application across varying proportions and communication media.
- Corporate identity focuses on consistency in using symbols, acronyms, and colors across all media.

**Notes:**

- The brand guidelines emphasize a unified visual identity based on symbols, logos, acronyms, and consistent usage across media.
- No alterations or distortions of typography (e.g., font changes or deformation) may be applied to the logotype or acronym.

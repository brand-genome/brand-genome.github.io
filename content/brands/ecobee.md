## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The ecobee logo is composed of the bee icon and the ecobee wordmark. Together, they create the core of our identity: our logo.
- Minimum clear space requirement around the ecobee logo is 2x the height of the icon. For the best representation, ensure that the logo always remains legible, even at smaller sizes. Clear space ensures the visibility of the logo.
- Do not distort, rotate, recolor, or alter the ecobee logo in any way. Maintain its original design elements, including colors and proportions.
- Examples of incorrect usage of the ecobee logo include changing the proportions of the bee icon, placing it on visually distracting backgrounds, and using unapproved colors.
- Specifications for the ecobee3 lite logo include clear spacing, as illustrated, with a minimum of 2x space around it. Ensure the logo remains legible when sizing down.
- Specifications for the ecobee4 logo include the minimum clear space and a requirement for maintaining proportions.

- Header font: Gotham Light, Body text font: Gotham Book, Legal Copy font: Gotham Book. Minimum font size for legal copy is 6pt/8pt. Ensure leading supports readability and display copy at an appropriate scale.

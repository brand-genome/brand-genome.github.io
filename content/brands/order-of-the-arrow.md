## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The seal is to remain consistent and not altered in any form. Standard seal usage is preferred, with alternate and reversed seals approved for single-color or dark background printing. Holes within the arrow must remain transparent.
- Minimum clear space for tagline usage is 0.5 inches. Vertical standard tagline has a minimum size of 0.8 inch x 1 inch. Alternate vertical tagline shares the same size specifications.
- Horizontal standard tagline minimum size: 3 inches x 0.24 inch. Alternate horizontal tagline minimum size: 3 inches x 0.375 inch.
- Incorrect image use specified: Avoid overlapping brand elements, altering typefaces or colors, adding effects like drop shadows or bevels, rotating logos, truncating elements, or placing elements too close to edges.
- Trademark guidelines: Arrowhead must point in specific angles (e.g., 45 degrees up-left). Correct representation of arrows and seals emphasized.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors specified: HEX #E31837 (CMYK: 0, 100, 81, 4), #36454F (CMYK: 78, 62, 51, 39), #FFFFFF (CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 0). Secondary colors include HEX #640813 (CMYK: 0, 100, 91, 52), #E6E7E8 (CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 10), #231F20 (CMYK: 40, 58, 20, 100), and #005596 (CMYK: 100, 58, 0, 21).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typefaces: Museo Slab 700 for headlines, Museo Sans 300 for body text. Tracking adjustments: Museo Slab 700 headline tracking at 20, Museo Slab 300 secondary headline at 20, Museo Sans 300 body text tracking at 30.
- Alternative typefaces: Rockwell Regular (alternative for Museo Slab) and Gill Sans Light (alternative for Museo Sans), for instances where Museo fonts are unavailable.
- Typography considerations: Avoid text smaller than 8pt. Museo Slab and Rockwell are unsuitable for body text. Museo Sans or Gill Sans Light should be used for body text.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum clear space around tagline: 0.5 inches.
- Do not place logos or brand elements too close to the edge of materials. Leave sufficient space.

**Notes:**

- Specific guidelines exist for embroidered designs to ensure quality (e.g., simplified trademark used for sizes smaller than 1 inch).
- American Indian references in designs must be respectful and historically accurate (e.g., no cartoonish or derogatory portrayals).

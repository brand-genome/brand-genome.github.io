## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The clear space around the logo is defined as 1.5x the height of the logo, ensuring no interference with visibility. Minimum size for the logo is 1 inch or 75px wide.
- The logo is available in full color, one-color, or reversed logo versions. Primary use is full color on white. When only one color is available, the white version should be used on a dark background, such as black or dark-colored backgrounds.
- PMS colors are available for coated and uncoated paper. The logo should only appear in the official primary color palette and never in other color variations or combinations.
- Incorrect usages: Do not add a drop shadow, alter the colors, rotate or angle the logo, distort/stretch the logo, allow background interference with visibility, or place color/gradient/pattern inside the logo. Always use approved artwork from the Agari website.
- Co-branding rules: The Agari logo should be aligned vertically or horizontally with other company logos. Vertical alignment should be center-aligned, while horizontal alignment should be along the baseline. Ensure sufficient clear space and equal distance between logos and divider lines.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes HEX, CMYK, and Pantone specifications for each color: HEX:#3EB1C8 (Pantone 631C, CMYK 74|0|13|0), HEX:#E24224 (Pantone 1788C, CMYK 5|89|100|0), HEX:#F68D2E (Pantone 715C, CMYK 0|54|87|0), HEX:#FFBE54 (Pantone 135C, CMYK 0|27|77|0), and HEX:#49438E (Pantone Cool Grey 10, CMYK 40|30|20|66).
- Secondary colors include HEX:#BEC6C4 (Pantone 441C, CMYK 22|4|15|8) and HEX:#474C56.
- Color gradients: Primary and secondary gradients are defined. Primary gradients should be prioritized for use above secondary gradients to preserve branding. Limit usage of orange gradients to prevent over-utilization and brand dilution.
- Color usage rules specify that only approved colors in the palette are to be used, with proper percentage allocations depending on the content. Contact brand@agari.com for clarifications on usage.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: Gotham, available in weights Medium for eyebrow text, Book for titles and headers, Light for large display text and body text, and Bold for subheaders. This sans-serif typeface conveys strength, accessibility, and modernity while being highly legible.
- Substitute font: Arial Regular and Bold. Arial should be used for headers, subheaders, quotes, callouts, display text, or any other collateral where Gotham is unavailable, such as in Microsoft Word or PowerPoint documents.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Agari's lifestyle imagery is casual, candid, neutral in color, and should reinforce email security themes (e.g., subjects handling devices). Such images are impactful for brochures, web banners, and presentations and should align with the existing collection.
- Do’s: Use casual and candid images that fit into the brand's existing collection without looking out of place. Avoid overly staged, isolated photographs or those with special effects (e.g., sparkles or non-approved color treatments).
- Gradient imagery: Defined gradients can be applied to approved images for specific use cases (e.g., website and trade show materials). Do not use orange gradients on top of images.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grid and alignment specifications for co-branding: Logos should have consistent clear space, equal spacing from divider lines, and vertical or baseline alignment depending on orientation.
- Clear space rules for ACID logo: Minimum clear space around the logo is set at 1.5x the height of the logo. Minimum width is set at 1 inch or 75px wide.

**Brand Voice:**

- Purpose (why Agari exists): 'We protect digital communications to ensure humanity prevails over evil.'
- Positioning statement: Agari uses predictive AI to model identity at internet scale and prevent the next zero-day attack.
- Brand promise: Agari provides the confidence to open, click, and trust everything in your inbox.

**Notes:**

- For any questions about usage or further clarifications, contact the brand team at brand@agari.com.
- The ACID logo should always lead as the primary logo for ACID-related collateral. The Agari logo may appear secondary but never directly adjacent to the ACID logo.

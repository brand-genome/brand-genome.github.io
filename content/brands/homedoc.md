## Brand Guidelines

### 2022

**Logo Information:**

- Tagline 'HomeDoc - Bác sĩ bên bạn' visible and associated with the brand logo in branding context.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tagline: 'HomeDoc - Bác sĩ bên bạn'. HomeDoc is described as friendly, providing online health consultations that give the comfort of being at home. Emphasized the notion of immediate doctor assistance when needed ('knocking on the door').
- Vision: To become the top trusted icon in Vietnam for health, care, and beauty services that improve Vietnamese lives with top-quality support available anytime, anywhere.
- Mission: To ensure everyone in Vietnam has access to top-quality health care anytime and anywhere.
- Values: 1) Commitment to professional, high-quality health care for every customer. 2) Respect, emphasizing that health care cannot wait, ensuring the fastest, most accurate advice for users.

**Notes:**

- The main brand service is defined as the fastest, most accurate 24/7 online health consultation app. Emphasis on user-friendly, immediate connection to doctors as required.

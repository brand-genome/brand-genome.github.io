### 2007

**Logo Information:**

- The logo comprises two fixed elements: the 'Speech Bubble' symbol and the wordmark 'SQUARE', which must not be altered in their relationship.
- Minimum size for logo reproduction is 10mm to ensure legibility.
- The exclusion zone around the logo must remain free from typographic or graphic elements and is based on the height of the 'Speech Bubble' (measured as 'X').
- Logo color variants include: full color (Pantone Cool Grey 6, 40% tint of black), mono black/white, and reversed out of solid backgrounds or images.
- 'Do Not' rules: Do not distort, outline, add effects, remove strapline, or place the logo over distracting or busy backgrounds.
- The 'Speech Bubble' symbol may be used alone as a versatile branding tool, and can contain text, photography, or illustration.
- The logo must always appear at the bottom-right corner for consistent branding across layouts.

**Color Information:**

- Primary neutral color: Pantone Cool Grey 6 or 40% tint of black. Suitable for branding and co-branding scenarios.
- Warm metallic colors include gold, sand, silver/grey, and copper/terracotta, aligned with interior design themes and can be applied in metallic finishes.
- Vibrant colors can be contrasted with neutral tones like Cool Grey for visual impact in communication materials.

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate font family is Helvetica Neue in three weights: Light 45, Roman 55, and Bold 75. Substitute typeface for digital applications is Arial (Regular for body text, Bold for headings).
- Titles must appear in uppercase and bold, body text in lowercase with no decorative effects like shadows or borders.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style emphasizes high-quality, engaging visuals with quiet, uncluttered areas for text overlay.
- Illustration style includes outlined objects that are color-filled or reversed out of solid backgrounds.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Layout uses a five-column grid system, with measurements and alignment tied to the 'X' height of the 'Speech Bubble' in the logo.
- Logo must always occupy the bottom-right position in layouts, with other elements right-aligned to reinforce brand consistency.
- In co-branding, the 'SQUARE' logo must dominate in size and positioning. Partner logos should use compatible colors like Pantone Cool Grey or 40% black.

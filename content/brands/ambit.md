## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The Ambit Full Logo uses PMS 021C. It is described as the most visible and most reproduced element of the brand. Available in three color variants: On Orange, On Grey and White, and On Black.
- The Ambit Wordmark uses PMS 021C and is available in three color variants: On Orange, On Grey and White, and On Black. It should be used only where the Ambit Full Logo is considered inappropriate.
- The Ambit Graphic Element uses PMS 021C and is available in three color variants for different applications: On Orange, On Grey and White, and On Black.
- Minimum clear space for the Ambit logo is defined by the height of the 'a'. The minimum size for print is 8.5mm and online use is 32 pixels.
- Incorrect uses of the logo include: altering the measurement between the icon and wordmark, changing the ratio between brandmark and wordmark, placing the logo in a box or keyline border, stretching, skewing, angling the logo, or changing the color of the logo.

**Color Information:**

- Core colors: AI Orange (Pantone 021 C; CMYK: C0 M80 Y100 K0; RGB: R254 G80 B0; HEX: #FE5102), Black (Pantone Black C; CMYK: C0 M0 Y0 K100 for standard black, C75 M68 Y67 K100 for rich black; RGB: R0 G0 B0; HEX: #000000), 60% Black (CMYK: C0 M0 Y0 K60; RGB: R134 G134 B134; HEX: #868685), 7% Black (CMYK: C0 M0 Y0 K7; RGB: R242 G242 B242; HEX: #f1f1f1).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Brand Typeface: Mark Pro in Bold and Book weights. Mark Pro should be used for all marketing and communication materials. Recommendation for body copy: Mark Pro Regular at 9pt font size on A4 documents. Use standard black as font color.
- PC Typeface: Montserrat, to be used as default when Mark Pro is unavailable. Montserrat includes Semibold and Medium weights and can be downloaded from Google Fonts.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Primary logo design references the legacy of Pacífico Energy while being bold and unique. The Pacífico 'P' symbol is also used independently for specific purposes.
- Clear space around the logo should be maintained. Minimum spacing rules are highlighted.
- Incorrect usage examples include: altering logo colors, placing the logo in a container, distorting, or adding drop shadows.

**Color Information:**

- Core color palette includes specific Pantone, HEX, RGB, and CMYK values. Example: PMS 2766 C, HEX #1c1e4d, RGB (28, 30, 77), CMYK (100, 96, 37, 39).
- Brand-specific palette adds distinct hues (e.g., PMS 130 C, CMYK (2, 38, 100, 0); HEX #ea9d13, RGB (234, 158, 34)).

**Typography Information:**

- Semplicita Pro is the official typeface, providing a modern aesthetic. Typeface uses include Regular, Medium, and Semibold weights with complete alphabet and numeral examples.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should be human-centric and accent specific brand elements. Imagery should align with services, industries, and use tints from the color palette.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear spacing rules for the logo ensure no encroachments by other elements. The logo should maintain prominence and spacing uniformity.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice is conversational and informative, witty yet educational, and inspirational. Messaging themes aim to empower businesses.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The Plan2go logo is available in two size formats depending on usage. Alternate color treatments include color and black/white versions. Clear space must be observed around the logo to maximize visual effectiveness.
- The Plan2go symbol acts as a secondary branding mark. It is available in both color and black/white treatments.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: Green (CMYK: 75, 0, 100, 0; RGB: 58, 180, 73; HEX: #3ab449), Blue (CMYK: 61, 0, 0, 0; RGB: 62, 197, 243; HEX: #3ec5f3), Charcoal (CMYK: 73, 62, 50, 35; RGB: 66, 73, 83; HEX: #424953). Supplemental colors: Dark Blue (CMYK: 93, 58, 18, 2; RGB: 0, 102, 153; HEX: #016699), White (CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 0; RGB: 255, 255, 255; HEX: #ffffff), Grey (CMYK: 17, 11, 13, 0; RGB: 209, 212, 211; HEX: #d1d4d3). Supplemental colors should be used for highlights and accents, not as dominant colors.

**Typography Information:**

- Print documents use Myriad Pro for headings (e.g., Heading 1: 30pt; Heading 2: 18pt; Heading 3 bold in all caps, 12pt) and Cambria for paragraphs (11pt).
- Worksheets use Myriad Pro for headings (e.g., Page heading: 30pt, chart title: 16pt) and Cambria for normal text (11pt). Specific colors are designated for these uses (e.g., heading color: green RGB (58, 180, 73) and charcoal RGB (66, 73, 83)).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography guidelines include inspiring, provocative, realistic, engaging, accessible, authentic, and safe imagery. Avoid overly busy images. Image formats should be JPG or PNG, and videos should be MPEG 4 (H.264).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Alignment and white space principles must be followed. Copy provided by Plan2go should include credits where applicable, and photography cannot be sold externally without proper regulatory approval.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand encourages the use of simple, clear, and approachable wording. Avoid jargon or complex phrases. The tone emphasizes positivity, functionality, and customer engagement.
- The branding phrase is: 'When you Plan2go, we’re already there!' It should be used in official communications and email signatures.

**Notes:**

- All design artwork representing Plan2go must be reviewed using the Artwork approval document to ensure accuracy in spelling, grammar, colors, layout, size, dimensions, and legal compliance.
- Contact details for brand-related queries are listed for key personnel, including Creative Services Leader, Marketing & Promotions Leader, and Design Officer.

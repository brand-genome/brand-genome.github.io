## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The primary Twitch logo includes the Extruded Wordmark (3D dimensional logo), which is used primarily for outbound communications. Additional logos include the Twitch wordmark, used internally and in partnerships, and 'Glitch', which is the mascot logo (non-wordmark). Clear space guidelines indicate spacing measured by 'x' (size of logo elements). Examples of lockups (logo with other text or logos) demonstrate pairings with proportional spacing around the cap height and x-height.
- Logo Lockups specify internal and partnership lockups, including safe spacing rules. Logos paired with other content use proportional spacing aligned to cap height and x-height.

**Color Information:**

- Twitch Core Palette includes three main colors: Twitch Purple (#9146FF), Black Ops (#000000), and Ice (#F0F0FF). Each palette specifies RGB, HEX, CMYK, and Pantone specifications for digital, print, or IRL use. Example color usage principles suggest using RGB for digital and PMS for physical media.
- Twitch Secondary Palette includes a range of accent and gradient colors, such as Dragon (#8205B4), Jiggle (#FAB4FF), Widow (#BFABFF), and others, with full RGB, HEX, CMYK, and Pantone specifications. These colors are designed to enhance expressiveness in 'turbo mode.'
- Core Palette pairing guidance indicates logo color combinations. Examples include Twitch Purple on Ice, Black Ops on Twitch Purple, and Twitch Purple on Black Ops. These combinations should be used for 'core communications.'

**Typography Information:**

- Brand typeface is Roobert, with six weights (Light, Regular, Medium, Semibold, Bold, Heavy) and 12 styles including italics. Regular, Medium, Bold, and Semibold are the most commonly used, while Light and Heavy are for special purposes. Headline specifications: Roobert SemiBold, 80/80/-20; Body specifications: Roobert Medium, 16/20/-10; Small body text is set to Roobert Medium, 11/14. Highlight/Callout type is set in Roobert Large, 30/33/-15.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Example layouts show typography placement principles: headline top left with body copy at the bottom, centered, or split layouts. Cropped and repetitive streaming effects illustrate bold layout styles intended for emphasis.

**Brand Voice:**

- Twitch's voice is described as Mischievous (playful and fun), Purposeful (helpful and hosting focus), Iterative (embracing a 'work in progress' mindset), and Revolutionary (differentiated and boundary-pushing). Tonal scales range from Casual (friendly slang use) and Encouraging (motivational) to Firm, Reassuring, and Celebratory tones. Each tone is adjusted for audience engagement such as social media posts or event announcements.

**Notes:**

- Contact Twitch design team for clarification regarding brand elements or communication guidelines. Photographic examples within the guide are illustrative only and may not be licensed for public-facing content.

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The Twitch wordmark is the primary logo, retaining DNA from previous iterations to evoke nostalgia and roots in gaming.
- Extruded wordmark is the primary outbound logo for expressive branding contexts.
- The Glitch icon represents Twitch in a simplified form and acts as the mascot of the brand.
- Clear space rules: 'x' indicates spacing around logos (such as glitch, wordmark, and extruded wordmark).
- Logo lockups include internal lockups and partnership lockups with guidelines for proper spacing (aligned to x height or cap height).
- Four logo color pairings used in core communications: Twitch Purple on Ice, Black Ops on Twitch Purple, Twitch Purple on Black.

**Color Information:**

- Core colors: Twitch Purple (PMS 814C, HEX #9146FF, RGB: R145 G70 B255, CMYK: C57 M60 Y0 K0), Black Ops (PMS Black 6C, HEX #000000, RGB: R0 G0 B0, CMYK: C100 M80 Y40 K90), Ice (PMS 656C, HEX #F0F0FF, RGB: R240 G240 B255, CMYK: C10 M4 Y2 K1).
- Secondary palette includes colors such as Dragon (PMS 2084C, HEX #8205B4, RGB: R130 G5 B180), Widow (PMS 270C, HEX #BFABFF, RGB: R190 G170 B255), Ice, and several others for 'turbo mode' activations.
- Usage rules: RGB for digital, PMS preferred for IRL designs, CMYK for fallback scenarios.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Twitch Roobert.
- Roobert font weights include Light, Regular, Medium, Semibold, Bold, and Heavy. Regular, Medium, Bold, and Semibold are commonly used.
- Typography roles: Headlines (SemiBold, size 80/80/-20), Body (Medium, size 16/20/-10), Small Body Text (Medium, size 11/14/0), Highlight/Call Out (Large, 30/33/-15).
- Typography styles include regular and italic versions to add emphasis.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Typography layout should be simple, punchy, and organized, avoiding intricate designs. Examples provided for headline and body text alignment, including headline top left, centered, split, or left-aligned body copy at the bottom.

**Brand Voice:**

- Voice guiding principles: Mischievous (fun with good-natured humor), Purposeful (helpful and supportive), Iterative (embracing rawness and progress), Revolutionary (striving for difference and challenging the status quo).
- Tone: Functional or emotional scales, ranging from casual (social media posts) to celebratory (events and milestones).

**Notes:**

- Brand assets referenced should match the Brand Package. Examples in the guidelines are illustrative and not licensed for public facing use.

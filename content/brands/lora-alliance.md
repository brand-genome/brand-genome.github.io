### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- BRAND IDENTITY Style guide V1.1
- Brand Identity Styleguide Version 1.1: Includes a section on LOGOS.
- Logo Overview and guidelines for LoRa Alliance™, LoRaWAN® marks.
- Logos: LoRa Alliance™ logo with parts 'LoRa' and 'Alliance'; variations with logos using color guidelines.
- LoRa Alliance™ member logos and usage rights for members in good standing.
- LoRaWAN® logo details: Not approved for use on products. Describes logo design with 'LoRa' and 'WAN' in stylized font and separate colors.
- LoRaWAN® ambassador logo rules and depiction: Contains three parts, including the term ‘Ambassador’ in all caps.
- LoRaWAN® color logo requirements: CMYK (C100/M0/Y0/K0 and C0/M0/Y0/K0), RGB (R0/G174/B239 and R255/G255/B255), Hex #00adef and #FFFFFF. Minimum width required is 1 inch.
- Depiction Rules: Logos must follow color guidelines, maintain proportional sizing, and not use non-approved colors or on low-contrast backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette: Yellow and Cyan. Yellow: CMYK C0/M0/Y93/K0, RGB R255/G238/B0, Hex #ffee00, Pantone 102 C.
- Primary color palette: Cyan - CMYK C100/M0/Y0/K0, RGB R0/G174/B239, Hex #00adef, Pantone Process Cyan.
- Secondary colors: Carbon, Granite, Nickel Greys. Carbon: CMYK C15/M15/Y0/K84, RGB R35/G35/B41, PMS Pantone 532 C; Granite: CMYK C13/M13/Y0/K62, RGB R85/G85/B98, PMS Pantone Cool Grey 2C.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Typefaces: Arial family - Arial Regular and Arial Bold. Secondary Typeface: Aperçu Bold for subheadings.
- Arial styles: Arial Regular used for body text. Arial Black for headers over 100 pt.
- Typography guidance includes kerning and tracking (-20) selection for captions and body copy.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery guidelines overview: Striking and bold imagery with yellow and black-and-white photography for cityscapes, landscapes, and IoT-related environments.
- Landscape photography with integrated typography, using color yellow prominently.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum width requirements for logos: Horizontal at least 1.5 inches and vertical at least 0.5 inches.

**Brand Voice:**

- General brand language tone with emphasis on using correct terms like LoRaWAN® for networks and protocols.

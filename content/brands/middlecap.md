## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The primary logotype version is displayed and includes both monochromic variants (dark gray and full white). Clear space/protection zone around the logo is specified to ensure no graphical interference, defined as minimal space around the logo that no other element can intrude. Minimum size for logo usage is 20mm for readability.
- The icon version of the logo is used for smaller formats and as a favicon.
- Division logos are composed of two parts: the base version supplemented with division name below, designed to distinguish different divisions yet retain uniform corporate identity.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors consist of two dark gray shades: RGB #29313e (Pantone 289 C, CMYK 79/67/50/55); RGB #616466 (Pantone Cool Gray 8 C, CMYK 62/52/50/21). These dominate to reflect a serious and elegant aesthetic with gradients formed between them.
- Secondary colors include: Blue (Pantone 299 C, CMYK 100/16/0/0, RGB #009ade), used for larger colorful areas; Orange (Pantone 7625 C, CMYK 8/93/75/0, RGB #e0684b), used exclusively for text and graphical highlights.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font is Soho Gothic Pro, a geometric sans-serif typeface with no emphasis on differentiation in strokes or ornaments. Commonly used weights are Light, Italic, and Bold, and sometimes Thin for large headings.
- Secondary font is Exo 2, a similar geometric sans-serif font that is freely available via Google Fonts, intended for use on devices without the primary font.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space/protection zone around the logo is mandatory to avoid graphical intrusion. This space ensures logo visibility in design applications.

**Notes:**

- Design manual reflects a visual identity evolution to emphasize darker shades, refined materials, and modest usage of cyan for a more professional and modern aesthetic.
- Downloadable assets for logos and divisions can be retrieved from provided links in the guideline.

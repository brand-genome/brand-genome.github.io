## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Color Information:**

- WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS
Deep blue, Coral, Aqua, Stone, and Sun colors defined with the following specifications:
Deep Blue: PMS 281C, CMYK: C:100 M:90 Y:0 K:60, RGB: R:0 G:0 B:55
Reef: PMS 2366C, CMYK: C:78 M:65 Y:0 K:0, RGB: R:93 G:100 B:255
Coral: PMS 2348C, CMYK: C:0 M:82 Y:72 K:0, RGB: R:251 G:71 B:58
Aqua: PMS 338C, CMYK: C:72 M:0 Y:48 K:0, RGB: R:0 G:191 B:162
Stone: CMYK: C:7 M:5 Y:6 K:0, RGB: R:240 G:240 B:240
Sun: PMS 102C, CMYK: C:7 M:0 Y:87 K:0, RGB: R:253 G:241 B:0
Color guidelines: RGB and CMYK values must not be altered, Stone is recommended as an alternative to white on dark backgrounds, and white is recommended for logos placed over images for maximum contrast.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style emphasizes storytelling beyond action shots, capturing the unique personality of ITF Beach Tennis. Focuses on beach culture, passion of fans, intensity of players, and dramatic environments. Captures freedom and respects natural environments. Contrasts between close-up details, panoramic, and aerial views.
- Fans are presented in authentic ways that capture their youthful spirit, togetherness, and energy of a festival vibe lifestyle.
- Players are featured in behind-the-scenes settings, emphasizing their personality, intensity, and dynamics beyond generic actions.

**Brand Voice:**

- Core values include:
- Explosive: High intensity, non-stop fast-paced action, loud and vibrant experiences.
- Dramatic: Adventurous settings, exotic and stunning global locations, celebration of freedom.
- Leading: Inspiring and inclusive with good governance and professionalism.
- Youthful: Energizing and fun, inspired by a healthy lifestyle and festival vibes.
Mission: Expand Beach Tennis as a leading beach sport globally, including Olympic recognition.
Vision: Become the world's most popular beach sport.

**Notes:**

- Usage rights are restricted to authorized parties. Intellectual property from third parties (e.g., players, photographers) requires prior written consent. All applications must align with guidelines and receive prior approval from the ITF Beach Tennis Brand Department.

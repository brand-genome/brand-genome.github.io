## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Clear space around the DOW logo must equal at least half the height of the logo ('1/2 X = clear space').
- Minimum size of logo should ensure optimal legibility, reviewed on real-world media, screens, and test prints.
- Logo misuse guidelines: Do not create additional lock-ups, do not modify logotype, do not add shadows, do not distort or use repeating patterns, no unauthorized configurations, among others.
- Background rules: Acceptable placements include solid colors with sufficient contrast, subdued or light textures, and clean photographic areas.
- Incorrect background usage: Examples include placing the logo on busy or poorly contrasting backgrounds, allowing the background to bleed through the logo text on the red Diamond, or using gradients improperly.
- Trademark enforcement: Always use the registered symbol '®' with the Dow Diamond logo and consult legal guidelines for logo use restrictions and exceptions.
- Logo with brandline combinations: Primary-use and limited-use lockups are detailed, with guidelines for vertical, horizontal, centered, or left-aligned configurations.
- Color versions of logo with brandline: Full-color positive, full-color reverse, one-color black positive, and one-color reverse versions are available for appropriate usage contexts.

**Color Information:**

- Brand color palettes include options for slate gray, white, black, and light/dark contrasts for various placements.
- ADA compliance guidelines specify maintaining sufficient color contrasts for legibility and functionality in print and on screen.

**Typography Information:**

- Brandline 'Seek Together' text has customized typography and must not be recreated manually. Explicit minimum size specifications include a width of 1 inch for print.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Logo backgrounds should be light, clean, and contrast effectively with logo elements (examples include gradient-free textures and soft photographic compositions).
- Examples of problematic images or backgrounds include busy textures, gradients, and insufficient differentiation.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space for the logo specified as '1/2 X' (half the height of the logo). For brandline usage, minimum spacing equals letter 'h' height around the text.
- For key lockups, spacing between the logo and brandline is specified (e.g. '1/2 X', '2 X'). Examples for horizontal and vertical alignments are illustrated.

**Brand Voice:**

- The 'Seek Together' brandline embodies collaboration, innovation, inclusion, and partnership principles.
- Avoid integrating the 'Seek Together' brandline directly into routine text, headlines, or editorial content.

**Notes:**

- Digital artwork files for logos and brand elements must always be sourced from the Brand Center. Unauthorized creations or modifications are strictly prohibited.
- Legal approvals are required for external use of the Dow Diamond logo by third-party organizations or subsidiaries.

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- Logo versions: Dow Diamond logo descriptions and restricted use, allowing no lock-ups of other logos or words with the Diamond.
- Clear space requirements: Minimum clear space for objects is 1/2X the height of the Diamond. Minimum for text is 2X the height.
- Logo color versions: Full-color (PMS 185 C Dow Red or CMYK/RGB equivalent), 1-color black, and 1-color reversed versions are defined for specific background contrasts.
- Do's and Don'ts: The logo must not have business names as lock-ups. Incorrect usages are explicitly prohibited.

**Color Information:**

- Dow Red as a primary color, representing leadership and heritage. Accent and neutral colors complement the red but do not compete with it.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Dow Corporate (a Swiss sans serif). Six approved weights are available.
- Secondary typeface: Swift Neue LT Pro (compact serif). Used for secondary copy applications like interior spreads and communications.
- System fonts when primary fonts are unavailable: Arial for Dow Corporate, Times New Roman for Swift Neue. Specific weights provided.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Image style emphasizes crisp, optimistic, clean, naturally lit, and authentic images. Dynamic cropping and micro/macro contrast are highlighted.
- Avoid abstract or heavily technical imagery to focus on human-centric storytelling.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rules: Minimum of 1/2X for objects and 2X for text based on the height of Dow Diamond. No exact grid specifications mentioned otherwise.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand voice prioritizes a blend of passion and purpose, aligning Dow's communications with its brand pillars and attributes (e.g., 'confident', 'engaging').
- Power words for communications include terms like 'adventurous', 'innovative', 'responsible', which embody Dow's brand values.

**Notes:**

- Dow uses a masterbrand strategy to ensure brand recognition across products. Individual product logos are generally prohibited.
- Dow brand visual materials include approved stationery, templates, and branded samples for consistent messaging.
- Contact the Dow Brand Team for specific brand-related queries at dowbrand@dow.com.

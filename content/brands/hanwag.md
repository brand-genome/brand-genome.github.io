## Brand Guidelines

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- The Hanwag logo consists of two parts: 'Hanwag' in a red circle and the brand claim 'Outdoor Footwear since 1921'.
- Two versions of the Hanwag logo: 1) Freestanding logo for use on white backgrounds, and 2) Logo in a frame for use on colored backgrounds or in photos.
- Minimum size requirements for printing: a freestanding logo requires a diameter of 13.5 mm and a framed logo requires a width of 17 mm.
- Outline variant of the logo for embossing on leather. Requires approval from marketing before use.
- Safe zone rules: When using the logo with a frame, the frame thickness (X) should equal the cap height of the brand claim text. The minimum distance to the next visual element must also be at least X (the frame thickness).
- Safe zone rules: For the freestanding logo, the minimum distance (Y) must be maintained to all other graphic elements, where Y is equivalent to the distance between the circle and the top edge of the second line of the claim.
- Incorrect usage guidelines: Do not alter the red circle, change colors, omit the white background, use only the wordmark without the claim, apply 3D effects, shadows, outline versions, or additional elements.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color 'Hanwag Red': CMYK 10/100/80/0, Pantone 186C, RGB 219/1/50, Hex #cc0033, HKS 15K, RAL 3020.
- Secondary color 'Hanwag Gray': CMYK 0/0/5/18, Pantone 400C, RGB 211/209/50, Hex #cccccc, HKS 89K (70%), RAL 7047.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary fonts: Caecilia LT (for body text) and Knockout (for headlines). Caecilia LT includes 'Roman', and 'Heavy' weights. Knockout is used for headlines, always in white on red or red on white backgrounds. Gray can only be used if it appears with larger red headlines.
- Settings for body text: Font - Caecilia Roman, size - 9 pt, line spacing - 12 pt, tracking - metric. Recommended alignment styles - left ragged or justified text; no italic, right-aligned, or centered text.
- Do not use other fonts like Times. Do not adjust spacing or tracking too narrowly (<25) or too widely (>25). Avoid using lowercase fonts or any font colors except red, white, or gray. Avoid using Knockout in black.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Hanwag imagery focuses on authentic alpine settings. Photos should feature active users in real outdoor situations with no emphasis on the camera gear.
- Avoid unsuitable photos, such as people in unnatural settings (e.g., Photoshop landscapes) or overly soft environments where subjects smile directly at the camera.
- Product photography should show shoes clearly from direct perspectives. A subtle shadow may be used. Defaults for drop-shadows are specified in Adobe InDesign.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Safe zones for the logo are described, with specific measurements (X for framed logos, Y for freestanding logos).
- Alignment and typographic spacing rules for body copy and headlines are outlined (e.g., flat left align and justified text for body copy, tracking values for headlines).

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand emphasizes authenticity and an alpine heritage. Imagery and tone should evoke a connection to the mountains.

**Notes:**

- Older logo variants must no longer be used under any circumstances.
- Icons designed exclusively for the corporate design are downloadable online and must not be modified or replaced.
- Hanwag's infographics have a unique style in line with the brand's visual identity; downloadable templates are available online.

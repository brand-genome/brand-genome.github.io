## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The logo is versatile and associated with marine and coastal environments, provided guidelines ensure professional representation.
- A two-color logo version is recommended for budget-sensitive placements (e.g., t-shirts, mugs, etc.) where clarity is affected. The colors of this variant must remain unchanged.
- A one-color logo variant is suitable for designs requiring high contrast. In this variant alone, changing colors is acceptable to meet design needs.
- Whitespace guidelines specify using the width of the letter 'M' as the scaling standard of measure to avoid obscuring visual elements.
- When displayed alongside the UN Decade of Ocean Science logo, whitespace required for their logo is equivalent to the height of two stacked zeros.
- Incorrect usage guidelines: reproduction using outlines, off-brand colors, special effects (e.g., shadows, bevels) or other alterations is not allowed.

**Color Information:**

- Pantone 7555, HEX #D09E11, RGB: 208, 157, 017.
- Pantone 3035, HEX #004153, RGB: 001, 062, 082.
- Pantone 631, HEX #3CB6CE, RGB: 006, 175, 196.
- The two-color logo variant colors must remain unchanged.

**Typography Information:**

- Vintage Roper (design font used in marketing materials).
- Montserrat Regular (design font used in marketing materials).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Whitespace guidance involves using the width of the letter 'M' as the scaling measure for the logo.
- When displayed next to the UN Decade of Ocean Science logo, the required whitespace is equivalent to the height of two stacked zeros.

**Notes:**

- Marine Life 2030 is aligned with the UN Decade of Ocean Science for Sustainable Development.
- Users should refer to the set of guidelines when displaying Marine Life 2030 alongside UN Decade logos.
- Reproduction of the logo inconsistent with guidelines should be reported, and proper guidance provided via this document.

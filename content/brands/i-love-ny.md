## Brand Guidelines

### 2008

**Logo Information:**

- Stacked logo shown with clearspace rules defined as the vertical height of the heart icon referred to as 'x'.
- Horizontal logo shown with similar clearspace rules (vertical height of the heart icon as 'x').
- Three color versions of the I LOVE NEW YORK logo exist: full color, entirely black, and entirely white.
- One-color printing uses 100% black or reversed to white. Two-color printing uses letters in 100% black and heart in PMS 485. Four-color printing uses magenta and yellow for the heart.
- EPS files are mandated for logo use and must not be recreated, cropped, deleted, or reconfigured.
- Minimum size for logos on ads: 1 inch wide for full pages, no smaller than 60 pixels for standard web banners.
- Guidelines for logo placement on Travel Guide Covers include location (upper right corner or upper left corner) with size specifications for stacked (.09” x 1”) and horizontal (1” x .5”) versions.
- Don'ts for logo usage include no distortion, bevel, emboss, cropping, color changes, outline presentation, placing logos on busy photographs, and recreating elements.
- Seasonal logos may appear as stacked or horizontal, but only in black/red color combinations; must not be recreated.
- Legal trademark requirements mandate the inclusion of ® or ™ based on logo type (stacked/horizontal vs thematic/seasonal).

**Color Information:**

- Logo requires PMS 485 for red coloration and exact specifications for one-color, two-color, and four-color options.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Travel Guide cover designs include region or county logos, optional banners for visual impact, and the 'Another reason' lock-up alongside imagery.
- Illustrations paired with cultural themes such as 'Waterfall', 'Vineyard', 'Mansion', and 'Carnival' for Travel Guide branding.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space surrounding logos is defined by the height of the heart icon ('x').
- Travel Guide covers require placement rules for logos, including size minimums and consistent spacing guidelines between logos and other graphical elements.

**Brand Voice:**

- The voice of I LOVE NEW YORK should be consistently smart, beautiful, unified, and motivating to establish New York as the ultimate travel destination.
- The bridgeline 'Another reason' serves as a connection between something uniquely New York State and the iconic logo.

**Notes:**

- Permitted use of I LOVE NEW YORK logo requires prior approval by the I LOVE NEW YORK office.
- Tourism websites must feature logo with URL lock-up placed in the upper right section.
- Radio spots require the bridgeline 'Another reason, I LOVE NEW YORK' to be read along with copy adherence to bridgeline guidelines.
- Television or video spots require logo placement following safe area rules with minimum dimensions provided for readability.
- Guidelines mandate usage of logo artwork against solid backgrounds for proper contrast and prohibit modification from prior printed materials.

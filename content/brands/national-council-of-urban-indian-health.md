## Brand Guidelines

### 2022

**Logo Information:**

- Minimum size for the logo is specified as follows: The logotype should never be smaller than 60px tall in digital or 22mm tall in print. The logomark should never be smaller than 100px in digital or 36mm tall in print.
- The logo exclusion zone equals half the height of the logo (marked as x in the diagram). This applies to all logo versions.
- Guidelines state that logo misuse must be avoided. They include instructions not to apply gradients, rotate, change colors, distort or warp, use text only without the logo or vice versa, outline, or change the typeface. Incorrect usage examples are provided.

**Color Information:**

- Primary and secondary brand colors are documented with color codes: Bone (#E3D3CA, C:10% M:15% Y:17% K:0%, R:227 G:211 B:202), Moccasin (#EFCE9D, C:5% M:18% Y:49% K:0%, R:239 G:206 B:157), Turquoise (#69B096, C:61% M:12% Y:49% K:0%, R:105 G:176 B:150), Stone (#143535, C:86% M:58% Y:63% K:58%, R:20 G:53 B:53), Redwood (#6C251F, C:34% M:88% Y:84% K:46%, R:108 G:37 B:31), Cascade (#16757F, C:86% M:39% Y:44% K:11%, R:22 G:117 B:127), Clay (#BA4536, C:20% M:85% Y:86% K:9%, R:186 G:69 B:54).
- Instructions for combining colors indicate certain combinations to avoid (e.g., Whalebone, Turquoise, and Clay/Redwood together unless intentionally referencing holiday themes). Guidelines suggest careful use of red-white-green and red-white-blue combinations.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Exclusion zone is defined as half the height of the logo on all its variants.

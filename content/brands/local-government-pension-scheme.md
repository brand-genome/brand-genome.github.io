## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The LGPS brand mark consists of two parts: the wordmark and the descriptor. All versions are available in both English and Welsh.
- Full color version (with descriptor): Wordmark and descriptor are positioned side by side with the wordmark on the left. These components are a locked unit and cannot be altered or recreated.
- Full color version (without descriptor): Used when the context already clarifies the meaning of LGPS, such as the LGPS website.
- Alternative versions: Mono-positive (black) and mono-negative (white) versions for use on dark backgrounds or when full-color printing is not possible.
- Minimum size: The brand mark width must be at least 55mm for print and 270 pixels for digital use.
- Clear space (safe zone): Clearance area is calculated by the height of the 'P' in the wordmark. This minimum area should surround the brand mark and descriptor and be increased wherever possible.
- File formats: EPS (vector, no quality loss, suitable for print), JPG (raster, may degrade in quality if scaled, suitable for print and digital), PNG (raster, transparent background, digital only), SVG (vector, no quality loss, suitable for digital).
- File naming convention: LGPS_Language_Brandmark_Type_Colour_.Format. Example: LGPS_ENG_Brandmark_+desc_black.jpg.
- Logo usage guidelines — do not: position other elements too close, distort, rotate, place on an image, change relationship of the components, change the color, change descriptor typeface, outline the logo, or use it on an illegible background.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: LGPS Orange (Pantone PMS 173, RGB: 208/63/24, HEX: #D13F16, CMYK: 10/85/100/0).
- Secondary color: LGPS Green (Pantone PMS 7742, RGB: 79/125/64, HEX: #4F7040, CMYK: 70/30/90/15).
- Secondary color: LGPS Blue (Pantone PMS 295, RGB: 32/35/74, HEX: #20234A, CMYK: 100/92/40/40).
- Secondary color: LGPS Dark Grey (Pantone PMS 424, RGB: 112/112/112, HEX: #707070, CMYK: 0/0/0/70).
- Color usage rules: Primary color (LGPS Orange) must dominate over secondary colors, which serve to complement and enhance communications without overpowering LGPS Orange.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clearance/safe zone: The area surrounding the brand mark is based on the height of the 'P' in LGPS wordmark. This is a minimum measurement and should be expanded whenever possible.

**Notes:**

- Introduction: The identity of the Local Government Pension Scheme (LGPS) symbolizes modernity, authority, and openness. A clear and professional brand identity reinforces LGPS as a credible public sector pension scheme.
- Guideline intent: These guidelines ensure consistent application of the brand identity by all local pension funds. They outline the appropriate use of the brand mark across different situations and formats.

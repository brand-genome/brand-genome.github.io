## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The official 'isologo' of Universidad Politécnica Salesiana combines an image of Don Bosco and a map of Ecuador. This variation must appear on all printed or digital materials of the UPS.
- The 'isotipo' is a complementary monogram variant of the isologo, primarily reserved for social media profiles. It may also be used as a watermark, editorial seal, or decorative detail, following chromatic and design constraints. It must not be used as the primary identifier.
- Clear space (area of resguardo) around the isologo is measured with 'X', the height of the isologo element.
- Minimum size for logo usage: 10mm for print use, 36.6mm for large printed applications, 28.5px for digital use, and 104px for high-resolution digital applications.
- Restrictions on logo usage include: no alterations to dimensions, proportions, or element orders, no cutting or removing parts of the logo, no 3D usage, no stretching or distorting.
- The full-color isologo must always be placed on a white background and cannot be used on colored backgrounds or photographs.
- Guidelines for combining the isologo with sub-brands or names dictate a symmetrical proportional space. Sub-brand names must be in Gill Sans Regular, all uppercase.
- Colors for isologo with sub-brands or names must adhere to the logotype's white background rule, with text in PANTONE 281 CVU.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors for the isologo are PANTONE 281 CVU (blue) and PANTONE 130 CVU (yellow). Additional variations include PANTONE 281 CVU at 60% and 20% opacity, and PANTONE 130 CVU at 30% opacity.
- Black-and-white applications simplify the isologo to a monochromatic format for better readability on smaller sizes and various materials.
- Positive and negative versions of the isologo use the following colors: PANTONE 281 CVU (dark blue), PANTONE P179-16 C, PANTONE 130 CVU (yellow), and PANTONE 8402 C (gray).
- Metallic variations are used exclusively for institutional decoratives in the three UPS campuses (Cuenca, Quito, Guayaquil). These include silver and gold versions.

**Typography Information:**

- The official typeface is Gill Sans, with weights Regular and Bold. It includes a full set of uppercase, lowercase letters, and numbers.
- Sub-brands or names paired with the isologo must use the Gill Sans Regular typeface in uppercase letters only.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rules require a buffer ('X') around the isologo.

**Notes:**

- The brand guideline document emphasizes the importance of following its rules to maintain the integrity of the Universidad Politécnica Salesiana's identity for internal and external communications.

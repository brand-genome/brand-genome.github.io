## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The monogram consists of two shapes representing East Grand Forks and Thief River Falls campuses with an 'N' symbolizing Northland.
- Logo angles are consistently constructed based on the middle angle of the 'N' for representing progress, innovation, and future orientation.
- Clear space around the monogram must be 1/4 of its height. The minimum size for printing is 0.25 inches wide or 40px for digital displays.
- Incorrect monogram usage: no color alterations (only Horizon Red, Shuttle White, or Space Blue are allowed), no 3D effects, stretching, distortion, rotation, outlines, or added illustrations are permitted.
- Clear space around wordmarks is defined as 1/4 of the height of the first letter. Minimum sizes: 0.125 inches wide (12px for digital displays) for smaller text and 0.25 inches wide (24px for larger).
- Incorrect wordmark usage: No color alterations (only Horizon Red, Shuttle White, or Space Blue are allowed). Avoid 3D effects, stretching, distortion, rotation, or adding embossing/glowing effects.
- Spirit marks are designed for apparel and marketing materials and are not the primary logo mark. Key restrictions: No color alterations, 3D effects, distortion, illustrations, embossing, glow effects, or improper photo placement.
- The program is monogram-led (primary identifier) with secondary marks (e.g., spirit or P-logo) supporting the identity system.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: Space Blue (Pantone 655 C #01203F), Horizon Red (Pantone 2347 C #FF0000), and Flight Blue (Pantone 2728 C #0344DC).
- Secondary colors include Shuttle White (Pantone Cool Gray 4 C #FFFFFF), Frontier Silver, and International Orange (Pantone Orange 021 C #FF4F00). CMYK, RGB, and HEX codes are provided for all.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Ginto Normal Regular and Bold for body copy. Accent typeface: Ginto Nord Bold and Regular for headlines and personality elements.
- Custom sports-specific typeface used for jersey numerals to ensure consistency.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Graphic motif: Northland triangle derived from the 'N' in the wordmark. Represents progress and serves as a brand compass pointing north.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space guidelines are defined by 1/4 height of logos. Minimum digital and print sizes are also provided for monogram, wordmarks, and spirit marks.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice is professional yet personal, using hashtags like #WeAreGo to emphasize the team spirit and forward-thinking approach.
- Guidelines for social media: Use conversational language while maintaining professionalism. Tailor the voice to either department-wide or specific sports accounts.

**Notes:**

- The brand manual emphasizes strict licensing rules. Logos are trademarks owned by Northland Community & Technical College.

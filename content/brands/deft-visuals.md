## Brand Guidelines

### 2022

**Logo Information:**

- The anatomy of the logo includes the Profile Head with negative space to portray the brand's meaning, the Nose/Arrow to show movement and fixed vision, and 'D' as the brand initial.
- The exclusion zone requires an area of clear space around the logo, calculated as 'x divided by three' (or one third x), where x is the height of the whole logo.
- The minimum display size of the logo ensures it remains legible and specifies sizes. The recommended minimum reproduction size is 30mm x 30mm.
- Logo usage rules specify do's and don'ts: no change to logo colors, distortion, gradient effects, contrast tampering, rotation, or drop shadow.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color codes include PANTONE 276C, HEX #5F249F, RGB (95, 36, 159), CMYK not specified.
- Secondary color codes include PANTONE 647C, HEX #7D9EC0, RGB (125, 156, 192).
- Deft Visuals Black is defined as Pantone Black 6 with HEX #101.

**Typography Information:**

- Font family is 'Satoshi', demonstrated across different weights.

**Brand Voice:**

- Voice and tone include being confident, conversational but appropriate, friendly, concise, clear, astute, and intelligent.

**Notes:**

- Tagline: 'Eye on Target'.
- Brand manifesto emphasizes skill and visual impact as essential for differentiation.
- Positioning statement: Focuses on helping businesses enhance their visual communication and advertising strategies.

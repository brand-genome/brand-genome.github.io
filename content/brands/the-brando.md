## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- Primary logo features a seahorse icon and wordmark. The logo is to be used as a subtle and premium branding device, not larger than necessary, and not smaller than 1 inch in width.
- Logo versions shown include vertical logo, horizontal logo, and locator tagline. Minimum usage widths: 1 inch (vertical), 1.3 inches (horizontal), and 1.5 inches (locator tagline).
- Reverse logos (full color and single color) are supported. Reversed white letters or logo can be used on medium/dark-colored backgrounds or brand colors.
- File format references: EPS, Pantone for colors.
- Safe area: Minimum space equal to the height of 'T' in the wordmark must be maintained around the primary logo or horizontal logo. Vertical logo requires space equal to the width of the 'H' in the wordmark.
- The minimum sizes for logos are specified: primary logo (1.25 inches), horizontal logo (1.3 inches), and vertical logo (1.0 inch).
- Incorrect logo usage guidelines: Do not stretch/compress, change elements, flip the icon, screen the logo, place it over busy backgrounds, or use the seahorse icon without the wordmark.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: Lagoon Blue (Pantone 632C, HEX: #0093B2, CMYK: 93, 2, 15, 7, RGB: 0, 147, 178) and Earth (Pantone 497C, CMYK: 30, 73, 74, 78, RGB: 81, 47, 46).
- Secondary colors: Lagoon Blue, Sea Green (Pantone 7473, CMYK: 75, 5, 48, 3, RGB: 39, 153, 137), Sea Blue (Pantone 647, CMYK: 96, 54, 5, 27, RGB: 35, 97, 146), Lush Green (Pantone 576, CMYK: 54, 5, 94, 24, RGB: 120, 157, 74), Warm Sand (Pantone 467, CMYK: 6, 15, 41, 10, RGB: 211, 188, 141), Exotic Red (Pantone 1675, CMYK: 5, 83, 100, 27, RGB: 169, 67, 30), and Tropical Orange (Pantone 165, CMYK: 0, 70, 100, 0, RGB: 255, 103, 31).

**Typography Information:**

- Display font: Democratica Serif (used for headlines, bold characters with elegant curves and geometric angles).
- Serif font: Mrs. Eaves XL Serif (used for body copy, subheads, captions, and occasionally headlines). Features a higher x-height for optimal readability.
- Sans-serif font: Mr. Eaves XL Sans-serif (used for body copy and other instances, companion to Mrs. Eaves XL). Designed for superior readability at smaller point sizes.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should evoke senses with detail and texture, emphasizing the unique nature of The Brando experience. Macro images of nature and luxury are encouraged.
- Images of people and culture should feel respectful and not overly posed. The camera should not interfere with the natural occurrences in the scene.
- Interior shots should focus on highlighting luxury elements and textures, avoiding wide perspectives.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Protective space for logos must be based on the letter height (T or H) of the wordmark. Minimum logo sizes and spacing guidelines provided.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone is lush, sensual, and related to the South Pacific. The brand avoids restating or describing photographs and emphasizes inviting the audience to imagine experiences supported by descriptive language.
- Avoid overused terms like 'sustainable resort,' instead referencing 'natural sanctuary.' Emphasize cultural and natural legacy using Polynesian terms with explanations.
- Language is respectful, responsible, exclusive, and educational. Always maintain respect for heritage, guests, and the environment.
- Always clarify where the resort is geographically, incorporating 'Tetiaroa Private Island – French Polynesia' in marketing materials.

**Notes:**

- This brand guideline outlines clear rules for usage of logos, typography, and colors to maintain strong brand identity rooted in Polynesian culture.
- Eco-station and emphasis on sustainability highlight brand commitment to environmental preservation alongside luxury.

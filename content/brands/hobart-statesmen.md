## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Primary and Secondary Logo usage rules: do not change proportions, skew, change colors, invade clear space, add effects or filters; misusage applies to all iterations of the logos.
- Clear space around icons: should be 20% of the mark's original width. Applies to all logo iterations.
- Minimum size requirement for embroidery: Icons should not be reduced to smaller than 1.75 inches high. For digital/print: not smaller than 50 pts high.
- Primary Icons presented in Full Color, Single Color, and Black & White on both white and black backgrounds.
- Secondary Icons presented similarly in Full Color, Single Color, and Black & White on white and black backgrounds.
- Wordmark guidelines: Includes Full Color, Black & White on white and black backgrounds.
- Sports-specific marks: Full Color, Orange, Purple, Black and White on white and black backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Primary branding color palette includes Pantone 2607 C (purple) and Pantone 165 C (orange). Presented with HEX (472663, ff6418), RGB (79,16,122 / 255,100,24), and CMYK (80, 100, 0, 10 / 0, 75, 99, 0). Color guidelines emphasize consistent ratios for brand representation.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family: TT Polls (Bold and Regular). TT Polls Bold is used for prominent communications to convey strength and excitement. Font cannot be italicized or skewed. Font is reserved for design applications, not Microsoft Office.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rules for logos: 20% of the mark's original width.
- Minimum logo size: 1.75 inches (embroidery), 50 pts (digital).

**Notes:**

- Extensive rules apply to logo misusage across all contexts and iterations of the branding elements.
- Detailed apparel guidance exists for different sports applications, including specific placements and sizes of logos for uniforms like jerseys, shorts, and helmets.
- Field and court application guidelines specify logo placement aligning with TV angles.

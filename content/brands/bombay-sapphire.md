## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Primary logo includes a sapphire icon and 'Stir Creativity' as a signature below the wordmark.
- Primary Lockup versions include full-color (light backgrounds), reversed (dark backgrounds), and all white (mid-tone backgrounds).
- Stir Creativity Signature versions are available in horizontal and stacked forms with three color options (mid blue, navy, and white). Minimum sizes: horizontal (72px / 25.5mm wide), stacked (52px / 18.2mm wide).
- Secondary Lockup features Queen Victoria in the centerpiece, available in full color and reversed versions.
- Clear space rules specify a minimum width of Screen 20px / Print 7mm for logos.
- Simplified monochrome versions of the Primary Lockup and Sapphire Icon are provided for foiling, embossing, and small-sized printing.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Light Blue (Pantone 635, CMYK 30/0/5/0, RGB 181/244/253, HEX #B5F4FD), White (RGB 255/255/255, HEX #FFFFFF), Mid Blue (Pantone 2995, CMYK 90/0/0/0, RGB 0/195/242, HEX #00C3F2), Navy (Pantone 282, CMYK 100/80/10/70, RGB 0/0/70, HEX #000046), and Copper Metallic (Pantone 8022, foil color 6731).
- Copper should only ever be reproduced in foil or metallic Pantone in print and never printed in CMYK. Digital usage follows a bespoke gradient made of three gradient colors, applied at a 38° angle.
- Color usage prioritizes Light Blue (core brand color), with supportive Mid Blue. Navy and White are accent colors for text, logos, and other details, while Copper is used as a print foil or in physical objects for premium finishes.

**Typography Information:**

- Bombay Display is used for headlines, and Sapphire Sans for subheadings and supporting copy. Body text is set in Messina Serif Book.
- Typographic hierarchy specifies Bombay Display at 4x body copy size, Sapphire Sans at 1.5x body copy size, and Messina Serif Book for body copy. Detailed spacing for font sizes and leading is provided.
- Text dividers are used to highlight or separate key messages and content, available in various fixed widths and colors from the primary palette.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Images should feel bright, colorful, and have a subtle blue tone. Selective editing steps should be applied to give images a brand-appropriate look (adjusting levels, adding cyan tones, and brushing extra blue).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space around logos and elements is defined, with specific minimum sizes for screen and print (e.g., 20px/7mm for primary logo).
- Brand Pattern assets include guidelines for positioning (centered/asymmetric) and scaling (smallest: 10 columns wide; largest: 4 columns wide).

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone of voice is crucial for brand perception. It speaks to different audiences for different purposes but must consistently reflect the brand essence and values.

**Notes:**

- Physical finishes and packaging should emphasize contrast and combinations of matte and reflective surfaces. Materials like metallic accents (copper, aluminium) and textures (glass, wood, paper) are suggested.

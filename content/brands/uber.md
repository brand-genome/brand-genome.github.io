### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Logo should be white on darker backgrounds and black on lighter backgrounds.
- Clear space around the logo is equal to the cap height of the 'U' in the logo.
- Spacing and alignment guidance is provided for aligning product names with the logo.
- Aligning partnership logos should follow clear space rules. The separating line between logos can be created using the vertical line glyph from Uber Move Display Light at the same size as the logo.
- Horizontal and vertical logo lockups for product and partnership use are shown as examples.
- Logo assets are available for download. URL provided in the document.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors are black and white, aimed at maintaining accessibility, simplicity, and consistency.
- Safety Blue is a unique Uber color used sparingly for key supportive moments between the user and the brand.
- Primary brand colors - White: RGB 255, 255, 255; CMYK 0, 0, 0, 0; HEX #FFFFFF; PMS White. Black: RGB 0, 0, 0; CMYK 70, 35, 40, 100; HEX #000000; PMS Black 6 C.
- Safety Blue specifications: RGB 39, 110, 241; CMYK 84, 54, 0, 0; HEX #276EF1; PMS 2174 C.
- Secondary colors consist of transportation-inspired tones. Usage is limited to illustrations and product contexts. Colors include: Uber Green (RGB 71, 179, 117, HEX #47B275), Uber Yellow (RGB 255, 192, 67, HEX #FFC043), Uber Red (RGB 230, 76, 53, HEX #F25138), Uber Brown (RGB 153, 100, 77, HEX #99644C), Uber Orange (RGB 255, 125, 73, HEX #FF7D49), Uber Purple (RGB 115, 86, 191, HEX #7356BF). Full CMYK and Pantone (PMS) specifications provided.
- Usage proportions emphasize maintaining brand balance: white should provide balance with black, while Safety Blue is reserved for critical moments.
- Color assets are available for download. URL provided in the document.

**Typography Information:**

- Uber Move is the key typeface, designed for consistency, clarity, and brand equity. Composed of two styles: Uber Move Display (for headlines and subheadlines) and Uber Move Text (for body text).
- Typography weights: Light, Regular, Medium, Bold. Pairings: Display Medium with Text Light or Display Bold with Text Regular.
- Hierarchy examples provided: headlines use Uber Move Display Medium, subheads use Uber Move Display Light, buttons use Uber Move Display Bold. Recommended sizes and line spacing included.
- Typography assets are available for download. URL provided in the document.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The clear space around the logo must maintain a distance equal to the cap height of the 'U'.
- Spacing rules for product lockups and partnership lockups adhere to defined logo alignment and proportional guidelines provided in the lockup sections.

**Notes:**

- All partner-led creatives must be pre-approved by the Uber brand team.
- Download links for assets (logo, color, typography) are provided on page 9.

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The logotype is always horizontal and should never be rotated in any direction. Letters must never stand alone, and spacing between characters must not be altered.
- The logotype is only available in black or white and must always contrast with the background. It is available in four color modes: HEX, RGB, CMYK, and Pantone. No gradients, artificial textures, drop shadows, or three-dimensional effects are allowed.
- Clear space is proportional to the height of the logotype, and no elements should be placed inside the defined clear space.
- Minimum size requirements: Digital - 12px height; Print - 0.2 inches/5mm height.
- Logo Bit combines the Uber logotype and the Bit, representing technology and Uber's logical side. The Logo Bit spacing must not be altered.
- Logotype sizes (digital and print) vary depending on canvas dimensions. Heights start as small as 12px and scale upwards. Canvas guidelines cover sizes up to 1960px or 4x6 inches and larger.
- Improper logotype usage examples include placement on items such as rugs, trashable items, consumables, inappropriate contexts (like sexual intentions), or harmful locations (urinals, dartboards).

**Color Information:**

- Black color specifications: HEX #000000, RGB 0/0/0, CMYK 60/40/40/100, Pantone Black 6U.
- White color specifications: HEX #FFFFFF, RGB 255/255/255.
- Grey 1 specifications: HEX #F8F8F9, RGB 248/248/249, CMYK 7/6/3/1.
- Grey 2 specifications: HEX #F1F1F1, RGB 241/241/241, CMYK 9/8/6/3.
- Grey 4 specifications: HEX #D6D6D5, RGB 214/214/213, CMYK 17/14/13/4.
- Color usage rules: The black logotype is used on light backgrounds, and the white logotype is used on dark backgrounds. Photography background usage must consider sufficient legibility, allowing black or white Logo Bits.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space around the logotype is proportional to its height and should allow a minimum specified space, ensuring no elements are placed within the space.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand elements should not be used in a degrading or inappropriate context. Examples provided include avoiding placement on sexual items, food, trash, or harmful surfaces.

**Notes:**

- Version 1.0 of the Uber Logotype Guidelines, dated 01.31.16.
- The Uber logotype is the cornerstone of brand identity, aiming for simplicity, quality, elegance, and standalone recognition.

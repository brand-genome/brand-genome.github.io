## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Full colour and monochrome versions of the BJSS logo are available. The full colour logo is used on white backgrounds, while the white monochrome logo is applied on non-white backgrounds or images. The logo must not be modified in any way, including altering form or color.
- A single-colour variant of the BJSS logo exists and is used only in applications where multiple colors are not feasible.
- Clear space rules: At least the height of the 'S' character in the logo should be maintained as clear space around the logo. Clear space should ideally be larger than this minimum.
- Preferred logo placements are either in the top left or bottom right corners. Centering is permitted only if the logo is the sole element on the page.
- Minimum sizes: For print, 12 mm high. For digital use, the minimum height is 38 pixels.
- Guidelines for placement with other logos: Minimum spacing equal to the height of 'S' in the BJSS logo. Ideally, 2-4 times the height of the 'S'. Logos should share roughly equivalent surface area.

**Color Information:**

- Pink: Pantone C:22 M:90 K:22, HEX #d90699, RGB: R217, G6, B153.
- Green: Pantone C:72, M:0, K:0, HEX #31b595, RGB: R49, G181, B149.
- Blue: Pantone C:90, M:68, K:0, HEX #0047bb, RGB: R0, G71, B187.
- BJSS Navy: Pantone C:100 M:90 K:68, HEX #041e42, RGB: R4, G30, B66.
- Rules for color usage: Navy and white are primary colors. Other colors are supportive and should be used conservatively to create hints of color. When used as overlays on images, set opacity to 65%.
- Gradient details: Each gradient includes two shades of the same color (left to right; pink, blue, green, teal, or purple). Equal distribution should be maintained for these shades. When used as an overlay, opacity must be set to 65%.

**Typography Information:**

- Font Family: Catamaran (Regular, Bold, Black).
- Body text should use Catamaran Regular. Subheadings, keywords, call-to-actions, and URLs should use Catamaran Bold. Catamaran Black is exclusive to headers and large numbers, and must be in uppercase.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography styles include Abstract, 'BJSS at Work', and 'BJSS in the Community (CSR)'. Abstract images should align with BJSS's color palette and avoid tones like red or orange.
- 'BJSS at Work' photography is for case study headers, testimonials, and people-focused materials. Gradient overlays are used (e.g., Blue for Financial Services, Green for Energy).
- 'BJSS in the Community' photography is for CSR-related banners and materials. Pink gradient overlay should be set at 65% opacity.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing between logos must be at least the height of the 'S' in the BJSS logo and should ideally be larger (2-4 times the logo height). Logos should be vertically aligned.

**Notes:**

- Icons are created with clean, modern one-line designs using Adobe Illustrator. Icons are either in full color or white. Specific gradients (Pink, Blue) are used in construction.

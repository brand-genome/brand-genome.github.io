## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The New Zealand Fern logo is central to MFAT's visual identity. Guidelines align with the New Zealand Masterbrand Guidelines and Specifications.
- Incorrect logo usage examples are provided, specifying that the fern logo must not be used without its supporting type or recreated in any way.

**Typography Information:**

- Open Sans font aligned for MFAT websites including Safe Travel and NZUNSC. Acceptable typesetting uses and roles not fully detailed.
- Minion Pro usage for all MFAT materials. Available weights include Light, Book, Regular, Medium, Bold, Black.

**Imagery Photography:**

- MFAT uses photography aligned with New Zealand's unique identity and contributions globally. Preference for large images consistent with the NZ Story approach. Guidelines restrict culturally/politically sensitive or stigmatizing images, with credits required.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Preferred centered 12-column grid for communications; flexible for text-heavy publications. Headers align to columns, offering 1-3 column text flow options and one-column captions.

**Notes:**

- Templates showcased include pull-up banners, business cards, glass decals, invites, and email signatures.
- Additional invite designs feature variants emphasizing NZ Fern, crest colors, and patterns. Includes RSVP and layout guidelines.

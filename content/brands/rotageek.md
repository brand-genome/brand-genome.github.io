## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Logo construction: Mark should be aligned using a blue rectangle around it. Clearspace defined by the size of X (half the size of the logo mark). Minimum sizes specified: Minimum mark width is 24px (digital), 14mm (print), and minimum logo width is 90px (digital), 24mm (print).
- Correct logo usage: Default use with white background is preferred. Acceptable alternatives include Deep Indigo background or black/white logos (not gray).
- Unacceptable logo usage: Do not use unapproved colors, add drop shadows or strokes. Do not align logo incorrectly or frame the logo. Avoid using default logo on non-white backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Ruby Red: HEX: #FF0046, RGB: (255, 0, 70), CMYK: (0%, 99%, 67%, 0%). Used mainly for logo and title texts that need to stand out. Should not be used for errors or backgrounds (exceptions apply).
- Web Blue: HEX: #0441C6, RGB: (4, 65, 198), CMYK: (91%, 78%, 0%, 0%). Used for interactive elements like links, buttons, or selected UI states. Should not be used for regular text.
- Deep Indigo: HEX: #211656, RGB: (33, 22, 86), CMYK: (74%, 33%, 32%, 8%). Used for text headlines or dark, high-contrast backgrounds. Avoid using for regular content text.
- Baby Powder: HEX: #E5F6FD, RGB: (229, 246, 253), CMYK: (9%, 0%, 0%, 0%). Mainly used as background color or text on a Deep Indigo background. Avoid using it for text on a white background.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Brandon Grotesque. For all text not used on the web-app. Used in documents, presentations, and marketing materials.
- Secondary typeface: Camphor Pro. Exclusive to web-app use. Should not be used for non-web-app related copy.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style: Light, energetic, warm, natural, and unstaged. Focus on great depth-of-field photography. Use fully saturated images in full color. Avoid overly corporate-looking images.
- Image treatment: Use Deep Indigo background at 90% opacity for text overlay. Always use Baby Powder text on Deep Indigo, never white. Ensure text overlay does not cover the focus area of images.
- Unacceptable photography: Do not use black-and-white, partially colored, faded or washed-out images, or stock photos.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clearspace and alignment: X is half the size of the logo mark and defines clear space. Alignment is based on a blue rectangle enclosing the mark.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone descriptors: Friendly, personal, helpful, honest, and uncomplicated.
- Messaging rules: Pay attention to detail, avoid long sentences, rambling language, and unnecessary complexity. Use neutral, non-gendered, and concise language. Prefers informal over formal tone.

**Notes:**

- Brand Description: Rotageek focuses on technology-driven solutions for intelligent scheduling. Highlights innovation, questioning the usual methods, and rethinking from scratch.
- Cultural Notes: Descriptors include curious, caring, personal, transparent, honest, approachable, playful, energetic, and exemplar.

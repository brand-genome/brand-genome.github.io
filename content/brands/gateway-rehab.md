## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- LOGO Primary Use: The primary logos, either horizontal or vertical, should be the first option when placed over a light, solid color or a simple background. The horizontal version should always be used as the primary logo. Reverse Logo: Logo version for dark backgrounds. Safe Zone: Clear space should equal the height of the 'G'.
- LOGO Misuse: Examples of incorrect usage include changing colors or font, repositioning elements, adding effects, or placing on complicated backgrounds. Also, improperly condensing/stretching logos.
- LOGO Descriptor: The Gateway logo should always appear with the tagline 'Addiction Recovery & Self Renewal' unless design space is constrained. Neil Kennedy Recovery Centers additionally include 'A Service of Gateway Rehab.'
- LOGO Color Use: The logo uses black and tonal variations of 'Gateway Green.' 'Gateway' is in black, and 'Rehab' is filled with 100% Gateway Green.
- LOGO File Formats and Assistance: Contact the Marketing Department for assistance in obtaining appropriate logo formats.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Colors: Gateway Green (CMYK: 65/0/100/42, RGB: 56/124/44, Pantone: 7741C) and Vibrant Green (CMYK: 50/0/100/10, RGB: 127/180/57, Pantone: 7737C). Gateway Green is designated for the logo; Vibrant Green is for communications.
- Secondary Colors Palette includes various colors like Pantone 547C, 7676C, 7569C, etc. Each represents different moods such as peace, vitality, life renewed, harmony, and balance.
- Colors represent positive change and reflect personal aspirations. Individual shades are used to convey themes of serenity, energy, passion, happiness, and a new beginning.
- LOGO Colors: The Gateway logo uses black and Gateway Green (comprising tonal percentages: 100% down to 50%). Gateway wordmark features black ('Gateway') and Gateway Green ('Rehab').

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Font: Avenir (weights: Light, Book, Roman, Medium, Heavy, and Black). Includes oblique and italic options. Roles: Establish clarity, legibility, and structure.
- Complementary Font: Roboto Slab (weights: Light, Regular, Bold) for multiple purposes. Proxima Nova or Arial can be used for web or PowerPoint.
- Typography conveys the brand's voice. Avenir was chosen for modern and universal functionality, ensuring visual hierarchy.

**Imagery Photography:**

- STYLE: Authentic, natural, and colorful photography is key. Images must reflect positivity and recovery achievement. Photos should convey the subject’s passions (e.g., music, children, health).
- GUIDELINES: Avoid overly posed or contrived stock images. Contact the Marketing Department to access an approved image library.
- Photographic Emphasis: Intimate photos that portray emotional rewards of recovery. Rich, vibrant settings. Ensure image subjects align with the ‘self-renewal’ brand theme.

**Brand Voice:**

- Messaging Standards: Always refer to the organization as 'Gateway Rehab.' Avoid abbreviations or using 'Gateway Rehabilitation.' Refer to the program as a 'Twelve Steps' program.
- Theme Line: 'Bring yourself to life.SM' Ensure proper usage of capitalization, punctuation, and trademarks in formal writing.
- General Messaging: Use language that embodies values of recovery, positivity, and self-renewal in all internal/external communications.

**Notes:**

- Contacts for Resources: Scott Shaw (Marketing Dept.) for queries on brand standards, materials, or design elements. Contact: scott.shaw@gatewayrehab.org (Ext: 1107).
- Avoid stock images that do not reflect the natural, authentic brand values. Use approved photography only (contact the Marketing Department).

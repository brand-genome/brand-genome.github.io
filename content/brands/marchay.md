## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- The logo can be displayed horizontally and vertically. Vertically, the logo must always be left-aligned with the 'M' at the bottom. Minimum size is specified as 4mm.
- Logotype and monogram can work together or independently. Minimum space around the monogram equals the height of the 'M'. Spacing between the logo and monogram equals the height of the logo.
- Usage of favicon and avatar is referenced under logo guidelines.

**Color Information:**

- Screen color palette includes: Dark blue (#0D2756, RGB: 0 36 93), Deep blue (#114B6C, RGB: 17 75 108), Blue (#186B9B, RGB: 24 107 155), Bright blue (#449BDC, RGB: 68 155 220), Orange (#FF744F, RGB: 255 116 79), Black (#262626), Dark grey (#666666), Grey (#999999), Light grey (#E6E6E6), Softer grey (#F8F8F8).
- Print color palette mentioned on page 9. Color specifics referenced include brand's dark blue and beige for visual elements.

**Typography Information:**

- Print typography includes the following details: Headlines use History 02 Regular font in all caps (recommended for headlines and titles only). Leading specified as 50pt. Body text uses Fakt Pro Blond as a secondary option, chosen based on the overall design.
- Screen typography guidelines specify font sizing and spacing. H1: 43px/60px, letter-spacing: 5px; H2: 30px/40px, letter-spacing: 4px; H3: 16px/22px, letter-spacing: 2px. Body text uses Fakt Pro Blond at 16px/18px size.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography must evoke a vintage travel feel using vintage airline pictures and monochrome treatment with the brand's dark blue as specified in the print palette.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing around monogram equals the height of the 'M' part of the logo. Logotype and monogram have specific spacing rules when paired.

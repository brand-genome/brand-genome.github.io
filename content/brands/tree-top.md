## Brand Guidelines

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- The Tree Top Signature is a traditional but relevant logo consisting of a friendly, bold, serif, italicized font with a ribbon tied to growers and heritage. Instructions to never stretch, skew, distort, or attempt to recreate the Signature are provided.
- Clear Zone for logo integrity is defined by the height of the 'P' in 'Top'. No elements should interfere within this area.
- Signature background and contrast rules are provided: alternate versions for different backgrounds. Appropriateness of contrast must allow for legibility.
- Minimum size requirements for logo usage provided for print, digital, and embroidered media; reproducing the logo below these sizes can jeopardize legibility.
- Do not squash, stretch, rotate, rearrange, re-proportion, recolor, resample, stylize, or alter the logo design.

**Color Information:**

- Tree Top's primary brand colors are Red (Pantone 186) and Green (Pantone 362). CMYK values: Red - C: 0, M: 100, Y: 81, K: 4; Green - C: 70, M: 0, Y: 100, K: 24.
- Secondary colors represent a broader variety of fruit tones, including Peach, Blueberry, Orange, Grapefruit, Pear, Lemon, Kiwi, Plum, Strawberry, Raspberry, Green Apple, and Cherry.
- Neutral and natural colors can be used for supporting text and elements.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface is the Lexia Family for headlines and prominent short-text applications, conveying strength and friendliness; Myriad Pro is the secondary font used for sub-headlines and large volumes of text.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery must evoke 'wholesome' and 'natural' sentiments, showing real fruit and real applications of Tree Top products. Overlays for de-saturation and lightening of images are suggested for traditional and heritage-feel aesthetics.
- Stock photography should be avoided; custom, high-resolution photography informed by the brand psyche is preferred.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Tree Top's layouts are clean, simple, and modern to achieve a balance between legacy and contemporary relevance.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand tone focuses on trustworthiness, traditional values tied to the land, and provider attributes driven by a caring and friendly nature.

**Notes:**

- Avoid using non-standard logos or deviating from specified colors, typography, imagery rules, and layout guidelines.

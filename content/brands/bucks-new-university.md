## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Preferred logo version shown alongside permissible alternatives for marketing, signage, and decoration. The roundel may be used separately or without the logotype with permission from the Brand and Design Manager.
- Exclusion zone specified as the height of the 's' in 'bucks.' Minimum size for print is 40mm width; for online use, no less than 145 pixels wide.
- Incorrect usage examples, such as using the color logo on dark backgrounds, reversing out of light colors or complex images, retyping text in other fonts, adjusting element distances, altering color values, or reordering colors within the roundel.
- Explicit instructions not to use the logo on improper backgrounds, or modify existing design elements.

**Color Information:**

- Primary logo colors: Pantone 485C (C14 M91 Y81 K3, R255 G0 B0, Hex #CC333), Process Cyan (C100 M0 Y0 K0, R0 G153 B255, Hex #0099FF), Process 268C (C80 M100 Y0 K10, R102 G0 B102, Hex #663399), Cool Grey 10C (C0 M0 Y0 K70, R102 G102 B102, Hex #333333), Cool Grey 8C (C0 M0 Y0 K50, R153 G153 B153, Hex #999999).
- Additional campaign-specific color palette includes Pantone 709c (C0 M78 Y36 K0, R241 G96 B120, Hex #f16078), Pantone 2905c (C45 M4 Y4 K0, R132 G203 B231, Hex #84cbe7), Pantone 7669c (C67 M73 Y4 K0, R109 G92 B162, Hex #6d5ca2), Pantone 360c (C61 M0 Y100 K0, R111 G190 B68, Hex #6fbe44), Pantone 5265c (C80 M73 Y40 K27, R64 G68 B96, Hex #404460), Warm Grey 3c (C22 M19 Y23 K8, R185 G179 B171).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary fonts: Bebas Neue for headings, Abril FatFace as an alternative for headings, Museo Slab 700 for subheadings, Museo Sans 300 for body text, Helvetica Neue Regular as an alternative for body text.
- Corporate usage: Use Arial for corporate documents, including letters, reports, minutes, presentations, and general correspondence.
- Additional decorative fonts include Thirsty Soft Regular and Thirsty Soft Medium, to be used sparingly.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography guidelines include using hero photos that represent the university, avoiding stock shots and cliches, using reportage style and natural shots, and showcasing normal students/people.
- Images should align with current social media trends and maintain a coherent visual style.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Exclusion zones for logos are determined by the height of 's' in 'bucks,' ensuring uncluttered visual layout.

**Brand Voice:**

- 'Build a Brighter Future' campaign slogan focuses on excitement, confidence, and creativeness tied to student recruitment.

**Notes:**

- Contact the Brand and Design Manager for queries related to guidelines and permissions, especially for logo and color usage.

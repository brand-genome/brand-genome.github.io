## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The institutional logo of the Ayuntamiento de Almería includes a redesigned and simplified version of the original coat of arms. Key compositional elements from the original are retained, such as the Cross of San Jorge and additional elements like the Sea and ‘almenas’ (battlements) to symbolize Almería’s historical heritage such as the Alhambra's Puerta de Justicia.
- The logo is designed to be used in primary duotones: yellow (Pantone 123 C / #FFC627) in the upper block and red (Pantone 485 C / #D52B1E) in the frame containing the Cross of San Jorge. Monochrome red, black, and white versions (positive and negative) are also approved. The reproduction should always be in official digital formats such as JPG, PNG, PDF, or vector files.
- Rules for positive and negative logo usage: Positive colors should be used on backgrounds with over 40% brightness; negative colors should be used for less than 40% brightness.
- The logo's minimum size for representation is 5mm width (14 pixels width) to ensure visibility and brand consistency. The minimum size may be reduced to 3mm (8.5 pixels width) for certain applications like merchandising.
- Clear space / safe zone: An area of respect should exist between the logo and other graphic elements in a composition. The area of respect should follow the spacing illustrated in the document.
- Usage rules: The bicolor version is to be used primarily, while the monochrome red version is to be used on white backgrounds. On colored or image backgrounds, the logo is to be placed within a white rectangular space, which must be at least as large as the clear space.
- Do's & don'ts: The logo must not be redrawn, and should only be reproduced using provided digital source files (JPG, PNG, PDF, vector). Incorrect usage of the logo includes modifying its design or distorting its proportions.

**Color Information:**

- Main Colors for the logo: Red (Pantone 485 C, HEX #D52B1E, CMYK 0/100/100/0, RGB 213/43/30) and Yellow (Pantone 123 C, HEX #FFC627, CMYK 0/23/91/0, RGB 255/198/39). These colors are used for the duotone version of the logo.
- Color usage standards: Monochrome red, black, and white color schemes are allowed as alternatives, with either positive or negative versions depending on the background brightness.

**Typography Information:**

- Establishment of a typography aligned with the new corporate identity and mandatory use of this typeface across official communication.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo has defined minimum size requirements: 5mm in width (14 pixels width) for general use. For specialized applications (e.g., merchandising), the minimum size can be adjusted to 3mm in width (8.5 pixels).
- Logo clear space (area of respect): Defined as the minimum space around the logo that must remain clear of other graphic elements, as standardized in the document's illustrations.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand voice seeks to reflect a modern, contemporary, and open city that is welcoming and culturally rich while respecting Almería's historical legacy.
- The new brand identity is designed to emphasize simplicity, adaptability across media, coherence, respect for diversity, and accessibility.

**Notes:**

- The coat of arms used in the logo dates back to 1147 and has had multiple revisions. Key symbolic elements such as the Cross of San Jorge, the Sea, and the battlements of the Puerta de Justicia are central to the updated design, underscoring the cultural and historical significance of Almería.
- The manual includes digital assets (JPG, PNG, PDF, vector formats) for official logo reproduction to maintain brand consistency.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2010

**Logo Information:**

- Two versions of the ENERGY STAR Partnership Mark: one with the symbol block and a messaging block separated by a white rule equal in thickness to the arc of the symbol. Each mark has a white keyline also equal in thickness to the arc. Clear space rule: 1/3 the height of the graphic box.
- Minimum size specifications: 0.375 inches (3/8") for print and legibility of lettering must be maintained for web.
- Preferred color for Partnership Mark: ENERGY STAR Blue (100% Cyan). Alternative versions in black or reversed white are allowable. Web equivalent for ENERGY STAR Blue is hex color #0099FF.
- Incorrect logo usage includes: changing colors, distorting, rotating, altering elements, using text adjacent, violating clear space rules, resizing improperly.

**Color Information:**

- ENERGY STAR Blue, listed as 100% Cyan in CMYK and Web equivalent as hex color #0099FF. Alternate versions in black or reversed white are allowed.

**Typography Information:**

- ENERGY STAR symbol includes specific typography within the messaging block. Alterations to the typeface and spacing are prohibited.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery for specific ENERGY STAR graphics (Seal and Insulate, Insulation Products Graphic, Home Performance) must show the mark in marketing materials, but not directly on products. Graphics must maintain their proportions and appropriate clear space.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rule: At least 1/3 the height of the graphic box must remain free of other graphical or textual elements.
- Minimum size for print: 0.375 inches (3/8") wide. Lettering legibility must be maintained for web usages.

**Brand Voice:**

- ENERGY STAR communication should emphasize energy efficiency, environmental benefit, and partnership. Incorrect terms include suggesting EPA/DOE endorsement, 'approved' or 'rated' products, and implying government funding.
- ENERGY STAR name or mark cannot be used in internet domain names or inferences without explicit authorization from EPA/DOE.

**Notes:**

- Various application-specific graphics rules are detailed for Seal and Insulate, Insulation Products Graphic, and Home Performance with ENERGY STAR Marketing Graphics.

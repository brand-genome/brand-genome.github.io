## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- LOGO FILE FORMATS: Available formats include EPS for professional design programs and CMYK layouts, and PNG for use in RGB-based applications. Variants listed include: ambev logo 1cs.eps (Pantone), ambev logo 4cp.eps (CMYK), ambev logo rgb.png (RGB).
- Additional logo file variants are provided in black and white options: ambev logo black.eps, ambev logo black.png, ambev logo white.eps, and ambev logo white.png.
- LOGO USAGE: Regional or departmental logos should position the text related to the region, department, or CDD below the Ambev logo, always aligned to the left. A horizontal line should appear above the text for the department/region name.
- LOGO USAGE: Recruitment program logos should have the program name positioned above the Ambev logo, aligned to the left, with a horizontal line below it. Additional icons can be used for graphical representation.

**Color Information:**

- Color codes for logos are specified, including Pantone, CMYK, and RGB formats. Relevant files for these variations are named appropriately for each format.

**Typography Information:**

- Example of typography used in stationery: Person's name and title are in 'Din Next Rounded medium,' size 9 pt with 94% line spacing. Contact details are in 'Din Next Rounded light,' size 7.5 pt with 94% line spacing. Company information uses 'Din Next Rounded medium,' size 4.5 pt with 94% line spacing.
- Typography guidelines for letterhead: ‘Din Next Rounded light’ font, size 12 pt with 94% line spacing, used for the organization’s name and address. Arial 11 pt is used for other letter content.

**Imagery Photography:**

- ONLINE EXPERIENCE: Imagery and typography are used to support key messages and important content. Sections are color-coded for navigation purposes.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Letterhead guidelines: The logo is placed in the upper-left corner, with a width of 40 mm and a top margin of 20 mm in accordance with the letter's x-height alignment.
- BUSINESS CARDS: The logo is placed at the top-right corner, aligned with a 4mm margin and width of 25mm. Information is aligned with a 4mm bottom margin.

**Brand Voice:**

- Ambev’s voice emphasizes inspiring people and demonstrating the achievements of its team’s big dreams. Corporate initiatives promote transformative projects aimed at making the world better.

**Notes:**

- All logo, banner, and canvas artwork files are managed by the Corporate Communications team. For questions or additional materials, contact the Corporate Communications team.
- Internal usage only. The manual images are for illustrative purposes and proprietary to third parties—permission is required for reproduction beyond internal use.

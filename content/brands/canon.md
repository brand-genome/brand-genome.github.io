### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Minimum exclusion zone is equal to 10% of the total width of the logo to ensure other elements do not get placed too close.
- Minimum recommended size for the logo is 15mm wide for print applications and 70 pixels wide for online applications.
- The Canon logo has three color variants: Canon Red (default), white (for dark backgrounds or imagery), and black (for light backgrounds or situations where full-color isn't possible).
- Legibility rules dictate the logo must be clearly visible against any background color and apply the clearspace rule for placement.
- Logo must be positioned according to specific sizing rules in extreme formats, taking up 12.5% of the longest dimension for landscape formats and 30% for portrait formats.
- Do not recolor, rotate, distort, outline, or alter the logo in any way that affects its recognition and iconic status.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette for the Canon logo includes Canon Red, alongside secondary and tertiary palettes based on legibility considerations.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Unlicensed images used within the guidelines are for reference only.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Canon Grid uses an eight-column layout with all measurements based around the size of the logo. Margins are 1.14X and gutters are 0.17X (X refers to logo width).

**Notes:**

- Canon logo is sacred and should always be prominently legible and correctly applied in strict adherence to the guidelines.
- Document version is 2.0, dated December 2017.

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- Canon logo should be placed on the right margin of any format. Here are the three recommended positions: bottom right, center right, top right. Exceptions: When the left margin usage is required (e.g., for handheld devices).
- There are five default versions of the white "Come and See" (CAS) device logo. Variations represent Canon Green, Aqua, Blue, Pink, and Yellow combinations with Canon Red accents. Used for exceptional photographic backgrounds to maintain compatibility.
- Specifications for the "Come and See" device logo include minimum exclusion zones (equal to measurement of 'and' circle), minimum print size of 27mm, and online size of 125px.

**Color Information:**

- Canon brand extensively uses primary colors (Canon Red, Grey tones) and differentiated secondary/accent palettes for flexibility (e.g., Canon Yellow, Aqua). All colors must use consistent 100% opacity.
- Background color uses tints from Canon supporting palettes with foreground circles always being in full-opacity.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography uses standard fonts (Century Gothic and Gotham). Gotham weights include Light, Book, Medium, and Bold for consistent roles across headlines, sub-headings, and captions.
- Headlines style above 24pt use Gotham Light/Book while captions adhere to Gotham Book. Legal copy shouldn't fall under 5pt for visibility.

**Imagery Photography:**

- All imagery used within marketing must reflect legal, ethical considerations and reinforce quality values representative of Canon equipment. Content referencing tobacco, nudity, etc., is prohibited.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Visual layout guides extensively reference grid intersections and empty space considerations around brand elements and assets for uniform alignment.

**Brand Voice:**

- Canon tone of voice prioritizes professional clarity while emphasizing brand reliability and innovation.
- Consistent Canon phrasing standards avoid exaggerative or overly emotive expressions but uphold functional aspirational language.

**Notes:**

- Notable exclusions involve misuse of secondary palettes excessively or unauthorized composition alterations beyond prescribed standards.

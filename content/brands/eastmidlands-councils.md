## Brand Guidelines

### 2010

**Logo Information:**

- Primary logo specification is detailed. The logo must always maintain a consistent design relationship between its elements. It must only be reproduced from the provided files.
- Black-and-white and reversed versions of the logo are illustrated for specific use cases.
- For single-color applications, Pantone DS 166-1 must be used. Reverse logos should be set against a background of Pantone DS 166-1 at full tint.
- The exclusion zone around the logo is defined as half the height of 'E'. This area must not be encroached upon. The exclusion zone is required even with partner logos.
- The minimum logo size is defined as 30mm for proper legibility and consistency.
- List of prohibited logo usage: cannot be redrawn or altered, must not use unapproved colors, must not appear cramped or overshadowed, primary logo cannot be used on a colored background or image, and components cannot be separated.
- Details for using the East Midlands Leaders’ Board logo in conjunction with the primary logo. Specific spacing and exclusion rules are applied, maintaining the minimum space equal to the height of the 'E'.

**Color Information:**

- Corporate logo colors include Pantone DS 166-1, or black under certain conditions. Pantone DS 166-1 process is specified as 50% Cyan, 100% Magenta, 0% Yellow, 20% Black.

**Typography Information:**

- Myriad is the primary corporate typeface and is recommended for all literature, signage, and advertising. Weights include: Myriad Roman, Myriad Italic, Myriad Bold, and Myriad Bold Italic.
- Arial is recommended for web, email, and letters. Weights include: Arial Roman, Arial Italic, Arial Bold, and Arial Bold Italic.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should communicate core organizational activities and aspirations. Preferred imagery includes people reflecting emotions, achievements, or evocative scenes. Avoid stock montage photography, low-resolution images, and photos requiring excessive explanation.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Exclusion zones around the logo must maintain defined spacing equal to half the height of 'E'. Even when used with partner logos, the exclusion zone spacing must be doubled.

**Notes:**

- This PDF explicitly summarizes all brand specifications including logos, colors, typography, imagery, and spacing rules. Additional rules for joint logo usage and recycled paper stock guidelines are highlighted.

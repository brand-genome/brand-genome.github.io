## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The Cree logo can appear only in one of three approved color options: Blue (Primary), Black (Secondary), and White (Preferred for advertising/premium collateral).
- Minimum size requirements: The Cree logo must be no less than 25.5mm wide (1 inch) for print and no smaller than 70 pixels wide for on-screen and video usage. For logos larger than 30cm (12 inches), the trademark (™) should be removed.
- Bleed logo lock-ups with left and right bleed versions are available; they follow 1x, 2x, and 3x safety distances from the wordmark and symbol for design flexibility. Crop marks are provided for ensuring proper bleed visibility.
- Logo safe areas require a clear space of at least the height of the logo's lettering around the logo. The Cree logo must not touch or appear to be part of another trademark or graphic element (incorrect usage example provided).

**Color Information:**

- Colors approved for the Cree logo are: Blue - Primary (C:100, M:58, Y:0, K:21; R:0, G:85, B:150; Pantone 294), Black - Secondary, and White - Preferred for advertising/premium collateral.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The Cree logo requires clear space equal to the height of the logo's lettering for protection. Bleed left and right versions adhere to 1x, 2x, and 3x safety distances from the wordmark and symbol.

**Notes:**

- Questions regarding appropriate usage for merchandising or other applications should be directed to branding@cree.com.
- For logos larger than 12 inches wide, the trademark symbol must be removed.
- Crop marks in bleed logo lock-ups indicate proper trimming for use on banners or pages.

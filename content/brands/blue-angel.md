## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The BLUE ANGEL logo consists of a blue ring with a laurel wreath and a blue angel in the center, accompanied by 'BLUE ANGEL (C)' and a surrounding text.
- Minimum dimensions include: Logo minimum size of Ø10 mm, minimum size of short link (30 x 3.5 mm, font 7pt - Bold/Regular), minimum size for explanatory box without logo (32 mm in length), minimum size for explanatory box with the logo (45.2 mm in length). Heights of explanatory boxes may vary depending on text range. No distortion is allowed.
- The BLUE ANGEL logo must always be depicted in full (monotone blue with white font and white contour). Deviations, cropped or modified versions, or added logos, images, or content are prohibited.
- The BLUE ANGEL logo may not be distorted, altered, shadowed, or have its form changed in any way. Incorrect usage examples include cropped, distorted, shadowed, or color-modified versions.
- The logo must retain a minimum spacing of 0.6mm or 6% of the image size from neighboring labels or logos when used alongside other logos.

**Color Information:**

- The corporate color of the BLUE ANGEL logo is specified as follows: CMYK: 100 Cyan, 60 Magenta, 0 Yellow, 0 Black; RGB: 26-91-165; Web color: #1a5ba5; Special color: HKS 44; RAL: 5005 (Signal blue).
- In special cases, a black version of the logo can be used upon agreement with RAL gGmbH.
- The box surrounding the explanatory content is white with a frame in the same blue as the BLUE ANGEL logo; if the black version of the logo is used, the box and link must be black.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography for short link and explanatory box: Font Neue Helvetica Condensed Regular; Web address section: Neue Helvetica Condensed Bold; Extension section (/uz...): Neue Helvetica Condensed Regular.
- Font size: Font size for the advantages section should be 80% of the web address font size.
- Font size for short link: 7pt in Bold/Regular style; Minimum size of explanatory box with logo is 45.2 mm in length, without the logo is 32 mm in length.

**Spacing Layout:**

- White space around the logo must maintain a clear space of 0.6mm or 6% of the image size to neighboring logos or labels.

**Notes:**

- All BLUE ANGEL logos, short links, and explanatory boxes are obtained from RAL gGmbH to ensure consistency.
- German and English variations of short links or explanatory boxes are available upon request from RAL gGmbH.

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The Byte Bao logo comprises an abstract 'B' made of parallelograms with base angles of 45° and 135°. The logo must not be less than 30mm / 114px for the full mark, and not smaller than 35px / 10mm for the brand mark. Clear space surrounding the logo must be equal to a minimum area of 1:4 of the brandmark size.
- Correct logo application guidelines include usage of the full logomark wherever possible, high contrast between background colors and logo, and placement in one corner when horizontal space is insufficient.
- Incorrect logo usage includes stretching or disproportionately resizing the logo, using it vertically, recreating it, using improper logo colors without contrast, applying graphical elements like shadow/glow, or altering its composition.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include #ff2525 (red), #80087b (purple), and a linear gradient blending these two colors at a 15° angle. Secondary colors include #1b1b1b (black) and #ffffff (white). The gradient should show faint purple at the bottom-right of compositions.
- Valid color pairings must prioritize aesthetic consistency and complement the brand's visual identity.

**Typography Information:**

- The primary font family is Butler, a serif font inspired by Dala Floda and Bodoni families, with weights including Ultra Light, Light, Regular, Medium, Bold, Extra Bold, and Black.
- The secondary font family is Campton, a friendly sans serif with weights including Thin, Extra Light, Light, Book, Medium, Semi Bold, Bold, Extra Bold, and Black.
- Typography guidelines emphasize hierarchy-building using font weights. Butler Bold/Extra Bold is used for main headings, while Campton Semi Bold/Bold is used for paragraph headings, with tracking adjustments at -50 or default.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style includes professionally taken and edited images with subjects in focus, avoiding excessive color saturation. Focus must capture detail, depth, and dimension for modern aesthetic appeal. Consider incidental photography that captures natural interaction.
- Incorrect photography usage includes stereotypical/staged photos, compositions that are overly complicated, use of low-resolution images, altered or filtered images, unrelated subject matter, or out-of-focus photography.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The visual design adopts a key element inspired by scatter plots and line graphs using the brand color palette. This balances dynamism and structure, and can be paired flexibly with gradients and palettes.

**Brand Voice:**

- Byte Bao's brand personality includes being intelligent, experienced, premium, honest, modern, enduring, and diverse. Its values include trust, authenticity, innovation, energy, teamwork, integrity, discretion.
- Messaging should express carrying expertise in cybersecurity and data privacy, particularly across Asia-Pacific for multi-jurisdictional transactions.

**Notes:**

- Primary brand application examples showcase usage across stationery, website design, and company brochures with structural alignment with logo and typography choices.

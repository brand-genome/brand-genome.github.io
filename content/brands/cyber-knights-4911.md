## Brand Guidelines

**Color Information:**

- Primary Palette: CyberKnights Red (Pantone PMS 1805 C, CMYK 5, 96, 80, 22, RGB 175, 39, 47, Hex #AF272F), CyberKnights Gray (Pantone PMS 423 C, CMYK 22, 14, 18, 45, RGB 137, 141, 141, Hex #898D8D), Black (Pantone Process Black, CMYK 0, 0, 0, 100, RGB 0, 0, 0, Hex #000000), White (CMYK 0, 0, 0, 0, RGB 255, 255, 255, Hex #FFFFFF).
- Color usage guidance - avoid combining CyberKnights Red and CyberKnights Gray as background and foreground simultaneously.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Typeface: Open Sans - clean and modern, used for body text across publications and websites. Font styles include Light, Light Italic, Regular, Italic, SemiBold, SemiBold Italic, Bold, Bold Italic, ExtraBold, and ExtraBold Italic.
- Body text should include spacing between lines, spacing after paragraphs, and not use Open Sans smaller than 9pt. Acceptable alternatives are Verdana, Helvetica Neue, or Arial.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Brochures: Incorporate photography in themes. Use dominant black & white photos on covers; clear social media, website information on the back; concise structured layouts inside.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Body text spacing: Ensure space between lines and paragraphs; avoid using Open Sans smaller than 9pt.
- Brochure layout guidance: Dominant photo covers, concise information sections, consistent team identity elements.

**Brand Voice:**

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The logo includes the Moodle wordmark and the mortarboard symbol (graduation cap), which represents education completion. Versions include the primary logo, single-color versions, reversed white logos for colored backgrounds, and a slogan-integrated logo. The logo's safe zone requires a free, unobstructed space equivalent to half the height of the logo.
- Examples of incorrect logo usage: Do not reproduce in unauthorized solid colors, change typefaces, rearrange parts, add drop shadows or gradients, squash, stretch, or rotate the logo. Avoid busy backgrounds and enclosing logos in boxes.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: Moodle Orange (#f98012, RGB 249 128 18, CMYK 0 61 100 0, Pantone 158 C). Other primary colors include Moodle Charcoal (#686566, RGB 104 101 102, CMYK 49 43 41 33, Pantone Cool Gray 10 C) and Headline Extra Dark (#282828, RGB 40 40 40, CMYK 71 64 64 68, Pantone 426 C). Secondary/accent colors: Light Blue (#7ac2d6, Pantone 630 C), Light Green (#9cbd50, Pantone 367 C), Blue (#239cae, Pantone 7689 C), Green (#2ca14f, Pantone 7739 C), Dark Blue (#005a75, Pantone 7470 C), and Dark Green (#316d5e, Pantone 555 C).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family: OpenSans. Available weights: Regular (body text), SemiBold (subheadings), Bold (headings), and ExtraBold (main headings). Designed for print, web, and mobile use with modern, clean, open, and legible characteristics.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should feature people and technology with bright, natural tones and diverse representation. Technology-focused images should include genuine Moodle interfaces edited into device screens. Photos with subtle orange hues are preferred. Ensure logos are unobstructed and placed on high-contrast neutral space.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice descriptors: Friendly, human, and respectful. Writing tones adapt for different contexts: Informative and impartial in press releases; friendly, engaging, and conversational for social posts; and emotive and inspiring for case studies.

**Notes:**

- General brand usage disclaimer: Moodle and its logo are trademarks owned by Moodle Pty Ltd. Unauthorized use is restricted. Official trademark policies can be reviewed at moodle.com/trademarks.

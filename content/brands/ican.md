## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- Logo split into two elements: Symbol (dynamic/graphic) and Logotype (typographic/verbal).
- Two logo variants mentioned: Institutional logo (priority) and Banner logo (to emphasize 'ICAN' name).
- Prohibited logo usages include distortion, exiting graphic norms, unapproved colors, custom shapes (e.g., outside hexagon), and white halos.
- Mobile version of the logo must respect the previously defined logotype principles.

**Color Information:**

- Logo colors depend on the background: Black logo on white/light backgrounds, and white reversed logo on dark backgrounds.
- Primary colors with specifications listed: Orange Pantone 1375 (HEX: #f59331, CMYK: 30% 72% 74% 84%, RGB: 245, 147, 49), Green Pantone 376 (HEX: #86c24a, CMYK: 30% 72% 74% 84%, RGB: 133, 194, 74), Violet Pantone 268 (HEX: #65318f, CMYK: 30% 72% 74% 84%, RGB: 101, 49, 143).
- Mobile logos use a black background and apply the same color rules for consistency.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography hierarchy: Myriad Pro family used. Bold for titles (Myriad Pro Bold), Regular for body and subheadings (Myriad Pro Regular), Italic for alternative/print subtitles (Myriad Pro Italic).
- Typography font samples explicitly shown, including alphabets and numerals in title, subtitle, and regular formats.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Graphic elements reflecting 'ICAN' identity are recommended for use in print and multimedia. Colors and shapes can be adapted to the medium.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Letterhead design: Logo centered at the top, with details (school name, address, references) centered at the bottom. Typeface used is Myriad Pro Regular.
- Business card layout: Logo present; Name in Myriad Pro Bold; title/service in Myriad Pro Regular; contact details in Myriad Pro Regular.

**Notes:**

- The document emphasizes consistency and adherence to defined visual principles to maintain brand homogeneity.

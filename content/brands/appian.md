## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Main logo variations shown in Appian blue and white for different contexts.
- Logo color options include 'Appian Blue', white for dark backgrounds, and black for black-and-white printing.
- Safe space defined using the height of the 'A' in 'Appian' as the unit for deriving clear space.
- Minimum logo size is 36px for preserving legibility; specific icons assigned for favicon, app chiclet, and social avatar.
- Logo misuse examples include distortion, stretching, outlining, adding gradients, changing letter spacing, recoloring, or changing angles without approval.

**Color Information:**

- Appian color palette includes: White (#FFFFFF, RGB: 255, 255, 255, CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 0), Dark Blue (#020A51, RGB: 2, 10, 81, CMYK: 100, 98, 27, 40), Vibrant Blue (#2322F0, RGB: 35, 34, 240, CMYK: 85, 76, 0, 0), Cheddar (#FCB858, RGB: 252, 184, 88, CMYK: 0, 34, 83, 0), Power Green (#38CF7F, RGB: 56, 207, 127, CMYK: 78, 0, 77, 0), Turbo Purple (#9D4DE3, RGB: 157, 77, 227, CMYK: 53, 76, 0, 0), Pomegranate (#DD3457, RGB: 221, 52, 87, CMYK: 0, 99, 57, 0), and Pale Blue (#F3F9FF, RGB: 243, 249, 255, CMYK: 11, 3, 0, 0).

**Typography Information:**

- Effra is the primary typeface for headlines and subheadlines, with bold and regular weights available. It has clean lines, rounded shapes, and balanced spacing.
- Mulish is a secondary typeface used for body copy, featuring a minimal, simple design. Regular and Light weights available.
- When primary typefaces are unavailable, Tahoma Bold is used for headlines, and Tahoma Regular is used for subheads and body copy.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Use graphic elements (e.g., 'nodes of action', computer chip, or capsule) to frame photography and content areas.
- Photography should emphasize Appian's focus on 'empowering the individual', with centered content and the use of 45-degree slants for forward momentum.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Examples of using one or two graphic shapes to divide space, creating layouts that organize photography and content clearly while maintaining balance and alignment.

**Brand Voice:**

- Key messaging should focus on demonstrating Appian's ability to maximize resources, improve business outcomes, and simplify operations through low-code automation.

**Notes:**

- Event lockups should follow the same usage guidelines as the corporate logo; event names will no longer include the year.
- For questions about brand content and creative guidelines, contact brand@appian.com or creative@appian.com.

When do these style guidelines apply?
All style guidelines apply to any communication or documentation which is representative of CCCU/UOWC-HK, be it for internal or external use.
The logo
 
-
The CCCU/UOWC-HK logo should be used with any piece that represents CCCU/UOWC-HK.
 
-
This includes internal documentation and any student material.
External use
 
-
This includes advertising, marketing collateral, student recruitment material, signs, posters/banners, selected presentations & slideshows.
 
-
This includes material displayed in the public areas of CCCU/UOWC-HK, such as the foyer and corridors.
 
-
These must follow all elements of the style guide and requires approval for any variation.
Internal use
 
-
This means any communication or documentation that is for select internal groups only.

This includes teaching material, classroom material, study material, classroom and internal group presentations.

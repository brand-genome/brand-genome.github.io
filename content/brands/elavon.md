## Brand Guidelines

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- The Elavon logo is composed of two elements: the Elavon double arch mark and the Elavon logotype. Available logo color versions include full-color, one-color black, and one-color reverse (white). Full-color should use PMS 542 U and PMS 2748 U for the double arch and be used wherever possible when background is white or light-colored. Reverse logos are for dark backgrounds (preferably black).
- Minimum logo size specified as 3/4 inches (19.05mm). Clear space surrounding logo is calculated as the height of the lowercase 'v'.
- Do not use taglines with logos unless presented under U.S. Bancorp as 'A U.S. Bancorp Company'. Avoid placing full-color logos on backgrounds or photos. Incorrect usages include altering the logo in any form.

**Color Information:**

- Corporate palette consists of primary and secondary colors. Colors include PMS 2748, PMS 321, PMS 376, PMS Cool Gray 11, PMS 267, PMS 173 for primary elements. Secondary colors and tints are strictly prohibited. Hex codes and RGB representations are specified for each color.

**Typography Information:**

- Whitney font family is specified for company materials. Primary typefaces for headlines and subheads: Whitney Semibold and Whitney Medium. Secondary typefaces for body copy: Whitney Book and Whitney Light. Desktop application font family specified as the Calibri series (Regular and Bold).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery should reflect actual users engaging in authentic activities, showing diversity of global mix and vertical markets they represent. Use full-bleed images for visual engagement. Preferred subjects are smiling, laughing, and interacting.
- Secondary imagery includes transaction visuals such as card swipes with human interaction. Avoid static equipment-only imagery in collateral.
- Custom photography is preferred, and licensing rules should be respected for third-party images.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Preferred placement of logos is in the lower-right or upper-left corners of applications. Placement exceptions may be made based on design discretion.

**Brand Voice:**

- The Elavon tone of voice is warm, welcoming, honest, and easy to understand—focused on helping customers succeed confidently. Copy should be jargon-free, actionable, and written in friendly clarity.
- Voice reflects brand personality traits such as: strong, sincere, trustworthy, witty, smart, helpful, creative, proactive. Main persona named 'The Business Advisor' aims at friendly, trusted engagements.
- Copy tone considerations include positivity, brevity, transparency, accessibility. Partner and customer-facing language is tailored to suit verticals, avoiding legal jargon in customer communication.

**Notes:**

- Digital usages have specific design and behavior guidelines alongside print materials. Updates for digital properties will be included as additional sections.
- Collaborative guidelines exist for co-branding with partners, outlining preferred joint ventures and alignment specifications. Contact Corporate Communications for co-branding queries.
- Visual design elements like icons are identified to assist collateral. Icons are categorized by products and vertical markets for cohesive branding.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Sanchez is the primary symbol of World Nomads, used universally in communications. The symbol can be used alone or with the logotype, but it should not be distorted, recreated, or changed. Master artworks are available digitally. Logo configurations include one-line and two-line variations. It is noted that the Sanchez symbol has a transparent center, requiring careful control of the background for clarity.
- Logo colors mentioned specifically as Manhattan Charcoal and Egyptian Sunrise. Reversed logos are to be used on dark backgrounds and photographs. Clarity rules are provided for controlling transparency against complex images.
- To protect logo clarity and visual integrity, exclusion zones are defined for the one-line and two-line logos. Logos should always appear legibly on a clear background.
- Sub-branding rules indicate extension of the one-line and two-line logos for sub-brands such as 'Community' and 'Scholarships.'

**Color Information:**

- Color palette includes primary and secondary colors inspired by the world. Primary colors should be used prominently while secondary colors are highlights. Manhattan Charcoal is a replacement for black. Colors can also use tints in specified percentages. Primary colors include Manhattan Charcoal (RGB: 68, 70, 66, HEX: #444642, CMYK: 70, 54, 62, 31), Istanbul Twilight (RGB: 97, 57, 139, HEX: #61398B, CMYK: 53, 65, 0, 0), and Egyptian Sunrise (RGB: 255, 156, 0, HEX: #FF9C00, CMYK: 0, 34, 87, 0). Secondary colors include Thai Island, Rio Carnivale, and Tokyo Lights.

**Typography Information:**

- The brand uses the Karbon typeface for printed communications, offering clean, distinctive, and legible qualities. Ubuntu serves as the online alternative. Both typefaces are available in a variety of weights. The World Nomads icon font is available for download, with most icons capable of having a circle applied.

**Imagery Photography:**

- The photographic approach emphasizes authenticity, in-the-moment storytelling, dynamic and vibrant visuals, focused on travelers or locals, evoking emotion. Imagery should feel authentic and spontaneous, and aim to create an emotional connection with viewers to inspire them to travel as World Nomads.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo exclusion zones specify margins around the one-line and two-line logos to protect visual integrity.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice reflects being adventurous, charismatic, confident, challenging, cultured, and authentic. The language is vibrant, conversational, inspiring, and trustworthy, avoiding arrogance.

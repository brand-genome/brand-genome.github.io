## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Logo must not be reproduced manually; use official logo files only. Scaling must maintain aspect ratio (width and height) equally.
- Clear space: Minimum equivalent to the height of the letter 'F' in the logo.
- Minimum size requirement: 7 mm for print, 50 pixels wide for digital.
- Acceptable color usages: normal color (gradient blue and green icons with black type), reversed to white on brand colors or images, black and white versions.
- File formats: RGB, CMYK, and mono versions available upon request.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Pantone 306C (C75 M0 Y0 K0, #00c2f3), Pantone 382C (C35 M0 Y100 K0, #cbd92e), and others.
- Secondary colors complement the primary palette and include additional Pantone values.

**Typography Information:**

- Typeface: Gotham, weights used include Light, Medium, and Bold for corporate use.
- Secondary typeface: Calibri, regular weight is also specified.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photographs must include a blue color filter (opacity between 60-80%) applied as an overlay.
- Icons should be simple, fresh with a black keyline for visibility.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Images or icons can be presented within a 'speech icon' style for thematic consistency. Photographs must always be inside this style unless used as a full-page image.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand voice is relaxed, fun, engaging, yet professional, informative, and honest. It avoids jargon or slang terms.

**Notes:**

- The strapline can be used with the logo or independently in Gotham Bold (all capitalized, black).

## Brand Guidelines

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- Sormat’s logo includes a logotype and a symbol. The logo must always be used correctly and consistently, preserving its original design. It cannot be modified or reproduced as a drawing. The symbol can be used separately but cannot replace the full logo.
- Clear space rules: To ensure the logo stands out, a clear space free of text or graphical elements is required around the logo. Minimum clear space is half the width and height of the symbol. Measurements specified as 0.5x width/height for border space and 1x width/height of the symbol overall.

**Color Information:**

- Key brand colors for Sormat include defined codes for orange, blue, dark grey, and light grey alongside corresponding CMYK, RGB, HEX, Pantone, and RAL values. The additional colors bring variety to the palette but are recommended to be used sparingly and thoughtfully.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography guidelines highlight the use of 'Museo' font family, featuring Museo Sans (sans-serif for clarity, numbers, and small text) and Museo Slab (serif for headings and marketing). Available font weights include 100, 300, 500, 700, 900.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photos reflect the construction industry and feature three categories: people, industry, and materials. Black and white photos, especially of people, are key elements, highlighting authenticity and a sense of enthusiasm. Subjects vary based on target groups (e.g., professionals or consumers).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space ensures logo distinction; key measurements are specified, including minimum safe distance for logo elements.
- Various logo color variations are provided for specific use cases such as white backgrounds, dark backgrounds, and cases requiring monochrome or negative versions of the logo.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand voice emphasizes smooth service through the concept of 'Sormat Super Service.' Messaging highlights determination, expertise, and tools for superior purchasing and service experiences.

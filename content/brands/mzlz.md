## Brand Guidelines

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- KNJIGA STANDARDA
COMPANY DESIGN GUIDELINES
MZLZ | 2014 | V1
- The MZLZ logo features a triangular design symbolizing motion, synergy, and diversity of services. It can be applied on white or dark monochrome surfaces. Specific schemes for protected zones (space around the logo) and minimum size are defined.
- Primary logo includes the acronym 'MZLZ', text 'Međunarodna zračna luka Zagreb / Zagreb International Airport', and the emblem. It must always be legible, even in compact sizes.

**Color Information:**

- Colors selected for the MZLZ identity are borrowed from Croatian and French flags to emphasize local partnerships and regional economic intent.
- Primary colors include blue and white, suitable for flags and other branded materials. Colors are used for adaptability to various contexts.

**Typography Information:**

- Font family Helvetica is used widely for its clean, technical appearance. Styles include Light, Medium, Bold. For digital or basic office use, Arial Regular and Bold are substitutes. Minion Pro is used in editorial contexts.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum size is specified with font sizes: 4pt, 12pt. Clear space rules emphasized with x measurement defining distance from other logos and elements.
- Group logo is positioned at the upper right corner. Layout examples include division into maximum 2/4 of space plus proportional spacing for elements down to 1/16.

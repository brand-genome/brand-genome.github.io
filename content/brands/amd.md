## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Logo versions include horizontal, black logo, reversed logo.
- Clear space for logo measured by 1x of the white square in the AMD Arrow mark. Minimum logo size: 0.675 inches width; Preferred size: 1.375 inches; Scaling recommendation: 20% of total width or height for best representation, with minimum requirement at 10%. Top right placement preferred.
- Logo usage restrictions: Do not stack the arrow mark or use as standalone. Do not add gradients, mix black and white versions, or outline the logo. Place solid black or white only, and respect corporate-approved templates. Do not remove the arrow or place the logo over busy photography. Avoid patterns.
- Tagline lockup examples feature black and reversed logos with tagline 'Enabling today. Inspiring tomorrow.' Clear space measured by 1x white square in the AMD arrow. Typography: Klavika Regular Italic. Notes: Tagline may be used separately but not as event name/theme; 'fit' must always be lowercase.
- Guidelines for tagline lockup: Do not scale or change orientation disproportionally. Do not change colors, add gradients, mix black and white versions. Avoid placement over busy photography or as a pattern. Do not create theme-specific lockups.

**Color Information:**

- Corporate color palette supports creative flexibility while product palette takes precedence in product-specific instances. Guidelines given to represent corporate and product colors prominently in creative assets.
- Colors include:
Primary Palette: PMS 186 C (#ED1C24, RGB: 237, 28, 36, CMYK: 0, 100, 100, 0), PMS 7468 C (#007C97, RGB: 0, 124, 151, CMYK: 100, 40, 35, 0), PMS 1595 C (#F26522, RGB: 242, 101, 34, CMYK: 0, 75, 100, 0), and more.
Neutral Palette includes PMS Cool Gray 8 C (#9d9fA2, CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 45), Process Black (#000000), and HEX value FFFFFF (White).

**Typography Information:**

- Core typeface: Klavika and Arial.
Alternate typeface for regions provided (e.g., Chinese - Noto Sans, Japanese - Meiryo, Korean - Gulim, etc.). Fonts used are meant to define clear hierarchy using extensive weight variations from Light to Bold Condensed.
- Typography best practices: Clear label for headline, body text, pull quotes emphasized (Medium Condensed font) following organized hierarchy. Triangle bullet not preferred anymore.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Video-related content: AMD logo frame specified explicitly in 16:9 ratio with 20% height. Logos can animate and resolve on white over black background.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Various metrics mentioned for logo and tagline clear spaces: measured as 1x defined by a square dimension around the AMD Arrow mark for clutter-free confidence in use.

**Brand Voice:**

- Logo tagline: 'Enabling today. Inspiring tomorrow.' Controlled tone guiding message alignment coherently.
- Video game marketing ensures AMD branding responsibilities following ESRB and ARC guidelines with strict dos and don’ts criteria applied professionally.

**Notes:**

- Contact details for branding queries, quality preservation specified at 'brand.team@amd.com'. Responsibility disclaimer extensively acknowledged against possible errors/changes.

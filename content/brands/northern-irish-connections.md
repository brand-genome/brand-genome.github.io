## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The logotype for NI Connections consists of a 'flower' icon and supporting typography. Space is an issue in some contexts (e.g., social media avatars), and the logo may be abbreviated to just the icon. The logotype should never be recreated and must always use the master artwork.
- Clear space rules: The logo must always be surrounded by a minimum area of space defined using the height of the letter 'N' from the logo. Minimum size requirements: 35mm wide for print use and 120 pixels wide for online use.
- Approved variations: Full color version, single color reversal, monotone black, and monotone reversal. Typography always white (reversal) or grey from the color palette.
- Incorrect usage examples: Do not distort the height or length of the logo, do not change its color, add drop shadows, alter proportions, or rotate the logo.
- Guidelines for multiple partner logos: Supporting logos to be set at approximately 50% of the NI Connections logo's presence or placed in secondary locations.

**Color Information:**

- Core logo color palette for NI Connections includes: Pantone 2189C (#192d45, CMYK 97/78/44/48, RGB 25/45/69), Pantone 2220C (#1899a0, CMYK 78/18/38/2, RGB 29/153/159), Pantone 425C (#4d4d4e, CMYK 62/52/50/48, RGB 77/77/78), Pantone 382C (#adc916, CMYK 41/0/99/0, RGB 174/202/13).
- Invest NI color palette includes additional colors for broader communication. Examples (CMYK/RGB/HEX visible): CMYK 40/96/84/67, RGB 79/18/13 (#4f120d), CMYK 39/7/14/0, RGB 168/207/217 (#a8cfd9), CMYK 30/15/7/0, RGB 189/204/224 (#bdcce0), etc.
- Color matching from imagery can expand the palette, allowing percentage tints to complement backgrounds or typography.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Place imagery attributes: Authentic environments, dramatic light, unique angles.
- People imagery attributes: Authentic environments, engaging, unique angles, reportage style.
- Product/service imagery attributes: Authentic environments, engaging, unique angles, hero product/service.
- Preferred stock imagery sources: Getty Images (premium), iStock (affordable but mixed quality), Unsplash (free but contemporary). Avoid staged or clichéd images.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Hex area flexibility for logo application includes layouts incorporating text, flat color, white space, or imagery.

**Brand Voice:**

- Key principles: Informative, positive, plain-speaking, and human. Language must inspire and inform. Approaches include influential (authority and gravitas), straight-talking (honest and clear), and approachable (two-way engagement).
- Tone of voice rules: Use first-person and active verbs, be specific with key facts, concise and simple, and lead with exciting news. Write with conviction.
- Maintain brand consistency: Avoid abbreviating 'Northern Ireland' (e.g., do not use 'NI'), and keep 'Northern Ireland' together on one line.

**Notes:**

- The toolkit provides consistent branding assets, including presentations, artwork, and logos, for branding venues and informing attendees.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The logo consists of a symbol representing diversity and culture as well as the logotype. The main dark logo is used on a white or colored background. A lighter version of the logo is used when the background color is dark.
- File formats recommended for the logo include: .eps, .ai, .png, .jpg, and .tiff.
- Clear space around the logo is computed as the height of the logo divided by three. This ensures visibility and prevents any graphic elements from invading the exclusion zone.
- Minimum size for the full logo is specified as 20 mm x 3.33 mm.
- Logo variants are required to retain fixed proportions and relationships; no modifications, stylizations, or hand-drawn versions are permitted.
- Examples of logo placement on backgrounds include black, blue, gray, and white/paper backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: Dark Blue, Pantone 662C, CMYK: C100 M92 Y33 K24, RGB: R36 G44 B91, HEX: #242c5b. Usage: Dominant color for visual presentations.
- Primary color: Light Blue, Pantone 442C, CMYK: C100 M65 Y0 K30, RGB: R0 G67 B128, HEX: #004380. Usage: Supporting primary color.
- Secondary colors include additional complementary shades (e.g., RGB #2f2978, #991d74, #0666b0). Usage: To accent and support the primary palette, not exceeding 40% in one piece.
- Color tones and gradients are provided for both primary and secondary color systems, with usage defined across percentages (100%, 80%, 60%, etc.).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: Montserrat, available in Regular and Bold styles. Usage: Suitable for headings and impactful text.
- Secondary font: Open Sans, available in up to 10 weights from Light to Extra Bold. Usage: Provides a clean professional feel and is well-suited for body text in digital formats.
- Alternative font Type 1: Nunito, available in different weights.
- Alternative font Type 2: Museo Sans, available in Regular and Bold. Licensed font.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Letterhead dimensions: 297 x 210 mm (DIN A4), 120 gsm uncoated white paper. Print method: Offset CMYK.
- Business card dimensions: 85 x 55 mm, 400 gsm uncoated white paper. Print method: CMYK.
- Envelope dimensions: 297 x 210 mm (DIN A4). Weight: 120 gsm, uncoated white. Print method: Offset CMYK.
- Correct logo placement is critical. Only approved styles as represented in the guidelines are allowed. Alternative placements are not permitted.

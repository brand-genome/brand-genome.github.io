**Typography Information:**

- Approved fonts for Toys 'R' Us branding include ComicStrip, Helvetica Neue, and Avenir. Helvetica Neue is contemporary yet classic, suggested for headlines, subheads, and body text for both corporate and retail applications. Avenir is fashionable and timeless, inspired by Futura and Erbar, used for headlines, subheads, and body copy. ComicStrip is whimsical and ideal for headlines, subheads, and call-outs, mainly for retail applications but not suitable for body text. Shag Lounge font is expressive and fun, suited for bold headlines but not for body text.
- Specific font weights and styles for Helvetica Neue: Light 45, Roman 55, Bold 75, Black 95, Light Italic 46, Roman Italic 56, Bold Italic 76, Black Italic 96. Fonts can be purchased from Monotype Imaging at fonts.com or Font Shop at fontshop.com. Avenir font weights include: Book 45, Book Oblique 46, Medium 65, Black 95. Avenir fonts can be purchased from Font Shop. ComicStrip font can be purchased from Monotype Imaging. Shag Lounge font can be purchased from House Industries at houseind.com.

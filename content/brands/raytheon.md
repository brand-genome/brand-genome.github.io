## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- The Raytheon logo incorporates distinctive, bold letterforms with precise letter spacing and ligatures. The tagline, 'Customer Success Is Our Mission,' defines the brand and may not be separated from the logo. The preferred full-color logo uses Pantone PMS 186. Alternate versions in black, white (reversed out), and PMS Cool Gray 10 are allowed. Customized versions for businesses and co-branding are strictly regulated.
- Acceptable logo versions include full color with PMS 186, black, white (reversed), and PMS Cool Gray 10. The logo-tagline combination must not be re-created. Use only master-approved artwork.
- Clear space around the logo must equal the height of two 'R's in the Raytheon logotype. No version of the logo may have a line length smaller than 0.75 inches.
- Logos must not be tilted, distorted, have added effects, locked into shapes, or combined with unauthorized elements. Only four approved colors are allowed.
- Co-branded logos must appear with a separation bar in PMS Cool Gray 10 that adheres to strict spacing guidelines, ensuring the Raytheon logo is not smaller than partnered logos.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand color: Raytheon Red (PMS 186), CMYK: c0, m100, y81, k4; RGB: r206, g17, b38; HEX: #CE1126. Secondary colors include Cool Gray 10 (c0, m2, y0, k60; #666465), Cool Gray 5 (#B5B5B5), PMS 5425 (#7C96A1), and additional accent colors.
- PMS 186 (Raytheon Red) should be used in moderation to emphasize the logo. Secondary and accent palettes, particularly Cool Gray tones, are preferred for graphical elements to ensure a clean and modern aesthetic.

**Typography Information:**

- Frutiger is the preferred font for corporate and logo-related designs. Arial is an acceptable alternative for certain applications. Typeface is standardized for product and program icons.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photographic and graphical elements should use color duotones from Cool Gray and secondary palettes. Adequate white space and minimal use of Raytheon Red ensure balance and focus on technical illustrations and bold corporate tones.

**Spacing Layout:**

- White space must be ample and contribute to a clean, open, and modern aesthetic. Clear space around the Raytheon logo must equal the height of two 'R's.
- When co-branding, a clear space of at least two 'R' heights must surround the Raytheon logo and any accompanying elements. Separation bars in PMS Cool Gray 10 must adhere to width and height guidelines relative to the logo.

**Brand Voice:**

- Raytheon values confidence, precision, responsibility, and collaboration in its brand positioning. Attributes include innovation and ethical practices, emphasizing reliability and customer success.
- The tagline 'Customer Success Is Our Mission' communicates Raytheon's promise and must be used consistently in advertising and marketing materials as the brand's sign-off.

**Notes:**

- Logos must be sourced from master electronic artwork obtained via the corporate identity website. Customized designs and business lockups must adhere to approved standards.
- Program and product icons must maintain ample clear space, follow corporate font and color standards, and not incorporate the logo directly.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- Overview of 'Make it Possible' logo variation, including elegant and sophisticated design inspired by Huawei products, with a 'Lotus Petal' variant for specific uses and solid color variants for others.
- The lotus petal brand signature is Huawei-exclusive and should only be used on white or black backgrounds.
- Solid color logo defined as part of brand design principles.
- Minimum size for MIP logo is defined as 33.5mm wide and 7mm tall.
- Minimum exclusion zone or clear space around the logo is equivalent to 'P' height from the Make it Possible name.
- Incorrect logo usage examples provided, including changing proportions, color scheme violations, and position between 'Make' and 'Possible'.

**Color Information:**

- Basic color usage defined with color codes: Red CMYK (30/100/100/0), RGB (155/34/34); Black CMYK (100), RGB (0/0/0); White CMYK (0/0/0/0), RGB (255/255/255).
- Bespoke color usage recommended for one-off designs with custom highlights.

**Typography Information:**

- Font recommendations listed: Frutiger Ultra Black Oblique and Frutiger Black.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Highlight use of petal texture in exhibition interiors and marketing materials to emphasize brand alignment.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Specifies the width of 'Make It Possible' to be 3x the width of Huawei brand logo, ensuring consistency in joint branding.
- Logo proportions validated with the height of 'Make It Possible' matching the main body of lotus petal.

**Brand Voice:**

- 'Make it Possible' is not just a slogan but a mantra emphasizing creativity, engineering, and pushing limits.

**Notes:**

- Email signature designs and business stationery designs to use red brand signature.
- Merchandise must include the lotus petal brand signature and Huawei logo, with white merchandise preferred.
- Legal requirement: 'Make It Possible' must always be paired with Huawei branding and its font and pattern must remain consistent.
- Design approvals for any new or adjusted logo must be sought from Huawei brand team.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The Manchester United Foundation logo must be used as directed in this guide. It must NOT be altered in any way and must only be resized in proportion. The portrait version of the logo should be used on all communications where possible. Use of the Foundation logo must be approved in advance of print and/or online publication.
- The landscape logo should only be used in instances when it is not possible to use the portrait version. The logo must be used as directed in this guide. It must NOT be altered in any way and must only be resized in proportion. Use of the Foundation logo must be approved in advance of print and/or online publication.
- The exclusion zone is always relative to the size of the logo. The safe area is clear from any background image contrasts, other logos, text, or graphic devices.
- The logo should not appear smaller than the following dimensions: Minimum print size is 22mm wide, minimum screen size is 70 pixels wide. For headers, the minimum print size is 47mm wide and 144 pixels on screen.
- Colour variations of the logo depending on the background are shown. Depending on whether the logo sits on a red, light, or dark background, the colors of registration mark and wordmark change appropriately.
- Examples of incorrect logo use include distortion, changes to colors, additional graphic devices, obstruction to the crest, and altering or separating elements of the logo.
- Composite logos for partners and funders must maintain the Foundation logo always on the left side. Composite logos must adhere to specific safe area and minimum size rules of 22mm print/70px web.
- Strapline 'Engage. Inspire. Unite.' must only be used when relevant and in alignment with specific graphics spacing and layout requirements.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color is red (Pantone 186c, CMYK: C:0, M:100, Y:100, K:5, HEX: #C70101, RGB: 199/1/1). Supporting colors are white (CMYK: C:0, M:0, Y:0, K:0, HEX: #FFFFFF) and black (CMYK: C:0, M:0, Y:0, K:100, HEX: #000000).
- Special finishes and premium event creatives may feature Pantone shades like 871c and 877c for metallic accents depending on the decor and branding style.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Bertold Akzidenz Grotesk. Used across all communications, bold for titles/headlines in title case, medium for sub-headings, and regular for body copy. Arial is permitted as a replacement for digital formats.
- Secondary typeface: Stone Sans ITC Medium. Reserved for body copy in corporate communications or for older audiences.
- Typographic misuse includes the use of all caps in headings or body copy, overly tight/loose leading and kerning, justifying text, and misusing Stone Sans in headings.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography prioritizes participants as the focus of images. Images must be positive, properly composed, and not depict individuals negatively. Approvals for use required via Foundation communications.

**Spacing Layout:**

- A strict exclusion zone exists for the logo's proportions, ensuring no text or graphic elements encroach within the marked space.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand tone is engaging, inspiring, positive, friendly, clear, and inclusive. It should avoid being boring, aggressive, or complicated.

**Notes:**

- Introduction explains that Manchester United Foundation engages and inspires young people, aligning its brand with Manchester United's values.

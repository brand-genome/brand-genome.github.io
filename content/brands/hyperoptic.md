- The swoosh is positioned behind the logo, always in full color when centered in the bottom of the page.

**Color Information:**

- Brand colors and their usage are defined for digital assets. Colors are categorized into primary, secondary, and accent. Includes RGB and HEX codes. Examples include Bright Cyan (R: 58 G: 185 B: 220, HEX: #3AB9DC) as a primary color.

**Typography Information:**

- Font used is Museo Sans in weights 700, 500, and 300. Headline font Museo Sans 700 is bold for confidence and impact.

- Hero images must be uploaded at no less than 1440px in width. Images require a 20% or 40% transparent black tint for text legibility. Text and placement rules include left-justified hero text or centered text for mobile.

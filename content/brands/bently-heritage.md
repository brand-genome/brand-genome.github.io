## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Our logo must be used as-is and cannot be modified under any circumstances. If you need a different file type, contact the design department.
- Context, contrast, and production requirements must be considered when applying color to the logo. Black or white versions are provided for single-color printing. Other applications may include silk-screening, embossing, etching, and engraving.
- The 'B' in Bently is used to define clear space dimensions — height and width ensuring legibility. For Logo Variation 3, the clear space is set at 50% of B's scale.
- Logo variations include: Wordmark (no subtext), Stacked (no subtext), and Monogram.
- Logo misuse guidelines: do not recreate, delete, or crop the logo; do not rotate, outline, or skew the logo; do not change colors; avoid placing logos in similar-color backgrounds or on busy patterns.
- Incorrect Uses: Logos cannot be reproduced from printed materials but only from original digital files.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Colors - PANTONE 7530C: CMYK (10, 18, 25, 32), RGB (163, 147, 130), Hex #a39382.
- Primary Colors - PANTONE BLACK 6C: CMYK (100, 79, 44, 93), RGB (16, 24, 32), Hex #101820.
- Accent Colors - PANTONE 876: CMYK (0, 41, 53, 42), RGB (139, 99, 75), Hex #8B634B.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Typefaces: Chronicle Text and Sweet Sans Pro. These are used to convey the brand's personality in headlines and body text.
- Chronicle Text Weights: Roman, Italic, Semibold, Semibold Italic, Bold, Bold Italic.
- Sweet Sans Pro Weights: Hairline, ExtraThin, Thin, Extra Light, Light, Regular, Medium, Bold, Heavy.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography must be approved by the marketing department. Stock images or images of another brand/product are prohibited.

**Spacing Layout:**

- No additional layout or spacing details beyond the logo's clear space rules.

**Brand Voice:**

- Copywriting style should embody excellence without pretension. The brand should sound like an award-winning master distiller: candid, authoritative, but not arrogant.
- Copywriting Guidelines: Be concise, conversational, use active voice, and tailor content to the audience. Avoid unnecessary superlatives, jargon, and foreign phrases.
- The brand uses 'whisky' instead of 'whiskey,' following the Scottish spelling.

**Notes:**

- Brand identity is linked to Nevada's pioneering culture, sustainable production standards, and luxurious spirits unique to Minden, Nevada.
- Marketing approvals are required for all imagery and branding applications to ensure consistency.

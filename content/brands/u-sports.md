## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The logo exists in multiple orientations including horizontal, vertical, stacked, and icon-only. Clear space should be consistent around the logo, measured as '0.5x the height of logo'.
- Logo versions include full color, black and white, and reversed. Incorrect usage includes recoloring the logo, stretching, distorting, or placing it on busy backgrounds.
- File formats mentioned for logo usage include EPS, PNG, and SVG for precise printing and digital applications.
- Safe zone or clear space rules are defined as '0.5x the height of the logo.'

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes black (#000000), red (#E4002B), and white (#FFFFFF). Usage examples demonstrate the importance of these colors in branding.
- Secondary/accent colors are provided to complement primary palettes for branding extensions. Examples include secondary color HEX and RGB codes.
- Rules prohibit mixing unapproved colors or altering saturation of primary brand colors.

**Typography Information:**

- The primary font family is 'Proxima Nova', used in weights such as Light, Regular, Medium, and Bold. Application includes headlines, body text, and captions.
- Typography attributes: Headlines must use bold weights and larger sizes, captions typically use small text. Guides are provided for font size in proportion to the layout.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should capture emotion, action, lifestyle, and details. Preferred styles include candid and documentary photography emphasizing university athletic experience.
- Image treatment includes bright and natural coloration, avoiding oversaturation or added effects. Images should focus on passion, dynamic perspectives, and moments of action or emotion.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grid specifications for layouts: 6-column grids for vertical formats; 12-column grids for horizontal layouts. Safe zone margins of 0.5 inches on all sides for standard prints.
- Margins, gutters, and live area specified for dynamic design. Column gutters are limited to 0.165 inches for standard sizes.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone of voice encourages concise, punchy, and memorable writing. Attributes include witty, authentic, progressive, and engaging storytelling.
- Special emphasis on storytelling featuring student-athletes' multidimensional lives, both academically and athletically.

**Notes:**

- Partner logos are required to maintain equal visual weight when locked up with the U SPORTS emblem. Lock-ups must be pre-approved before use.
- Team Canada emblem usage includes specific rules for permissible color combinations, and the emblem may appear with or without lock-ups.
- International branding guidelines suggest that the primary focus of products should be the international branding elements, with the domestic U SPORTS emblem in a secondary position.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Primary Brandmark available in Black and White. Black to be used on corporate communication (e.g., website, internal documents, advertising, digital applications). White to be used on black backgrounds or dark images (e.g., press, outdoor advertising, staff uniforms).
- Premium Brandmark available in Black + Silver and Silver. Black + Silver features a silver symbol printed using matte silver foil or Pantone 877 metallic, on white backgrounds. Silver version printed using matte silver foil or Pantone 877 metallic, applied to black backgrounds. Used for high-production items like corporate stationery, folders, and event materials.

**Color Information:**

- Primary palette consists of Black, White, and Silver (Pantone 426, White, Pantone 877). Silver enhancements include matte silver foil or metallic for tactile effect. Colors reproduced as Pantone spot colors for print; in CMYK, RGB for digital use.
- Secondary palette inspired by company values, including Pantone 360, Pantone 265, Pantone 310, Pantone 137, Pantone 115, and Pantone 2727. Used for highlighting key information or differentiation, and applied more expressively for internal and branding purposes.

**Typography Information:**

- English font is 'Objektive' (Light, Regular, Bold); Arabic font is 'Frutiger Arabic' (Light, Regular, Bold). Arial Unicode is used digitally for internal use. Fonts are clean and contemporary and are available for purchase online.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Visual language includes kinetic elements derived from the brand's symbol and angles, used subtly on corporate materials. Super graphics boldly apply symbols to items like brochures and folders. Enhanced tactile visuals using techniques like silver foil and UV varnishing.

**Notes:**

- Some color depictions are indicative only and require reference to actual Pantone standards. Pantone is a registered trademark of Pantone, Inc.
- The guidelines emphasize a modern, clean, and tactile approach to corporate and visual identity.

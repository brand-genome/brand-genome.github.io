## Brand Guidelines

### 2008

**Logo Information:**

- The Honda Powerhouse logo is defined as the official logo for both corporate and dealer use. It is always to be used in the stacked configuration, and 'Powerhouse' must always be preceded by 'Honda.'.
- Clear space rules for the Honda Powerhouse logo dictate that horizontal clear space must be equal to the logo height (X), while vertical spacing must be Y=1/2 of X.
- Incorrect logo usage examples include altering the logo artwork in terms of size, proportions, color, incorporating it into other designs, or adding embellishments. These elements are prohibited to maintain brand integrity.

**Color Information:**

- Preferred Honda Red (Pantone 186) is to be used wherever possible for its high impact on coated paper; CMYK breakdown: C:3 M:100 Y:90 K:0. Alternate black and reversed versions allowed depending on the use case.

**Typography Information:**

- Dealer names should use the Berkeley font as follows: 'XYZ Dealer Honda Powerhouse.' Font size depends on the template.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The logo should be positioned in the black band separating the main visual and product features in print. It should always be enclosed in a white box with a clear space defined.
- Examples of correct logo placement on stationery items provided, including letterheads, envelopes, and business cards.

**Notes:**

- The guidelines emphasize brand consistency and accuracy in logo and typography usage to maintain corporate identity.

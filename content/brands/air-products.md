## Brand Guidelines

### 2024

**Logo Information:**

- The logo is a combination of the Air Products type style and symbol with a fixed relationship, not to be separated, embellished, outlined, or altered in any way. Horizontal placement is preferred.
- When using the logo, it should never be scanned as it may become distorted or modified. Only use approved electronic versions.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Colors: Air Products Green (Pantone 347C, CMYK: 85/13/100/2, RGB: 0/154/73, Hex: 009a49) and Air Products Blue (Pantone 2995C, CMYK: 73/16/0/0, RGB: 0/168/225, Hex: 00a8e1).
- Accent Color: Air Products Merlot (CMYK: 29/100/56/18, RGB: 157/1/72, Hex: 9d0148).
- Secondary Colors include Air Products Evergreen (CMYK: 89/44/97/53, RGB: 0/67/32, Hex: 004320), Air Products Water (CMYK: 29/0/1/0, RGB: 170/233/255, Hex: aae9ff), Air Products Ice (CMYK: 3/1/1/0, RGB: 245/248/249, Hex: f5f8f9), and others listed with tints.
- Vibrant complementary colors are designed to work with ample white space to create a bold, contemporary feel.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typefaces include TheSerif LP (Light, Plain, SemiBold, Bold) for body text and TheSansLF (Light, Plain, Semi Bold, Black) for headlines and short blocks of text.
- Open Sans (Light, Regular, Bold, ExtraBold) is used for web and desktop applications.
- Typography consistency enhances communication effectiveness and strengthens brand identity.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Abstract and people-focused images that depict industries (e.g., buses, trucks, ships, steel, chemicals, metals) should be natural and in-the-moment, matching the theme of 'Generating a Cleaner Future'.
- Images should be symbolic of speed, forward movement, and progress.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Momentum lines, both solid and dotted, are key design elements representing states of matter and movement towards a cleaner future. They should lead the viewer's eye and enhance dynamism.
- Graphic elements are shown working together consistently, including application to websites, vehicles, literature, and exhibitions.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand tone of voice emphasizes understanding, integrity, and passion. Communication should be in clear, everyday, conversational language, avoiding jargon and acronyms.
- Put the customer at the center by addressing their problems directly using active, engaging language.

**Notes:**

- Use only pre-designed graphic files for the positioning statement 'Generating a Cleaner Future' in one- or two-line versions.
- Further brand identity guidelines are available online at airproducts.com/identity.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- TransitCenter Visual Identity Guidelines Version 0.1 January 4, 2017
- TransitCenter has a new brand identity to reflect and support its raised profile and voice in the public transportation ecosystem. A strong visual identity, applied consistently across all communications, will support the brand and strategic aims of TransitCenter to advocate for better public transit across America. These guidelines explain how the elements of our identity come together as a coherent system.
- Contents structure of the brand guideline document: 1. Brand Strategy 2. Identity 3. Color 4. Typography 5. Visual Language 6. Layout 7. System in Use.
- Logo Naming convention: Name, Asset Name, Color, Color Space, File Format. Example file name: TC_ID_Line_Btm_Pos_CMYK.eps.

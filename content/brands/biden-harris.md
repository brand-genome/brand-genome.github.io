## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- Two external logo versions - Biden and Team Joe. Clear space free from disturbing elements: top-half height of the B in BIDEN or the A in TEAM.
- Primary logo usage color combinations: (1) One of three main blue colors with red on white/light backgrounds. (2) On dark backgrounds, the logo is off-white with red. E in Biden should always be used as red unless authorized by the Design Team.
- Logo and assets use is restricted by Biden for President Graphics’ Limited License, including non-derivative personal and non-commercial use only.

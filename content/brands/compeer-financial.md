## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Logo anatomy featuring Logo Mark and Word Mark.
- Clear space requirement equal to the height and width of the 'O'. Minimum size of 130 px wide for digital; 1.5-inch width for print.
- Logo variations include Horizontal Logo, Vertical Logo, and Logo Mark. Minimum vertical logo size: 130x130 pixels (digital), 1.25x1.25 inches (print).
- Color versions include: 4-color CMYK, RGB web/screen, Pantone, 1-color black, 1-color white.
- Approved and unapproved usage instructions: Do not alter proportions, colors, gradients, overlay without contrast, apply shadows or additional effects.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors feature Pantone 381 U (HEX: BAD636; RGB: R186 G213 B054), Pantone 347 C (HEX: 319B4C; RGB: R49 G156 B76) and Pantone 3155 U (HEX: 00615A; RGB: R000 G097 B090).
- Secondary colors include HEX: 92C230, HEX: 73757F, HEX: b1b1b1, and HEX: 179DAB for web and accents.
- Gradient usage: A gradient made from Pantone 381 U and 347 C may be used minimally at a 90-degree angle with the lighter tone on top.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Brandon Grotesque (weights: Black, Medium).
- Secondary typeface: Lato Regular for long-form text, with Arial as fallback for internal documents.
- Typography hierarchy includes Heading 1 (36pt, uppercase, Brandon Grotesque Black), Heading 2 (13pt, uppercase), Heading 3 (11pt sentence case), Body 1 (9pt, Lato Regular).
- Web typography example shows Brandon Grotesque (Black, uppercase, 22px size) and Lato Regular for body text.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery treatments: High-resolution, authentic, casual portraitures featuring human connection. Bright full-color imagery preferred. Avoid staged photos, selective coloring, effects like vignettes or flares. Black-and-white images only under print restrictions.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space equal to the height and width of the 'O' mark in the logo used as a baseline for spacing.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of imagery emphasizes authenticity, human connection, aspirational yet casual depictions. Visuals must align with agricultural and financial heritage of the brand.

**Notes:**

- Iconography complements logo with rounded forms and layered styles usable in print and digital apps. Icons should align with customer operations and day-to-day relevance.

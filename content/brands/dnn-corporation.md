## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- The DNN brandmark consists of two components: the 'D' logo and the 'DNN' logotype. Preferred display is the primary full-color expression, followed by monochromatic and grayscale options. Monochromatic versions include PMS Black 5, white, and black. The primary palette red or blue may only be used in DNN-produced materials complementing a preceding primary full-color expression.
- Acceptable logo lockups include the Primary Horizontal (preferred) and Alternate Stacked. Limited horizontal space allows use of Alternate Stacked, but requires prior DNN Marketing Department approval. Clear space around the logo must equal at least the height or width of an 'N' from the logotype. Minimum brandmark size is specified.
- Minimum size requirements: the Primary/DNN brandmark should not be displayed smaller than .5N (vertical version) and .25h (horizontal version) for clarity.
- Unacceptable uses of the logo include rotating the brandmark, altering the size or relationships of logo components, adding gradient/dropshadow/glow effects, customization, and changing colors. Other forbidden actions include stretching, compressing, skewing elements, or placing the brandmark in a containing shape.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes Pantone 2995 (blue) for backgrounds and fills, and Pantone 185 (red) for accents and secondary design elements. Neutral tones, tints, and shades expand and moderate the palette. Pantone codes with RGB, CMYK, and HEX values are provided.
- Secondary color palette is strictly for accents or call-to-action elements only, and must never be used for fills or background colors. Prior approval from DNN Marketing is required.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Avenir Next Pro family. Weight options include Regular, Medium, Demi, Bold, Demi Condensed, and Bold Condensed, applied systematically. General usage balances conversational and formal styles. The primary color palette can be used for headings if necessary.
- Specific typography uses: H1 uses Bold weight and Sentence Case for assertive yet warm statements; H2 alternates to Regular weight for lighter tones; H3 employs All Caps for structured hierarchy; H4 utilizes condensed styles and extended tracking for labelling and tags.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space guideline: Minimum clear space around the logo is equal to the height or width of 'N' from the logotype.
- Grid and layout rules ensure systematic application of logo proportions, including minimum sizes and specified lockups (horizontal vs vertical). Spacing is critical to maintaining brand impact.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of Voice (TOV): DNN embodies genuine empowerment through connection, creativity, and passion. Communications should resonate with qualities such as openness, trust, and a symbiotic balance between commerce and community.
- Messaging reflects DNN's core values: openness, focused on customer success, and metrics-based improvement. Empowerment and passion should be at the forefront of all brand initiatives.

**Notes:**

- For customization of brand assets or new DNN-branded designs, prior approval must be obtained from DNN Marketing. Unauthorized alterations undermine brand consistency.
- For branding inquiries or requests, contact: dnnbrand@dnncorp.com or call during office hours (Monday-Friday, PST).

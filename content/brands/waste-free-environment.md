## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The Waste Free Environment (WFE) logo must have a minimum clear space of 50% of its height to ensure it breathes.
- The WFE logo must not be reproduced with unauthorized colors, grayscale, outlines, drop shadows, or graphic embellishments. It must not be used vertically, in sentences, or placed over images.
- Minimum size for the WFE logo: 300 mm width for printed material, and 80 pixels for digital applications.
- A 1-color version of the logo can be used when budget or reproduction restrictions prevent using the full-color logo.
- The height of 'An Initiative By GPCA' logo should match the height of the WFE typeface in both English and Arabic layouts.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include:
- WFE Orange: Pantone 144 C, CMYK 0/48/100/0, RGB 244/150/0, HEX #F49600
- WFE Green: Pantone 376 C, CMYK 50/0/100/0, RGB 149/193/31, HEX #95C11F
- WFE Blue: Pantone 640 C, CMYK 100/0/0/22, RGB 0/134/190, HEX #0086BE
- Secondary colors include:
- WFE Yellow: Pantone 7406 C, CMYK 0/18/100/0, RGB 255/208/0, HEX #FFD000
- WFE Blue: Pantone 2915 C, CMYK 59/7/0/0, RGB 101/190/31, HEX #65BEEC
- WFE Green: Pantone 354 C, CMYK 80/0/90/0, RGB 0/166/75, HEX #04A64B
- One-color logo specifications:
- Pantone 7540
- CMYK 0/0/0/70
- RGB 111/111/110
- HEX #6F6F6E

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Typeface: Round Pro
Available weights:
- Black
- Bold
- Medium
- Light
- Secondary Typeface: Arial
Available weights:
- Black
- Bold
- Regular
- Light
Arial is effective for digital and screen communications.
- Primary Arabic Typeface: Mothanna
Designed for software UIs with sans-serif-like characteristics.
- Secondary Arabic Typeface: Janna™
A humanist Kufi style designed by Nadine Chahine, incorporating Ruqaa and Naskh influences for a friendly and informal appearance.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Suggested WFE logo positioning:
- Partner logo and company details aligned appropriately in print collateral.
- Placement rules for GPCA event artwork:
- 'An initiative by' logo must be at the top-right corner for main GPCA events.
- 'An initiative by' logo must be at the lower-right corner for partner events.
- The height of partner logos should match the WFE typeface height and be placed on the right side for English artwork and left side for Arabic artwork.

**Notes:**

- Incorrect WFE logo usage examples and guidelines are provided. The logo must always use approved electronic artwork and should never be altered.
- Guidelines for giveaways include placement of partner logos in alignment with branding rules.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The logo includes two elements: the L symbol and the LatitudePay wordmark. It exists in four formats: horizontal (preferred), stacked, LPay, and L symbol. Preferred versions are blue, white, or black depending on usage contexts. The logo must not be recreated or altered in any way.
- Recommended logo versions include: horizontal version (preferred), stacked, and smaller formats like LPay and L symbol for limited space. Minimum size for horizontal logo is 150px; for stacked logo, 100px.
- Color versions include LatitudePay Blue (preferred), white, and black. Blue should be the first choice unless it clashes. White is for colored backgrounds while black is for monochrome printing.
- Detailed rules for placement include using the full logo where possible; smaller versions are acceptable for narrower spaces, such as square formats in banners and footers.
- Common mistakes include: altering the logo, downloading unofficial logo files (e.g., from Google Images), and distorting or cutting the logo shapes.

**Color Information:**

- LatitudePay Blue is the primary corporate color. Black and white are secondary options for specific use cases — such as monochrome printing and colored backgrounds where the blue logo might clash.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Homepage hero assets recommend banners with 3 key format ratios: 2.35:1, 16:9, and 1:1. Templates allow lighter and darker variations, with or without photography and brand illustration.
- Imagery available for download includes assets tailored for Facebook and Instagram posts. These can be customized.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Recommended safe zone: Keep the logo fully visible and avoid cutting or overlaying text or imagery.

**Brand Voice:**

- Use conversational, friendly, human, and energetic tone of voice. Add warmth and humor when promoting LatitudePay.
- Approved taglines include: 'Shop now. 10 weekly payments.' and 'Life doesn’t have to wait.' Stick to these taglines for legal and branding consistency.

**Notes:**

- General social promotion tips include tagging the LatitudePay account, adding hashtags (#LatitudePay, #lifedoesnthavetowait), boosting local posts for $5 minimum, and ensuring accessible links to LatitudePay resources (e.g., buttons and banners).
- LatitudePay processes 10% upfront payment, followed by 9 weekly automatic payments. Merchants are paid within two business days, with LatitudePay absorbing all consumer finance risks.
- LatitudePay provides flexible payment options and promotes financial wellbeing through '10 installments' payment models.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The 4-H logo combines the tagline Learn To Do By Doing into a visually unified layout. Boxed tagline is preferred, flush left or flush right positioning. Text-only version permissible only when boxed arrangement isn't feasible. Logo files provided by 4-H Canada should not be altered.
- Electronic files for 4-H Logo, Wordmark, Motto, and Pledge must use vector EPS versions for most electronic files; JPG or PNG permitted for web. Resizing should maintain aspect ratio to avoid skewing.
- Examples of logo and motto integration in various formats such as letterhead, envelopes, fact sheets, and social media, highlighting correct appearance styles.

**Color Information:**

- 4-H Canada’s leadership pillars are associated with specific colors: Community Engagement & Communications (Pantone 297, Light Blue), Science & Technology (Process Cyan), Environment & Healthy Living (Pantone 285, Dark Blue), Sustainable Agriculture & Food Security (Pantone 360, Light Green). These pillar colors guide the design of The Angle and display text.

**Typography Information:**

- Whitney is the central type family for body copy and headings, with weights including Light, Book, Medium, and Semibold. Specific leading and size standards are outlined for letterhead, business cards, and collateral.
- Clarendon Bold is designated for specific headers.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style emphasizes authenticity, featuring diverse youth participating in outdoor 4-H activities that reflect values of leadership and positive development. Prefer real photographs taken in full-frame formats for impact, allowing occasional clipped imagery as accents.
- Historical photography utilizes sepia tone for consistency. Specific Photoshop steps provided for achieving tone consistency.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The Angle must consistently align with all visual elements at a precise 5° upward incline, using prescribed measurements to maintain brand consistency.
- Social media and cover photos require adherence to platform-specific dimensions to avoid distortion: Facebook (851x315px), YouTube (2560x1440px), Twitter (1500x500px). Alignment and proportions must follow guidance.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tagline Learn To Do By Doing reflects the 4-H motto and establishes a tone conveying action and leadership. It is a trademark and must not be altered.
- Messaging emphasizes positive youth development, teamwork, and building leaders. Describes diversity and values using approachable, inspiring tone.

**Notes:**

- Applications emphasize bringing together logo, tagline, colors, and graphic elements cohesively across formats such as annual reports, banners, and digital media. Examples provided are concepts and not production-ready.
- The brand guidelines are exclusively owned by 4-H Canada; violators of trademark use will be notified, and failure to comply may lead to legal action.

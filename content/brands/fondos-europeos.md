## Brand Guidelines

### 2024

**Logo Information:**

- The logo has a horizontal version marked as the primary, applied on light backgrounds.
- For dark backgrounds, the negative version of the logo is applied.
- Logo construction relies on alignment along guidelines. It aligns with Source Sans Pro-Regular typography.
- Minimum clear space (safe zone) requirement around the logo uses the full width of its symbol.
- Minimum size requirements: 35 mm width for print and 200 px for digital for the full logo; 18 mm print and 54 px digital for the isolated logo symbol.
- An isolated logo symbol is reserved for exceptional use in corporate materials and merchandise.
- Bilingual logo versions are available in official co-languages like Euskera, Catalán, Valenciano.
- Incorrect logo uses include: distortion, rotation, adding effects/degradations, using non-approved colors, modifying typography, placing negative logo on non-approved gray or color backgrounds, and using images that interfere with logo visibility.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Dorado (RGB: 176, 156, 86; HEX: #B09C56; CMYK: 0/18/65/39; Pantone: 7503 C) and Azul Corporativo (RGB: 18, 18, 58; HEX: #12123A; CMYK: 100/78/0/50; Pantone: 2766 C).

**Typography Information:**

- The main font used is Source Sans Pro-Regular for textual alignments, supplemented by others for hierarchy like Source Serif Pro-Light and Source Sans Pro-SemiBold.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The clear space rule specifies an exclusion zone of 100% of the symbol's width around the logo.
- Minimum reproduction sizes: 35 mm (print), 200 px (digital); isotopes minimum is 18 mm in print.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand message emphasizes the emotional and tangible contributions of European funds, expressed through the claim 'Europa se siente.'

**Notes:**

- The bilingual versions of the logo must maintain its format and are essential for representing co-official languages.
- Explicit rules govern logo consistency, including color restrictions and no modifications to typography or symbol.

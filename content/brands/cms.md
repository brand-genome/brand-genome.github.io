## Brand Guidelines

### 2024

**Logo Information:**

- Full color logo composed of the letters CMS and a two-color graphic image, preferred use includes 'Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services.'
- Reverse logo in black or reversed out to white on contrasting background.
- Minimum size requirement is 120 pixels wide. Clear space must be at least the height of the 'C' in CMS around the logo. The logo should not bleed off any edge, placed 1/8 inch inside the edge.
- Logo usage rules: the CMS logo must not be altered in position, typeface, combined with other elements, rotated, stretched, distorted, texturized, or recolored.
- CMS logo and DHHS logo lockup rules: DHHS logo must be more prominent and placed on the left, with specified spacing.
- CMS organizational units are branded under the CMS identity, avoiding standalone marks for subdivisions.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: CMS Blue (CMYK: 99/74/16/3, RGB: 1/83/144, HEX: #015390), CMS Gold (CMYK: 5/16/88/0, RGB: 244/206/59, HEX: #f3ce3b).
- Secondary color: CMS Bright Blue (CMYK: 78/14/0/9, RGB: 0/154/208, HEX: #009ad0).
- Extended color palette assigned to Strategic Plan Pillars and initiatives with individual CMYK, RGB, and HEX code specifications.

**Typography Information:**

- Recommended font for CMS name usage: Sans Serif fonts like Frutiger Bold or Arial Bold, all uppercase except for 'for'.

**Spacing Layout:**

- CMS logo clear space rules: maintain a clear distance as the height of the letter 'C' in the CMS logo.
- DHHS and CMS logo lockup spacing: minimum of two 'X's between logos, where 'X' equals the height of CMS letters.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice (TOV) must be consistent, audience-appropriate, and ensure CMS communication is trustworthy, helpful, stable, and fair.

**Notes:**

- Requests for CMS logo use or development should be directed to Logos@cms.hhs.gov or MultimediaServices@cms.hhs.gov.
- CMS prohibits use of logos to promote private or for-profit entities. Misuse can lead to legal action under the Social Security Act Section 1140.
- CMS standard templates available for presentations, business cards, and other communications. Requests can be sent to MultimediaServices@cms.hhs.gov.

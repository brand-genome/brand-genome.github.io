## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- The B PAY logo is composed of a B device and the word 'PAY'. The relationship between these elements is fixed and must not be altered.
- The B PAY blue portrait logo should be used whenever possible. It has a white border that is part of the design, visible on colored backgrounds, and invisible on white backgrounds.
- The B PAY mono portrait logo can be used when the blue version is not possible due to printing or media limitations. Black is the only allowed alternative color.
- Logo isolation guidelines and minimum size requirements are mentioned for both portrait and landscape logos; details are available across specific sections of the document.

**Color Information:**

- The master B PAY blue color specs: Pantone (PMS) 2768, CMYK (C100 M78 Y0 K44), RGB (R24 G49 B104), and Web Hex (#0F204B).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Specific isolation guides for both portrait and landscape logos are noted in the document; ensures proper spacing and visibility alignments.

**Brand Voice:**

- The B PAY brand aims for visual consistency and integrity across digital and print mediums. Consistency across mediums is emphasized.

**Notes:**

- The document acts as a supplementary marketing guide but does not override the B PAY Identity Standards Manual, which takes precedence in case of conflict.
- Usage of any logos and wordmarks (including B PAY View logos) must undergo approval through partner financial institutions.

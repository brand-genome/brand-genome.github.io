## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Logos are presented with three background variations: black, white, and image background.
- Safe zone rules: The Symphonic logo should always be surrounded with clear space measured using the height and width of the letter 'O' in the Symphonic logo.
- Do's and don'ts for logo usage: The logo cannot be squished, stretched, rotated, flipped, shortened, or used in inconsistent colors. Full color changes to suit design needs are acceptable.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: #000000 (RGB: R:0, G:0, B:0).
- Primary color: #f4f4f4 (RGB: R:244, G:244, B:244).
- Primary color: #35d850 (RGB: R:53, G:216, B:80; CMYK: C:67, M:0, Y:94, K:0).
- Secondary color: #d1d3d4 (RGB: R:209, G:211, B:212; CMYK: C:0, M:0, Y:0, K:20).
- Secondary color: #333333 (RGB: R:51, G:51, B:51; CMYK: C:69, M:63, Y:62, K:58).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Raleway. Characteristics: sans-serif, unique, clean, and readable. Recommended for headlines and short text. Tracking: 10. Not suitable for long body text.
- Primary typeface font weights: Regular, SemiBold, Bold, ExtraBold, Heavy.
- Secondary typeface: Open Sans. Characteristics: modern and contemporary sans-serif. Provides contrast with primary typeface. Suitable for body text. Recommended style for large body text: 10pt font size, 15pt leading, tracking of 10.
- Secondary typeface font weights and styles: Regular, Regular Italic, Bold, Bold Italic.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography role: Lifestyle photography should showcase real people in real situations with an ambience of mystery and extravagance. Music may or may not be the focus.
- Photography style: Images should predominantly use full, natural color or black and white. Colors should be bold yet muted and slightly unsaturated. Foregrounds should be detailed and crisp, while backgrounds should be clean, uncluttered, or out of focus.
- Photography crops: Vary from intimate close-ups to broad, wide-angle views. Crops should be adventurous and interesting.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The safe zone around the logo must be equal to the height and width of the letter 'O' in the Symphonic logo. No text or visual elements should intrude into this space.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand voice descriptors: Inspirational, Intriguing, Simple, Powerful, and Genuine. Communication style: Conversational, cheerful, real, and relatable, avoiding technical jargon unless the audience is familiar with it.

**Notes:**

- The Symphonic brand emphasizes connecting people to music through both marketing and distribution and values customer service as a critical component of achieving customer satisfaction.

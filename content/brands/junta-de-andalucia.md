**Spacing Layout:**

- Safe zones are applied for all elements to ensure readability and maintain proportions. Proportions and spacing standards are defined in templates provided for signs and other applications.

**Brand Voice:**

- The tone of voice guidelines prioritize legibility and clarity while adhering to austerity principles. The branding avoids excessive designs and aims for practicality and cost-efficiency, particularly on textiles like hospital bedding and uniforms.

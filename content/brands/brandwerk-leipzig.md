## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The Brandwerk Leipzig logo is characterized by timeless, uncluttered elegance. The wordmark is reduced to its essentials, as per the founders' philosophy.
- The logo includes an abstracted drop represented as a dot. Its size, placement, and refinement vary depending on the medium, but are generally flexible.
- Clear space rules: The protection zones of the logo, visualized using dots, are derived from the height of the 'B.' Positioning can be at the top, bottom, or center, depending on usage and medium.

**Color Information:**

- House colors are pure black and white for print (100% K) and web (#000000). Foil stamping colors for labels are determined by the respective spirit characteristics and can be freely chosen.

**Typography Information:**

- House font: 'Avenue Mono,' used in one style. Hierarchy, such as headings, signatures, and body text, is emphasized through size adjustments.
- Highlight font: Used to denote spirits (e.g., 'K20/1'). Additional uses include merchandise and greeting cards.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Illustrations for the mobile version include variable designs for the distillery, bottle, and phone.
- Illustrations of glasses for web and print use, meant for visualizing recipes.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space defined by the height of the 'B' in the logo. Positioning options include top, bottom, or centered placement, varying based on medium and application.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand voice emphasizes timeless elegance and minimalism, aligned with the visual identity of the uncluttered wordmark.

**Notes:**

- Labels for the 350ml and 40ml premium spirits are inspired by individual product characteristics (e.g., champagne rye spirits).
- 3D animations include distinct versions for desktop/tablet and smartphone views for the K20/1 product line.

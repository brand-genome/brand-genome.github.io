## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The identity elements and how to use them: Our brand identity consists of the following elements; country of origin mark, photography, typography, colours, and grid. By using these elements correctly and consistently, we ensure a strong and recognizable brand identity across all applications.
- The country of origin mark is a strong and simple typographic mark that reflects how the sea, the mountains, and the sky connect along the Norwegian coastline. We use both positive and negative versions of the country of origin mark. There are also black and white versions for use whenever colour printing is not possible.
- Logo usage guidance: Always make sure that the background is calm and choose the version that stands out most clearly against the background.

**Color Information:**

- Color guidance: The blue shades represent the cold, clear waters and communicate authority and confidence. Images contribute a significant color impression and are always in cold tones. White surfaces add purity and space. Red highlights are used in the Norwegian flag in our country of origin mark.
- Color specifications: Pantone colors (294 U, 298 U, 186 U) and respective codes are provided: CMYK, RGB, and HEX values for professional printing, digital printing, screen use, and web use.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography guidance: Profile fonts are distinctive and build identity. Main font is Romain Headline Regular for titles and stand-firsts, Radikal Medium for subtitles, and Radikal Light for body text. Romain communicates quality and history, while Radikal provides an elegant, easy-to-read balance. Specific font usage guidelines based on examples are provided.
- Directions for Arabic Typography: Recommends using 29LT Zeyn Regular for headlines and standfirsts, Aktiv Grotesk Medium for subheadings, and Aktiv Grotesk Light for body text. Includes font availability details.
- Directions for Chinese Typography: Recommends using Source Han Serif SC Regular for headlines and standfirsts, Source Han Sans SC Bold for subheadings, and Source Han Sans SC Normal for body text. Provides font purchase information.
- Directions for Japanese Typography: Recommends using Source Han Serif JP Regular for headlines and standfirsts, Source Han Sans JP Bold for subheadings, and Source Han Sans JP Normal for body text. Includes font specifications and purchase details.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography guidelines: Photos should capture cold, clean sea, Norwegian landscapes with towering mountains, and clear, straight horizons without distracting elements. Images must be in cold, blue tones with both dark and bright contrasts. Examples include fishing boats and pen nets.
- Portrait and people photography: People should demonstrate pride and express personality. Photos should feature close-up shots of people in natural poses with clear focus on the individual and a blurred horizon in the background. Wardrobe and context must reflect authenticity, and colors should remain cold.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grid and Layout Design: Sky/Mountains occupy 2/3 of the frame, and the Sea occupies 1/3. This ensures design consistency and resonance with the brand's essence.

**Brand Voice:**

- Core Messages: 'Origin matters,' 'People matter,' and 'Taste matters' are key messages that reflect the essence of Norwegian seafood's origin.
- Tone of voice: Norwegian tone is described as honest, clear, and knowledgeable. Material must avoid being overly jovial and instead focus on inspiring trust and clarity.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- Information about displaying Accoya icons: the top three icons (‘Dimensionally Stable,’ 'Outstanding Durability,’ and 'Perfect for Coating’) must be highlighted in white against a dark gray background, and be presented in order above other details.
- Requirements for the 'Made With' logo: it must be prominently positioned in marketing communications, with size specifications of 20mm or 76px, and clearance rules to avoid placing near headers.
- Guidelines are provided for using the Accoya logo in conjunction with licensee branding, allowing flexibility for different design orientations, but maintaining set proportions.

**Color Information:**

- The color used for the 'Made With' logo: standard dark gray with specific size requirements.

**Typography Information:**

- Print Typeface Guidelines: Neo Sans font family is required for Accoya communications. Neo Sans Light is used for headlines in uppercase, while Neo Sans Regular is for all other text in sentence case. Special effects like italics or underlines are not permitted.
- The web typeface is specified to be Neo Sans Light for headlines and Neo Sans Regular for body copy. When unavailable, Arial may be used instead.
- Headline examples feature upper case and include a graphic element: a .5pt angled line (28°) extending from the top left or bottom right corner, at a distance half the size of the nearest character height.
- Different font sizes for online use are specified, e.g., H2: 60px, Neo Sans Light; H3: 46px, etc.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography guidelines state that images owned by Accsys Technologies may only be used with prior written consent and in relation to the Accoya wood promotion, including proper credit.

**Spacing Layout:**

- For marketing use, specific arrangements are outlined for the strapline placement in relation to key visual elements such as the 'Green Tab' logo. Strapline text must remain legible at all times and observe specified typographic rules.
- Positioning rules for the 'Made With' logo include minimum size, clearance, and placement along the base edge on either the left or right of marketing materials.

**Brand Voice:**

- Accoya brand messaging must be factual and consistent, clearly reassuring customers about Accoya wood's authenticity. Negative associations, contradictory statements, and unapproved usage of product websites are strictly forbidden.
- Accsys Technologies actively targets architects and manufacturers with email marketing campaigns to promote Accoya wood's features and benefits. Customization and co-branding are available with prior approval.

**Notes:**

- Accoya brand toolkit provides resources for authorized customers and distributors. Access requires login credentials from the Accsys Technologies marketing team.
- Accoya encourages the use of the 'Accoya logo' or trademark within guidelines. Intellectual property usage must follow the rules outlined in brand protection and display requirements.

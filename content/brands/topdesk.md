## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Description of logo components: 'Robin' (Logomark), Logo type, and Tagline. Main logo usage. Tagline for materials lacking context. 'Robin' icon as favicon or standalone icon.
- Clear space rules for logo and tagline: Calculated using the square size of 'Robin' rotated 45° for all sides.
- Logo color versions: Full color, white (75% opacity on top mark), and black (90% opacity on top mark). Alternative logo for unconventional surfaces.
- Logo do's and don'ts: Always use logo and mark together; don't rotate, separate parts, alter colors, or add effects like drop shadows.

**Color Information:**

- Primary logo colors: Merlot (#C24530), TOPdesk Blue (#008EAF), Vermillion (#E65038); Secondary colors: Fern (#73C59E), Aqua (#85CFCB), Coral (#EE7B5F), etc.
- Digital colors: Specific web-safe HEX colors (e.g., Denim 400 #008EAF, Ash 600 #525252). Contrast ratio rules for legibility (AA standard).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary fonts: Bree Serif (Headings/Quotes), Open Sans (Body/Subheadings/Captions). Example weights include 'Regular', 'Light', and 'Bold'.
- Typography rules by role: Headings are in Bree Serif; body text, captions in Open Sans. Digital typography uses divisors of 6 for spacing.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style: Studio shots with colorful palettes, cheerful and relatable. Situational images showcase authentic office experiences.
- Illustration guidelines: Geometric and hand-drawn components, avoidance of stereotypes, emphasis on simplicity and collaboration.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Digital layout uses 12-column grid with divisible margins and gutters (24px, 36px). Responsive padding shown with common values.

**Brand Voice:**

- Voice: Simple, active, and jargon-free. Friendly but professional, honest, and emphasizes down-to-earth communication.
- Tone: Informal, friendly, and clear. Positive without exaggeration; minimal exclamation marks allowed.

**Notes:**

- Highly structured design system balancing aesthetics with readability and accessibility. Encourages consistent branding across digital and physical touchpoints.

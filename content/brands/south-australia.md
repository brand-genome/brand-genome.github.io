## Brand Guidelines

Minimum clear space requirements are defined as 'x', with a minimum size recommendation of 15mm for clear identification. Formats specified include EPS, PNG, and RGB. - Guidelines for Brand Adelaide logo specify variations like full color, black, reversed, and special formats for embossing. Minimum clear space 'x' and minimum sizes are outlined as 30mm for specific applications. Acceptable formats listed are EPS, PNG, and SVG.

**Color Information:**

- South Australia's color palette includes PMS, CMYK, RGB, and Hex code specifications. Key primary colors include PMS 485 (C:0 M:100 Y:100 K:0, Hex #D52B1E) and PMS 152 (C:0 M:62 Y:100 K:0, Hex #E17000). Secondary examples include PMS Cool Gray 11 (C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:60) and PMS Black (C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:100).

**Typography Information:**

- Recommended typeface for South Australia branding is specified, with font guidelines for print and digital applications. Kerning, tracking, and leading adjustments are noted for flexibility in applications across campaigns.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Textural imagery guidelines emphasize tonal balance to maintain branding consistency. Images should avoid high-contrast levels and white areas to preserve edge framing integrity.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rules specified uniformly for logo, icon applications, and textual areas. Minimum sizes such as 15mm or specific dimensional ratios (e.g., x-height) are detailed for optimal layout in branding contexts.

**Brand Voice:**

- South Australia brand seeks to capture innovative, creative, and industrious storytelling to elevate state recognition globally. Businesses are encouraged to integrate branding elements for unified messaging.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- The Tani logo consists of a symbol and 'Tani' text. The symbol and text must always be used together, and the logo must adhere to specified proportions.
- The logo should prioritize white backgrounds when possible.
- Black-and-white usage is allowed under specific circumstances. Otherwise, the logo should always be in color.
- Incorrect logo usage examples include using improper colors, distorted proportions, gradient effects, incorrect contrast, and other violations.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette for Tani logo includes: Pantone 311 PC (#0CC6DE) - RGB(12, 198, 222); Pantone 2577 PC (#A47CC9) - RGB(164, 124, 201); Pantone 7545 PC (#51626F) - RGB(81, 98, 111); Pantone 3145 PC (#007C92) - RGB(00, 124, 146); Pantone 2665 PC (#7D5CC6) - RGB(125, 92, 198).
- The logo should only use specified colors to maintain consistency.

**Typography Information:**

- The corporate typeface used is Myriad Pro, chosen for its clarity, modernity, and simplicity.
- The secondary typeface is Calibri, selected for its readability and suitability for longer text. It complements the primary typeface when both are used together.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Tani maintains a visual library of images and videos that align with the brand identity. These assets must be used in compliance with licensing rules, depending on usage.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logos are to be used according to standard proportional guidelines. Proportion between symbol and text is specified explicitly.
- Design layouts such as letterheads, business cards, and other templates must adhere to these guidelines.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tani upholds 'Efficiency' as its mission, symbolizing core values of energy, expertise, innovation, and harmony.

**Notes:**

- The guidelines document can be downloaded in high-resolution formats from tani.com.tr/kurumsal. Exceptional situations should be coordinated via the email: kurumsal@tani.com.tr or phone: 0216 556 02 00.
- Tani's sub-brands in marketing materials must feature the phrase 'bir Tani markasidir' in small fonts at the bottom. Tani itself should not appear as a logo in such cases.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The MIQrotech logo mark is a critical representation of the brand. Primary features include brackets on the 'M' symbolizing monitoring units, an M shape for a short NEMA resistor, and the capitalized 'IQ' emphasizing intelligent technology. Secondary mark with 'M' in brackets is for scenarios with scale, legibility, and contrast challenges.
- Clear space for the logo is determined by the height and width of the through-hole within the logo. All logo files maintain this clear space. Minimum sizes specified to avoid illegibility are 100px or 30mm.
- Logo positioning guidelines recommend anchoring the logo in the top-left or bottom-left. If not possible, the other two corners may be used.
- Using the full logomark is preferred, but the secondary 'token' mark can be used in space-limited scenarios. High contrast between logo and background colors is recommended, and only one logo should be used per application.
- Incorrect uses of the logo include tilting, skewing, disproportional scaling, adding effects (e.g., drop shadow), patterning, or changing the composition or colors to an unspecified combination.
- Partnership guidelines define that logos should be optically similar in size, with placement determined based on equal or primary/secondary partnership. Clearances and scaling rules for partnership logos are detailed.

**Color Information:**

- Primary, secondary, and neutral color palettes outlined. Examples of colors and their codes provided: Keppal (#03BCAA, RGB 3, 188, 170, CMYK 98, 0, 10, 26), Jet Black (#2C2C2C, RGB 44, 44, 44, CMYK 0, 0, 0, 83), Dark Orange (#FF8800, RGB 255, 136, 0, CMYK 0, 47, 100, 0), among others.
- Color pairings and ratios specify consistent application for 2-color, 3-color, and 4-color pairings. Neutral colors should make up at least 80% of the composition in most instances (50% in some cases).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface: Helvetica Neue, chosen for its role in creating a unified and professional identity. Multiple weights are mentioned: Ultra Light, Light, Thin, Roman, Medium, Bold, Heavy, and Black.
- Secondary typeface: Roboto, developed by Google. Weights include Thin, Light, Regular, Medium, Bold, and Black.
- Font hierarchy recommends Helvetica Neue Medium or Bold for headings, Helvetica Neue Medium for subheads/emphasized text, and Roboto Light for body copy. Roboto Medium/Bold to emphasize body text. Automatic line spacing preferred.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style is dynamic, with a focus on detail, depth, and dimension. Avoid excessive color for a clean and professional look.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space prevents interference with the logo's visibility and legibility, as defined by the through-hole's dimensions in the logo design.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand voice is authoritative, intelligent, and approachable. Messaging should reflect reliability, confidence, innovativeness, and forward-thinking while remaining friendly and authentic.
- Example copy includes: 'Optimize your pipeline operations with comprehensive monitoring technology. Introducing the world’s first smart monitoring system for oil and gas pipelines, mIQroAware.'

**Notes:**

- Partnership guidelines specify logo arrangements for equal, primary, and secondary partnerships, including size and position adjustments.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- Logo consists of two elements: the Nautilus graphic and a type element. Full color version is the preferred version. Always use digital master references. File formats available include EPS (professionally printed applications), JPG (Microsoft programs), PNG (online usage).
- Logo size rules: Minimum logo size is 20mm width, recommended sizes include 35mm (A5), 42mm (A4), 50mm (A3). Exclusion zone: Defined by the width of the blue Nautilus segment; no text or graphics should encroach this space. Placement can be in any page corner.
- What not to do with logo: do not distort the logo, remove the Nautilus element, use incorrect variants based on background, tint, recolor, alter size relationship, or rearrange elements.
- Journal lock-up logos include names combined with the Company of Biologists logo for specific journals. These logos must use original digital artwork and should not be altered. Reproduction only using digital master references.
- Animated logo usage: Nautilus rotates during fade-in and fade-out transitions for video openings and closings. Continuous looping rotation can be used for third-party slides.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface is a bespoke font for 'The Company of Biologists' (not named but described as embodying the innovative nature of the organization). Secondary typeface is Trebuchet MS, used where materials are not professionally produced (e.g., presentations, reports, online materials). Guidelines include using the name as 'The Company of Biologists' with a capitalized 'T' and avoiding abbreviations.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Nautilus graphic is a distinctive brand element. It may be rotated, scaled, cropped, or used as small graphic elements such as bullet points or favicon. Examples provided show its versatility.

**Spacing Layout:**

- White space is important for clarity and to let design elements breathe. This applies especially to headings, quotes, photographs, and the logo itself.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tagline: 'supporting biologists, inspiring biology.' Can be used in single-line, two-line, or four-line versions depending on available space. Must not be redrawn or altered.

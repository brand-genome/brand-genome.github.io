## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The logo can be used in its classical form ('klassisch') or as the 'Sonnenblume' symbol. Minimum height for the classical logo is 100 pixels. Logos should not include localizations when smaller than 100 px, and the simplified Sonnenblume version should be used.
- Logos must be scaled appropriately and are recommended for responsive web design. If scaled below 100 px height, the classic form should be avoided.
- The logo is optimized for safe usage across different digital assets, including websites and sharepics.

**Color Information:**

- The background color option includes 'Grüne Struktur' with an RGB variant suited for 720p formats. The main green color code is specified for consistency (#559448).
- Text on the website generally appears over a white background. Alternatives for links are provided as dark green (#46962b), with lighter or darker mouseover variants (#0a321e).

**Typography Information:**

- Arvo is the primary font family with two specific styles: 'Arvo Grün' for header titles in uppercase, and 'Arvo Regular' for article headers and subheaders. The main body text is written in 'PT Sans Regular' or 'PT Sans Bold'.
- Fallback system font is 'Trebuchet MS' for cases where web-enabled typography fails, ensuring a clean and consistent appearance.
- Header font styles use specifications: font-family: 'Arvo Gruen' or 'Arvo Regular;' text-transform: UPPERCASE; size 2.5em.

**Imagery Photography:**

- The 'Grüne Struktur' background variant (RGB) is preferred for larger screens or promotional graphics. Alternatively, softened photo backgrounds with full coverage and minimum 1280px are allowed.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The digital newsletter and mailing formats follow responsive layouts with specific structured zones for body text, headers, and action areas.
- PowerPoint and Keynote templates for presentations are offered in 16:9 and 4:3 ratios. Two layout options are given: 'Grüne Struktur' full background and a white background with green frame.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand tone is clear, visually distinctive, and consistent across platforms. Messaging elements include share-pics for social relevance and a focus on communicative aesthetics.

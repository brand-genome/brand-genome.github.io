## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The USAV Corporate Mark is an identifying mark of USAV and represents the brand identity and values of USA Volleyball to the volleyball community, the sports industry and the general public. The USAV Corporate Mark consists of the USAV Ballmark and the USAV Wordmark. In certain creative situations, pre-approved USAV Marks may stand alone, provided the entire USA Mark is shown in its entirety in the context of the design.
- PRIMARY CORPORATE MARK VARIATIONS: includes PMS Full Color, Full Color, 1-Color Black, 1-Color White, PMS White Wordmark (contrast version).
- EXCLUSION ZONE: The exclusion zone is the perimeter surrounding the mark. No other elements (like margins) are allowed in this area at any time. Size is determined by the 'S' of the wordmark around all sides.
- MINIMUM SIZE: Corporate Primary Mark has minimum sizes of width (.95 inch / 69px for the mark, 1.25 inch / 90px with exclusion). Corporate Secondary Mark has minimum sizes of width (1.67 inch / 120px for the mark, 2.08 inch / 150 px with exclusion).
- INCORRECT USAGE: Do not tilt, invert colors, outline, add taglines, change or adjust colors, transpose colors, reposition components, distort, or place on competing/distracting backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- PRIMARY COLOR PALETTE: Pantone 187 C (CMYK: 7, 100, 82, 26, RGB: 166, 25, 46, HEX: #A6192E), White (CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 0, RGB: 255, 255, 255, HEX: #FFFFFF), Pantone 2768 C (CMYK: 100, 90, 13, 71, RGB: 7, 29, 73, HEX: #071D49).
- MONOCHROMATIC & METALLIC Shift Colors: Pantone Black 6 C, Pantone Cool Gray 10 C, Pantone Cool Gray 6 C, Pantone Cool Gray 2 C. Additional colors include Pantone 117C (Hex: C99700), Pantone 877C (Hex: 8A8D8F), and Pantone 8023C Bronze (Hex: AA6443).
- ALTERNATE COLORS: Corporate Marks may use one-color options, including 187C RED, 2768C NAVY, Cool Gray 2, Cool Gray 6, Cool Gray 10, 8023C Bronze, and METALLICS, such as Silver (877C) or Gold (117C).

**Typography Information:**

- PRIMARY FONT: Arial Nova (Regular, Bold, Light, and Oblique); used universally and compatible with Windows. Prohibition (Regular and Oblique) is also noted for stylistic emphasis.
- TYPE USAGE GUIDELINES: Prohibition font primarily used for titles, slogans, and limited word count phrases. Typical tracking of 25 points is suggested, only necessary in select cases.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Iconography and Symbolism: Guideline includes usage of patriotic imagery, like stars, stripes, and the U.S. Flag, which emphasize the sport's connection to national identity.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing guidelines: Ensure exclusion zones around logos; measured precisely based on one capital 'S' size from the wordmark.

**Brand Voice:**

- Core voice values emphasize growth, support of US teams, and brand consistency. Communication must maintain the goodwill and promote the sport effectively.

**Notes:**

- USAV is strict about following brand use standards and requires all designs submitted for approval to their Brand Manager (Barbara Grice) for review. Materials allowed usage include promotional, licensed merchandise, and marketing.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2022

**Logo Information:**

- The Nantes Université logo consists of a graphical sign based on the initials 'N' and 'U', which symbolize the institution. 'N' and 'U' initials include a visual play that represents the Roman numeral IV symbolizing the 4 poles of the establishment. The sign is placed above an amphitheater-like shape, representing the shared knowledge base (socle common de la connaissance).
- The logo is constructed with precise alignment rules. Letters in the logotype are aligned vertically, ensuring the full titled text matches the height of the graphic sign part.
- Clear space zone: There is an obligatory minimum clear space zone around the logotype equivalent to half the height of the logotype. No elements are allowed inside this space.
- Minimum size requirement: The complete logotype must not be used below a width of 25mm in print or below 150px in digital formats.

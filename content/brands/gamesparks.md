## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Powered by GameSparks logo for games. Hex color for main lettering: #56565a, RGB: 86, 86, 90.
- Clear space rule: Height and width of the GameSparks icon define the minimum clear space requirement around the logo. Space must be flat, free of design elements, and clear of page edges.
- Minimum size requirement for screen: 120px x 30px. Minimum size requirement for print: 11mm x 43mm. Alternate small display logo required for smaller dimensions (less than 160px x 45px or 56mm x 16mm).
- Acceptable logo uses include primary logo in color, primary logo in color reversed (black/grey), reverse grey/black version, and 100% grey logo.
- Unacceptable uses: Do not switch specified colors, stretch or distort the logo, change the composition, alter or substitute text, use a different typeface, combine with unrelated graphics, outline the logo, or use arbitrary colors/tints.

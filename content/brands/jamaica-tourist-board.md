## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The corporate logo should be used on corporate communication pieces such as stationery, presentations, and newsletters. It is treated as vector art with no adjustments to size, placement of the registration mark, or font typesetting.
- The JAMAICA® Heartbeat of the World logo is the campaign logo and is used for all creative production. It includes the corporate brand logo, the registration mark, and the 'Heartbeat of the World' campaign tagline.

**Brand Voice:**

- Jamaica is represented as a cultural giant despite its small geographical size. Its essence stems from vibrant people, a strong work ethic, and global recognition in music, sport, food, and literature.
- The 'Heartbeat of the World' campaign is supported by seven key pillars showcasing Jamaica's uniqueness: Brand, Music, Food, Sport, Nature, History, and Film.

**Notes:**

- Pages 5 and 6 detail corporate and campaign-specific logo usage rules, including when to apply the tagline lockup. These logos are categorized separately.

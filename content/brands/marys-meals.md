## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Logo elements and versions include name, strapline, and bright blue box; in variations such as standard, square, horizontal alternatives (multiple), white text, and black/white versions.
- Minimum clear space required around the logo equals the height of the 'm' in 'meals'.
- Screen minimum height is 58px, and print minimum height is 15mm.
- A white keyline border can be added to the logo when placed on a busy background. Maximum border width equals the stems of the 'm' in 'meals'.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color is Mary’s Meals blue (Pantone 299C, Print: C85 M19 Y0 K0, Web: R0 G157 B220, Hex: #009ddc).
- Secondary colors include crisp white, warm yellow, and simple grey for an uplifting appearance. Complementary colors are available for additional design needs.

**Typography Information:**

- The logo uses the FF Meta font exclusively to stand out from other copy elements.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum clearance space around the logo equals the height of the 'm' in 'meals', providing integrity and emphasis.
- Size considerations for logo use: Screen minimum height is 58px, while print minimum height is 15mm.
- A white keyline border is allowed where needed for visual clarity on busy backgrounds.

**Notes:**

- These brand guidelines aim to ensure authentic, transparent, and effective communication of Mary’s Meals' mission.
- The aesthetics and use cases for the logo should maintain their integrity and adhere to defined rules.

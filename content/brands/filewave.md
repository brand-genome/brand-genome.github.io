## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The FileWave logo has the following configurations: full logo, symbol only, wordmark, and icon. The logo is designed to represent the visual identity of FileWave and must be treated as a company signature with strength and integrity.
- Logo versions: FileWave red and blue logo on white background, greyscale logo for higher quality B/W print reproduction, solid black logo for commercial printing (etching, stamping, engraving, embossing, etc.), and one-color versions if printing restrictions demand it. Reversed versions allowed on colored backgrounds.
- Clearance and composition: The logo must be surrounded by clear space equivalent to the x-height of the 'w' in 'FileWave'. Minimum acceptable print size is 1.5 inches; for digital, 120px.
- Do's and Don'ts: 1) Do not split logo type into two lines. 2) Do not invert or alter the logo symbol, type style, proportions, or size relationship between elements. 3) Avoid skewing or distorting the logo in any way.
- Logo with tagline version includes the tagline 'Where great minds are forged!' Tagline font is Poppins Regular. Co-branding uses a 1-point gray rounded-edge pipe for separation, with equal balance between co-branded logos.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: PANTONE 186C (CMYK: C 13, M 100, Y 90, K 3; RGB: R 205, G 32, B 48; HEX: #CD2030), PANTONE 2965 (CMYK: C 100, M 79, Y 48, K 53; RGB: R 0, G 38, B 62; HEX: #00263E).
- Secondary colors: PANTONE 187C (CMYK: C 23, M 100, Y 87, K 15; RGB: R 170, G 30, B 46; HEX: #AA1E2E), PANTONE 634C (CMYK: C 98, M 71, Y 21, K 5; RGB: R 0, G 85, B 139; HEX: #00558B), PANTONE 7465C (CMYK: C 66, M 0, Y 41, K 0; RGB: R 70, G 191, B 171; HEX: #46BFAB), PANTONE 155C (CMYK: C 4, M 21, Y 53, K 0; RGB: R 243, G 202, B 135; HEX: #F3CA87), and PANTONE 157C (CMYK: C 5, M 41, Y 83, K 0; RGB: R 238, G 161, B 70; HEX: #EEA146).
- Use bright, pure primary hues paired with neutral gray or secondary colors. Gradients are allowed using FileWave's color palette.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font for headlines, titles, and body text: Mr Eaves XL Mod OT (Regular and Bold). Substitute font for digital: Open Sans Regular. Default substitute: Helvetica Neue Light.
- Text hierarchy: Headlines use Mr Eaves XL Mod OT Bold (80pt, uppercase), subtitles use Mr Eaves XL Mod OT Regular (40pt, uppercase/lowercase), and body text uses Mr Eaves XL Mod OT Regular (10pt, 12 leading).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photographs should feature natural lighting and be expressive and eye-catching, communicating clear messages to support FileWave's technology-oriented subject matter.
- Black and white photography: Must have high contrast and desaturated colors, ensuring a professional look.
- Blending modes for images: Apply FileWave’s red and blue colors over black and white photography using gradients (e.g., in InDesign with overlay effects at 100%).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo co-branding requires a 1-point gray rounded-edge pipe for separation and equal balance between logos.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tagline: 'Where great minds are forged!'

**Notes:**

- Corporate icons are developed as a graphic tool to make content visually engaging and recognizable. Specific themes must align with the marketing guidelines.
- Social media guidelines emphasize proper image attribution, permission for event postings, and disclaimers when discussing FileWave online. Specific optimal image dimensions for various social media channels are outlined (e.g., 820x310 px for cover photos, 180x180 px for profile images).

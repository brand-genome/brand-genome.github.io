## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Artwork files should be reproduced in approved formats (EPS, PNG, JPEG) available through FFA resources. Modifications to logo are not permitted.
- Logo variations include stacked and linear formats. Orientation choice depends on design preference; stacked format is preferred.
- Specifications for minimum sizes: Stacked (15 mm), Linear (20 mm). Smaller reproduction compromises quality.
- Logo constructions use grids with spatial relationships defined in units. For alternative applications, grids must be maintained. Grid unit = 1/20 of logo width.
- Clear space/isolation area rules defined by a 1.5X height around the logo, ensuring no other text/graphics encroach.
- Incorrect usage includes scaling (e.g., vertical/horizontal), color alterations, rotations, combining or disassembling brand elements, cropping, and noisy or low contrast backgrounds.
- Logo can be embroidered with thread colors matching Pantone specifications (Madiera and Royal threads available). Specific plot sheets provided.
- Logo usage with photographic imagery is permitted if integrity and visibility are maintained; options provided for full color, single color, and reverse applications against high-contrast or simple backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors specified with Pantone, CMYK, RGB, and HEX values:
- Pantone 300 C (CMYK: C99 M50 Y0 K0, RGB: R0 G94 B184, HTML: #005EB8)
- Pantone 289 C (CMYK: C100 M76 Y12 K70, RGB: R12 G35 B64, HTML: #0C2340)
- Black (CMYK: C0 M0 Y0 K100, RGB: R45 G41 B38, HTML: #2D2926)
- White (CMYK: C0 M0 Y0 K0, RGB: R255 G255 B255, HTML: #FFFFFF)
- Pantone 2995 C (CMYK: C83 M1 Y0 K0, RGB: R0 G169 B224, HTML: #00A9E0).
- Logo gradients recommended for CMYK (print) and RGB (screen) applications. Flat Pantone files optimized for superior reproduction in scenarios where gradients are not feasible.

**Typography Information:**

- Typeface for official designation in composite logos is 'Gotham Bold' in uppercase and must not span more than one line.
- Typography for Division Logo template uses Gotham Bold and must fit one line, with point size proportional to base guidelines.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Logo approved formats (full color, single color, reversed) can be used on photographic imagery. Selection based on image complexity, background contrast, and visual compatibility.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Isolation area rules around logos and composite logos dictate clear space proportional to logo dimensions.
- Composite logo with partner branding must maintain equal spacing and step-and-repeat configurations on media backdrops.

**Brand Voice:**

- Logo story emphasizes unity across Australian football, represented with boomerang shapes and a progressive, dynamic visual language.
- Consistency in logo use is essential to maintain brand strength and loyalty across all applications.

**Notes:**

- All applications of the logo in any format must be submitted, reviewed, and approved in writing by Football Federation Australia before being distributed.
- Online resource files for artwork available through FFA account managers; typefaces and photographic imagery independently sourced.

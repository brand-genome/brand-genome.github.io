## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- Clear space around the Indian Motorcycle Riders Group® (IMRG) logo must be maintained at a minimum equivalent to the height of 'RIDERS GROUP'.
- Minimum size of IMRG logo is recommended at 1.5 inches in height; scaling is subject to approval, ensuring readability of all elements including '®'.
- Do's for IMRG logo customization include using approved colors, typefaces, and additional design elements while maintaining clear space around the logo.
- Don'ts for logo usage: Do not change logo colors, proportions, or create lockups by adding text or symbols to the logo. Do not deconstruct the logo or type.
- Incorrect usage examples include redesigning, combining, altering IMRG logo, or adding unauthorized text/symbols around the logo.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Color: Crimson Steed Red - PMS 202 C; CMYK: C:9, M:100, Y:64, K:48; RGB: R:134, G:38, B:51; HEX not provided.
- Secondary Color: Brown - PMS 476 C; CMYK: C:30, M:71, Y:75, K:81; RGB: R:78, G:54, B:41; HEX not provided.
- Secondary Color: Cream - PMS 155 C; CMYK: C:0, M:12, Y:34, K:0; RGB: R:239, G:209, B:159; HEX not provided.
- Secondary Color: Gold - PMS 131 C; CMYK: C:2, M:39, Y:100, K:10; RGB: R:204, G:138, B:0; HEX not provided.
- Secondary Color: Cool Gray - PMS Cool Gray 5; CMYK: C:13, M:9, Y:10, K:27; RGB: R:169, G:171, B:172; HEX not provided.
- Secondary Color: Bone - CMYK: C:0, M:1, Y:10, K:0; RGB: R:255, G:251, B:234; HEX not provided.

**Typography Information:**

- Body Copy Typeface: Rockwell
- Body Copy Use: Not recommended for feature font use (e.g., image descriptions, legal term details).
- Ornamental Copy Typeface: Knockout
- Headline Use Typeface: Specific approved typefaces with style settings for consistency.
- Headline Style Settings: ALL CAPS; Tracking: 25-50 points.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Exclusion zone or clear space around the IMRG logo must be equal to the height of 'RIDERS GROUP'. Minimum 1.5 inches in height for logo usage.

**Brand Voice:**

- The IMRG uses a tone of pride and exclusivity, emphasizing heritage and building brand character through riding.
- Messaging emphasizes IMRG's history, values, and a focus on community and developing the brand through shared experience.

**Notes:**

- Native American references, imagery, or iconography (e.g., arrows, headdresses) are strictly prohibited in communication and branding.
- Use of trademarks must not be combined or altered without Indian Motorcycle's full approval. Dealers and IMRG chapter presidents are limited to specific guidelines per agreements.
- All chapter customizations must adhere to approved colors and typefaces. Customizations are subject to review by Indian Motorcycle®.

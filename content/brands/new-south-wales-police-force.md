## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Corporate logo includes NSW Police Force crest, insignia, and logotype. Variants include: color logo (for full color communications), mono logo (for single color communications), badge logo (alternative full color for contemporary feel), silver foil logo (for formal applications). The slogan "With your help, a safer community" is not part of the logo but may be used adjacent to the co-branded design.
- Co-branded logo combines NSW Government and NSW Police Force designs with a defined keyline separating logos, used for external, advertising, and project communications. Variants include mono logo and project signage logo.
- Proud Traditions logo is reserved for historical purposes only, derived from foundation designs since 1862. Variants include color logo (full color), mono logo (black), badge logo (alternative full color, not for mono use).
- Chequered band (Sillitoe tartan) in blue and white (preferred three rows), or black, variants also include reverse tint backgrounds. Used in rectangular, square, or circular frames as a design styling.
- Clear space rule: Corporate logo and Proud Traditions logo require half-circle clear spacing. Co-branded logo clear space equals height of "N" in "NSW."
- Incorrect logo usage includes improper scaling/stretching, rotation, cropping, embellishments, pattern overlays, and color alterations.
- Minimum size reproduction for corporate logo is 20mm including clear space. For co-branded logo, it is 24mm. Use insignia alone if smaller dimensions are required.

**Color Information:**

- Logo color schemes: full color (preferable for internal and external communications), mono (black for non-color printing, applied to white/light backgrounds), reverse (white or reverse color against dark backgrounds).
- Corporate colors derived from logo designs including PMS, CMYK, and RGB values: Dark Blue (PMS 282, RGB 0, 30, 66), Blue (PMS 287, RGB 0, 48, 135), Gold (PMS 123, RGB 255, 199, 44), Green (PMS 341, RGB 0, 122, 83), Red (PMS 485, RGB 218, 41, 28), Dark Brown (PMS 476, RGB 78, 54, 41), Light Blue (PMS 2706, RGB 190, 202, 234), Brown (PMS 7531, RGB 122, 104, 85), Light Brown (PMS 7528, RGB 197, 185, 172), Black (Process Black RGB 0, 0, 0).

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate fonts for print communications: ITC Franklin Gothic (Book for body text, Demi for subtitles, alternatively Arial/Helvetica), ITC Franklin Gothic Book/Black used for headings.
- Web fonts recommendation: Default Sans Serif or Arial, respective bold versions for emphasis.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Chequered band styling applied preferably at bottom of communication (below logo). Background differentiation: color/badge logos appear on white/light tints (<20%), mono logo (<50%) must sit on blue/black or pale photo spaces; reverse logos (>50% tint dark colors, preferably black/blue).

**Brand Voice:**

- Corporate slogan: "With your help, a safer community" may be added near logos purely for marketing contexts.

**Notes:**

- Email signature block formatting is required for professionalism; HTML signature configurations accessible externally or internally for customization.

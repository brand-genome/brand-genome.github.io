## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Kansas State University has four primary visual marks: Kansas State University Wordmark, K-State Wordmark, Seal, and Spirit Mark.
- Kansas State University Wordmark is the primary mark. Alternate approved versions include PMS 268, reversed-to-white on PMS 268, reversed-to-white on black, 100 percent black, and 40 percent black. No other color combinations are allowed.
- Minimum size for print media is 1 inch wide; for electronic media, the logo must not be reproduced smaller than 72px wide. Clear space rules apply to maintain design integrity.
- Usage do's and don'ts for Kansas State University Wordmark: Do use approved versions, maintain proportions during scaling, and use in a way it stands out among other logos. Don't combine it with other graphical elements, re-create it, or outline it.
- K-State Wordmark is for limited use. It must not be used on university stationery, publications, websites, or communications. Approved color versions include PMS 268, reverse-to-white, 100% black, and 40% black.
- University Seal has two versions: General seal for broad use and President's Seal for restricted use. Permitted colors include PMS 268, black, and white. Seal can be cropped while maintaining proportional integrity.
- The Spirit Mark (Powercat) is the primary mark of K-State Athletics, Inc. It must appear in official university colors and stand as a singular graphic element, uncombined with other logos or visuals.

**Color Information:**

- Official Kansas State University colors include Pantone 268+ (Primary Purple) with HEX #512888, 100% Black (HEX #000000), 40% Black (HEX #A7A7A7), 20% Black (HEX #D1D1D1), and White (HEX #FFFFFF).
- Secondary color palette includes neutral and accent colors for printed materials. Example neutrals: Pantone 7528 (CMYK: C:0%, M:3%, Y:10%, K:10%) and Cool Gray 9 (CMYK: C:0%, M:0%, Y:1%, K:51%). Accent colors must not exceed 15% usage.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font is Myriad Pro, known for its clean, contemporary, and humanistic design. Common variants include Regular, Italic, Bold, and Bold Italic.
- Fallback font for online use is Lucida Sans when Myriad Pro is unavailable.

**Notes:**

- Trademark Licensing information mentions that approval for usage of marks is required via the Division of Communications and Marketing or Trademark Licensing Office.

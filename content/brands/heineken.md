## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The Heineken logo in full color with red star, Heineken wordmark, and green background.
- Clear spacing and unique logo alignment rules for Heineken brand logos.

**Color Information:**

- Heineken green (Pantone 347, RGB: 36, 142, 87) and red star (Pantone 485, RGB: 237, 28, 36).
- Complementary colors: Cool Grey and white (for logo backgrounds where needed).

**Typography Information:**

- Custom typeface for Heineken wordmark; rounded 'e' is emphasized, creating a distinct identity.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Guidelines on photography treatment: focus on natural and fresh imagery that emphasizes the premium quality and heritage aspects of the brand.

**Brand Voice:**

- Heineken maintains a consistent tone of authority, highlighting its premium quality and authenticity.

**Notes:**

- Historical origins of the green bottle and red star; significance rooted in brand heritage.
- Never use the 'smiling e' or other logo elements in isolation.

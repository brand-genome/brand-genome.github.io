## Brand Guidelines

### 2012

**Logo Information:**

- Full color marque (CMYK), Two-color marque (Gold: Pantone 873 or Hot foil Kurz 232 and Light Blue: Process Cyan), Single color marque (Pantone 873 or Hot foil Kurz 232), White marque (for photographic backgrounds), Black marque (for non-printing reproduction). The full color marque is the preferred version. Clear space is the height of the 'I' letter form.
- Marque minimum size requirements: 44mm (full-color marque), 33mm (two-color and one-color marque). Clear space area equals the height of the 'I' letter form.
- Stamp versions include full color, two-color, single color, white, and black, with detailed specifications. Black stamps should only be used for non-print reproduction. Minimum size for full color stamp: 26mm, two-color and one-color stamps minimum size: 20mm. Clear space is equal to the height of 'I'.

**Color Information:**

- Brand colors include: Dark Blue (Pantone 2755, RGB: R35 G0 B105, CMYK: C100 M90 Y0 K40), Mid Blue (Pantone 2736, RGB: R40 G40 B170, CMYK: C90 M80 Y0 K0), Light Blue (Process Cyan, RGB: R0 G160 B228, CMYK: C100 M0 Y0 K0), Gold (Pantone 873/Hot Foil Kurz 232, RGB: R160 G140 B100, CMYK: C5 M25 Y70 K10).
- Proportional color palette: Dark Blue 75%, Mid Blue 15%, Gold 9%, Light Blue 1%.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface is a custom cut of Jonathan Barnbrook's Priori Sans. Priori Bold should be used for headlines and sub-headlines, preferably in capitals or where emphasis is needed. Priori Italic should be used for names, credits, and quotations. Priori Regular should be used for long copy. Digital typeface: Verdana (system font). Detailed hierarchy for Priori Sans weights provided: Regular, Italic, Light, Bold, Black.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Marque clear space must be equal to the height of the 'I' letterform. Marque must not be crowded by any graphic, pattern, or typography.
- Stamp clear space must equal the height of the 'I' letterform. Stamp must always stand out prominently.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone emphasizes contemporary, bold, inspiring, and audacious communication. Messaging focuses on sophistication and standing out with unique style and taste.

**Notes:**

- Chivas 18 Guidelines focus on a modern luxury style. All brand materials should convey audacity and originality while respecting the brand's consistency across channels.
- Contact information for the brand manager: Tom Dusseldorp, International Senior Brand Manager, Chivas Regal 25 & Chivas 18.

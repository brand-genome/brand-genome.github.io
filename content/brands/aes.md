## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- AES Logo Usage Guide VERSION 1.1   |  14 April 2021
- After many years of faithful service, the famous AES shield has received some non-invasive plastic surgery. A fresh face for 2021. Cleaner, more concise yet still instantly recognizable and unique.
- Sub logos: Over the years the original AES shield has been used as part of a vast number of sub logos, for events, workshops, affiliations and so on. AES members can create these using the AES shield left-aligned and titles neatly balanced alongside. Roboto is preferred but creative use of fonts is allowed.
- Custom branding guidelines for events using an AES shield with text and an emphasis on creative designs while following basic rules.

- Prohibition against using multiple colors for core logos unless it's marked as custom branding.

**Typography Information:**

- Fonts: The AES font is Roboto – Regular or Bold. Available weights: Light 300, Regular 400, Medium 500, Bold 700. Italics to be used minimally: Regular Italics 400, Bold Italics 700.
- Sub logos: Defaults to Roboto with creative font usage if necessary. Maintain proportionality and weight as per examples.
- Custom branding allows for flexibility with fonts but prohibits ornate, script, or handwritten styles.
- Guidelines prohibit novelty fonts and emphasize clean text for brand continuity.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Logo placement emphasizes clear areas with a height of the shield (‘A’ height) around it. Positioning flexibility: corners, centered, or aligned to main content.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand guidelines emphasize a tone of simplicity, consistency, and coherence. Short messages, strong contrast, and clean visuals are recommended.

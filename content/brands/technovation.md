## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Three main logo configurations provided: Primary Lockup, Roundel Mark Only, and Stacked Lockup. Represents paths entwined on the Technovation journey: girl, mentor, sponsor.
- Logo color treatments specified: 2-color preferred treatment used for positive/reverse application, and 1-color option using corporate Shamrock green or white.
- Clear space margin rules: equal to one-half of the large circle in the symbol. Minimum dimensions for logos specified: Primary Lockup must be at least 1.25 inches wide; Stacked Lockup must be 0.7 inches wide.
- Do's and Don'ts for logo usage: Do not change font, colors, directions, separate elements, or violate clear space rules. Examples of incorrect implementations: rotating logo, altering font, changing placement, outlining logo, using unapproved colors, removing elements, and placing it on improper backgrounds.
- Specific World Pitch event logo provided, including a Chapter Lockup methodology for adding location to the logo using Eagle Book font.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors provided: Shamrock (Pantone 2247 C, HEX #5ABF94), Mulberry (Pantone 7640 C, HEX #903D54), Jade (Pantone 7723 C, HEX #28A880), Boysenberry (Pantone 7642 C, HEX #7C324D), Pthalo (Pantone 349 C, HEX #006540), Rosewood (Pantone 690 C, HEX #53042D).
- Supporting neutral colors provided: Light Grey (Pantone 413, HEX #ECECEC), Laser Lemon (Pantone 379, HEX #EAE46F), Silver (Pantone Cool Gray 10, HEX #666666), Mid Grey (Pantone 414 C, HEX #CCCCCC), Citrine (Pantone 583 C, HEX #C1C321), Granite (Pantone 446 C, HEX #444444), Dark Grey (Pantone 415 C, HEX #878787), Chalkboard (Pantone 419 C, HEX #333333).
- Supporting accent colors provided: Wisteria (Pantone 5135 C, HEX #895E86), Marine (Pantone 325 C, HEX #4BC2C6), Antiqua (Pantone 5126 C, HEX #6F466B), Sea (Pantone 326 C, HEX #00ABAD), Byzantium (Pantone 262 C, HEX #421B41), Oceana (Pantone 7721 C, HEX #006667).
- Patterns guidelines: Primary and secondary patterns used to convey different feelings and enhance design messaging. Patterns must match their specific brand color and should not be rotated or scaled dramatically.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography guidelines: Eagle Book (primary headline font, title-case only) and Source Sans Pro (subheads in all caps and body copy, with full family weights).
- Source Sans Pro supports a wide range of Latin-based languages, useful for translation purposes.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Ideally, branding design occupies at least 50% of chosen documents or promotional materials, with grey space for body copy on the backside or any desired imagery. Minimum state occupies at least 25% of the document.
- Preferred placement for logos in non-marketing printed digital documents (e.g., letterhead, contracts, guides): primary lockup or horizontal alignment outside central document space reserved for content/text.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand tone descriptors include: fun, energetic, empowering, smart, bold, encouraging, inspiring. Excludes: exclusive, boring, timid, limiting.
- Key brand mantra: focus on empowering girls in technology by teaching them entrepreneurial and app development skills.

**Notes:**

- Guidelines specify marketing collateral examples: use strong brand colors (Shamrock and Mulberry), header font Eagle, subhead/body font Source Sans Pro.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The SPD logo consists of three characters 'S', 'P', 'D' written in a bold, high visibility style within a red square as their primary visual identity. A protective space (Schutzzone) around the logo is defined as 3A where no other graphic elements are permitted. Minimum size specifications are provided: 5x5mm for small giveaways and 10x10mm for print materials. Color variations include grayscale versions that apply when printing in black and white.
- Logo file formats available for download are PDF, EPS, and JPEG. These files can be customized using the Druckportal and downloaded individually.

**Color Information:**

- The SPD brand uses a distinct palette: Primary: SPD Red (CMYK: 0-100-100-0, RGB: 227-0-15, HTML/CSS: #E3000F, Pantone 185 C/U, HKS 14), White (CMYK: 0-0-0-0, RGB: 255-255-255, HTML/CSS: #FFFFFF). Secondary: Dark Red (CMYK: 30-100-70-0, RGB: 182-28-62, HTML/CSS: #B61C3E), Cyan Blue (CMYK: 74-0-20-0, RGB: 0-180-204, HTML/CSS: #00B4CC). Accent color: Purpur (CMYK: 28-100-0-25, RGB: 152-2-103, HTML/CSS: #980267). Color applications include CMYK for print and RGB/HTML for digital use. Specific rules dictate the dominance of SPD Red in all color schemes, with accompanying secondary colors sparingly applied.

**Typography Information:**

- The house typeface is SPD TheSans, with primary weights being Extra Bold and Regular. SPD TheSans Versal is used for large, impactful headlines (Extra Bold) and sublines (Regular Italic). Copy text employs SPD TheSans Regular. Font sizes and line spacing vary depending on format and text volume (e.g., 24pt font size paired with 25pt line height).
- For office communication or open documents, the default typeface is Calibri.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Images must appear authentic, natural, and close to real-life experiences. Subjects should showcase human-centered activities in dynamic yet relaxed scenarios. The emphasis lies on natural tones and lighting without artificial editing. Red elements in the imagery subtly reinforce brand identity.
- Avoid over-edited images, unnatural compositions, overly colorful elements, and distracting perspectives.

**Notes:**

- The manual specifies that temporary campaign designs may differ from these guidelines and are to be managed separately.

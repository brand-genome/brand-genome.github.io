## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Color Information:**

- Primary color used in branding: Pantone 186 C / RAL 3027 (potentially red). This is explicitly cited for façade signage.
- Use of optical white for interior elements such as directories and signage indicated as PANTONE.

**Typography Information:**

- The family 'Roboto' is specified with the variants Regular, Italic, Medium, Bold, and Bold Italic. Fonts are freely accessible from Google Fonts (https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Roboto).

**Notes:**

- The manual includes extensive guidelines for vehicle branding, uniform design (Parks, Jackets, and Shirts), and external signage.

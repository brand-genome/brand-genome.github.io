## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- GOAT logo designed using typographic grid & circumferences. Logotype highlights sustainability, emphasizing a cycle for reducing food waste.
- Black & White logo versions (Dark and Light Versions) included.
- Clearspace defined with construction grid. Matrix circumference value: 2X; grid by X/4. Clearspace ensures visibility.
- Minimum logo sizes: Regular logo 6mm height; Logo with slogan 6mm height, minimum slogan font size: Montserrat 4pts.
- Incorrect logo use examples: Don't use primary colors as monochromatic, modify slogan/tagline position, apply gradient incorrectly, or use primary colors as backgrounds.
- Logo with slogan versions: Regular, Extended, and Compact, emphasizing 'SHARE_EAT_REPEAT'.
- Logo can be applied in full color, black & white, and reverse on colored backgrounds. Variations include gradients and primary background colors like green, orange, and red-orange.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: Montserrat, a modern Sans Serif with geometric simplicity. Open source licensed.
- Logo typography: Custom-designed using circumferenced grid for geometric balance, represents stakeholder dynamics.
- Example text showcasing Montserrat font usage in uppercase and lowercase: SHARE_EAT_REPEAT.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Images should allow free space for branding; GOAT logo use recommended in full color. Images can blend with secondary grey background when superimposing logos (blending at 85%-100%).
- For presentations, avoid exceeding two-thirds of the layout with images. Light gray (black 10%) is allowed as a background.
- Blending secondary colors ensures easier logo readability in photography settings.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Business card dimensions: 3.5" x 2" or 9x5cm. Front side includes person details, back side includes logo over dark grey.
- Presentation dimensions: Widescreen 13.333 x 7.5 inches (default resolution 1280x720 pixels). Recommendation to emphasize visuals with large support text; minimum font size 22pts for text boxes.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand communicates sustainability and fostering community through food-sharing cycles. 'SHARE_EAT_REPEAT' conveys goal and messaging consistency.
- Use clear, bold typography (Montserrat ExtraBold) for emphasis in displaying facts, statistics, and charts.

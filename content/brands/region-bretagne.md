## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The logotype has a square shape. It contains an ermine, a strong symbolic icon for the territory. The ermine symbolizes the geographical shape of Brittany and its three points represent the region’s many coastal islands. The ascending aspect of the ermine signifies the dynamic nature of the region, resembling a shooting star. Additionally, the refined curves are reminiscent of floral patterns, reflecting the institution's commitment to environmental preservation.

**Color Information:**

- The defined color palette includes 24 tones based on 8 hues, each with three different luminosities. The color codes include CMYK (for print with four-color process), Pantone (for direct color printing), RGB (for office applications), HTML (for web use), and RAL (for signage). Black or white squares indicate which tone contrasts better for text on colored backgrounds and ensures greater accessibility in HTML design.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: 'Region Bretagne'. It contains different font weights, including Black, Bold, Medium, Regular, Thin, Condensed Regular, and other variations like Alternate BoldItalic and Alternate Ornaments.
- Secondary font: 'Abril'. This serif font is presented in several styles, including Regular, Semi-Bold Italic, Fatface Regular, and ornaments. It’s available via distributors like Typetogether and Adobe Typekit.

**Imagery Photography:**

- The pictogram library includes icons for various categories: transportation (e.g., TGV, TER, roads, cars, trucks, ships), buildings (e.g., schools, hospitals, churches, villages), facilities (e.g., cafeteria, camping, parking), and symbols representing geographical or cultural landmarks like dolmens, menhirs, and historical sites.

**Spacing Layout:**

- For compositions, the rule specifies that the square logo must occupy 10% to 15% of the smaller side of the format, depending on the width-to-height ratio and visual balance. The logo is always placed on a quarter-circle (cropped) positioned in corners. The circle's center is placed at the corner of the composition, while the logo’s center is aligned with the curve of the circle, at a 45-degree axis from the center.

**Brand Voice:**

- The branding strategy for Brittany aims to enhance the territory's attraction nationally and internationally. The goal is to distinguish Brittany’s identity, charm visitors, investors, students, and professionals, and position the region as a dynamic and recognizable brand on a global scale.

**Notes:**

- The graphic charter's objective is to strengthen the identity of communication support materials for the Regional Council of Brittany and make them visually more homogeneous. The visual identity consists of several elements: 1) A logotype placed on a base (quarter-circle or third-circle shape), 2) Two complementary typefaces ('Region Bretagne' and 'Abril'), and 3) A color palette (24 tones). The base is optional for promotional objects (e.g., textiles, gadgets, etc.) but is required for other materials.

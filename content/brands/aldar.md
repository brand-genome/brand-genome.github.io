## Brand Guidelines

### 2024

**Logo Information:**

- The logo includes a square frame as an integral part and should not be detached from the logotype.
- Minimum size requirements for the logo: 18mm for print. 50% clear space must surround the logo at all times.
- Logo color variations: Full color, black, white options are mentioned; details of colors are in the color palette section.
- The frame and logo elements must always remain connected and proportional. The logo cannot be distorted, rotated, or altered.
- Logo should always remain legible and must appear against a contrasting background.
- Incorrect usages of the logo: Do not detach the frame, do not apply effects, do not distort or rotate the logo, do not use without sufficient contrast, and do not use the logo without the frame component.

**Color Information:**

- The color palette is inspired by the UAE's natural diversity. It consists of neutral hues and architectural tones for a clean and simple look.
- Primary colors include Black (HEX: #000000, CMYK: C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:100), White (HEX: #FFFFFF, CMYK: C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:100), and various shades inspired by architectural materials, such as 'Clay', 'Ceramic', 'Concrete', and 'Beige'.
- Secondary colors, labeled as dynamic tones, include various blues, greens, reds, and beige hues with specified HEX, RGB, and CMYK values for each.
- Color usage guidelines: Dynamic tones can be blended with primary colors for an energetic effect.

**Typography Information:**

- The brand's English typeface is 'Poppins', with font weights: Thin, Extra Light, Regular, Bold, Extra Bold.
- Poppins Thin and Poppins Regular are the default weights for English-language communications. Other weights are reserved for specific use cases.
- The brand's Arabic typeface is 'Almarai,' primarily using both Light and Bold weights.
- Instructions for downloading and installing the English (Poppins) and Arabic (Almarai) fonts on Mac and Windows are provided.

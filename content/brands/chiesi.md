## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The Chiesi logo has a customized logotype, with variations in primary, reverse, black, and white. The colors include Pantone 227C, Black C, Bright White, and Cool Grays. Available formats include Illustrator, EPS, and high-resolution PNG.
- Clear space or 'breathing space' for logo requires one icon width minimum. Minimum size is 100 pixels wide. Guidelines provided for greyscale and watermark versions.
- Do's and Don'ts for logo usage: Use specified variations (primary, reverse, white), avoid stretching, recoloring, stacking, or using icon/logotype alone.
- White logo recommended for image backgrounds with low contrast; avoid busy or non-brand colors.
- Detailed ARC Logo guidelines: Includes 'Air, Rare, Care' pillars. Minimum size is 200 pixels wide.
- Brand Pillar Logos: Specific logos for Air, Care, and Rare with detailed brand guidelines. Rare logo development is ongoing.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Pantone 227C (Chiesi Pink). Additional structural and secondary colors provided: Cool Greys, Pantone 7530C, 7679C, 2111C, etc., with CMYK, RGB, and HEX codes.
- White is the main background color. Black is used for text and highlights.

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate fonts: 'Gotham Narrow' (formal), 'Verdana Pro Light' (informal), 'Montserrat' (web). Use Gotham Narrow for formal communications, Verdana for everyday use.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery emphasizes human-centric emotive visuals to reflect brand values. Use clean white space and simple color palettes for sophistication.
- For B Corp communications, vector illustrations and handwritten fonts are permissible in addition to imagery guidelines.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo clear spacing requires at least one icon width around the logo. Maintain generous margins and clean layouts.

**Brand Voice:**

- The Chiesi brand voice is reflected through a professional, clean, and customer-oriented appearance, emphasizing trust and care.
- B Corp branding includes a focus on ethics, social responsibility, and incorporating human-centric design to reinforce their values.

**Notes:**

- No unauthorized departmental logos may be created or used without approval. Contacts specified for logo or branding queries.
- B Corp certification logo has distinct branding guidelines available in multiple languages.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Primary logo composed of two elements: Symbol and Wordmark. Symbol and Wordmark can be used separately but must follow guidelines.
- Approved logo variations: Secondary Logo, Reduced Logo, and Stacked Lockup.
- Clear space rules: Minimum clear space around the logo is defined as 4X, where X comes from the width of the symbol.
- Monochrome and reversed versions of the logo are approved for usage.
- Minimum size requirements: For print, the logo must be at least 1 cm. For digital, it must be at least 25 px.
- Logo usage 'Don'ts': Do not change colors, rotate, alter element order, use outlines, use effects or gradients, or distort the logo.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette: Shades and tints of #EF3148. Additional colors include #333333 (CMYK: 69|63|62|58), #999999 (CMYK: 43|35|35|01).
- RGB and CMYK codes provided for primary colors including red variants (#EF3148, #F03047, etc.).

**Typography Information:**

- Font family: Montserrat. Available weights: Black, Bold, Regular, Light, Thin.
- Typography sample provided with uppercase, lowercase, and numeric characters.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Brand applications display examples of how logos and colors are applied on items such as business cards, polo shirts, hats, water bottles, and other collateral.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rules specify 4X measurement around the logo to maintain visual clarity and consistency.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand described as data-driven, leveraging location-based technology and mobile analytics to connect brands with innovative ad placements and meaningful experiences.

**Notes:**

- Version 1.0 of brand guidelines created in June 2019.
- Examples of brand applications include stationaries and promotional items like polo shirts, hats, and water bottles.

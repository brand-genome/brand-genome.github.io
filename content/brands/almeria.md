## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- ESCUDO: The logo of the Ayuntamiento de Almería (coat of arms) retains key historical elements such as the inclusion of the city's maritime history and cultural aspects. The guidelines emphasize that the escudo should strictly adhere to provided original vector files and must not be redrawn.
- Main color: The escudo uses duochrome colors (yellow on the top and red for the frame). Alternative versions include complete black, white, positive, and negative coloring. The colors are provided in specific formats: Pantone, CMYK, RGB, and HTML for display consistency.
- Minimum Size Requirements: The minimum width for reproduction of the escudo is 5mm or 14 pixels for visibility, and in exceptional cases, this can be decreased to 3mm or 8.5 pixels for strictly controlled cases such as merchandising.
- Clear Space Requirements: An area of respect (safe zone) around the escudo is specified for maintaining its visibility and ensuring no other elements intrude. It is precisely defined in terms of dimensions (X width).
- The escudo must always appear on a white rectangle when placed on colored backgrounds or images. The minimum dimensions for the rectangle should correspond to the area of respect.
- Do's and Don'ts: Examples of both proper and improper uses of the escudo are detailed, such as avoiding stretching, tilting, recoloring, or adding effects.
- Horizontal Marca Institucional: The marca institutional (horizontal form) represents the Ayuntamiento of Almería and is used when the city is the primary institution in communication. It consists of the escudo and the wordmark in the specified typography. Safe zones and minimum sizes are defined.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors of the brand include:
 - Pantone 485 C (Red): CMYK: C:0% M:100% Y:100% K:0%; RGB: R:213 G:43 B:30; HEX: #D52B1E
 - Pantone 123 C (Yellow): CMYK: C:0% M:23% Y:91% K:0%; RGB: R:255 G:198 B:39; HEX: #FFC627
- Alternate colors include a grayscale version (80% black), represented both in positive and negative contrasts. Specific formats of these colors are shared as CMYK, RGB, and HEX codes.
- Contrast rules for color: Positive color should be used on backgrounds with luminosity over 40%, and negative colors for luminosity under 40%. Yellow (Pantone 123 C) is not used in negative contrast.

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate typeface for branding elements: Helvetica Neue family used for institutional wordmarks and uneditable elements:
 - Helvetica Neue 55 Roman
 - Helvetica Neue 45 Light
 - Helvetica Neue 65 Medium
 - Helvetica Neue 75 Bold
 - Helvetica Neue 56 Italic
- Complementary Typeface: Roboto is used for general communications. It is a free-to-use font available on Google Fonts. Alternate typeface: Arial when Roboto is unavailable. Several styles are specified for Roboto including Light, Regular, Medium, and Bold variations with their italic counterparts. Similar weights are available for Arial alternatives.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style and theme emphasizes the essence of Almería's cultural diversity and identity as a Mediterranean welcoming city. Imagery is recommended to convey a modern, inclusive, and open atmosphere.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Grid and spacing rules ensure proper alignment and cohesion in branding. Safe zones around logos and typographical elements are specified for visual clarity.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice is meant to reflect inclusivity, modernity, cultural openness, and Almería's long-standing history of more than 1,000 years.

**Notes:**

- File formats for digital output are specified, including CMYK (print) and RGB/HEX (screen), ensuring consistency for all implementations of colors.
- The escudo and institutional wordmarks are mandated to never be altered digitally or redrawn. Only official digital assets should be used for reproduction.

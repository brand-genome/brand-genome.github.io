## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Main logo consists of a logotype and a symbol. It includes a Portrait version (vertical layout) for default use and a Landscape version (horizontal layout) for horizontal formats. Brand proportions should be maintained regardless of size, and no modifications are allowed.
- Official versions of the logo exist for different background types: FULL COLOUR version (for light and dark backgrounds), GREYSCALE version (for light and dark backgrounds), FLAT 1 COLOUR version (black on white or white on black), and FLAT PANTONE version (for reproductions without appropriate color capabilities).
- Clear space rules: No elements can be placed in the clear area around the logo. Measurements are depicted on page.
- Minimum size requirements: Specific minimum dimensions ensure the integrity and legibility of the brand and must be respected. There's no maximum magnification limit.
- Unauthorized uses: Examples of incorrect usage include changing colors, deforming the shape, adding effects, rotating the logo, and not adhering to safe zones.

**Color Information:**

- Full Color specifications: CMYK (100/65/0/40), RGB (26/62/114), HEX (#1a3e72) for blue. White is CMYK (0/0/0/0), RGB (255/255/255), HEX (#ffffff). These are official background and logo colors for the Conmebol brand.
- Pantone 2935 C blue for flat color version. Additional specifications include RAL (5005) and VINYL (3630-127 for translucent films). White specifications listed as RAL (9003) and VINYL (3635-20B for light management films).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Typeface: Branding (designed by Alfonso García, distributed by Latinotype). A modern sans-serif spurless typeface with medium-large x-height, straight curves, and convex terminals. Font weights: Light, Light Italic, Medium, Bold, Medium Italic, Bold Italic.
- Secondary Typeface (Digital Platforms): Ubuntu (designed by Dalton Maag). Weights: Light, Light Italic, Medium, Bold, Medium Italic, Bold Italic.
- Support Typeface: Arial including variants like Arial, Arial Bold, Arial Italic, Arial Bold Italic, and Arial Black.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Contrast requirement for random image backgrounds: The official brand version must respect the contrast between the logo and the background.
- Official Backgrounds: Gradient and Flat CMYK versions specified. Gradient CMYK specifications include CMYK (100/65/0/40), RGB (26/62/114), HEX (#1a3e72). Flat CMYK version uses CMYK (100/60/0/0), RGB (0/102/179), HEX (#0066b3). These are the two backgrounds approved for full-color version.
- Random color backgrounds: When applied, the contrast must be respected to ensure recognition.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rules: Protection area specified around logos to ensure no element encroaches on the space.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand Essence: Emphasizes emotional connection, defining a bold and diplomatic brand vision that integrates emotional storytelling ('It’s time to play for what keeps fans alive. Emotions.'), belief-driven initiatives ('It’s time to make the believers, and non-believers believe.'), and challenges against stereotypes ('It’s time to break the portrait of despondency').
- Tagline: 'Cree en Grande' ('Believe in Greatness'), emphasizing identity, empowerment, and South America's connection to football.

**Notes:**

- The brand is a representation of uniqueness based on South American football. Features such as a fingerprint and ball silhouette help connect identity and sports culture.
- Design endorsement guides for competitions include maintaining consistent proportions and visual identity for events in any city or location and include specifications for layouts with sponsors.
- Application examples include stadium banners, flags, stationary, and merchandise designs.

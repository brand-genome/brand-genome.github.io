## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- Clear space must equal the height of the 'U' in 'UN-Habitat', maintained on all sides.
- Minimum size rule ensures logo remains legible, no smaller dimensions provided explicitly.
- Examples of incorrect logo usage include distortion, rotation, color change, and poor placement over illegible backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand color is Pantone 306C, CMYK: 72, 5, 4, 0; RGB: 0, 178, 227; HEX: #00B2E3.
- Secondary accent colors include Pantone 360C (CMYK: 62, 0, 86, 0; RGB: 106, 191, 75; HEX: #6ABF4B) and others used for calls to action and infographics.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font is Roboto, available in weights: Light, Regular, Bold, Semibold, and ExtraBold. Supports multiple languages except Arabic and Chinese. Calibri is the system font fallback.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography focuses on human interactions, landscapes, and authentic portrayals, avoiding staged images. Accompany photos with descriptive captions.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Margins must adhere to minimum specifications; no visual elements placed too close to edges of banners or materials.

**Brand Voice:**

- Vision: 'A better quality of life for all in an urbanizing world.' Mission: Promote transformative change in cities to leave no one and no place behind.

**Notes:**

- Brand guidelines emphasize consistency across templates, including downloadable assets such as PowerPoint slides and email signatures.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The Versatile logo is the fifth trademark in the company history, designed as a modernized iteration of versions from 1977 to 1987. Known also as 'The Wings,' consistency in all applications is crucial, and alternative variations are strictly prohibited.
- Incorrect uses of the logo include reversing colors, unapproved color usage, stretching/distorting, rotating (except 90°), or altering the relationship between logo and wordmark.
- Buffer zone specified to ensure no interference. Minimum size for horizontal format is 1.25 inches wide, and vertical format is 1 inch wide. Buffer zone defined by 'X' proportions around logo.
- Two-color logo usage for high-quality printing (300 dpi, glossy, four-color process). Logos suitable for cream or white background when printing in full color.
- One-color logo variants for black and white printing. Examples include red wordmark logo (red only), reverse (white) logo for black backgrounds, or yellow logo variants. Specific rules are provided for background percentages and color combinations.

**Color Information:**

- Versatile uses three primary colors in its scheme: 'Versatile Red' as Process Colors (C:12, M:100, Y:90, K:4), 'Rich Black' as C:75, M:68, Y:67, K:90, and 'Versatile Yellow' as C:0, M:33, Y:92, K:2. Various RGB and HEX codes correspond: Red (RGB: 204, 0, 0 / HEX: #CC0000), Yellow (RGB: 255, 168, 16 / HEX: #FFA810). Pantones 186 for red and 130 for yellow.

**Typography Information:**

- Myriad Pro Regular is an Adobe OpenType font, chosen for its availability in various weights and styles. It ensures a timeless and fresh look.
- Myriad Pro Black is used exclusively in all caps for page heads and headlines to convey power and strength.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The buffer zone dictates the minimum distance around the logo, ensuring clear visibility without interference. Calculated as proportional to logo size.
- Horizontal and vertical signage layouts shown, ensuring brand consistency at dealer locations.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand represents power, sturdiness, and longevity to align with the name 'Versatile.' Strength and reliability are key tones communicated by logos, colors, typography, trade names, and product hierarchies.
- Versatile emphasizes brand quality, immediate customer recognition, and presents the company as a quality choice.

**Notes:**

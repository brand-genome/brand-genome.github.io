## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The logo includes brand marks and lockups. Guidelines for logo usage include clear space rules, usage over simple images or textures, and avoiding overly busy backgrounds. Don't crop, distort, recolor, embellish, or use old logos without proper approval. An approved grayscale version is available upon request.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font family: Comfortaa. Available weights include Bold (for headings), SemiBold, Medium, Regular, and Light. All caps allowed for subheadings with 200pt tracking. The font is free on Google Fonts.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Avoid placing logos on busy or cluttered backgrounds or colors that clash with the logo's design. Use simple images or textures that complement the logo.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space and lockup guidelines specified for logo use. Logos should respect safe zones and clear space requirements.

**Brand Voice:**

- Sezzle promotes the idea of spreading payments with no interest and a focus on financial well-being. Avoid associating the brand with gambling, sexual/violent material, overspending, unmanageable debt, criminal activity, or consumers under 18. Official tone emphasizes ease of use, transparency, and responsible financial practices.
- Taglines approved for use include: 'Sezzle, The Way Forward' and 'Buy Now, Pay Later with Sezzle.' Educational messaging emphasizes 4 interest-free payments over 6 weeks with no impact on credit.

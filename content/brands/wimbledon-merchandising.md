## Brand Guidelines

### 2011

**Logo Information:**

- The Wimbledon brand uses two main logos: the 'Crossed Rackets' logo (event logo for suppliers, broadcasters, merchandising licensees) and the 'Flying W' logo (reserved for merchandising and lifestyle products). These logos are trademarked, and applications outside their designated contexts require approval.
- The Crossed Rackets logo has three variations based on size: small (below 20mm diameter), regular (20mm - 300mm diameter), and large (above 300mm diameter). A minimum size of 12mm diameter is recommended for all applications.
- The 'Flying W' logo has one standard artwork but allows for reproduction in various colors; these variations must be approved case by case. Built-in white keyline ensures legibility on different backgrounds.
- There are two simplified options for the 'Flying W' logo to offer greater flexibility for merchandise applications.
- The Wimbledon logotype can be used separately or incorporated with either the Crossed Rackets or 'Flying W' logos. The logotype must be scaled proportionally and adhere to the same principles as standalone logos.
- Do's and Don'ts for logos: Do not create new variations of the 'Flying W' logo, alter logo element relationships, or distort logos.

**Color Information:**

- Core colors are Wimbledon Green (Pantone 349 C, R0 G102 B51, C100 M0 Y91 K42) and Wimbledon Purple (Pantone 268 C, R84 G0 B139, C82 M100 Y0 K12). White should be used alongside the primary colors to add freshness and modernity.

**Typography Information:**

- The core brand typeface is Gotham, chosen for its clean, legible, and classic appearance. It includes weights: Thin, Light, Book, Medium, and Bold, along with italic versions. Custom typefaces for products require prior Club approval.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The regular branding stripes (Standard or Thin) can be used vertically or horizontally. Green should primarily be to the left of purple (vertically) or above (horizontally), though exceptions may apply.

**Brand Voice:**

- Wimbledon brand identity reflects 130 years of British sporting tradition, focusing on qualities like heritage, quality, and elegance, with a tone matching its prestigious image.

**Notes:**

- All proposed artwork, including products, packaging, and materials, must be submitted to IMG (the Club’s licensing agent) for approval before production. Final samples must also be approved.

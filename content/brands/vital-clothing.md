**Logo Information:**

- Full logo and symbol-only versions are detailed.
- Logo monograms are depicted in lockup formats.
- Logo correct usage instructions: Always a single color, set to a corner no less than 0.5 inches from the nearest edge in ads, covering no more than 5% of the size.
- Logo color rules: White when background is 50% grey or higher; black otherwise; logo may be in any brand-specified color or tint, including tints of background colors.
- Incorrect usage instructions: Logo may not use more than one color, exhibit a color logo against a color background, or have logotype placed anywhere apart from under the logo.
- Incorrect usage details: Logo should not be in a color or tint not specified by the manual, stretched, flipped, outlined, or dominate the design of the print ad.

**Typography Information:**

- Font family: Zona Pro. Weights shown: Light, Bold, Extra Bold.
- Typography hierarchy: Headline text styled in Zona Pro Light, justified, with final line left-aligned. Bold can separate sections. Hyphenation disabled, type size ranges from 10 to 18 points, varying tracking depending on layout space, readable leading at approximately 19pt.
- Subtitles styled lowercase and bold. Names in Light Italic, occupations in Bold Italic.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Business card specification: Card size 3.5" x 2"; logo size 1.3" x 0.7"; Zona Pro Bold (9pt), Light (7pt); UV spot printing applied for slash graphics; angled line at 58 degrees.

- Letterhead details: Standard size 8.5" x 11"; sidebar 3" x 11"; logo size 2" x 1"; UV spot printing applied for side graphic.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand tone: Minimalist yet elegant, focusing on necessary and desired simplicity in fashion. Appeals to women seeking elegance and comfort in every design.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- The logo represents the unity of the Grand Lyon, Saint-Étienne Métropole, Viennagglo, and Capi Porte de l’Isère under one brand (Pôle Métropolitain). Its central structure combines these four entities visually and uses a dynamic typeface with an 'Egyptian' style for a cultural touch.
- The logo includes a primary block labeled 'Pôle Métropolitain' and features an oblique axis signifying development, mobility, and positive growth.
- Clear space/safe zone rule: Ensure a protection zone equivalent to 'h' height around the logo.
- Minimum logo utilization size set to 30 mm.
- Incorrect color usage includes using colors not consistent with designated branding (specified further in examples).
- Approved usage: On dark-colored backgrounds, the logo should be white with 'Pôle Métropolitain' text in black. On light-colored backgrounds, the logo should be black with 'Pôle Métropolitain' text in white.
- Do not distort, skew, or alter the logo proportions or its elements, as noted in examples of incorrect applications.

**Color Information:**

- Logo primary color: Pantone 254 C (HEX: #981D97, RGB: R:152 G:29 B:151). It symbolizes a federation of different regional colors.
- Secondary color for logo type: Pantone Process Black (HEX: #000000, RGB: R:0 G:0 B:0).
- CMYK for Pantone primary color: Cyan 52, Magenta 96, and Black 100.
- Rules for adjustment: Always ensure sufficient contrast of the logo between background colors, alternating between black and white text as required.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography for the logo is described as 'Egyptian' in inspiration, lending a cultural and robust character. The typeface uses multiple weights and dynamic styles for visual energy.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The logo must maintain consistent spacing referred to as 'h' height as the protection zone.
- Ensure alignment of the oblique axis with logo elements for coherence.

**Notes:**

- Violet was chosen as the primary logo color because it synthesizes the regional colors while staying unique to the Federation.

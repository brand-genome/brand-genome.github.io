## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- FLIPBOARD BASIC BRAND GUIDELINES 2017
These guidelines are accompanied by a brand assets toolkit, downloadable at flip.it/logos, where you’ll find the latest logos, app icons, and colors to use in your materials. For questions concerning the use of Flipboard brand assets, or to inquire about a co-marketing opportunity, contact guidelines@flipboard.com. DOWNLOAD THE LOGO KIT: FLIP.IT/LOGOS
- Origins: Our name is inspired by the idea of setting things in motion, by the technology and information that keeps the world moving forward. Our identity represents the place where ideas and perspectives are shared, and comes to life as a window onto great content.
Construction: The 5X5 grid.
The Logo Mark.
- Artwork: The logo exists in two different lockups (horizontal and vertical). These lockups are fixed. Do not tweak or recreate the logo from its core elements. Logomark is primarily used as an app icon or in corporate communications. Horizontal Lockup and Vertical Lockup illustration referenced. Round Avatar to be used where other round avatars are present, such as in products or on social networks like Google+.
- Clear space: The clear space is the smallest distance allowed between the logo and any other graphic objects including page edges, copy, etc. Think of it as the logo’s comfort zone.
- Sizing (The 1:5 Rule):
For digital use, always use the logo at dimensions that are multiples of 5 to avoid aliasing.
Minimum sizes:
100px, 75px, 120px (Web requirements)
0.25in, 0.35in (Print requirements)
- Color Options: The logo versions exist in 4 color options:
1. Full Color – primary use of logo (red version is preferred).
2. Black – used in greyscale designs or alongside other greyscale logos.
3. White – transparent, used as a window onto content and placed on full-color imagery.
4. One-Color – reserved for embroidery, screen printing, embossing.
- Use rules: Correct usage ensures brand consistency. Avoid common errors when using logo versions:
Do not recolor.
Do not stretch or skew.
Do not apply added textures, bevels, or other effects.
Do not rotate.
Do not extract the ‘F’ from the container.
Do not use transparency with full-color logo.
- Guidelines for relationships between the Flipboard logo and other logos placed alongside. Ensure equal visual weight and proper alignment:
1. Vertical stack alignment - suitable for square or vertical format logos.
2. Horizontal stack alignment - suitable for horizontal format logos.
- Restrictions: Logo cannot be used for promoting pornography, violence, illegal activities, spam, or content reflecting poorly on the Flipboard brand. Flipboard marks should not imply endorsement or partnership unless formally approved in writing.

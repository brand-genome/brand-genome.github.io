## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Standard and alternative logo usage is detailed, covering usage on dark and white backgrounds. The typeface should be white on dark backgrounds, and the standard logo is used on white backgrounds. Monochromatic logos are allowed for traditional or limited-color applications.
- Clear space and minimum size for logos: Minimum clear space is defined as 0.5x the height of the logo. Minimum size for print media is 1.5 inches, and for digital media is 40px.
- Incorrect usage examples include altering colors, picking own colors, outlining, rotating, skewing, shearing, adding effects, modifying spacing, using alternative fonts, changing typeface size, switching order of elements, using wordmark alone, and recreating variations.
- Discontinued logos: Some logos are no longer in use and should be replaced with the newer versions designed for the same purposes.
- Internal branding logos are for official Kirusa email communication, newsletters, and marketing collateral, with unique identities for each service.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Colors: Kirusa Red (HEX: #D81F26, RGB: 216, 31, 38, CMYK: 9, 100, 99, 1) is the primary brand color. Supporting Colors: Kirusa Grey (HEX: #414141), Light Grey (HEX: #F9F9F9), Blue (HEX: #00A4FF), Green (HEX: #7CD845), Yellow (HEX: #FFDF01).
- Gradients and complementary shades for primary colors are defined. Gradient examples and their HEX codes are provided for creating consistent media content.
- Color usage rules specify avoiding overlap of secondary colors on red, avoiding text in secondary colors placed on red backgrounds, and restricting tint usage. Secondary colors cannot dominate or clash with primary brand elements.
- Product-specific color palettes: Each product and service is associated with specified HEX codes, e.g., InstaVoice (#EC1C24), InstaVoice Channels (#F3B71A), Kirusa Konnect (#F0515E), etc.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Fonts: Proxima Nova (for headlines and titles) and Muli (for body content) are the primary brand fonts.
- Secondary Fonts: Commonly available fonts such as Arial Black and Verdana are used when the primary fonts are unavailable. These fonts ensure compatibility across Mac and Windows platforms.
- Minimum Typography Sizes: Print media minimum font size is 10px, and digital media minimum font size is 11px.
- Spacing Guidelines: Minimum spacing between headlines and body text is 20pt. Body text spacing should be 20pt. Additional layout spacing guidelines for headlines and body text are provided.
- Naming Usage: Guidelines are provided for case consistency in brand and product naming, as well as for conjoined words used in product names (e.g., InstaVoice, myGenie).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo clear space is defined as 0.5x the height of the logo (x). No other elements should infringe on this space.
- Minimum spacing between headlines and body text is defined as 20pt, with specific values provided for layouts.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand tone of voice: Factual, precise, succinct, and interesting. Mission: To build mobile services enriching people's lives via communication, entertainment, and work anytime, anywhere.
- Core values: Innovation, progression, simplicity, and commitment.

**Notes:**

- Background design element, the 'Kirusa Wave,' symbolizes brand values and can be used across communications as a visual metaphor. Transparency and thin widths are key aspects.
- Several brand collateral rules include maintaining logo integrity, avoiding overlap of specific visual elements, and ensuring consistency in application of typography and colors.

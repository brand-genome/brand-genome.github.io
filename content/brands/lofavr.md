## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- This is LOfavør's primary logo to be used for all internal and external communications.
- Logo clear space (beskyttelsesområde) is shown, defining an area where no elements can interfere with the logo's presentation.
- Descriptor logo is used in profile campaigns and to demonstrate primary portfolio products of LOfavør.
- Minimum sizes for print and screen are specified:
- Primary logo minimum print width: 15mm; screen width: 75px
- Descriptor logo minimum print width: 20mm; screen width: 100px
- Logo guidelines specify appropriate use for different background types (white, light, dark).
- Incorrect logo usage examples are provided, indicating no changes to proportions, form, or color are allowed. Additionally, the clear space for the logo must always be maintained.
- The logo placement rule specifies placement at the bottom-right of all communications where possible, with exceptions for envelopes and postal items, where it is placed at the top-left.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color - Red:
- CMYK: C0 M100 Y100 K6
- RGB: R222 G26 B33
- HEX: #DE1A21
- Pantone: 186 C / 186 U
- Primary color - Gray:
- CMYK: C30 M30 Y30 K75
- RGB: R68 G64 B63
- HEX: #44403F
- Pantone: Cool Grey 11 C / 426 U
- Secondary colors include Blue, Yellow, Light Green, Light Gray, and Orange, each with corresponding HEX, RGB, CMYK, and Pantone specifications.
- Color usage advice specifies balancing primary and secondary colors to avoid a corporate appearance. Significant white space is essential for lightness and maintaining a professional, airy design.

**Typography Information:**

- Helvetica Neue is the profile font for LOfavør, featuring various weights and styles such as Thin, Regular, Bold, Condensed, etc.
- Arial is the fallback font when Helvetica Neue is unavailable, supporting Regular, Italic, Bold, and Bold Italic weights.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Examples of layouts and document formats (e.g., letterheads, envelopes, PowerPoint templates) demonstrate consistent use of margins and clear guidelines for white space.

**Brand Voice:**

- The 'mengdekonsept' reinforces the idea of unity and collective strength, tying into LOfavør's messaging: 'The advantage of being many is significant.'

**Notes:**

- Rules for co-branding are specified, assigning primary or secondary visual hierarchy to LOfavør or partner brands based on ownership of materials.
- Ad templates are designed with strict dimensions (e.g., half-page: 246x181mm; full-page: 246x365mm) and consistent use of creative space.

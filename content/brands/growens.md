## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- The Growens logo has two versions: Left Aligned and Centered. The Left Aligned logo is to be used aligned or centered on a page, and the Centered version is for use in square-shaped spaces.
- Clearspace rules require the logo to have a margin equal to its height (X) on all sides. The minimum clear space is highlighted as 1/2 X.
- The logomark can be used independently only when the Growens brand has already been established, e.g., second pages of a slide deck.
- Color Variations: The full-color logo is primary, with all-white preferred for dark backgrounds or images. The all-black version is permissible only if the full-color logo was used earlier.
- Logo misuse guidelines include: Do not rotate, reverse, distort, warp, manipulate, change colors, or change tones.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors are defined with the following codes: Rubine Red - CMYK: 0, 97, 28, 0; RGB: 230, 20, 105; HEX: #e61469; Pantone: Rubine Red C.
- Secondary colors include Cool Gray, Persian Blue, and others, with specific HEX, RGB, CMYK, and Pantone codes.
- Color proportions are recommended as: White (90% for backgrounds), Black/Gray (8%), Rubine Red (1.5%), and Other brand colors (0.5%).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Font: Montserrat (used for modern, sophisticated branding). Secondary Font: Lato (used for smaller elements and body text).
- Numerical Font: Roboto Mono (optimized for readability across digital devices).
- Font Usage: Montserrat Bold is for headings and titles (sizes: 20pt+). Lato Regular is for body text (sizes: 12pt or smaller).

**Imagery Photography:**

- High-quality portraits for social and marketing materials. Preferred neutral colors aligned with brand colors. Avoid noise and bright contrasts.
- Portrait Instagram template includes white frames and pattern overlay.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The presentation template aligns text and images left, with equal white space and horizontal spacing (minimum 3 columns between grid components).
- Bits Pattern: The brand graphic (representing Morse code) is used strictly in alternating rows as color accents.

**Brand Voice:**

- The Growens messaging tone emphasizes modernity, simplicity, and sophistication, with clear structure and balance.

**Notes:**

- The email design template includes fixed header/footer, Rubine Red buttons, and predefined fonts (Montserrat Bold for titles, Lato Regular for text). Internal emails use department-specific color codes.
- Table formatting adheres to two styles based on Rubine Red and Gray brand tones across documents and presentations.
- Icons comply with a fixed color palette (Rubine Red, White, Cool Gray). Misuse of icons includes distorting, recoloring, or reversing their direction.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- Main identity logo minimum size guidelines: Minimum width of 45mm for A4 portrait and landscape formats. Right/left margin set to 12.5mm and top/base margin of 12.5mm. For A5 versions, minimum width of 40mm, margins set to 10mm.
- Sub-brand positioning rules: Sub-brand positioned 12.5mm from the top and right-hand side of the document, at a width of 75mm minimum for external applications. On smaller formats, it must be 65mm wide and located bottom-right with 12.5mm spacing from the right and bottom edges.
- Primary position guidelines for logo usage specify centered placement as standard with a secondary lock-up option for use in digital applications when space is limited.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors include Pantone 1805 C (HEX: #b82233, C:5 M:96 Y:76 K:22), Pantone 124 C (HEX: #ebb415, C:0 M:32 Y:100 K:0), White (HEX: #FFFFFF, C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:0), and Black (HEX: #000000, C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:100). They are central to the visual identity, especially within the core logo/brand mark but should not overwhelm design elements.
- Secondary palette includes tones like Pantone 105-16 C (HEX: #003973, C:100 M:68 Y:0 K:39) and other vibrant shades intended for varied applications such as brochures and collateral.
- Tertiary palette includes Pantone 152-15 C (HEX: #588e3b, C:61 M:0 Y:86 K:31) and others inspired by natural elements like stone, slate, and meadow to reflect the university's environment.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface is Aktiv Grotesk in regular, medium, and bold weights for modern typographic style. Used universally across collateral including headlines, brochures, body copy, ads, exhibitions, and campaigns.
- Secondary typeface is Arial (Regular for body copy, Medium for subheadings, Bold for main headings) for presentation purposes when the primary typeface is unavailable.
- Recommendations for design layout: Headlines in Aktiv Grotesk Bold with all-uppercase letters and tracking of -20pt. Subheadings in lower-case medium weight with consistent layout alignment.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style uses contemporary images with strong, modern aesthetics to support values like openness, opportunity, extraordinary results, learning, and growth. Themes should guide stock image selection.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing guidelines for documents: For A4 layouts (portrait and landscape), left and right margins of 12.5mm for main identity logo. Top and bottom margins must also be 12.5mm consistently. For A5 formats, left and right margins are reduced to 10mm along with top and bottom margin spacings of 10mm.
- Sub-brand logo spacing rules specify exact position: Sub-brand identity maintains a width of at least 75mm in external communications. Alignment must adhere to the 12.5mm margin guideline for the edge of the design layouts.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice promotes clarity, modern energy, ambition, and extraordinary achievement. Key phrases like 'Be Extraordinary' reinforce the brand theme.

**Notes:**

- Examples of shield outline (Device #1) and segmented shield (Device #2) applied creatively in recruitment materials, corporate branding, and promotional templates.
- Logos, sub-brand identities, and graphic shield templates are consistently applied across formats like flyers, brochures, recruitment banners, and vehicle livery.
- Approved font files for Aktiv Grotesk can be downloaded from official foundry sources like Adobe Fonts.
- University contact information and design credits are listed on the final page, referencing design agency involvement and marketing details.

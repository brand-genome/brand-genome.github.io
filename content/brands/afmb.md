## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Logotype introduction
- Logo components include Symbol, Wordmark, and Tagline.
- Rules for symbol usage: must have an exclusion zone (ZONE MINIMALE D’AFFICHAGE) and can be used autonomously.
- Prohibited logo usage (les interdits): no color modification or interchanging. Monochrome logos must maintain consistency in title color and contrast.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors specified: Bleu AFMB (Blue) and Orange AFMB (Orange). Codes: Blue - C:88 M:79 Y:45 K:54 | R:41 G:41 B:62 | #29293e. Orange - C:12 M:67 Y:88 K:2 | R:216 G:107 B:48 | #d86b30.
- Color usage guidelines: Bleu AFMB is primary and replaces black for text and accent elements. Lighter variations soften contrasts. Orange AFMB is for highlighting important elements.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography roles: Encode Sans family used for different purposes - Light for body text, Black for titles, etc.
- Office typography is set to Tahoma (Tahoma Regular and Bold) for correspondence, emails, presentations, and internal documents.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Exclusion zone ('ZONE MINIMALE D’AFFICHAGE') must be observed around the symbol.
- Spacing considerations include ensuring sufficient contrast for monochrome logos on dark backgrounds.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2011

**Logo Information:**

- The A.R.T. logo is used in black, black with magenta 'EXPERIENCE,' white, or white with magenta 'EXPERIENCE.' It should not include more than two colors, and colors should not mix within 'EXPERIENCE' or 'the A.R.T.'. The EPS format is recommended for scalability and ensuring background transparency. The logo should not be drop-shadowed, outlined, or rotated. For non-white backgrounds, only EPS format should be used.
- Logo placement on posters, advertisements, and one-page collateral should be in the bottom left corner, at least 0.25 inches from the edge. The text accompanying the logo should align with specific typography elements ('E' and 'I' in 'EXPERIENCE') and should maintain one x-height distance from the logo.
- Clear space rules specify 'one x-height away' from the logo, where one x-height equals the height of the 'E' in 'EXPERIENCE.' Text should not obscure or adapt the logo. The logo must be accompanied by the theater's full name, URL, or box office information.
- Incorrect usage includes skewing, obscuring, adapting, mixing colors improperly, and drop-shadowing the logo.

**Typography Information:**

- The main institutional typefaces are Lubalin Graph and Gotham. Lubalin Graph is suggested for headings and accents, with limited use for no more than two lines of text; tracking increases of up to 25 are recommended for smaller sizes. Gotham serves as the default body text. Gotham Bold (not Medium) should be used for emphasis or bold text. Arial is designated as the web-safe fallback font.
- Specific weights of Lubalin Graph include Book, Book Oblique, Demi, and Demi Oblique. Gotham weights include Light, Light Italic, Book, Book Italic, Bold, and Bold Italic.
- For printed materials and correspondence, Gotham should be used. For emails, Arial is the appropriate typeface. Show-specific fonts may exist and must correspond to the relevant shows only, requiring approval from the marketing department.

**Spacing Layout:**

- On posters, advertisements, and other one-page collateral, the minimum margin for placing the logo is 0.25 inches from the edge. Placement must align with specific guidelines relative to text (e.g., aligning text with specific letters in 'EXPERIENCE').

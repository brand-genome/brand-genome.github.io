## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The NAHREP main logo is the constant used in all communications. It is considered a pictorial mark (not just text and graphic). Modifications to margins, fonts, or elements are not allowed. Accent logos should only be against NAHREP blue. In cases that require a dark background, the full white version of the logo is to be used. The black logo is meant for use only in scenarios where color printing is unavailable. The mark is reserved for digital collateral, including social media icons, postings, and app icons. On print collateral, the mark must be accompanied by NAHREP's full name.
- Available versions of the logo: Accent Logo against NAHREP Blue, Main Logo in Black, Full White Logo.

**Color Information:**

- NAHREP color palette specifies colors for reproduction of the logo in variations: PANTONE 390 C (CMYK: 27/0/100/3, RGB: 181/189/0, HEX: #b5bd00), PANTONE 7597 C (CMYK: 11/92/100/2, RGB: 214/59/39, HEX: #d8351A), PANTONE 3265 C (CMYK: 66/0/39/0, RGB: 0/199/177, HEX: #00c7b1), PANTONE 2955 C (CMYK: 100/55/10/48, RGB: 0/59/111, HEX: #003b6f), PANTONE 401 C (CMYK: 10/11/17/27, RGB: 175/169/160, HEX: #afa9a0).

**Typography Information:**

- Preferred fonts include Bebas Neue, Gotham, and Futura. Fonts are specified for branding purposes depending on the nature of creative work (e.g., headlines versus longer paragraphs). Fonts are available in the Logo Pack at nahrep.org/branding.

**Notes:**

- To clarify any ambiguities or doubts about the usage of branding elements in print or digital formats, approval requests must be submitted to NAHREP. Contact Meghan Lucero, Senior Marketing Manager, via email at mlucero@nahrep.org.

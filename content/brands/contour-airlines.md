## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- No logo mark trademarked by Contour Aviation should be altered in any way. It should not be scaled, stretched, colorized, or otherwise changed without approval from the marketing department. The word Contour may be used on its own as an alternative to the logo mark for one-color applications.
- High-resolution images for logos are available at brand.flycontour.com. This logo mark represents the company in internal and external media and should be used whenever Contour Aviation or its subsidiaries are mentioned.
- Service marks of Contour Aviation include marks for branded airline service and charter, on-demand charter, aircraft management, aircraft maintenance, avionics, parts/components, and fixed base operations. Authorized artwork is available at brand.flycontour.com.
- Contour Aviation service marks must be used in conjunction with their appropriate service. Maintaining consistency throughout branding is emphasized.

**Color Information:**

- Contour Aviation defined colors with specified codes include CMYK, Pantone, and HEX:
1. C27 M26 Y12 K02, Pantone 5295C, Web #B9B3C5
2. C57 M48 Y46 K14, Pantone 7477C, Web #707274
3. C41 M46 Y27 K01, Pantone 7653C, Web #9D8898
4. C29 M00 Y06 K00, Pantone 317C, Web #AFE0EC
5. C33 M15 Y09 K00, Pantone 551C, Web #A9C2D6
- Logo marks should be placed on acceptable background colors meeting branding consistency requirements.

**Typography Information:**

- Font samples include both uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols displayed in primary branding typography.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Contour Aviation's approach is photo-driven with high-resolution wide-angle photography to showcase the size and scope of operations. Close-up photography may accentuate details abstractly.
- Stock photos of aircraft not in the Contour fleet are prohibited. Photos must truthfully represent products/services offered, avoiding embellishments such as catering, limousines, or race cars.
- For Contour Airlines, illustrations highlight branding over photos of destinations or aircraft, emphasizing ticket prices, convenience, reliability, and friendly service.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand principles emphasize integrity, honesty, clear communication, commitment to safety, meaningful relationships tailored to client needs, and excellence in service for operations and interactions.
- Contour's tagline emphasizes ambition with the phrase 'Contour is ambition in flight.'

**Notes:**

- Contour Aviation high-resolution branding resources are available at brand.flycontour.com.
- Corporate brand representation is allowed for overall company context.

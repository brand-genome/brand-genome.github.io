## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- Two logo lockup versions: Lockup A (centered format - preferred usage) and Lockup B (horizontal format). Lockup A should be used whenever possible.
- Primary logo is represented in 2-color, 1-color black, and 1-color reverse. 2-color is the preferred version for consistency. 1-color black is for single color printing or non-white backgrounds, and 1-color reverse is for solid dark backgrounds or high-contrast areas.
- Clear space around the logo must be equal to the height of the first letter 'O' in the logo.
- Minimum logo size requirements: Centered logo with tagline - 1.142 inches wide; Horizontal logo with tagline - 1.5 inches wide; Centered logo without tagline - 0.625 inches wide; Horizontal logo without tagline - 1 inch wide.
- Guidelines for application over backgrounds: Preferred 2-color version (Deep Grey and Red) should be used only on white or taupe backgrounds. 1-color reverse logo should be used for dark and/or solid backgrounds to maintain contrast and legibility.
- Logo violations include modifying colors, typefaces, or proportions; or using unapproved digital artwork.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: OneBlood Red (PMS 187C, HEX: #A6192E, RGB: R171 G25 B45, CMYK: C7 M100 Y82 K26), OneBlood Taupe (PMS 7534C, HEX: #EDEAE0, RGB: R237 G234 B224, CMYK: C5 M5 Y15 K8), and OneBlood Deep Grey (PMS 447C, HEX: #373A36, RGB: R55 G61 B60, CMYK: C50 M30 Y40 K90).
- Secondary colors include Burnt Orange (PMS 7412C, HEX: #D38235), Aqua (PMS 7473C, HEX: #279989), Sea Foam (PMS 557C, HEX: #85B09A), Sunrise (PMS 143C, HEX: #F1B434), Pure Gold (PMS 7752C, HEX: #CFB023), Hope Blue (PMS 7690C, HEX: #0076A8), and Sky Blue (PMS 2985C, HEX: #5BC2E7).
- Color association with blood types: Red for O-, Burnt Orange for O+, Pure Gold for A-, Sunrise for A+, Aqua for B-, Sea Foam for B+, Hope Blue for AB-, Sky Blue for AB+.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary type family: Nexa Sans. Weights include Light, Light Italic, Regular, Regular Italic, Bold, Bold Italic, Extra Bold, and Extra Bold Italic. Recommended for headlines, subheads, and call-to-action text.
- Secondary type family: Adobe Garamond Premier Pro. Weights include Regular, Regular Italic, Medium, Medium Italic, Semibold, Semibold Italic, Bold, and Bold Italic. Suitable for body copy and disclaimers.
- Additional typeface: PT Sans. Weights include Regular, Regular Italic, Bold, Bold Italic, Narrow, and Narrow Bold. Useful for limited body copy and callouts.
- Examples provided of aesthetic relationships in titles, body text, and callouts to maintain the brand's personality.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space for the logo: Space must equal the height of the first letter 'O' in the logo.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tagline: 'Share Your Power'. To be used graphically as part of the logo or typeset in an approved font. Do not use the tagline graphic by itself, and exceptions require approval.

**Notes:**

- Big Red Bus® paint specifications: Sherwin Williams G2-65124-00-B (Red), Sherwin Williams WA-8624 (White). CMYK values were calibrated based on the 2010 Pantone Coated Color Bridge.
- Contact the brand team at brandteam@oneblood.org for exceptions or questions regarding brand applications.

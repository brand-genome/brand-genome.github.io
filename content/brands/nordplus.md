## Brand Guidelines

### 2008

**Logo Information:**

- Two primary logo versions are shown: horizontal brand mark and vertical brand mark. Horizontal version is preferred where possible. Vertical used only when space is restricted.
- Logo must be used as provided — not redrawn or altered. Available in electronic formats for Mac and PC.
- Primary colorways: Full color on white background. A negative (white) version may be used on defined profile colors but never on photographic or colored backgrounds.
- Clear space: A rectangular box containing the Nordplus brand mark must have minimum space based on the width of the letter 'N'.
- Minimum sizes: Horizontal version must be at least 10mm in width, and vertical version must be at least 15mm in width.
- Logo placement: For horizontal version, preferred position is bottom right, with additional space around clear space area. For vertical version, use only when space is restricted and ideally centered.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: Sea Blue (Pantone 320, CMYK 100/0/30/10, RGB 0/154/166, HEX #009AA6) and White (CMYK 0/0/0/0, RGB 255/255/255, HEX #FFFFFF). Primary colors act as important identifiers for the brand.
- Secondary colors: Yellow (Pantone 7405), Cyan (Pantone 299), Tangerine (Pantone 165), Magenta (Pantone 226), Apple Green (Pantone 369), Violet (Pantone 526), and Brown (Pantone 469). Used as highlights and to complement primary colors.
- Additional neutral colors include Light Grey, Medium Grey, Dark Grey, and Light Green. CMYK, RGB, and HEX values are provided for all colors.
- Color breakdowns include Pantone, CMYK, RGB, and HEX values. Coated Pantone colors act as the master reference.

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate font: F P Dancer Pro. Combines a constructed and scriptface style. Designed by Morten R. Olsen. Licensed to all Nordplus offices.
- F P Dancer Pro font family includes Light, Book, and Bold weights. Bold and Black weights are used for emphasis.
- Tracking settings: 6-9pt (-10), 10-13pt (-15), 14-17pt (-20), 18-21pt (-25), 22-25pt (-30). Larger text should be reviewed for maximum optical results.
- Headings: Use Light or Book weights in upper and lower case or capitals; avoid Bold and Black. Suggested sizes for body text include 8/10pt, 9/11pt, or 10/12pt.
- Subhead text uses the same size as body text.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should have a Nordic or Baltic origin, expressing modern, light, vivid aesthetics. Images should reflect values such as cooperation, focus on results, accessibility, and community.
- Use of photos showcasing people from Nordplus programme target groups. Photos should not be black and white or monotone.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Preferred format for Nordplus programme leaflets is A4 (297x210 mm), folded into a 6-page leaflet (100x210 mm). Use no more than two secondary colors in addition to Sea Blue as the main color. Print on uncoated paper.
- Sender information includes country flag and institution details styled using F P Dancer Pro Light/Book with specific line heights and spacing.

**Notes:**

- The document refers to the use of additional graphic elements such as photo and color background tags (used sparingly), national flags, maps of the Nordic and Baltic regions, decorative branches, and background patterns.
- Contact details for further guidance are provided for the Norwegian Centre for International Cooperation in Higher Education (SIU).

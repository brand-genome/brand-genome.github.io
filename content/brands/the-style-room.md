## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The Style Room logo uses the headline typeface of the website, Schnyder, to evoke editorial elegance and a touch of fun. It incorporates the tagline 'Powered by Zappos' styled as per the parent brand. Each word in the logo includes one letter with a different width, exemplifying Schnyder's unique multiple-width design.
- Three logo variants are introduced: (1) Primary horizontal logo, suitable for standard use. (2) Stacked version, ideal for square compositions. (3) 'TSR' icon logo that inherits the character of the full logo (with 'T' narrower than other letters).
- Minimum size: The logo should not appear smaller than 50px in height to ensure legibility. For clear space, an area equivalent to the height of the 'T' must surround the logo. Proportions must remain unaltered, and the logo must not be rotated or embellished.
- Color: On the website, the logo only appears in Grey 90 color (see 3.2). It should only be placed on a white background.

**Color Information:**

- Primary and secondary background colors: White (#FFFFFF) and Grey 4 (#F5F5F5). These create a neutral canvas for editorial and product photography emphasis.
- Tertiary background colors: Orange (#F9F0E5), Yellow (#F9F7EA), Green (#EBF2ED), Blue (#EDF2F7), Lavender (#F2EDF2), and Warm Grey (#F5F0EB). These colors complement specific editorial and photography content.
- Text and icon colors: Grey 90 (#1A1A1A) for primary text, icons, and UI elements; Grey 56 (#707070) for secondary text and UI elements; and Grey 42 (#949494) for tertiary UI elements. These colors ensure readability with accessible contrast ratios (4.5:1 for text, 3:1 for UI).
- Additional colors: Red (#DA1E28) is used for error messages and markdown prices. Dividing rules are styled with Grey 12 (#E0E0E0).

**Typography Information:**

- Schnyder S Demi is the typeface used for headlines. It conveys an elevated, editorial feel with a touch of organic quirkiness. Prominent headlines are styled in all caps to establish hierarchy. Schnyder font widths can be mixed within headlines or words to enhance their playful, unique character. It was released by Commercial Type in 2018.
- Styrene B Regular is the typeface used for body copy. It is a precise, geometric complement to Schnyder. Styrene allows readability at small sizes while retaining subtle idiosyncrasies. Hierarchy is created by contrasting small caps with title or sentence case. It was released by Commercial Type in 2016.
- Type styles and specifications for website typography: Font sizes, line heights, and letter spacing are listed for styles such as Headline 1-6, Subtitle 1, Body 1-2, Label 1-3, and UI elements. Line heights are expressed as percentages to maintain proportions consistently across breakpoints. Font scaling is detailed for viewports varying from XS to XL.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Details about logo placement, versions, and usage guidelines, including horizontal, vertical, and icon-only versions.
- Clear space and minimum sizing rules outlined for logos and taglines on both print and digital mediums.
- Do's and don'ts for logo usage, including incorrect layouts, changing colors, and placement on strong backgrounds.
- Minimum dimensions are specified: stacked tagline widths of 30px (digital) and 10mm (print), and inline tagline widths of 85px (digital) and 30mm (print).

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes Blue (PMS 7461C / 3005U, HEX #007FC2, RGB (0, 127, 194), CMYK (95, 35, 0, 0)) and Grey (PMS 431C / 431U, HEX #71787D, RGB (113, 120, 125), CMYK (10, 0, 0, 65)).
- Printed materials should use Pantone or CMYK colors, while digital formats should rely on HEX or RGB codes.

**Typography Information:**

- Supporting typeface includes Desyrel, which adds an emotional element to the brand's tagline and country names.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Guidelines emphasize authentic photography showing volunteers in action, capturing pride, purpose, and local context. Images should primarily feature people in natural settings and highlight their contributions.
- Recommendations for photography use in presentations and communication materials include adding emotional connection, avoiding overuse of effects, and leveraging templates to ensure consistency.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space should equal the height of the letter 'i' in the logo on all sides. Logos and taglines must not appear cluttered or cramped.
- T-shirt designs position logos prominently on the front and use the stacked tagline across the shoulder blades.

**Brand Voice:**

- Communication principles include understanding the audience, maintaining clarity with concise and jargon-free language, and ensuring consistency using the brand guidelines and toolkit.
- Tone is conﬁdent, professional, and coherent, reinforcing the UNV brand as optimistic, fresh, and consistent across all materials.

**Notes:**

- Promotional items should adhere to brand guidelines. Logo and tagline usage must maintain clear space and appropriate scaling on materials like banners, T-shirts, and digital templates.
- Templates for presentation slides, infographics, and promotional materials streamline visual consistency and accessibility for branding.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Handshake logo described as the official signature of the brand — confident, simple, and trustworthy.
- Primary mark defined as a horizontal lockup of the wordmark and icon. Represents trustworthiness and professionalism with an authentic human touch.
- Logo colorways restricted to black, white, and Handshake red. Red logo only permitted on white or light gray backgrounds for contrast.
- Minimum clear space around the logo determined by the height of the logo [X].
- Examples of incorrect logo usage: Do not distort, rotate, alter colors, add outlines, use different typefaces, rearrange elements, or add shadows/effects.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors: Handshake red (#FF3F3C, Pantone RED 032 C), white (#FFFFFF), and black (#000000).
- Extended vibrant secondary palette for personality: Coral (#FFC5B3, Pantone 177 C), Indigo (#4E4D7A, Pantone 7443 C), Canary (#F5E04B, Pantone 114 C), etc.
- Recommended color pairings provided for maintaining contrast and consistency.

**Typography Information:**

- Main font: Suisse Int'l for body text, subheads, buttons, and callouts. Features multiple weights and is modern and clean.
- Display typeface: Ginto Nord for headlines and impactful text. Avoided for small text or long passages.
- Typographical hierarchy maintained using specific combinations of Ginto Nord (headlines/callouts/quotes) and Suisse Int'l (subheadings/body).

**Imagery Photography:**

- When combining logo with photography, use white/black versions on simple and clean compositions with ample negative space for visibility.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space rules for the logo: Minimum distance from other graphic elements should be equivalent to the height of the logo [X].

**Brand Voice:**

- Manifesto emphasizes core principles for students: inclusivity, exploration, and assistance in their career journeys (e.g., 'For the seekers, finders, doers, and explorers,' 'Look ahead at what’s possible').

- Glossary provides explanations for terms like CMYK, lockup, logo, Pantone, etc., valuable for understanding branding terminologies.

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The Handshake logo is the official signature of the Handshake brand. Our logo is confident, simple, and trustworthy.
- This chapter outlines the principles behind the logo and describes how to use it to represent Handshake in the most powerful and engaging way.
- Our logo is designed to work across a vibrant color palette. The positive and negative logo should be paired with a Handshake color. On light backgrounds, use the positive logo, and on medium and dark backgrounds, use the negative or positive logo.
- Examples of improper logo usage: Do not distort, rotate, change colors, use other typefaces to recreate, outline, place on busy backgrounds, apply effects or shadows to the logo.

**Color Information:**

- Welcome to our new brand with a fresh design system, vibrant color palette, clean typographic style, and a fun graphic language.
- Color is a critical aspect of the Handshake brand and visually conveys personality and excitement.
- For maximum impact, we only use our logo in black or white. On light backgrounds, use the positive logo, and on medium and dark backgrounds, use both as long as there is enough contrast.
- Color palette details: HS Blue - PMS 7455 C, CMYK 90/7/1/0, RGB 41/89/171, HEX #2959AB; HS Pink - PMS 217 C, CMYK 6/30/0/0, RGB 232/189/217, HEX #E8BDD9; HS Cyan - PMS 3262 C, CMYK 57/0/24/0, RGB 84/212/209, HEX #54D4D1; HS Red - PMS Warm Red C, CMYK 0/88/79/0, RGB 250/66/59, HEX #FA423B; Black - CMYK 0/0/0/100, RGB 0/0/0, HEX #000000; White - CMYK 0/0/0/0, RGB 255/255/255, HEX #FFFFFF;
- Color pairing guidance: Color pairings should be selected from provided combinations to achieve optimal contrast and branding consistency.

**Imagery Photography:**

- To aid the visibility of our logo, make sure not to place it on any busy background areas. This is especially important when using photography.

**Notes:**

- Our new vibrant brand infuses personality and excitement through bold colors and design style.
- Logo and color guidelines highlight good design practices for optimal representation of the Handshake brand.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Horizontal logo version specified as immutable in design - the relationship between elements should not be altered.
- Vertical logo version specified as immutable in design - the relationship between elements should not be altered.
- Single-color logo version (black and white). Designed for optimal contrast usage on applications.
- Clear space/margin rules developed for the logo to ensure readability and avoid interference from other graphic elements.
- Application of logo over colored backgrounds permitted within color-tones grid, ensuring maximum contrast for visibility.
- Application on photographic backgrounds requires maximum contrast for chromatic integrity. Avoid areas where legibility is compromised.
- Minimum size guidelines defined for the logo: Horizontal versions require a minimum size of 20mm (80px), and vertical single-color versions 15mm/10mm based on black and white colors.

**Color Information:**

- Color codes for branding purposes: Pantone, HEX, CMYK, RGB provided for five colors, ensuring chromatic consistency.
- Directional guidelines for gradients, including angular specifications (e.g. 45 degrees angle must be respected).

**Typography Information:**

- Institutional typeface is 'Graphik' with variants in weight (Bold, Regular, Light). 'Arial' is used for long texts and in unsupported interfaces for web fonts.
- Typography used for signage is LL Circular Std (Medium, Black, Bold, Book). These fonts are praised for humanized contours and geometric forms that align with pictograms.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery usage demands clarity where logos are legible when applied over photographic backgrounds. Avoid sections where logo readability is negligible.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Marginal grid-based development ensures uniformity between pictograms and other graphic assets for logical articulation.

**Brand Voice:**

- Describes the brand tone - modern and respectful of Coimbra's historical identity. Appeals to emotions, pride, and connection with inhabitants of Coimbra.

**Notes:**

- Stationery standards covered including A4 sheets, DL envelopes (windowed and non-windowed formats), and business cards with dimensions of 85mm x 50mm.
- Visual coherence in branding assured by ensuring pictogram design elements match the logo structure.

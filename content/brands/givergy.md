## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Full-color master logo with primary gradient used within the logo or as a background. The gradient contains four colors: cyan, navy, purple, and magenta.
- Primary gradient breakdown: Cyan (C76, M21, Y0, K0; RGB: R8, G157, B217), Navy (C100, M87, Y21, K7; RGB: R36, G55, B118), Purple (C67, M90, Y22, K10; RGB: R108, G52, B113), Magenta (C28, M93, Y0, K0; RGB: R188, G43, B135).
- Logo versions include: black mono logo for black and white publications, full-color logo for white or light-colored backgrounds, white-out logo for dark backgrounds, logos with and without strapline.
- The Givergy logo with the strapline is the primary logo and should generally be used for digital and printed collateral (stationery, business cards, advertising, online materials).
- Clear space rule: Minimum clear space required is the height of the Venn icon from the logo. No graphic message or text should be closer than this distance.
- Minimum logo size requirements: Print – 30mm width; Digital – 150 pixels at 72dpi; Resizing guidelines for large formats like banners and signage are stated.

**Color Information:**

- The primary gradient angle is set to 45°, with colors equally spaced: Cyan (Pantone Process Cyan C), Navy (Pantone 287c), Purple (Pantone 520), Magenta (Pantone 2395c).
- Color usage guidelines include detailed breakdowns for CMYK, RGB, and HEX values for solid colors and 25% tints.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: FS Elliot (weights: Light, Book, Bold, Black). Roles include print and digital applications like corporate brochures, headlines, and body text.
- Secondary font: Open Sans (weights: Light, Regular, Semi-bold, Bold). Used for digital and print collateral such as websites and online content.
- Microsoft Office font: Arial (weights: Regular, Bold). Standard font for Word and PowerPoint documents.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery style uses the Venn icon as a graphic device for vibrancy and flexibility. Composition requires overlapping two icons and maintaining a balance of magenta, cyan, and purple.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space must match height of Venn icon around logos, ensuring no interference with other elements.
- Minimum spacing and size rules ensure proper presentation of logos across materials. E.g., 30mm for print, 150px for digital screens.

**Brand Voice:**

- The 'Giving Brand' aligns with themes of giving, technology, and energy to appeal to the 'Giving Generation'.

**Notes:**

- Pantone colors should only be used where a spot color is mandatory. CMYK is preferred for printed collateral.

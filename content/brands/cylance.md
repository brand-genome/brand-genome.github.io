## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Branding Guidelines
2019 REV. 3.0. 20190426
- LOGO USAGE
The BlackBerry Cylance logo must not be stretched, rotated, recolored, or modified. Follow all safe area and minimum size guidelines listed throughout this section.
- Icon-only logo variants are permitted for restricted use in scenarios where the full logo cannot fit. Maintain proportions and spacing rules.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include Black (HEX: #000000, RGB: 0,0,0) and Green (Pantone 376). Secondary colors include white and lighter green gradients.

**Typography Information:**

- Main typeface: Helvetica Neue (Weights—Light, Regular, Bold). Intended for main headers; body text uses Regular weight.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery guidelines emphasize clean, candid photographs in natural light. Avoid over-edited or stock-looking images.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Safe space rule: Surround logo with a clear area equal to half its height.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone is technical yet approachable. Messaging should emphasize trust, innovation, and simplicity.
- Tagline: 'Cylance: Prevention-First Security'

**Notes:**

- General guidelines emphasize adherence to technical precision for all design elements and consistency in implementing the logo and typography.

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Ensure the correct logo is used; follow clear space regulations and unacceptable usage guidelines.

**Color Information:**

- Cylance's primary colors must be the hero colors in collateral.

**Typography Information:**

- The Titillium type family is used as the main font. Follow typeface rules.
- Email templates use Arial or Arial Narrow, not Titillium, for compatibility with operating systems and mail clients.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography should emphasize innovative personality and include human, real-life elements.
- Tradeshow booths require black and gray textured backgrounds, taglines, URLs, paragraphs, and 4-6 key points.
- Pull-up banners should have simple messaging integrated on black hex backgrounds.
- Landing pages should contain a headline, logos, a call to action with data-capturing form, one instance of light approved graphic imagery, and follow strict brand formatting standards.

**Brand Voice:**

- Messaging should reflect the mission to protect globally through AI-based malware prevention. Style includes clear, direct language avoiding technical jargon.
- Approved descriptions for Cylance, CylancePROTECT, and CylanceOPTICS follow a prevention-first theme highlighting their AI-driven approach. They should use consistent language per the Associated Press Stylebook and Cylance Messaging Guidelines.

**Notes:**

- Details on branded templates for web, email, tradeshow booths, pull-up banners, and landing pages are provided in pages 34-38. Approval from Design and Marketing is required for public use.
- Contact details for branding support are listed—Ryan Golden (VP of Design) and Drew Hoffman (Creative Director).

- The Impinj logo is available in two-color (red and gray) and one-color versions. Two-color is preferred on a white background, while the white logo is used on dark backgrounds and the gray logo for one-color applications. Minimum size requirements for the logo are 3/4 inch (100px). Clear space around the logo is defined as the height from the baseline to the ascender of the logotype. The logo can scale up to any size.
- Logo 'Don'ts' include improper execution such as stretching, distortion, rotation, using incorrect colors, adding effects, and placing it in cluttered or conflicting settings.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Brand photography guidelines emphasize clear, uncluttered, and bold imagery matching the Impinj color palette. Avoid muted, black-and-white photography and heavily manipulated images.
- Photography types:
- Authentic: Real people in genuine interactions without visual clichés.
- Abstract: Light and airy, featuring natural textures.
- Product: Technical imagery showcasing Impinj attributes.
- Photography 'Don'ts': Avoid clichéd stock photos, heavily stylized effects, manipulated imagery, stereotypical depictions, and unclear narratives. Ensure diverse, authentic representation.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- Xbox Live Brand guidelines mention various configurations and examples, including logo versions (four-color, one-color) and usage contexts for services like Xbox Live Gold.
- Minimum clear space rules for the logo: at least 0.43 inches in print and 30 pixels on screen.
- Do not combine the Xbox Live logo with Xbox One or Xbox 360 logos. Proper layouts for merchandise featuring Xbox Live are highlighted, showing how to integrate the logo with 50% green backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Slate Gray is the primary color for Xbox Live (Pantone Cool Gray 11, HEX #000000). Broadcast black (RGB 16/16/16) and white (RGB 235/235/235) are alternate colors for TV, UI, and dashboards.
- Xbox Green (Pantone 362 C, RGB 16/124/16, HEX #107C10) is used for emphasis. Guidelines for combining green with other elements are specified.

**Typography Information:**

- Segoe Pro typeface is recommended. For type size 24pt and larger, Light is advised; SemiLight or Regular should be used when legibility is an issue. Regular is recommended for body copy.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery should focus on clean compositions, tight copy, and striking visuals representing Xbox's premium and differentiated identity.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Ensure layouts maintain 50% green for Xbox Live promotions outside Xbox-branded spaces. Guidelines include using slate gray backgrounds whenever possible.

**Brand Voice:**

- Xbox Live's tone emphasizes focus, clarity, and iconic representation with memorable language reflecting the brand's premium identity.

**Notes:**

- Contact for brand help: xboxbrand@microsoft.com. Asset help: xboxbrandsup@microsoft.com.

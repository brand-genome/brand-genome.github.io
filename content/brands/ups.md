## Brand Guidelines

### 2003

**Logo Information:**

- UPS brandmark must be presented in its standard and customary fashion for recognition. It includes two versions: 3-D primary version and alternate one-color version.
- Clear space defines the 'breathing room' around the brandmark kept free from graphics, text, and marks.
- Minimum size for the brandmark versions: Primary 3-D version is 0.5 inches wide, Alternate one-color version is 0.25 inches wide.
- Trademark symbol must accompany the brandmark whenever it appears at 0.375 inches (9.5mm) or larger.
- Approved artwork for the UPS brandmark must be used from the UPS Brand Exchange.
- Unacceptable brandmark practices: Do not add effects, delete the shield background, redraw elements, place it on photographs/patterns, outline it in color, add new elements, change colors, crop, change orientation/gradient direction, violate clear space, or use as motif/design element.

**Color Information:**

- UPS brand marking guidelines indicate the color brown is a registered trademark.
- UPS attribution statement for any communication involving the color brown: UPS, the UPS brandmark, and the color brown are registered trademarks of United Parcel Service of America, Inc.

**Imagery Photography:**

- UPS images must only be used in connection with news stories about UPS or its products and services. Attribution: 'This image is reproduced with permission of United Parcel Service of America, Inc.'
- UPS B-roll video usage guidelines: Use only for news stories about UPS or its products and services; attribution required: 'This video is broadcast with permission of United Parcel Service of America, Inc.'

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space must be maintained around the brandmark, free of graphics, text, or marks. Minimum size stated as minimum width.

**Notes:**

- UPS trademarks and service names must not be altered or played upon. Incorrect example provided: 'Life has its UPS and downs.'
- UPS service names must be treated as proper names with initial capital letters (e.g., UPS Next Day Air) and paired with a descriptive category name (e.g., UPS shipping services).
- UPS trademarks must be appropriately labeled with symbols (e.g., UPS Next Day Air®, UPS Worldwide Express℠).

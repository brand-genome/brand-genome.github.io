## Brand Guidelines

### 2024

**Logo Information:**

- Logos: clearspace - Clear space of 1x height around the logo.
- Logo prohibited usage includes avoiding distortion, recoloring, adding special effects, and using the logo on busy or inappropriate backgrounds.
- Available logo variations include the standard shield, favicon, and stretch logo formats.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include a vibrant blue and white color palette. Supplementary secondary colors follow a humanistic tone for adaptability.
- Usage guidelines: Ensure sufficient contrast between text and backgrounds for accessibility, and pair colors appropriately to maintain brand integrity.
- Do not use clashing colors or incorrect variations of the defined color palette.

**Typography Information:**

- Font families include Borna Regular, Medium, and Semibold. Primary usage includes regular text for body copy and Semibold for headings.
- Font hierarchy should establish visual structure, with titles and subtitles clearly distinguished.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery should reflect authentic depictions with vibrant yet natural tones from the secondary color palette.
- Avoid overlaying text on key portions of an image like faces; instead, position text in areas with visual breathing space.
- Product photography should focus on demonstrable, real-world applications to highlight authenticity and functionality.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Consistent spacing and padding should be maintained between text blocks and UI elements in digital applications.
- Grid layout aligns content based on 12-column grids for structured and balanced designs.

**Brand Voice:**

- Tone of voice reflects a balance between confident authority and approachability. Communicate clearly without excessive jargon.
- Emphasize positive language in messages while addressing the audience in the second person for a direct and personal approach.
- Avoid overly casual language, humor that undermines professionalism, or overuse of emojis.

**Notes:**

- Secondary color palette tones emphasized in all visual identity to align with brand's professional yet accessible feel.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2008

**Logo Information:**

- The E4 logo primarily appears in purple and white. In cases where color is not possible, it can appear in black monochrome. The bold keyline and 'E' of the logo remain white and cannot be transparent. The logo cannot appear in grey or a tint of purple.
- Minimum logo size is 8mm in height. No maximum size.
- The E4 logo as a sticker includes a second white keyline of identical width to the inner keyline. This sticker must look dimensional and can appear to be peeling.
- The logo should appear in the bottom-right of print materials, no closer than 10mm from edges. When used with tape design elements, it must overlay one or both tape pieces.
- The logo requires a minimum isolation area of 10mm around it, ensuring no visual elements encroach.
- When appearing alone in print advertising, the logo is placed equidistant from the bottom-right edges, with margins of one-tenth of the ad width. When used with tape, it should match tape size proportionally and maintain a 'unit'.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color is Purple (Pantone 2602c). Additional color codes: CMYK: C 71 M 95 Y 4 K 0, RGB: R 109 G 29 B 124, Web: #663399.
- Black (Pantone Black 6) is used as mono: CMYK: C 98 M 40 Y 0 K 100, RGB: R 0 G 0 B 0, Web: #000000.

**Typography Information:**

- E4 uses ITC American Typewriter in Light, Medium, and Bold weights. The typeface is supplied in TrueType and Postscript formats.
- Headlines use ITC American Typewriter Bold, always in title case, and can vary in size. Leading equals font size minus 10pt.
- Body copy uses ITC American Typewriter Medium. If an italic stylistic element is needed, Light weight may substitute. Leading equals font size plus 3pt.
- Minimum type size is 5pt, with no maximum size.
- Headline color is either purple or white, depending on the background.

**Imagery Photography:**

- The design incorporates strips of purple-colored tape for TX and program metadata, adhering to a casual design style. Tape can have imperfections, including creases and peeling edges, to add character.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Type is left-aligned on strips of tape, justified to the tape edges.
- The top piece of tape is slightly longer than the bottom. Additional tape layers can add updated information, mimicking fly posting and should not exceed four layers.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand voice dictates numerals in E4 (not spelled out words). Titles for programs are in caps rather than enclosed in quotation marks. Lower case is used for general technical and professional terms (e.g., 'creative director').
- Number rules include: one to ten written out, numerals used for 11 and beyond. Decades written as 70s, 80s, 90s.

**Notes:**

- The E4 logo must always present the '4' as a numeral, not as the word 'Four.' Incorrect iterations include 'E Four' or splitting the letter and numeral apart.
- The URL 'channel4.com/e4' or 'e4.com' is a required advertising detail, range-left aligned and sized as the smallest element on the page.
- Purple-colored tape and logo overlay form a design 'unit' in advertising. Tape customization is encouraged.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2008

**Logo Information:**

- The Carhartt logo is shown in two configurations: a primary configuration featuring the 'C' symbol and 'Carhartt' wordmark, and a secondary horizontal configuration.
- The Carhartt Woman logo configuration is similar to the primary Carhartt logo, with 'Woman' added to the wordmark.
- Clear space for the Carhartt logo is defined as follows: preferred clear space is 4x and minimum clear space is 2x, where x is the height of the lowercase 'a'.
- Clear space guidelines for the Carhartt Woman signature are proportional to the height of the lowercase 'a', with minimum clear space of 2x.
- Preferred presentation is Carhartt Brown (Pantone #7511) and Black over a light background. Alternative colors include single-color Black for light backgrounds and White for dark backgrounds.
- Minimum-size guidelines are set for ensuring legibility: Primary Signature Size is 92 x 92px (+33px Clear Space) for digital use, Secondary Signature Size is 156 x 30px (+42px Clear Space). For print, Primary Signature minimum is 0.85” x 0.85” + clear space, while Secondary minimum is 1.45” x 0.56” + clear space.
- Incorrect usage examples include distorting the signature, rotating it, adding graphic effects, using unspecified colors, placing on busy backgrounds, ignoring clear space guidelines, confining the logo within borders, or reconfiguring logo elements.
- Correct usage examples demonstrate adherence to configuration, color, and clear space rules, including two-color options for light and dark backgrounds.

**Color Information:**

- Preferred logo colors are Carhartt Brown (Pantone 7511) and Black for light backgrounds. Single-color options are Black (light background) and White (dark background).
- Complementary color palettes include Cool, Warm, Earth, Safety, Performance, and Comfort tones. Examples include Pantone 5405, Pantone 1655, Pantone 381, Pantone 5395, etc.
- Pantone color codes for coated paper: Pantone 7511 C (CMYK: 5, 48, 93, 23 / RGB: R194 G123 B19). 100% Black (CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 100 / RGB: R0 G0 B0).
- Pantone color codes for uncoated paper: Pantone 153 U (CMYK: 0, 46, 100, 18 / RGB unspecified). 100% Black (CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 100).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface is Helvetica Neue, including variations such as Light, Light Italic, Roman, Italic, Bold, Bold Italic, Medium Condensed, etc. Secondary typeface is Melior, including Regular, Bold, and Bold Italic styles.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo clear space (preferred 4x, minimum 2x) must not be violated to maintain visual impact.
- Minimum-size dimensions for print and web are provided to ensure legibility and uniform presentation.

**Brand Voice:**

- Carhartt's brand voice is masculine, honest, proven, innovative, and authentic. It emphasizes clear, direct, conversational, active language, while avoiding excessive marketing jargon or impersonal tone.
- Examples of tone and phrasing adjustments provided for catalog, brochure, and web copywriting.
- Tagline: 'Work Strong' communicates the brand essence and positioning.

**Notes:**

- The Carhartt Signature and 'Work Strong' graphic are trademarks of Carhartt, Inc., and are legally protected.
- Carhartt provides best-in-class apparel for active workers.

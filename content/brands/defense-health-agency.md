## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The DHA logo is contemporary and clean, consisting of the Defense Health Agency abbreviation and a three-colored design based on the U.S. flag colors (red, white, blue). Its proportions: 'Health.mil' width is twice the width of the MHS unit. Clear space: one full width of the lowercase 'e' in 'Health' for spacing from other graphic elements.
- Square version of the logo can be used for smaller social media images where the full logo is inappropriate.
- Do not change the size, alignment, arrangement of elements, or dimensions of the DHA logo. Do not use special effects, rotate, add strokes, or change typography.
- Incorrect usage noted: distorted proportions, rearranged elements, altered typographic or stroke weight.
- The DHA seal is for official use and is displayed in a circular form. Clear space around the seal equals one-half of the seal's height.
- File formats and authorized artwork must be directly downloaded from the organization's platform, ensuring consistency.
- No reverse coloring, tints, elongation, or effects like scaling or overlay should be applied to the DHA seal.

**Color Information:**

- Primary brand colors include Pantone 2768 C, CMYK (100, 91, 37, 45), HEX #051D49, RGB (5, 29, 73), and red accent color Pantone 181 C, CMYK (19, 100, 90, 9), HEX #B8202E, RGB (184, 32, 46). Other colors include HEX #FFD03F (yellow) and HEX #5AAB46 (green). Avoid using red, gray, and blue in the same product.

**Typography Information:**

- DHA primary fonts: ITC Franklin Gothic for headers/subheaders, and Minion Pro for body text.
- ITC Franklin Gothic: Sans serif font, legible at distances and small sizes, ideal for headings.
- Minion Pro: Classic serif font, suitable for body text and adaptable layouts.
- Guidelines: Do not use effects like drop shadow or distort typefaces (expand, condense, etc.).

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space around the DHA seal: Minimum of half the seal's height.
- Clear space around the MHS logo: Equal to the width of the lowercase 'e' in 'Health'.

**Brand Voice:**

- The DHA brand represents consistency, professionalism, and clean communication to maintain trust and clarity within its audience. Its messaging avoids cobranding unless explicit permission is given.

**Notes:**

- Seal and logo are registered trademarks of the Department of Defense, with penalties for misuse under the Trademark Act.
- Trademarks must be used in their exact form, with no alterations.

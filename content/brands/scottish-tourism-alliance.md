## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The Scottish Tourism Alliance logo comprises two elements: the logo symbol evoking strength and unity, and the logo type in Futura Medium font. The primary corporate colors are purple and blue.
- Logo versions include: LOGO DARK VERSION for light backgrounds or images, and LOGO LIGHT VERSION for dark backgrounds or images.
- Clear space rule: The logo should be surrounded by clear space equal to the height of the type, ensuring no graphic elements invade this zone.
- Minimum size rules: Full logo with strapline has a minimum size of 60mm/170px (print/digital). Adjusted logo without strapline has a minimum size of 25mm/7px.
- Do's and Don'ts for logo usage: Do not change proportions of the logo vertically or horizontally, invert, alter the symbol/type, or change the size relationship between symbol and type.
- Scottish Tourism Alliance Member Logo: Adaptation of master logo identifying the members with similar exclusion zone guidelines.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Color: Blue - CMYK: C100 M080 Y000 K053, Pantone: 2757 C, RGB: R000 G031 B096, HEX: #001F60.
- Primary Color: Purple - CMYK: C039 M100 Y032 K009, Pantone: 7648 C, RGB: R153 G030 B102, HEX: #991E66.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Typeface: Futura BT by Paul Renner, Foundry: Bitstream. Font weights include Light, Medium, Bold, and Heavy.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space around logo: Maintain clear space equal to the height of the type in logo.

**Notes:**

- Supporting graphics use geometry from the logo to create interlinking waves, symbolizing alliance strength and unity. Application of wave is recommended in white with 50% opacity.
- Applications include magazine cover design for The Talker magazine, front and back business cards (dimensions: 85 x 55mm), and letterhead (dimensions: 210 x 297mm). Print specifications use CMYK.

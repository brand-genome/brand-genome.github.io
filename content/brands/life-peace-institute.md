## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Logotype in blue and white version. Elements should never be separated. Position, size, color, and proportional relationships are predetermined and must not be altered.

**Color Information:**

- Main heritage palette consists of Knowledge Blue (Pantone 2748, CMYK: 100/92/15/5, RGB: 1/33/105). Supporting palette includes Positive Light Blue (Pantone 2748, CMYK: 50/15/0/0, RGB: 120/184/229), Vision Yellow (CMYK: 0/16/87/0, RGB: 247/211/30), Grounded Grey (CMYK: 22/21/37/0, RGB: 206/200/178), Collaboration Green (CMYK: 84/32/67/15, RGB: 31/120/100), and Action Coral (CMYK: 7/84/65/0, RGB: 224/81/83). Colors can also be used in tints (70%, 50%, 30%).

**Typography Information:**

- Three typefaces are used. Calibri is the primary everyday typeface for headlines, subheads, captions, and body copy in PC-based applications. Georgia is the primary body copy typeface for long-form text, body copy, and quotes. Semplicita Pro is used for headlines, sub-headlines, captions, and may be combined with Georgia. Semplicita Pro is a licensed typeface, available from Adobe or MyFonts, intended for external design work.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography is a tool to showcase diversity and dynamic work, connecting with people beyond words.

**Brand Voice:**

- Storytelling emphasizes genuine and authentic narratives, focusing on emotions, individuals, hope, and dignity. Stories should avoid organizational jargon, provide a moment in time, and address specific audiences.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- The full logo is composed of two overlapping panels, with an option to add the word 'Revealed' underneath for promotional and marketing materials. It should only be reproduced from master artworks supplied by Team London Bridge.
- The abridged logo is used for business-facing or non-marketing materials, and must be reproduced from master artworks supplied by Team London Bridge.
- The logos are available in green (general marketing), blue (business-related), and orange (leisure and tourism use). They must use official master artworks provided by Team London Bridge.
- Clear space rules: The logos must always be surrounded by a clear space equivalent to 'x' units, as demonstrated in the provided diagram. This area should be kept free from text and other elements. Placement over images must avoid overly busy visuals.
- Minimum size recommendations: Print (25mm width), Digital (180px width). Recommended positioning: Always in the top left or right corners. Examples include Flyers (A6 at 30mm width), A5 brochures (50mm width), and Posters (A4 at 65mm width).
- Do's and Don'ts for logo application include adhering to color, clear space, alignment, and placement guidelines (e.g., avoid distortion, rotation, cropping, complex backgrounds, and central placement).

**Color Information:**

- Three palettes are defined: Green for generic and overarching marketing, Orange for leisure and tourist communications, and Blue for business-related materials. Includes RGB, HEX, CMYK, and PMS codes for shades of these palettes.

**Typography Information:**

- Museo Sans is the primary typeface, available in weights 100, 300, 500, and 900. Museo Sans 900 is reserved for titles and headings (always in block capitals), while lighter weights are used for body text, captions, and credits. Hierarchy rules stipulate distinct size difference between headlines and supporting text.
- Manipulation of typography (stretching, condensing, shadowing) is prohibited. Left alignment is mandatory, and individual letter spacing for titles must be manually tightened within reason.
- Minimum font sizes are 6pt for Museo Sans 100–500 and 10pt for Museo Sans 900. No upper size limit applies, and text color can be set to grey or white-out on solid backgrounds.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Identity montages consist of bold typography, vibrant colors, and distinctive imagery based on square grids. Montages should use identity colors and feature landmarks cropped or photographed in intriguing ways.
- Avoid standard photography styles, public faces, and non-square or non-rectangular image shapes. Montages should incorporate abstract or textural images related to the subject and adhere to square grid layouts.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Square grid forms the basis for identity layout design, ensuring visual uniformity across montages and materials.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand's tone aligns with themes such as vibrancy, cultural connectivity, and historical significance. Imagery and communications must reflect this 'revealed' essence and combine modern enterprise with creative storytelling.

**Notes:**

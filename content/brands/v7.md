## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- Clear space rules state that the logo needs clear space around it at a minimum measurement of '1X height of logo.'
- Logo format includes both vertical and horizontal versions. Vertical use is recommended for tall layouts like posters, and horizontal use is recommended for horizontal layouts like banners.
- For products, a simple one-color version of the logo using PMS Cool Grey 5 is specified. Sizing rule suggests '6mm width,' but must be reviewed based on product specifics.
- Examples of incorrect logo usage include altering colors, rotating the logo, placing it on low-contrast backgrounds, adding drop shadows, and stretching/distorting the logo.

**Color Information:**

- Pantone color 2925C is mentioned in association with logo guidelines.
- PMS Cool Grey 5 is specified for the one-color version of the logo for product usage.
- The V7 Select brand uses a color palette including V7 Orange and Grey colors, as specified in the sleeve packaging examples.
- The V7 Classic brand sleeve example uses the V7 Blue color palette.

**Typography Information:**

- Font family selected for branding messages is Myriad Pro, known for versatility and readability.
- Typography styles include Myriad Pro Regular, Semi-Bold, Condensed, and Bold Condensed.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum clear space for logo defined as '1X height of logo.' No design elements should overlap or crowd the logo.

**Notes:**

- Approval process requires V7 branding approval before production, with a turnaround time of 2 business days.
- V7 retains proprietary ownership of all packaging files created by the manufacturer for V7 products. Approved files must be sent to V7 for archiving.

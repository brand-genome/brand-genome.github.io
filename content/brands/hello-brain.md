## Brand Guidelines

### 2014

**Logo Information:**

- Logo should be reproduced in color where possible and positioned above photographic elements with plenty of white space around it. Single color (black) and reversed (white) versions can be used in specific contexts. File formats mentioned for color versions: EPS, JPEG. For black versions: EPS, JPEG. For reversed (white) versions: EPS, PNG.
- A small screen version of the logo has been created for digital media in JPEG and PNG formats, available in two sizes: 36x36 px and 48x48 px, and should not be used for print media.
- Minimum clear space is determined by the height of the 'H' in the word 'HELLO'. Minimum size for printing is 15mm wide to ensure legibility.
- URL variant of logo for restricted spaces. Available in color, black (eps, jpeg formats), or white (eps, png).
- Incorrect Logo Usage: Do not use elements in isolation, change logo colors, adjust, rotate, stretch, or distort the logo, use the wrong version for the background, or place it on unsuitable backgrounds that affect legibility. Do not 'blow up' small screen versions for normal size.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors listed with codes: Pantone 1235 (C:0, M:36, Y:98, K:0, R:255 G:182 B:18, HEX:#FFB612); Process Magenta (C:0, M:100, Y:0, K:0, R:209 G:0 B:116, HEX:#D10074); Pantone Cool Grey 10 (C:30, M:23, Y:17, K:58, R:116 G:118 B:120, HEX:#747676). Secondary colors: Pantone 400 (C:6, M:7, Y:11, K:16, R:203 G:199 B:191, HEX:#CBC7BF); Pantone 234 (C:18, M:100, Y:4, K:18, R:161 G:0 B:107, HEX:#A1006B), etc.
- Color usage rules: Vibrant, energetic combinations with orange, pink. Gradients may be used but not mixed; use reversed out (white) logo on gradients.

**Typography Information:**

- Corporate font: Museo Sans Rounded 900—used for headlines and straplines. Reflects friendly, accessible tone.
- Avenir (Book 45, Heavy 85) for subheadings and body copy. Arial Regular and Arial Rounded Bold recommended alternatives when Avenir is unavailable.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography style: Portraits with inquisitive expressions on white backgrounds; cropped at the bridge of the nose to spotlight faces. Photographs aligned to engage with the logo. Always prioritize curiosity, vibrancy, and human-centered imagery.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Layouts should be clean and crisp with ample white space around logo. Photos should be cropped to the bottom page edge; logo placement ideally in the top-left corner.

**Brand Voice:**

- Friendly and accessible tone, with a focus on encouraging proactive and positive actions for brain health. The tone is clear and engaging to support easy understanding.

**Notes:**

- Print recommendations: Standard 'A' sizes for artwork. All artwork in CMYK color process. Use specific paper qualities for different materials (e.g., 400 gsm silk stock for folders; 200 gsm silk stock for flyers and posters).
- Translation of printed content: Requests should be directed to hellobrain@tcd.ie. Content translations must maintain scientific integrity with professional standards and be proofed by native language speakers.
- Additional resources (printable app, flyers, ambassador letters) available at www.hellobrain.eu/en/resources. Supported formats include print-ready PDFs and packaged artwork (InDesign CS6).

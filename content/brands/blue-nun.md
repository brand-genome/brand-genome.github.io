## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The Blue Nun logo has been specifically developed for corporate, promotional, and internal materials. It must not be recreated or altered in any way.
- The logo is available in Full Colour and Monochrome versions. The Monochrome variant should only be used when the Full Colour version cannot be reproduced or will not reproduce well.
- There is a special 'Line' version of the logo created for restricted space scenarios (e.g., printed pens or similar merchandise). It should only be used when Full Colour or Monochrome options are not viable.
- 'Monochrome' logo includes specific examples for small promotional items such as pencils and wine glasses.
- Minimum size for the logo width is 20 mm.

**Color Information:**

- Logo colors in CMYK: {Blue: C=100, M=68, K=84}, {Alternate Blue: C=93, M=67, K=29}, {Light Blue: C=93, M=67}, {Gold: C=20, M=30, Y=70, K=15}

**Typography Information:**

- The primary typeface for printed collateral (headline or display) is 'Trajan', available in bold and regular weights.
- Body copy and small print use 'Frutiger' font, available in the following weights: bold, light, bold condensed, light condensed.
- Font substitution or unavailability must be addressed through consultation with the Langguth marketing team.

**Brand Voice:**

- 'Blue Nun' is positioned as delivering accessibility (elegance, quality, and affordability), simplicity, and confidence. Its values include fun, sociability, and good times.
- 'Blue Nun' brand reflects core values of quality, style, memories, and straightforward human traits such as honesty and simplicity.

**Notes:**

- Marketing materials and brand image are actively protected under brand book compliance. Assistance is available from the Langguth marketing team.

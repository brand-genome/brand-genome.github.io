**Color Information:**

- Color breakdown guidelines (PMS codes): PMS 349 C, PMS 107 C, PMS 5395 C, PMS 126 C, PMS Black C, PMS 484 C. Usage includes company and program logos.
- Color rules: Black/white logos are provided for instances where full color cannot be displayed. Black should be used on light backgrounds (<50% grayscale), and white logos only on darker backgrounds (≥50% grayscale). Logos cannot be used in non-approved colors.

**Typography Information:**

- Font choice preferences include Minion Pro, Adobe Garamond Pro, Gill Sans MT, and Helvetica LT Com. These fonts should be used for ISA related wordmarks and materials for consistency.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Guidance for placing logos over images: Must maintain free space rules; color logos are recommended. Logos must not overlap edges of images or two different backgrounds. Backgrounds should extend beyond the logo and free space area.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Logo spacing and sizing: Maintain 5% of logo height as free space around logos; text must be set outside free space area. Minimum height for logos is ½-inch, suitable for cards. Resize logos proportionally without distortion.

**Notes:**

- Notes on obtaining logos: Electronic versions are accessible via logging into the ISA website in 'My Profile' section. Specify file type, color preference (black/white) during requests. Verification of credentials required.

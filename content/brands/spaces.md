### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Master Logo
This is the Spaces primary logo. It is set in Spaces Rock Grey. Always apply on a white or clean background without clashing colours or complex imagery. Always set the total height of the logo as the minimum spacing around all edges (see Clear Space on the next page).
Logo Variations
If required, you can also apply the logo in reverse: Figure 2: on Spaces Dark Grey background.
- Misuse
Logo Misuse
Do not use the Spaces logo in any way that damages the brand, including but not limited to: removing or altering the hexagon symbol or full-stop, changing the typography or applying an off-brand colour (see page 8). 
Do not change logo colour
Do not move the hexagon
Do not use a different font
Do not place on complex imagery
Do not remove full stop
Do not use this logo
- Minimum spacing between co-branded logos should match the height of the hexagon. The logos should align to cap height or three times the height of the Spaces hexagon logo for visual consistency.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography Styles: Three Styles
Spaces Heading One style uses all caps, in black weight, set optically at minus 50. Heading Two is sentence case in bold. Body copy is set in light weight; dramatic hierarchy contrast preferred.
- Typography Misuse
Heading One must not be used in lowercase or sentence case. Heading Two must remain in sentence case only. Overusing bold within body text is discouraged; visual lightness and hierarchy preferred.

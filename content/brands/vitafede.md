## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- The logo embodies movement, diversity with compositional elements (point, arrow, line, cross). It includes the name link (Federation identity) and connection between letters forming a dynamic design. Variations include full color and potentially composites for clubs and federations.

**Color Information:**

- The color palette revolves around modernized variations of the Federation colors, incorporating blue and green tones.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography includes Gilroy Black (used for headlines at 54pt), Gilroy Medium (used for body text at various sizes). Tracking is specified (-20), and leading adjusts based on size (e.g., 12pt for smaller text applications).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery applications suggest consistency and alignment rules for visual representation across templates and communication templates such as paper headers and signs.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Spacing rules on several templates, including defined margins for scenarios like headers (20mm, 42mm width rules) and consistent positioning for typography in layouts.

**Brand Voice:**

- A positive tone highlighting values: vitality, pleasure, conviviality, and inclusivity. Vision emphasizes inclusivity (sport for all) and using sport as a social bonding medium.

**Notes:**

- The guideline states legal ownership by the Federation Française d'Education Physique et Gymnastique Volontaire. Adherence is mandatory, and dissemination outside authorized parties is prohibited, specifying department-level contact for exceptions or special needs.

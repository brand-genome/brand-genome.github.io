## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The DECA brand identifies a remarkable experience in the preparation of emerging leaders and entrepreneurs. Official DECA logos are the intellectual property of the entire DECA community, with guidelines for acceptable use published to ensure protection from unauthorized use. Logos can only be used by recognized members, chapters, and associations.
- The DECA brand features horizontal logos for branding documents, signage, posters, and displays. Vertical logos are suggested for profile images or where horizontal logos do not fit. Separate versions exist for high school and collegiate divisions.
- Trademark policies allow limited use of DECA logos (various formats like horizontal and with chapter/association variations) on printed materials and websites by officially affiliated groups only. Logos require prior permission for promotional use.
- Clear space guidelines mandate a minimum of half the height of the 'E' in the logo to be maintained on all sides. The logo should not appear below 0.5 inches on screen or in print.
- Incorrect usage examples for DECA logos include altering colors, stretching or distorting the logo, placing elements within the clear space, unauthorized text additions, and rotating or redrawing parts of the logo.

**Color Information:**

- The DECA logo may be printed in blue (PMS 285 C, CMYK: 84, 54, 0, 0, RGB: 0, 114, 206, HEX: #0072ce), black, white (CMYK: 0, 0, 0, 0, RGB: 255, 255, 255), metallic gold (PMS 871 C, CMYK: 44, 45, 74, 17, RGB: 134, 117, 79, HEX: #85754e), and metallic silver (PMS 877 C, CMYK: 48, 39, 39, 3, RGB: 139, 141, 141, HEX: #8c9091).

**Typography Information:**

- The official typeface for DECA is Gotham Bold. A secondary typeface is Source Sans Pro, which can be used for subtitles, subheadings, and lengthy text.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Clear space for the logo must be at least half the height of the 'E' in the DECA logo. Minimum size for the logo is 0.5 inches for both screen and print. There is no maximum size limit.

**Brand Voice:**

- The DECA brand embodies a tradition of excellence, identifying a remarkable experience in preparing emerging leaders and entrepreneurs, retaining historic connections and name recognition. Messaging focuses on leadership and entrepreneurship preparation.

**Notes:**

- DECA guidelines include detailed rules for logo use, aligned with intellectual property laws. Promotional use requires specific permissions. Items with DECA insignia should only come from authorized vendors (Shop DECA or Awards Unlimited).
- DECA promotes experiential learning through real-world projects, connections with businesses, and competition, preparing members as academically driven, community-oriented, and experienced leaders.
- Official DECA websites and publications contain protected logos and cannot be reproduced without DECA Inc.'s consent.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Logo information is extensively covered, mentioning specific versions (square, horizontal, monotone, black, white), exclusion zones, and minimum size. For example, exclusion zones for logos should always maintain one-quarter of the UKRI symbol height around them, as shown on page 23.

**Color Information:**

- UKRI employs a comprehensive color palette system, including Pantone®, CMYK, HEX, and RGB variants. Primary colors are defined for councils along with accent colors based on a color wheel principle. Guidance is provided for usage across print and digital media.

**Typography Information:**

- Fonts used include primary typefaces and supporting options, with specifications for headline roles and body text. Pages 33 through 36 specify font families and styles.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Imagery is categorized into Micro, Macro, subject detail, and portraits. Detailed guidelines recommend maintaining natural tones, avoiding overly staged compositions, and selecting colors from the UKRI-approved palette.

**Spacing Layout:**

- UKRI defines rules for grid alignment and spacing. Core shapes and their uses are outlined alongside rules for layout consistency. These details are provided on pages 38 through 42.

**Brand Voice:**

- UKRI communicates with a tone emphasizing collaboration, innovation, and impact. Clear, objective messaging helps align with the organization's purpose of creating knowledge with impact.

**Notes:**

- Contact the brand team at brand@ukri.org for guidelines and support. Sustainability considerations for print production and paper/ink choices are discussed in the final sections along with the availability of download assets on the UKRI website.
- Extensive discussion on the mission and organizational structure of UKRI and its nine councils, as well as the importance of brand consistency across communications.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2024

**Logo Information:**

- The Reed College logotype is versatile and timeless, available in horizontal and stacked formats and suitable for both print and digital materials.
- Clear space/safe zone rules for logos are indicated using 'X' as a measurement—a buffer of 1X distance is shown around the logo.
- The circular griffin logomark can accompany the logotype. It is a distinctive graphic element used on formal materials such as the college seal and merchandise.
- The recommended logo lockup combines the logotype and logomark for balance and legibility.
- The presidential seal is reserved for specific institutional uses (President's Office, Dean's Office, convocation/commencement programs, and college diplomas).
- Partner logos follow a consistent format using Reed's brand colors and fonts alongside the official logo.
- Historical griffins are part of the Reed identity but must not replace the current branding. The new griffin logomark is a simplified silhouette based on a 1998 illustration.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors: Reed Red (PMS 1807, CMYK C0 M100 Y96 K35, RGB R167 G14 B22, HEX #A70E16) and Black Ink (PMS Black, CMYK C0 M0 Y0 K100, RGB R0 G0 B0, HEX #000000). Reed Red is especially emphasized.
- Secondary colors include: Canyon Fern (CMYK C52 M33 Y67 K10, RGB R126 G138 B101, HEX #7E8A65), Golden Laurel (CMYK C25 M38 Y73 K2, RGB R191 G153 B93, HEX #BF995D), No.2 Pencil (CMYK C0 M22 Y83 K0, RGB R255 G200 B70, HEX #FFC846), Parchment (CMYK C0 M2 Y8 K0, RGB R255 G247 B233, HEX #FFF7E9), Reactor Core (CMYK C10 M3 Y6 K1, RGB R223 G231 B231, HEX #DFE7E7), Blue Bridge (CMYK C79 M51 Y42 K17, RGB R63 G101 B118, HEX #3F6574), Newsprint (CMYK C7 M5 Y8 K0, RGB R236 G234 B228, HEX #ECEAE4), Adirondack (CMYK C3 M87 Y86 K0, RGB R232 G74 B55, HEX #E84A37).

**Typography Information:**

- Primary font: GT Sectra Fine, a serif typeface available in Book and Medium weights. Known for high legibility and distinctive character influenced by calligraphy and a rigorous academic aesthetic.
- Secondary font: National 2, a sans-serif with distinctive but simple details, offering timelessness and functionality for print and digital applications. Available in Regular and Medium weights.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photography and video should capture authentic, candid moments of the Reed community engaging in everyday activities. Imagery should convey warmth and authenticity with natural colors and softness resembling the human eye's perception.
- Photo/video editing: Alterations that mislead or misrepresent are prohibited. Quality enhancements are acceptable as long as the integrity of the content remains unchanged.
- Stock photography/videography should only be used when absolutely necessary.
- Branded videos must begin and/or end with the Reed logotype, logomark, or logo lockup.
- Reed emphasizes honesty and inclusivity in demographic representation while striving to avoid harm in its photo and video use.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Marginal spacing for logos defines clear space using 'X' notation.

**Brand Voice:**

- The Reed brand identity reflects curiosity, authenticity, and integrity, aligning with the college's distinctive traditions and values.

**Notes:**

- Resources (e.g., self-service downloads, request forms, website guidelines) are available on the Public Affairs website: reed.edu/public-affairs/
- The Reed College griffin was derived from the coat of arms of its founder, Simeon Reed, and symbolizes knowledge.
- Contact details for Reed College Public Affairs: public.affairs@reed.edu, Phone: 503-777-7591.

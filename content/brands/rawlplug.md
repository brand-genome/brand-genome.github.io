## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The Rawlplug logo consists of two elements: the Rawlplug symbol and the Rawlplug wordmark. Logo must be accompanied by the appropriate ® symbol. Basic logo versions include: basic version, minimal version (24 mm wide), reverse versions (white on blue background), alternate badge version used for packaging layouts and photographic images.
- Color variations of the logo include Rawlplug Blue (Spot Color), White (Reversed on Rawlplug Blue), Black (for non-color print), and Gray (for economical print).
- Clear space rule: Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the logo.
- Logo misuse rules: Do not typeset the wordmark, change the color of the logo except for specified manuals, rearrange logo elements, stretch the logo disproportionately, change the shape of the badge, change proportions of logo elements, or use patterns within the logo.
- Incorrect logo usage examples shown: stretching, re-coloring, adding patterns, and rearranging elements.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors for branding: Rawlplug Blue (Pantone 541, CMYK: 100-57-0-38, RGB: 0-51-102, HEX: #003366), Black (Pantone Black, CMYK: 0-0-0-100, RGB: 0-0-0, HEX: #000000), Gray (Pantone Cool Gray, CMYK: 0-0-0-70, RGB: 104-103-108, HEX: #68676c).
- The logo should ideally be used on Rawlplug Blue or white backgrounds for maximum impact. On dark photos or backgrounds, use the white logo version; on light colored backgrounds, use the Rawlplug Blue logo or alternate badge version.
- Quality of reproduction materials impacts brand perception. High-quality materials (paint, foil, paper, cardboard) and modern printing techniques are recommended to maintain precision in color reproduction.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography for the brand: Ubuntu Font Family specified for all marketing communications. Regular and Condensed Ubuntu typefaces recommended. Ubuntu Monotype fonts may be used for terminals/programmers. Alternative: Tahoma for online documents, emails, PowerPoint presentations.
- Ubuntu fonts available: Regular, Regular Italic, Regular Bold, Regular Bold Italic, Condensed, Monotype Normal, Monotype Italic, Monotype Bold, Monotype Bold Italic, Light, Light Condensed.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Background imagery should not compromise clarity of the logo. Use light backgrounds for Rawlplug Blue logo or dark backgrounds for reversed white logo. Ensure photographic elements do not obstruct logo appearance.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Minimum clear space rule: Clear space must equal the height of the logo at all scaled sizes.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand attributes include trust, innovation, reliability, and safety, emphasizing construction anchors and ancillary products used in diverse markets.

**Notes:**

- Rawlplug brand guidelines emphasize usage of approved digital artwork for logo reproduction, caution against mediocrity in materials, and uphold precision in brand elements across all mediums.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Logotype includes 'RENATA' emphasizing connectivity, science, education, and technology. The word 'COLOMBIA' is integrated for international identity.
- Isotype developed using an 8x8 module grid tilted at 15°. Includes 'COLOMBIA' for alignment with organization identity.
- Clear space is defined by spacing equal to the distance between 'R' in RENATA and the baseline of 'COLOMBIA.'
- Logo variations: Full version for formal use, repetition requiring simplified version, and square format for social media.
- Prohibited usages: No vertical layouts, rotations, color or composition changes, shadows, decompositions, or perspective distortion.
- Minimum sizes for various versions: Full version (print: 35 mm x 6.83 mm; screen: 180 px x 35 px), Isotype with 'COLOMBIA' (print: 20 mm x 5.86 mm; screen: 120 px x 35 px), 'R' without 'COLOMBIA' (print: 8 mm x 8 mm; screen: 50 px x 50 px).

**Color Information:**

- Institutional colors: Navy Blue (CMYK C100 M87 Y0 K20, HEX #001A70, RGB R0 G26 B112, Pantone 662C), Aguamarine (CMYK C59 M0 Y22 K0, HEX #2DCCD3, RGB R45 G204 B211, Pantone 319C), Fuchsia (CMYK C20 M89 Y0 K0, HEX #C5299B, RGB R197 G41 B155, Pantone 240C).
- Secondary colors are darker variations of institutional palette, intended for adjacent compositions. Example: Pantone 2758C (CMYK C100 M95 Y0 K40).
- Complementary colors are reserved for diversification without replacing institutional tones, e.g., Green (CMYK C81 M0 Y92 K0, HEX #00B140, RGB R0 G177 B64, Pantone 354C).
- Gradients allowed for backgrounds using institutional tones only.
- Website palette adheres to corporate colors for navigation buttons, banners, and icons with Open Sans and Roboto fonts.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary fonts: Open Sans (titles) and Roboto (body text and subtitles). Recommendations for compression and bold formatting for readability.
- Secondary fonts: Program OT, Calibri, Arial; used for specific contexts such as body text when main fonts are unavailable.
- Fonts for service icons: Ubuntu Normal for first line, Ubuntu Bold for second line with compressed spacing.
- Fonts recommended for proposals and presentations: Open Sans with varied spacing and sizes (14–36 points).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Photos on the website should use duotones for consistency.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Letterhead margins: Top 4 cm, others 2.5 cm. Content suggested using Calibri font (size 11 points).
- Proposal margins: Top 3 cm; sides and bottom 2.5 cm.
- Communication materials use specific sizes for letterheads, folders, envelopes, invoices, and business cards.

**Brand Voice:**

- Brand aims to project trust, advancement, and connection. Guidelines ensure uniformity across media while supporting creativity.

**Notes:**

- Uniforms allow custom designs or institutional-colored apparel with logos. Embroidery and silk-screen prints align to Pantone references.
- Office branding supports vinyl applications or 3D lettering using corporate identity consistency.
- Promotional objects such as pens, mugs, and hats utilize corporate colors and logos effectively.

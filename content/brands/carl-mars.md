## Brand Guidelines

### 2023

**Logo Information:**

- The logo consists of the text 'ARS' combined with two curves symbolizing sound waves. The curves are horizontally centered by the text.
- Recommended to always use the logo in its 100% transparency unless material or printing limitations arise. Forms with lesser transparency, but no less than 25%, are considered acceptable.
- Minimum clear space around the logo equals the height of the letter 'A' in 'ARS'.
- The inverse version of the logo alters the color of the entire logo to ensure legibility in dark settings.
- Incorrect usage includes: using colors other than those defined, deforming the logo, rearranging elements, rendering it illegible, altering proportions of elements, or using the logo at too small of a size.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: Pantone 2738 C - CMYK: 100/91/0/13, RGB: (6, 3, 141), HEX: #06038D.
- Secondary color: Pantone 1785 C - CMYK: 0/82/51/0, RGB: (248, 72, 94), HEX: #F8485E.
- Inverse version uses Pantone Cool Gray 11 C.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary headline font for print: Avenir Next Rounded, weights: Medium/Bold. Body text font: Open Sans, Regular weight.
- For web use: Headline font: Nunito Sans, Bold weight. Font for regular web paragraphs: Open Sans, Regular weight.

**Spacing Layout:**

- For business cards, the size is 85 x 55 mm. Text specifications: name in Avenir Next Rounded 8pt, other details in Avenir Next Rounded 7pt, line spacing as AUTO/normal. Colors: White background with Pantone 2738 C. Backside: Pantone 2738 C background.
- For letterheads: A4 size. Fonts and colors must follow guidelines. Footer (left-aligned) minimum font size is 7pt. Paragraph text (right-aligned) is 10pt. Sizes specified in millimeters.
- For envelopes: C5 dimensions - logo placed 10 mm from the top and left edge with a width of 50 mm. C4 dimensions - logo placed 15 mm from the top and left edge with a width of 50 mm. Lower color stripe is optional, dependent on printing technology.

**Notes:**

- Printing methods prioritize the use of Pantone color matching, followed by CMYK for large print specifications. For digital platforms, HEX codes are preferred. Monochromatic printing requires consulting the logo designer.
- Merchandising rules recommend using a blue logo on white backgrounds or its inverse (white logo on blue).

## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- The logo consists of two elements: the wordmark 'Exposure Ninja' which represents branding; and an icon, a Shuriken (throwing star), symbolizing the brand's essence.
- Logo colors include black, white, various shades of grey, and the primary base color #034694. Different shades of blue (#0c6bbb, #0598e8, #017bce, #034694) are used within the logo. Do not use colors outside these tones, or apply gradients.
- Incorrect logo usage includes: altering components, applying shadow effects, changing proportions or placement of elements, using unauthorized colors, applying gradients, cutting out portions, stretching, rotating, slanting, or placing the logo inside shapes.
- The logo is shown in positive space with standard treatment.
- The logo is shown in negative space with reversed treatment.

**Color Information:**

- Primary colors include #5ac5d0, #53b2c7, #24bff1, #1895d7, #ea1e54, #fec930, #cbcc2a. The palette consists of fun, colorful pastels, used for clean, minimalistic layouts.
- Supported colors for the logo include shades of blue (#034694, #0c6bbb, #0598e8, #017bce) and shades of grey (#919191, #cbcbcb, #e7e7e7, #ffffff). RGB values: #919191 (R:145, G:145, B:145), #cbcbcb (R:203, G:203, B:203), #e7e7e7 (R:231, G:231, B:231), #ffffff (R:255, G:255, B:255).

**Typography Information:**

- Font choices: Lato for HEADER 1 (Bold, Font size: 72); Montserrat for HEADER 2 (Bold, Font size: 30) and Paragraph text (Font size 10-17, all weights); Muli for Paragraph text (Font size 10-17, all weights).
- The website button style uses the Montserrat font enclosed in a sharp-edged box. The font size should be one-third of the total button height. The box width has no limit.

**Imagery Photography:**

- The Shinobi mascot is a ninja-themed character featured in marketing materials, advertisements, and designs. He has a range of facial expressions to suit different themes and always includes an oblong-shaped shadow for visual emphasis.
- Shinobi mascot images can depict real-life or professional settings (e.g., working tabletop scenarios) and are used in promotional materials.

**Notes:**

- This brand manual includes logo elements and compositions, color palettes, typography, mascot usage, and website button styles. It emphasizes cohesive and consistent brand identity across all touchpoints.
- Employees can access PNG, JPG, or PSD files of Shinobi mascot characters, with customizable facial expressions or props accessed via design team assistance.

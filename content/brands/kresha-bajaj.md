## Brand Guidelines

### 2021

**Logo Information:**

- Logo clear space must be maintained using a zone calculated from the height of 'KB' in the logo, referred to as 'X'. Other elements should not invade this zone.
- The logo-mark (KB initials) is used where the full logo cannot appear. It works well in small spaces as a seal, small tags, or app icons.
- Minimum size for logo must be 14.4pt in print and 14px in digital environments to ensure readability.
- Logo placement is restricted to four positions: flush against the left or right-hand margins or aligned to the top or bottom margins.

**Color Information:**

- Pantone Black, RGB: 0/0/0, HEX: #101820, CMYK: 0-0-0-100. This appears as the primary color.
- Colors should be used in combinations that avoid low readability, e.g., avoid black text on dark backgrounds or white on light colors. Five predefined color combinations exist.

**Typography Information:**

- Typeface: Montserrat. Created by Julieta Ulanovsky. Regular weight is used as one primary font.
- Typeface: Montserrat. Created by Julieta Ulanovsky. Semi-Bold weight is also specified for use.
- Hierarchy is established through varying font sizes and weights. Larger sizes for headlines, smaller sizes for body text and captions.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Images should reflect the brand colors and match the tone defined by the color palette.
- Brand mood imagery is used to depict the ideal look and feel. Focuses on evoking specific emotions or themes and aligning with brand aesthetics such as color, typography, and textures.
- Specified use of overlays in imagery to align with brand tone.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Stationery layout: The logo is centered on the front of business cards; personal details are set on the back. Dimensions for business cards are specified as 85x55mm.
- Grid system: Posters use a 4-column grid for alignment, with footer information aligned to the bottom of the grid.

**Brand Voice:**

- Core brand values include: Ethereal, Soft, Honest, Warm.
- Brand messaging is positioned to influence and motivate buyers. The message emphasizes value propositions and differentiation from competitors.

**Notes:**

- The Fairytale Funding EMI program allows bridal wear purchases on payment plans, reflecting a commitment to making luxury accessible.
- The ‘Care for Karigars’ initiative, launched in collaboration with another brand, supports artisans by creating exclusive pieces with proceeds aiding their livelihood.
- Mobile homepage and inner page design details provided, including dimensions (2436x1125px for homepage, 750x1334px for inner pages) and alignment guidelines.
- Website homepage design for mobile tablets (such as iPads and Surfaces) includes features like Fairytale Funding and Love Story categories.

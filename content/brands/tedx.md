## Brand Guidelines

### 2009

**Logo Information:**

- The TEDx logo should adhere to specific alignment requirements. Placenames should always align in relation to the TEDx logo using dotted lines and must be sized to the exact height of the logo.
- Only use the 2-line template for longer placenames. Do not change the color of the 'x' in TEDx, the tagline, or the placename color. Avoid all-caps or size alterations.
- The TEDx logo should only be used on solid white or black backgrounds. Avoid placing the logo on photographic, patterned, or illustrative backgrounds.
- Clear-space requirements state that a minimum buffer zone must be maintained around the entire perimeter of the logo. No graphics, copy, or other elements should intrude on this space.
- The minimum size for the TEDx logo is 2.0 inches in width to ensure legibility, especially for the tagline.
- Incorrect logo usage examples provided: Do not change colors, capitalization, alignment, or font styling of the logo elements.
- Official TEDx logo templates (vector EPS) are provided and must be accessed via the TEDx partner site. Access is limited to licensed TEDx partners.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color: TED Red - Pantone 485, CMYK 0/100/100/0, RGB 255/43/6, Web FF2B06.
- Primary color: TED Black - Pantone Black, CMYK 0/0/0/100, RGB 0/0/0, Web 000000.
- Background colors: Only solid white or black backgrounds can be used for the TEDx logo or branding.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary typeface for TEDx communications is Helvetica Regular. If Helvetica is unavailable, Arial is the permissible substitute. Helvetica Neue (Regular weight) can also be used.
- Letter-spacing (kerning) and alignment guidelines for placenames are explicitly defined in relation to the logo. Placenames must align horizontally and be the same height as the TEDx logo.

**Spacing Layout:**

- A minimum clear-space buffer must be maintained around the logo at all times, with consistent spacing from the logo's edges.
- The minimum width for the TEDx logo is 2.0 inches. The tagline should remain legible at all sizes.
- Placename alignment to the TEDx logo is strict and must follow positioning rules dictated by the provided templates.

**Brand Voice:**

- The TEDx voice is professional, clear, and consistent with the ethos of 'ideas worth spreading.' Descriptions of TEDx must emphasize its local, self-organized nature under a TED license.
- Correct copy usage: TEDx must always be written as 'TEDx' with no variations (e.g., not TEDX, TedX, or TED-x). Placenames must be written as 'TEDxPlacename' (no space between TEDx and the location).

**Notes:**

- Examples of incorrect logo use are provided: changing colors, altering alignment or scale, setting placenames in all caps, or recreating the logo in a different typeface.
- Website guidelines: TEDx websites should include the TEDx logo on every page, link back to ted.com/tedx, and provide text in the footer stating 'This independent TEDx event is operated under license from TED.'
- Social media usage: Event names should include 'TEDx' followed by the location, with proper spacing (e.g., 'TEDx Tokyo' rather than 'TEDxTokyo').
- Partnership guidelines: TEDx partnerships emphasize community support and prohibit advertising or revenue generation outside the scope of event costs.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2019

**Logo Information:**

- Jet logo. Download logos at purple.jet.com. Primary and secondary logo options include ensuring legibility—place secondary logo over a solid Jet purple #360060 color block for better contrast. Spacing: At least 20 px padding around the logo. Minimum size: Digital - 75 pixels height, Print - 0.75 inches height. Placement: Over image or solid colors.

**Color Information:**

- PRIMARY: Purple, SECONDARY FOR BACKGROUND: Neutral Colors. CREATIVE CONTENT COLORS include supplementary, light, bright, warmer, cooler tints. Examples of key Jet Purple: #360060 (C97 M100 Y0 K18, PMS 2745).

**Typography Information:**

- Font: Bloomfield family includes Light, Regular, and Bold weights. Downloadable through purple.jet.com.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Jet photography approach focuses on candid, empathetic, and relatable imagery. Scenes feature a 'lightly lived-in' appearance like toys in a room, magazines on chairs, or ice cream for breakfast, conveying a real-life connection.

**Spacing Layout:**

- EMAIL layouts focus on a mobile-first rule: straight headlines with direct response deals using lifestyle imagery and emojis.

**Brand Voice:**

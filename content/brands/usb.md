## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- Basic-Speed USB Logo with 1-color, black, and reversed versions.
- Hi-Speed USB Logo includes 2-color, 1-color black, and reversed options.
- SuperSpeed USB Logo preferred as 2-color or black, with reversed options.
- SuperSpeed USB 10 Gbps Logo preferred as 2-color, black, with reversed options.
- Logo Don’ts: guidelines explicitly prohibit variation across all logo versions.

**Color Information:**

- USB Blue (Pantone 653C) with CMYK (C:100 M:62 Y:0 K:20) and RGB (R:0 G:82 B:149).
- USB Red (Pantone 185C) with CMYK (C:0 M:91 Y:76 K:0) and RGB (R:239 G:62 B:66).
- Wattage Gray: 70% Black, CMYK (C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:70), and RGB (R:120 G:120 B:120).

- Horizontally and vertically centered logo grouping examples for USB Type-C™ port.

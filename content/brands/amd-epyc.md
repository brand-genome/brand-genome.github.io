## Brand Guidelines

### 2017

**Logo Information:**

- Stylized, animated, hand-drawn, or alternate versions of logos are strictly prohibited to ensure brand consistency.
- Logo versions available for different media: 4 color (light background), 1 color (light background), 4 color (dark background), and 1 color (dark background).
- Logo provided in file formats: RGB for web and CMYK for print. PMS spot color available upon request.
- Minimum size requirements: Digital applications - 72 pixels wide; Out-of-home media - 10% of total area; Print media - 0.75 inches (19.05 mm) wide.
- Clear space defined around logo: Minimum clear space is specified to ensure visibility and impact, with no elements allowed to intrude into this space.
- AMD logo extensions: Maximum of 8 characters for extension names. Extension width must not exceed AMD EPYC logotype width.
- Co-branding guidelines: Clear space must be equal to 1x the white square in the AMD EPYC logo around each logo lock-up. Minimum logo width for AMD EPYC is 0.75 inches.

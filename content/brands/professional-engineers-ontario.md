## Brand Guidelines

### 2015

**Logo Information:**

- The logo consists of a diamond-shaped abstract symbol and the signature "Professional Engineers Ontario," typeset in Adobe Garamond (bold and light weights). Signature must always accompany the symbol. The logo cannot be incorporated into text or used with other graphic elements.
- The relationship between the symbol and the signature in terms of size and space must remain constant and cannot be changed.
- Minimum size for the logo: Symbol must be 2 picas wide, and signature in 8 point type. No maximum size specified.
- Reproduction guidelines: The logo should only be reproduced from first-generation proofs or electronic file formats.
- When used with chapter or committee names, both elements of the logo must be present, and guidelines for minimum space requirements, typeface, and color must be followed.

**Typography Information:**

- Typography used for the logo signature includes Adobe Garamond (A Garamond), bold and light weights.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Relationships between the symbol and signature, including spacing, must remain constant across all applications.

**Brand Voice:**

- Professional Engineers Ontario (PEO)'s logo conveys the dynamic spirit of the engineering profession and represents the dimensions of engineering enhancing life quality.
- Text references for the organization include 'Professional Engineers Ontario' and 'PEO' in proper formatting conventions.
- The legal name, 'Association of Professional Engineers of Ontario,' must be used in legal documents and appears on official materials like letterheads and business cards.

**Notes:**

- External organizations require express permission before using the PEO logo, including science camps, schools, or sponsors.
- The logo is corporate in nature and cannot be used by individuals for personal identification purposes.
- Questions about logo use or standards should be directed to the Publication Coordinator at Professional Engineers Ontario.

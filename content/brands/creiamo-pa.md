## Brand Guidelines

### 2018

**Logo Information:**

- The logo is the core element of the visual identity system and represents the sun through a circular distribution of smooth lines resembling rays of light. The logo is combined with the CReIAMO PA logotype to form the brand's logo.
- The logo can be arranged in two configurations: vertical composition (epigraphe) and horizontal composition (a bandiera).
- The logo has positive color versions, black and white versions, and negative color and white versions. Positive colored versions should only be used on white or light-colored backgrounds. Negative color versions should use institutional dark blue background.
- The clear space around the logo should be equal to the height of the 'CReIAMO PA' logotype. This clear space is a minimum requirement and should be increased wherever possible.
- The minimum size recommendations for the logo are provided explicitly, ranging from a base size of 5mm to a required size of 38mm depending on the context and version.
- Alignment rules state that the minimal clear space around the logo should always be ensured when placed adjacent to other text or elements. Placement with institutional logos should maintain the hierarchy and proper spacing.
- Recommended usage of digital originals released only by the appointed office to use logos correctly, adhering to clear space and minimum size rules.
- Examples of incorrect logo use include reducing its legibility, incorrect versions or dimensions, manipulating internal elements or typography, deforming or distorting the logo, ignoring area spacing rules, and modifying logo colors.

**Color Information:**

- Institutional colors include: Blu CReIAMO PA (Pantone 2945 C, CMYK 100, 53, 2, 16, RGB 0, 76, 151), Verde CReIAMO PA (Pantone 355 C, CMYK 91, 0, 100, 0, RGB 0, 150, 57), Blu scuro CReIAMO PA (Pantone 540 C, CMYK 100, 57, 12, 66, RGB 0, 48, 87), Bianco (CMYK 0, 0, 0, 0, RGB 255, 255, 255), Nero (CMYK 0, 0, 0, 100, RGB 0, 0, 0), Celeste CReIAMO PA (Pantone 2915 C, CMYK 60, 9, 0, 0, RGB 98, 181, 229), Giallo CReIAMO PA (Pantone 7549 C, CMYK 0, 22, 100, 2, RGB 255, 181, 0).
- The institutional color gradient uses a linear vertical progression involving the Blu scuro CReIAMO PA (0%), Giallo CReIAMO PA (100%), Celeste CReIAMO PA, Verde CReIAMO PA (75%), and Blu CReIAMO PA (25%).

**Typography Information:**

- The primary typeface is Source Sans Pro. It is an open-source font designed by Paul D. Hunt and part of the Google Fonts library. Recommended weights include Light, Regular, Semibold, and Bold, with optional ExtraLight and Black. When unavailable, Arial may be used as an alternative.

**Imagery Photography:**

- Textures featuring brand motifs can be used creatively across all communication materials, in both institutional colors or light variations for backgrounds. These textures include patterns created from repetitive uses of the logo elements and pictograms.
- Four illustrations derived from foundational logo elements can be utilized across communication materials. They may feature institutional colors or recolored variations, contributing to consistent branding.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The minimum safe zone around the logo equals the height of the 'CReIAMO PA' wordmark. This clear area must remain unoccupied.
- Text and imagery must adhere to clear spacing rules relative to the logo, ensuring proper composition and adherence to the minimum spacing guidelines.

**Notes:**

- Incorrect logo usage includes modifying its size, typography, colors, or internal arrangement, distorting the logo, or disregarding spacing and alignment rules.
- Additional examples of brochure layout and branded application formats (e.g., stationery, newsletter) appear in sections dedicated to applications.

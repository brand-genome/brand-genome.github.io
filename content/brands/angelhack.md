## Brand Guidelines

### 2013

**Logo Information:**

- The AngelHack logo is preferred to be used on a white or light background. If placed on a dark background, it can be 'knocked out' (used in white color). Usage examples are visually shown.
- Incorrect Logo Usage Cases: Avoid changing the logo's orientation, adding extraneous effects, placing it on busy photography, changing logo colors, attempting to recreate the logo, or scaling the logo disproportionately.

**Color Information:**

- AngelHack logo colors are mentioned as follows: White (HEX: #FFFFFF, RGB: R:255 G:255 B:255, CMYK: C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:0), Red (HEX: #BE1622, RGB: R:190 G:22 B:34, CMYK: C:15 M:100 Y:90 K:10), Black (HEX: #1D1D1D, RGB: R:29 G:29 B:29, CMYK: C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:100). HSB codes are also specified (White: HSB 192 000 100, Red: HSB 354 088 074, Black: HSB 059 006 011).
- Colors are recommended to be used sparingly to maintain the prominence of the logo.

**Typography Information:**

- AngelHack uses Helvetica font family. Recommended typefaces include Helvetica Neue Light and Helvetica Neue Medium for use across web, mobile apps, posters, and graphics.
- Examples of Helvetica font and its weight variations are visually represented.

**Brand Voice:**

- AngelHack organizes hackathon competitions for web developers and entrepreneurs, fostering developer learning, mentorship, and startup opportunities. Winners are mentored and introduced to Silicon Valley incubators.

**Notes:**

- The AngelHack crown serves social media purposes and is more versatile than the logo, adaptable to rounded and non-rounded corners. It can be styled subtly to integrate into icon sets.
- Iconography guidelines specify white icons on a red background occupying 60% of the icon area.

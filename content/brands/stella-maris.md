## Brand Guidelines

### 2020

**Logo Information:**

- The logo contains symbolism derived from the context of maritime and Catholic foundations, such as an anchor (hope), lifesaver (faith), heart (charity), rays of light (light of Christ), and waves (mission environment).
- Minimum clear space around the logo is equal to the height of the heart within the mark. Minimum size is 15mm or 42 pixels high. Horizontal variant can be used in areas with restricted space.
- Logo versions: Full Color, Single Color, Black and White, and Reversed (Full Color and White for dark backgrounds are allowed).
- Examples of incorrect usages include changing logo colors, resizing, altering proportions/elements, converting Full Color to Black and White improperly, and placing the logo on complex backgrounds.
- The logo can include a strapline: "Supporting Seafarers and Fishers around the World." Font is Nunito Sans Semi Bold with tracking (+50), set in all caps, and centered below the vertical logo.
- The logo can be combined with a location name, positioned and sized beneath "Stella Maris" following defined spacing rules. Either strapline OR location name can be used—not both.
- File formats: RGB formats (JPG, PNG, EPS) for digital use, CMYK/Pantone formats (JPG, PNG, EPS) for print. EPS recommended for large-scale reproduction.

**Color Information:**

- Primary Palette: Dark Blue (Pantone 540C, HEX #0c3451, RGB 12/52/81), Orange (Pantone 158C, HEX #ee7424, RGB 238/116/36). Secondary Palette includes Bright Blue, Red, Green, Yellow with Pantone, HEX, and RGB codes provided.
- Neutral Palette: Includes Black, White, and Pale Warm Grey with defined CMYK, HEX, and RGB codes. Neutral colors can be tinted; Primary and Secondary Palette must remain at full strength.

**Typography Information:**

- Primary Font for brand: Nunito Sans. Font weights available: Light, Semi-Bold, Extra-Bold. To be used for headlines and/or text in communications.
- Secondary Font: Halant. Font weights: Regular, Medium, Semi-Bold. An alternative for headlines or text where additional styles are necessary.
- Verdana (Regular, Bold) is suggested for using in environments where Nunito Sans is unavailable (e.g., computers lacking proper font installation).
- Non-Latin character languages should use Google Fonts like Noto Sans for Chinese, Japanese, Cyrillic with download links provided.

**Spacing Layout:**

- Strapline spacing rules: Vertically positioned with calculations based on the logo size (e.g., 1.5x, 0.5x).
- Location names spacing positioned beneath the logo with specific multipliers (e.g., x, .6x spacing).

**Notes:**

- Examples provided for using the logo and branding across stationery, report designs, website layouts, pull-up banners, clothing, and livery designs.
- Additional graphic elements using angular shapes and primary/secondary palette colors for differentiation within specific campaigns or appealing content.

## Brand Guidelines

### 2016

**Logo Information:**

- The LIFE logo has white text on a red background and is iconic and recognizable. For print use, PMS 485C is specified; for digital, RGB (213, 43, 30); and for web, Hex Code #D52B1E. The 'protected area' around the logo is defined as a clear margin based on the width of the letter 'I' in 'LIFE'. It should not be recolored, rotated, distorted, or placed on a colored background. Placement generally should be in the top left on a white background or image as long as legibility is maintained.
- Logo restrictions: The 'LIFE' logo should always appear in uppercase letters. It should not be used within sentences or form new words. Mixing uppercase and lowercase letters (e.g., 'Life') is prohibited.

**Color Information:**

- Primary color palette includes LIFE RED (Pantone 485C): PMS 485C | CMYK: 0/96/100/0 | RGB: 213/43/30 | HEX: #D52B1E. Additional colors: White, Black C, 427C, 430C, 432C, 7741C, 2296C, 7689C, 5473C, 7694C, 2226C, 122C, 7413C, 493C, 2046C, 7659C, and 229C.
- These colors are utilized throughout the branding and are developed to give the LIFE brand a fresh and bold appearance.

**Typography Information:**

- Folio® font family is recommended. Font weights include Light (body text), Medium (body text), Bold (headings and text highlights), Extra Bold (headings and text highlights), and Bold Condensed (titles and subtitles).

**Imagery Photography:**

- Images should adhere to the LIFE aesthetic with a focus on iconic visuals, clean arrangements, and minimal distractions. Photography should replicate the style of the iconic LIFE magazine.

**Spacing Layout:**

- The 'protected area' around the LIFE logo is defined by the width of the letter 'I' from the word 'LIFE'. No elements may intrude into this protected space.
- Logo placement should be top-left with equal borders unless printed large enough to allow alternative placement while maintaining proportions.

**Brand Voice:**

- The brand voice embraces LIFE's historical legacy, invoking an iconic and bold tone that emphasizes its dedication to excellence in photojournalism.

**Notes:**

- Legal information outlines that the LIFE Picture Collection owns photos but not the rights to sculptures, illustrations, or paintings. Users must assume risk and indemnify LIFE against claims.
- Packaging guidelines suggest simplicity and minimalism to maintain the magazine style, with careful attention to imagery and text placement.
